## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.** The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until December 6, 2028 (the “**Expiration Date**”) (such period, the “**Initial Term**”).

4.2 **Renewal.** Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement may be renewed for additional one (1)-year periods (each a “**Renewal Term**”) by mutual written agreement of the Parties at least ninety (90) days prior to the Expiration Date or the then-current expiration date. Any Renewal Term shall be on the terms and conditions of this Agreement, except that Fees may be adjusted as mutually agreed.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.** Contoso may terminate this Agreement, any Order Form, or any SOW for convenience at any time upon not less than thirty (30) days’ prior written notice to Summit Tech. Summit Tech may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon not less than sixty (60) days’ prior written notice to Contoso, provided that any such termination by Summit Tech shall not become effective prior to the completion of any then in-progress SOW unless Contoso agrees otherwise in writing.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement, any Order Form, or any SOW upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof (ten (10) days in the case of a failure to pay undisputed amounts when due). If the breach is not capable of cure, the non-breaching Party may terminate immediately upon written notice.

4.5 **Insolvency.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) becomes insolvent or generally unable to pay its debts as they become due; (b) files or has filed against it a petition for bankruptcy or similar relief which is not dismissed within sixty (60) days; (c) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors; or (d) ceases to carry on business in the ordinary course.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**

4.6.1 **Cessation of Use.** Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason, all licenses and rights granted to Contoso with respect to the Software shall immediately terminate, and Contoso shall cease all access to and use of the Software and Documentation (except to the extent necessary during a limited transition period expressly agreed by the Parties in writing).

4.6.2 **Return of Materials.** Each Party shall promptly return or destroy (at the disclosing Party’s election) all Confidential Information of the other Party in its possession or control, except that each Party may retain one (1) archival copy solely for record-keeping and compliance purposes, subject to ongoing confidentiality obligations.

4.6.3 **Fees and Payments.** Termination shall not relieve Contoso of the obligation to pay any Fees accrued or payable prior to the effective date of termination. In the event of termination by Contoso for Summit Tech’s uncured material breach, Summit Tech shall refund to Contoso any prepaid, unused Fees on a pro rata basis, calculated from the effective date of termination. In the event of termination by Summit Tech for Contoso’s breach or for Contoso’s convenience, all Fees paid are non-refundable, and any unpaid amounts for Services performed or commitments made shall become immediately due and payable.

4.6.4 **Survival.** The rights and obligations of the Parties under Sections 3.4, 3.5, 4.6, 5, 6, 7, 8, 9, 11, 12.4, 14, 15, 16, and any other provisions which by their nature should survive, shall survive expiration or termination of this Agreement.

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT

12.1 **Service Availability.** Summit Tech shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the hosted components of the Software are available at least 99.9% of the time in each calendar month, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Excluded Downtime (as defined below) (“**Service Availability Target**”).

12.2.1 **Scheduled Maintenance.** Summit Tech may perform scheduled maintenance on the hosted environment, typically during off-peak hours. Summit Tech shall use commercially reasonable efforts to provide Contoso with at least forty-eight (48) hours’ prior notice of any scheduled maintenance expected to cause material downtime, and to limit scheduled maintenance to no more than eight (8) hours per month.

12.2.2 **Excluded Downtime.** For purposes of calculating service availability, downtime shall not include: (a) Scheduled Maintenance; (b) failures or delays attributable to Contoso’s systems, networks, or third-party providers; (c) Force Majeure Events; or (d) suspension of services in accordance with this Agreement due to Contoso’s breach or non-payment (collectively, “**Excluded Downtime**”).

12.3 **Response and Resolution Times.** Summit Tech shall respond to support requests in accordance with the following guidelines (time measured during support hours):

- **Severity 1 (Critical):** Complete loss of service or severe impact to production operations, no workaround available.  
  - Initial Response: within 1 hour  
  - Target Workaround/Resolution: continuous efforts until resolved or workaround identified.

- **Severity 2 (High):** Significant impairment of major functionality, workaround possible but not sustainable.  
  - Initial Response: within 4 hours  
  - Target Workaround/Resolution: within 2 Business Days.

- **Severity 3 (Medium):** Moderate impact on non-critical functions, workaround available.  
  - Initial Response: within 1 Business Day  
  - Target Workaround/Resolution: within 10 Business Days or included in next scheduled release.

- **Severity 4 (Low):** Minor issues, cosmetic defects, or general inquiries.  
  - Initial Response: within 2 Business Days  
  - Target Workaround/Resolution: as reasonably scheduled.

12.4 **Service Credits.** If the Service Availability Target is not met in a given calendar month, Contoso shall be entitled, as its sole and exclusive remedy for such failure (subject to Article IX), to a service credit equal to a percentage of the monthly subscription Fees for the affected Software for such month, as follows:

- Availability of at least 99.0% but less than 99.9%: 5% credit  
- Availability of at least 97.0% but less than 99.0%: 10% credit  
- Availability of less than 97.0%: 20% credit

To receive a service credit, Contoso must request it in writing within thirty (30) days after the end of the month in which the availability failure occurred. Service credits shall be applied against future Fees due under this Agreement and shall not be redeemable for cash. In no event shall service credits exceed 20% of the monthly subscription Fees for the affected month.
