## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed **under and subject to** the terms of the Parent Agreement, including **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, except as expressly modified herein. Pursuant to **Section 2.7 (Statements of Work)**, this Addendum shall be deemed an SOW referencing the Parent Agreement, and, in the event of conflict, the terms of this Addendum shall control with respect to the additional products and services described herein, solely to the extent of such conflict.

2.1.2 The Services under this Addendum form part of the overall **Project** as defined in **Section 1.14** of the Parent Agreement, and are intended to expand and deepen the modernization effort across additional business units, geographies, and technology platforms.

2.2 **General Scope.**  

2.2.1 Pinnacle shall furnish to Contoso, in consideration for payment of the Fees described in **Article III**, the following expanded Services (collectively, the “**Addendum Services**”):

  (a) **Extended Assessment and Strategy** – Building upon the assessment activities described in **Section 2.2.1 (Discovery and Assessment)** of the Parent Agreement, Pinnacle will conduct additional workshops, technical assessments, and business stakeholder interviews focusing on Contoso’s newly acquired business units and international operations. Pinnacle will evaluate integration points with existing modernized systems, identify duplication of functionality, and recommend consolidation strategies.

  (b) **Advanced Solution Design** – In addition to the design activities described in **Section 2.2.2**, Pinnacle will design a layered architecture for a multi-region deployment, including high-availability topologies, disaster recovery approaches, global data residency considerations, and security reference architectures. Design deliverables will include updated architecture diagrams, data flow maps, integration blueprints, and security models.

  (c) **Implementation, Integration, and Migration** – Expanding the implementation and configuration scope described in **Section 2.2.3**, Pinnacle will (i) implement Additional Products (see **Section 2.3.2** below) for monitoring, observability, and centralized configuration management; (ii) integrate legacy international systems with the existing integration backbone; and (iii) perform data migration and cutover activities for the newly integrated systems into the Production Environment.

  (d) **Enhanced Managed Services and Support** – In addition to the support described in **Section 2.2.7** and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, Pinnacle will provide Enhanced Managed Services, including 24x7 monitoring of Critical Systems, advanced incident management, proactive capacity planning, performance tuning, and quarterly health checks and optimization reviews.

  (e) **Training and Change Enablement** – Building on **Section 2.2.6**, Pinnacle will develop and deliver enhanced training programs tailored to international end-users, administrators, and local support personnel, including localized documentation where agreed, along with change management advisory services to increase adoption.

2.2.2 Pinnacle shall perform the Addendum Services in a **professional and workmanlike manner**, consistent with **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, and in compliance with Applicable Laws, including Data Protection Laws as defined in **Section 1.7** of the Parent Agreement and as expanded upon in **Article XI** of this Addendum.

2.2.3 **Work Packages and Phases.** The Addendum Services will be organized into Work Packages across the following phases:

  (a) **Phase 1 – Discovery and Strategy Alignment (Months 1–3)**  
  - Work Package 1.1: International Systems Assessment  
  - Work Package 1.2: Consolidated Roadmap and Priority Matrix  
  Deliverables: Updated assessment report, risk register, roadmap presentation.

  (b) **Phase 2 – Design and Architecture (Months 3–6)**  
  - Work Package 2.1: Global Architecture Blueprint  
  - Work Package 2.2: Integration and Data Strategy  
  Deliverables: Design specification documents, architecture diagrams, integration specification, security and compliance addendum.

  (c) **Phase 3 – Implementation and Migration (Months 6–18)**  
  - Work Package 3.1: Additional Products Deployment  
  - Work Package 3.2: Integration and Data Migration  
  - Work Package 3.3: Pilot and Production Rollout  
  Deliverables: Configured platforms, runbooks, migration plans and reports, go-live sign-off documents.

  (d) **Phase 4 – Enhanced Managed Services (Months 9–End of Addendum Term)**  
  - Work Package 4.1: 24x7 Monitoring & Incident Response  
  - Work Package 4.2: Quarterly Health Checks & Optimization  
  Deliverables: Monthly and quarterly performance reports, incident trend analyses, capacity plans, optimization recommendations.

2.3 **Deliverables.**

2.3.1 In addition to the categories identified in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, Pinnacle shall produce the following Deliverables under this Addendum (the “**Addendum Deliverables**”):

  (a) **Global Assessment and Strategy Deliverables** – Updated assessment report including gap analyses for international systems, dependency mapping, and a prioritized roadmap aligning with Contoso’s enterprise strategy.

  (b) **Architectural and Design Deliverables** –  
  - Detailed multi-region system architecture diagrams;  
  - Data residency and compliance design documents;  
  - High-availability and disaster recovery runbooks;  
  - Security control matrices and threat modeling documentation.

  (c) **Implementation and Configuration Deliverables** –  
  - Configured Additional Products (monitoring, observability, configuration management);  
  - Integration adapters, APIs, and data mapping specifications;  
  - Deployment runbooks, rollback procedures, and cutover plans;  
  - Post-migration verification reports and performance baselines.

  (d) **Managed Services Deliverables** –  
  - Monthly operational performance reports;  
  - Incident response reports including root cause analysis for Severity 1 and 2 incidents;  
  - Quarterly health-check and capacity planning reports;  
  - Annual strategic review presentation.

  (e) **Training and Knowledge Transfer Deliverables** –  
  - Curriculum outlines, localized user guides, and administrator manuals;  
  - Training session materials and recordings where permitted;  
  - Knowledge transfer documentation, including operational checklists and best practices.

2.3.2 **Additional Products.** Pinnacle shall deliver and configure the Additional Products, which may include Vendor Pre-Existing IP, third-party tools, and custom configurations. Ownership and licensing of such Additional Products shall be governed by **Article VI** of this Addendum and **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 6.2 (Vendor Pre-Existing IP)**, **6.3 (License to Vendor Pre-Existing IP)**, and **6.4 (Third-Party Materials)**.

2.3.3 **Acceptance.** Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables shall follow the process in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, with the following clarifications:

  (a) The ten (10) Business Day review period shall apply to each Addendum Deliverable, unless otherwise specified in a Work Package.  

  (b) Contoso shall provide any rejection notice describing material non-conformities with the applicable specifications in the relevant Work Package or design document.  

  (c) Pinnacle shall correct any validly rejected Deliverables and resubmit for acceptance within a commercially reasonable timeframe, taking into account the severity and complexity of the non-conformity.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.4.1 Each Work Package shall include target start and completion dates, milestones, and any associated milestone-based payment triggers consistent with **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, as further refined in **Section 3.2** of this Addendum.

2.4.2 Contoso acknowledges that “Time is not of the essence” as stated in **Section 2.5.1** of the Parent Agreement; however, Pinnacle shall use **commercially reasonable efforts** to meet agreed dates and, in the event of material delay, shall comply with **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent Agreement, providing explanations and mitigation plans.

2.5 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  

Contoso’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement apply to the Addendum Services, including timely provision of information, system access, and approvals, and are expanded as follows:

  (a) Contoso shall designate global and regional project managers to coordinate with Pinnacle.  

  (b) Contoso shall ensure that necessary third-party consents or licenses for access to international systems are obtained.  

  (c) Contoso shall promptly identify Critical Systems and data residency constraints in writing.  

  (d) Contoso shall support change management activities by providing internal communications channels and access to key stakeholders.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The Parties agree that the estimated aggregate value of the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables under this Addendum is **US $4,477,953** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), which is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US $3,687,468** set forth in **Section 3.1** and the Recitals of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 The Addendum Contract Value is subject to increases or decreases arising from approved Change Orders pursuant to **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

The Fees payable by Contoso to Pinnacle under this Addendum shall follow the categories described in **Section 3.2** of the Parent Agreement, comprised of:

  (a) **Fixed Fees** – For specified Work Packages and Deliverables:  
  - Phase 1 & 2 Strategy and Design: US $1,100,000  
  - Phase 3 Implementation and Migration: US $2,200,000  

  (b) **Time and Materials (“T&M”) Fees** – For certain consulting, change management, and unscoped activities:  
  - Estimated T&M Budget: US $477,953, billed at the hourly rates set forth in a rate card agreed in writing by the Parties.

  (c) **Recurring Fees** – For Enhanced Managed Services:  
  - Monthly Managed Services Fee: US $70,000 per month, commencing upon the earlier of (i) completion of the first Major Release deployment, or (ii) nine (9) months after the Addendum Effective Date, and continuing through the Addendum Term.

  (d) **Reimbursable Expenses** – As described in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement and in **Section 3.5** of this Addendum.

3.3 **Payment Terms (Net 45).**

3.3.1 **Invoices.** Pinnacle shall invoice Contoso for:

  - Fixed Fee components upon completion of specified milestones and acceptance of associated Deliverables;  
  - T&M Fees on a monthly basis in arrears; and  
  - Recurring Managed Services Fees monthly in advance.

3.3.2 **Net 45.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.2 (Net 30)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that for this Addendum, all undisputed amounts invoiced by Pinnacle shall be payable by Contoso within **forty-five (45) calendar days** from the date of receipt of the invoice (“**Net 45**”).

3.3.3 **Disputed Amounts.** The procedures set forth in **Section 3.3.3** of the Parent Agreement regarding disputed amounts shall apply to invoices under this Addendum, except that the fifteen (15) day notice period remains unchanged. Contoso shall pay undisputed portions within Net 45, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve disputes.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

For any undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Addendum:

  (a) Interest shall accrue in accordance with **Section 3.4.1 (Interest)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (b) Pinnacle may exercise suspension rights consistent with **Section 3.4.2 (Suspension Rights)**, provided that such suspension shall apply specifically to Addendum Services unless undisputed defaults are broader.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.5.1 Contoso shall reimburse Pinnacle for reasonable, actual out-of-pocket travel and related expenses incurred in connection with the Addendum Services, in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement, and subject to Contoso’s travel and expense policy as updated from time to time and provided in writing.

3.5.2 Pinnacle shall provide documentation of such expenses consistent with **Section 3.5.2** and shall not mark up expenses in accordance with **Section 3.5.3**.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. For the avoidance of doubt, Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes.

3.7 **No Set-Off.**  

The **No Set-Off** provision in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement applies fully to Fees under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

The term of this Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (September 30, 2024)** and shall remain in effect until **July 18, 2026**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  

4.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement has a Term ending **May 29, 2025** (Section 4.1 of the Parent Agreement). Notwithstanding the Expiration Date of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree as follows:

  (a) The Parent Agreement shall be deemed **automatically extended** solely for purposes of governing this Addendum and the Addendum Services through the Addendum Term, unless the Parties mutually agree in writing to replace the Parent Agreement with a successor master agreement; or  

  (b) In the alternative, if Applicable Law requires a formal renewal, the Parties undertake to enter into such renewal or successor agreement on terms substantially similar to the Parent Agreement, with any necessary modifications, before May 29, 2025.

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is not renewed or replaced as provided above, this Addendum shall nevertheless remain in effect and the terms of the Parent Agreement, as amended by this Addendum, shall continue to govern the relationship between the Parties solely with respect to the Addendum Services until the earlier of (i) completion of all Addendum Services and payment of all Fees, or (ii) the Addendum Termination Date.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (45 days)** of the Parent Agreement, either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience, without cause, upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party.

4.3.2 Termination for convenience of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement unless expressly stated in the notice.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause if the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement as it relates to the Addendum Services, and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice, consistent with **Section 4.4.1 (Material Breach)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon the occurrence of any of the insolvency events described in **Section 4.4.2 (Insolvency)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**

4.5.1 **Payment Obligations.** Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum, Contoso shall pay Pinnacle for:

  (a) All Addendum Services performed and Addendum Deliverables delivered and accepted up to the effective date of termination;  

  (b) Any non-cancellable commitments or pass-through costs incurred with Contoso’s approval; and  

  (c) Any approved expenses incurred prior to termination.

In the case of termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall also pay Pinnacle for any reasonable and documented wind-down costs directly attributable to such termination and not reasonably avoidable, consistent with **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.2 **Return of Materials.** The obligations regarding the return or destruction of Confidential Information in **Section 4.5.2** of the Parent Agreement and **Article V** of this Addendum shall apply.

4.5.3 **Transition Assistance Services.** If requested by Contoso at least sixty (60) days prior to the effective date of termination of this Addendum, Pinnacle shall provide Transition Assistance Services for up to ninety (90) days after termination at Pinnacle’s then-current T&M rates, which extends the sixty (60) day period referenced in **Section 4.5.3** of the Parent Agreement for the high-risk nature of this Addendum.

4.5.4 **Survival.** In addition to the survival provisions of **Section 4.5.4** of the Parent Agreement, the following provisions of this Addendum shall survive termination or expiration: Articles III (as to accrued payment obligations), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, and XVI.

4.6 **Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety under **Article IV** thereof, then, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in writing, this Addendum shall also terminate as of the same effective date of termination.

4.6.2 Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Parties may agree in writing that this Addendum shall survive the termination of the Parent Agreement and be governed by its terms as modified by this Addendum through the remainder of the Addendum Term.
