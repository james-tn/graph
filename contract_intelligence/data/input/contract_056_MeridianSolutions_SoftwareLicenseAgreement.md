  a. Core transaction processing and workflow management;  
  b. Analytics and reporting dashboards;  
  c. Role-based access control and user management;  
  d. Integration APIs to connect with Contoso’s existing CRM, ERP, and HRIS systems;  
  e. Audit logs, system monitoring, and administrative tools.

Further details regarding module configurations, capacity (e.g., number of Users, transaction volumes), hardware and software prerequisites, and other technical specifications are set forth in **SOW #1**, which shall be attached hereto as **Exhibit A** and incorporated herein by reference.

  a. Contoso shall remain liable for all Fees accrued and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination;  
  b. Any prepaid, unused Fees for the period after the effective date of termination shall be refunded on a pro rata basis, except for any non-refundable setup, implementation, or professional service Fees expressly stated as such in the applicable SOW.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement for cause by providing written notice to the other Party if:

  a. The other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail (the “**Cure Period**”); or  
  b. The other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, has a receiver appointed, or ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

4.5 **Termination for Security or Legal Concerns.** Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, Contoso may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:

  a. Meridian experiences a material security incident or data breach that, in Contoso’s reasonable judgment, materially threatens the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of Contoso’s data and Meridian fails to implement reasonably satisfactory remedial measures within thirty (30) days of notice; or  
  b. Continuing to use the Software or Services would, in Contoso’s reasonable opinion, cause Contoso to be in material violation of applicable law or regulatory requirements.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.** Upon any expiration or termination of this Agreement:

  a. All licenses granted to Contoso under this Agreement shall immediately terminate, and Contoso shall cease all use of the Software and Documentation;  
  b. Contoso shall promptly return or certify destruction of any copies of the Software and Documentation in its possession or control, except as required for archival, legal, or regulatory compliance purposes;

  d. Each Party shall promptly return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 5.4**.

4.7 **Survival.** The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration of this Agreement shall so survive, including but not limited to **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XVI (General Provisions)**, and any payment obligations accruing prior to such termination or expiration.

  c. **Priority 3 (Medium)** – Moderate impact or workaround available: Initial response within one (1) Business Day;
