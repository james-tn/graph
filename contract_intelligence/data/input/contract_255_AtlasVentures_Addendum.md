## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum supplements and refines the “Services” described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. All obligations, standards, and processes set forth in **Sections 2.4 (Performance Standards)**, **2.5 (Client Responsibilities)**, **2.6 (Personnel and Subcontracting)**, **2.7 (Acceptance of Deliverables)**, and **2.8 (Location of Services)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables, except where expressly modified herein.

2.1.2 This Addendum does not modify or reduce any Services or Deliverables already committed under SOW-ATL-202402-246; instead, it provides additional scope and associated funding (the Addendum Contract Value) for further phases of the Program, particularly beyond the initial stabilization period contemplated under **Section 2.2.4** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Services.**  

Vendor shall provide the following categories of Addendum Services to Client:

2.2.1 **Post-Implementation Optimization Services.**  
Building upon the cloud infrastructure, migrated applications, analytics use cases, and operational runbooks delivered pursuant to **Phase 3 – Implementation & Migration (Section 2.2.3)** and **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Knowledge Transfer (Section 2.2.4)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

  (a) Conduct periodic performance and cost reviews of the cloud infrastructure and analytics workloads implemented under the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) Identify optimization opportunities related to resource sizing, storage tiering, network topology, caching, and data pipeline efficiency;  

  (c) Produce quarterly **Optimization Assessment Reports**, including recommendations prioritized by impact and effort, and estimated savings;  

  (d) Implement agreed optimization recommendations, subject to Client’s written approval (email sufficient) and the Change Management process under **Article XIII** where material changes to scope or cost arise; and  

  (e) Maintain and update the **Security and Compliance Framework Documentation (D6)** and **Operational Runbooks (D11)** delivered under the Parent Agreement to reflect optimization changes and evolving Data Protection Laws.

2.2.2 **Advanced Analytics Services.**  
In addition to the analytics use cases and data pipelines envisioned in **Section 2.2.3 (Phase 3)** and Deliverables **D9 (Data Models, Data Pipelines, and Analytics Dashboard Specifications)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

  (a) Design and implement up to **ten (10)** additional analytics use cases over the Term of this Addendum, each with defined business value hypotheses, success metrics, and stakeholder sponsors identified by Client;  

  (b) Extend existing data models and pipelines (D9) to incorporate new data sources, including external and third-party data sets, subject to licensing and Data Protection Laws referenced in **Sections 1.7, 2.4.3, and Article XI** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (c) Develop and deploy new dashboards, reports, and self-service analytics components aligned with Client’s governance and security policies;  

  (d) Provide advanced analytics enablement, including training and knowledge transfer sessions (aligned with **Deliverable D12 – Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Sessions**) targeted at Client’s analytics, business, and operations teams; and  

  (e) Assist Client in establishing and refining analytics development and deployment standards, including code repositories, DevOps/ML Ops practices, and testing approaches, consistent with the “Testing Strategy, Test Plans, and Test Reports (D10)” framework.

2.2.3 **Managed Services Add-On.**  
To extend the support contemplated by **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Knowledge Transfer** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, the Vendor shall provide optional Managed Services under this Addendum as follows:

  (a) **Production Monitoring & Incident Management.** Vendor shall provide monitoring configuration, alert triage, and incident resolution support for the cloud and analytics components delivered under the Program, subject to the Enhanced Service Levels in **Article XII** of this Addendum;  

  (b) **Minor Enhancements and Configuration Changes.** Vendor shall perform minor changes (estimated effort less than forty (40) person-hours per change) to configurations, dashboards, and non-core data models as part of the Managed Services scope, with larger efforts handled under the Change Management process;  

  (c) **Release Management Support.** Vendor shall assist with coordinating releases, including migration runbooks (extending **Deliverable D7**) and conducting pre- and post-release validation aligned with the testing framework previously agreed; and  

  (d) **Knowledge Transfer and Documentation Updates.** Vendor shall update the **Operational Runbooks and Support Procedures (D11)** and **Training Materials (D12)** to reflect new configurations, enhancements, and operational practices introduced under the Managed Services.

2.3 **Addendum Deliverables.**  

Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, Vendor shall provide the following additional Deliverables under this Addendum (collectively, the “Addendum Deliverables”), each subject to Client’s review and acceptance in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement:

- **AD1 – Extended Optimization Assessment Reports** (quarterly), summarizing performance, cost, risk, and recommended changes;  
- **AD2 – Updated Architecture & Roadmap Supplement**, reflecting optimization and additional analytics capabilities, and complementing **D4 (Target Enterprise Architecture Diagrams and Specifications)** and **D5 (Multi-Year Transformation Roadmap)**;  
- **AD3 – Advanced Analytics Use Case Blueprints**, for up to ten (10) use cases, including data flows, logical models, and KPI definitions;  
- **AD4 – Additional Data Pipelines and Dashboard Implementations**, including documentation, configuration details, and acceptance criteria;  
- **AD5 – Managed Services Runbooks Addendum**, detailing monitoring, escalation, and resolution procedures consistent with the Enhanced Service Levels;  
- **AD6 – Training and Enablement Packages**, including updated training materials and recordings designed to build Client’s internal capability to own and extend the implemented solutions; and  
- **AD7 – Quarterly Governance and Performance Review Packs**, documenting SLA performance, incident trends, enhancement backlog status, and continuous improvement recommendations.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.4.1 The Addendum Services are anticipated to commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue through the Addendum Expiration Date, subject to early termination as described in **Article IV**.  

2.4.2 Key Milestones include, at a minimum:

  (a) **M1 – Initial Optimization Baseline Assessment:** Within sixty (60) days of the Addendum Effective Date, Vendor shall complete a baseline assessment and deliver the first **Optimization Assessment Report (AD1)**.  

  (b) **M2 – First Wave of Advanced Analytics Use Cases:** Within one hundred eighty (180) days of the Addendum Effective Date, Vendor shall design, implement, and transition into production at least three (3) new analytics use cases (AD3/AD4).  

  (c) **M3 – Managed Services Onboarding Completion:** Within ninety (90) days of the Addendum Effective Date (or such later date as mutually agreed if onboarding is phased), Vendor shall finalize the Managed Services Runbooks Addendum (AD5) and commence full Enhanced Service Level obligations.  

  (d) **M4 – Annual Governance Review:** On each anniversary of the Addendum Effective Date during the Term, Vendor shall provide the Quarterly Governance and Performance Review Pack (AD7) summarizing the preceding twelve (12) months and proposing a forward-looking roadmap.

2.4.3 Each Milestone shall be associated with specific payment events as described in **Article III**. Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables shall follow the same process as set forth in **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement (including the fifteen (15) Business Day Review Period, re-submission rights, and remedies).

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services:

  (a) In accordance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) Using personnel and, where applicable, subcontractors consistent with **Section 2.6 (Personnel and Subcontracting)** of the Parent Agreement, including continuity of Key Personnel;  

  (c) In compliance with all applicable laws, including the Data Protection Laws defined in **Section 1.7** and detailed in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum; and  

  (d) In accordance with the Enhanced Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum for the Managed Services scope, which shall be deemed “Service Levels” under **Section 1.17** of the Parent Agreement for such scope.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities (Supplement).**  

In addition to the Client Responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Client shall:

  (a) Identify business sponsors, subject matter experts, and data owners for each Advanced Analytics use case (AD3);  

  (b) Provide timely access to any new data sources (including third-party data) required for Advanced Analytics Services, subject to appropriate license arrangements and Data Protection Laws;  

  (c) Participate in quarterly governance and performance review sessions and provide feedback on proposed optimization and enhancement priorities; and  

  (d) Maintain its own internal user access controls and data governance procedures in alignment with the Security and Compliance Framework (D6/AD2).

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Addendum.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue until the **Addendum Expiration Date of June 27, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or as a result of expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement, as described in **Section 4.5**.

4.2.1 The Term of the Parent Agreement currently extends to February 4, 2026, as set forth in **Section 4.1** thereof. The Parties acknowledge that performance of certain Addendum Services may extend beyond the current Parent Term and, consequently, agree that they will negotiate in good faith any necessary renewal or extension of the Parent Agreement to align with the Addendum Term.  

4.2.2 Any extension of the Parent Agreement shall be documented in writing, consistent with **Section 4.2** and **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly).**  

4.3.1 **Client Right.** Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Vendor. This reflects a client-favorable risk profile, consistent with the “Low” Risk Level designated for this Addendum.  

4.3.2 **Vendor Right.** Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client, and only after completion of any active critical Milestones under this Addendum that have been mutually identified in writing as critical, consistent with the approach in **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (ten (10) days for payment breaches) after receiving written notice specifying the breach, consistent with **Section 4.4.1** of the Parent Agreement.  

4.4.2 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party becomes insolvent or is the subject of certain bankruptcy events described in **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:  

  (a) Vendor shall cease performing Addendum Services (except as expressly requested by Client for transition assistance under **Section 4.6**), but shall continue performing any remaining obligations under the Parent Agreement, unless that agreement has also been terminated;  

  (b) Vendor shall deliver to Client all Addendum Deliverables completed and accepted as of the termination date, as well as any in-progress work that Client has paid for or agrees to pay for; and  

  (c) Client shall pay Vendor for all undisputed Fees and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination for Addendum Services, consistent with **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.2 If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination, except to the extent the Parties expressly agree in writing to preserve parts of this Addendum (e.g., certain IP licenses, confidentiality or post-termination obligations) beyond such date.

4.5.3 In case of termination of this Addendum due to Vendor’s uncured material breach, Client shall have no obligation to pay Fees for any materially non-conforming Addendum Services or Addendum Deliverables not accepted, consistent with **Section 4.5.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6 **Transition Assistance.**  

In the event of expiration or termination of this Addendum (other than due to Client’s uncured material breach), Vendor shall, upon Client’s request, provide reasonable transition assistance with respect to the Addendum Services for up to sixty (60) days, at Vendor’s then-current time-and-materials rates, subject to a mutually agreed transition plan, consistent with **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement. This transition obligation reflects the low-risk, client-friendly nature of this Addendum.

4.7 **Survival.**  

The following provisions of this Addendum shall survive its expiration or termination: **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XII (Service Level Agreements), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), XVI (General Provisions)**, and any provisions which by their nature should survive, in addition to the survival provisions of the Parent Agreement referenced in **Section 4.7** thereof.
