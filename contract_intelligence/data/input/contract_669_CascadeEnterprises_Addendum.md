## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS, SERVICES, AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This **Article II** supplements **Article II (Scope of Products, Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Unless expressly modified herein, all obligations, standards, and responsibilities relating to Products, Services, and Deliverables set forth in the Parent Agreement (including **Sections 2.1 – 2.9**) apply equally to the Addendum Products, Addendum Services, and Addendum Deliverables. In case of conflict between the Parent Agreement and this Addendum with respect to the matters covered herein, this Addendum shall control solely for those matters, subject to **Section 16.13 (Order of Precedence)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Addendum Products.**  
Under this Addendum, Cascade shall provide the following Addendum Products, all of which are “Products” as defined in Section 1.13 of the Parent Agreement and subject to the Product warranties in **Section 7.3** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Additional Infrastructure Hardware.**  
  Cascade shall supply new, enterprise-grade infrastructure hardware, including:  
  (i) Up to 60 additional rack-mount servers with redundant power supplies and enterprise-grade processors, memory, and storage, configured for Contoso’s approved standard builds;  
  (ii) Up to 40 network switches and 12 firewalls to support expanded data center capacity and branch connectivity;  
  (iii) Storage arrays providing not less than 1 PB of usable storage capacity, including SSD and HDD tiers, with replication features enabled as required by Contoso.

  (b) **Cloud Management Platform (CMP) Components.**  
  Cascade shall provide and license, or resell as Third-Party Products where applicable, CMP software and tools for centralized management, monitoring, patching, and automation across the above infrastructure, including:  
  (i) Monitoring agents and dashboards;  
  (ii) Configuration management tools;  
  (iii) Automation/orchestration modules;  
  (iv) Reporting and analytics components.

  (c) **Compliance and Security Add-Ons.**  
  Cascade shall provide security and compliance-related Products, including:  
  (i) Endpoint protection and host-based firewall software;  
  (ii) Vulnerability scanning tools;  
  (iii) Log aggregation and Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) connectors.

All Addendum Products shall be new (not refurbished), conform to manufacturer specifications, and be suitable for Contoso’s use cases as specified in the Project Plan, consistent with **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement. Title and risk of loss shall pass in accordance with **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement, unless expressly modified in **Article III** of this Addendum.

2.3 **Addendum Software and Licenses.**  
The Addendum Products include software and licenses governed by **Section 2.3** and **Section 6.4** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise specified:  
  (a) All license grants are non-exclusive, non-transferable (except as permitted under **Section 16.7** of the Parent Agreement), and limited to Contoso’s internal business purposes;  
  (b) Where CMP or security software is a Third-Party Product, Contoso’s rights are subject to the third-party license terms communicated by Cascade and accepted by Contoso in writing, provided such terms do not conflict with or diminish Contoso’s rights under the Parent Agreement or this Addendum;  
  (c) Any Third-Party Product that would impose obligations inconsistent with **Section 6.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., requiring Contoso to open-source its proprietary code) shall not be implemented without Contoso’s prior written consent.

2.4 **Addendum Deliverables and Documentation.**  
In addition to the Deliverables contemplated in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall provide, at a minimum, the following Addendum Deliverables:

  (a) **Architecture and Design Documentation.**  
  - Detailed reference architecture diagrams for the Addendum Products and CMP;  
  - Network topology and security zoning diagrams;  
  - Capacity and scalability planning documents.

  (b) **Configuration and Implementation Documentation.**  
  - “As-built” configuration documentation for servers, network devices, storage arrays, CMP components, and security tools;  
  - Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for routine operations, patching, and incident handling;  
  - Runbooks for critical incident scenarios and disaster recovery operations.

  (c) **Testing and Validation Documentation.**  
  - Test plans and test scripts for functional, performance, failover, and security testing;  
  - Test reports summarizing test results, issues discovered, and remediation steps taken;  
  - User acceptance test (UAT) sign-off forms.

  (d) **Training Materials.**  
  - Administrator training guides;  
  - End-user quick reference materials (where end-user impact exists);  
  - Recorded training sessions, where feasible, for later reference.

All Addendum Deliverables shall be subject to the acceptance testing provisions and timeframes set out in **Section 2.6(b)** of the Parent Agreement, except that the review period shall be twenty (20) Business Days for major architecture and configuration deliverables identified as such in the Project Plan. Deemed acceptance principles shall apply as stated in **Section 2.6(c)** of the Parent Agreement, without prejudice to Contoso’s warranty rights under **Article VII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article VII** of this Addendum.

2.5 **Enhanced Support Services.**  
The Addendum Services include Enhanced Support Services that supplement the support and maintenance services described in **Section 2.5** and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Scope of Enhanced Support.**  
  - 24x7x365 monitoring of the SLA Environment, including the Addendum Products;  
  - Dedicated technical account manager (TAM) responsible for oversight of the Addendum Services;  
  - Quarterly health checks, security posture reviews, and capacity planning sessions.

  (b) **Service Channels.**  
  Cascade shall maintain, at a minimum, the support portal, telephone, and ticketing system described in **Section 2.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement, with priority routing for Incidents related to the Addendum Products and CMP.

  (c) **Service Levels.**  
  Enhanced Service Levels specific to the SLA Environment are set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum and shall be coordinated with the Service Levels defined in **Section 12.1 et seq.** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Implementation Services and Project Plan.**  
Cascade shall perform implementation, installation, configuration, migration, and integration services (collectively, the “Implementation Services”) in accordance with **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement and the following:

  (a) Within thirty (30) days after the Addendum Effective Date, Cascade shall prepare and submit to Contoso a detailed Project Plan that:  
  - Identifies all tasks, dependencies, and resource assignments;  
  - Defines Implementation Milestones and associated deliverables;  
  - Identifies Contoso responsibilities (e.g., providing access, test data, approvals) consistent with **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement;  
  - Provides an indicative timeline targeted for completion within eighteen (18) months after final Project Plan approval, subject to mutually agreed changes under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

  (b) The first version of the Project Plan shall be subject to review and written approval by Contoso, which shall not be unreasonably withheld or delayed. Upon approval, the Project Plan shall be deemed incorporated into this Addendum by reference.

  (c) Cascade shall assign appropriately skilled personnel, including project managers and engineers, consistent with **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement, and shall provide monthly status reports during active project phases.

2.7 **Performance Standards.**  
In addition to **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) Cascade shall ensure that all Implementation Services and Enhanced Support Services are performed with a level of skill, care, and diligence consistent with leading providers of comparable services in the industry;  

  (b) For Implementation Milestones designated as “critical” in the Project Plan, time shall be of the essence. In the event of anticipated delays, Cascade shall promptly notify Contoso and propose mitigation steps, in accordance with **Section 2.7(c)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (c) Repeated failure to meet critical Implementation Milestones or Service Levels shall constitute a material breach, entitling Contoso to exercise rights under **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.4** of this Addendum, subject to cure rights stated therein.

2.8 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  (a) Provide timely access to relevant systems, facilities, and personnel necessary for Cascade’s performance of the Implementation Services and Enhanced Support Services;  

  (b) Designate a primary project manager and technical contacts to coordinate with Cascade’s personnel;  

  (c) Provide timely approvals or feedback on Addendum Deliverables within the review periods specified herein;  

  (d) Comply with reasonable prerequisites and environmental requirements set forth in the Project Plan, provided such requirements are consistent with industry practice and do not materially deviate from the agreed scope.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The aggregate not-to-exceed value of this Addendum (the “Addendum Contract Value”) shall be **USD $1,270,532**, exclusive of Taxes, unless increased by a written amendment to this Addendum, signed by both Parties, in accordance with **Section 16.4** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  
This **Section 3.2** supplements **Section 3.2** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Products Fees.**  
  Fees for Addendum Products shall be based on unit prices and quantities set forth in a pricing schedule or SOW attached to or referencing this Addendum. Unless otherwise agreed, hardware and perpetual software licenses shall be invoiced upon delivery, in accordance with **Section 3.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Implementation Services Fees.**  
  Implementation Services shall be charged on a mixed basis:  
  - Certain defined deliverables and milestones shall be fixed-price, as identified in the Project Plan;  
  - Professional services not covered by fixed-price line items shall be billed on a time-and-materials (“T&M”) basis using rate cards attached to this Addendum.

  (c) **Enhanced Support and CMP Subscription Fees.**  
  Enhanced Support Services and CMP-related services may be billed as recurring fees, typically monthly or annually in advance, and shall be clearly identified as recurring in the applicable pricing schedule.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
This **Section 3.3** supplements and is consistent with **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Net 60.**  
  Contoso shall pay undisputed amounts within **sixty (60) days** from receipt of a valid invoice (“Net 60”), in accordance with the Parent Agreement and the Addendum-specific invoicing schedule.

  (b) **Invoicing Milestones.**  
  By way of illustration and without limitation:  
  - 30% of the fixed-price Implementation Services for each major phase may be invoiced upon achievement of the corresponding Implementation Milestone and Contoso’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the associated Deliverables;  
  - Remaining 70% for that phase may be invoiced upon completion and acceptance of all Deliverables for that phase;  
  - T&M services shall be invoiced monthly in arrears with detailed time and expense records;  
  - Recurring Enhanced Support fees shall be invoiced in advance (monthly or annually as agreed).

  (c) **Invoice Content.**  
  In addition to the requirements in **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement, each invoice under this Addendum shall expressly reference both (i) the Parent Agreement (**PUR-CAS-202510-590**, contract_590) and (ii) this Addendum (**ADD-CAS-202510-669**), and shall identify the applicable milestone, subscription period, and/or T&M period.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  
Late payment penalties shall be as set forth in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement. Cascade shall not suspend Enhanced Support Services unless the conditions in **Section 3.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement are satisfied and the suspension is proportionate, and shall coordinate any such suspension with Contoso to minimize operational impact.

3.5 **Taxes.**  
Tax treatment shall follow **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement. Cascade shall separately state Taxes on invoices. Contoso shall provide valid exemption certificates where applicable.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
The expense and reimbursement policy stated in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement applies to this Addendum. Travel, lodging, and out-of-pocket costs reasonably incurred by Cascade in performing on-site Implementation Services shall be reimbursed by Contoso, provided such expenses are (i) pre-approved in writing, (ii) in compliance with Contoso’s travel and expense policies, and (iii) supported by appropriate receipts.

3.7 **Setoff.**  
Contoso’s setoff rights under **Section 3.9** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to amounts payable under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
The term of this Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date (June 06, 2026) and shall continue in full force and effect until **May 06, 2029**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the Parent Agreement. For clarity, the Addendum Term may extend beyond the Initial Term of the Parent Agreement (which expires July 09, 2027), provided that the Parent Agreement is renewed or otherwise extended as permitted under **Section 4.2** of the Parent Agreement, or the Parties expressly agree in writing that certain provisions of the Parent Agreement shall survive for purposes of this Addendum.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
This Addendum may be renewed for additional periods as agreed in writing by the Parties, aligned with or independent from any Renewal Term under **Section 4.2** of the Parent Agreement. Any such renewal shall specify updated pricing, scope, and other relevant terms, and shall expressly reference both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Consistent with the client-friendly rights in **Section 4.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **By Contoso.**  
  Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason, upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Cascade.

  (b) **By Cascade.**  
  Cascade may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, provided that Cascade shall honor its obligations under any ongoing Implementation Milestones and Enhanced Support Services during such notice period unless Contoso agrees otherwise.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or such longer period not exceeding sixty (60) days, if reasonable given the nature of the breach), consistent with **Section 4.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

  (a) All outstanding undisputed invoices for Addendum Products delivered or Addendum Services performed up to the effective date of termination shall become due and payable in accordance with **Article III** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum;  

  (b) For any Implementation Services terminated for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall pay Cascade (i) for Addendum Products delivered and accepted; (ii) for Addendum Services performed and accepted up to the effective date of termination; and (iii) for any documented, non-cancellable commitments incurred by Cascade specifically for the Addendum, to the extent they cannot be reasonably mitigated, consistent with **Section 4.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (c) Cascade shall provide reasonable transition assistance, at Contoso’s request and subject to a mutually agreed SOW or Change Order, to facilitate a smooth transfer of services to Contoso or a third party, at T&M rates not to exceed the rate card associated with this Addendum;

  (d) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information related to this Addendum, consistent with **Section 5.4** of the Parent Agreement and **Article V** of this Addendum;

  (e) Any perpetual software licenses purchased under this Addendum shall survive termination, subject to continued compliance with applicable license terms and full payment of applicable Fees, consistent with **Section 4.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6 **Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement (PUR-CAS-202510-590) is terminated or expires and is not renewed or replaced:

  (a) Unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in writing, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such termination or expiration, except for those obligations that expressly survive under the Parent Agreement and this Addendum;  

  (b) Notwithstanding the foregoing, the Parties may agree in writing that certain Addendum Services (e.g., ongoing Enhanced Support or CMP subscriptions) shall continue to be governed by the surviving terms of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum for the remainder of the Addendum Term;  

  (c) The survival provisions in **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 16.8** of this Addendum shall apply to any surviving obligations.

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLAs)

12.1 **SLA Environment.**  
The SLA Environment under this Addendum consists of the CMP and associated monitoring, automation, and reporting functions managed by Cascade, together with the infrastructure components (servers, network devices, storage arrays) designated in the Project Plan as being subject to Enhanced Support SLAs.

12.2 **Availability.**  
For the SLA Environment, Cascade shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve a minimum monthly uptime of **99.9%**, excluding scheduled maintenance and agreed maintenance windows, consistent with **Section 12.1** of the Parent Agreement.

12.3 **Incident Response and Resolution Times.**  
For Incidents affecting the SLA Environment, Cascade shall adhere to at least the following target response and resolution times (aligned with, and in certain cases strengthening, **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement):

  - **Priority 1 (Critical)** – Complete outage or severe degradation of CMP or critical infrastructure:  
    - Initial Response: within 30 minutes  
    - Target Resolution or Workaround: within 6 hours  

  - **Priority 2 (High)** – Major functionality impaired but operations can continue in a limited fashion:  
    - Initial Response: within 2 hours  
    - Target Resolution or Workaround: within 1 Business Day  

  - **Priority 3 (Medium)** – Non-critical issue with limited impact:  
    - Initial Response: within 1 Business Day  
    - Target Resolution or Workaround: within 3 Business Days  

  - **Priority 4 (Low)** – Minor issue or general inquiry:  
    - Initial Response: within 2 Business Days  
    - Target Resolution: in next planned maintenance or release, as agreed.

12.4 **Service Credits.**  
If Cascade fails to meet the uptime commitments or Incident response/resolution timeframes set out in this Article XII for any given calendar month, Contoso may be entitled to Service Credits as follows:

  (a) For each full 0.1% of uptime below 99.9% in a given month (but above 99.0%), Contoso shall receive a Service Credit equal to 2% of the monthly recurring Enhanced Support fees for that month;  

  (b) If uptime falls below 99.0% in a given month, Contoso shall receive a total Service Credit equal to 25% of the monthly recurring Enhanced Support fees for that month;  

  (c) If Cascade fails to meet Priority 1 target resolution times on three (3) or more separate Incidents in any rolling three (3) month period, Contoso shall receive a one-time Service Credit equal to 10% of the monthly recurring Enhanced Support fees for the month in which the third such failure occurs;  

  (d) Service Credits shall be applied against future invoices and shall be Contoso’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for Service Level failures, without prejudice to Contoso’s right to terminate for repeated material failures under **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

12.5 **Exclusions.**  
Service Levels and Service Credits shall not apply to unavailability or performance issues caused by:  
  (a) Misuse or failure to use the SLA Environment in accordance with documentation;  
  (b) Contoso’s systems or third-party systems not under Cascade’s control;  
  (c) Force Majeure Events under **Article XV**;  
  (d) Scheduled maintenance performed in accordance with **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement or mutually agreed maintenance windows under this Addendum.
