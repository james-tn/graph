(c) is rightfully disclosed to the Receiving Party by a third party without restriction on use or disclosure and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality; or  
(d) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party.

(a) promptly notifies the Disclosing Party in writing to allow the Disclosing Party to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy; and
