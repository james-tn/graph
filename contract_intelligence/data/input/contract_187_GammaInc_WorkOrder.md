## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) Pursuant to **Section 2.2 (Statements of Work)** and **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, this Work Order sets forth the specific scope of the additional **consulting, advisory, implementation, analytics, and program management Services** to be performed by the Service Provider for the Company.  

(b) This Work Order **supplements** and is **entered into pursuant to** the Parent Agreement (Ref: **CSA-GAM-202509-160 / contract_160**), and the Services defined herein constitute “Services” and “Deliverables” under **Article II** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of a conflict between this Work Order and the Parent Agreement, the Parent Agreement shall govern unless this Work Order explicitly states that a particular provision of the Parent Agreement is superseded with respect to the subject matter hereof.

2.2 **Project Description.**  
(a) The Project under this Work Order is titled **“Phase II Integrated Transformation, Analytics Enablement, and PMO Expansion Initiative.”** Building upon the strategic roadmaps, process assessments, and initial implementation activities performed under previous SOWs pursuant to the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall:

  1. Extend the Company’s digital transformation roadmap into prioritized implementation tracks, consistent with the strategic consulting contemplated in **Section 2.1(a) (Strategic Consulting)** of the Parent Agreement;  
  2. Design and implement advanced analytics capabilities and data models supporting the Company’s enterprise KPIs, consistent with the process and operational consulting set forth in **Section 2.1(b)**;  
  3. Implement and integrate specific enterprise applications and middleware components (including CRM, analytics platforms, and integration services) in line with **Section 2.1(c)** of the Parent Agreement;  
  4. Provide comprehensive change management, training, and communications support for the expanded user base, consistent with **Section 2.1(d)** of the Parent Agreement; and  
  5. Enhance and expand the Company’s PMO capabilities to support multi-track program execution across global business units, as described in **Section 2.1(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) Within thirty (30) days following the WO Effective Date, the Service Provider shall prepare a detailed **Project Plan** that includes a work breakdown structure, dependencies, resource allocation, and milestone schedule. The Project Plan will be submitted to the Company’s Project Manager for review and approval. Once approved, the Project Plan shall be deemed incorporated into this Work Order by reference and may be updated only via the Change Management process in **Article XIII**.

2.3 **Detailed Services and Deliverables.**  
The Services under this Work Order shall include, at a minimum, the following workstreams and associated Deliverables:

(a) **Strategic Roadmap Extension and Portfolio Prioritization**

  - Conduct stakeholder workshops across the Company’s technology, operations, sales, and finance functions to refine the multi-year digital transformation roadmap.  
  - Deliverable D1: **Updated Enterprise Transformation Roadmap (Version 2.0)**, including prioritized initiatives, estimated benefits, and resource implications.  
  - Deliverable D2: **Portfolio Prioritization Matrix and Investment Playbook**, enabling the Company to sequence projects based on value, cost, complexity, and risk.

(b) **Process Optimization and Operating Model Design**

  - Analyze current end-to-end processes in at least three functional areas (e.g., Sales Operations, Customer Support, and Finance) and design optimized target-state workflows aligned with **Section 2.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  - Deliverable D3: **Target-State Process Flow Diagrams and SOPs** for each selected function.  
  - Deliverable D4: **Governance Model and KPI Catalog**, including definitions, ownership, and reporting frequencies.

(c) **Technology Implementation and Integration**

  - Configure and implement agreed modules of the Company’s selected CRM and analytics platforms.  
  - Design integration patterns between core ERP, CRM, and data warehouse systems.  
  - Deliverable D5: **Solution Design Document and Integration Specifications**, aligned with the configuration documentation described in **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  - Deliverable D6: **Configured Application Instances and Test Scripts**, including user stories and test cases.  
  - Deliverable D7: **Data Migration and Validation Report**, documenting migration approach, reconciliations, and quality results.

(d) **Analytics Enablement and Reporting**

  - Design and implement a scalable analytics data model supporting dashboards and reports for executive, regional, and functional stakeholders.  
  - Deliverable D8: **Enterprise Data Model and Semantic Layer Documentation**.  
  - Deliverable D9: **Set of Priority Dashboards and Reports (minimum 10 dashboards)**, including documentation and user reference materials, consistent with **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

(e) **Change Management, Training, and Communications**

  - Develop and execute a comprehensive change management and communications plan including stakeholder mapping, impact analysis, training strategy, and communication campaigns, as contemplated in **Section 2.1(d)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  - Deliverable D10: **Change Management and Communication Plan**, with timeline, channels, and key messages.  
  - Deliverable D11: **Training Curricula, User Guides, and Reference Materials**, including both end-user and administrator training assets.  
  - Deliverable D12: **Training Delivery and Adoption Report**, detailing training sessions delivered, attendance, and adoption metrics.

(f) **PMO Expansion and Program Management**

  - Establish or enhance PMO processes, templates, and governance to support multi-track program delivery.  
  - Provide ongoing program and project management resources, aligned with **Section 2.1(e)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  - Deliverable D13: **PMO Framework, Templates, and Governance Handbook**, including risk registers, issue logs, status report templates, and RAID processes.  
  - Deliverable D14: **Periodic Program Status Reports (monthly)**, as required by **Section 2.5(b) (Project Governance and Reporting)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Milestones and Timeline.**  
The Services and Deliverables shall be performed in phased Milestones as follows (indicative; subject to refinement in the Project Plan):

- **Milestone 1 – Mobilization & Discovery** (Target Completion: August 31, 2026)  
  - Key outputs: Approved Project Plan, baseline assessments, stakeholder alignment.  
  - Deliverables: Draft Project Plan, discovery findings summary.

- **Milestone 2 – Strategy & Design** (Target Completion: February 28, 2027)  
  - Key outputs: Updated Transformation Roadmap, Target-State Process Designs, high-level architecture.  
  - Deliverables: D1, D2, D3, D4, preliminary architecture portion of D5.

- **Milestone 3 – Build & Configuration** (Target Completion: December 31, 2027)  
  - Key outputs: Configured environments, integration designs, data model.  
  - Deliverables: D5, D6, D8, partial D9.

- **Milestone 4 – Testing, Migration & Go-Live** (Target Completion: October 31, 2028)  
  - Key outputs: Functional and UAT completed, data migrated, production go-live.  
  - Deliverables: D7, final D9, go-live readiness checklist.

- **Milestone 5 – Change Management, PMO Embedding & Stabilization** (Target Completion: June 30, 2029)  
  - Key outputs: Trained users, operational PMO, post-go-live stabilization.  
  - Deliverables: D10, D11, D12, D13, recurring D14 (monthly through WO Expiration Date, unless otherwise agreed).

2.5 **Key Personnel.**  
The Service Provider shall assign and maintain, for the duration of this Work Order, the following Key Personnel, subject to replacement only with comparably qualified personnel and with the Company’s prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld), in accordance with the performance standards in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) Unless otherwise stated herein, the Company shall have **ten (10) Business Days** from receipt of each Deliverable to review and accept or reject such Deliverable based on the Acceptance Criteria defined in the Project Plan and relevant Deliverable specification.  

(b) The Company’s failure to respond within the review period shall result in deemed acceptance, except in the case of latent defects not reasonably discoverable during such period, consistent with **Section 2.8(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Rejected Deliverables shall be corrected by the Service Provider at no additional cost, and resubmitted for re-review in accordance with **Section 2.8(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Project Governance and Reporting.**  
(a) The Parties shall establish a **Project Governance Committee** comprised of at least two (2) representatives from each Party, including senior executives empowered to make binding decisions.  

(b) The Governance Committee shall meet at least **monthly** (or more frequently as agreed) to review progress against Milestones, budget, risks, issues, and Service Levels, consistent with **Section 2.5(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

Upon expiration or termination of this Work Order:

(a) Service Provider shall cease performing the terminated Services, except as necessary to wind down in an orderly manner, consistent with **Section 4.5(a)** of the Parent Agreement;

(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety pursuant to **Article IV** thereof, this Work Order shall **automatically terminate** on the effective date of such termination, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing that certain provisions of the Parent Agreement will survive for purposes of allowing this Work Order to continue.

(b) In the event of such automatic termination, the obligations in **Section 4.5** of this Work Order and any applicable surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement shall apply.

4.7 **Survival.**  
Any provisions of this Work Order that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including without limitation Fees due and payable, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, XVI, and XVII**, and all incorporated provisions of the Parent Agreement indicated therein.
