### 4.1 Term

4.1.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **September 09, 2027** (the “Initial Term”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Client may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience, in whole or in part, by providing Vendor with at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice.

4.2.2 Vendor may similarly terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon providing Client with at least one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) materially breaches any provision of this Agreement or applicable SOW and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  

(b) becomes insolvent, is unable to pay its debts as they fall due, enters into liquidation (other than for the purpose of a bona fide solvent amalgamation or reconstruction), or has a receiver or similar officer appointed over all or any part of its assets.

4.3.2 Without limiting the foregoing, Vendor shall have the right to terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately upon written notice if Client fails to pay any undisputed amounts due under this Agreement within forty-five (45) days after receiving a second written notice of non-payment from Vendor.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination or Expiration

4.4.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement for any reason:

(a) All outstanding payment obligations incurred by Client prior to the effective date of termination or expiration shall become immediately due and payable;  

(b) Client shall pay Vendor for all Services performed and Deliverables provided up to the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments made by Vendor in reliance on the Agreement or SOW; and  

(c) Each Party shall promptly return or, at the disclosing Party’s written request, destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information, subject to Section 5.5.

4.4.2 Termination of a particular SOW shall not, by itself, terminate this Agreement or any other SOW, unless expressly stated in the termination notice.

4.4.3 The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to provide an orderly wind-down of the Services and, if requested by Client, Vendor shall provide reasonable transition assistance at Vendor’s then-current rates or as otherwise agreed in writing.

### 4.5 Survival

4.5.1 The provisions of this Agreement which by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including without limitation Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions), as well as all payment obligations accrued prior to termination.

5.3.1 The obligations in Section 5.2 shall not apply to any information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate:

5.6.1 The obligations of confidentiality set forth in this Article V shall commence on the Effective Date and survive for a period of **five (5) years** following the termination or expiration of this Agreement, except with respect to trade secrets and Personal Data, which shall be kept confidential for so long as such information remains a trade secret or Personal Data under Applicable Law.
