## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Services

2.1.1 Service Provider shall perform for Client certain consulting and related professional services, which may include, without limitation:  

- (a) enterprise architecture assessment and roadmap development;  
- (b) digital transformation strategy and planning;  
- (c) systems integration consulting, including integration between Client’s existing ERP, CRM, data warehouse, and cloud services;  
- (d) software configuration, customization, and implementation services related to third-party enterprise platforms selected by Client;  
- (e) development of custom software components, APIs, scripts, and tools;  
- (f) project management, change management, and governance advisory services;  
- (g) data migration planning, execution, and validation;  
- (h) testing, quality assurance, and performance tuning; and  
- (i) knowledge transfer, training, and post-implementation support.

2.1.2 The specific nature, scope, duration, and objectives of the Services shall be further described in one or more Statements of Work entered into by the Parties and incorporated herein by reference. In the event of any conflict between this Agreement and a Statement of Work, this Agreement shall control unless such Statement of Work explicitly states that it is intended to supersede a specific provision of this Agreement.

### 2.2 Deliverables

2.2.1 Service Provider shall produce and deliver to Client the Deliverables described in the applicable SOW, which may include, without limitation:  

- (a) written assessments, strategy documents, and architecture blueprints;  
- (b) detailed project plans, including timelines, milestones, resource plans, and risk registers;  
- (c) configuration documents, system design specifications, interface control documents, and data mapping specifications;  
- (d) software builds, integrations, and code repositories;  
- (e) test plans, test cases, test results, and performance reports;  
- (f) user guides, operational runbooks, and training materials; and  
- (g) post-implementation review reports and continuous improvement recommendations.

2.2.2 Each SOW shall specify, as applicable, for each Deliverable: (i) a description of the Deliverable; (ii) the format and medium (e.g., electronic, cloud-based repository); (iii) delivery dates or milestone dates; (iv) acceptance criteria; and (v) any dependencies or assumptions relevant to the Deliverable.

2.3.1 Service Provider shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with generally accepted industry standards for similar consulting engagements and in compliance with Applicable Law. ### 4.1 Term

4.2.1 Client may terminate this Agreement, or any SOW, for convenience, in whole or in part, upon **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Service Provider.

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement, or any SOW, immediately upon written notice if the other Party:  

- (a) materially breaches this Agreement or the applicable SOW and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  
- (b) becomes insolvent, files or has filed against it a petition for bankruptcy (which is not dismissed within sixty (60) days), makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or ceases to conduct business in the normal course.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement:  

- (a) all SOWs shall automatically terminate, except to the extent expressly stated otherwise in a termination notice or as otherwise agreed in writing;  
- (b) Client shall pay Service Provider for all Services performed and approved Expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Service Provider on Client’s behalf, to the extent such commitments were pre-approved by Client;  
- (c) Service Provider shall promptly deliver to Client all completed and in-progress Deliverables for which Client has paid, and, upon Client’s request, shall reasonably assist Client in transition of the Services to Client or a third-party provider at Service Provider’s then-current time-and-materials rates (unless termination is due to Service Provider’s uncured material breach, in which case transition assistance for a reasonable period shall be provided at no additional charge); and  
- (d) each Party shall promptly return or destroy (at the disclosing Party’s option) the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with Section 5.5.

4.4.2 Termination or expiration of this Agreement shall be without prejudice to any rights or obligations of either Party that have accrued prior to the effective date of termination, including payment obligations, and any provisions that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration shall survive, including but not limited to Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI.
