## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.** This Addendum is **entered into pursuant to and supplements** the Parent Agreement (PUR-SUM-202402-420 / contract_420). The Parties expressly agree that the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services described herein are subject to, and shall be governed by, all applicable terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, **Article VII (Representations and Warranties)**, **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, and **Article XIII (Change Management)**, except as specifically modified in this Addendum.

2.2 **Summary of Additional Scope.** Under this Addendum, Summit Tech shall provide the following Additional Deliverables and Additional Services:

  2.2.1 **New Hardware Procurement and Supply (Expansion of Section 2.2.1 of Parent Agreement).**  
  Summit Tech shall source, procure, and deliver to Contoso additional enterprise-grade infrastructure, including but not limited to:

  - Up to **60** rack-mounted servers with specified CPU, RAM, and NVMe storage configurations tailored to Contoso’s new analytics and AI workloads;
  - **Core and aggregation network switches**, next-generation firewalls, and SD-WAN appliances designed to integrate with the network security standards described in the Parent Agreement;
  - Additional SAN/NAS storage arrays and backup appliances sized to support an incremental **5 petabytes** of raw storage capacity, including deduplication and encryption capabilities;
  - Associated racks, PDUs, network cabling, and other peripheral equipment necessary for a fully integrated deployment at Contoso’s primary and secondary data centers.

  The New Hardware shall meet or exceed the Specifications mutually agreed in writing and aligned with the performance and security standards referenced in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement.

  2.2.2 **Software and Licensing (Expansion of Section 2.2.2).**  
  Summit Tech shall provide additional software and license entitlements, which may include:

  - Data virtualization and container orchestration platforms (e.g., Kubernetes distributions) for the new infrastructure;
  - Enhanced monitoring, logging, and SIEM integrations to extend the existing tools contemplated under Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement to the newly deployed systems;
  - Additional operating system, database, and application server licenses for the New Hardware;
  - Client access licenses and user subscriptions required for Contoso’s expanded internal user base and system integrations.

  Rights to use such software shall be governed by applicable third-party licenses (per Section 6.3 of the Parent Agreement) and any explicit license grants under this Addendum.

  2.2.3 **Implementation, Integration, and Migration (Expansion of Sections 2.2.3 and 2.2.4).**  
  Summit Tech shall perform Additional Services to install, configure, integrate, and migrate workloads to the New Hardware and software, including:

  - Site readiness validation and environmental assessments for additional racks and power requirements;
  - Installation and physical racking of New Hardware at Contoso’s designated facilities;
  - Configuration of operating systems, hypervisors, container platforms, IP addressing, VLANs, firewall policies, and routing to ensure seamless integration with Contoso’s existing environment and the infrastructure deployed under the Parent Agreement;
  - Data migration from legacy systems to the new storage arrays, including dry runs, test migrations, performance validation, and documented runbooks consistent with Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement;
  - Coordination with Contoso’s security and network teams to ensure compliance with internal security baselines and applicable regulations.

  2.2.4 **Extended Managed Services (Supplementing Section 2.3).**  
  For the New Hardware, associated software, and the combined environment (existing plus new), Summit Tech shall provide **Extended Managed Services** from the Addendum Effective Date through the Addendum Expiration Date, including:

  - 24x7 monitoring and incident management for the newly deployed systems, integrated with Contoso’s existing monitoring and alerting framework described in Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement;
  - Ongoing patch management, firmware updates, and minor version upgrades (per Section 2.3.2 of the Parent Agreement), more specifically tailored to the new hardware and software versions;
  - Tier 2 and Tier 3 technical support and escalation handling aligned with the **Service Levels** set forth in Article XII of the Parent Agreement and as enhanced in **Article XII of this Addendum**, including support integration with Contoso’s existing ITSM platform;
  - Capacity planning, performance tuning, and quarterly health assessments with documented recommendations and action plans.

  2.2.5 **Training and Knowledge Transfer (Expanding Section 2.2.5).**  
  Summit Tech shall provide additional training and knowledge transfer for Contoso’s technical and operations teams specifically related to:

  - New orchestration, virtualization, and storage management tools introduced under this Addendum;
  - Updated operational procedures, incident runbooks, and escalation flows for the expanded environment;
  - Security best practices, backup and recovery procedures, and performance optimization techniques for the new infrastructure.

  Such training shall include a combination of on-site workshops (where feasible), virtual sessions, and detailed documentation aligned with Contoso’s existing operational standards.

2.3 **Project Management and Reporting (Refinement of Section 2.5).**  

  2.3.1 Summit Tech shall designate a dedicated **Addendum Project Manager** who will work in coordination with the project manager designated under the Parent Agreement (if still active), and who shall be responsible for tracking:

  - Delivery and installation milestones;
  - Migration timelines and cutover events;
  - Progress against the project plan developed for this Addendum.

  2.3.2 The Addendum Project Manager shall provide **bi-weekly status reports** during active deployment phases and at least **monthly reports** during steady-state managed services, detailing:

  - Progress against milestones, including any deviations from the schedule specified in this Addendum or in applicable New SOWs;
  - Risks, issues, and dependencies, including any dependencies on Contoso as described in Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement;
  - Service Level performance metrics for Extended Managed Services.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

  2.4.1 The Parties anticipate the following high-level milestones (subject to adjustment via Change Order consistent with Article XIII of the Parent Agreement and Article XIII of this Addendum):

  - **Hardware Delivery Completion:** Within ninety (90) days of the Addendum Effective Date;
  - **Core Installation and Configuration:** Within one hundred twenty (120) days of the Addendum Effective Date;
  - **Initial UAT Commencement:** Within one hundred fifty (150) days of the Addendum Effective Date;
  - **Production Cutover / Go-Live:** Within one hundred eighty (180) days of the Addendum Effective Date;
  - **Transition to Extended Managed Services Steady State:** Within two hundred ten (210) days of the Addendum Effective Date.

  2.4.2 Summit Tech shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the above milestones. In the event of anticipated delays, Summit Tech shall promptly notify Contoso in writing, identify the root cause, and propose mitigation steps, in accordance with Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Acceptance of Additional Deliverables (Application of Section 2.7).**  

  2.5.1 Summit Tech shall issue a **Delivery Notice** for each major Additional Deliverable or milestone as described in Section 2.7.1 of the Parent Agreement.

  2.5.2 Contoso shall have no less than ten (10) Business Days from receipt of a Delivery Notice to review and test the Additional Deliverable and either:

  - Provide written acceptance; or  
  - Provide a written list of deficiencies as contemplated in Section 2.7.2 of the Parent Agreement.

  2.5.3 The cure and resubmission process set forth in Section 2.7.3 and Section 2.7.4 of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to the Additional Deliverables under this Addendum.

2.6 **Exclusions.**  

  Any Services or Deliverables not expressly identified in this Addendum or a New SOW referencing this Addendum shall be deemed out of scope and may only be performed pursuant to a mutually executed Change Order, as set forth in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

  3.1.1 The Parties agree that the aggregate not-to-exceed contract value for the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services under this Addendum shall be **US$2,886,057** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”).

  3.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, the Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US$865,668** specified in Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement. The combined not-to-exceed value across the Parent Agreement and this Addendum shall therefore be **US$3,751,725**, unless further modified by a duly executed Change Order or subsequent addendum.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

  The fee structure for this Addendum shall be as follows, complementing the fee framework in Section 3.2 of the Parent Agreement:

  3.2.1 **Fixed Fees for Deployment and Implementation.**  
  - A fixed fee for the New Hardware deployment and core implementation activities, representing **US$1,450,000** of the Addendum Contract Value.  
  - Payment of this portion shall be milestone-based, in accordance with Section 3.3.1 below.

  3.2.2 **Managed Service Fees.**  
  - Recurring monthly fees for Extended Managed Services totaling up to **US$1,136,057** over the Addendum Term.  
  - Monthly managed service fees (e.g., **US$47,335.71** per month for 24 months) shall be invoiced in advance or as otherwise mutually agreed and memorialized in a New SOW.

  3.2.3 **Pass-Through Costs for Third-Party Products.**  
  - Up to **US$300,000** of pass-through costs for Third-Party Products, licenses, and support, billed at cost plus any permitted margin expressly specified in the relevant New SOW, consistent with Section 3.2.3 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Terms and Milestones.**  

  3.3.1 **Deployment / Implementation Milestones (Fixed Fee).**  
  The fixed-fee implementation portion (US$1,450,000) shall be invoiced as follows:

  - **20% (US$290,000)** upon execution of this Addendum and issuance of the initial purchase order by Contoso;  
  - **30% (US$435,000)** upon completion of Hardware delivery and installation in Contoso’s facilities (Hardware Delivery Completion milestone);  
  - **30% (US$435,000)** upon completion of UAT and acceptance of core infrastructure as per Section 2.5;  
  - **20% (US$290,000)** upon successful Production Go-Live and transition to Extended Managed Services.

  3.3.2 **Managed Services.**  
  - Summit Tech shall invoice Contoso monthly in advance for Extended Managed Services, consistent with Section 3.3.1 of the Parent Agreement, with Net 60 payment terms.  
  - Any temporary or burst capacity services, if agreed, shall be invoiced monthly in arrears on a time and materials basis.

  3.3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
  Unless otherwise specified in a New SOW referencing this Addendum, **all undisputed invoices** issued under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** of Contoso’s receipt (Net 60), consistent with Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

  Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest in accordance with Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement, at the lesser of (a) one percent (1%) per month or (b) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law. Summit Tech shall not suspend Extended Managed Services for late payment unless amounts remain undisputed and unpaid for more than thirty (30) days following written notice and an opportunity to cure, consistent with Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

  Invoices disputed in good faith shall be handled in accordance with Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall timely notify Summit Tech of any disputed portion, pay the undisputed portion, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve the dispute.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

  Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes, and Taxes shall be handled as described in Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  

  Reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Summit Tech in performing on-site work under this Addendum shall be reimbursable under the same conditions set forth in Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement.

3.8 **No Additional Charges.**  

  Summit Tech shall not impose additional fees or surcharges beyond those expressly stated in this Addendum or applicable New SOWs, except as provided in a duly executed Change Order, consistent with Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

  This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in effect until **December 19, 2026** (the “**Addendum Term**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement. For the avoidance of doubt, the Addendum Term extends beyond the original Term of the Parent Agreement (which currently expires on June 25, 2025), and the Parties agree that the Parent Agreement is **hereby extended solely for the purposes of supporting and governing this Addendum and any New SOWs**, through the Addendum Expiration Date, unless otherwise terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

  Any renewal or extension of Extended Managed Services beyond the Addendum Expiration Date shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties and may be documented by an additional amendment or addendum referencing both this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

  4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum or any New SOW issued under it, in whole or in part, **for convenience** upon **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Summit Tech, consistent with the client-friendly rights set forth in Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.  

  4.3.2 Summit Tech may terminate this Addendum or any New SOW not yet commenced, for convenience, upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, consistent with Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

  Either Party may terminate this Addendum or any affected New SOW immediately upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement, and such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days following written notice, consistent with Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Persistent Service Failures.**  

  In addition to any rights under Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement, if Summit Tech fails to meet critical Enhanced Service Levels for Extended Managed Services in three (3) or more months in any rolling six (6)-month period, Contoso may, upon thirty (30) days’ written notice, terminate the Extended Managed Services portion of this Addendum for cause if such failures are not cured and prevented from recurring.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**  

  Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

  - Summit Tech shall cease performing the Additional Services and Extended Managed Services, except for any agreed transition services;
  - Contoso shall pay Summit Tech for Additional Services actually and satisfactorily performed and for any non-cancellable third-party commitments incurred on Contoso’s behalf, consistent with Section 4.6.2 of the Parent Agreement;
  - Any perpetual licenses granted under this Addendum shall survive in accordance with Section 4.6.3 of the Parent Agreement;
  - The confidentiality, IP, data protection, and limitation of liability provisions that by their nature should survive shall so survive, including relevant provisions of **Articles V, VI, VIII, IX, XI**, and **XVI** of this Addendum, as well as corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

  4.7.1 If the Parent Agreement (PUR-SUM-202402-420 / contract_420) is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date of termination, unless:

  - The Parties expressly agree in writing to preserve and continue this Addendum under equivalent terms; and  
  - Such agreement clearly sets out the continuing framework for the relationship.

  4.7.2 In the event of any conflict between this Section 4.7 and the termination provisions of the Parent Agreement, the more protective provision for the operation of this Addendum shall control, provided that such construction does not violate Applicable Law.

4.8 **Transition Assistance.**  

  At Contoso’s request, Summit Tech shall provide reasonable transition assistance as described in Section 4.7 of the Parent Agreement, for up to ninety (90) days, to ensure an orderly transition of Extended Managed Services.
