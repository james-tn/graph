## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relation to Parent SOW and Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed under and forms part of the Parent SOW (SOW-ATL-202406-278 / contract_278) and is subject to the Parent Agreement (CSA-ATL-202406-248 / contract_248), in accordance with **Section 2.1 (Relation to Parent Agreement)** and **Section 16.1 (Reference to Master/Parent Agreement)** of the Parent SOW. The Extended Services described herein shall be treated as “Services” under **Section 1.17** and **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, and as an extension of the Program defined in **Section 1.2.2 (Program)** of the Parent SOW.

2.1.2 The Extended Services supplement, but do not alter, the Workstreams A–D defined in **Sections 2.3.1–2.3.4** of the Parent SOW. For clarity, the Vendor remains responsible for completing the original Deliverables and Milestones set forth in **Sections 2.3 and 2.6** of the Parent SOW, while this Addendum introduces additional work packages and governance mechanisms predominantly in the **post‑implementation and optimization phase**.

2.2 **Overview of Extended Services.**  

Under this Addendum, the Vendor shall provide a set of Extended Services intended to (a) support Contoso beyond the original SOW Expiration Date of December 28, 2026; (b) drive sustained benefits realization from the global digital transformation; (c) institutionalize continuous improvement practices; and (d) enhance governance and reporting in support of the Project Governance Committee described in **Section 2.8** of the Parent SOW.

The Extended Services will:

- Monitor, analyze, and optimize processes implemented under **Workstream B – Process Optimization** and **Workstream C – Implementation Readiness & Governance** of the Parent SOW, with particular focus on Order‑to‑Cash, Procure‑to‑Pay, and Record‑to‑Report domains;
- Extend change management and adoption support under **Workstream D – Change Management & Training**, including refreshed training content and targeted interventions in Additional Regions;
- Establish and operate a Benefits Realization Framework to quantify, track, and regularly report on KPIs and value drivers associated with the transformation roadmap originally delivered under **Workstream A – Strategy & Roadmap**;
- Provide specialized advisory sessions and health checks for Regional Rollouts, including new or Additional Regions beyond those outlined in **Section 2.6.3** of the Parent SOW.

2.3 **Detailed Extended Services and Deliverables.**

2.3.1 **Post‑Implementation Optimization (Process and Governance).**  
The Vendor shall perform ongoing optimization activities once core rollout phases under the Parent SOW have achieved go‑live status. Such activities shall include, without limitation:

(a) **Process Performance Diagnostics:** Periodic reviews (at least semi‑annual) of KPIs established in **Deliverable B4 (Process Performance KPI Definitions & Templates)** under the Parent SOW to identify performance gaps, bottlenecks, and control weaknesses;

(b) **Root Cause Analysis:** Conduct detailed analyses to determine underlying causes for deviations from target KPIs, involving targeted workshops with Client stakeholders as contemplated in **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent SOW;

(c) **Optimization Recommendations:** Prepare written recommendations and improvement backlogs for each major process domain, incorporating lessons learned from Phase 3 (Q3 2026 – Q4 2026) of the Regional Rollout (Parent SOW, Section 2.6.1);

(d) **Governance Enhancements:** Review and refine the **Program Governance Framework & Charter (Deliverable C1)** and governance structures originally developed under **Workstream C** of the Parent SOW, to ensure that governance remains fit‑for‑purpose during the extended period of this Addendum;

(e) **Extended Deliverables – Optimization:**  
   - **E1:** Post‑Implementation Process Performance Diagnostic Reports (per domain and region, at least annually);  
   - **E2:** Process Optimization Backlog and Prioritization Matrix;  
   - **E3:** Updated Governance Framework Addendum (amendments to Deliverable C1).

2.3.2 **Benefits Realization and Value Tracking.**  

The Vendor shall design, implement, and operate a structured Benefits Realization Framework, aligned with the multi‑year digital transformation roadmap produced as **Deliverable A3** under **Workstream A** of the Parent SOW. Activities shall include:

(a) **Benefits Mapping:** Align process and system changes implemented under the Program with specific financial and non‑financial benefits (e.g., cycle time reduction, error reduction, working capital improvements);

(b) **KPI Refinement:** Refine and, where appropriate, expand the KPIs defined in Deliverable B4 to ensure they adequately capture program benefits and are aligned with the Client’s evolving strategic objectives;

(c) **Benefits Tracking Dashboards:** Develop interactive dashboards and reporting templates (the “Benefits Dashboards”) to monitor benefits realization quarterly, leveraging the “data analytics and reporting services” referenced in **Section 2.2.6** of the Parent Agreement;

(d) **Quarterly Benefits Reviews:** Facilitate quarterly review sessions with the Project Governance Committee (Parent SOW, Section 2.8.1) to discuss benefits realization status, variances from plan, and remediation actions;

(e) **Extended Deliverables – Benefits Realization:**  
   - **E4:** Benefits Realization Framework Design Document;  
   - **E5:** Benefits Dashboards and Reporting Templates;  
   - **E6:** Quarterly Benefits Realization Reports (executive‑level summaries for the duration of this Addendum).

2.3.3 **Continuous Improvement and Additional Regions.**  

The Vendor shall support Contoso in establishing a Continuous Improvement Cycle for the Program, and may extend Regional Rollout support to Additional Regions, subject to mutual agreement and, where required, formal Change Orders under **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum:

(a) **Continuous Improvement Operating Model:** Define roles, responsibilities, and escalation paths for continuous improvement teams, building on the RACI matrices in **Deliverable B3 (Future‑State Process Designs & RACI Matrices)** under the Parent SOW;

(b) **Improvement Sprints:** Facilitate improvement sprints (e.g., 8–12 weeks) focused on targeted issues or regions, delivering prioritized enhancements to processes or governance structures, without engaging in hands‑on configuration or coding, consistent with **Section 2.9(d)** of the Parent SOW (Vendor acts in advisory capacity only);

(c) **Additional Regions Support:**  
   - Provide advisory support to adapt the existing Target State Capability Model and process designs (Deliverables A2 and B3) to Additional Regions, taking into account local regulatory constraints and business practices;  
   - Deliver region‑specific recommendations, risk assessments, and rollout playbooks;

(d) **Extended Deliverables – Continuous Improvement:**  
   - **E7:** Continuous Improvement Operating Model and Playbook;  
   - **E8:** Improvement Sprint Outcome Reports;  
   - **E9:** Additional Region Rollout Advisory Packs (where applicable).

2.3.4 **Enhanced Change Management and Training.**  

In extension of **Workstream D – Change Management & Training** of the Parent SOW, the Vendor shall:

(a) Update and refine the **Change Management Strategy & Plan (Deliverable D1)** and **Stakeholder Analysis & Communications Plan (Deliverable D2)** to reflect new stakeholder groups and organizational changes occurring after the original SOW Expiration Date;

(b) Develop **Enhanced Training Materials** to supplement **Deliverables D3 and D4** (Training Curriculum & Materials and Train‑the‑Trainer Sessions) from the Parent SOW, including micro‑learning modules, e‑learning content, and updated user guides;

(c) Conduct periodic refresher training sessions and targeted adoption interventions in regions where KPIs indicate low adoption, as identified in the Benefits Realization Framework;

(d) **Extended Deliverables – Change Management:**  
   - **E10:** Updated Change Management and Communications Plan;  
   - **E11:** Enhanced Training Materials Library;  
   - **E12:** Training and Adoption Impact Assessment Reports.

2.4 **Timeline and Milestones under this Addendum.**

2.4.1 **High‑Level Timeline.**  
The Extended Services under this Addendum shall generally cover the period from the Addendum Effective Date through the Addendum Expiration Date, with the understanding that initial ramp‑up may overlap with Phase 3 of the Parent SOW (Q3 2026 – Q4 2026). Illustrative phases include:

- **Phase E1 (Q4 2025 – Q4 2026):** Design and initial implementation of the Benefits Realization Framework; early post‑implementation diagnostics in pilot regions;  
- **Phase E2 (Q1 2027 – Q4 2027):** Full operation of Benefits Dashboards; continuous improvement sprints in EMEA and North America; enhanced training rollouts;  
- **Phase E3 (Q1 2028 – Q3 2029):** Extension to Additional Regions (if applicable); ongoing optimization and governance enhancements; formalization of steady‑state continuous improvement practices.

2.4.2 **Milestones.**  
Milestones under this Addendum (distinct from Milestones 1–6 in **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent SOW) may include:

- **Extended Milestone E‑1:** Delivery and acceptance of Benefits Realization Framework Design (Deliverable E4) – Target: March 31, 2026  
- **Extended Milestone E‑2:** Delivery and acceptance of first complete set of Benefits Dashboards (Deliverable E5) and initial Benefits Realization Report (Deliverable E6) – Target: June 30, 2026  
- **Extended Milestone E‑3:** Delivery and acceptance of Continuous Improvement Operating Model and Playbook (Deliverable E7) – Target: December 31, 2026  
- **Extended Milestone E‑4:** Delivery and acceptance of first Additional Region Rollout Advisory Pack (Deliverable E9), if applicable – Target: June 30, 2027  
- **Extended Milestone E‑5:** Delivery and acceptance of Enhanced Training Materials Library (Deliverable E11) – Target: December 31, 2027  

2.5 **Performance Standards; Acceptance.**  

2.5.1 The Vendor shall perform the Extended Services in accordance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.7 (Performance Standards and Service Levels)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this Addendum.

2.5.2 Acceptance of Extended Deliverables shall follow the acceptance process specified in **Sections 2.4.2** of the Parent SOW and **Sections 2.5.2–2.5.4** of the Parent Agreement, including the Client’s ten (10) Business Day review period and deemed acceptance mechanism, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to alternative acceptance criteria for specific Extended Deliverables.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities for Extended Services.**  

In addition to the responsibilities specified in **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, the Client shall:

(a) Provide ongoing access to updated process documents, KPI data, system usage statistics, and other relevant Client Materials required for performance diagnostics and benefits tracking;

(b) Ensure active participation of regional process owners, business leaders, and members of the Project Governance Committee in quarterly Benefits Realization reviews and continuous improvement workshops;

(c) Designate a benefits owner or function to act as primary liaison with the Vendor for all matters related to the Benefits Realization Framework;

(d) Provide reasonable advance notice of entry into Additional Regions or major organizational changes that may significantly impact the Extended Services.

2.7 **Classification of Risk.**  

For the avoidance of doubt, the Parties agree that:

(a) The **High‑Risk Classification** and related uncapped liability provisions in **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent SOW and Parent Agreement continue to apply to the original Program as scoped in the Parent SOW;  

(b) The Extended Services under **this Addendum** are designated as **Medium‑Risk**. Accordingly, the limitation of liability for claims arising **solely and directly** from the Extended Services shall be governed by **Article IX** of this Addendum (which introduces a financial cap as set forth in Section 9.1), while all other risk allocation provisions of the Parent Agreement and Parent SOW remain applicable.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **August 09, 2029** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or with Article IV of the Parent SOW and Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal or Extension.**  

Any extension or renewal of this Addendum beyond the Addendum Expiration Date shall be subject to the Parties’ mutual written agreement, consistent with the amendment requirements under **Section 16.4 (Amendments)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 The Client may terminate this Addendum for convenience, in whole or in part, upon providing at least **sixty (60) days** prior written notice to the Vendor. This notice period is specific to this Addendum and is intended to reflect its Medium‑Risk classification and extended planning cycles.

4.3.2 The Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon providing at least **sixty (60) days** prior written notice to the Client. The Parties may, in good faith, negotiate any transitional arrangements to mitigate adverse impacts.

4.3.3 Termination for convenience of this Addendum shall not automatically terminate the Parent SOW or Parent Agreement, which shall continue in accordance with their respective terms unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.4** of the Parent SOW, including termination for a material breach not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice, or upon the occurrence of specified insolvency events.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent SOW or Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 In accordance with **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent SOW and corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement, expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement shall result in automatic termination of the Parent SOW, and thus this Addendum, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in writing.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of the **Parent SOW** shall automatically terminate this Addendum as of the Parent SOW’s effective date of termination or expiration, unless the Parties explicitly agree to continue specific Extended Services under alternative arrangements.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

(a) The Vendor shall cease performance of all Extended Services, except as necessary to complete any agreed transition assistance;  

(b) The Client shall pay the Vendor (i) all Addendum Fees and reimbursable expenses accrued but unpaid up to the effective date of termination, and (ii) all Addendum Fees for work in progress (including T&M services) performed up to such date;  

(c) Any partial Deliverables or work papers created as part of Extended Services shall be made available to the Client, subject to payment of applicable Fees and compliance with the intellectual property provisions in **Article VI**.

4.7 **Transition Assistance.**  

Upon the Client’s written request, the Vendor shall provide reasonable transition assistance relating to the Extended Services for a period not exceeding sixty (60) days following termination or expiration of this Addendum, subject to payment of the Vendor’s then‑current rates and expenses. Such transition assistance shall be consistent with the principles set forth in **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Survival.**  

The provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, those relating to payment obligations, confidentiality, intellectual property, indemnification, limitation of liability, and dispute resolution) shall survive in accordance with **Section 4.8 (Survival)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement.
