## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Overview**

2.1.1 This Addendum **supplements the Statement of Work dated 21 September 2025 (Ref: SOW-PIN-202509-222)** and expands the services defined in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent Agreement. The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement has already established a comprehensive services framework that includes discovery, solution design, implementation, data migration, training, and managed support.  

2.1.2 Under this Addendum, the Vendor shall provide (a) **Extended Support Services**; (b) **Optimization Services**; and (c) **Platform Expansion Services**, together with the **Additional Deliverables**, as detailed below. These services are intended to (i) extend the support coverage beyond the Parent Term into the Addendum Term; (ii) enhance and optimize the systems deployed under the Parent Agreement; and (iii) provide a structured framework to roll out the same or related solutions to additional Client business units and geographies.

2.1.3 For avoidance of doubt, the Services under this Addendum are **in addition to** and not a replacement for the Services described in Article II of the Parent Agreement, except where explicitly stated otherwise in this Addendum. In case of conflict between this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, Section 16.2 of this Addendum shall apply.

---

### 2.2 Extended Support Services

2.2.1 The Vendor shall extend and enhance the **Managed Support and Maintenance Services** described in Section 2.2.6 of the Parent Agreement and governed by **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** as follows:

  (a) **Extended Term Support**:  
  - Provide continuous managed support for the in-scope systems implemented under SOW-PIN-202509-222 from the later of (i) completion of Milestone 5 under Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, or (ii) the Addendum Effective Date, through the **Addendum Expiration Date of 15 November 2030**, subject to earlier termination as provided in **Article IV** of this Addendum.  
  - This includes coverage beyond the Parent Agreement’s Expiration Date of 24 December 2028, thereby establishing an integrated support service line for the entire combined period.

  (b) **Scope of Systems**:  
  - Extended support shall cover the Client’s CRM, ERP, Data Warehouse, Customer Portal, Identity Management systems, and up to ten (10) additional integrations or modules implemented under the Platform Expansion Services, as mutually agreed and documented in a schedule to this Addendum.  

  (c) **Enhanced Service Desk and Monitoring**:  
  - Operate a centralized service desk for incident and request management, consistent with Section 2.2.6(c) of the Parent Agreement, and enhance monitoring to proactively identify performance degradation and capacity issues.  
  - Perform system availability and performance monitoring 24x7 for critical components, with on-call response as specified in Article XII of this Addendum.

  (d) **Preventive Maintenance and Patching**:  
  - Perform scheduled preventive maintenance, patch management, and minor configuration changes consistent with Section 2.2.6(d) of the Parent Agreement but with additional quarterly capacity reviews and security patch assessments.  

  (e) **Reporting**:  
  - Provide **Monthly Service Performance Reports** as required under Section 2.2.6(e) and Section 12.6 of the Parent Agreement, enhanced to include trend analysis, KPI dashboards, and recommendations for improvement.  

2.2.2 The Extended Support Services shall be delivered in accordance with the enhanced Service Levels in **Article XII** of this Addendum and shall be subject to the Change Management process set forth in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

---

### 2.3 Optimization Services

2.3.1 The Vendor shall provide ongoing **Optimization Services** to maximize the value and efficiency of the Client’s enterprise systems implemented under the Parent Agreement. These services shall include, at a minimum:

  (a) **Quarterly Optimization Reviews**:  
  - Conduct quarterly sessions with the Client’s Project Sponsor and Project Lead (as defined in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement) to review system performance, incident trends, user feedback, and business objectives.  
  - Produce a **Quarterly Optimization Report** summarizing findings, recommended enhancements, prioritized backlogs, and estimated effort.

  (b) **Performance Tuning**:  
  - Analyze system logs, transaction metrics, and capacity utilization, and implement tuning measures to improve response times, throughput, and stability, provided that any major changes are subject to prior approval and change control.

  (c) **Process Enhancements**:  
  - Review business processes supported by CRM, ERP, and related applications, and propose configuration or workflow changes to streamline operations, reduce manual effort, and enhance controls.

  (d) **Security and Compliance Review**:  
  - On at least an annual basis, assess security configurations and access controls implemented under Section 2.2.3(d) of the Parent Agreement for continued compliance with Data Protection Laws and the Client’s policies, providing a **Security & Compliance Status Report**.

2.3.2 Optimization Services shall be provided during the Addendum Term and shall be coordinated through the governance meetings described in Section 2.3.3 of the Parent Agreement, which shall continue at not less than quarterly intervals during the Addendum Term.

---

### 2.4 Platform Expansion Services

2.4.1 The Vendor shall provide **Platform Expansion Services** to extend the solution footprint implemented under the Parent Agreement to additional Client divisions, regions, or product lines. These services shall be aligned with the methodologies used under Sections 2.2.2 (Solution Design and Architecture), 2.2.3 (Implementation and Configuration), and 2.2.4 (Data Migration) of the Parent Agreement and may include:

  (a) **Additional Design Work**:  
  - Prepare incremental design documents covering new business units or geographies, reusing the core architecture defined under Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement and documenting any delta requirements.  

  (b) **Incremental Configuration & Integration**:  
  - Configure additional instances, modules, or functionalities within the Client’s CRM/ERP and related systems, and implement up to an additional twenty-five (25) integration workflows, subject to agreed Change Requests and the Addendum Contract Value.  

  (c) **Localized Data Migration**:  
  - Undertake data migration for newly in-scope entities, including up to two (2) migration cycles (test and production) per new business unit or geography, following the principles in Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement.

  (d) **Additional Training and Handover**:  
  - Deliver further end-user and administrator training consistent with Section 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement, and update the administrative handover pack to cover new configurations.

2.4.2 The detailed scope of each Platform Expansion initiative, including specific Deliverables, Milestones, and Fees, shall be set out in a **Platform Expansion Work Order** (PEWO) referencing this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, and will be treated as a Change Request under Article XIII.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.2 **Fee Structure**

3.2.1 The Fees under this Addendum shall be structured as follows:

  (a) **Extended Support Fees**:

  (b) **Optimization Services Fees**:  
  - An aggregate amount of **USD 276,300** allocated to Optimization Services, including quarterly reviews and performance tuning activities.

  (d) **No Additional Contingency Budget**:  
  - Unless otherwise agreed in a Change Request, the contingency and change budget described in Section 3.2.3 of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply **only to the Parent Agreement**. Any additional contingency for this Addendum shall be established via Change Requests as needed.

---

3.3 **Payment Milestones and Invoicing**

3.3.1 **Extended Support Fees – Recurring Invoicing**

  (a) Extended Support Fees of **USD 720,000** shall be invoiced **quarterly in advance** over the Addendum Term, commencing on the first day of the first full calendar quarter following the Addendum Effective Date.  

  (b) The standard quarterly instalment shall be **USD 45,000** per quarter, subject to pro-rata adjustment for any partial quarter at the commencement or termination of the Extended Support Services.

3.3.2 **Optimization Services Fees**

  (a) Optimization Services Fees shall be invoiced **quarterly in arrears** in equal instalments of **USD 17,268.75**, corresponding to the quarterly optimization and performance tuning activities described in Section 2.3.  

  (b) Each invoice shall be supported by a summary of activities performed and deliverables provided during the relevant quarter.

3.3.3 **Platform Expansion Fees**

  (a) Platform Expansion Fees shall be invoiced based on **Milestones** to be defined in each Platform Expansion Work Order, which shall align, where practicable, with milestone concepts in Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement (e.g., design completion, configuration completion, go-live).  

  (b) Unless otherwise specified in the relevant Work Order, the following indicative milestone payments shall apply to each expansion initiative:  
   - 20% upon Client’s acceptance of incremental design documents;  
   - 40% upon completion of configuration and integrations for the expansion;  
   - 40% upon successful go-live and completion of production data migration.

3.3.4 All invoices issued under this Addendum shall (i) clearly reference **ADD-PIN-202509-259** and (ii) indicate any connection to **SOW-PIN-202509-222 (contract_222)** where relevant.

---

3.4 **Payment Terms**

3.4.1 Notwithstanding Section 3.4.1 of the Parent Agreement (Net 45), the Client shall pay all undisputed invoices issued under this Addendum within **sixty (60) calendar days** of the invoice date (**“Net 60”**), by electronic funds transfer or other mutually agreed method.

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Addendum shall accrue interest from the due date until the date of actual payment at a rate equal to **two percent (2%) per annum above the Bank of England base rate**, as provided in Section 3.5.1 of the Parent Agreement, or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, whichever is lower.

3.5.2 The suspension rights in Section 3.5.2 of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Extended Support Services, Optimization Services, and Platform Expansion Services under this Addendum, provided that the Vendor shall give a further ten (10) Business Days’ written notice prior to suspension.

Travel, accommodation, and subsistence expenses incurred in connection with on-site optimization or expansion activities shall be pre-approved by the Client and reimbursed at cost.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term**

4.1.1 The term of this Addendum (the **“Addendum Term”**) shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (7 January 2026) and shall continue in full force and effect until **15 November 2030** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Addendum Term partially overlaps with, and then extends beyond, the Parent Term (which ends on 24 December 2028). During the overlap, both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum shall be in force, and after 24 December 2028, the Addendum shall continue to apply to the Extended Support Services and any continuing obligations that survive the Parent Agreement, subject to Section 4.6 below.

---

4.2 **Renewal**

4.2.1 The Parties may mutually agree in writing, at least ninety (90) days prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, to extend or renew this Addendum for additional periods, on such terms as the Parties may negotiate, consistent with Section 4.1.2 of the Parent Agreement.

---

4.3 **Termination for Convenience**

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party. This reflects a **client-friendly** termination right, given the low risk level of this Addendum.

4.3.2 In the event of termination for convenience by the Client, the Client shall pay the Vendor:  
  (a) all Fees earned for Services performed and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination; and  
  (b) any non-cancellable costs reasonably incurred by the Vendor in reliance on this Addendum, provided such costs were pre-approved by the Client and are supported by appropriate documentation.

4.3.3 In the event of termination for convenience by the Vendor, the Vendor shall take commercially reasonable steps to mitigate adverse impacts on the Client, including providing Transition Assistance as described in Section 4.7.

---

4.4 **Termination for Cause**

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum with immediate effect upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum and such breach (if remediable) is not cured within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach, consistent with Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 Either Party may terminate this Addendum with immediate effect if the other Party becomes insolvent or subject to insolvency proceedings as described in Section 4.3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.3 The Client may terminate this Addendum with immediate effect if the Vendor suffers a material data breach or persistent Service Level failures under Article XII of this Addendum that materially compromise the security or integrity of Client Data and fails to implement corrective measures to the Client’s reasonable satisfaction within a timeframe agreed in writing, consistent with Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement.

---

4.5 **Effect of Termination**

4.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum for any reason:  
  (a) The Vendor shall cease performing the Services under this Addendum (except to the extent reasonably requested by the Client for Transition Assistance);  
  (b) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information and Client Data in accordance with **Article V** of this Addendum and **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement; and  
  (c) All accrued but unpaid Fees and approved expenses up to the effective date of termination shall become immediately due and payable, subject to the Client’s dispute rights under Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement and Section 3.4 of this Addendum.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of this Addendum shall not affect any rights, remedies, obligations, or liabilities of the Parties that have accrued up to the date of termination, including the right to claim damages for any breach occurring prior to termination.

---

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement**

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement (SOW-PIN-202509-222 / contract_222) is terminated for any reason prior to its Expiration Date, this Addendum shall:

  (a) Automatically terminate concurrently, **unless** the Parties expressly agree in writing that certain Extended Support or Optimization Services shall continue under this Addendum independently of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (b) In the event of such automatic termination, the provisions of Section 4.5 of this Addendum shall apply.

4.6.2 If the Parent Agreement expires on 24 December 2028 without earlier termination, this Addendum shall continue in effect until the Addendum Expiration Date, but only in respect of the Extended Support Services and any ongoing Optimization or Platform Expansion Services that do not require the Parent Agreement to remain in force.

---

4.7 **Transition Assistance**

4.7.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum, for any reason other than a termination by the Vendor for the Client’s uncured material breach, the Vendor shall provide reasonable Transition Assistance for up to **ninety (90) days** (or such other period as the Parties may agree) to support an orderly transition of the Services to the Client or a replacement provider.

4.7.2 Transition Assistance shall be provided at the Vendor’s then-current time and materials rates (or as otherwise agreed) and shall include, as applicable, knowledge transfer, documentation handover, and cooperation in the transfer of monitoring and support processes, subject to confidentiality and data protection obligations.

---

4.8 **Survival**

4.8.1 The provisions of Articles **V (Confidentiality)**, **VI (Intellectual Property Rights)**, **VII (Representations and Warranties)**, **VIII (Indemnification)**, **IX (Limitation of Liability)**, **XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, **XIV (Dispute Resolution)**, **XV (Force Majeure)**, and **XVI (General Provisions)** of this Addendum, and any other provisions which by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration, shall continue in full force and effect.
