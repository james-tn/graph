## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement; Governing Framework.**  

2.1.1 Pursuant to **Section 2.1 (Engagement)** and **Section 2.8 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso hereby engages Vertex, and Vertex hereby accepts such engagement, to perform the WO Services described in this Article II, subject to and in accordance with the terms of the Parent Agreement, **as supplemented and specified** by this Work Order.

2.1.2 To the extent not expressly modified or supplemented herein, the general description of Services in **Section 2.2** and the specific activities enumerated in **Sections 2.3.1 through 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement apply to the WO Services and are hereby incorporated by reference.

2.2 **Overall Description of WO Services.**  

Under this Work Order, Vertex shall provide a set of **Phase 2 Expansion Services** focusing on the design and implementation of Contoso’s cloud-based integration platform, expansion of the transformation roadmap into executable workstreams, and post-implementation optimization. The WO Services shall include, without limitation, the following categories of work:

(a) **Expanded Discovery and Current-State Validation.** Building upon the discovery and assessment activities referenced in **Section 2.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex will validate, refine, and expand prior findings by conducting additional stakeholder interviews, data analyses, and technical assessments targeted at Contoso’s order management, billing, and customer support processes.  

(b) **Solution Design and Architecture for Integration Platform.** Consistent with **Section 2.3.3 (Solution Design and Architecture)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex will architect a scalable integration platform utilizing Contoso’s selected cloud provider. Deliverables shall include integration design documents, data flow diagrams, and reference architectures for at least three (3) critical systems: ERP, CRM, and Customer Portal.

(c) **Implementation Support and Project Management.** Pursuant to **Section 2.3.4 (Implementation Support)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex shall provide project management, sprint planning, backlog management, testing support, cutover planning, and go-live readiness for the integration platform and related process changes.

(d) **Process Redesign and Change Management.** In line with **Sections 2.2 (Process assessment and redesign)** and **2.3.5 (Change Management and Training)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex will redesign selected end-to-end processes (e.g., lead-to-cash, incident management), develop change impact assessments, communication plans, training materials, and conduct training sessions for Contoso personnel.

(e) **Performance Measurement and Optimization.** As contemplated in **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex will implement key performance indicators (KPIs), monitoring dashboards, and provide periodic optimization recommendations for a period of at least six (6) months following initial go-live.

2.3 **Detailed Work Breakdown and Deliverables.**  

The WO Services shall be organized into the following major workstreams and associated WO Deliverables:

2.3.1 **Workstream A – Discovery & Validation (Estimated Duration: June 1, 2026 – August 31, 2026).**

- Activities:  
  (i) Conduct up to twenty (20) additional stakeholder interviews;  
  (ii) Review existing process documentation and Parent Agreement Deliverables;  
  (iii) Perform system landscape analysis focusing on ERP, CRM, and Customer Portal;  
  (iv) Identify data quality issues and integration constraints.

- WO Deliverables:  
  (A1) **Updated Discovery Report** summarizing current-state processes, systems, and pain points (PDF and editable format);  
  (A2) **Validated Risk Profile and Assumptions Document** referencing the high‑risk nature acknowledged in the Parent Agreement but adjusting for the medium-risk classification for this Work Order;  
  (A3) **Stakeholder Matrix** identifying roles, responsibilities, and escalation paths, aligned with **Section 2.6 (Project Governance)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.2 **Workstream B – Solution Design & Architecture (Estimated Duration: September 1, 2026 – December 31, 2026).**

- Activities:  
  (i) Define business and technical requirements for integration platform;  
  (ii) Develop at least three (3) integration design documents and associated data flow diagrams;  
  (iii) Produce reference architecture documents outlining components, interfaces, and security considerations;  
  (iv) Conduct design review workshops with Contoso’s architecture review board.

- WO Deliverables:  
  (B1) **Business Requirements Document (BRD) – Integration Platform** (aligned with **Deliverables** listed in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement);  
  (B2) **Technical Design Specification (TDS)** including integration patterns, security design, and error-handling approaches;  
  (B3) **Reference Architecture Deck** suitable for executive review;  
  (B4) **Design Decision Log** capturing key architectural decisions and Contoso approvals.

2.3.3 **Workstream C – Implementation & Cutover (Estimated Duration: January 1, 2027 – May 31, 2027).**

- Activities:  
  (i) Provide project management support, including weekly status reporting, RAID (Risks, Actions, Issues, Decisions) logs, and steering committee materials;  
  (ii) Support configuration and development of integration components (Vertex provides advisory and oversight; Contoso and/or third parties perform coding unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order);  
  (iii) Assist in test strategy, test plan creation, UAT coordination, and defect triage;  
  (iv) Develop detailed cutover plan and support go-live activities.

- WO Deliverables:  
  (C1) **Project Plan and Gantt Chart** updated monthly;  
  (C2) **Weekly Status Reports and RAID Log**;  
  (C3) **Test Strategy and Test Plan** documents;  
  (C4) **Cutover Plan and Runbook**;  
  (C5) **Go-Live Readiness Assessment**.

2.3.4 **Workstream D – Process Redesign & Change Management (Estimated Duration: Parallel with B and C).**

- Activities:  
  (i) Map current-state and future-state processes (at least five end-to-end processes);  
  (ii) Develop communication and change management plan;  
  (iii) Create training materials (User Guides, FAQs, quick-reference cards);  
  (iv) Deliver up to ten (10) training sessions (virtual or on-site) as agreed.

- WO Deliverables:  
  (D1) **Process Maps and Workflow Documentation**;  
  (D2) **Change Impact Assessment and Communication Plan**;  
  (D3) **Training Materials and User Guides**;  
  (D4) **Training Delivery Report**, including attendee lists and feedback.

2.3.5 **Workstream E – Performance Measurement & Optimization (Estimated Duration: June 1, 2027 – July 3, 2027).**

- Activities:  
  (i) Define KPIs and baseline metrics aligned with Contoso’s business objectives;  
  (ii) Configure performance dashboards (using Contoso’s selected tooling);  
  (iii) Conduct monthly optimization review sessions;  
  (iv) Provide final optimization recommendations.

- WO Deliverables:  
  (E1) **KPI Framework and Measurement Plan**;  
  (E2) **Performance Dashboard Configuration Documentation**;  
  (E3) **Optimization Reports** (at least two (2) reports);  
  (E4) **Final Lessons Learned and Recommendations Report**.

Dispute procedures in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement apply. Contoso must notify Vertex in writing of any disputed portion of an invoice within fifteen (15) days of receipt, specifying the nature and basis of the dispute. 3.6.1 In addition to the Fees described above, Contoso shall reimburse Vertex for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the WO Services (including travel, lodging, meals, and other project-related expenses), as provided in **Section 3.6 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Work Order.**  

This Work Order shall commence on the **WO Effective Date** (**June 1, 2026**) and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV and Article IV of the Parent Agreement, shall remain in full force and effect until **July 3, 2027** (the **“WO Expiration Date”**, and together with the Effective Date, the **“Work Order Term”**).

4.2 **Renewal / Extension.**  

Any extension of the Work Order Term shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties. The Term renewal provisions in **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement apply at the master level; the Parties may agree to extend this Work Order via amendment or a successor Work Order, provided the Parent Agreement remains in effect or is renewed.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (This Work Order).**  

4.3.1 Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement (which allows Contoso to terminate the entire Agreement upon 180 days’ notice and Vertex upon 60 days’ notice), the Parties agree that with respect to this Work Order only:

(a) Contoso may terminate this Work Order, in whole or in part, for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Vertex; and  
(b) Vertex may terminate this Work Order, in whole or in part, for convenience upon **ninety (90) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso.

4.3.2 During such notice period, the Parties shall continue to perform their respective obligations hereunder, and Contoso shall remain responsible for payment of all Fees and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, plus any non-cancellable commitments, in line with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Work Order for cause immediately upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Work Order or the Parent Agreement as it relates to this Work Order and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice, consistent with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement. Insolvency and related events in Section 4.4(b) of the Parent Agreement constitute cause for termination of this Work Order as well.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Work Order:

(a) Vertex shall cease performance of WO Services (except as reasonably requested by Contoso for wind-down activities);  
(b) Contoso shall pay all Fees and expenses accrued but unpaid as of the effective date of termination, including Fees for work-in-progress and reasonable demobilization costs, consistent with **Section 4.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(c) The Parties shall return or destroy Confidential Information upon request, as described in **Section 4.5(c)** of the Parent Agreement and Article V of both agreements; and  
(d) Provisions intended to survive (including payment obligations, confidentiality, IP rights, indemnification, limitation of liability, dispute resolution, and data protection) shall survive to the extent applicable to this Work Order.

4.5.2 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.** If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires, this Work Order shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such termination or expiration, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue some or all of the WO Services under a successor agreement. In such case, the effects of termination described above shall apply.

4.6 **Transition Assistance.**  

If requested by Contoso in writing at least thirty (30) days prior to termination or expiration of this Work Order, Vertex shall provide Transition Assistance for up to sixty (60) days post-termination, on a time-and-materials basis at then-current rates, consistent with **Section 4.6 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement, but limited to the WO Services and Deliverables.
