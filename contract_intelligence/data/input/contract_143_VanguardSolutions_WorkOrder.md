## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Work Order is executed **under and subject to** the terms of the Parent Agreement, reference number **CSA-VAN-202404-116 (contract_116)**, and constitutes a **“Statement of Work”** as contemplated in **Section 2.2 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 This Work Order **authorizes additional consulting work dated April 27, 2024 (Ref: CSA-VAN-202404-116)** by expanding the scope of previously contemplated activities under **Section 2.1 (General Description of Services)** and **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, particularly in the domains of roadmap refinement, advanced analytics, and pilot implementation.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Work Order and the Parent Agreement, the terms of the Parent Agreement shall control, **except** to the limited extent that this Work Order expressly modifies a specific provision of the Parent Agreement, in which case such modification shall apply solely to the Services provided under this Work Order.

### 2.2 Project Overview

2.2.1 Under this Work Order, the Service Provider shall provide supplemental and extended consulting Services in connection with the Company’s enterprise technology and data analytics initiatives, including:

- refinement and extension of the existing **digital transformation roadmap** developed under prior SOWs;  
- additional **analytics use-case design** and pilot implementation, with a focus on executive dashboards and operational performance metrics; and  
- development of training and enablement materials for broader internal adoption.

2.2.2 The Services under this Work Order are structured around three main **Phases**:

- **Phase A – Roadmap Extension & Prioritization**  
- **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Pilot Implementation**  
- **Phase C – Training, Handover & Advisory Support**

These Phases are aligned substantively with the types of activities described in **Section 2.1(a)-(g)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.3 Detailed Scope of Services (Minimum ~500 Words)

2.3.1 **Phase A – Roadmap Extension & Prioritization**

(a) Conduct targeted workshops with key Contoso stakeholders (including IT leadership, operations, and finance) to reassess assumptions, dependencies, and priority initiatives in the existing digital transformation roadmap developed under prior work authorized by the Parent Agreement.

(b) Review and analyze updates to the Company’s technology landscape, including new systems deployed since the Effective Date of the Parent Agreement, changes to data architecture, and revised business strategy elements.

(c) Develop an **Extended Transformation Roadmap** deliverable, which will:

- incorporate additional initiatives in the domains of data governance, self-service analytics, and integration with newly adopted cloud platforms;  
- refine timelines, sequencing, and dependencies for at least twelve (12) months post-Effective Date of this Work Order;  
- provide updated cost estimates and benefit hypotheses consistent with the methodology used for the original roadmap; and  
- include an updated risk assessment reflecting the high-risk profile acknowledged in the Parent Agreement, with specific mitigation strategies.

(d) Produce a **Roadmap Prioritization Matrix** that scores initiatives based on business impact, complexity, risk, and resource requirements, enabling Contoso leadership to make informed decisions regarding sequencing and investment.

(e) Provide a **Phase A Executive Summary Presentation** suitable for presentation to the Company’s Executive Steering Committee.

2.3.2 **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Pilot Implementation**

(a) In accordance with **Section 2.1(d)-(e)** of the Parent Agreement, design and implement one (1) to three (3) new or extended pilot use cases to demonstrate advanced analytics capabilities in areas such as customer churn prediction, operational efficiency, or financial performance analysis, as mutually agreed in writing.

(b) Activities in this Phase shall include:

- requirements gathering sessions with business and technical stakeholders;  
- definition of key performance indicators (KPIs), metrics, and data sources;  
- design of data models and integration specifications, consistent with **Solution Design Documentation** contemplated in **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- configuration and limited customization of analytics tools, dashboards, and reports;  
- development and deployment of prototype dashboards and reports, consistent with **Pilot Implementation Artifacts** described in **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- documentation of pilot architecture, configuration parameters, and operational instructions.

(c) Deliverables under this Phase shall include:

- **Pilot Design Document** (requirements, design, and technical approach);  
- **Prototype Dashboards and Reports** (up to six (6) dashboards and related reports, unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order);  
- **Data Integration Specifications** describing data pipelines, transformations, and integration to source systems; and  
- **Pilot Evaluation Report**, including business outcomes observed, key learnings, and recommendations for scaling.

(d) Vanguard will additionally provide **project management services**, as contemplated in **Section 2.1(g)** of the Parent Agreement, including bi-weekly status reports and tracking of risks, issues, and decision logs.

2.3.3 **Phase C – Training, Handover & Advisory Support**

(a) As an extension of **Section 2.1(f)** and **Section 2.3(e) (Training and Workshop Materials)** of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall conduct:

- up to four (4) virtual or on-site **training sessions** for Contoso’s analytics and business teams;  
- one (1) executive-level briefing session focused on the roadmap and pilot outcomes; and  
- one (1) handover workshop with Contoso’s internal analytics team and/or third-party partners.

(b) Deliverables in Phase C include:

- **Training Materials** (slide decks, quick reference guides, and related documentation);  
- **Handover Package**, summarizing architecture, configuration, and operational processes; and  
- **Advisory Support Memorandum**, outlining recommended next steps, potential future phases, and a prioritized backlog of enhancements.

(c) The Service Provider shall provide up to **eighty (80) hours** of post-implementation advisory support on a time-and-materials basis (unless otherwise provided in a Change Order), to assist with fine-tuning dashboards, addressing defects, and advising on expansion.

### 2.4 Timelines and Milestones

2.4.1 Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

- **Phase A** shall commence on or shortly after the Effective Date and is expected to be completed within ninety (90) days;  
- **Phase B** shall commence promptly after approval of the Extended Transformation Roadmap and is expected to be completed within one hundred twenty (120) days thereafter;  
- **Phase C** shall commence upon substantial completion of Phase B and shall continue for up to six (6) months, subject to the Term and Expiration Date.

2.4.2 The Parties acknowledge that successful and timely performance is dependent upon the Company’s cooperation, as described in **Section 2.7 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement. Delays attributable to the Company, Force Majeure Events, or other circumstances beyond the reasonable control of the Service Provider shall entitle the Service Provider to reasonable schedule adjustments and, if applicable, Fee adjustments via Change Orders.

2.4.3 Key **Milestones** and associated Deliverables and payments are set forth in **Article III** below.

### 2.5 Performance Standards

The Service Provider shall perform the Services under this Work Order in accordance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, including use of appropriately skilled personnel and compliance with Applicable Law and the Company’s written policies, to the extent provided in advance and not inconsistent with this Work Order or the Parent Agreement.

### 2.6 Reporting and Meetings

2.6.1 The Parties’ project managers shall be responsible for day-to-day coordination of the Services and shall meet, in person or via teleconference/video conference, at least bi-weekly during Phases A and B, and at least monthly during Phase C, unless otherwise mutually agreed.

2.6.2 The Service Provider shall provide periodic written status reports consistent with **Section 2.6 (Reporting and Meetings)** of the Parent Agreement, including:

- progress against milestones;  
- issues, risks, and mitigation plans;  
- summary of time-and-materials hours (where applicable); and  
- upcoming tasks and dependencies.

### 2.7 Company Responsibilities

The Company’s obligations under **Section 2.7 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Work Order. Without limiting the foregoing, the Company shall:

- provide timely access to relevant personnel, systems, and data;

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Contract Value

3.1.1 The total estimated value of this Work Order (the **“WO Contract Value”**) is **One Hundred Sixty-Two Thousand One Hundred Twenty U.S. Dollars (US $162,120)**.

3.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that this WO Contract Value is a **good-faith estimate** only and not a guaranteed maximum or minimum, consistent with **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the Parent Agreement. Actual Fees may differ based on approved Change Orders and actual time-and-materials usage.

### 3.2 Fee Structure

3.2.1 The Fees under this Work Order shall be structured as follows (amounts are indicative and may be refined by mutual agreement in writing):

(a) **Phase A – Roadmap Extension & Prioritization**  
- Fixed fee of **US $48,000**, inclusive of all labor associated with workshops, analysis, and production of the Extended Transformation Roadmap, Prioritization Matrix, and Executive Summary Presentation.

(b) **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Pilot Implementation**  
- Baseline fixed fee of **US $78,000** for design, configuration, and delivery of pilot use cases and associated Deliverables; and  
- Additional time-and-materials charges for work beyond the baseline scope (e.g., more than six (6) dashboards, additional data integrations), billed at agreed blended hourly or daily rates to be specified in an annex to this Work Order.

(c) **Phase C – Training, Handover & Advisory Support**  
- Fixed fee of **US $24,000** for training sessions, handover workshop, and preparation of training and handover Deliverables; and  
- Time-and-materials fees for up to eighty (80) hours of advisory support, estimated at **US $12,120**, based on an average blended rate to be set forth in an annex.

3.2.2 Any changes to the scope or assumptions underlying the above fees shall be managed via **Change Orders** in accordance with **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Work Order.

### 3.3 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.3.1 The Service Provider shall invoice the Company for the fixed-fee components as follows:

- **Phase A**:  
  - 40% (US $19,200) upon execution of this Work Order;  
  - 40% (US $19,200) upon delivery of the draft Extended Transformation Roadmap;  
  - 20% (US $9,600) upon the earlier of (i) the Company’s written acceptance of the final Extended Transformation Roadmap or (ii) ten (10) Business Days after delivery of the final roadmap if no material objections have been raised by the Company.

- **Phase B**:  
  - 30% (US $23,400) upon formal kickoff of Phase B;  
  - 40% (US $31,200) upon delivery of the Pilot Design Document and initial prototype dashboards;  
  - 30% (US $23,400) upon completion of the Pilot Evaluation Report.

- **Phase C**:  
  - 50% (US $12,000) upon commencement of Phase C;  
  - 50% (US $12,000) upon completion of the training sessions and delivery of the Handover Package.

3.3.2 Time-and-materials Services (including additional scope in Phase B and advisory support in Phase C) shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears** based on actual hours worked, consistent with **Section 3.4 (Invoicing)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.3 **Payment Terms – Net 60.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement (Net 30), the Parties hereby agree that, for this Work Order only, all undisputed invoices shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** after the invoice date (**“Net 60”**). For clarity, this provision **supplements and modifies** Section 3.3 solely as applied to invoices issued under this Work Order.

### 3.4 Late Payments; Disputed Amounts

3.4.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Work Order shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.5 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement (1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law), calculated from the due date until paid in full.

3.4.2 Disputed amounts shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement. The Company shall timely pay all undisputed portions of any invoice.

### 3.5 Expenses

3.5.1 The Company shall reimburse the Service Provider for reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with performing the Services under this Work Order, in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 Any travel must comply with the Company’s then-current travel and expense policies provided in advance. The Service Provider shall include supporting documentation with its expense invoices.

### 3.6 Taxes

The tax provisions of **Section 3.8 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Work Order. All Fees herein are exclusive of applicable taxes, which shall be borne by the Company except for taxes on the Service Provider’s income.
