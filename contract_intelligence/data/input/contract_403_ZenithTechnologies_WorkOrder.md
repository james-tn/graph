## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Parent Statement of Work.**  

Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355 and **Section 2.2 (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Master Services Agreement, Vendor shall perform the additional Services described in this Work Order (the **“Extended Program Scope”**). This Work Order:

- **Adds scope** to the Program defined in **Section 2.3 (Program Description and Project Phases)** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355;
- Introduces new **Additional Deliverables** that build upon or extend Deliverables D1–D10;
- Establishes new **WO Milestones** and associated Fees, without altering the existing Milestones M1–M6 except as explicitly stated herein; and
- Applies the performance standards of **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355 and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Services Agreement to the Extended Program Scope, supplemented as specified below.

Except as expressly modified in this Work Order, the obligations, roles, responsibilities, and performance standards under **Article II** of the Parent Statement of Work remain in full force and apply to the Extended Program Scope.

2.2 **Extended Program Scope – Workstreams.**  

Vendor shall provide the following additional workstreams under this Work Order:

2.2.1 **Workstream A – Advanced Analytics Platform Extension.**  

Building on Deliverables D2 (Functional Design Specifications), D3 (Technical Architecture Documents), D6 (Technical Configuration & Integration Documentation), and D9 (Quarterly Operations and Performance Reports) described in **Section 2.4** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355, Vendor shall:

a. Design and implement an **Advanced Analytics Platform** that aggregates data from the implemented ERP, CRM, and line-of-business systems assessed in Phase 1 of the Parent Statement of Work (**Section 2.3.1**), as well as additional sources agreed in writing.

b. Deliver enhanced **functional and technical design documents** for the analytics components, including KPI catalogs, data models, semantic layers, and dashboard specifications (see **Additional Deliverable A1** in Section 2.4).

c. Configure and deploy at least three (3) executive dashboards (finance, operations, and customer experience) and ten (10) operational dashboards across APAC, EMEA, and Americas regions.

d. Implement role-based access controls for analytics consistent with **Section 11.3 (Security Measures)** of the Master Services Agreement and the security model defined during **Phase 2 – Design & Architecture** of the Parent Statement of Work (**Section 2.3.2**).

e. Integrate the analytics platform with the quarterly reporting processes underlying Deliverable D9 and implement automation to generate Quarterly Operations and Performance Reports.

2.2.2 **Workstream B – Automation Enhancements and Workflow Orchestration.**  

Building on **Phase 3 – Build, Integration & Migration** and **Phase 4 – UAT & Go-Live** (Sections 2.3.3 and 2.3.4 of the Parent Statement of Work), Vendor shall:

a. Identify, in collaboration with Client, at least fifteen (15) high-impact business processes across order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, and incident management flows suitable for automation.

b. Design and implement **Automation Enhancements** using Vendor’s integration accelerators and Vendor Pre-Existing IP as contemplated by **Section 6.3 (Vendor Pre-Existing IP)** of the Master Services Agreement and **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Statement of Work.

c. Configure workflow orchestration across multiple systems and departments, ensuring traceability and audit capabilities aligned with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Master Services Agreement.

d. Conduct process-level UAT, performance testing, and pilot releases in at least two regions prior to full global rollout.

2.2.3 **Workstream C – Regional Rollout Services (Additional Regions).**  

In addition to the global coverage originally contemplated, Vendor shall:

a. Extend the implemented solutions and managed services model to **additional regions**, including at least Latin America and two (2) designated emerging markets to be identified by Client in writing.

b. Perform **Regional Rollout Services** including local requirements gathering, configuration localization, language support where required, and integration with in-region third-party systems.

c. Provide onsite or remote training sessions and localized Training Materials, building upon Deliverable D8 (Training Materials and Knowledge Base Articles) under **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent Statement of Work.

d. Align regional go-lives with the overall Program roadmap and Milestones defined in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Statement of Work, as supplemented by WO Milestones in this Work Order.

2.2.4 **Workstream D – Enhanced Managed Services & Continuous Improvement.**  

Further to **Phase 5 – Managed Services & Continuous Improvement** (Section 2.3.5 of SOW-ZEN-202403-355), Vendor shall:

a. Extend L1–L3 support coverage to the additional regions and functionalities introduced under this Work Order.

b. Provide **proactive monitoring and predictive incident detection** for the Advanced Analytics Platform and Automation Enhancements, integrated with Vendor’s managed services processes.

c. Deliver incremental minor enhancements and optimizations to the analytics and automation components under a managed **continuous improvement backlog**, governed via WO Change Orders under **Article XIII**.

d. Comply with the **Enhanced Service Levels** defined in **Article XII** of this Work Order, which supplement the Service Level obligations in Article XII of the Parent Statement of Work and Article XII of the Master Services Agreement.

2.3 **Dependencies and Client Responsibilities.**  

The Extended Program Scope depends on Client’s timely fulfillment of its responsibilities under **Section 2.9 (Client Responsibilities for this SOW)** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355 and **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Master Services Agreement, including:

- Providing timely access to additional regional subject matter experts;
- Ensuring required system environments and data extracts for new regions and analytics sources;
- Obtaining internal approvals for process automation and analytics publication.

Vendor’s obligations under this Work Order are expressly conditioned on Client’s fulfillment of such responsibilities.

2.4 **Additional Deliverables.**  

In addition to Deliverables D1–D10, Vendor shall produce the following **Additional Deliverables** under this Work Order (all of which shall constitute “Deliverables” and “Work Product” as described in **Sections 1.10 and 6.4** of the Master Services Agreement and **Article VI** of the Parent Statement of Work):

- **A1 – Advanced Analytics Design & Data Model Specification**: Detailed functional and technical specifications for the analytics platform, KPIs, data models, and security model.

- **A2 – Executable Analytics Dashboards & Reports Package**: Configured dashboards, reports, and associated metadata, ready for production deployment.

- **A3 – Automation Blueprint & Workflow Catalog**: A catalog of automated processes, workflow diagrams, exception handling rules, and integration points.

- **A4 – Automation Implementation Package**: Configured workflows, integration scripts, and automation jobs deployed in non-production and production environments.

- **A5 – Regional Rollout Plans & Localization Specifications**: Region-specific rollout plans, localization requirements, and configuration matrices.

- **A6 – Regional Training and Enablement Materials**: Training decks, localized documentation, and knowledge base content for regional users and administrators.

- **A7 – Enhanced Monitoring & Predictive Analytics Configuration**: Monitoring rules, thresholds, and predictive models used for incident detection on analytics and automation components.

- **A8 – Extended Managed Services Runbook**: Updates to the Go-Live Runbook and Operational SOPs (D7) to cover the Extended Program Scope.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

Vendor shall perform the Services under this Work Order:

2.5.1 In accordance with the performance standards in **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355 and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Services Agreement, including the obligation to perform in a professional and workmanlike manner.

2.5.2 Utilizing personnel with skills and experience appropriate to advanced analytics and automation, including any **Key Personnel** designated under **Section 2.8 (Key Personnel and Governance)** of the Parent Statement of Work.

2.5.3 In compliance with the **Enhanced Service Levels** in **Article XII** of this Work Order and the Parent SLA Framework.

2.5.4 In coordination with Client’s responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.9** of SOW-ZEN-202403-355; delays attributable to Client shall be managed via WO Change Orders in accordance with **Article XIII** of the Master Services Agreement.

2.6 **WO Timelines and Milestones.**  

The following **WO Milestones** and target dates apply specifically to this Work Order (all dates are target dates and may be adjusted through WO Change Orders):

- **W1 – Completion of Advanced Analytics Design (A1):** Target – 31 March 2025  
- **W2 – Deployment of Initial Executive Dashboards (A2, partial):** Target – 30 June 2025  
- **W3 – Completion of Automation Blueprint (A3):** Target – 30 September 2025

- **W7 – Full Deployment of Enhanced Monitoring & Predictive Analytics (A7) and Extended Managed Services Runbook (A8):** Target – 31 December 2026
