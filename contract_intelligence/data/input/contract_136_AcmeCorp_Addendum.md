## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the **Purchase Agreement** reference number **PUR-ACM-202407-048** (Contoso internal identifier: **contract_048**) dated **9 July 2024** (the **“Effective Date”** of the Parent Agreement), pursuant to which Acme agreed to provide certain **Products** and **Services** to Contoso, including enterprise software licenses (the **“Acme Suite”**), hardware components, implementation, configuration, data migration, training, and support, as more fully described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, Acme has represented that it has the expertise, personnel, and resources necessary to provide such additional products and services, and is willing to do so under the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented and modified by this Addendum;

WHEREAS, **this Addendum adds products or services dated July 09, 2024 (Ref: PUR-ACM-202407-048)** and is intended to operate as a **Statement of Work and amendment** under the Parent Agreement, expanding the scope of Products and Services and specifying additional Fees, milestones, and service levels consistent with **Sections 2.9 (Statements of Work)** and **13.3 (Approval and Change Orders)** of the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein and in the Parent Agreement, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed as a **Statement of Work** under and pursuant to **Section 2.9 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement and shall be deemed a “SOW” for all purposes thereunder. In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Addendum and the main body of the Parent Agreement, the order of precedence set out in **Section 16.10 (Order of Precedence)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply, except where this Addendum expressly states that it supersedes a provision of the Parent Agreement with respect to the services described herein.

2.1.2 This Addendum **adds products and services** to those already contemplated in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. The Additional Products and Additional Services described herein are part of the “Products” and “Services” as defined in **Sections 1.1.15** and **1.1.16** of the Parent Agreement and are subject to all applicable terms therein, including **Sections 2.3 (Implementation and Configuration Services)**, **2.4 (Testing and Acceptance)**, **2.5 (Training)**, and **2.6 (Support and Maintenance Services)**, except as specifically varied in this Addendum.

2.2 **Objectives.**  

The objective of this Addendum is to:

   a. Extend Contoso’s deployment of the **Acme Suite** to include advanced analytics, warehouse management, and field service management functionalities;  
   b. Provide associated implementation, integration, data migration, testing, training, and Managed Services to enable Contoso to use the Additional Products in its UK and selected EU operations;  
   c. Align the delivery of the Additional Products and Additional Services with the acceptance mechanisms outlined in **Section 2.4 (Testing and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, supplemented by the Milestones (Addendum) defined below; and  
   d. Ensure that all such additions are delivered in a manner consistent with **Section 2.7 (Performance Standards)** and **Section 2.8 (Cooperation and Dependencies)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Additional Products.**  

Subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Acme shall provide the following Additional Products:

2.3.1 **Advanced Analytics Module Licenses.**

   a. 250 named-user licenses for the **Advanced Analytics Module** for finance, supply chain, and sales analytics;  
   b. Access rights for up to 5 TB of analytics data storage in Contoso’s existing Acme Suite environment;  
   c. Standard product documentation, including user guides and configuration manuals.

2.3.2 **Warehouse Management Module (WMS) Licenses.**

   a. 150 concurrent user licenses for the **WMS Module**;  
   b. Functionality including inventory control, inbound and outbound logistics, cycle counting, and RF scanner integration;  
   c. Integration adapters for Contoso’s existing ERP and legacy warehouse systems as identified during discovery.

2.3.3 **Field Service Management Module (FSM) Licenses.**

   a. 120 mobile technician licenses and 30 dispatcher/scheduler licenses;  
   b. Mobile application access for Android and iOS platforms;  
   c. Integration with Contoso’s identity management and scheduling systems.

2.3.4 **Hardware Components (if required).**

   To the extent required for on-premises or hybrid deployment, Acme shall supply:  

   a. Up to 4 additional server appliances compatible with the Acme Suite, each with specifications not less than those currently in use at Contoso;  
   b. Associated networking equipment as reasonably necessary for the Additional Products, consistent with **Section 2.2.2 (Hardware Components)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   c. Manufacturer warranties as described in **Article VII (Representations and Warranties)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Additional Services.**  

Acme shall provide the following Additional Services in accordance with **Section 2.3 (Implementation and Configuration Services)** of the Parent Agreement:

2.4.1 **Discovery and Requirements Analysis.**

   a. Conduct focused workshops with Contoso’s finance, operations, warehouse, and field service teams to capture detailed functional and technical requirements for the Additional Products;  
   b. Document business processes, integration points, security roles, and reporting needs specific to the Advanced Analytics, WMS, and FSM Modules;  
   c. Produce a **Requirements Specification Document** to be approved in writing by Contoso.

2.4.2 **Solution Design and Project Plan.**

   a. Prepare a consolidated **Solution Design Document** covering system architecture, data models, integration flows, and configuration standards for the Additional Products;  
   b. Prepare a **Project Plan**, including tasks, dependencies, Milestones (Addendum), and resource allocations;  
   c. Submit both documents to Contoso for review and written approval, consistent with **Section 2.3(b) (Solution Design)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 **Configuration, Customisation, and Managed Services.**

   a. Configure the Additional Products, including user roles, workflows, business rules, and standard dashboards;  
   b. Perform limited customisation (e.g., additional fields, reports, and integration scripts), provided such customisation is documented and approved via Change Management as necessary under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement;  
   c. From the date of Production Go-Live (as defined in **Section 2.6.3** of this Addendum) and during the Addendum Term, provide **Managed Services**, including:  
      i. Monitoring of system health for the Additional Products;  
      ii. Application of patches and minor updates, consistent with **Section 2.6(b) (Bug Fixes and Updates)** of the Parent Agreement;  
      iii. Performance tuning and capacity recommendations;  
      iv. Administration of user roles and access for the Additional Products;  
      v. Up to 40 hours per month of minor configuration changes and report modifications.

2.4.4 **Integration Services.**

   a. Design and implement integrations between the Additional Products and Contoso’s existing ERP, CRM, HR, email, and identity management systems, as identified in the Requirements Specification Document;  
   b. Use APIs or middleware consistent with **Section 2.3(d) (Integration Services)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   c. Provide documentation of integration interfaces and error handling procedures.

2.4.5 **Data Migration.**

   a. Plan and execute data extraction and migration from Contoso’s legacy warehouse and field service systems into the WMS and FSM Modules;  
   b. Provide mapping specifications and test migration cycles;  
   c. Support Contoso in validating migrated data, with Contoso remaining responsible for data quality as per **Section 2.3(e) (Data Migration)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.6 **Testing and Acceptance Support.**

   a. Conduct unit and system integration testing in Acme’s environment;  
   b. Support Contoso’s UAT in the Pilot Environment;  
   c. Apply the acceptance mechanisms described in **Section 2.4 (Testing and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, with specific acceptance criteria for each Milestone (Addendum).

2.4.7 **Training.**

   a. Provide administrator training for the Additional Products for up to 20 Contoso IT staff;  
   b. Provide end-user training sessions for finance, warehouse, and field service personnel;  
   c. Deliver training materials in electronic form, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Training)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.8 **Support and Maintenance.**

   a. Extend existing support arrangements to cover the Additional Products, in line with **Section 2.6 (Support and Maintenance Services)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   b. Provide first and second-line support for issues specifically related to the Additional Products, subject to the Addendum SLA in **Article XII**.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

Acme shall perform the Additional Services in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, i.e.:

   a. with due skill, care, and diligence;  
   b. in a timely and professional manner, consistent with industry best practices for enterprise software implementation and support;  
   c. using appropriately qualified, experienced, and trained personnel; and  
   d. in compliance with all Applicable Laws and Contoso’s reasonable written policies.

2.6 **Milestones (Addendum) and Timelines.**  

2.6.1 The following indicative Milestones (Addendum) shall apply:

   a. **Milestone 1 – Discovery & Requirements Sign-Off:** Completion and Contoso approval of the Requirements Specification Document – target: within 45 Business Days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
   b. **Milestone 2 – Design & Project Plan Approval:** Completion and Contoso approval of the Solution Design Document and Project Plan – target: within 30 Business Days after Milestone 1.  
   c. **Milestone 3 – Pilot Environment Deployment:** Deployment of Additional Products in the Pilot Environment and completion of internal testing – target: within 60 Business Days after Milestone 2.  
   d. **Milestone 4 – UAT Completion:** Successful UAT and written acceptance by Contoso in accordance with **Section 2.4.2 (Acceptance Criteria)** of the Parent Agreement – target: within 45 Business Days after Milestone 3.  
   e. **Milestone 5 – Production Go-Live:** Deployment of Additional Products to the Production Environment and commencement of Managed Services – target: within 30 Business Days after Milestone 4.

2.6.2 Each Milestone shall have detailed acceptance criteria to be agreed in writing in the Project Plan; in the absence of such detail, acceptance shall be governed by **Section 2.4 (Testing and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.3 For the purposes of the Addendum:  

   - **“Production Go-Live”** means the first date on which Contoso uses any of the Additional Products in the Production Environment for live business operations (which may coincide with deemed acceptance under **Section 2.4.4 (Deemed Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement).

2.7 **Dependencies and Client Responsibilities.**  

Contoso shall comply with its obligations in **Section 2.8 (Cooperation and Dependencies)** of the Parent Agreement, including:

   a. providing timely access to relevant personnel, systems, and information;  
   b. ensuring the IT environment meets minimum technical requirements specified by Acme;  
   c. providing test data, participating in UAT, and providing sign-offs in a timely manner;  
   d. managing internal change and communications to its end-users.

Delays due to Contoso’s failure to perform its obligations may result in schedule extensions and Fee adjustments as set out in **Section 2.8** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

   a. Implementation, configuration, integration, data migration, and training Services: USD $60,000, based on agreed fixed-fee work packages;  
   b. Additional professional services, if requested beyond the agreed scope, to be charged on a time-and-materials basis at daily or hourly rates consistent with the Parent Agreement and the applicable Change Order.

3.2.3 **Managed Services Fees.**

   a. Managed Services (as defined in **Section 2.4.3**) for the Additional Products: USD $10,000 per annum, payable in advance in accordance with **Section 3.3** below;  
   b. Managed Services will commence upon Production Go-Live and continue for the Addendum Term unless terminated earlier.

3.2.4 **Hardware Fees.**

   a. Hardware components (if supplied) shall be charged at a one-time purchase price not to exceed USD $13,010 in aggregate, inclusive of delivery to Contoso’s designated location, in line with **Section 3.2.2 (Hardware and Product Fees)** and the Incoterms referenced therein.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  

3.3.1 **Invoicing Schedule.**

   a. 40% of fixed License Fees upon the earlier of: (i) delivery of license keys; or (ii) activation of the Additional Products in the Pilot Environment;  
   b. 40% of fixed License Fees upon Production Go-Live;  
   c. Remaining 20% of fixed License Fees upon completion of Milestone 5 (Production Go-Live acceptance) or 30 days after Production Go-Live, whichever is earlier;  
   d. Professional Services Fees billed as follows:  
      i. 30% upon completion of Milestone 1 (Discovery & Requirements Sign-Off);  
      ii. 40% upon completion of Milestone 3 (Pilot Environment Deployment);  
      iii. 30% upon completion of Milestone 5 (Production Go-Live);  
   e. Managed Services Fees invoiced annually in advance on or about the anniversary of Production Go-Live;  
   f. Hardware Fees invoiced upon shipment, consistent with **Section 3.3.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.2 **Payment Terms.**  

Notwithstanding **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that the payment terms for all undisputed Addendum invoices shall be **Net sixty (60) days** from the date of receipt of a valid invoice, unless otherwise specified in the relevant invoice. All other provisions of **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** and **3.6 (Late Payment)**, shall apply to this Addendum.

3.3.3 **Late Payment.**  

Late payment of undisputed Addendum Fees shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement. Acme shall not suspend the Additional Services for late payment unless the conditions in **Section 3.6** have been met, and any suspension shall be coordinated to minimise impact on Contoso’s operations.

3.4 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

Out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Acme in connection with on-site services under this Addendum shall be reimbursed in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses and Reimbursement)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to prior approval by Contoso and provision of appropriate receipts.

3.5 **Taxes.**  

Taxes shall be handled as set forth in **Section 3.8 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. The Addendum Fees are exclusive of VAT and similar taxes, which shall be added where applicable and payable by Contoso.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall remain in effect until **5 February 2027**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  

4.2.1 This Addendum is **co-terminous with, and in all cases subject to, the Parent Agreement**. If the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated for any reason in accordance with **Article IV** thereof, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, without the need for separate notice, except to the extent expressly provided otherwise in writing by the Parties.

4.2.2 Notwithstanding the Initial Term expiration date of **17 May 2027** in the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that the Addendum Term shall end on **5 February 2027**, unless extended by mutual written agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Acme, consistent with **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.2 Acme may terminate this Addendum for convenience by providing not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso; provided that such termination shall not take effect prior to completion of any then-active critical Milestones (Addendum) identified in the Project Plan, unless Contoso consents in writing, consistent with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement in the event of:

   a. a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement in relation to the Additional Products or Additional Services, not cured within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice; or  
   b. the insolvency events set forth in **Section 4.4.2 (Insolvency)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  

Upon expiry or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

   a. Acme shall cease providing the Additional Services and, unless otherwise agreed, disable access to the Additional Products, subject to any perpetual license rights granted under **Article VI** of this Addendum and **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement;  
   b. All outstanding invoices for Addendum Fees shall become immediately due and payable, and Acme shall submit a final invoice for all Additional Products delivered and Additional Services performed up to the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.5(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   c. Contoso shall pay all undisputed Addendum Fees for Additional Products delivered and Additional Services performed prior to termination, including any non-cancellable third-party charges reasonably incurred by Acme on Contoso’s behalf, in accordance with **Section 4.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   d. Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 5.5 (Return or Destruction)** of the Parent Agreement;  
   e. Acme shall provide reasonable termination assistance related to the Additional Products and Additional Services, if requested by Contoso, for up to ninety (90) days on a time-and-materials basis, consistent with **Section 4.5(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6 **Survival.**  

The provisions of this Addendum which by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiry, including **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI**, as well as any payment obligations accrued as of the effective date of termination, shall survive, together with the surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as specified in **Section 4.6 (Survival)** thereof.
