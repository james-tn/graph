## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Master Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Work Order is a “Statement of Work” as contemplated in **Section 1.17** and **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Master Agreement. In accordance with **Section 2.1(a)** of the Master Agreement, this Work Order:  

  (i) references the Master Agreement and contract reference number **MSA-QUA-202404-148 / contract_148**;  
  (ii) describes the Services and Deliverables;  
  (iii) specifies timelines, milestones, and acceptance criteria; and  
  (iv) states applicable Fees and payment terms consistent with **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Master Agreement.  

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified or supplemented herein, all provisions of the Master Agreement remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Services performed under this Work Order.

2.2 **Overview of Project.**  

Vendor shall provide a comprehensive, multi-phase program to modernize Client’s data and analytics capabilities, implement and manage machine learning workflows, and deliver business-facing insights. The Project consists of:

  (a) **Data Platform Modernization.** Design and implementation of a cloud-based data platform encompassing data ingestion, transformation, storage, and access layers, in alignment with Vendor’s obligations under **Section 2.2(b) (Data Analytics and Modeling)** and **Section 2.2(c) (Software Development and Integration)** of the Master Agreement;

  (b) **Machine Learning Experimentation & Model Factory.** Development of reusable ML experimentation pipelines, feature repositories, model training and evaluation frameworks, consistent with the experimental nature acknowledged in **Section 2.1(b)** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) **Customer Insights & Reporting Suite.** Creation of dashboards, visualizations, and analytical reports that leverage the underlying data platform and models to generate actionable insights for Client’s business stakeholders;

  (d) **Managed MLOps & Support.** Provision of Platform Services including hosting, monitoring, maintenance, and continuous improvement of models in production, subject to the Service Levels under **Article XII** of the Master Agreement and **Article XII** of this Work Order.

2.3 **Phases, Activities, and Deliverables.**  

The Project shall be executed in four (4) Phases. Each Phase includes specific activities and Deliverables.

2.3.1 **Phase 1 – Discovery & Architecture (Estimated: Feb 23, 2025 – May 31, 2025).**

  (a) **Activities:**
  - Conduct stakeholder interviews and workshops (at least 6 sessions) to document business objectives, data sources, and current analytics pain points.
  - Inventory existing data assets and analytics tools.
  - Define target-state architecture for data platform, including selection of preferred cloud provider, storage technologies, and integration patterns.
  - Identify privacy, security, and compliance requirements consistent with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Master Agreement.
  
  (b) **Deliverables:**
  - **D1.1 – Discovery Report.** A written report summarizing business objectives, data sources, constraints, and recommendations.
  - **D1.2 – Target Architecture Blueprint.** High-level and logical architecture diagrams, data flow diagrams, and technology stack recommendations.
  - **D1.3 – Implementation Roadmap.** A phased implementation plan with milestones, dependencies, and resource estimates.

  (c) **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Deliverables align materially with the documented business objectives and constraints.
  - Architecture and roadmap are internally consistent and feasible as reasonably determined by Client’s designated technical reviewer.
  - Any regulatory or data protection constraints identified in workshops are addressed explicitly.

2.3.2 **Phase 2 – Build & Integration (Estimated: June 1, 2025 – Feb 28, 2026).**

  (a) **Activities:**
  - Implement core data pipelines (batch and/or streaming) from at least three primary source systems.
  - Configure data lake or warehouse schemas and security model.
  - Develop base ML experimentation environment (code repositories, CI/CD pipelines, feature store).
  - Integrate authentication and access controls consistent with Client’s policies communicated under **Section 2.5(a)** of the Master Agreement.

  (b) **Deliverables:**
  - **D2.1 – Deployed Data Platform MVP.** Operational data platform supporting ingestion from at least three source systems.
  - **D2.2 – ML Experimentation Environment.** Configured repositories, pipelines, and documentation.
  - **D2.3 – Technical Runbooks.** Operations runbooks, deployment procedures, and troubleshooting guides.

  (c) **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Data platform ingests and processes sample datasets from designated systems within agreed performance thresholds.
  - ML environment supports at least two concurrent experimentation projects.
  - Runbooks are sufficiently detailed for Client’s technical staff to execute standard operations.

2.3.3 **Phase 3 – Pilot & Optimization (Estimated: Mar 1, 2026 – Dec 31, 2026).**

  (a) **Activities:**
  - Co-develop at least three ML models focused on Client’s priority use cases (e.g., churn prediction, demand forecasting).
  - Conduct A/B tests or controlled pilots, measure performance and business impact.
  - Tune pipelines, data transformations, and model hyperparameters based on pilot results.
  - Develop initial executive dashboards and operational reports.

  (b) **Deliverables:**
  - **D3.1 – Model Pilot Packages.** Documented models, configuration, and pilot results for at least three use cases.
  - **D3.2 – Pilot Evaluation Report.** Summary of performance metrics, business KPIs, and recommendations.
  - **D3.3 – Insights Dashboards (Pilot).** Interactive dashboards for pilot use cases.

  (c) **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Models and pilots are executed end-to-end on the platform implemented in Phase 2.
  - Pilot Evaluation Report includes clear metrics and interpretations.
  - Dashboards are accessible to designated business users and reflect agreed KPIs.

2.3.4 **Phase 4 – Scale & Managed Operations (Estimated: Jan 1, 2027 – March 16, 2029).**

  (a) **Activities:**
  - Operationalize and scale successful models into production workloads.
  - Provide ongoing monitoring, incident management, and performance tuning.
  - Extend dashboards and reports to additional business units.
  - Provide periodic training and knowledge transfer sessions.

  (b) **Deliverables (recurring and periodic):**
  - **D4.1 – Production MLOps Setup.** Deployed MLOps workflows for at least three models.
  - **D4.2 – Monthly Operations Reports.** Summaries of model performance, incidents, changes.
  - **D4.3 – Quarterly Business Review Decks.** Presentation materials assessing overall program performance.
  - **D4.4 – Training Materials.** Documented training content and recordings, as applicable.

  (c) **Acceptance Criteria:**
  - Production models operate within agreed performance thresholds and error budgets.
  - Operations Reports and QBR decks are delivered on time (within ten (10) Business Days after end of period) and include agreed metrics.
  - Training sessions are conducted at least twice per year.

2.4 **Platform Services and Service Levels.**  

2.4.1 Vendor shall provide Hosted Services and Platform Services as part of the Project. Such Hosted Services are subject to **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Master Agreement. The Parties expressly agree that:

  (b) Incident severity and response targets shall follow **Section 12.4** of the Master Agreement (Critical, High, Medium/Low incidents), with further details in **Article XII** of this Work Order.
