## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

**Contract Reference Number: ADD-TIT-202402-323**

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Lease Agreement**, Contract Reference Number **LSE-TIT-202402-209**, file identifier **contract_209**, dated **February 10, 2024** (the “**Parent Agreement**” or the “**Master Agreement**” or the “**Lease**”), pursuant to which Titan leases to Contoso, and Contoso leases from Titan, the Premises as defined in Article I, Section 1.1(m) of the Parent Agreement, and Titan provides certain facility-related Services as described in **Article II (Scope of Leased Premises and Services)** and **Exhibit A (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, under Section **2.1 (Lease of Premises)** of the Parent Agreement, Titan leased to Contoso approximately **120,000 rentable square feet** of industrial/office space located within Titan Logistics Center, 750 Titan Industrial Way, Building A, Newark, Delaware 19702, together with associated non-exclusive rights to use common areas;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement has a Term (as defined in Section **4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement) commencing on the Commencement Date (as defined in Section **1.1(e)**) and expiring on the **Expiration Date** of **July 31, 2026** (as defined in Section **1.1(h)**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with **Article IV (Term and Termination)** thereof;

WHEREAS, Contoso has requested, and Titan has agreed, subject to the terms and conditions of this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, to (a) add and reconfigure certain leased space within the Building, (b) extend the term of occupancy for such added and reconfigured space beyond the initial Expiration Date of the Parent Agreement, and (c) adjust certain economic, operational, and service-related terms in connection with such additional and modified leased space;

WHEREAS, this Addendum **adds or modifies leased space dated February 10, 2024 (Ref: LSE-TIT-202402-209)** and sets forth the Parties’ agreement regarding the additional premises, extended term as applicable, revised rent and fees, and related adjustments to the Services and obligations described in **Articles II, III, IV, and XII** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the total estimated contract value associated with this Addendum, inclusive of base rent and estimated additional rent and services fees applicable to the additional and modified premises and associated Services hereunder, is **Two Million Nine Hundred Fifteen Thousand Five Hundred Thirty-Eight U.S. Dollars (US$2,915,538)** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”);

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, agreements, and undertakings contained in this Addendum and in the Parent Agreement, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE III  
SCOPE OF LEASED PREMISES, SERVICES, AND WORK UNDER ADDENDUM

3.1 **Modified Premises; Expansion Premises Description.**

(a) **Existing Premises.** The Parties acknowledge and confirm that, as of the Addendum Effective Date, Contoso leases from Titan the Existing Premises consisting of approximately 120,000 rentable square feet as defined in Section **1.1(m)** of the Parent Agreement and described in **Section 2.1** thereof.

(b) **Expansion Premises.** Effective as of the Addendum Effective Date, Titan hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Titan, additional premises consisting of approximately **80,000 rentable square feet** of industrial, warehousing, logistics, and associated office space located within **Titan Logistics Center, 750 Titan Industrial Way, Building A (North Wing and Mezzanine Level)**, Newark, Delaware 19702. The Expansion Premises shall be contiguous to, or functionally integrated with, the Existing Premises to permit efficient operations and movement of goods and personnel.

(c) **Modified Premises.** From and after the Addendum Effective Date, the term “Premises” as used in the Parent Agreement and this Addendum shall mean the Modified Premises, comprised of the Existing Premises and the Expansion Premises, totaling approximately **200,000 rentable square feet**, except where the context clearly indicates otherwise (e.g., when referring to obligations that arose solely with respect to the Existing Premises prior to the Addendum Effective Date).

3.2 **Use of Modified Premises.**

(a) **Permitted Use.** Contoso shall use the Expansion Premises and Modified Premises solely for the purposes set forth in **Section 2.2(a) (Use of Premises)** of the Parent Agreement, i.e., the operation of a corporate operations, light industrial, warehousing, logistics, and associated administrative office facility, and for no other purpose without Titan’s prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed).

(b) **Operational Integration.** Contoso may integrate operations across the Existing Premises and Expansion Premises, including but not limited to shared logistics processes, inventory storage, cross-docking, and employee circulation, provided that such integration does not violate Applicable Law, applicable building codes, or Titan’s reasonable building rules and regulations promulgated pursuant to **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Addendum Services and Facilities.**

(a) **Building Structure and Systems.** Titan shall maintain, repair, and replace the structural components and base building systems serving the Expansion Premises in the same manner and to the same standards as set forth in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement for the Existing Premises. This includes ensuring sufficient HVAC, electrical capacity, and fire protection systems appropriate for the expanded logistics and light industrial operations contemplated herein.

(b) **Common Areas and Circulation Adjustments.** Titan shall reconfigure, as necessary and commercially reasonable, access routes, signage, and wayfinding within the Building to reflect Contoso’s expanded occupancy. Such reconfigurations shall be performed in a manner consistent with **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement, ensuring adequate parking, loading dock access, and safe circulation for Contoso employees, suppliers, and visitors.

(c) **Utilities for Expansion Premises.** Titan shall ensure that potable water, electricity, sewer, and other essential utilities are extended and made available to the Expansion Premises with capacities commensurate with the intended use, in accordance with **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement. Utility consumption within the Expansion Premises shall be separately metered or reasonably allocated based on square footage or actual consumption, and such costs shall constitute Addendum Additional Rent payable under this Addendum and **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement.

(d) **Security and Access Control.** Consistent with **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement, Titan shall extend base building security systems (including access control card systems and CCTV coverage in common areas) to the Expansion Premises. Titan shall provide Contoso with an additional allocation of access cards, keys, or credentials adequate for Contoso’s expanded workforce, with costs for excess or replacement cards treated as Addendum Additional Rent.

(e) **Janitorial and Common Area Services.** Titan shall provide janitorial services for common areas serving the Expansion Premises in the same manner as set forth in **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall remain responsible for janitorial services within the interior of the Expansion Premises, unless otherwise agreed in writing and treated as an additional Service subject to fees under **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 3.5** of this Addendum.

(f) **Maintenance Management and Reporting.** Titan shall configure its maintenance ticketing/helpdesk system (as described in **Section 2.3(f)** of the Parent Agreement) to receive, track, and report on maintenance and Service requests originating from the Expansion Premises. All SLA response and resolution times set forth in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and as adjusted in **Article XII** of this Addendum shall apply to the Modified Premises.

3.4 **Improvements and Build-Out for Expansion Premises.**

(a) **Landlord Work.** Titan shall perform, at its cost unless otherwise specified in this Addendum, initial build-out and Improvements to the Expansion Premises necessary to provide a base condition consistent with the Existing Premises, including but not limited to distribution of power, lighting, basic office areas, restrooms, and life-safety systems. Such work shall be undertaken in accordance with **Section 2.5(a) (Landlord Work)** of the Parent Agreement and any mutually agreed work letter specific to this Addendum.

(b) **Tenant Improvements.** If Contoso desires to make additional alterations or Improvements to the Expansion Premises beyond the base build-out, Contoso shall comply with **Section 2.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement, including submission of plans, Titan’s approval (not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed), and performing work in a good and workmanlike manner and in compliance with Applicable Law.

(c) **Ownership of Improvements.** Ownership of Improvements in the Expansion Premises shall be governed by **Section 2.5(c)** of the Parent Agreement. Titan may, in accordance with that Section, require removal of certain Improvements at the end of the Addendum Term or Extended Term, subject to the notice and restoration provisions described therein.

3.5 **Performance Standards.** All Addendum Services related to the Expansion Premises and Modified Premises shall be performed:

(a) In accordance with **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, including adherence to generally accepted standards for comparable industrial/office facilities in the region;  

(b) In compliance with Applicable Law, including any updated or additional health, safety, environmental, and accessibility requirements arising as a result of the Modified Premises; and  

(c) In accordance with the Service Levels applicable under **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented and clarified in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS UNDER ADDENDUM

4.1 **Addendum Contract Value.** The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **Addendum Contract Value** for the Expansion Premises and Modified Premises is **US$2,915,538** for the Addendum Term and Extended Term, which is separate from and in addition to the **Contract Value of US$3,039,526** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. The Addendum Contract Value includes Addendum Base Rent and estimated Addendum Additional Rent and Services fees as described in this Article IV.

4.2 **Addendum Base Rent.**

(a) **Base Rent Structure.** For the Expansion Premises and the Extended Term for the Modified Premises, Contoso shall pay monthly **Addendum Base Rent** in accordance with the following illustrative schedule (which may be more fully detailed in a rent schedule attached as **Exhibit A** to this Addendum):

- **Addendum Period 1** (July 18, 2024 – July 31, 2025): US$120,000 per month  
- **Addendum Period 2** (August 1, 2025 – July 31, 2026): US$126,000 per month  
- **Extended Term Year 1** (August 1, 2026 – July 31, 2027): US$132,300 per month  
- **Extended Term Year 2 (Partial)** (August 1, 2027 – November 9, 2028): Pro-rated at US$132,300 per month  

The Parties acknowledge that these amounts, combined with estimated Addendum Additional Rent, are consistent with the Addendum Contract Value. To the extent of any discrepancy between the narrative above and an attached **Exhibit A (Addendum Rent Schedule)**, the attached exhibit shall control, except that the total estimated Addendum Contract Value stated in **Section 1.2(i)** shall remain the Parties’ agreed estimate for purposes of risk allocation under **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

(b) **Payment of Base Rent.** Addendum Base Rent shall be due and payable in advance on or before the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Addendum Term and Extended Term, without setoff, deduction, or counterclaim, except as expressly permitted under **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement or as otherwise expressly stated in this Addendum.

4.3 **Addendum Additional Rent and Operating Expenses.**

(a) **Proportionate Share.** In addition to Addendum Base Rent, Contoso shall pay its proportionate share of Operating Expenses and Taxes allocable to the Modified Premises as Addendum Additional Rent in accordance with **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso’s proportionate share shall be calculated on the basis of approximately 200,000 rentable square feet occupied by Contoso relative to the total rentable area of the Building, or such other reasonable allocation method as Titan may adopt, consistently applied to similarly situated tenants.

(b) **Estimates and Reconciliation.** Titan shall provide Contoso, for each calendar year falling within the Addendum Term and Extended Term, with a good faith estimate of Operating Expenses and Taxes associated with the Modified Premises. Monthly installments shall be payable on a **Net 45** basis as provided in **Section 4.4** below, subject to annual reconciliation within 180 days following the end of each calendar year, in the manner set forth in **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Payment Terms – Net 45 (Addendum).**

(a) **General Payment Terms.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.4(a) (Payment Terms – Net 30)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that, for all invoiced amounts under this Addendum other than Addendum Base Rent (which remains due in advance), Contoso shall pay undisputed amounts on a **Net 45** basis, i.e., within forty-five (45) days after the date of Titan’s invoice.

(b) **Invoice Disputes.** If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice issued under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion by the due date and provide written notice of the dispute within fifteen (15) days after receipt, in accordance with procedures substantially similar to those set forth in **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Late Payments and Interest.** Any payment due under this Addendum that is not received by Titan within five (5) Business Days after the applicable due date (and not subject to a bona fide dispute) shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement (i.e., the lesser of one percent (1%) per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law), from the due date until paid in full. Titan’s rights under **Section 3.5**, including the right to suspend non-essential Services after applicable notice and cure periods, shall apply equally to amounts due under this Addendum.

4.6 **Taxes, Expenses, and Reimbursement.** 

(a) Taxes applicable to rent and fees under this Addendum shall be payable in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) To the extent Titan incurs costs at Contoso’s specific written request in relation to the Expansion Premises (for example, additional security coverage, specialized cleaning, enhanced loading dock operations, or after-hours HVAC), such costs shall be reimbursed by Contoso as Addendum Additional Rent, in accordance with **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement, subject to prior written approval by Contoso where reasonably practicable.

4.7 **Security Deposit.** If Titan determines in good faith that an increase in the Security Deposit (as defined in **Section 1.1(s)** and described in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement) is warranted as a result of the Expanded Premises and Extended Term, the Parties shall confer in good faith to adjust the Security Deposit amount; any such adjustment shall be documented by a written amendment or change order referencing this Addendum.

## ARTICLE V  
TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 **Addendum Term; Extended Term.**

(a) The **Addendum Term** shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall, with respect to the Expansion Premises and modified economic terms, run concurrently with the remaining Term of the Parent Agreement through **July 31, 2026**.

(b) The **Extended Term** applicable solely to the Modified Premises shall commence on **August 1, 2026** and shall expire on **November 9, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement or this Addendum.

5.2 **Renewal Options.** The renewal option provided in **Section 4.2 (Renewal Options)** of the Parent Agreement shall be deemed to apply to the Extended Term, such that, provided Contoso is not in material default beyond any applicable cure period at the time of exercise, Contoso may request a further renewal of its lease of the Modified Premises. Any such renewal shall be on mutually agreed terms, including Base Rent and key economic terms, taking into account both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

(a) **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Titan, provided that such termination date shall not be earlier than **July 31, 2025**, unless otherwise mutually agreed by the Parties in writing.

(b) **By Titan.** Titan may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, provided that such termination shall be exercised in good faith, shall not be arbitrary or capricious, and Titan shall cooperate with Contoso to mitigate relocation and operational impacts, similar to Titan’s obligations in **Section 4.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

(c) **Economic Consequences.** In the event of termination for convenience under this Addendum, Contoso shall remain liable for all amounts accrued and unpaid through the effective date of termination and any agreed early termination fees or unamortized landlord contributions specifically associated with Improvements in the Expansion Premises, if such amounts are specified in a separate work letter or addendum.

5.4 **Termination for Cause.** The termination for cause provisions of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement are incorporated herein by reference and shall apply to this Addendum, such that:

(a) Titan may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice if Contoso fails to pay sums due hereunder within the cure periods specified in **Section 4.4(a)(i)** of the Parent Agreement, materially breaches non-monetary obligations related to the Modified Premises, or becomes insolvent as described in **Section 4.4(a)(iii)**; and

(b) Contoso may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice if Titan materially fails to perform Addendum Services for the Modified Premises in accordance with this Addendum and the Parent Agreement (including **Article II** and **Article XII**), such failure materially impairs Contoso’s ability to conduct its business from the Modified Premises, and Titan fails to cure within the applicable cure period set forth in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

5.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

(a) **Automatic Termination.** Except as expressly provided in this Addendum, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement will automatically result in the concurrent termination of this Addendum, and Contoso’s rights to occupy the Modified Premises shall cease in accordance with **Section 4.5 (Effect of Termination or Expiration)** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) **Survival Provisions.** Those provisions of this Addendum that, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration—including without limitation payment obligations for amounts accrued and unpaid, confidentiality, IP rights, indemnification, limitation of liability, data protection, dispute resolution, and general provisions—shall survive and remain in effect, consistent with **Section 4.5(c)** and **Section 16.13** of the Parent Agreement.

5.6 **Surrender of Modified Premises.** Upon expiration or earlier termination of this Addendum and/or the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall surrender the Modified Premises in accordance with **Section 4.5(a)–(b)** and **Section 2.5(c)** of the Parent Agreement, including removal of trade fixtures and restoration obligations.
