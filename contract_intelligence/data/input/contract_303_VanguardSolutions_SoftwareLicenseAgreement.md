## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS

12.1 **Uptime Commitment.**  
Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the SaaS components of the Software are available at least **99.9%** of the time in each calendar month, excluding **Permitted Downtime**. **“Permitted Downtime”** includes: (a) scheduled maintenance (with at least 48 hours’ prior notice, performed during non-peak hours where feasible); (b) emergency maintenance required to address critical security or performance issues; and (c) unavailability due to Client’s systems, third-party network failures outside Vendor’s reasonable control, or Force Majeure Events.

12.2 **Support Hours and Response Times.**  
Vendor shall provide Support Services during normal business hours (9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Eastern Time, Monday through Friday, excluding Vendor-recognized holidays), and shall respond to support requests according to the severity classification below:

- **Severity 1 (Critical):** Complete unavailability or major outage affecting all users. Target initial response within 1 hour; continuous efforts until resolution or workaround.  
- **Severity 2 (High):** Significant loss of functionality affecting many users but with a workaround. Target response within 4 hours; target workaround or resolution within 24 hours.  
- **Severity 3 (Medium):** Limited impact or non-critical functionality issues. Target response within 1 Business Day; resolution as part of normal update cycle.  
- **Severity 4 (Low):** Cosmetic issues, minor queries, or enhancement requests. Response within 2 Business Days; resolution or inclusion in roadmap at Vendor’s discretion.

12.3 **SLA Credits.**  
If monthly uptime falls below the committed 99.9% (excluding Permitted Downtime), Client shall be entitled, upon written request, to service credits as follows:

- 99.0% – 99.9%: Credit equal to 5% of the monthly subscription Fees;  
- 98.0% – 98.99%: Credit equal to 10% of the monthly subscription Fees;  
- Below 98.0%: Credit equal to 20% of the monthly subscription Fees.

SLA credits shall be applied against future invoices and shall not be redeemable for cash. SLA credits constitute Client’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for failures to meet the uptime commitment, without prejudice to Client’s rights under **Article IV** in the event of persistent or material failures.

12.4 **Exclusions.**
