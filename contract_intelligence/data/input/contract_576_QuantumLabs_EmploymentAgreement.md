  3.2.1 **Time and Materials (“T&M”)**: An hourly or daily rate per category of Personnel (e.g., Senior Engineer, Data Scientist, Architect, Project Manager), as set forth in the applicable SOW;

  3.2.2 **Fixed Price**: A fixed total fee for defined Deliverables or project phases, as specified in the applicable SOW;

  3.2.3 **Retainer / Managed Services**: A recurring monthly or quarterly fee for a defined pool of resources or support Services, as specified in the applicable SOW.

  3.3.1 Invoices shall be issued monthly in arrears for T&M Services, and in accordance with milestone completion or scheduled payments for fixed price or retainer arrangements;

  3.3.3 Payments shall be made in United States Dollars (USD) by bank transfer to the account designated by Quantum Labs in the invoice or in writing.

Any undisputed invoice amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of **one percent (1%) per month** or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, whichever is lower, from the due date until payment is received in full. Without prejudice to any other rights, if any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than sixty (60) days after the due date, Quantum Labs may, upon ten (10) Business Days’ written notice, suspend the Services until all outstanding amounts and applicable interest are paid in full.

  3.5.2 Pay all undisputed portions of the invoice in accordance with Section 3.3.

All fees are exclusive of all applicable taxes, duties, levies, and similar governmental charges (collectively, “Taxes”). Contoso shall be responsible for all Taxes arising from or related to the payments under this Agreement, excluding taxes based on Quantum Labs’ income. Reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Quantum Labs in the performance of the Services, including travel, accommodation, per diem, and third-party fees, shall be reimbursed by Contoso, provided that:

  3.7.1 Such expenses are pre-approved in writing by Contoso (email sufficing); and

  3.7.2 Quantum Labs provides reasonable documentation (e.g., receipts) in support of such expenses.

Reimbursable expenses shall be invoiced monthly and shall be payable in accordance with the Payment Terms.

3.8 **No Set-Off.**  
Contoso shall not be entitled to set off, withhold, or deduct any amounts from invoices due to Quantum Labs under this Agreement, except where required by law or expressly agreed in writing.

Either Party may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part, upon written notice to the other Party if:

  4.4.1 The other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement or any SOW, and such breach, if capable of remedy, is not cured within sixty (60) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach; or

  4.4.2 The other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, has a receiver appointed over its assets, or enters into any composition or arrangement with its creditors.

Without limiting Section 3.4, Quantum Labs may terminate this Agreement or any SOW upon thirty (30) days’ written notice if any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than ninety (90) days after the due date, and such non-payment is not cured within such thirty (30) day notice period.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement or any SOW for any reason:

  4.6.1 Contoso shall pay Quantum Labs for all Services performed and Deliverables provided up to the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Quantum Labs on Contoso’s behalf;

  4.6.2 Quantum Labs shall deliver to Contoso, within a reasonable period, any Deliverables completed and accepted as of the effective date of termination, subject to Contoso’s payment obligations;

  4.6.3 Each Party shall, upon the other Party’s written request, return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with **Article V**; and

  4.6.4 Any licenses granted under **Article VI** shall continue or terminate in accordance with their terms.

4.7 **Survival.**  
Any provision of this Agreement which by its nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including but not limited to Articles III (to the extent of unpaid fees), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI, and and any related indemnity and limitation provisions.

## FORCE MAJEURE

15.1 **Definition.**  
A “Force Majeure Event” means any event or circumstance beyond the reasonable control of the affected Party, including but not limited to acts of God, natural disasters, epidemics or pandemics, war, terrorism, civil unrest, strikes or labor disputes (excluding those involving the affected Party’s own workforce), governmental actions, embargoes, failure of utilities or telecommunications, or widespread cyberattacks or infrastructure failures.

15.2 **Notice.**  
The Party affected by a Force Majeure Event shall:

  15.2.1 Promptly notify the other Party in writing of the occurrence, nature, and expected duration of the Force Majeure Event; and

  15.2.2 Use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects of the Force Majeure Event and resume performance as soon as reasonably practicable.

15.3 **Suspension of Obligations.**  
The obligations of the affected Party shall be suspended (but not excused) during the period of the Force Majeure Event to the extent they are directly impacted by the event. The time for performance shall be extended for a period equal to the duration of the Force Majeure Event.

15.4 **Termination for Extended Force Majeure.**  
If a Force Majeure Event continues for a period of more than one hundred twenty (120) consecutive days and materially affects the performance of this Agreement, either Party may terminate the affected SOW or this Agreement upon thirty (30) days’ prior written notice.
