## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF LEASE AND SERVICES  

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment **modifies and supplements** **Article II (Scope of Lease and Services)** of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to Sections 2.1 (Grant of Lease), 2.3 (Services Provided), 2.6 (Timelines and Milestones), and 2.7 (Additional Services). Except as expressly modified herein, all provisions of Article II of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect.

2.1.2 The Parties expressly acknowledge that this Amendment is **executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement (Ref: LSE-STE-202509-680 / contract_680)**, which shall continue to govern the Lease and all Services, as amended.

2.2 **Expansion of Premises.**  

2.2.1 With effect from the Amendment Effective Date, Section 1.1.13 (**“Premises”**) of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended and restated** as follows (additions shown conceptually in italics for reference only and not as redline):

> “**Premises** means the commercial premises and related facilities being leased to Client under this Agreement, consisting initially of approximately 1,200 square metres of office and support space located on Levels 7 and 8 of Vendor’s building at 200 Canary Wharf, London E14 5AB, United Kingdom, together with an additional approximately 800 square metres of office and support space located on Level 9 of the same building (collectively, the ‘Revised Premises’), together with the use of specified common areas, and any additional or substitute premises as may be agreed in writing.”

2.2.2 The Additional Premises on Level 9 shall be made available to Client in a condition substantially consistent with the fit‑out standards described in Schedule 3 of the Parent Agreement, including base lighting, raised flooring, and standard office finishes, subject to any additional bespoke fit‑out ordered by Client under a Change Order.

2.2.3 The lease of the Additional Premises shall be co‑terminous with the Extended Term described in Article IV of this Amendment, unless otherwise agreed in a written Change Order.

2.3 **Permitted Use; No Change to Fundamental Use.**  

2.3.1 The Permitted Use of the Revised Premises shall remain as described in **Section 2.2 (Permitted Use)** of the Parent Agreement (general commercial office use, corporate administration, technology operations, and associated activities).  

2.3.2 Nothing in this Amendment shall be construed as authorising Client to use the Premises for any purpose prohibited under Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement (including residential or industrial purposes).

2.4 **Enhanced Services and Capacity.**  

2.4.1 In addition to the Core Services set forth in **Section 2.3 (Services Provided)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide the following **Amended Services** to support Client’s expanded occupation of the Revised Premises:

  (a) **Increased Power and Cooling Capacity.** Vendor shall increase electrical power provisioning and associated cooling capacity allocated to Client’s Revised Premises by not less than thirty percent (30%) above the baseline specified in Schedule 1 to the Parent Agreement. Vendor shall maintain redundancy levels consistent with the Service Levels in Article XII of the Parent Agreement as enhanced by Article XII of this Amendment.  

  (b) **Extended IT and Network Services.** Vendor shall provision additional rack space, structured cabling, and internet bandwidth to support the Additional Premises. The minimum committed bandwidth shall be increased by at least 50% over the initial commitment described in Schedule 2 of the Parent Agreement, with the exact levels and pricing set out in Section 3.3 of this Amendment.  

  (c) **Enhanced Security Services.** Vendor shall extend 24x7 secure access (Section 2.3.1(a) of the Parent Agreement) to the Additional Premises and shall expand CCTV coverage and physical access controls to include Level 9, consistent with “good industry practice” as referenced in Section 2.3.1(b) of the Parent Agreement.  

  (d) **Extended Facilities Management.** Vendor shall provide cleaning, maintenance, and waste disposal services for the Additional Premises on the same basis as for Levels 7 and 8, as set out in Section 2.3.2 of the Parent Agreement, ensuring equivalent service quality, frequency, and Work Standards (Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement).

2.4.2 Vendor acknowledges that Client’s operations on Level 9 will include additional data processing and light technical installations comparable to those on Levels 7 and 8. Vendor shall ensure that building services (including HVAC, fire detection, and access control) are dimensioned and maintained to support these operations in accordance with Sections 2.3.3 and 2.3.5 of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards and SLA Enhancements.**  

2.5.1 Vendor’s obligations under Section 2.5 (Performance Standards) of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to all Amended Services and to the Revised Premises.  

2.5.2 In addition, Vendor shall comply with the SLA Enhancements set out in Article XII of this Amendment, which shall, where applicable, **supersede** the corresponding provisions in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements (SLAs))** of the Parent Agreement to the extent of any conflict, in accordance with Section 13.4 (Priority) of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Timelines and Milestones for Additional Premises.**  

2.6.1 Vendor shall make the Additional Premises ready for beneficial occupation by Client (including completion of base fit‑out to the agreed specification) no later than **90 days** after the Amendment Effective Date (the “**Level 9 Commencement Date**”).  

2.6.2 The acceptance procedure in **Section 2.6.3 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Additional Premises and all Amended Services. If Client does not notify Vendor of material deficiencies within ten (10) Business Days following Vendor’s notice of readiness, the Additional Premises and associated Services shall be deemed accepted as of the Level 9 Commencement Date.

2.7 **Additional Services; Optional Items.**  

2.7.1 Client may request additional services specific to the Additional Premises, including but not limited to dedicated security personnel, extended cleaning hours, or specialised IT services. Such services shall be ordered and governed in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and Article XIII of this Amendment.  

2.7.2 Any such optional services shall be charged in accordance with the fee and expense structure in Article III of the Parent Agreement as modified by Article III of this Amendment and the applicable Change Order.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS  

3.1 **Amended Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that, as of the Amendment Effective Date, the estimated total contract value for the Extended Term, exclusive of Taxes but including the additional lease, service, and IT fees described herein, is **US$3,670,633** (the “**Amended Contract Value**”).  

3.1.2 This Amended Contract Value supersedes the **Contract Value of US$2,400,651** referenced in Sections 1.1.6 and 3.1 of the Parent Agreement, provided that any fees, charges, or credits accrued prior to the Amendment Effective Date shall remain governed by the original Contract Value assumptions and shall not be retroactively recalculated.

3.2 **Revised Base Rent.**  

3.2.1 Section 3.2 (Base Rent) of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended** to reflect the lease of the Revised Premises as follows:

  (a) For the period from the Amendment Effective Date until the Level 9 Commencement Date, the annual **Base Rent** shall remain **US$520,000** for Levels 7 and 8.  

  (b) From the Level 9 Commencement Date through **26 October 2030**, the annual **Revised Base Rent** for the Revised Premises shall be **US$780,000**, payable in equal quarterly instalments in advance.  

3.2.2 The annual review mechanism described in Section 3.2 of the Parent Agreement (allowing Vendor to increase Base Rent by the lesser of 4% per annum or the CPI increase) shall continue to apply, but any such increase shall be applied to the Revised Base Rent. Vendor shall continue to provide reasonable written notice of any Base Rent adjustments, consistent with the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Service Charges and IT/Network Fees.**  

3.3.1 The annual **Service Charge** estimate of **US$120,000** referenced in Section 3.3.1 of the Parent Agreement is increased to **US$180,000** per annum to reflect Client’s proportionally increased share of building operating expenses associated with the Additional Premises. The reconciliation mechanism in Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply.

3.3.2 Fees for IT and network services under Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement and Schedule 2 are amended to include:

  (a) An additional monthly recurring fee of **US$18,000** for increased bandwidth and additional rack space supporting Level 9; and  

  (b) Any incremental optional managed services ordered by Client for Level 9, as described in the relevant Change Orders.  

3.3.3 These additional IT and network fees shall be billed monthly in arrears, consistent with Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Payment Terms; Net 60.**  

3.4.1 Section 3.6 (Payment Terms; Invoicing) of the Parent Agreement is affirmed and shall continue in full force. All amounts due under this Amendment shall be invoiced and payable in accordance with **Net 60** terms (payment within sixty (60) days from the date of the invoice), as set out in Section 3.6.1 of the Parent Agreement.

3.4.2 Client’s obligation not to set off or withhold amounts except as expressly permitted in Section 3.6.3 of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to all amounts arising under this Amendment.

3.5 **One‑Time Charges and Fit‑Out Contributions.**  

3.5.1 In addition to the Initial Fit‑Out Contribution of **US$250,000** under Section 3.5.1 of the Parent Agreement, Client shall pay a one‑time **Additional Fit‑Out Contribution** of **US$150,000** toward Vendor’s costs of fitting‑out the Additional Premises to the agreed specification.  

3.5.2 The Additional Fit‑Out Contribution shall be payable (i) fifty percent (50%) within thirty (30) days after the Amendment Effective Date, and (ii) fifty percent (50%) within thirty (30) days after the Level 9 Commencement Date.

3.5.3 All other one‑time charges, installation fees, and reimbursable expenses shall remain governed by Sections 3.5 and 3.10 of the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Late Payment; Disputed Invoices.**  

3.6.1 The late payment interest provisions in Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all undisputed amounts due under this Amendment.  

3.6.2 The disputed invoice procedure in Section 3.9 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any invoice or portion thereof that relates to Amended Services or Revised Base Rent.

3.7 **Taxes.**  

3.7.1 The Tax treatment set out in Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all amounts payable under this Amendment.  

3.7.2 For avoidance of doubt, the Amended Contract Value and all new or revised fee components described herein are **exclusive of Taxes**, and Client shall be responsible for any applicable Taxes consistent with Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION  

4.1 **Extended Term; Superseding Certain Provisions of Article IV.**  

4.1.1 Section 1.1.10 (“Initial Term”) and Section 4.1 (Term) of the Parent Agreement are hereby supplemented and, to the extent of inconsistency, superseded as follows:  

  (a) The Parties agree that, notwithstanding the original Initial Term ending on **9 January 2029**, the overall contractual arrangement (Lease plus Services) shall continue until **26 October 2030**, which is hereby designated as the **Expiration Date** of the combined Parent Agreement and this Amendment.  

  (b) For avoidance of doubt, the period from 10 January 2029 through 26 October 2030 shall constitute a **Renewal Term** under the Parent Agreement as modified by this Amendment, without the need for further written notice of renewal.

4.1.2 All rights and obligations under the Parent Agreement shall continue during the Extended Term, as expressly modified by this Amendment.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.2.1 Section 4.2 (Termination for Convenience by Vendor) and Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience by Client) of the Parent Agreement remain in effect, but are **modified** as follows to reflect the Parties’ agreed low‑risk, client‑friendly allocation:

  (a) **Vendor’s Termination for Convenience.** Vendor may terminate the Parent Agreement (as amended) for convenience only upon at least **two hundred seventy (270) days’** prior written notice to Client (replacing the 180‑day period in Section 4.2), unless otherwise agreed in writing by Client.  

  (b) **Client’s Termination for Convenience.** Client may terminate the Parent Agreement (as amended) for convenience upon at least **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Vendor (replacing the 180‑day period in Section 4.3), subject to payment of the early termination fee described below.

4.2.2 The early termination fee in Section 4.3.2 of the Parent Agreement is hereby reduced to reflect the low‑risk profile:

> “Client shall pay an early termination fee equal to **three (3) months** of the average monthly Total Fees (Base Rent plus Service Charges plus average IT and network fees) payable in the six (6) months immediately prior to the termination notice.”

4.3 **Automatic Renewal after Extended Term.**  

4.3.1 Following the Expiration Date of **26 October 2030**, the Agreement (as amended) shall, unless either Party provides at least **six (6) months’** prior written notice of non‑renewal, automatically renew for successive **twelve (12) month** Renewal Terms on the same terms and conditions, subject to the then‑current Revised Base Rent and Service Charges (as adjusted pursuant to Section 3.2.2 and 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement, as amended).

4.3.2 The twelve (12) month prior notice period for non‑renewal in Section 4.1 of the Parent Agreement is hereby replaced with a six (6) month prior notice period for any Renewal Term commencing after 26 October 2030.

4.4 **Termination for Cause; Force Majeure.**  

4.4.1 Sections 4.4 and 4.5 (Termination for Cause and Termination for Extended Force Majeure) of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply without change and shall govern the Parent Agreement as amended hereby.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement on this Amendment.**  

4.5.1 This Amendment forms an integral part of the Parent Agreement. Termination or expiry of the Parent Agreement in accordance with Article IV thereof shall automatically result in termination or expiry of this Amendment, without need for separate notice, subject to any Transition Period agreed under Section 4.7 below.

4.5.2 Termination of this Amendment independently of the Parent Agreement shall not be permitted except (a) as expressly provided in a subsequent written amendment or Change Order, or (b) as required by applicable law.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiry; Survival.**  

4.6.1 Upon termination or expiry of the Parent Agreement (as amended):  

  (a) Client shall vacate the Revised Premises and return possession in accordance with Section 4.6.1 of the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) Client shall pay all outstanding amounts, including any early termination fee as modified by this Amendment, in accordance with Section 4.6.2 of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (c) Vendor shall cease provision of Services, subject to any Transition Period support agreed under Section 4.7.

4.6.2 The survival provisions in Section 4.6.4 of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment, such that Articles V–XI, XIV, and XVI of the Parent Agreement and Articles V–XI, XIV, and XVI of this Amendment shall survive termination or expiry.

4.7 **Transition Period.**  

4.7.1 At Client’s written request made at least sixty (60) days prior to termination or expiry (other than for Client’s uncured material breach), the Parties shall negotiate in good faith a limited Transition Period of up to **six (6) months** following the effective date of termination or expiry, during which Vendor shall provide transitional facilities and IT services to enable Client to migrate from the Revised Premises.

4.7.2 Any Transition Period services shall be subject to mutually agreed terms and fees, documented in a Change Order, and shall be without prejudice to the Parties’ rights and obligations under Article IV of the Parent Agreement and Article IV of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IX  
## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY  

9.1 **Modification of Article IX of Parent Agreement.**  

9.1.1 Notwithstanding **Sections 9.2 and 9.3 of the Parent Agreement**, which provided for **no contractual cap** on liability and inclusion of consequential damages, the Parties, in light of the **Low Risk Profile** of this Amendment and the overall arrangement, hereby **replace** Article IX of the Parent Agreement in its entirety with the following balanced limitation of liability framework.  

9.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, this Article IX shall apply to all claims arising under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment from and after the Amendment Effective Date, but shall not retroactively limit liability for claims finally resolved prior to the Amendment Effective Date.

9.2 **Aggregate Liability Cap.**  

9.2.1 Subject to Sections 9.3 and 9.4 below, the total aggregate liability of each Party to the other Party (including liability for the acts and omissions of its employees, agents, and subcontractors) arising out of or in connection with the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), breach of statutory duty, or otherwise, shall be limited in any twelve (12) month period to an amount equal to **two (2) times** the total fees (including Base Rent, Service Charges, IT/network fees, and other recurring fees) **actually paid by Client to Vendor under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**.

9.3 **Exclusion of Certain Damages.**  

9.3.1 Subject to Section 9.4 below and except as expressly provided otherwise, **neither Party** shall be liable to the other for any **loss of profits, loss of revenue (other than direct fee refunds), loss of data (to the extent recoverable from available backups), loss of goodwill, or pure economic loss**, nor for any **indirect, incidental, special, or consequential damages**, in each case arising out of or in connection with the Parent Agreement or this Amendment, even if such Party has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

9.4 **Exceptions to Limitations.**  

9.4.1 The limitations and exclusions in Sections 9.2 and 9.3 shall **not** apply to:  

  (a) A Party’s liability for death or personal injury caused by its negligence;  

  (b) A Party’s liability for fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation;  

  (c) Client’s obligation to pay undisputed fees due under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment;  

  (d) A Party’s liability for wilful misconduct or intentional breach of Article V (Confidentiality) or Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy) of the Parent Agreement as applicable to this Amendment; or
