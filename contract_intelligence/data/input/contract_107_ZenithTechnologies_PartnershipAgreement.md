## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **General Scope.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Zenith shall provide to Contoso the Services and Deliverables described in this **Article II** and as further set forth in one or more SOWs or Order Forms executed by the Parties. The Parties acknowledge that the Services are strategic and mission-critical to Contoso’s operations and growth initiatives.

2.2 **Categories of Services.** The Services may include, without limitation:

  2.2.1 **Technology Strategy & Consulting.** Zenith shall provide advisory and consulting services to design and refine Contoso’s technology roadmap, covering areas such as cloud migration, microservices architecture, data platform modernization, cybersecurity posture, and DevOps practices.

  2.2.2 **Systems Integration & Implementation.** Zenith shall design, implement, configure, and integrate enterprise systems, including but not limited to e-commerce platforms, CRM and ERP systems, data warehouses, and analytics solutions. This may include custom development, integration with legacy systems, API development, middleware configuration, and system testing.

  2.2.3 **Managed Services & Support.** Zenith shall provide ongoing managed services to monitor, maintain, and support the production environment(s) as mutually agreed in each SOW. Such services may include 24x7 monitoring, incident management, problem resolution, patching, performance tuning, and capacity planning.

  2.2.4 **Cloud Hosting & Platform Services.** Where applicable, Zenith shall arrange for and/or directly provide hosting of certain components of Contoso’s applications and data on cloud infrastructure, including management of environments, provisioning, backup, and disaster recovery in accordance with the SLA in **Article XII**.

  2.2.5 **Data & Analytics Services.** Zenith shall assist Contoso with data ingestion, transformation, modeling, and analytics implementations, including dashboards, reporting, and AI/ML-based insights, subject to data privacy and security obligations in **Article XI**.

  2.2.6 **Training & Knowledge Transfer.** Zenith shall provide training to Contoso’s designated personnel on the use, configuration, and administration of the implemented solutions, as reasonably requested and described in each SOW.

2.3 **Deliverables.** Each SOW shall include a detailed list of Deliverables, which may include, without limitation:

  (a) solution design documentation;  
  (b) architecture diagrams and data models;  
  (c) configured or customized software components;  
  (d) integration interfaces, APIs, and connectors;  
  (e) test plans, test cases, and test reports;  
  (f) operational runbooks, procedures, and support documentation; and  
  (g) training materials and user manuals.

Each Deliverable shall, where applicable, be associated with explicit acceptance criteria (see **Section 2.6**).

2.4 **Project Governance and Responsibilities.**

  2.4.1 **Zenith Responsibilities.** Zenith shall:  
  (i) allocate suitably qualified personnel with relevant expertise;  
  (ii) perform the Services with reasonable skill, care, and diligence consistent with industry standards;  
  (iii) designate a project manager with authority to make day-to-day decisions relating to the Services; and  
  (iv) comply with Contoso’s reasonable security, facility, and conduct policies provided in advance in writing.

  2.4.2 **Contoso Responsibilities.** Contoso shall:  
  (i) provide Zenith with timely access to necessary facilities, systems, data, and personnel;  
  (ii) designate a project sponsor and project manager with authority to provide approvals and decisions;  
  (iii) ensure that all Client Data and other inputs provided to Zenith are accurate, complete, and provided in a timely manner; and  
  (iv) perform any tasks and responsibilities assigned to Contoso in the applicable SOW.

Contoso’s failure to meet its responsibilities may result in delays and additional Fees, as described in **Section 13.4**.

2.5 **Timeline and Milestones.**  

  2.5.1 Each SOW shall set forth a project schedule including start dates, key milestones, and target completion dates. Timelines shall be estimates only unless the SOW expressly designates a milestone as a binding deadline.  

  2.5.2 Zenith shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the timelines set forth in the SOW, subject to dependencies on Contoso’s performance of its obligations and any Force Majeure Events (see **Article XV**). Any adjustments to timelines due to scope changes or delays caused by Contoso shall be handled under **Article XIII** (Change Management).

2.6 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**

  2.6.1 **Review Period.** For each Deliverable identified as subject to acceptance testing in the applicable SOW, Contoso shall have a review period specified in the SOW (or, if not specified, fifteen (15) Business Days) from Zenith’s delivery of the Deliverable (the **“Review Period”**) to evaluate and either accept or reject such Deliverable.

  2.6.2 **Acceptance or Rejection.** Contoso shall notify Zenith in writing prior to the end of the Review Period of its acceptance or rejection of the Deliverable. A rejection must include a reasonably detailed description of material non-conformities with the applicable acceptance criteria. If Contoso fails to provide such notice within the Review Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

  2.6.3 **Correction of Non-Conformities.** If a Deliverable is properly rejected, Zenith shall, at no additional cost to Contoso (unless the non-conformity is caused by Contoso or a third party under Contoso’s control), use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the non-conformity and deliver a revised version within a mutually agreed timeframe. The corrected Deliverable shall then be subject to a new Review Period.

  2.6.4 **Final Acceptance.** A Deliverable shall be deemed finally accepted upon the earlier of (a) Contoso’s written notice of acceptance, or (b) expiration of the Review Period without a written notice of rejection.

2.7 **Performance Standards.**

  2.7.1 Zenith shall perform the Services (a) in accordance with the standards of care and diligence generally exercised by reputable providers of similar services, (b) in compliance with applicable laws and regulations as described in **Section 7.2**, and (c) in a manner consistent with all applicable SLAs in **Article XII**.

  2.7.2 Contoso acknowledges that the success of the Services depends materially upon Contoso’s timely decisions, approvals, and provision of information. Zenith shall not be liable for performance failures to the extent attributable to Contoso’s delays or failures to fulfill its obligations hereunder.

2.8 **Subcontracting.** Zenith may use Subcontractors to perform the Services, provided that (a) Zenith remains responsible for the performance of each Subcontractor and for compliance with the terms of this Agreement; and (b) any Subcontractor with access to Confidential Information or Personal Data shall be bound by written obligations of confidentiality and data protection at least as protective as those contained in this Agreement.

3.1 **Contract Value.** The Parties acknowledge that the total estimated contract value for all Services and Deliverables anticipated under this Agreement is **Three Million Six Hundred Thirty-Two Thousand One Hundred Thirty U.S. Dollars (US $3,632,130)** (the **“Contract Value”**).   3.2.2 **Fixed-Price.** For certain defined Deliverables or project phases, the Parties may agree to a fixed fee, payable upon completion of milestones or acceptance as specified in the SOW.

  3.2.3 **Recurring Fees.** For managed services, hosting, or ongoing support, Contoso shall pay recurring monthly or annual service fees as set forth in the applicable SOW or Order Form.

  - Implementation Phase I (mixed T&M and milestone-based): US $1,200,000  
  - Implementation Phase II: US $1,000,000  
  - Managed Services (24 months at US $50,000/month): US $1,200,000  
  - Contingency / Change Requests (estimated): US $52,130

  3.4.1 **Invoices.** Zenith shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the billing schedule specified in each SOW or Order Form. Zenith may, upon written notice and without liability, suspend performance of the Services or withhold Deliverables if undisputed amounts remain outstanding for more than thirty (30) days after the due date, until such amounts are paid.

3.7 **Disputed Amounts.** If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Contoso shall notify Zenith in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the reasons for the dispute. Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion of the invoice by the due date, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve the dispute promptly. Any amounts later agreed or determined to be payable shall be paid within fifteen (15) days after resolution.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  

  3.8.1 **Reimbursable Expenses.** Contoso shall reimburse Zenith for reasonable, documented out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including travel, lodging, meals, and materials, provided that such expenses comply with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policy provided in advance.

  3.8.2 **Pre-Approval.** Zenith shall obtain Contoso’s prior written approval for any individual expense in excess of US $2,500 or any travel involving air travel beyond the continental United States, unless otherwise specified in the SOW.

3.9 **No Set-Off.** Except as otherwise expressly provided in this Agreement or required by law, Contoso shall not withhold, set off, or deduct any amounts from payments due to Zenith on account of any claim or dispute with Zenith.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.** The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **February 03, 2027** (the **“Term”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**.

4.2 **Renewal.** The Parties may, by mutual written agreement executed at least ninety (90) days prior to the Expiration Date, renew or extend this Agreement on terms and conditions to be negotiated in good faith.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.** Contoso shall have the right to terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part (including any SOW or Order Form), for convenience by providing at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to Zenith. Zenith shall have a reciprocal right to terminate for convenience with the same 180-day notice period. During such notice period, the Parties shall continue to fulfill their respective obligations and Contoso shall pay all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable third-party commitments entered into by Zenith in reliance on this Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

  4.4.1 **Material Breach.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement (or any SOW or Order Form) upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail.

  4.4.2 **Insolvency.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party (a) becomes insolvent, (b) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, (c) is subject to any bankruptcy or insolvency proceeding that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days, or (d) ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.** Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

  (a) All SOWs and Order Forms shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination, unless otherwise agreed in writing;  
  (b) Contoso shall pay Zenith for all Services performed and Deliverables delivered (and accepted or deemed accepted) as of the effective date of termination, plus any approved expenses and any agreed termination or wind-down fees;  
  (c) Each Party shall promptly return or, at the disclosing Party’s option, destroy all Confidential Information of the other Party in its possession or control, subject to the archival retention rights in **Section 5.5**; and  
  (d) All licenses granted to Contoso under **Article VI** that are identified as perpetual shall survive termination, subject to Contoso’s continued compliance with the applicable license terms.

4.6 **Survival.** The rights and obligations of the Parties under **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), XVI (General Provisions)** and any other provisions that by their nature are intended to survive, shall survive any expiration or termination of this Agreement.

  - **Severity 2 (High)** – Major functionality impaired, temporary workaround available:
