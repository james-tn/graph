The Services to be provided by the Vendor may include, without limitation:

  2.2.1 Strategic business consulting, including market analysis, competitive positioning, growth strategy, and organizational design;

  2.2.2 Technology consulting, including IT transformation, systems architecture, digital strategy, and cloud migration advisory;

  2.2.3 Business process review and optimization, including mapping, gap analysis, and the design of improved workflows and controls;

  2.2.4 Implementation support, including project management, coordination with third‑party vendors, configuration advisory, and go‑live readiness;

  2.2.5 Change management and training services, including stakeholder engagement, communication planning, training curriculum and materials, and delivery of training workshops;

  2.2.6 Data analytics and reporting services, including requirements gathering, data model design recommendations, and analytical reports;

  2.2.7 Ongoing advisory and support services, including periodic executive briefings, performance reviews, and continuous improvement recommendations;

  2.2.8 Any other consulting or professional services that the Parties may agree upon from time to time and document in an SOW.

  2.3.1 The Parties shall enter into one or more SOWs that shall form part of this Agreement.   2.4.1 The Vendor shall perform the Services (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner; (b) in accordance with generally accepted industry standards for consulting services of a similar nature; (c) in compliance with Applicable Law; and (d) in accordance with the Service Levels set forth in Article XII.

  2.5.1 The Vendor shall deliver the Deliverables in accordance with the schedule set forth in the applicable SOW. Deliverables may include, without limitation:  
  (a) detailed assessment reports;  
  (b) strategy documents and roadmaps;  
  (c) process documentation and standard operating procedures;  
  (d) presentations and executive summaries;  
  (e) training materials, user guides, and knowledge‑transfer documentation; and  
  (f) any other work product set forth in the SOW.

  2.5.2 Unless otherwise specified in the applicable SOW, the Client shall have ten (10) Business Days after the Vendor’s delivery of each Deliverable to review and either (a) provide written acceptance or (b) provide a written notice of deficiencies specifying in reasonable detail any material non‑conformance with the requirements set forth in the SOW.

  2.5.4 If the Client does not provide any written notice of deficiencies within the applicable review period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted as of the expiration of such review period.

  2.6.1 The Client shall provide the Vendor with timely access to Client Materials, systems, facilities, and personnel as reasonably required for the Vendor to perform the Services.
