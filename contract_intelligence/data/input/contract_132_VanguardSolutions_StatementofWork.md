## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overall Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

This SOW is executed under and subject to the Parent Agreement and is intended to define specific Services and Deliverables contemplated by **Section 2.2 (Statements of Work)** and **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly supplemented or modified in this SOW, the provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement apply to this SOW. In the event of a conflict between the Parent Agreement and this SOW, **the Parent Agreement shall control**, unless this SOW expressly states that a specific provision of the Parent Agreement is modified with respect to this SOW and such modification is executed pursuant to Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement and Section 16.3 thereof.

2.2 **Project Objective.**  

The objective of this SOW is to design, implement, and operationalize an enterprise-wide digital transformation and advanced analytics platform for the Company, building upon the assessments, roadmap, and initial pilot work performed (or to be performed) under prior SOWs executed under CSA-VAN-202404-116. This includes:

- Implementing scalable cloud-based data infrastructure and integration pipelines;  
- Developing production-grade analytics assets (dashboards, models, reports) for executive and operational stakeholders;  
- Optimizing core business processes across finance, operations, and customer experience;  
- Establishing governance, training, and change management capabilities that allow the Company to sustain and extend the transformation.

2.3 **High-Level Description of Services.**  

Consistent with **Section 2.1(a)-(g)** of the Parent Agreement, the Services under this SOW shall include, without limitation:

a. **Assessment Extension & Data Discovery** – Deep-dive diagnostic analysis of current systems and data sources not previously covered, including new lines of business, external data feeds, and legacy platforms acquired or implemented after April 27, 2024.  

b. **Target-State Architecture and Solution Design** – Specification and design of target-state architectures for cloud data platforms, data lakes/warehouses, integration frameworks, and associated analytics tools, consistent with **Section 2.1(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  

c. **Implementation & Configuration** – Execution of solution build-out, including configuration, scripting, data ingestion pipelines, data modeling, and integration with the Company’s existing systems (ERP, CRM, HR, and operational systems).  

d. **Analytics Use-Case Development** – Design and delivery of prioritized analytics use cases, dashboards, and reporting solutions, consistent with **Section 2.1(e)** of the Parent Agreement, for multiple stakeholder groups.  

e. **Process Optimization & Automation** – Identification and redesign of key business processes, with implementation of automation workflows where appropriate.  

f. **Training, Workshops & Change Management** – Facilitation of workshops, training sessions, and executive briefings as contemplated in **Section 2.1(f)**, including user training, adoption support, and governance design.  

g. **Project Management & Reporting** – Structured project management, including periodic status reports and steering committee updates consistent with **Section 2.6 (Reporting and Meetings)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Phases.**  

The Program under this SOW will be executed in the following Phases, which collectively support the SOW Contract Value in Article III:

- **Phase A – Extended Assessment & Architecture Finalization**  
- **Phase B – Platform Build-Out & Integrations**  
- **Phase C – Analytics Use Cases & Operational Optimization**  
- **Phase D – Training, Handover & Optimization Support**

Each Phase may be subject to further refinement via mutually agreed Project Plan and, if necessary, Change Orders under Article XIII.

2.5 **Detailed Scope by Phase.**  

**(a) Phase A – Extended Assessment & Architecture Finalization (Approx. 16–20 weeks)**  

i. Perform extended current-state assessment of systems, processes, and data beyond the scope originally contemplated in the Parent Agreement’s example **Phase 1 – Assessment & Roadmap (Estimated US $190,000)**, including any new subsidiaries, business units, or data sources.  
ii. Conduct stakeholder interviews and workshops to validate requirements and prioritize use cases.  
iii. Produce an **Extended Current-State Assessment Report** that complements and updates the “Current-State Assessment Report” described in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  
iv. Refine and finalize the **Strategic Roadmap** and **Solution Design Documentation** (see Parent Agreement, Section 2.3(b)-(c)) with more granular workstreams, dependencies, and sequencing for multi-year execution.  
v. Prepare a comprehensive **Target-State Architecture Blueprint**, including:  
   - Cloud reference architecture;  
   - Data governance and security design;  
   - Integration patterns;  
   - Tooling selection and rationale.

**(b) Phase B – Platform Build-Out & Integrations (Approx. 9–12 months)**  

i. Implement the target-state data platform in the Company’s selected cloud environment, including provisioning of environments (dev/test/prod) and initial security configurations.  
ii. Design and build data ingestion pipelines from at least ten (10) priority source systems, including ERP, CRM, and selected operational platforms.  
iii. Create logical and physical data models for key subject areas (e.g., Sales, Finance, Operations, Customer Experience).  
iv. Configure and integrate selected analytics and visualization tools (e.g., BI platform of Company’s choosing).  
v. Implement monitoring, logging, and basic support processes for the data platform.  

**(c) Phase C – Analytics Use Cases & Operational Optimization (Approx. 8–10 months, overlapping Phase B)**  

i. Develop at least twelve (12) production-grade analytics use cases (e.g., revenue forecasting, churn analysis, operational KPI dashboards) prioritized jointly with the Company.  
ii. For each use case, provide:  
   - Use-case design documentation;  
   - Dashboard and report configurations;  
   - Data transformations and business rules;  
   - Acceptance test scripts and user testing support.  
iii. Identify high-value process optimization opportunities informed by analytics findings and design improved process flows.  
iv. Implement selected process improvements, which may include automation tools (e.g., workflow engines, RPA) as agreed in writing.  

**(d) Phase D – Training, Handover & Optimization Support (Approx. 3–6 months, may overlap Phases B and C)**  

i. Develop and deliver tailored **Training and Workshop Materials** as contemplated by **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
   - Executive briefings;  
   - End-user and power-user training;  
   - Technical runbook and administrator training.  
ii. Provide hypercare and optimization support for a period of not less than ninety (90) days after initial go-live of the core platform.  
iii. Establish governance forums and cadence (e.g., analytics steering committee, data governance council) with documented charters.  
iv. Prepare and deliver a **Transition & Handover Package**, including architecture documentation, configuration baselines, support procedures, and open issues log.

2.6 **Deliverables.**  

Major Deliverables under this SOW include, without limitation:

- Extended Current-State Assessment Report;  
- Updated and Detailed Strategic Roadmap;  
- Target-State Architecture Blueprint;  
- Platform Configuration and Implementation Documentation;  
- Data Models and Integration Specifications;  
- Use-Case Design Documents and Testing Artifacts;  
- Production-Ready Dashboards and Reports;  
- Training Materials (slides, guides, recordings if applicable);  
- Governance and Operating Model Documentation;  
- Transition & Handover Package.

2.7 **Timeline and Milestones.**  

The Parties shall agree upon a Project Plan within thirty (30) days of the SOW Effective Date. Key targeted milestones (subject to refinement) include:

- M1 – Approval of Extended Assessment Scope and Workplan (within 30 days);  
- M2 – Delivery of Extended Current-State Assessment Report and Updated Roadmap (within 16–20 weeks);  
- M3 – Completion of Core Platform Build and Initial Source Integrations (within 9–12 months);  
- M4 – Go-Live of at least six (6) priority analytics use cases (within 12–14 months);  
- M5 – Completion of all twelve (12) use cases and identified process improvements (within 18–22 months);  
- M6 – Completion of training, transition, and hypercare period (by or before the SOW Expiration Date).

2.8 **Key Personnel.**  

The Service Provider shall assign Key Personnel (by role) including:

- Program Manager;

- Data Engineering Lead;  
- Analytics Lead;

Consistent with Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall use personnel with appropriate skills and may replace Key Personnel with equally qualified personnel upon prior notice to the Company.

a. The Company shall review and either accept or reasonably reject each Deliverable within **ten (10) Business Days** of delivery, providing specific written reasons for any rejection.  
b. If the Company fails to respond within such period, the Deliverable shall be deemed Accepted.
