## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Statement of Work** dated **16 June 2024**, bearing Contract Reference Number **SOW-ZEN-202403-200** and internal identifier **contract_200** (the **“Original SOW” or “SOW-ZEN-202403-200”**), executed pursuant to the Master Services Agreement Ref. **MSA-ZEN-202403-197 (contract_197)** (the **“Master Agreement”**), pursuant to which Vendor agreed to perform an enterprise systems implementation, systems integration and managed application support services project for Client, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Original SOW;

WHEREAS, the Original SOW establishes, among other things, (a) the **SOW Effective Date** of 16 June 2024 and **SOW Expiration Date** of 15 June 2026 (Section 4.1), (b) a **SOW Contract Value** of **USD $2,981,827** (Section 1.16 and Article III), (c) payment terms of **Net 45** (Section 3.3.3), (d) a **high‑risk** and **uncapped liability** framework (Article IX), and (e) detailed Project phases, Deliverables and Service Levels (Article II and Article XII);

WHEREAS, Client now desires to adjust and extend certain aspects of the Services, Deliverables, timelines, Service Levels and commercial terms set forth in the Original SOW, including to add an extended optimization phase, to modify the Managed Services scope, to revise the payment terms to **Net 60**, and to align the risk allocation to a **medium** risk profile with a monetary liability cap;

WHEREAS, Vendor is willing to accommodate such changes, subject to the terms and conditions of this Amendment, including revised fees, revised Service Level remedies, and an adjusted term and limitation of liability framework;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to have this Amendment **modify, supplement and, where expressly stated, supersede** specific provisions of the Original SOW, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article IV (Term and Termination)**, **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, while leaving all other provisions of the Original SOW in full force and effect;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises set forth in this Amendment, the Original SOW, and the Master Agreement, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Original SOW and Master Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Amendment is **entered into pursuant to** and **under** the Original SOW (Ref: **SOW-ZEN-202403-200**, **contract_200**) and the Master Agreement (Ref: **MSA-ZEN-202403-197**, **contract_197**) and shall be deemed an integral part of the Original SOW. The provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Original SOW shall continue to apply except to the extent expressly modified or supplemented herein.

2.1.2 In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Amendment and the Original SOW, the terms of this Amendment shall prevail **solely with respect to the subject matter of this Amendment**, and the Original SOW shall prevail in all other respects, consistent with Section 2.1.3 of the Original SOW.

2.1.3 All Services and Deliverables under this Amendment constitute “Services” and “Deliverables” as such terms are defined in Sections 1.4 and 1.5 of the Original SOW and Sections 1.17 and 1.18 of the Master Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Additional and Modified Services.**

2.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Original SOW already contemplated a multi‑phase Project, including **Phase 1 – Initiation and Discovery**, **Phase 2 – Design and Build**, **Phase 3 – Testing, UAT and Go‑Live**, **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Transition to Managed Services**, and **Phase 5 – Managed Services** as described in Sections 2.3.1–2.3.5 of the Original SOW.

2.2.2 Under this Amendment, Vendor shall perform additional Services and Deliverables that build on, refine and extend the work performed under Phases 2, 3, 4 and 5, including:

  a. an **Extended Optimization Phase (Phase 4B)** of post‑Go‑Live optimization, performance tuning and enablement;  
  b. implementation of **New Functionalities** in the ERP and CRM solution;  
  c. expansion of the **Managed Services** scope to include additional monitoring, automation, and reporting; and  
  d. alignment of Service Levels, risk allocation, and commercial terms with the **Medium Risk Profile** described in this Amendment.

2.3 **New Functionalities – Detailed Description.**

2.3.1 **Advanced Analytics and Reporting.** Vendor shall design and implement advanced analytics capabilities building upon Deliverables D3 (Configuration Workbook) and D6 (Test Reports) of the Original SOW, including:

  - Development of at least **six (6)** role‑based analytics dashboards (finance, sales, service, operations, executive, and IT);  
  - Integration of key ERP and CRM data into a consolidated data model compatible with Client’s Data Warehouse referenced in Section 2.3.2 of the Original SOW (integration with “Data Warehouse” as one of the five systems);  
  - Configuration of scheduled reports and alerts, with parameters and thresholds agreed in the Project Plan.

2.3.2 **Customer Journey Automation.** Vendor shall configure CRM workflows to streamline customer onboarding and lifecycle management, including:

  - Setup of automated email and in‑app notifications tied to customer events;  
  - Integration with a third‑party marketing automation platform and payment gateway (collectively “Third Party Platforms”);  
  - Updating the **Integration Design Specifications and API Catalog (D4)** from the Original SOW to reflect new endpoints and data flows.

2.3.3 **Controls, Compliance and Audit Enhancements.** In light of Client’s internal control needs, Vendor shall:

  - Enhance role‑based access controls and approval workflows within the ERP modules;  
  - Implement audit logging for critical financial and customer data changes;  
  - Provide updated **Operational Runbooks and SOPs (D9)** that incorporate new control procedures.

2.3.4 **User Experience and Adoption Enhancements.** Vendor shall deliver:

  - Additional training sessions and materials (“Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Pack – Wave 2”) supplementing Deliverable D10;  
  - Refined UI/UX configurations based on user feedback captured during Phase 4 hypercare;  
  - Support for change adoption campaigns, including frequently asked questions (FAQ) documents and quick‑reference guides.

2.4 **Extended Optimization Phase (Phase 4B).**

2.4.1 **Timing and Duration.** The Extended Optimization Phase shall commence immediately following completion of Phase 4 – Stabilization & Transition to Managed Services described in Section 2.3.4 of the Original SOW and shall run for a period of approximately **twelve (12) weeks**, subject to adjustments via the Change Management process under Article XIII of the Original SOW and this Amendment.

2.4.2 **Scope of Phase 4B.** Activities shall include:

  - **Performance Tuning:** re‑assessment of application performance and database optimization, focusing on Incident trends from Deliverable D11 (Monthly Service Performance Reports);  
  - **Backlog Reduction:** prioritized resolution of medium‑severity defects and enhancement requests identified during the first six (6) months of Managed Services;  
  - **Analytics Enablement:** build‑out of the advanced analytics and dashboards described in Section 2.3.1;  
  - **Process Optimization Workshops:** structured sessions with business stakeholders to refine “to‑be” processes originally defined in Phase 1 of the Original SOW.

2.4.3 **Milestone Extension Deliverables.** The following additional Deliverables are introduced:

  - **D13 – Advanced Analytics Blueprint and Dashboards Pack;**  
  - **D14 – Optimization & Enhancement Backlog Register and Burn‑Down Reports;**  
  - **D15 – Training Materials & Adoption Toolkit (Wave 2);**  
  - **D16 – Updated Operational Runbooks & SOP Addendum (Phase 4B).**

Each of D13–D16 shall have agreed Acceptance Criteria, to be documented in the updated Project Plan pursuant to Section 1.9 of the Original SOW and Section 2.3 of the Master Agreement.

2.5 **Enhanced Managed Services Scope.**

2.5.1 **Additional Managed Services Components.** For the remainder of the SOW Term, Vendor shall, in addition to the commitments in Sections 2.2(e), 2.3.5 and 2.6 of the Original SOW:

  - Provide **proactive incident prevention** through monthly trend analysis and root‑cause reviews;  
  - Implement **automated monitoring** of key ERP/CRM processes, including scheduled job monitoring and interface health checks;  
  - Deliver **quarterly optimization reviews** (supplementing D12 – Semi‑annual Optimization & Roadmap Reports), resulting in an additional **Quarterly Optimization Brief**;  
  - Support new integrations and workflows introduced under this Amendment, including Level 2 and Level 3 support for Third Party Platforms integrations.

2.5.2 **Service Levels.** The general Service Level framework in **Article XII of the Original SOW** remains applicable, including the **99.9% Uptime** requirement in Section 12.2.1 and the Incident response times in Section 12.3.1. However, the **Service Credit Regime** set forth in Section 12.4 of this Amendment shall apply to Service Level failures attributable to scope introduced by this Amendment, superseding Section 12.4 of the Original SOW solely in relation thereto.

2.5.3 **Client Responsibilities.** Client’s obligations under Section 2.6 of the Original SOW remain in full force and effect and are expanded to include:

  - providing timely access to business process owners for optimization workshops;  
  - ensuring Client’s Third Party Platform contracts support the required integrations;  
  - coordinating change communications to end users regarding New Functionalities.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  
Vendor shall perform the Services under this Amendment in accordance with the performance standards set out in Section 2.5 of the Original SOW and Section 2.4 of the Master Agreement, including performing the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards and utilizing personnel with the requisite skills and experience.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amended Contract Value.**

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that, in addition to the **SOW Contract Value of USD $2,981,827** set forth in Section 3.1 of the Original SOW, the incremental Services and Deliverables introduced by this Amendment have an **aggregate estimated value** of **USD $2,134,209** (the **“Amended Contract Value”** or **“Revised SOW Contract Value”** for this Amendment).

3.2.1 The Fees under this Amendment shall comprise:

  a. **Extended Optimization Fixed Fees (Phase 4B):** USD **$780,000** for D13–D16 and related activities;  
  b. **Enhanced Managed Services Uplift Fees:** an incremental monthly fee of USD **$35,000** for additional monitoring, analytics, and quarterly optimizations from the Amendment Effective Date through the Amendment Expiration Date;  
  c. **Additional T&M Fees:** an estimated pool of USD **$289,209** for optional enhancements, additional dashboards, integration extensions, and training beyond the baseline scope defined herein.

3.2.2 These Fees are in addition to, and do not replace, the Fixed‑Price Implementation Fees, T&M Fees and Managed Services Fees described in Section 3.2 of the Original SOW (including M1–M6). However, to the extent that any work originally contemplated under T&M is now absorbed into the fixed Extended Optimization scope, the Parties shall adjust the T&M estimates via Change Orders as appropriate.

3.3 **Invoices and Payment Terms.**

3.3.1 **Milestone‑Based Fees (Phase 4B).** Vendor shall invoice:

  - **USD $260,000** upon Client’s acceptance of D13 (Advanced Analytics Blueprint & Dashboards Pack);  
  - **USD $260,000** upon Client’s acceptance of D14 and D15;  
  - **USD $260,000** upon Client’s acceptance of D16 and completion of the Extended Optimization Phase.

3.3.2 **Enhanced Managed Services Uplift Fees.** Vendor shall invoice the incremental USD $35,000 per month **monthly in arrears**, aligned with existing M5 invoicing under Section 3.2 of the Original SOW, for each full or partial month in which Enhanced Managed Services are provided.

Section 3.3.3 of the Original SOW (which provides for “Net 45” payment terms) is hereby **amended and restated** solely with respect to invoices issued on or after the Amendment Effective Date as follows:

> “All Invoices issued under this SOW on or after 9 February 2025, including invoices for Fees under AMD‑ZEN‑202403‑220, shall be payable by Client within **sixty (60) days** from the date of the Invoice (**‘Net 60’**) without setoff or deduction (except as permitted under Section 3.6 of the Master Agreement).”

3.3.4 **Late Payment and Suspension.**  
Section 3.5 of the Original SOW and Section 3.5 of the Master Agreement regarding late payment, interest and potential suspension of Services shall apply fully to Fees under this Amendment, with the due date measured from the revised Net 60 Terms.

3.4 **Taxes.**  
All Fees under this Amendment are **exclusive of Taxes** and shall be handled in accordance with Section 3.4 of the Original SOW and Section 3.4 of the Master Agreement, including any obligations regarding withholding Tax and gross‑up.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
Client shall reimburse Vendor for reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses actually incurred in connection with Phase 4B and Enhanced Managed Services, consistent with Section 3.7 of the Original SOW and Section 3.7 of the Master Agreement. 3.6 **Price Adjustments.**  
The price adjustment mechanism in Section 3.8 of the Original SOW and Section 3.8 of the Master Agreement shall apply to the Enhanced Managed Services Uplift Fees, provided that any annual rate increase shall not exceed five percent (5%) per annum unless otherwise agreed by the Parties in a Change Order.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term; Revised SOW Expiration Date.**

4.1.1 The **Amendment Term** shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date** (9 February 2025) and, unless earlier terminated as provided herein, shall continue in full force and effect until **10 July 2026** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**).

4.1.2 To the extent the Original SOW specified a **SOW Expiration Date of 15 June 2026** (Section 4.1), such SOW Expiration Date is hereby **extended** for the scope governed by this Amendment to **10 July 2026**. The Parties acknowledge that any Managed Services contracted under the Original SOW beyond this date shall be subject to mutual written agreement as per Section 4.2 of the Original SOW.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.2.1 Section 4.3 of the Original SOW (which provides for termination for convenience on one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice) is hereby modified, solely with respect to the incremental Services and Fees under this Amendment, to provide that either Party may terminate the **Amendment‑related scope only** for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party.

4.2.2 For avoidance of doubt, termination for convenience under this Section 4.2 shall not automatically terminate the Original SOW in its entirety, unless expressly stated in the termination notice, and the Original SOW shall continue in force as to any remaining scope not covered by this Amendment.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**  
Termination for cause, including for material breach or non‑payment, shall be governed by Section 4.4 of the Original SOW and Section 4.4 of the Master Agreement. Any such termination may be made with respect to this Amendment alone, or to the Original SOW and Master Agreement in their entirety, as specified in the termination notice.

4.4 **Effect of Termination of Original SOW or Master Agreement.**

4.4.1 If the **Master Agreement** or the **Original SOW** is terminated or expires, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue the Extended Optimization and/or Enhanced Managed Services on stand‑alone terms.

4.4.2 Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment, Client shall pay Vendor all Fees and reimbursable expenses due and payable for Services performed and Deliverables delivered (or in progress) under this Amendment up to the effective date of termination, including non‑cancellable third‑party costs, consistent with Section 4.5.2 of the Original SOW and Section 4.5 of the Master Agreement.

4.4.3 At Client’s written request, Vendor shall provide transition assistance as described in Section 4.5.3 of the Original SOW and Section 4.5.2 of the Master Agreement. Fees for transition services related to this Amendment shall be at Vendor’s then‑current rates under a mutually agreed Change Order.

4.5 **Survival.**  
Those provisions of this Amendment which by their nature should survive termination or expiration, including payment obligations, confidentiality, IP provisions, limitation of liability, data protection, dispute resolution, and any accrued Service Credits, shall survive in accordance with Section 4.6 of the Original SOW and Section 4.6 of the Master Agreement.
