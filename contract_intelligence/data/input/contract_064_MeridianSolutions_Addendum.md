WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement specifies, among other things, the initial license scope for up to **250 named Users** of the Licensed Software (as referenced in **Section 3.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement), the **Contract Value** for the initial term in the amount of **US$769,092**, the **Initial Term** ending on **September 18, 2028** (as defined in **Section 1.12** and **Section 4.1**), the applicable **Service Level Agreement** under **Article XII**, and detailed provisions on **Fees and Payment Terms (Article III)**, **Confidentiality (Article V)**, **Data Protection and Privacy (Article XI)**, and other terms and conditions;

WHEREAS, Contoso now desires to **add additional licensed Users and additional software modules** to its deployment of **Meridian CoreSuite Enterprise**, and Meridian is willing to provide such additional User licenses and modules, together with associated services, upon the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Addendum and in accordance with the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, this Addendum is expressly intended to constitute an “addendum” and “written amendment” to the Parent Agreement within the meaning of **Section 1.2 (Definition of ‘Agreement’)** and **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement, and shall be deemed an integral part of, and incorporated into, the “Agreement” as defined therein;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge and agree that the **total contract value** for the additional license rights and services granted under this Addendum for its term shall be **One Million Seven Hundred Eighty-Five Thousand Two Hundred Eighty-Four U.S. Dollars (US$1,785,284)** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), which is **in addition to** and not in substitution of the Contract Value under the Parent Agreement, subject to adjustments, true-ups, and additional fees as expressly provided herein and in the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties further acknowledge that the **risk level** associated with this Addendum is deemed **“High”**, and the Parties intend that, with respect to the matters covered by this Addendum, the allocation of rights, duties, and risk shall be **Vendor-favorable** relative to the Parent Agreement, including without limitation broader indemnification obligations for Contoso, more restrictive intellectual property terms, and enhanced liability exposures for both Parties, all as expressly set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained in this Addendum, and intending to be legally bound hereby, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

(a) This Addendum **adds licensed users/modules dated September 18, 2025 (Ref: SLA-MER-202509-056)** and is intended to extend and expand the **Scope of Services and Work** described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Except as expressly provided herein, all rights and obligations relating to the Software, Services, Deliverables, and SLA under the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to the Additional Users, Additional Modules, and Scope Extension Services provided under this Addendum.  

(c) In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, **the terms of this Addendum shall prevail** with respect to the Additional Users, Additional Modules, Scope Extension Services, and the Addendum Contract Value, unless otherwise explicitly stated herein.

2.2 **Additional Users.**  

(a) As of the Addendum Effective Date, Meridian hereby grants to Contoso the right to increase its licensed User capacity under **Section 6.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement from **250 named Users** to a total of **750 named Users** (the “Updated User Capacity”).  

(b) The incremental **500 named Users** (the “Additional Users”) shall be subject to all restrictions and conditions applicable to “Users” under the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to the limitations set forth in **Section 6.3 (Restrictions)**.  

(c) Additional Users shall have access to the same base functionality of **Meridian CoreSuite Enterprise** as existing Users, subject to any Additional Modules acquired by Contoso under this Addendum.

2.3 **Additional Modules.**  

Meridian shall make available to Contoso, and Contoso hereby licenses, the following Additional Modules of the Licensed Software, which supplement the modules described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement:

  (i) **Advanced Analytics and Predictive Insights Module** – providing machine-learning driven forecasting, anomaly detection, and predictive alerts, integrated into the analytics and reporting dashboards referred to in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (ii) **Regulatory Compliance and Audit Module** – providing enhanced compliance workflows, policy attestation, and extended audit logging capabilities that build upon the audit logs and monitoring tools described in **Section 2.2(e)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (iii) **Extended Integration Suite** – providing additional pre-built connectors and integration templates for third-party systems beyond those identified in the initial **SOW #1 (Exhibit A)**, including but not limited to additional CRM, ERP, and HRIS platforms, all based on the integration APIs referred to in **Section 2.2(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Scope Extension Services.**  

In connection with the Additional Users and Additional Modules, Meridian shall perform the following Scope Extension Services, which shall be deemed “Services” under **Section 1.17** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Requirements and Solution Design.** Conduct workshops with Contoso’s designated project team to gather requirements specific to the Additional Users and Additional Modules, and prepare a solution design document detailing User role mappings, module configurations, data flows, and integration points, consistent with the practices described in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (b) **Configuration and Deployment.** Configure the Additional Modules, including setting up predictive models, compliance workflows, and new integrations, and apply appropriate role-based access controls as described in **Section 2.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (c) **Data Migration and Enrichment.** Where necessary, perform incremental data migration to support new module functionality and new Users, including historical data ingestion for predictive analytics and compliance records, subject to scope and data volumes mutually agreed in a Work Order, consistent with **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (d) **Testing and UAT Support.** Assist Contoso in conducting system integration testing and User acceptance testing (UAT) for the Additional Modules and capacity, providing remediation of defects identified during UAT as described in **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (e) **Training.** Deliver training sessions and updated Documentation for Contoso’s administrators and Users regarding the Additional Modules and Extended Integration Suite, including administrator training, train-the-trainer sessions, and role-specific user training.  

  (f) **Go-Live and Hypercare.** Provide go-live support for the initial deployment of the Additional Modules and Additional Users, followed by a hypercare period of a minimum of **30 calendar days** post go-live, during which support will be prioritized for issues arising from the extended scope.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

(a) The Parties acknowledge that time is of commercial importance for the deployment of Additional Users and Additional Modules but not of the essence.  

(b) Detailed project timelines, milestones, and dependencies for the Scope Extension Services shall be set forth in one or more Work Orders/SOWs executed under this Addendum and referencing **SLA-MER-202509-056**.  

(c) Meridian shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet such timelines, subject to Contoso’s timely cooperation, as further described in **Section 2.6 (Contoso Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, which is hereby incorporated by reference.  

(d) Any delays attributable to Contoso, including delayed approvals, changes in requirements, or failure to provide necessary data or access, shall entitle Meridian to schedule adjustments and, where applicable, Fee adjustments consistent with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

(a) Meridian shall perform the Scope Extension Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, and in accordance with generally accepted industry standards.  

(b) For hosted or SaaS deployments, the Parent SLA defined in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Addendum, shall apply to the environment supporting the Additional Users and Additional Modules.  

(c) Contoso’s sole and exclusive remedy for a breach of the performance standards in this Section shall be **re-performance of the affected Services at no additional charge**, subject to any limitations and remedies specified in the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, and subject further to the High Risk Allocation described herein.

2.7 **Relationship to SOWs and Work Orders.**  

(a) Any Work Order executed under this Addendum shall be considered a “Statement of Work” for purposes of the Parent Agreement and shall:  

  (i) reference both **ADD-MER-202509-064** and **SLA-MER-202509-056 (contract_056)**;  
  (ii) describe the scope, deliverables, assumptions, timelines, and Fees specific to the Scope Extension Services; and  
  (iii) be governed by, and incorporated into, both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.  

(b) In the event of conflict between a Work Order and this Addendum, this Addendum shall prevail, except where the Work Order expressly states that it overrides a specific provision of this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

(a) In consideration for the Additional Users, Additional Modules, and Scope Extension Services provided under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay Meridian the **Addendum Contract Value** of **US$1,785,284** for the Addendum Term, subject to adjustments and increases as provided herein.  

(b) The Addendum Contract Value is in addition to, and does not replace, the Contract Value of **US$769,092** specified in **Section 1.5** and **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  

For clarity, the Addendum Contract Value is allocated approximately as follows (illustrative allocation to be refined in Work Orders/SOWs):

  (a) **Additional License/Subscription Fees:** Approximately **US$1,250,000** for the Addendum Term for the incremental **500 named Users** and the Additional Modules described in **Section 2.3** of this Addendum;  

  (b) **Scope Extension Services Fees (Implementation and Professional Services):** Approximately **US$435,284** for requirements gathering, configuration, integration, data migration, testing, and training under this Addendum;  

  (c) **Additional Maintenance and Support Fees:** Approximately **US$100,000** for ongoing support, updates, and enhancements related to the Additional Modules and Additional Users during the Addendum Term.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms (Net 30).**  

(a) Notwithstanding **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that all undisputed invoices issued under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 30**”).  

(b) Unless otherwise specified in a Work Order/SOW:  

  (i) License/Subscription Fees related to Additional Users and Additional Modules for the first year of the Addendum Term shall be invoiced on or promptly after the Addendum Effective Date; subsequent annual license/subscription Fees shall be invoiced **thirty (30) days** prior to the start of each anniversary year of the Addendum Term;  

  (ii) Implementation and professional services Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears based on time and materials at the agreed rates, or upon completion of specified milestones, consistent with the framework in **Section 3.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (iii) Additional Maintenance and Support Fees shall be invoiced annually in advance.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  

(a) Any undisputed amount not received by Meridian by the due date shall accrue interest at **one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month**, or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is less, from the due date until paid in full, as per the methodology in **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Meridian may suspend provision of additional Services, access for Additional Users, and activation or continued use of Additional Modules if any undisputed amounts under this Addendum remain unpaid for more than **thirty (30) days** past the due date, provided that Meridian has given at least **ten (10) days’ prior written notice** and Contoso has not cured such non-payment.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

Contoso may withhold payment of any amounts reasonably and in good faith disputed under this Addendum, provided that Contoso:

  (a) Notifies Meridian in writing of the dispute within **fifteen (15) days** after receipt of the invoice, specifying the grounds for dispute; and  

  (b) Pays all undisputed portions of the invoice in accordance with **Section 3.3** of this Addendum.  

The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve invoice disputes promptly, and any amounts ultimately determined to be payable shall be paid within **fifteen (15) days** after resolution.

3.6 **Taxes and Expenses.**  

(a) All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be governed by **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Contoso shall reimburse Meridian for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket travel and related expenses incurred in performing on-site Scope Extension Services under this Addendum, in accordance with **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement.

3.7 **Fee Adjustments.**  

Upon any renewal or extension of the Addendum Term (if mutually agreed), Meridian may adjust its Fees consistent with the principles in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement; however, for the high-risk profile of this Addendum, the standard cap on annual increases (5%) may be exceeded with Contoso’s prior written approval where materially justified by scope increases, regulatory changes, or substantial cost increases.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

(a) This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **April 7, 2030** (the “Addendum Term”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**.  

(b) The Addendum Term may extend beyond the Initial Term of the Parent Agreement. The Parties agree that, to the extent necessary, they shall execute any amendments to the Parent Agreement required to harmonize the Parent Term with the Addendum Term, or they shall expressly provide in writing that the rights and obligations under this Addendum survive beyond termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement, as applicable.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

The Addendum may be renewed for successive one (1) year renewal terms by mutual written agreement of the Parties, which may be documented by a renewal order form, Work Order, or amendment referencing both this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

Notwithstanding **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement, either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience, without cause, by providing the other Party at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**. In such event:

  (a) Contoso shall remain liable for all Fees accrued and expenses incurred under this Addendum through the effective date of termination; and  

  (b) Any prepaid, unused license or support Fees under this Addendum for periods after the effective date of termination shall be refunded on a pro rata basis, except for non-refundable setup or professional service Fees expressly designated as non-refundable in the applicable Work Order/SOW.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice to the other Party if:

  (a) The other Party materially breaches this Addendum (including any obligation expressly incorporated from the Parent Agreement) and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  

  (b) The other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, has a receiver appointed, or ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course, consistent with **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Security or Legal Concerns.**  

Contoso may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if:

  (a) Meridian experiences a material Security Incident or Data Breach (as described in **Section 11.5** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Addendum) that materially threatens the confidentiality, integrity, or availability of Contoso’s data linked to the Additional Modules or Additional Users, and Meridian fails to implement reasonably satisfactory remedial measures within **thirty (30) days** of notice; or  

  (b) Continuing to use the Additional Modules or Additional Users would, in Contoso’s reasonable opinion, cause Contoso to be in material violation of applicable law or regulatory requirements.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Addendum shall **automatically terminate** as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing that some or all of the rights and obligations under this Addendum shall survive or be novated into a new agreement.  

(b) Upon termination of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, all licenses granted to Contoso under both documents shall terminate, and Contoso shall cease all use of the Software, including the Additional Modules and Additional Users, subject to any limited archival rights specified in **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Addendum Only.**  

If this Addendum is terminated without terminating the Parent Agreement:

  (a) The additional license rights for Additional Users and Additional Modules shall terminate as of the effective date of termination of this Addendum;  

  (b) Contoso’s license rights under the Parent Agreement to the original 250 Users and existing modules shall continue, subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (c) The Parties shall cooperate to remove or disable access to the Additional Modules and Additional Users in a controlled manner, while preserving Contoso’s ongoing rights under the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Survival.**  

Provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including without limitation **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XVI (General Provisions)**, and any payment obligations accruing prior to termination.
