## ARTICLE XII – SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLA)

### 12.1 Service Availability

12.1.1 Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the production instance of the Software is available at least **99.9%** of the time in each calendar month, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and permitted downtime (the “**Service Availability Commitment**”).

12.1.2 “Availability” means that Authorized Users are able to log in and perform primary functions of the Software. Unavailability shall be measured based on Vendor’s system logs.

### 12.2 Response Times

Vendor shall respond to support requests within the following target response times from the time Vendor receives a support ticket:

- **Critical (Severity 1)** – production system down: initial response within 1 hour, continuous work until resolution or workaround;  
- **High (Severity 2)** – major functionality impaired: initial response within 4 hours;  
- **Medium (Severity 3)** – minor issue or partial degradation: initial response within 1 Business Day;  
- **Low (Severity 4)** – general questions: initial response within 2 Business Days.

### 12.3 Scheduled Maintenance

12.3.1 Vendor may perform Scheduled Maintenance on the Software during designated maintenance windows. Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to schedule maintenance outside of Client’s normal business hours and will provide at least **forty-eight (48) hours** advance notice for any Scheduled Maintenance expected to cause more than thirty (30) minutes of downtime.

12.3.2 Scheduled Maintenance, emergency security patches, and downtime due to force majeure events as described in Article XV shall not count toward unavailability when measuring the Service Availability Commitment.

### 12.4 Service Credits

12.4.1 If Vendor fails to meet the Service Availability Commitment in any given calendar month, Client shall be eligible to receive a service credit as follows:

- Availability < 99.9% but ≥ 99.0%: credit of 5% of the monthly subscription Fees;  
- Availability < 99.0% but ≥ 98.0%: credit of 10% of the monthly subscription Fees;  
- Availability < 98.0%: credit of 20% of the monthly subscription Fees.

12.4.2 Service credits shall be applied to the next invoiced period. Service credits are Client’s sole and exclusive remedy for failure to meet the Service Availability Commitment, subject to Client’s termination rights under Article IV for persistent failures.
