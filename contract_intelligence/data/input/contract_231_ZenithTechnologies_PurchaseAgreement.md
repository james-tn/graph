## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

### 2.1 General Description

2.1.1 During the Term, Zenith shall sell and deliver to Contoso, and Contoso shall purchase from Zenith, the Equipment and licenses specified in the applicable SOWs, and Zenith shall perform the Services described in such SOWs, in each case in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

2.1.2 The initial scope of work is expected to include:  
(a) the design and supply of enterprise-grade network routers, switches, and secure gateway devices;  
(b) installation, configuration, and integration of such Equipment within Contoso’s data centers and selected field locations;  
(c) deployment and configuration of Zenith’s proprietary network management software;  
(d) data migration and cutover support;  
(e) ongoing maintenance, monitoring, and support services, including patching and upgrades; and  
(f) training of Contoso’s technical personnel in the operation and administration of the supplied solution.

### 2.2 Statements of Work

2.2.1 Each SOW executed under this Agreement shall at a minimum specify: (a) a detailed description of the Equipment, Deliverables, and Services; (b) project milestones and delivery timelines; (c) acceptance criteria; (d) applicable Service Levels; (e) fees, expenses, and invoicing schedule; and (f) any SOW-specific assumptions, dependencies, or exclusions.

2.2.2 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Agreement and the terms of any SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall govern, except to the extent that the SOW expressly states that it is intended to modify a specific provision of this Agreement and identifies such provision by section number.

### 2.3 Deliverables and Milestones

2.3.1 Zenith shall produce and deliver to Contoso the Deliverables described in the applicable SOW, in accordance with the milestone schedule set forth therein. Such Deliverables may include, by way of example, infrastructure design documents, configuration runbooks, integration test plans, security hardening guidelines, and operational playbooks.

2.3.2 Each Deliverable shall conform in all material respects to the specifications, functional requirements, and acceptance criteria set forth in the applicable SOW. Zenith shall follow generally accepted industry standards and best practices in performing the Services and producing the Deliverables.

2.3.3 Unless otherwise stated in an SOW, Contoso shall have a period of thirty (30) days (or such other period as specified in the SOW) after receipt of any Deliverable to review and either (a) accept the Deliverable in writing, or (b) provide written notice of rejection specifying in reasonable detail the deficiencies and the applicable acceptance criteria that have not been met. In the absence of such notice within the review period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

2.3.4 If Contoso provides a timely notice of rejection, Zenith shall, at no additional cost to Contoso, use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the identified deficiencies and resubmit the Deliverable within a mutually agreed period. The review and acceptance process described in Section 2.3.3 shall then be repeated.

### 2.4 Performance Standards

2.4.1 Zenith shall perform all Services: (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with generally accepted industry standards; (b) in accordance with the project plans and timelines set forth in the applicable SOW; (c) using personnel with the requisite skills, experience, and qualifications; and (d) in compliance with applicable laws, rules, and regulations.

2.4.2 Zenith shall allocate sufficient resources, including personnel and equipment, to meet the timelines and Service Levels specified in this Agreement and the applicable SOWs. In the event Zenith becomes aware of any circumstances that may materially delay the performance of any Services or the delivery of any Deliverables, Zenith shall promptly notify Contoso in writing and propose a mitigation plan.

2.4.3 The Parties acknowledge that certain dependencies may rely on Contoso’s timely performance of its obligations, including providing necessary access to systems, facilities, and personnel, and making timely decisions and approvals. Zenith shall not be liable for delays or failures in performance caused by Contoso’s failure to fulfil such obligations, provided that Zenith has given Contoso prompt written notice of the dependency and the potential impact of any delay.

### 2.5 Changes to Scope

Any changes to the scope of Services, Deliverables, timelines, or fees shall be handled in accordance with the change management process set forth in Article XIII. No change shall be effective or binding unless documented in a written Change Order executed by both Parties.

### 2.6 Subcontracting

2.6.1 Zenith may, with Contoso’s prior written consent (such consent not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed), engage subcontractors to perform certain Services; provided, however, that Zenith shall remain fully responsible for the performance of all obligations under this Agreement and any SOW, and for the acts and omissions of its subcontractors.

2.6.2 Zenith shall ensure that all subcontractors are bound in writing by obligations of confidentiality, data protection, and security that are at least as protective as those imposed on Zenith under this Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Contract Value and Fee Structure

3.1.1 The total estimated contract value under this Agreement, inclusive of all SOWs executed as of the Effective Date, is **Three Million Two Hundred Ninety-Three Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty-Three U.S. Dollars (US$3,293,833)** (the “**Contract Value**”). The Parties acknowledge that additional SOWs may increase or decrease the total amounts ultimately payable, subject to written agreement.

3.1.2 Fees shall be set forth in detail in each SOW and may include, without limitation: (a) one-time fees for Equipment; (b) one-time implementation and configuration fees; (c) recurring license, hosting, or subscription fees; and (d) ongoing support and maintenance fees.

### 3.2 Payment Terms

3.2.1 Unless otherwise expressly stated in an SOW, Zenith shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the following general schedule:

- **Equipment**: 50% of the applicable Equipment fees upon execution of the corresponding SOW, 40% upon delivery of the Equipment to Contoso’s designated site, and 10% upon successful completion of installation and initial acceptance.  
- **Implementation and Professional Services**: In accordance with the milestone schedule set forth in the SOW (e.g., 30% upon project kickoff, 40% upon completion of configuration and testing, 30% upon final acceptance).  
- **Support and Maintenance**: Annually in advance, unless otherwise agreed in the applicable SOW.

3.2.2 Except as otherwise provided herein, payments shall be due **net sixty (60) days** from the date of receipt of a correct and undisputed invoice by Contoso (“**Net 60**”). Contoso shall remit payments via wire transfer or other mutually agreed method.

3.2.3 If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice, it shall notify Zenith in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt, specifying the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute. Contoso shall timely pay any undisputed portion of the invoice, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve the dispute. Any amounts resolved in Zenith’s favor shall be due within thirty (30) days of resolution.

### 3.3 Taxes

3.3.1 All Fees are exclusive of sales, use, excise, value-added, or other similar taxes, duties, or charges now or hereafter imposed by any governmental authority (collectively, “**Taxes**”). Contoso shall be responsible for all Taxes applicable to the Fees or the transactions contemplated by this Agreement, excluding any taxes based on Zenith’s income, property, or employees.

3.3.2 Zenith shall itemize any applicable Taxes on its invoices, and shall reasonably cooperate with Contoso in providing documentation or information necessary for Contoso to claim tax credits or exemptions, to the extent applicable.

### 3.4 Late Payment

3.4.1 Any undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of (a) one percent (1.0%) per month, or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, from the due date until the date of actual payment.

3.4.2 Zenith may, upon at least fifteen (15) days’ prior written notice to Contoso and provided Contoso has not cured such non-payment within such period, suspend the performance of Services and the delivery of Deliverables in the event any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after its due date. Zenith’s right to suspend is without prejudice to its other rights and remedies under this Agreement.

### 3.5 Expenses

3.5.1 Contoso shall reimburse Zenith for reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket travel and living expenses incurred by Zenith’s personnel in performing the Services, provided such expenses comply with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies, which shall be provided to Zenith upon request.

3.5.2 Zenith shall provide supporting documentation (such as receipts or expense reports) with any request for reimbursement of expenses. Contoso shall pay approved expenses in accordance with Section 3.2.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

4.1.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **January 29, 2028** (the “**Expiration Date**”).

4.1.2 The Parties may, by mutual written agreement executed no later than sixty (60) days prior to the Expiration Date, renew this Agreement for one or more additional periods, on such terms as the Parties may agree in writing.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Contoso may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part (including any SOW), for any reason or no reason, upon **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Zenith. This client-friendly termination right is in recognition of the low risk profile of the engagement.

4.2.2 Zenith may terminate this Agreement only for convenience upon at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, and only if such termination will not materially disrupt any critical services identified by Contoso. Zenith shall continue to perform any then-active critical services, as reasonably requested by Contoso, during such notice period.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement (or any affected SOW) upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement or such SOW and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail.

4.3.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party (a) becomes insolvent, (b) files or has filed against it a petition for bankruptcy that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days, (c) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or (d) ceases to carry on business in the ordinary course.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:  
(a) Contoso shall pay Zenith for all Equipment delivered and accepted, and for all Services performed and Deliverables accepted, up to the effective date of termination;  
(b) Zenith shall promptly refund any prepaid but unearned Fees for Services that were to be performed after the effective date of termination;  
(c) Each Party shall return or, at the disclosing Party’s written request, destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information, in accordance with Section 5.4.

4.4.2 In the event of termination by Contoso for cause pursuant to Section 4.3.1, Contoso shall have no obligation to pay for any Services or Deliverables that are non-conforming or not yet accepted, and may exercise any other remedies available at law or in equity.

4.4.3 In connection with any termination or expiration, Zenith shall provide reasonable transition assistance, at Contoso’s request, for up to sixty (60) days following the effective date of termination, at Zenith’s then-current time and materials rates, to facilitate the orderly transfer of Services to Contoso or a third party selected by Contoso.

### 4.5 Survival

Provisions of this Agreement which by their nature are intended to survive expiration or termination (including, without limitation, Sections 3.4, 4.4, Article V, Article VI, Article VII, Article VIII, Article IX, Article XI, Article XIV, Article XV, and Article XVI) shall survive any expiration or termination of this Agreement.
