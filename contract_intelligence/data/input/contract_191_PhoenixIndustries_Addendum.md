---

## PREAMBLE

This ADDENDUM TO DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (this “**Addendum**”), reference number **ADD-PHO-202402-191**, is entered into as of **November 28, 2024** (the “**Addendum Effective Date**”), by and between:

1. **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at **1200 Market Street, Suite 2400, Wilmington, DE 19801, USA** (hereinafter, “**Contoso**”, the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”); and  

2. **Phoenix Industries LLC**, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at **5500 Westbridge Plaza, Floor 9, Newark, DE 19711, USA** (hereinafter, “**Phoenix**”, the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Phoenix are hereinafter individually referred to as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Addendum is entered into **pursuant to and in supplementation of** that certain **Data Processing Agreement** between the Parties titled “DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT,” **Contract Reference Number: DPA-PHO-202402-157**, dated **February 17, 2024** (the “**Parent Agreement**” or the “**DPA**”), identified internally by the Client as **“contract_157”**.

This Addendum adds processing activities dated February 17, 2024 (Ref: **DPA-PHO-202402-157**) and is executed under and subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, except as expressly modified herein.

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent Agreement (Reference Number **DPA-PHO-202402-157**, internal identifier **contract_157**) as of **February 17, 2024** to govern the processing of Personal Data by Phoenix as Processor/Service Provider on behalf of Contoso as Controller/Business in connection with Phoenix’s provision of certain data processing, hosting, analytics, and related services, as more fully described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, pursuant to **Section 2.2 (Description of Services)** and **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, Phoenix currently provides a secure Platform instance, standard dashboards and reports, system performance reports, and related documentation to Contoso under a total contract value of **US$51,474**, as set forth in **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, Contoso now desires to expand and further define the nature and scope of the processing activities, including additional data domains, data sources, analytics use cases, and international processing, beyond those expressly described in **Sections 2.2.1–2.2.6** of the Parent Agreement, and Phoenix is willing to undertake such additional processing activities under the terms of this Addendum;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that the engagement, already characterized as **high-risk** under **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement by virtue of the explicit **absence of a monetary liability cap** and the express inclusion (rather than exclusion) of consequential and related damages, will be further expanded in scope, volume, and geographic reach by this Addendum;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to document the additional processing activities, additional services, expanded Service Levels, and corresponding commercial terms, including an additional **contract value of Four Million Sixty-Five Thousand Six Hundred Ten U.S. Dollars (US$4,065,610)**, payable in accordance with the **Net 60** payment terms consistent with **Section 3.3.2 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained in this Addendum and in the Parent Agreement, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that this Addendum **supplements and expands** the Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly modified herein, all rights and obligations relating to Services, Deliverables, and Client responsibilities under **Article II** of the Parent Agreement continue to apply to the Additional Services.

2.1.2 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this **Article II** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, the terms of this Addendum shall prevail solely with respect to the Additional Services and Addendum Processing Activities.

2.2 **Description of Additional Services.** Vendor shall provide to Client the following Additional Services, which extend and augment the Services described in **Sections 2.2.1–2.2.6** of the Parent Agreement:

2.2.1 **Expanded Data Hosting and Storage Capacities.**  
(a) In addition to the data hosting and storage obligations described in **Section 2.2.1 (Data Hosting and Storage)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provision **multi-region, high-availability clusters** for production, staging, and disaster recovery environments.  
(b) Vendor shall support at least a **tenfold (10x) increase** in data volume and throughput compared to the baseline configuration under the Parent Agreement, including long-term retention of raw event logs, behavioral tracking data, and detailed audit trails for no less than **seven (7) years**, subject to Client’s documented retention requirements and Applicable Data Protection Law.  
(c) Vendor shall provide geographically redundant storage spanning at least **two (2) distinct data centers** in the United States, and, where required by Client and permitted by law, selected additional regions (e.g., EU, UK) in compliance with **Section 11.9 (International Transfers)** of the Parent Agreement and the Data Residency Commitments under this Addendum.

2.2.2 **Enhanced Platform Services and Advanced Analytics.**  
(a) Vendor shall implement additional Platform modules for **real-time streaming analytics, identity resolution, cohort analysis, risk scoring, and predictive modeling**, building upon the **data transformation and analytics** capabilities described in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) Vendor shall deliver:  
   (i) A minimum of **twenty (20) additional standardized dashboards** and **thirty (30) additional custom reports**, beyond the five (5) standardized dashboards and ten (10) custom reports required under **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.  
   (ii) A configurable **data science sandbox** environment enabling Client-authorized users to run custom models and simulations on de-identified or pseudonymized datasets, consistent with the security obligations under **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.  
(c) Vendor shall provide ongoing **model monitoring and performance tuning**, including quarterly evaluations of predictive model performance, bias, and drift, with summary findings reported in the monthly system performance reports described in **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.3 **Complex Integration Projects.**  
(a) Building on the **Data Integration and Ingestion** Services described in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall design and implement **Complex Integration Projects** with at least **six (6)** additional Client systems and **four (4)** third-party systems (e.g., CRM, ERP, marketing automation, payment processors, and fraud detection services).  
(b) Each Complex Integration Project shall be documented in an Applicable SOW or Project Plan referencing this Addendum, and shall include detailed mapping of data fields, transformation logic, and security controls consistent with **Sections 11.4 and 11.7** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.  
(c) Vendor shall support **near real-time data synchronization** for at least three (3) high-priority integrations, with end-to-end latency targets not exceeding **five (5) minutes** under normal operating conditions.

2.2.4 **User Access, Interface, and Role Hierarchies.**  
(a) In addition to the **User Access and Interface** obligations under **Section 2.2.4** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall implement granular **role-based access control (RBAC)** and **attribute-based access control (ABAC)** schemes to support complex organizational hierarchies and segregation of duties as directed by Client.  
(b) Vendor shall support **single sign-on (SSO)** integrations with Client’s identity providers (e.g., SAML, OAuth2/OpenID Connect), and shall enforce **multi-factor authentication (MFA)** for all administrative roles, aligning with the security measures in **Section 11.4.2** of the Parent Agreement and the Security Enhancements in this Addendum.  
(c) Vendor shall provide auditability for all administrative actions, including access changes, configuration updates, and data exports, with logs retained in accordance with **Section 2.2.1(b)** above.

2.2.5 **Processing of Regulated Data Domains.**  
(a) Client will instruct Vendor to process certain **Regulated Data Domains**, including but not limited to:  
   (i) Limited health-related data (e.g., wellness program participation indicators, but not full medical records);  
   (ii) Financial transaction metadata and risk scores (excluding full payment card numbers, which shall be tokenized prior to receipt by Vendor wherever possible);  
   (iii) Location data, behavioral event streams, and preference data for end users; and  
   (iv) Certain categories of Personal Data relating to minors, where permitted by Applicable Data Protection Law and subject to safeguards specified by Client.  
(b) Vendor shall process such Regulated Data Domains strictly in accordance with **Section 11.2 (Processing Instructions)** and **Section 11.3 (Compliance with Law)** of the Parent Agreement, as further detailed in **Article XI** of this Addendum.  
(c) Vendor shall not expand the Regulated Data Domains beyond those explicitly authorized in Client’s written instructions without a prior Change Request and updated data protection impact assessment, where required by Applicable Data Protection Law.

2.2.6 **Data Residency Commitments and International Processing.**  
(a) Vendor shall maintain primary data hosting for U.S. Data Subjects within the United States, consistent with the **Territory** defined in **Section 1.21** of the Parent Agreement, unless Client explicitly instructs otherwise.  
(b) For Personal Data originating from the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, Vendor shall ensure that any transfer to a third country is covered by appropriate data transfer mechanisms under **Section 11.9 (International Transfers)** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses, UK international data transfer addendum).  
(c) Vendor shall document and maintain a **data residency matrix** specifying where key categories of Personal Data are stored and processed and shall provide such matrix to Client upon reasonable request.

2.2.7 **Professional Services and Advisory Support.**  
(a) In addition to the **Professional Services** referenced in **Section 2.2.6** and the **Professional Services Pool** in **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide expanded professional services, including:  
   (i) Data governance consulting and documentation;  
   (ii) Assistance with Data Protection Impact Assessments and privacy-by-design workshops;  
   (iii) Custom analytics and visualization design; and  
   (iv) Training programs for Client’s technical, compliance, and business users.  
(b) Such services shall be scoped and priced under this Addendum pursuant to **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** and may be further detailed in Applicable SOWs.

2.3 **Deliverables Under This Addendum.**  
Without limiting the generality of **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall, under this Addendum, at a minimum deliver:

2.3.1 **Enhanced Platform Instance(s).** One or more production-ready, multi-region Platform instances incorporating Enhanced Platform Services, high-availability configurations, and required integrations, as detailed in the Project Plan.

2.3.2 **Additional Dashboards and Reports.** At least **twenty (20)** additional standardized dashboards and **thirty (30)** additional custom reports, to be defined, prioritized, and approved by Client within one hundred twenty (120) days following the Addendum Effective Date.

2.3.3 **Integration Artifacts.** Integration specifications, mapping documents, API documentation, and test results for each Complex Integration Project.

2.3.4 **Governance and Security Documentation.** Updated data flow diagrams, security architecture overviews, and operational runbooks reflecting the Addendum Processing Activities and Security Enhancements.

2.4 **Implementation Timeline Under This Addendum.**  

2.4.1 **Addendum Project Kickoff.** Within **ten (10) Business Days** following the Addendum Effective Date, aligning with the implementation practices in **Section 2.4 (Implementation Timeline)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.2 **Initial Environment Expansion.** Within **thirty (30) Business Days** following the Addendum Effective Date, Vendor shall deploy expanded environments and begin onboarding additional data sources necessary for the Enhanced Platform Services.

2.4.3 **First Wave of Additional Dashboards and Integrations.** Within **one hundred twenty (120) days** following Client’s provision of required inputs, Vendor shall deliver:  
(a) At least **ten (10)** of the additional dashboards and **ten (10)** of the additional custom reports; and  
(b) At least **two (2)** Complex Integration Projects to production.

2.4.4 **Full Operational Readiness for Addendum Scope.** Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve full operational readiness of all Additional Services no later than **twelve (12) months** after the Addendum Effective Date, subject to Client’s timely cooperation and decisions. Delays attributable to Client shall not constitute Vendor breach, consistent with **Section 2.4 (Implementation Timeline)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities (Addendum).**  

In addition to the Client responsibilities under **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Client shall:

2.5.1 Provide timely and accurate specifications for the Addendum Processing Activities, including data classification, retention requirements, and jurisdictional constraints.

2.5.2 Ensure that any Regulated Data Domains provided to Vendor are lawfully collected and that adequate notices and consents have been obtained, consistent with **Section 2.5.2** and **Section 7.3 (Client Warranties)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.3 Designate a single point of contact (and backups) for architecture decisions, security approvals, and integration priorities.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

Vendor shall perform the Additional Services:

2.6.1 In a professional and workmanlike manner and in accordance with industry standards, consistent with **Section 2.6.1** of the Parent Agreement;

2.6.2 In compliance with Applicable Data Protection Law and the security and privacy obligations in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum; and

2.6.3 In accordance with the **SLA Enhancements** set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum, which supplement the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total contract value for the Additional Services under this Addendum is **US$4,065,610** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), exclusive of any applicable Taxes, in addition to and separate from the **US$51,474** contract value under **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that the Addendum Contract Value reflects the expanded scope of processing, enhanced Service Levels, additional security and compliance measures, and high-risk nature of the engagement as articulated in **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

The Addendum Contract Value shall be allocated as follows:

3.2.1 **Enhanced Platform Subscription Fees.**  
(a) A total of **US$2,600,000** for multi-region Platform access, advanced analytics modules, and expanded hosting and storage capacities over the Addendum Term.  
(b) These fees shall be payable in equal quarterly installments over the Term of this Addendum, unless otherwise agreed in an Applicable SOW.

3.2.2 **Complex Integration and Implementation Fees.**  
(a) A total of **US$900,000** allocated to Complex Integration Projects and initial configuration, including design, development, testing, and deployment.  
(b) Such fees shall be milestone-based, invoiced as follows:  
   (i) Twenty percent (20%) upon execution of this Addendum;  
   (ii) Thirty percent (30%) upon completion of initial environment expansion under **Section 2.4.2**;  
   (iii) Thirty percent (30%) upon production deployment of at least half of the Complex Integration Projects; and  
   (iv) Twenty percent (20%) upon final acceptance of all integration deliverables in accordance with the applicable Project Plan.

3.2.3 **Professional Services and Advisory Support Fees.**  
(a) A **dedicated professional services pool** of **US$400,000** for data governance, advisory, custom analytics, and training.  
(b) Such services shall be billed on a time-and-materials basis at Vendor’s then-current rates, applied against this pool. Overages shall require written approval by Client pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

3.2.4 **Risk and Compliance Premium.**  
A **risk and compliance premium** of **US$165,610** recognizing the extended processing of Regulated Data Domains, higher Service Levels, and Security Enhancements, payable in annual installments over the Addendum Term.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms (Net 60).**  

3.3.1 **Invoicing Schedule.**  
(a) Vendor shall invoice the Enhanced Platform Subscription Fees quarterly in advance.  
(b) Integration and implementation fees shall be invoiced upon completion of milestones as set forth in **Section 3.2.2(b)**.  
(c) Professional services shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, consistent with **Section 3.3.1(d)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(d) The risk and compliance premium shall be invoiced annually in advance on each anniversary of the Addendum Effective Date.

3.3.2 **Payment Terms.**  
Client shall pay all undisputed amounts within **sixty (60) days** of the invoice date (**Net 60**), in accordance with **Section 3.3.2 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement. Payment shall be in U.S. dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or another mutually agreed method.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

Any undisputed invoice amount not received by Vendor when due shall accrue interest at the rate set forth in **Section 3.4 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement (1.5% per month or the maximum permitted by law), and Vendor shall have the right to **suspend** all or part of the Additional Services (including SLA Enhancements) upon ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice, consistent with the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Taxes and Expenses.**  

3.5.1 Taxes shall be handled consistent with **Section 3.5 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. Client is responsible for all Taxes (excluding Taxes based on Vendor’s net income).

3.5.2 Vendor’s reasonable out-of-pocket travel and living expenses incurred in connection with on-site services requested by Client under this Addendum shall be reimbursable pursuant to **Section 3.6 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement, provided such expenses are pre-approved in writing.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  

Disputed amounts shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement. Client shall timely pay all undisputed portions of any invoice issued under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Addendum.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue in full force and effect until **May 1, 2028** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Term defined in **Section 1.20** of the Parent Agreement is not automatically extended by this Addendum; however, the Parties agree that if the Parent Agreement is renewed or extended pursuant to **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof, this Addendum may be correspondingly extended by mutual written agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Termination.**  

4.2.1 This Addendum is **subordinate** to the Parent Agreement. Termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement shall automatically result in termination of this Addendum, effective as of the Parent Agreement’s termination or expiration date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain obligations (e.g., data return/deletion) for a limited post-termination period.

4.2.2 In the event of any inconsistency between this **Section 4.2** and the termination provisions in **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement, the Parent Agreement shall control with respect to the underlying contractual relationship, and this Addendum shall be interpreted to give effect to such control.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

Consistent with **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement:

4.3.1 Client may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon providing **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to Vendor.  

4.3.2 Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to Client.  

4.3.3 Upon such termination, the termination fee specified in **Section 4.5.2** of the Parent Agreement (twenty percent (20%) of the remaining contract value) shall apply to the **outstanding Addendum Contract Value** as a reasonable estimate of Vendor’s lost profits and administrative costs, in addition to any similar obligations relating to the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause consistent with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement, in the event of material breach of this Addendum, subject to the cure periods specified therein (thirty (30) days for non-payment breaches; ten (10) Business Days for material payment defaults).

4.4.2 Termination of this Addendum for cause shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement unless the grounds for termination also constitute a material breach of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  

Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum for any reason:

4.5.1 Client shall promptly pay all Fees and reimbursable expenses accrued and payable under this Addendum through the effective date of termination, plus any applicable termination fees in accordance with **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.2 Vendor’s obligations to provide Additional Services and SLA Enhancements shall cease, but Vendor shall continue to perform any minimal services necessary to facilitate data export and transition for up to thirty (30) days, subject to mutually agreed professional services Fees.

4.5.3 Vendor shall return or delete Personal Data processed exclusively under the Addendum Processing Activities in accordance with **Section 11.7 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 11.7** of this Addendum, unless further retention is required by law.

4.5.4 Sections of this Addendum that by their nature should survive (including without limitation Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall survive termination or expiration.
