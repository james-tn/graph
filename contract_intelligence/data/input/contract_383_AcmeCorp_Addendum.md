# ARTICLE II  
# SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Parent SOW.**  
Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent SOW and **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Consulting Agreement, Acme shall perform the Addendum Services and provide the Addendum Deliverables described in this Article II. The Addendum Services are intended to:

- Extend and deepen the **Data Analytics Enablement** activities outlined in **Section 2.4.4 (Phase 4 – Data Analytics Enablement)** of the Parent SOW;
- Provide enhanced **Training, Transition & Optimization** support aligned with **Section 2.4.5 (Phase 5 – Training, Transition & Optimization)** of the Parent SOW;
- Introduce a structured **Value Realization and Benefits Tracking** workstream to measure the impact of the Transformation Program against the business objectives defined in **Section 2.2 (Objectives)** of the Parent SOW; and
- Establish a dedicated **Operational Readiness and Support Design** capability extending Acme’s responsibilities into early post‑implementation advisory support, consistent with the high‑level services described in **Sections 2.1.3 and 2.1.5 of the Consulting Agreement**.

The Addendum Services shall be performed in close coordination with the SOW Services already contemplated by the Parent SOW, and the Parties shall ensure consistency with the Project Phases, Milestones, and governance structures set forth in **Sections 2.4 and 2.9** of the Parent SOW.

2.2 **Objectives of Addendum Services.**  
The primary objectives of the Addendum Services are to:

2.2.1 **Analytics Industrialization.**  
Transition the initial analytics capabilities developed under **Section 2.3.6 (Data Models, Dashboards, and KPI Catalog)** of the Parent SOW into a scalable, sustainable analytics operating model, including standardized development processes, reusable components, and governance for ongoing analytics enhancement.

2.2.2 **Enhanced Training and Adoption.**  
Design and deliver additional training, coaching, and adoption support beyond the baseline training described in **Section 2.3.7 of the Parent SOW**, with a focus on power users, analytics champions, and business unit leadership to drive adoption of dashboards, KPIs, and new business processes.

2.2.3 **Value Realization Framework.**  
Define and implement a value realization framework for the Transformation Program, including alignment to Contoso’s key performance indicators (KPIs) cataloged under **Section 2.3.6 of the Parent SOW**, creation of benefits baselines, and periodic measurement and reporting of improvements in efficiency, revenue, cost, and risk.

2.2.4 **Operational Readiness and Hypercare Design.**  
Extend the planning around “hypercare” referenced in **Section 2.4.5** of the Parent SOW by designing detailed support processes, escalation paths, runbooks, and knowledge repositories to be used by Contoso’s internal support teams and any third‑party support providers.

2.2.5 **Continuous Improvement Roadmap.**  
Develop a rolling 18‑month roadmap of prioritized improvements and enhancements, aligned with the three‑year Technology Roadmap referenced in **Section 2.3.2 of the Parent SOW**, but focused on incremental optimization and value capture after the initial go‑lives.

2.3 **Addendum Deliverables.**  
Without limiting the broader concept of Deliverables as defined in the Parent SOW and Consulting Agreement, Acme shall provide at least the following Addendum Deliverables (each to be further specified in a mutually agreed project plan and/or attached exhibit, if needed):

2.3.1 **Analytics Operating Model Playbook.**  
A documented operating model for analytics including roles and responsibilities, demand intake procedures, development standards, release management processes, and quality controls.

2.3.2 **Advanced KPI and Benefits Tracking Dashboard.**  
One or more dashboards that extend the KPI catalog under **Section 2.3.6 of the Parent SOW** to include baseline metrics, target values, benefit categories (e.g., cost reduction, revenue growth, risk reduction), and trend analysis over time.

2.3.3 **Change Adoption and Training Toolkit.**  
Expanded training decks, self‑paced learning modules, quick‑reference cards, and facilitator guides extending the training materials contemplated under **Section 2.3.7 of the Parent SOW**, tailored for end users, power users, and managers.

2.3.4 **Operational Runbooks and Support Processes.**  
Detailed operational runbooks, RACI matrices, incident triage guides, and escalation paths to be used by Contoso’s support teams, aligned with the Service Levels described in **Article XII** of this Addendum and the Parent SOW.

2.3.5 **Value Realization Baseline and Measurement Reports.**  
A baseline value realization report and at least three (3) periodic measurement reports during the Addendum Term, each summarizing observed benefits, supporting assumptions, and recommendations to further increase value realization.

2.3.6 **Continuous Improvement Roadmap Document.**  
A prioritized roadmap of enhancements and process optimizations with estimated effort, expected benefits, dependencies, and recommended timing, aligned with the Project Plan referenced in **Section 1.13 and Section 2.4** of the Parent SOW.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  
The Addendum Services will be organized into the following workstreams and indicative timelines within the Addendum Term. Detailed dates, dependencies, and resource allocations shall be incorporated into the Project Plan referenced in **Section 1.13 of the Parent SOW**, as updated by mutual agreement within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date.

2.4.1 **Workstream A – Analytics Operating Model (Months 1–4).**  
- Activities: stakeholder workshops, current process review, design of operating model, documentation of playbook.  
- Milestones: (a) Draft Analytics Operating Model Playbook delivered; (b) Review and feedback session completed; (c) Final Playbook accepted in accordance with **Section 2.6 of the Parent SOW**.

2.4.2 **Workstream B – Training and Adoption (Months 2–10).**  
- Activities: needs assessment, curriculum design, content development, training sessions, coaching circles, feedback surveys.  
- Milestones: (a) Training curriculum and schedule approved; (b) Completion of first training wave; (c) Completion of final training wave with adoption survey; (d) Training and Adoption Close‑Out Report accepted.

2.4.3 **Workstream C – Value Realization (Months 3–18).**  
- Activities: KPI alignment, baseline data collection, definition of benefit categories, measurement data integration, periodic reporting.  
- Milestones: (a) Value Realization Baseline Report delivered; (b) First periodic Measurement Report delivered; (c) Second and third Measurement Reports delivered; (d) Final Value Realization Summary accepted.

2.4.4 **Workstream D – Operational Readiness and Support Design (Months 4–12).**  
- Activities: operating model design for support, runbook creation, RACI mapping, integration with existing incident management tools, mock drills.  
- Milestones: (a) Draft Operational Runbooks delivered; (b) Completion of mock support drill; (c) Final Support Design Package accepted.

2.4.5 **Workstream E – Continuous Improvement Roadmap (Months 12–18).**  
- Activities: gap analysis, stakeholder prioritization workshops, cost‑benefit estimation, roadmap documentation.  
- Milestones: (a) Draft Continuous Improvement Roadmap delivered; (b) Executive review and prioritization session completed; (c) Final Roadmap accepted.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
The performance standards set forth in **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.4 of the Consulting Agreement** shall apply to the Addendum Services. In addition:

2.5.1 Acme shall perform the Addendum Services with the same level of professional skill and care described in **Section 7.2.1 of the Parent SOW** and shall assign appropriately qualified personnel, including Key Personnel defined in **Exhibit B of the Parent SOW**, where applicable.

2.5.2 Acme shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the milestones listed in **Section 2.4** above and as reflected in the updated Project Plan, recognizing that delays caused by Client Dependencies or Force Majeure Events shall excuse corresponding delays.

2.5.3 Any SOW Service Levels relevant to ongoing support or analytics operations, as described in **Article XII of the Parent SOW**, shall be extended, as applicable, to cover the Addendum Services, as further specified in **Article XII of this Addendum**.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities and Dependencies.**  
In addition to the responsibilities in **Section 2.7 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 2.5 of the Consulting Agreement**, Contoso shall:

2.6.1 Provide timely access to business stakeholders in finance, sales, operations, and IT for value realization, analytics, and training activities.

2.6.2 Ensure that relevant data required for KPI baselining and ongoing measurement is made available in a reasonably usable format and with appropriate data quality controls.

2.6.3 Designate a “Value Realization Sponsor” and “Analytics Product Owner” to make decisions on KPI definitions, thresholds, and prioritization of improvements.

2.6.4 Facilitate use of Contoso’s collaboration, learning management, and incident management tools necessary to host training content and support runbooks.

2.6.5 Review and provide consolidated feedback on Addendum Deliverables within the ten (10) Business Day review period specified in **Section 2.6.1 of the Parent SOW**, unless otherwise mutually agreed in writing.

2.7 **Governance and Reporting.**  
The PGC described in **Section 2.9 of the Parent SOW** shall be expanded to oversee this Addendum:

2.7.1 The PGC agenda shall include status of the Addendum Services, review of Addendum Deliverables, and tracking of Addendum Service Levels.

2.7.2 Acme shall provide monthly status reports for the Addendum Services, integrated with the status reports described in **Section 2.3.8 of the Parent SOW**, including progress against milestones, key risks, issues, and dependencies.

2.7.3 Major scope or scheduling issues related to this Addendum shall be escalated through the PGC before invoking the formal dispute resolution procedures in **Article XIV** of this Addendum or **Article XIV of the Parent SOW**.

# ARTICLE IV  
# TERM AND TERMINATION

The Addendum Term shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue until the Addendum Expiration Date, unless earlier terminated as provided in this Article IV or pursuant to the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement.

4.3.1 In contrast to the 180‑day notice period for termination for convenience described in **Section 4.3 of the Parent SOW**, and in recognition of the **Medium Risk Profile** for this Addendum, either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.3.2 Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement unless so stated in the notice and agreed by the Parties.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 4.4 of the Consulting Agreement**, including in the event of:

4.4.1 A material breach of this Addendum by the other Party that remains uncured thirty (30) days after written notice specifying the breach; or

4.4.2 An insolvency or bankruptcy event affecting the other Party as described in the Parent SOW or Consulting Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  
Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum:

4.5.1 Acme shall cease performance of the Addendum Services, except as reasonably necessary to transition or wind down such services, consistent with **Section 4.5.1 of the Parent SOW**.

4.5.2 Contoso shall pay Acme for all Addendum Services properly performed and approved expenses incurred up to the effective termination date, as well as non‑cancelable commitments that cannot be reasonably mitigated, consistent with **Section 4.5.2 of the Parent SOW**.

4.5.3 No early termination or disengagement fee specific to this Addendum shall apply unless expressly stated in a mutually executed Change Order or fee schedule.

4.5.4 Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information related to the Addendum Services in accordance with **Article V of this Addendum** and **Article V of the Parent SOW**.

4.6 **Survival.**  
Any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall survive, together with any corresponding surviving provisions of the Parent SOW and the Consulting Agreement.
