## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) This Addendum is executed **under and subject to** the terms and conditions of the **Parent Agreement (PUR-GAM-202510-133 / contract_133)**, as expressly required by **Section 2.10(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) The Additional Services and Additional Software Modules described herein are deemed part of the “Software”, “Deliverables”, and “Services” as such terms are used in **Article II of the Parent Agreement**, and shall be governed by all applicable provisions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Articles II, III, V, VI, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, and XVI**.

2.2 **Purpose and Business Context.**  
The purpose of this Addendum is to extend the deployment and capabilities of Gamma’s enterprise platform originally contemplated under the Parent Agreement, to support Contoso’s expanded regional and global operations. In particular, this Addendum contemplates:

- Deployment of additional analytics, reporting, and integration modules (“Additional Software Modules”) to support cross-region data consolidation and advanced reporting;  
- Provision of Additional Services, including configuration, integration with Contoso’s existing systems, data migration support, and user training; and  
- Ongoing Extended Support Services for the Additional Software Modules in accordance with the SLA framework set out in **Article XII of the Parent Agreement**.

2.3 **Description of Additional Software Modules.**  
Gamma shall provide Contoso with access to and the right to use the following Additional Software Modules (collectively, the “Additional Software Modules”), as part of the Platform referenced in **Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement**:

(a) **Global Analytics Module** – enabling consolidated real-time analytics across multiple Contoso business units, including customizable dashboards and advanced querying tools;  

(b) **Regulatory Reporting Module** – providing pre-configured templates and workflows for compliance reporting in multiple jurisdictions (including Singapore, the EU, and the U.S.), along with audit-trail capabilities;  

(c) **Integration Gateway Extension** – extending existing integration capabilities to additional third-party systems used by Contoso, including additional API connectors and ETL pipelines; and  

(d) **Admin & Security Enhancements** – including fine-grained role-based access controls, advanced logging, and extensible configuration for single sign-on (SSO) integration.

All Additional Software Modules shall be considered “Software” under **Section 1.17** and shall be subject to the license grant and restrictions set forth in **Article VI of the Parent Agreement** and **Article VI of this Addendum**.

2.4 **Deliverables.**  
Gamma shall develop, configure, and deliver to Contoso the following Deliverables in connection with the Additional Services:

(a) **Solution Design Document** – capturing business and technical requirements, architecture diagrams, integration mappings, and configuration specifications for the Additional Software Modules;  

(b) **Configuration Artifacts** – including parameter configurations, template files, and rule sets for the Global Analytics and Regulatory Reporting Modules;  

(c) **Integration Components** – including APIs, connectors, transformation scripts, and related documentation for the Integration Gateway Extension;  

(d) **Test Plans and Test Reports** – including UAT scripts, test data sets (excluding live production Personal Data where reasonably avoidable), test results, and defect logs;  

(e) **Training Materials** – slide decks, quick-reference guides, and recorded sessions for end users and administrators; and  

(f) **Operational Runbooks** – documenting standard operating procedures, escalation paths, and monitoring guidelines for production operations of the Additional Software Modules.

Deliverables shall be subject to the acceptance procedures described in **Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement**, as supplemented in **Section 2.9** of this Addendum.

2.5 **Additional Services.**  
Gamma shall provide the following Additional Services to Contoso:

(a) Requirements gathering and solution design specific to the Additional Software Modules and their integration with Contoso’s environment;  
(b) Configuration and set-up of the Additional Software Modules in Contoso’s designated environments (dev, test, staging, and production as applicable);  
(c) Integration with Contoso’s existing core systems, data warehouses, identity management systems, and reporting tools;  
(d) Data migration planning and limited migration assistance for initial load and final cutover;  
(e) Support for system testing and UAT, including defect triage and remediation;  
(f) Go-live preparation, cutover support, and post-go-live hypercare support for a period of thirty (30) days after each go-live; and  
(g) Training sessions (remote or on-site) for up to four (4) cohorts of Contoso personnel.

2.6 **Implementation Plan and Timeline.**  
Gamma shall deliver the Additional Services in accordance with a detailed Implementation Plan, to be mutually agreed within fifteen (15) Business Days of the Addendum Effective Date. The Implementation Plan shall, at a minimum, include:

(a) A work breakdown structure outlining all major tasks and sub-tasks;  
(b) A timeline specifying planned start and end dates for each phase (design, build, test, deploy);  
(c) Identification of key Milestones tied to Deliverable completion and payment events;  
(d) Resource allocation, including key Gamma and Contoso personnel and their roles; and  
(e) Dependencies on Contoso’s responsibilities under **Section 2.9 of the Parent Agreement** (e.g., timely access to systems and data).

Gamma acknowledges that **time is of the essence** with respect to critical Milestones (as per **Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement**) relating to regulatory reporting deadlines, which shall be specifically designated as “Critical Milestones” in the Implementation Plan.

2.7 **Milestones.**  
The Parties anticipate the following primary Milestones under this Addendum (each a “Milestone”):

- **Milestone 1 – Design Completion:** Delivery and acceptance of the Solution Design Document.  
- **Milestone 2 – Configuration Completion:** Completion of configuration of the Additional Software Modules in staging environment.  
- **Milestone 3 – Integration & UAT Completion:** Successful completion of integration testing and UAT, evidenced by signed UAT completion report.  
- **Milestone 4 – Production Go-Live (Phase 1):** Go-live of Global Analytics Module and Integration Gateway Extension for initial regions.  
- **Milestone 5 – Production Go-Live (Phase 2):** Go-live of Regulatory Reporting Module and remaining regions.  
- **Milestone 6 – Post-Go-Live Acceptance:** Completion of post-go-live hypercare period and resolution of all Severity 1 and Severity 2 defects.

Each Milestone will be associated with specific Deliverables and acceptance criteria as set out in the Implementation Plan and **Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement**.

2.8 **Performance Standards.**  
Gamma shall perform the Additional Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards, and in accordance with **Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement**. Specifically, Gamma shall:

(a) Assign personnel with appropriate skills and experience;  
(b) Use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain continuity of key personnel; and  
(c) Comply with all Applicable Laws and Contoso security and compliance policies notified to Gamma in writing.

2.9 **Acceptance of Deliverables and Milestones.**  
(a) Acceptance of Deliverables and Milestones under this Addendum shall follow the process set out in **Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement**, with an Acceptance Period of not less than ten (10) Business Days per Deliverable or Milestone.  

(b) If Contoso reasonably determines that a Deliverable does not conform to the agreed acceptance criteria, Contoso shall provide Gamma with a written notice of rejection, specifying all material non-conformities in reasonable detail.  

(c) Gamma shall promptly correct such non-conformities at its own cost and resubmit the Deliverable for re-testing and acceptance. If a Deliverable or Milestone fails acceptance after three (3) attempts, Contoso may exercise the remedies set out in **Section 2.8(c) of the Parent Agreement**, including price reduction or termination of the relevant portion of this Addendum, without prejudice to other rights and remedies.

2.10 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  
Contoso’s responsibilities under **Section 2.9 of the Parent Agreement** apply equally to this Addendum and include:

(a) Providing timely access to relevant personnel, systems, and information;  
(b) Designating a project manager to coordinate interactions with Gamma;  
(c) Obtaining and maintaining any third-party licenses, consents, or approvals necessary for integration with non-Gamma systems; and  
(d) Performing any specific tasks identified in the Implementation Plan as Contoso’s responsibilities.

Gamma shall not be liable for delays or failures to meet Milestones or Service Levels to the extent caused by Contoso’s failure to fulfill its responsibilities, provided Gamma has given prompt written notice of such dependencies and their impact.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
(a) The total maximum fees payable by Contoso to Gamma under this Addendum shall not exceed **US$195,310** (the “Addendum Contract Value”), inclusive of all license/subscription fees for the Additional Software Modules, implementation/professional services fees, and maintenance/support fees as described herein.  

(b) For clarity, this Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US$3,839,415** specified in **Section 1.6 and Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement, and together they form the aggregate maximum fees payable under the Parent Agreement as amended by this Addendum. Any increase beyond the Addendum Contract Value shall require a further written amendment or Change Request in accordance with **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement** and **Article XIII of this Addendum**.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  
Unless otherwise agreed in a signed Change Request, the fees under this Addendum shall be structured as follows:

(a) **Additional Software License/Subscription Fees:**  
   - A one-time license or first-year subscription fee for the Additional Software Modules in the amount of **US$90,000**.  
   - Any subsequent annual subscription/maintenance fees for such modules shall be specified in a mutually agreed renewal schedule, but shall not exceed Gamma’s then-current standard rates, subject to any caps or discounts agreed in writing.

(b) **Implementation and Professional Services Fees:**  
   - Fixed-price fees for the Additional Services in the amount of **US$80,000**, covering requirements analysis, configuration, integration, testing support, and go-live assistance.  

(c) **Extended Support and Maintenance Fees:**  
   - Support fees for Extended Support Services in the amount of **US$20,000** for the initial support period of twelve (12) months following the first production go-live, apportioned as mutually agreed.  

(d) **Contingency / Change Budget (Optional):**  
   - An optional contingency budget of **US$5,310** reserved for approved Change Requests under **Article XIII**, to be used only with Contoso’s prior written approval.

3.3 **Payment Milestones.**  
Gamma may invoice the Addendum fees in accordance with the following milestones, subject to acceptance under **Section 2.9**:

- **20%** of the Addendum Contract Value upon Contoso’s acceptance of Milestone 1 (Design Completion);  
- **25%** of the Addendum Contract Value upon Contoso’s acceptance of Milestone 2 (Configuration Completion);  
- **25%** of the Addendum Contract Value upon Contoso’s acceptance of Milestone 3 (Integration & UAT Completion);  
- **20%** of the Addendum Contract Value upon Contoso’s acceptance of Milestone 4 and Milestone 5 (Production Go-Live Phases 1 and 2);  
- **10%** of the Addendum Contract Value upon Contoso’s acceptance of Milestone 6 (Post-Go-Live Acceptance).

3.4 **Payment Terms; Net 45.**  
(a) Consistent with **Section 3.3(b) of the Parent Agreement**, all undisputed amounts invoiced under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) calendar days** from the date of receipt of a correct and undisputed invoice (“**Net 45**”).  

(b) Invoices related to this Addendum shall (i) reference **Contract Reference Nos. PUR-GAM-202510-133 and ADD-GAM-202510-156**, (ii) identify this Addendum by title, and (iii) specify the relevant Milestone, Deliverables, and fee components.

3.5 **Taxes.**  
Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement**. All fees under this Addendum are exclusive of applicable taxes, and such taxes shall be separately stated on Gamma’s invoices.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
(a) Unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing, Gamma shall bear its own expenses in performing the Additional Services under this Addendum.  

(b) Any travel or out-of-pocket expenses that are pre-approved in writing by Contoso shall be reimbursable in accordance with **Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement**, subject to Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies and upon provision of appropriate receipts and documentation.

3.7 **Late Payments and Disputed Amounts.**  
The provisions of **Sections 3.7 and 3.8 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply to all invoices issued under this Addendum, including interest for late payments (after a ten (10) Business Day cure period) and the procedures for disputing invoices.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Addendum.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue in full force and effect until **21 January 2030**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the Parent Agreement. The Addendum Term is coterminous with, and in no event shall extend beyond, the Term of the Parent Agreement under **Section 4.1** thereof, unless the Parent Agreement is renewed and the Parties expressly agree in writing to extend this Addendum.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Any renewal of the Additional Software Modules or Extended Support Services beyond the Addendum Term shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties, consistent with **Section 4.2 of the Parent Agreement**, and shall be documented in a further amendment or addendum referencing both **PUR-GAM-202510-133** and **ADD-GAM-202510-156**.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
(a) Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience at any time upon not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Gamma, consistent with the client-friendly rights granted under **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement**.  

(b) Gamma may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, and only if (i) no Critical Milestones remain outstanding under the Implementation Plan, or (ii) Contoso consents in writing to termination of any such outstanding Milestones.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement in relation to the Additional Services or Additional Software Modules and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of a written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail, in accordance with **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.5 **Termination for Persistent SLA Failures.**  
Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, upon thirty (30) days’ written notice if Gamma fails to meet critical Service Levels (as defined in **Article XII**) related to the Additional Software Modules in three (3) or more consecutive months or in any four (4) months in a rolling twelve (12)-month period and fails to implement a mutually agreed remediation plan, consistent with **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing that specific rights (such as perpetual licenses) shall survive.  

(b) Upon termination of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the provisions of **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** and **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Additional Software Modules and Additional Services under this Addendum.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) Contoso shall pay Gamma for all Additional Services properly performed and Deliverables properly delivered and accepted as of the effective date of termination, subject to any set-off rights or credits arising from Gamma’s breach;  
(b) Any prepaid fees for Extended Support Services not yet rendered shall be refunded to Contoso on a pro-rata basis;  
(c) Gamma shall promptly return or securely destroy all Contoso Confidential Information and Contoso Data related to the Additional Software Modules in accordance with **Article XI**; and  
(d) Any perpetual or fully paid-up licenses expressly granted under **Article VI** for Deliverables or Software components shall survive, in accordance with **Section 4.6(e) of the Parent Agreement**.

4.8 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration, including but not limited to **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, XVI**, and Sections 3.5, 3.6, 4.6, 4.7, shall survive.
