## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW.**  

  a. This Work Order **adds scope** to the Parent SOW by defining Extended Services and Extended Deliverables that are in addition to, and not in substitution of, the Services and Deliverables described in **Article II** of the Parent SOW.  
  b. Except as expressly stated herein, all obligations, assumptions, and Client responsibilities defined in **Sections 2.5, 2.6, and 2.7** of the Parent SOW shall apply equally to the Extended Services performed under this Work Order.  
  c. The Extended Services shall be treated as part of the same overall **Project** described in **Section 1.13** and **Section 2.1** of the Parent SOW, with adjusted timelines and additional milestones as set forth herein.

2.2 **Overview of Extended Services.**

Vendor shall provide the following Extended Services under this Work Order:

  a. **Advanced Analytics Expansion.** Expansion of the analytics capabilities originally contemplated under **Section 2.3(f)** of the Parent SOW, including:  
     i. development of predictive and prescriptive models for revenue forecasting, churn prediction, and operational risk scoring;  
     ii. implementation of machine learning pipelines integrated with the data warehouses/data lakes designed under **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent SOW;  
     iii. design and deployment of an “Executive Analytics Hub” aggregating cross-functional KPIs from finance, operations, and customer service; and  
     iv. configuration of self-service advanced analytics capabilities for power users, including parameterized reports and analytical “sandboxes.”

  b. **Workflow and Process Automation Expansion.** Extension of “Workflow Automation” under **Section 2.3(g)** of the Parent SOW by:  
     i. designing and implementing at least ten (10) additional end-to-end workflows across quote-to-cash, incident escalation, and vendor onboarding processes;  
     ii. provisioning complex conditional routing, multi-level approvals, and escalation triggers; and  
     iii. integrating automated workflows with Client’s ERP and CRM systems already targeted for integration under **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent SOW.

  c. **Data Quality and Governance Enhancements.** Building upon the data integration and migration work described in **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent SOW, Vendor shall:  
     i. implement automated data quality rules, monitoring, and scoring;  
     ii. establish and configure a data catalog and business glossary; and  
     iii. produce governance dashboards showing data quality trends, stewardship assignments, and exception resolution metrics.

  d. **Performance Optimization and Scalability.** In alignment with the “Performance Standards” outlined in **Section 2.5** of the Parent SOW, Vendor shall:  
     i. perform capacity and performance tuning of data pipelines, queries, and reports;  
     ii. design and implement scaling patterns for peak workloads; and  
     iii. establish performance baselines and thresholds tied to Service Levels under **Article XII** of the Parent SOW, as extended herein.

  e. **Extended Managed Support and Optimization.** Building upon **Phase 6 – Managed Support and Optimization** (Section 2.2(f) of the Parent SOW), Vendor shall:  
     i. provide dedicated optimization sprints each quarter during the Term of this Work Order;  
     ii. maintain and enhance Extended Deliverables, including models, workflows, and governance artifacts; and  
     iii. provide continuous improvement recommendations and implement approved enhancements via the change management process.

2.3 **Project Phases and Timelines under this Work Order.**

The Extended Services shall be organized into the following phases, which shall run **concurrently and in coordination** with the existing phases under the Parent SOW:

  a. **WO Phase A – Extended Design and Backlog Definition (Estimated: November 1, 2024 – January 31, 2025).**  
     - Activities: deep-dive design sessions, backlog creation per Program Increment, refinement of Specifications, selection of candidate processes for Enhanced Workflow Automations.  
     - Dependencies: Requires Client’s timely provision of Client Materials as per **Section 2.6(a)** of the Parent SOW.  
     - Key outputs: Extended Design Addendum, PI Roadmap, Updated Architecture diagrams.

  b. **WO Phase B – Build, Configuration, and Modeling (Estimated: February 1, 2025 – October 31, 2025).**  
     - Activities: iterative development of Enhanced Analytics Features and Enhanced Workflow Automations; data quality enhancement implementation; performance optimization.  
     - Execution: organized into **three (3) PIs**, each approximately twelve (12) weeks, with agreed backlog items and acceptance criteria at the start of each PI.  
     - Alignment: complements **Phase 3 – Build and Configuration** in **Section 2.2(c)** of the Parent SOW.

  c. **WO Phase C – Integrated Testing and UAT (Estimated: November 1, 2025 – February 28, 2026).**  
     - Activities: integration testing with existing Solution components, regression testing, performance testing; support for Client’s UAT as described in **Section 2.2(d)** of the Parent SOW.  
     - Deliverables: consolidated test plans and test summary reports that incorporate Extended Deliverables, in addition to those required under **Section 2.4(g)** of the Parent SOW.

  d. **WO Phase D – Extended Go-Live, Stabilization, and Optimization (Estimated: March 1, 2026 – May 22, 2026).**  
     - Activities: phased go-live of Enhanced Analytics Features and Enhanced Workflow Automations, incremental rollout to business units, and heightened hypercare support aligned with **Phase 5 – Go-Live and Stabilization** in **Section 2.2(e)** of the Parent SOW.  
     - Continuity: during this period, Vendor will also provide Extended Managed Support as an overlay to the Parent SOW’s Phase 6 activities.

2.4 **Extended Deliverables.**

Vendor shall provide at least the following Extended Deliverables (in addition to Deliverables listed in **Section 2.4** of the Parent SOW):

  a. **Extended Design Addendum** documenting additional solution components, data models, workflow diagrams, and integration specifications, referencing the **Detailed Design Documentation** under **Section 2.4(b)** of the Parent SOW.

  b. **Predictive and Prescriptive Model Suite**, including model documentation, training data specifications, and deployment runbooks, integrated with the **Configured Platform Environments** referenced in **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent SOW.

  c. **Executive Analytics Hub Dashboards**, including at least six (6) executive-level dashboards spanning financial, operational, and customer service KPIs, delivered in accordance with the reporting framework described in **Section 2.3(f)** of the Parent SOW.

  d. **Additional Workflow Configuration Packages**, covering at least ten (10) new or materially enhanced workflows, each with associated **Workflow Configuration Documentation** as per **Section 2.4(f)** of the Parent SOW.

  e. **Data Quality Rules Catalog and Monitoring Dashboards**, integrated with existing Solution components, including documentation of data validation logic and remediation processes.

  f. **Performance Optimization Report and Tuning Scripts**, detailing baseline performance metrics, tuning actions taken, and resulting performance improvements.

  g. **Quarterly Optimization Sprint Reports**, summarizing completed optimization work, backlog items addressed, incidents, and Service Level performance for the Extended Services.

  h. **Updated Operational Runbooks**, supplementing those described in **Section 2.4(i)** of the Parent SOW, covering the operation and support of Extended Deliverables.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**

In addition to the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.5** of the Parent SOW, Vendor shall:

  a. ensure that all Extended Deliverables meet the mutually agreed Specifications and acceptance criteria documented in the Extended Design Addendum;  
  b. adhere to agreed timelines set forth in **Section 2.3** of this Work Order, subject to change management under **Article XIII**;  
  c. maintain development and deployment practices consistent with industry standards and the practices described in **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent SOW; and  
  d. ensure that the Extended Services do not materially degrade the performance or availability of the Solution as governed by **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent SOW and **Article XII** of this Work Order.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities (Supplemental).**

In addition to Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW, Client shall:

  a. designate business owners for each Enhanced Analytics Feature and Enhanced Workflow Automation to provide timely decisions and sign-offs;  
  b. provide access to additional data sources and systems needed for predictive modeling and new workflows;  
  c. participate in PI planning sessions and sprint reviews for Extended Services; and  
  d. ensure that relevant stakeholders attend training and knowledge transfer sessions relating to Extended Deliverables.

2.7 **Assumptions (Supplemental).**

The Fees and timelines in this Work Order are based on the following additional assumptions (in addition to those in **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW):

  a. the number of new data sources does not exceed eight (8) and the incremental data volume does not increase by more than thirty percent (30%) beyond the baseline assumed in the Parent SOW;  
  b. Client will provide required business SMEs for PI planning and design workshops within agreed timeframes;  
  c. no additional regulatory or compliance requirements outside those referenced in **Section 1.8 (Data Protection Laws)** of the Parent SOW are imposed that materially affect design or implementation; and  
  d. any significant deviation from these assumptions shall be addressed through the change management process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Work Order.

  b. **WO Phase B – Build, Configuration, and Modeling (US$1,050,000).**  
     - Twenty percent (20%) (US$210,000) upon completion of the first PI and delivery of agreed backlog items for that PI.  
     - Thirty percent (30%) (US$315,000) upon completion of the second PI.  
     - Thirty percent (30%) (US$315,000) upon completion of the third PI.  
     - Twenty percent (20%) (US$210,000) upon delivery of the Predictive and Prescriptive Model Suite and Executive Analytics Hub Dashboards.

  c. **WO Phase C – Integrated Testing and UAT (US$320,000).**  
     - Fifty percent (50%) (US$160,000) upon completion of integrated system testing and delivery of consolidated test plans and test reports.  
     - Fifty percent (50%) (US$160,000) upon Client’s written acceptance of Extended Deliverables based on successful UAT, applying the same acceptance principles as in **Section 3.3(d)** of the Parent SOW.

  d. **WO Phase D – Extended Go-Live and Stabilization (US$300,000).**  
     - Fifty percent (50%) (US$150,000) upon completion of initial extended go-live for at least fifty percent (50%) of the targeted user population.  
     - Fifty percent (50%) (US$150,000) upon completion of the stabilization period of sixty (60) days following extended go-live and delivery of an Extended Stabilization Summary Report.

3.4 **Payment of Extended Managed Support and Optimization Fees.**

  a. The extended managed support and optimization portion of **US$920,000** for the Term of this Work Order shall be invoiced in advance in **quarterly installments of US$65,000**, commencing on March 1, 2025, with a final installment pro-rated through the Expiration Date (May 22, 2026).  
  b. These amounts are **in addition to** the managed support fees described in **Section 3.4** of the Parent SOW (US$30,000 per quarter), and both sets of fees shall apply concurrently to the extent the time periods overlap.

3.5 **Payment Terms.**

All undisputed amounts invoiced by Vendor under this Work Order shall be due and payable by Client within **forty-five (45) days** of Client’s receipt of the applicable invoice (**“Net 45”**), in U.S. dollars, via wire transfer or other method agreed by the Parties, consistent with **Section 3.5** of the Parent SOW.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.**

This Work Order shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent SOW, shall remain in full force and effect until **May 22, 2026** (the **“Expiration Date”**).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW Term.**

  a. The Term of this Work Order is **within and coextensive with** the Term of the Parent SOW, which, under **Section 4.1** of the Parent SOW, expires on April 30, 2027.  
  b. Notwithstanding the foregoing, if the Parent SOW is terminated prior to its scheduled Expiration Date, this Work Order shall automatically terminate effective as of the same date, subject to **Section 4.5** below.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

Either Party may terminate this Work Order for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. Termination for convenience under this Work Order shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent SOW unless expressly stated in the termination notice and agreed by the Parties.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

Either Party may terminate this Work Order immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

  a. materially breaches any provision of this Work Order or any material provision of the Parent SOW as it relates to the Extended Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice specifying the breach; or  
  b. becomes subject to any of the insolvency events described in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent SOW.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent SOW.**

Upon expiration or termination of this Work Order:

  a. Vendor shall cease performing the Extended Services, except as necessary to effect an orderly transition if requested by Client, in line with **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent SOW;  
  b. Client shall pay Vendor for all Extended Services performed and approved Extended Deliverables provided up to the effective date of termination, plus any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Vendor with Client’s prior written consent, consistent with **Section 4.3** and **Section 4.6(b)** of the Parent SOW;  
  c. each Party shall return or, at the other Party’s written request, securely destroy Confidential Information related to this Work Order in accordance with **Section 4.6(c)** and **Article V** of the Parent SOW; and  
  d. termination of this Work Order shall not, by itself, terminate or otherwise affect the continuing obligations of the Parties under the Parent SOW regarding Services and Deliverables not covered by this Work Order.

4.6 **Survival.**

The following provisions of this Work Order shall survive expiration or termination: **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XVI (General Provisions)**, and any payment obligations accrued prior to termination. These survival provisions are in addition to those identified in **Section 4.8** of the Parent SOW.
