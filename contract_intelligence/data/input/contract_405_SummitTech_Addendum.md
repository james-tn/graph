## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Article II supplements and further details the Services described in **Article II – Scope of Services / Work** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of any conflict between the Parent Agreement and this Addendum with respect to the scope of the Additional Processing Activities, the terms of this Addendum shall prevail solely with respect to such Additional Processing Activities; otherwise, the Parent Agreement shall control.

2.1.2 For clarity, this Addendum does not diminish or restrict Summit Tech’s obligations under **Section 2.3 (Performance Standards)** and **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement. All such obligations continue to apply to the Addendum Services.

2.2 **Data Lake Environment Implementation.**

2.2.1 **Provisioning and Architecture.** Summit Tech shall design, provision, and maintain a dedicated **Data Lake Environment** as part of the Platform described in **Section 2.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement. The Data Lake Environment shall support:

- ingestion of structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data (including CSV, JSON, XML, log files, and API-based feeds);  
- storage tiers classified according to Contoso’s Data Classification Policy;  
- secure partitioning of data by Business Unit and region, where required by Data Protection Laws;  
- role-based access control integrated with Contoso’s identity provider.

2.2.2 **Data Sources.** The Data Lake Environment shall initially support the following categories of data sources (which may include Personal Data as defined in **Section 1.13** of the Parent Agreement):

- **Customer Data:** Names, contact details, account identifiers, transaction histories, and online identifiers;  
- **HR Data:** Employee identifiers, job titles, performance metrics, and limited sensitive data (e.g., diversity characteristics) where justified under Data Protection Laws;  
- **Supplier and Partner Data:** Contact details and contract-related metadata;  
- **Operational Telemetry Data:** System logs, device identifiers, IP addresses, and event metadata.

2.2.3 **Processing Activities.** Summit Tech shall Process Personal Data within the Data Lake Environment for the following purposes, consistent with **Section 2.2(a) (Nature and Purpose)** of the Parent Agreement:

- data ingestion, normalization, and schema mapping;  
- data quality checks, deduplication, and reconciliation;  
- transformation and enrichment with reference data;  
- preparation of datasets for use by the Marketing Analytics Module and HR Analytics Module;  
- secure exposure of curated datasets to Contoso’s authorized analysts and BI tools.

2.2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.** In addition to the **Initial Implementation** timeline in **Section 2.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement, Summit Tech shall:

- complete the core Data Lake Environment provisioning within **ninety (90) days** of the Addendum Effective Date;  
- onboard at least two initial Business Units (marketing and HR) within **one hundred twenty (120) days** of the Addendum Effective Date;  
- deliver environment configuration documentation, including data flow diagrams and retention configurations, within **thirty (30) days** after each Business Unit onboarding.

2.3 **Marketing Analytics Module.**

2.3.1 **Scope.** Summit Tech shall enable and configure the **Marketing Analytics Module** within the Platform, extending the capabilities described in **Section 2.1(a) and (f)** of the Parent Agreement to support:

- customer segmentation based on demographics, behavioral data, and transactional history;  
- campaign attribution analysis across email, web, and offline channels;  
- automated generation of marketing performance dashboards;  
- support for A/B testing analysis and recommendation of optimized campaigns.

2.3.2 **Data Subjects and Personal Data Types.** The Marketing Analytics Module may involve Processing of Personal Data relating to:

- Contoso customers and prospects;  
- website and mobile app users;  
- loyalty program members.

Types of Personal Data may include: contact details, purchase histories, browsing behavior, device identifiers, IP addresses, and marketing preferences. These are consistent with the categories specified in **Section 2.2(b) (Types of Personal Data)** of the Parent Agreement, but they are here grouped and configured specifically for marketing analytics.

2.3.3 **Use Restrictions.** Summit Tech shall not use Personal Data processed in the Marketing Analytics Module for its own independent marketing purposes, consistent with **Section 6.5 (License to Summit Tech)** and **Section 11.13 (CCPA/CPRA Obligations)** of the Parent Agreement. Summit Tech shall implement measures to respect Contoso’s suppression lists and opt-out preferences as communicated by Contoso.

2.3.4 **Deliverables.** Summit Tech shall deliver:

- a set of standard marketing dashboards (minimum of eight dashboards) covering customer journey, conversion funnels, and campaign ROI;  
- data models and documentation for at least four key subject areas (e.g., customer, campaign, channel, transaction);  
- training sessions (minimum of three, each two hours) for Contoso’s marketing analytics team.

2.4 **HR Analytics Module.**

2.4.1 **Scope.** Summit Tech shall configure the **HR Analytics Module** to support:

- workforce demographics and diversity reporting;  
- attrition and retention analytics;  
- headcount, hiring pipeline, and capacity planning dashboards;  
- performance and compensation trend analysis (where Personal Data is adequately pseudonymized or aggregated in line with **Section 11.4** of the Parent Agreement).

2.4.2 **Special Categories of Data.** To the extent the HR Analytics Module involves Processing of special categories of Personal Data (e.g., data relating to racial or ethnic origin, health, or trade union membership), Summit Tech shall implement appropriate additional safeguards, including:

- field-level encryption of such attributes;  
- strict access controls limiting access to authorized HR and legal personnel only;  
- implementation of segregation of duties and access logs retained in accordance with **Section 11.4(d)**.

Contoso remains responsible for ensuring that any such special category data is lawfully collected and provided to Summit Tech as required by **Section 7.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 **Deliverables.** Summit Tech shall provide:

- at least six HR analytics dashboards;  
- configuration of baseline retention and deletion policies for HR data consistent with Contoso’s HR policies;  
- one dedicated workshop with Contoso’s HR and legal teams to validate legal bases and minimization approaches.

2.5 **Operational Telemetry Data Processing.**

2.5.1 Summit Tech shall extend the Platform’s capabilities under **Section 2.1(a) and (b)** of the Parent Agreement to ingest and Process Operational Telemetry Data for:

- monitoring system performance;  
- identifying potential fraud or abuse patterns;  
- capacity planning;  
- incident analysis and root cause investigation.

2.5.2 Where Operational Telemetry Data contains Personal Data, Summit Tech shall treat such data as Personal Data under the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, applying appropriate pseudonymization, IP truncation, and retention limitations, consistent with **Sections 11.4 and 11.10** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Support and Maintenance for Addendum Services.**

Summit Tech’s obligations under **Section 2.1(d)** and **Article XII – Service Level Agreements (SLA)** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to the Addendum Services. In addition:

- dedicated second-line support resources shall be assigned for Data Lake, Marketing Analytics Module, and HR Analytics Module issues;  
- Summit Tech shall maintain configuration and runbooks specific to the Addendum Services;  
- incident reporting shall distinguish incidents affecting Addendum Services from other Platform components.

2.7 **Onboarding Schedule and Phasing.**

2.7.1 **Phase 1 (0–90 Days):** Data Lake Environment provisioning and onboarding of marketing datasets.  

2.7.2 **Phase 2 (90–180 Days):** HR Analytics Module deployment and ingestion of HR datasets.  

2.7.3 **Phase 3 (180–360 Days):** Expansion to additional Business Units (e.g., supply chain analytics and customer support analytics), subject to Change Requests and mutual agreement.

2.7.4 Contoso shall provide timely access to required source systems and data dictionaries, consistent with its responsibilities under **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement. Delays attributable to Contoso may adjust the above milestones on a reasonable basis.

2.8 **Compliance with Data Classification and Minimization.**

Summit Tech shall implement data ingestion and processing controls that reflect Contoso’s Data Classification Policy, including:

- tagging data sets with classification labels upon ingestion;  
- applying more restrictive security controls and retention limits to higher-sensitivity categories;  
- supporting data minimization by excluding non-required fields upon Contoso’s written instructions, in line with **Section 11.2 (Processing Instructions)**.

2.9 **Use of Aggregated Analytics Outputs.**

2.9.1 Summit Tech may generate **Aggregated and Anonymized Data** derived from the Addendum Services, consistent with and subject to **Section 11.11 of the Parent Agreement**, provided that such data does not identify an individual.

2.9.2 Any Aggregated Analytics Outputs used by Summit Tech for internal product improvement, benchmarking, or research shall remain free of Personal Data and shall not enable reverse-identification of Contoso or any Data Subject.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

The total estimated contract value for the Addendum Services over the Addendum Term is **USD $2,089,338** (the "**Addendum Contract Value**"), which is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **USD $4,058,584** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise agreed in writing:

3.2.1 **Subscription Fees – Addendum Modules.**

- Thereafter, subscription fees may be adjusted as provided in **Section 3.8 (Adjustments to Fees)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to a maximum annual increase of five percent (5%) without a Change Request.

- Implementation and configuration services relating to the Data Lake Environment, Marketing Analytics Module, and HR Analytics Module shall be provided on a time-and-materials basis at the rates set forth in a mutually agreed rate card, initially budgeted at **USD $689,338** for the initial eighteen (18) months.  
- Additional professional services beyond the initial scope (e.g., onboarding of additional Business Units beyond those listed in **Section 2.7**) shall be subject to a Change Request pursuant to **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement**.

3.2.3 **Support and Maintenance Fees.**  

- Ongoing support and maintenance for the Addendum Services are included in the subscription fees set out in **Section 3.2.1**, provided that any enhanced or dedicated support requested by Contoso beyond the standard SLA in the Parent Agreement may be subject to additional fees as mutually agreed.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**

3.3.1 **Invoicing.** Summit Tech shall invoice:

- Annual subscription fees in advance, on or about the Addendum Effective Date and each anniversary thereof during the Addendum Term;  
- Professional services monthly in arrears, with reasonable detail of hours worked and tasks completed; and  
- Any reimbursable expenses as described in **Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.3.2 **Payment Terms.**  

Payment shall be **Net 60** from the invoice date, consistent with **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement. All payments shall be made in U.S. dollars via bank transfer or other agreed method.

3.3.3 **Disputed Invoices.**  

Contoso may dispute invoice line items in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Disputed Invoices)** of the Parent Agreement. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve such disputes while Contoso pays all undisputed amounts by their due date.

3.4 **Late Payment.**

Any overdue amounts (other than amounts disputed in good faith) shall accrue interest as set forth in **Section 3.4 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement (i.e., the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law). Summit Tech may suspend Addendum Services for material non-payment, subject to the same conditions and notice obligations applicable to suspension of Services under the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Taxes and Withholding.**

The provisions of **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Addendum Fees. All Addendum Fees are exclusive of taxes, and Contoso remains responsible for any applicable taxes or withholdings, with Summit Tech’s reasonable cooperation to obtain exemptions where possible.

3.6 **Expenses.**

Reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses for on-site workshops, training, or implementation services related to the Addendum Services shall be reimbursed in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement and Contoso’s applicable travel and expense policies.

3.7 **Fee Adjustments and Change Requests.**

Any change to the Addendum Services that materially affects Fees, milestones, or resource commitments shall be addressed through the Change Management process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**

This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall continue in full force and effect until **March 21, 2029** (the "**Addendum Expiration Date**").

4.2 **Alignment with Parent Agreement Term.**

4.2.1 If the Parent Agreement is renewed pursuant to **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof, this Addendum may be renewed or extended by mutual written agreement of the Parties, provided such extension is expressly documented in writing and references this Addendum.

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is not renewed and expires, this Addendum shall automatically expire on the same date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to extend the Addendum under substantially similar terms and conditions.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

Either Party may terminate this Addendum (but not necessarily the entire Parent Agreement) for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. The Parties acknowledge that this notice period is consistent with the medium risk profile and the nature of the Addendum Services and is aligned with the approach contemplated in **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum in a manner that materially impacts the Addendum Services and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receiving written notice thereof, consistent with **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Data Protection Reasons.**

Without limiting **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement**, Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, with immediate effect if Summit Tech is in persistent or material breach of its data protection obligations relating to the Additional Processing Activities and fails to cure such breach (if curable) within thirty (30) days after written notice.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the same date, without the need for additional notice, except to the extent the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain certain elements (e.g., data return services) for a limited transition period.

4.6.2 Upon termination of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Summit Tech shall provide termination assistance, data return and deletion services, and related activities as set forth in **Sections 4.6 and 4.7 of the Parent Agreement**, specifically with respect to the data processed under the Addendum Services.

4.7 **Survival.**

The following provisions of this Addendum shall survive termination or expiration: **Articles III (to the extent of accrued payment obligations), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIV, XV, XVI**, and this **Section 4.7**, together with all relevant surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as described in **Section 4.8 (Survival)** thereof.
