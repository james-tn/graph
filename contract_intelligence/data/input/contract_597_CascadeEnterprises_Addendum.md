WHEREAS, under Article I (Definitions) of the Parent Agreement, the **“Premises”** are defined as approximately **7,500 square feet** of net lettable area on the **26th floor (Suite 26-03)** of the building at **1 Raffles Place, One Raffles Place, Singapore 048616**, together with non-exclusive rights to use common areas;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement (Ref: **LSE-CAS-202411-494**, identifier **contract_494**) is effective as of **November 02, 2024** (the **“Effective Date”** as defined in Section 1.1.6), with an **Expiration Date** of **July 24, 2028** (Section 1.1.7) and a total estimated **Contract Value of USD 1,524,919** (Section 1.1.8 and Article III);

WHEREAS, Contoso has requested, and Cascade has agreed, subject to the terms and conditions of this Addendum, to (i) lease **additional office premises** in the same building, and (ii) modify certain provisions of the Parent Agreement relating to the definition of **Premises**, **Lease Term**, **Fees and Payment Terms**, and related obligations;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to record their mutual agreement to expand and modify the leased space, to align the services, fees, and term with such expansion, and to set forth the terms and conditions governing such additional and modified leased space;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises herein contained, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK AND MODIFIED LEASED SPACE

2.1 **Reference to Existing Lease of Premises.**  
Section 2.1 of the Parent Agreement (**“Lease of Premises”**) provides that Cascade leases to Contoso “the Premises” defined in Section 1.1.13 as approximately 7,500 square feet on the 26th floor (Suite 26-03). The Parties hereby agree that such lease continues in full force and effect and is expressly **supplemented** by this Addendum.

2.2 **Description of Additional Premises.**  
Subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Cascade hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Cascade, the following **Additional Premises**:

2.2.1 **Location and Size.**  
(a) Approximately **8,250 square feet** of net lettable area located on the **27th floor (Suite 27-02)** of the Building; and  
(b) Approximately **1,500 square feet** of net lettable area located on the **26th floor (Suite 26-04)**, contiguous to the existing Premises (Suite 26-03),  
each together with the right to non-exclusive use of the same common areas, facilities and services as described in Section 2.1 of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 **Use of Additional Premises.**  
The Additional Premises shall be used solely for purposes consistent with Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement (**“Use of Premises”**), including general commercial office purposes, administrative and managerial functions, research and development and other lawful professional activities. All restrictions, prohibitions and obligations under Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement (including no illegal or immoral use, no heavy industrial or retail use, and restrictions on nuisance activities) shall apply equally to the Additional Premises.

2.3 **Amended Premises.**  
With effect from the Addendum Commencement Date:

2.3.1 The term **“Premises”** as used in the Parent Agreement shall, where the context so admits, be deemed to include the **Amended Premises**, i.e., the original Premises (Suite 26-03, 7,500 square feet) plus the Additional Premises (Suite 27-02, 8,250 square feet and Suite 26-04, 1,500 square feet), resulting in a combined approximate net lettable area of **17,250 square feet**.

2.3.2 The Parties acknowledge that, except as expressly modified herein, all obligations of Cascade in relation to the Premises under Article II of the Parent Agreement (including Sections 2.3 to 2.8) apply equally to the Additional Premises and the Amended Premises.

2.4 **Services for Additional Premises.**  
Cascade shall provide the Services defined in Section 1.1.15 and Article II of the Parent Agreement in respect of the Additional Premises, including:

2.4.1 **Building Operations and Maintenance** (Section 2.3.1 of Parent Agreement) covering structural elements, main electrical systems, plumbing, HVAC plant, fire protection systems, elevators, and security systems servicing floors 26 and 27.

2.4.2 **Cleaning Services** (Section 2.3.2) including routine cleaning of the Additional Premises, removal of normal office waste, and cleaning of restrooms and corridors servicing the Additional Premises.

2.4.3 **Security Services** (Section 2.3.3) including access control, CCTV surveillance for entrances to the Additional Premises, and issuance of additional access cards as reasonably required by Contoso’s expanded headcount.

2.4.4 **Utilities** (Section 2.3.4) including provision of electricity, water, and air-conditioning during normal business hours, and after-hours air-conditioning as an additional charge for the Additional Premises, in accordance with building tariffs.

2.4.5 **Repairs within Premises** (Section 2.3.5) in relation to base building systems within the Additional Premises, with Contoso remaining responsible for its own furniture, fixtures, IT equipment and tenant-specific installations therein.

2.5 **Fit-Out and Alterations for Additional Premises.**  

2.5.1 **Initial Fit-Out.**  
(a) Cascade shall perform initial fit-out works for the Additional Premises, substantially in accordance with a mutually agreed fit-out plan aligned with Section 2.4.1 of the Parent Agreement, to deliver a functional open-plan office space, meeting rooms and pantry on Suite 27-02, and supplemental team workspace on Suite 26-04.  
(b) The scope of such fit-out shall include partitioning, flooring, basic ceiling works, lighting, structured cabling pathways (but not active IT equipment), and integration with building HVAC and fire systems.  
(c) The cost of Cascade-funded fit-out contributions for the Additional Premises shall be included in the **Contract Value (Addendum)** and detailed in **Schedule 2 – Fit-Out Scope and Budget** to this Addendum.

2.5.2 **Tenant Fit-Out.**  
Consistent with Section 2.4.2 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may, at its own cost unless otherwise agreed, undertake additional fit-out and alterations (e.g., specialized meeting rooms, server room on Suite 27-02, branding and signage), subject to Cascade’s prior written approval, building rules, and compliance with law. Restoration obligations under Section 4.5.1(b) of the Parent Agreement apply to such tenant-specific works within the Additional Premises.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  
Cascade shall perform the Services for the Additional Premises in accordance with:

2.6.1 The standards in Sections 2.5.1–2.5.3 of the Parent Agreement (including use of trained personnel, compliance with applicable laws and building codes); and

2.6.2 The Service Levels under Article XII of the Parent Agreement, as extended and supplemented by Article XII of this Addendum (including uptime commitments and response/resolution times for issues affecting the Additional Premises).

2.7 **Access and Cooperation.**  

2.7.1 Cascade shall ensure that Contoso has secure access to the Additional Premises consistent with Section 2.6.1 of the Parent Agreement, including issuance of additional access cards required for Contoso’s expanded occupancy.

2.7.2 Contoso shall provide access to the Additional Premises for inspection, maintenance and repairs in accordance with Section 2.6.2 of the Parent Agreement, and shall use reasonable efforts to coordinate scheduling to minimize disruption across both the original Premises and the Additional Premises.

2.8 **Deliverables for Additional Premises.**  
In addition to the Deliverables listed in Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall provide:

(a) Updated floor plans and as-built drawings for Suites 26-03, 26-04 and 27-02;  
(b) Separate or consolidated maintenance logs evidencing services provided to the Additional Premises;  
(c) Utility sub-metering reports (if applicable) for the Additional Premises; and  
(d) Any additional reports mutually agreed in writing, particularly those required for Contoso’s internal audits or regulatory compliance relating to its expanded occupation of the Building.

2.9 **No Diminution of Existing Rights.**  
Nothing in this Addendum shall be construed to diminish or impair any rights of Contoso under the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to the **client-friendly provisions** regarding termination for convenience (Section 4.3), limitation of liability (Article IX), and service credits (Article XII), except to the extent explicitly modified herein.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS (MODIFICATIONS)

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The Parties acknowledge and agree that, subject to the terms of this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, the total estimated contract value for the Additional Premises and related Services under this Addendum shall be **USD 2,253,193** (the **“Contract Value (Addendum)”**), which is in addition to (and not in substitution for) the Contract Value of USD 1,524,919 under Article III of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Base Rent for Additional Premises.**

3.2.1 **Rent Schedule (Addendum).**  
Contoso shall pay to Cascade a monthly Base Rent for the Additional Premises in the amounts specified in **Schedule 1 – Addendum Rent and Service Charge Schedule**, which schedule shall be deemed an extension of the Rent Schedule referenced in Section 3.2.1 of the Parent Agreement and shall be drafted so as to be consistent with the Contract Value (Addendum).

3.2.2 **Rent Commencement.**  
Base Rent for the Additional Premises shall commence on the Addendum Commencement Date. For any partial month, Rent shall be prorated on a daily basis based on a 30-day month, consistent with Section 3.2.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Service Charges and Utilities for Additional Premises.**

3.3.1 Contoso shall pay Service Charges attributable to the Additional Premises on a monthly basis, allocated using Cascade’s standard methodology consistent with Section 3.3.1 of the Parent Agreement, and as further detailed in Schedule 1 of this Addendum.

3.3.2 Utility charges (electricity, water, and, where applicable, separate metered services) for the Additional Premises shall be charged on a **metered or sub-metered basis**, where technically feasible, or otherwise on a pro-rata basis reflecting floor area, as indicated in Schedule 1 and consistent with Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.3 Additional air-conditioning outside normal business hours for the Additional Premises shall be invoiced as additional services at the prevailing building rates as notified by Cascade to Contoso from time to time, in accordance with Section 3.3.3 of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Modified Payment Terms – Net 60.**

3.4.1 **Amendment to Payment Terms for Addendum.**  
Notwithstanding Section 1.1.20 and Section 3.4.1 of the Parent Agreement (which specify **Net 30** terms), the Parties agree that, with respect to all invoices relating to the Additional Premises and Services rendered under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts **within sixty (60) calendar days** of the invoice date (**“Net 60”**). The Parties may mutually agree in writing to extend Net 60 terms to the original Premises, but unless expressly so stated, Net 30 under the Parent Agreement remains applicable to the original Premises.

3.4.2 All payments shall be made in USD (or such other currency as mutually agreed) by bank transfer to the account designated by Cascade, free from any set-off, deduction or withholding except as required by law, consistent with Section 3.4.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Late Payment.**

3.5.1 If Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount relating to this Addendum by the Net 60 due date, Cascade may charge interest on the overdue amount from the due date until full payment at the lesser of: (a) one percent (1%) per month, or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, consistent with Section 3.5.1 of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 Cascade shall provide a written reminder with a grace period of at least ten (10) Business Days prior to exercising any rights of suspension of Services or rights of re-entry in respect of the Additional Premises, consistent with Section 3.5.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Sections 3.6.1 and 3.6.2 of the Parent Agreement (regarding invoice disputes, payment of undisputed portions, and good faith resolution) shall apply to all invoices issued under this Addendum. For clarity, Contoso shall notify Cascade of any disputed amounts relating to the Additional Premises within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt, specifying the basis of dispute.

3.7 **Taxes.**

3.7.1 All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for any applicable GST or other indirect taxes, consistent with Section 3.7.1 of the Parent Agreement.

3.7.2 If withholding tax applies, Contoso may deduct such tax in accordance with Section 3.7.2 of the Parent Agreement, provided that Contoso furnishes Cascade with appropriate documentation to enable credit or refund claims.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.8.1 To the extent Cascade incurs out-of-pocket expenses specifically requested by Contoso in relation to the Additional Premises (e.g., expedited fit-out, additional security for the 27th floor), such expenses shall be reimbursed by Contoso at cost plus any agreed administrative charge, subject to prior written approval, consistent with Section 3.8.1 of the Parent Agreement.

3.8.2 Cascade shall provide reasonable supporting documentation for reimbursable expenses upon Contoso’s request, consistent with Section 3.8.2 of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDED FOR ADDITIONAL PREMISES)

4.1 **Addendum Term and Relationship to Parent Agreement.**

4.1.1 The Parent Agreement’s Lease Term (Section 4.1) for the original Premises remains from the Commencement Date (November 02, 2024) to the Expiration Date (July 24, 2028), unless earlier terminated.

4.1.2 The **Addendum Term** for the Additional Premises shall commence on the **Addendum Commencement Date**, being **September 10, 2025** or such later date on which possession of the Additional Premises is delivered by Cascade per Section 2.5 of this Addendum.

4.1.3 The **Addendum Expiration Date** shall be **March 18, 2029**, such that the lease of the Additional Premises under this Addendum extends beyond the original Expiration Date of the Parent Agreement.

4.1.4 To the extent necessary, the Parties agree that Article IV of the Parent Agreement shall be deemed **extended** and **applied** to the Additional Premises until March 18, 2029, and all references to the “Term” in the Parent Agreement, when used in relation to the Additional Premises, shall be construed as including the Addendum Term.

4.2 **Renewal of Additional Premises.**  
Section 4.2 of the Parent Agreement (Renewal) shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Additional Premises, save that Contoso’s option to request renewal of the lease of the Additional Premises may be exercised no later than nine (9) months prior to the Addendum Expiration Date (i.e., by **June 18, 2028**), unless a different timeframe is agreed in writing.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum **for convenience**, in whole or in part (i.e., with respect to one or both components of the Additional Premises), at any time after the first twelve (12) months of the Addendum Term by providing Cascade with at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice, mirroring Section 4.3.1 of the Parent Agreement but expressly applied to the Additional Premises.

4.3.2 In the event of such termination for convenience, Contoso shall be liable for:  
(a) all Rent and charges due up to the effective termination date for the affected portion of the Additional Premises; and  
(b) any documented, non-recoverable capital costs incurred by Cascade specifically for Contoso’s fit-out of the Additional Premises and amortized over the Addendum Term, limited to the unamortized portion as at the termination date, unless otherwise waived by Cascade, consistent with Section 4.3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.3 Cascade’s right to terminate for convenience with respect to the Additional Premises shall be as set forth in Section 4.3.3 of the Parent Agreement, including requirements relating to redevelopment or major works and the obligation to use commercially reasonable efforts to offer comparable alternative premises.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement (Termination for Cause) shall apply to this Addendum and the Additional Premises. Any material breach of this Addendum shall be treated as a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa, to the extent such breach affects the Amended Premises or the Services broadly.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

4.5.1 **If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires prior to the Addendum Expiration Date**, the Parties agree as follows:  
(a) Unless the Parties mutually agree otherwise in writing, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement shall automatically result in termination of this Addendum and the lease of the Additional Premises, effective on the same date, except where the Parties agree to novate or restate the terms of this Addendum into a standalone lease.  
(b) Where such automatic termination would otherwise result in an inconsistency with the Addendum Expiration Date, Section 16.3 (Severability) of the Parent Agreement and Section 16.3 of this Addendum shall be applied, and the Parties shall negotiate in good faith to preserve, where commercially viable, Contoso’s continued occupancy of the Additional Premises.

4.5.2 Sections 4.5.1(a)–(c) of the Parent Agreement (requiring vacating the Premises, removal of Contoso’s property, reinstatement obligations and refund of prepaid Rent and deposits) shall apply equally to the Additional Premises upon any termination or expiration of this Addendum, with appropriate adjustments for the Amended Premises.

4.5.3 Termination or expiration of this Addendum shall not affect any accrued rights, obligations or liabilities of either Party, nor any provisions of the Parent Agreement or this Addendum which, by their nature, are intended to survive.

## ARTICLE IX – LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Cap on Liability (Addendum).**  
Without prejudice to Article IX of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that, with respect solely to claims arising out of or in connection with this Addendum and the Additional Premises, the total aggregate liability of each Party shall not exceed **three (3) times** the total Fees actually paid by Contoso to Cascade **under this Addendum** during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9.2 **Aggregate Caps.**  
For clarity, the liability caps for the Parent Agreement and this Addendum shall be applied **separately**, unless a claim arises from conduct or events common to both the original Premises and the Additional Premises, in which case the Parties shall allocate liability in good faith, taking into account the relevant proportion of Fees.

9.3 **Exclusion of Certain Damages; Exceptions.**  
The exclusions stated in Section 9.2 of the Parent Agreement (no indirect, consequential, incidental, special or punitive damages, no loss of profit, revenue, business or goodwill) and the exceptions listed in Section 9.3 (including indemnification for bodily injury, confidentiality/data protection breaches, and fraud) shall apply equally to this Addendum.

9.4 **Medium Risk Classification.**  
Given the **medium risk** classification of this Addendum, the limitations and exclusions shall be interpreted to provide a balanced allocation of risk between the Parties, consistent with Section 9.4 of the Parent Agreement, while maintaining the agreed liability cap.
