## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Overview.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Vanguard shall provide to Contoso, and Contoso shall purchase from Vanguard, the following:

  (a) Design, configuration, and deployment of a secure enterprise technology platform (including hardware and software components) to support Contoso’s operations;

  (b) Integration of Vanguard’s proprietary software modules with Contoso’s existing systems and infrastructure;

  (c) Ongoing managed services, maintenance, and support;

  (d) Training and knowledge transfer for Contoso’s designated personnel;

  (e) Related professional services, consulting, and project management.

2.2 **Detailed Services Description.** Without limiting the generality of Section 2.1, the Services shall include at least the following activities, which may be further detailed in one or more SOWs:

  (a) **Requirements Gathering and Analysis.** Vanguard shall conduct discovery sessions with Contoso’s business and technical stakeholders to confirm system requirements, integration points, security mandates, performance expectations, compliance obligations, and data flows. Vanguard shall deliver a “Requirements Specification Document” summarizing these requirements.

  (b) **Solution Design.** Based on the Requirements Specification Document, Vanguard shall design a technology architecture that includes:  
  - Network topology and security zoning;  
  - Application and database layers;  
  - Data integration architecture and interfaces;  
  - User access and authentication models; and  
  - High availability and disaster recovery configurations.  
  Vanguard shall document the proposed architecture in a “Solution Design Document” which shall be subject to Contoso’s review and written approval (not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed). Vanguard’s obligations to proceed to subsequent phases may be conditioned on such approval.

  (c) **Hardware and Software Provisioning.** Vanguard shall procure and deliver (or cause to be delivered) to Contoso certain hardware, licensed software, and related components as specified in applicable POs and SOWs. Title and risk of loss for hardware shall pass to Contoso as set forth in Section 3.4. Software will be licensed, not sold, under Article VI.

  (d) **Implementation and Configuration.** Vanguard shall install, configure, and customize the solution in accordance with the Solution Design Document and agreed project plan. This includes:  
  - Installing hardware in Contoso’s designated data centers or cloud environment;  
  - Installing and configuring Vanguard software and Third Party Materials;  
  - Configuring user roles, permissions, and integrations with Contoso’s identity systems;  
  - Setting up monitoring, logging, and alerting capabilities; and  
  - Performing initial test data loads as appropriate.

  (e) **Integration Services.** Vanguard shall integrate the system with Contoso’s existing applications and systems as specified in applicable SOWs, which may include ERP, CRM, data warehouses, and security tools. Vanguard shall design, build, and test integration interfaces, including APIs, data mappings, and scheduled or real-time data synchronization.

  (f) **Testing and Acceptance.** Vanguard shall conduct unit testing, system testing, and integration testing of the solution. Contoso shall perform user acceptance testing (“UAT”) based on mutually agreed test cases and acceptance criteria defined in the applicable SOW. The Deliverables shall be deemed accepted upon the earlier of: (i) Contoso’s written acceptance; or (ii) ten (10) Business Days following delivery if Contoso has not provided a written rejection specifying material non-conformities.

  (g) **Training and Knowledge Transfer.** Vanguard shall develop and deliver training sessions and materials for Contoso’s administrative and operational personnel as specified in a SOW. Training may include on-site instruction, remote sessions, and self-paced materials. Vanguard shall provide up to the number of training hours specified in the applicable SOW; additional training shall be billable on a time-and-materials basis.

  (h) **Support and Maintenance.** Following Acceptance, Vanguard shall provide ongoing support and maintenance services consistent with the SLA in Article XII. Support services shall include incident response, troubleshooting, patch management, minor enhancements, and system performance monitoring.

  (i) **Managed Services.** Vanguard shall perform ongoing managed services, including system administration, health checks, capacity planning, backup verification, and security log reviews, as described in the SOW and subject to the SLA.

2.3 **Performance Standards.** Vanguard shall perform all Services:

  (a) In a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards for similar services;  

  (b) In compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including Data Protection Laws as set forth in Article XI;  

  (c) Using personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and qualifications;  

  (d) In accordance with the project plans, timelines, and Milestones specified in the SOW(s).  

Delays caused by Contoso (including failure to provide timely access, information, or approvals) shall entitle Vanguard to reasonable schedule extensions and may result in additional fees as provided in Article XIII.

2.4 **Contoso’s Responsibilities.** Contoso shall, at its own expense:

  (a) Provide Vanguard with timely access to Contoso’s sites, systems, personnel, and relevant documentation necessary for Vanguard to perform the Services;  

  (b) Designate a qualified project manager and key stakeholders who shall be responsible for decisions, approvals, and coordination with Vanguard;  

  (c) Ensure that its systems and infrastructure meet any minimum requirements specified by Vanguard;  

  (d) Obtain and maintain any third-party licenses or consents required for Vanguard’s integration with or access to Contoso’s systems, unless otherwise specified in the SOW;  

  (e) Promptly review and respond to Deliverables and documentation (including the Requirements Specification Document and Solution Design Document) within the timelines specified in the project plan.  

Vanguard shall not be responsible for delays or failures in performance caused by Contoso’s failure to meet its obligations under this Section 2.4.

2.5 **Statements of Work and Priority.** Specific details of the Services, Deliverables, project schedules, and Milestones shall be set forth in one or more SOWs executed by the Parties. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and any SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall control unless the SOW expressly identifies the specific provision being modified and states that such SOW takes precedence.

2.6 **Changes in Scope.** Any changes to the scope of Services, Deliverables, or project schedules shall be handled in accordance with Article XIII (Change Management). Vanguard shall have no obligation to perform any additional services or provide additional Deliverables unless agreed in a written Change Order signed by both Parties.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Fees and Pricing.** Contoso shall pay Vanguard fees in accordance with:

  (a) The **Contract Value** of **US $843,012**, which represents the initial estimate for the Services and Deliverables under this Agreement; and  

  (b) The detailed fee schedules set forth in each applicable SOW and any Change Orders.  

Unless otherwise stated, all amounts are in U.S. dollars and are exclusive of applicable taxes, duties, and other governmental charges.

3.2 **Fee Structure.** Fees may include one or more of the following components as specified in the applicable SOW:

  (a) **Fixed-Price Fees** for specified Deliverables and Milestones;  

  (b) **Time-and-Materials Fees** based on actual hours worked at the rates specified in the SOW;  

  (c) **Subscription or Recurring Fees** for ongoing support, maintenance, licenses, or managed services;  

  (d) **Pass-Through Costs** for Third Party Materials, hardware, or other items purchased by Vanguard on Contoso’s behalf.

3.3 **Payment Milestones.** Unless otherwise set forth in a SOW, the following payment Milestones shall apply:

  (a) **Initial Deposit:** Twenty percent (20%) of the then-estimated Contract Value payable within fifteen (15) days of the Effective Date;  

  (b) **Design Completion:** Twenty percent (20%) upon Contoso’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the Solution Design Document;  

  (c) **Implementation Completion:** Thirty percent (30%) upon completion of implementation and configuration and prior to UAT;  

  (d) **Final Acceptance:** Twenty percent (20%) upon Acceptance of the final Deliverables;  

  (e) **Retention:** Ten percent (10%) within thirty (30) days following successful completion of a thirty (30)-day post-acceptance stabilization period, provided that no material unresolved issues attributable to Vanguard remain.

3.4 **Invoicing; Payment Terms.** Vanguard shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the Milestones and schedules set forth herein and in the applicable SOW. Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts within **forty-five (45) days** of the date of invoice (“**Net 45**”). Payment shall be made via wire transfer or other mutually agreed method.

3.5 **Late Payments.** Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month or the maximum rate allowed by law, whichever is less, from the due date until paid in full. In addition, if Contoso fails to pay undisputed amounts within fifteen (15) days after receiving written notice of non-payment from Vanguard, Vanguard may:

  (a) Suspend performance of the Services and withhold Deliverables until all overdue amounts are paid; and/or  

  (b) Condition future performance on receipt of advance payments or other assurances of payment reasonably acceptable to Vanguard.

3.6 **Disputed Invoices.** If Contoso disputes any invoice in good faith, it shall notify Vanguard in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt, specifying the basis of the dispute in reasonable detail. Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts in accordance with Section 3.4. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve any disputed amounts promptly, and upon resolution, any amounts determined to be owing shall be paid within ten (10) Business Days.

3.7 **Taxes.** Fees do not include any sales, use, value-added, or other taxes, levies, or duties of any nature (collectively, “**Taxes**”). Contoso shall be responsible for all Taxes arising from this Agreement, excluding Taxes based on Vanguard’s net income, property, or employees. If Vanguard is required by law to collect or pay any Taxes on Contoso’s behalf, Vanguard shall invoice Contoso for such Taxes, and Contoso shall pay such amounts in accordance with this Article III.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursement.** Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard for all reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including travel, lodging, meals, and third-party fees. Vanguard shall provide reasonable supporting documentation for reimbursable expenses. Such expenses shall be invoiced monthly and paid in accordance with Section 3.4.

3.9 **No Withholding.** All payments shall be made without set-off, counterclaim, or withholding, except as required by law. If any withholding is required, Contoso shall gross up such payments so that Vanguard receives the full amount it would have received absent such withholding.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.** The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **April 06, 2030** (the “**Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the “**Term**”).

4.2 **Renewal.** The Parties may, upon mutual written agreement executed no later than ninety (90) days prior to the Expiration Date, renew this Agreement for one or more additional periods, on terms to be mutually negotiated. Absent such written agreement, this Agreement shall automatically terminate on the Expiration Date.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience by Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part, for convenience at any time by providing at least **one hundred eighty (180) days** prior written notice to Vanguard. Contoso shall remain liable for:

  (a) All fees and expenses incurred or committed by Vanguard as of the effective date of termination;  

  (b) Any non-cancellable third-party costs; and  

  (c) An early termination fee equal to twenty percent (20%) of the unpaid portion of the then-current Contract Value, as liquidated damages reflecting the Parties’ reasonable estimate of Vanguard’s lost opportunity and not as a penalty.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience by Vanguard.** Vanguard may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior written notice to Contoso. In such event, Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption and shall cooperate in transition as described in Section 4.7. Contoso shall be liable only for fees and expenses incurred or committed through the effective date of termination.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if:

  (a) The other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach (ten (10) days for payment breaches); or  

  (b) The other Party becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or is subject to any bankruptcy or similar proceeding that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.** Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement:

  (a) All rights and licenses granted to Contoso under this Agreement shall terminate, except as expressly provided otherwise in Article VI;  

  (b) Contoso shall promptly pay Vanguard all amounts due and unpaid for Services rendered, Deliverables provided, and expenses incurred prior to the effective date of termination, plus any applicable early termination fees;  

  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with Section 5.4; and  

  (d) Vanguard shall provide reasonable transition services as described in Section 4.7, subject to additional fees as mutually agreed.

4.7 **Transition Assistance.** Upon Contoso’s request made prior to or within thirty (30) days after termination or expiration, Vanguard shall provide reasonable transition assistance (e.g., data export, handover documentation, cooperation with a successor provider) to support an orderly transition of Services. Such services shall be provided at Vanguard’s then-current time-and-materials rates unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.8 **Survival.** The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration shall survive, including without limitation Sections 3.5, 3.7, 4.6, 4.8, and Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XII (with respect to accrued SLA credits), XIV, XV, and XVI.

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS

12.1 **Uptime Commitment.** Subject to the terms of this Agreement, Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the hosted components of the solution are available **99.9%** of the time, measured on a monthly basis, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Excluded Downtime as defined in Section 12.2.

12.2 **Definitions.**

  (a) “**Availability**” means that the hosted solution is accessible and usable by Contoso’s authorized users over the internet.  

  (b) “**Scheduled Maintenance**” means planned maintenance activities notified to Contoso at least forty-eight (48) hours in advance and conducted outside of Contoso’s normal business hours where reasonably possible.  

  (c) “**Excluded Downtime**” means any unavailability caused by: (i) Scheduled Maintenance; (ii) acts or omissions of Contoso or its users; (iii) failures of Contoso’s internet connectivity or systems; (iv) Force Majeure Events; or (v) any third-party services not controlled by Vanguard.

12.3 **Response and Resolution Times.** Vanguard shall respond to support incidents in accordance with the following targets:

  - **Priority 1 (Critical):** Response within 1 hour; best efforts to resolve within 4 hours.  
  - **Priority 2 (High):** Response within 4 hours; best efforts to resolve within 1 Business Day.  
  - **Priority 3 (Medium):** Response within 1 Business Day; best efforts to resolve within 3 Business Days.  
  - **Priority 4 (Low):** Response within 2 Business Days; resolution in a future release or as agreed.

12.4 **SLA Credits.** If Availability falls below the 99.9% target in any calendar month (excluding Excluded Downtime), Contoso shall be entitled to the following service credits, upon written request within thirty (30) days after the end of the month:

  - Availability 99.0% to 99.89%: 5% of the monthly recurring fee;  
  - Availability 98.0% to 98.99%: 10% of the monthly recurring fee;  
  - Availability below 98.0%: 20% of the monthly recurring fee.

SLA credits shall be applied to future invoices and shall not be redeemable for cash. SLA credits are Contoso’s sole and exclusive remedy for Vanguard’s failure to meet the uptime commitments in this Article XII.

12.5 **Exclusions.** Vanguard shall have no liability under this Article XII to the extent any service level failure is caused by factors outside Vanguard’s reasonable control, including Excluded Downtime and Contoso’s acts or omissions.
