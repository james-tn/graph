## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the **Master Agreement** dated 24 March 2024 (**MSA-ZEN-202403-197, contract_197**), which sets forth the general terms and conditions under which Vendor provides professional, technology, implementation, managed, and consulting services to Client;

WHEREAS, pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** and Section 2.1 (**Master Framework**) of the Master Agreement, the Parties executed the Existing SOW, bearing Contract Reference Number **SOW-ZEN-202403-200 (contract_200)**, as a Statement of Work under the Master Agreement for a high‑risk enterprise ERP/CRM implementation and managed services engagement;

WHEREAS, the Existing SOW establishes, among other things, the **SOW Effective Date** (16 June 2024), the **SOW Expiration Date** (15 June 2026), the **SOW Contract Value** of **USD $2,981,827** (Section 1.16 and Article III), the detailed **Scope of Services / Work** in **Article II**, the phased Project approach in **Section 2.3**, and the **Managed Services** regime and Service Levels in **Article XII**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to extend, expand, and refine the scope of the Services, Deliverables, Service Levels, and commercial terms applicable to the Project described in the Existing SOW, including (a) enhancement of the Managed Services phase beyond the prior SOW Expiration Date, (b) expansion of geographic coverage and supported systems, and (c) adjustments to certain fees, payment terms, and governance processes;

WHEREAS, **this Addendum supplements the Statement of Work dated June 16, 2024 (Ref: SOW-ZEN-202403-200, contract_200)**, is **entered into pursuant to** the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement, and is intended to modify, clarify and add to the terms of the Existing SOW as expressly set forth herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that the Project continues to be deemed **high-risk** under the risk allocation framework established in **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Master Agreement and reaffirmed in **Article IX** of the Existing SOW, under which the Parties agreed to **uncapped liability** and **inclusion of consequential damages**, and the commercial terms in this Addendum have been negotiated with such risk allocation in mind;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained in the Master Agreement, the Existing SOW and this Addendum, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Existing SOW and Master Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Addendum is **entered into pursuant to** and **supplements** the Existing SOW (Ref: **SOW-ZEN-202403-200, contract_200**), which in turn is executed under and subject to the **Master Agreement (MSA-ZEN-202403-197, contract_197)** as described in **Section 2.1** and **Article XVI** of the Existing SOW. Except as expressly modified herein, the terms and conditions of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement remain in full force and effect and shall apply to all Services and Deliverables under this Addendum.

2.1.2 In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between this Addendum and the Existing SOW, **this Addendum shall prevail solely with respect to the Services, Deliverables, and commercial terms expressly addressed herein**. In the event of any conflict between this Addendum and the Master Agreement, the order of precedence set forth in Section 2.1.3 of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement shall continue to apply.

2.2 **Overview of Additional and Expanded Services.**

2.2.1 **Extension of Managed Services Term.**  
The Parties agree to extend the Managed Services described in **Phase 5 – Managed Services (Month 12 – SOW Expiration)** in **Section 2.3.5** and **Section 2.6** of the Existing SOW beyond the original SOW Expiration Date of **15 June 2026**, through the **Extended SOW Term ending 22 July 2027**. During the Extended SOW Term, Vendor shall continue to provide **Level 1, 2 and 3 support**, periodic health checks, reporting, performance optimization and minor enhancements consistent with the standards and Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of the Existing SOW and expanded in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

2.2.2 **Geographic and Functional Expansion.**  
Vendor shall expand the scope of the Managed Services to cover:  
  a. Additional business regions, including at least (i) EMEA, (ii) North America, and (iii) APAC regions beyond those initially covered under **Section 2.3.3 (Phase 3 – Testing, UAT and Go‑Live)** of the Existing SOW;  
  b. Additional integrated systems beyond the five (5) specified systems (HRIS, Data Warehouse, Legacy Billing, Identity Management, and Customer Portal) listed in **Section 2.3.2** of the Existing SOW, including newly integrated marketing automation, supplier portal, and mobile field-service applications; and  
  c. Multi‑language and multi‑currency support within the ERP/CRM solution implemented pursuant to the Existing SOW.

2.2.3 **Advanced Monitoring and Automation.**  
Vendor shall design, implement, and operate advanced monitoring, automation and observability capabilities for the production ERP/CRM environment, including:  
  a. Deployment of infrastructure and application monitoring tools configured to track **Uptime** (as defined in Section 1.18 of the Existing SOW) and key performance indicators (KPIs);  
  b. Implementation of automated alerting and incident triage consistent with the **Incident** severity framework set out in **Section 1.17** and **Section 12.3** of the Existing SOW;  
  c. Introduction of runbook‑driven automated remediation scripts for predefined, recurring issues; and  
  d. Monthly **Optimization & Roadmap Reports** (supplementing Deliverable D12) incorporating predictive analytics and capacity‑planning forecasts.

2.2.4 **Regulatory and Compliance Enhancements.**  
Given the potential processing of **Regulated Data**, Vendor shall:  
  a. Perform data‑flow mapping and maintain updated records of processing activities related to ERP/CRM and integrated systems;  
  b. Assist Client in implementing technical and organizational measures to support compliance with GDPR, CCPA and other Applicable Laws, consistent with Article XI of the Master Agreement and **Article XI** of this Addendum; and  
  c. Support periodic compliance audits initiated by Client pursuant to **Section 11.8 (Audit Rights)** of the Existing SOW and Section 11.9 of the Master Agreement.

2.3 **Phased Scope under this Addendum.**

2.3.1 **Phase A – Onboarding to Expanded Managed Services (Approx. 3 Months from Addendum Effective Date).**  
Vendor shall:  
  a. Conduct workshops with Client’s IT and business stakeholders to refine the **Enhanced Project Plan** for EMS;  
  b. Analyze the current state of Managed Services operations established pursuant to **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Transition to Managed Services (Weeks 37–48)** and Phase 5 of the Existing SOW;  
  c. Develop an **EMS Transition & Onboarding Plan** (Additional Deliverable D13) specifying resource assignments, handover activities, and any updated RACI matrices; and  
  d. Document updated Service Level metrics and reporting formats consistent with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Existing SOW and this Addendum.

2.3.2 **Phase B – Implementation of Advanced Monitoring and Automation (Approx. 4–6 Months).**  
Vendor shall:  
  a. Design and implement monitoring dashboards, alerting rules, and synthetic transaction tests for the supported ERP/CRM systems;  
  b. Develop up to ten (10) automated remediation workflows (e.g., cache resets, job restarts, log rotation, automated scaling rules) and integrate them with the incident management platform;  
  c. Prepare and deliver **Monitoring & Automation Design Specifications** (Additional Deliverable D14) and **Operational Playbooks** (Additional Deliverable D15); and  
  d. Conduct joint testing of monitoring and automation capabilities and obtain Client’s acceptance thereof in accordance with the Acceptance Criteria procedures referenced in **Section 1.9 and Section 2.5.2** of the Existing SOW.

2.3.3 **Phase C – Steady-State Expanded Managed Services (From Completion of Phase B through Extended SOW Term).**  
During the steady-state EMS period, Vendor shall:  
  a. Provide 24x7 Level 1 and Level 2 support for Severity 1 and Severity 2 **Incidents** and Business Day support for Severity 3 and 4 Incidents, consistent with **Section 12.3.2** of the Existing SOW and subject to the expanded regional coverage;  
  b. Provide continuous monitoring and monthly **Service Performance Reports** (enhancing Deliverable D11) that reflect the new KPIs and metrics;  
  c. Provide quarterly **Compliance and Risk Reports** (Additional Deliverable D16) focusing on data protection, security posture, and audit findings; and  
  d. Provide semi‑annual **Strategic Roadmap Workshops** and associated documentation (Additional Deliverable D17), to refine the ERP/CRM and integration roadmap.

2.4 **Additional Deliverables.**

In addition to the Deliverables (D1–D12) listed in **Section 2.4** of the Existing SOW, Vendor shall produce at least the following **Additional Deliverables** under this Addendum:

- **D13 – EMS Transition & Onboarding Plan** (detailed plan for expansion of Managed Services);  
- **D14 – Monitoring & Automation Design Specifications** (architecture, rules, thresholds, and integration points);  
- **D15 – Operational Playbooks and Automated Runbooks** (detailed procedures for incident response, escalation, and automation workflows);  
- **D16 – Quarterly Compliance and Risk Reports** (covering security incidents, audit findings, remediation progress, and regulatory issues);  
- **D17 – Strategic Roadmap Workshop Materials and Outcome Reports**;  
- **D18 – Extended Capacity and Performance Planning Reports**;  
- **D19 – Regional Support and Coverage Matrix** (reflecting expanded geographic scope);  
- **D20 – Transition Services Exit Plan** (detailed plan for Transition Services Period, to be updated no less than six (6) months prior to the end of the Extended SOW Term).

2.5 **Performance Standards.**

2.5.1 Vendor shall perform the Services under this Addendum in accordance with:  
  a. The performance standards set forth in **Section 2.5.1** of the Existing SOW and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Agreement; and  
  b. The enhanced Service Levels and commitments stipulated in **Article XII** of this Addendum and the Existing SOW.

2.5.2 The Parties acknowledge that the Project remains **high-risk**, and Vendor shall assign personnel with experience commensurate with this risk profile, consistent with **Section 2.7 (Key Personnel)** and **Section 7.2(c)** of the Existing SOW.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities under the Addendum.**

In addition to Client’s obligations under **Section 2.6 of the Existing SOW**, Client shall:

  a. Provide timely access to additional business and technical stakeholders in each newly onboarded region;  
  b. Ensure that all necessary connectivity, VPN, and firewall configurations are in place for Vendor’s monitoring tools and support personnel;  
  c. Promptly review and provide written feedback or acceptance of Additional Deliverables in accordance with the acceptance procedures referenced in **Section 2.6(e)** of the Existing SOW; and  
  d. Implement reasonable security and operational recommendations issued by Vendor in the Compliance and Risk Reports (D16), subject to internal governance and Applicable Law.

2.7 **Governance and Escalation.**

2.7.1 The Parties shall formalize the **Governance Committee** structure (which supplements the informal escalation provisions implied in **Article XIV** of the Existing SOW) to oversee delivery, performance, and risk management for the Extended SOW Term. The Governance Committee shall meet at least quarterly (and more frequently as reasonably required) and shall review:

  - Service performance against Service Levels;  
  - Risk and compliance issues;  
  - Roadmap prioritization and Change Requests;  
  - Transition planning and exit readiness as the Extended SOW Term approaches its end.

2.7.2 Unresolved disputes escalated through the Governance Committee shall be further handled in accordance with **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **aggregate estimated value** of the incremental and expanded Services under this Addendum (**“Addendum Contract Value”**) is **USD $3,758,026**. This amount is an estimate only and does not limit the total Fees that may become payable under this Addendum, consistent with **Section 3.1** of the Existing SOW and **Section 3.1 of the Master Agreement**.

3.1.2 The Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the original **SOW Contract Value** of **USD $2,981,827** described in **Section 1.16 and Section 3.1** of the Existing SOW. For clarity, the total estimated value of the Project, as amended by this Addendum, is the sum of the SOW Contract Value and the Addendum Contract Value.

3.2 **Fee Structure under the Addendum.**

3.2.1 The Fees for the Services under this Addendum shall comprise:

  a. **EMS Onboarding and Implementation Fees (Fixed Price)** – covering Phases A and B: **USD $1,350,000**;  
  b. **Expanded Managed Services Recurring Fees** – for the Extended SOW Term, at a recurring monthly fee of **USD $95,000**, estimated total **USD $2,183,026** (assuming 23 months of EMS from May 2025 through March 2027 and pro-rated for the remainder through the Expiration Date); and  
  c. **Additional T&M Fees** – for Change Requests and optional enhancements specifically related to EMS, estimated at **USD $225,000**.

| Milestone | Description | Target Date (Indicative) | Fee (USD) | Payment Trigger |
|----------|-------------|--------------------------|-----------|-----------------|
| A1 | Completion & Client acceptance of D13 (EMS Transition & Onboarding Plan) | 8 weeks from Addendum Effective Date | 300,000 | 100% upon acceptance |
| A2 | Completion & acceptance of D14 and D15 (Monitoring & Automation Design + Operational Playbooks) | 20 weeks from Addendum Effective Date | 550,000 | 100% upon acceptance |

3.3 **Payment Terms; Modification of Section 3.3.3 of Existing SOW.**

3.3.1 **Net 30.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.3** of the Existing SOW (which provides for Net 45 payment terms), the Parties hereby agree that all invoices issued in connection with the Services and Deliverables covered by this Addendum shall be payable by Client within **thirty (30) days** from the date of the invoice (**“Net 30”**), without setoff or deduction (except as permitted under Section 3.6 of the Master Agreement).

3.3.2 **Invoicing Schedule.**  
  a. Fixed‑price EMS Onboarding and Implementation Fees (Milestones A1–A3) shall be invoiced upon achievement of the applicable Milestone and Client’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the associated Additional Deliverables;  
  b. Expanded Managed Services Fees (A4) and Additional T&M Fees (A5) shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears**, consistent with the approach in **Section 3.3.2** of the Existing SOW; and  
  c. All invoices shall clearly distinguish between Fees related to the Existing SOW (M1–M6) and Fees related to this Addendum (A1–A5).

3.4 **Taxes.**

All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes and shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.4 (Taxes)** of the Existing SOW and **Section 3.4 of the Master Agreement**. If Client is required to withhold Taxes, Client shall gross up payments so that Vendor receives the full amount as if no withholding had occurred.

3.5 **Late Payment and Suspension.**

Any undisputed amount not received by Vendor within the Net 30 period shall accrue interest and may result in suspension of Services in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement. Given the high‑risk nature and criticality of the Services, Vendor may, after giving ten (10) days’ written notice of non‑payment, suspend or limit the Expanded Managed Services until all undisputed amounts are paid in full.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**

Invoice disputes shall continue to be governed by **Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement. Client shall pay all undisputed portions of any invoice when due, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve any disputed portions.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**

Client shall reimburse Vendor for reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses actually incurred in performing the Services under this Addendum, consistent with **Section 3.7** of the Existing SOW. Any travel or onsite support required for additional regions shall be subject to Client’s travel and expense policies.

3.8 **Price Adjustments.**

Vendor may adjust the Expanded Managed Services rates annually in accordance with **Section 3.8 (Price Adjustments)** of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement; provided that any increase in the monthly EMS fee shall not exceed seven percent (7%) per annum during the Extended SOW Term, unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Extended SOW Term; Modification of Section 4.1 of Existing SOW.**

The Parties agree that, solely with respect to the Services and Deliverables covered by this Addendum, the SOW Term defined in **Section 1.15** and elaborated in **Section 4.1** of the Existing SOW is hereby **extended** such that this Addendum, together with the Existing SOW as modified hereby, shall continue in full force and effect until **22 July 2027** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or Article IV of the Existing SOW and/or Master Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal for Expanded Managed Services.**

The Parties may mutually agree in writing to further extend the Expanded Managed Services beyond the Addendum Expiration Date on terms and conditions to be agreed, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal for Managed Services)** of the Existing SOW.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

Either Party may terminate this Addendum (and/or the Expanded Managed Services under the Extended SOW Term), in whole or in part, for convenience by providing at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party, in accordance with **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Existing SOW. Client acknowledges that early termination for convenience may result in additional wind‑down and transition Fees to be agreed in good faith, particularly in light of the advanced monitoring and automation components.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

Termination for cause, including for material breach or non‑payment, shall be governed by **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Existing SOW and the Master Agreement. Any such termination notice shall specify whether it applies to (a) this Addendum only, (b) the Existing SOW as amended, or (c) the Master Agreement and all related SOWs and addenda.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition Services.**

4.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum (or the Existing SOW as amended hereby), Client shall pay Vendor all Fees and reimbursable expenses due and payable for Services performed and Deliverables delivered (or in progress) up to the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.5.2** of the Existing SOW.

4.5.2 At Client’s written request received no later than ninety (90) days before the Addendum Expiration Date or effective date of termination, Vendor shall provide **Transition Services** during the Transition Services Period. Such services shall, at a minimum, include:  
  a. Knowledge transfer and handover of operational documentation (including D15 and D20);  
  b. Cooperation with any replacement service provider designated by Client;  
  c. Reasonable assistance in migrating monitoring and automation tooling to Client or such replacement provider.

4.5.3 Transition Services shall be charged on a time‑and‑materials basis at Vendor’s then‑current rates, or at pre‑agreed rates in a Change Order, consistent with **Section 4.5.3** of the Existing SOW.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Master Agreement or Existing SOW.**

If the Master Agreement or the Existing SOW is terminated or expires, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue this Addendum under substantially the same terms, consistent with **Section 4.5.1** of the Existing SOW.

4.7 **Survival.**

Those provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination or expiration, including Articles III (to the extent of accrued payment obligations), V, VI, VIII, IX, XI, XII (to the extent of earned credits and post‑incident obligations), XIV, XVI, and any provisions which by their nature are intended to survive, shall survive.

The confidentiality provisions of **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the Existing SOW and **Article V of the Master Agreement** are incorporated by reference and shall apply in full to all Confidential Information exchanged under this Addendum, including any Additional Deliverables, Service Performance Reports, Compliance and Risk Reports, and EMS‑related pricing.

The Parties agree that:  
  a. The content, terms, pricing, and **Addendum Contract Value of USD $3,758,026** under this Addendum shall be deemed **Confidential Information** of both Parties, as contemplated in **Section 5.2(a)** of the Existing SOW; and

  - For uptime below 99.0% in a given month: service credit equal to **30%** of the monthly Expanded Managed Services Fees;  
  - For repeated failure (three (3) or more months within any rolling twelve (12) month period) to meet Severity 1 response time commitments: additional credit equal to **5%** of the average monthly EMS Fees for such period.

12.4.2 Service credits shall be applied against future Expanded Managed Services Fees and shall constitute Client’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for Service Level failures, without prejudice to Client’s rights to terminate for cause under Article IV or to seek other remedies for breach unrelated to Service Level performance.
