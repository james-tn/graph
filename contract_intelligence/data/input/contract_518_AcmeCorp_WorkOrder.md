## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **General Scope; Relationship to Master Agreement.**  

2.1.1 Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Master Agreement, Vendor shall provide the Services described in this Article II (the “**Services**”) and deliver the associated Deliverables (the “**Deliverables**”) in accordance with the Specifications, timelines, and Service Levels set forth herein.  

2.1.2 This Work Order **supplements and operates under** the framework established by the Master Agreement. In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Work Order and the Master Agreement, the provisions of the Master Agreement shall control in accordance with **Section 2.2.2** and **Section 16.11** of the Master Agreement, except where this Work Order expressly states an intention to supersede a specific provision of the Master Agreement with respect to this Work Order only (e.g., **Net 60 Payment Terms** in **Section 3.2.4** below).

2.2 **Program Objectives.**  

Vendor shall design, implement, and support an enterprise technology solution for Client with the following key objectives:

(a) Consolidation of multiple legacy platforms into a unified, cloud-based environment;  
(b) Integration of Client’s core systems of record with new workflow and analytics capabilities;  
(c) Migration of defined data sets from legacy systems with appropriate validation and reconciliation;  
(d) Establishment of robust operational runbooks, monitoring, and support processes; and  
(e) Provision of ongoing managed services, including incident management, problem management, minor enhancements, and periodic optimization.

2.3 **Project Phases.**  

The Program shall be executed in the following Project Phases. Each Phase may overlap with others as mutually agreed, provided that dependencies are respected.

2.3.1 **Phase 1 – Assessment & Design (Estimated Duration: 12 weeks)**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Conduct discovery workshops with key Client stakeholders across business, IT, and security functions;  
(b) Document current-state business processes and technical architecture;  
(c) Define future-state solution architecture, including system components, integrations, and data flows;  
(d) Produce high-level and detailed design documents, including user stories and acceptance criteria;  
(e) Develop a consolidated Project Plan, including Milestones, dependencies, and resource allocations; and  
(f) Prepare a Risk and Issues log and initial mitigation plan.

2.3.2 **Phase 2 – Build & Integrations (Estimated Duration: 24 weeks)**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Configure the core platform in accordance with approved design documents and Specifications;  
(b) Develop integrations with Client’s designated systems using mutually agreed integration technologies;  
(c) Implement agreed security controls and user role definitions;  
(d) Develop custom workflows, reports, and dashboards as specified by Client; and  
(e) Prepare technical documentation, including configuration workbooks, integration specifications, and deployment guides.

2.3.3 **Phase 3 – Testing, Training & Cutover (Estimated Duration: 16 weeks)**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Support Client in developing test plans and scripts, consistent with the acceptance process in **Section 2.4** of the Master Agreement;  
(b) Conduct system integration testing (“SIT”) and support Client-led user acceptance testing (“UAT”);  
(c) Resolve defects identified during SIT and UAT and prepare release notes;  
(d) Provide training (train-the-trainer and end-user sessions) and deliver associated training materials;  
(e) Assist with cutover planning, go-live readiness assessments, and execution of go-live activities; and  
(f) Provide post-go-live hypercare support for a period of four (4) weeks following initial production deployment.

2.3.4 **Phase 4 – Managed Services & Optimization (Estimated Duration: from Go-Live through December 27, 2030)**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Provide ongoing Level 2 and Level 3 support services, including incident response, triage, and resolution consistent with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Master Agreement and **Article XII** of this Work Order;  
(b) Perform routine maintenance activities, including periodic patching and updates as authorized by Client;  
(c) Provide minor enhancements and configuration changes within an agreed annual enhancement capacity;  
(d) Conduct quarterly service reviews with Client, including KPI reporting, incident trends, and improvement opportunities; and  
(e) Participate in annual strategic planning sessions to align the solution with Client’s evolving business needs.

2.4 **Phase Deliverables.**  

Without limiting the generality of **Section 1.9 (Deliverables)** and **Section 2.4 (Deliverables and Acceptance)** of the Master Agreement, Vendor shall deliver at least the following Deliverables:

(a) **Phase 1 Deliverables:**  
   (i) Current-State Assessment Report;  
   (ii) Future-State Architecture Diagram;  
   (iii) Detailed Solution Design Document;  
   (iv) Project Plan and RAID (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies) Log.

(b) **Phase 2 Deliverables:**  
   (i) Configuration Workbook(s);  
   (ii) Integration Specifications and API Documentation;  
   (iii) Security and Access Control Matrix;  
   (iv) Technical Design and Deployment Guides.

(c) **Phase 3 Deliverables:**  
   (i) Test Plan and Test Scripts;  
   (ii) UAT Issue Log and Resolution Report;  
   (iii) Training Materials (slide decks, user guides, quick reference sheets);  
   (iv) Go-Live Runbook and Cutover Plan;  
   (v) Hypercare Completion Report.

(d) **Phase 4 Deliverables:**  
   (i) Monthly Incident and Performance Reports;  
   (ii) Quarterly Service Review Reports;  
   (iii) Annual Optimization Roadmap;  
   (iv) Updated runbooks and documentation as changes are implemented.

2.5 **Milestones and Timelines.**  

2.5.1 The Parties shall mutually agree in writing, within thirty (30) days of the Service Commencement Date, on a detailed Milestone schedule that aligns with the Phase durations described above. Each Milestone shall clearly reference associated Deliverables, acceptance criteria, and payment triggers as set forth in **Article III** below.

2.5.2 For each Deliverable or Milestone, Client shall have the **Acceptance Period (WO)** specified in **Section 1.17** above, which is consistent with the Acceptance Period set forth in **Section 2.4.2** of the Master Agreement, to review and test the Deliverable or Milestone for material conformity with the Specifications.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

Vendor shall perform the Services:

(a) In a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with **Section 2.5.1** of the Master Agreement and industry best practices;  
(b) In accordance with the Specifications, WO Service Levels, and other requirements set forth herein and in the Master Agreement; and  
(c) In compliance with all applicable laws, including Data Protection Laws, as required by **Section 7.2(d)** and **Article XI** of the Master Agreement.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities Under this Work Order.**  

In addition to Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6** of the Master Agreement, Client shall:

(a) Provide timely access to subject-matter experts and decision-makers to support workshops, design approvals, and testing;
