## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Services

2.1.1 Titan shall provide to Contoso certain industrial technology integration and related professional services, which may include, without limitation: (a) assessment of Contoso’s existing systems, equipment, and processes; (b) design and documentation of an integrated solution architecture; (c) configuration, customization, and implementation of agreed technologies; (d) development of integrations between Contoso’s existing systems and new solutions; (e) testing, acceptance support, and deployment assistance; and (f) ongoing support and maintenance services as may be agreed.

2.1.2 The Services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with generally accepted industry standards for providers of similar services, and in substantial conformity with the Specifications and any applicable SLA. Titan shall provide personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and qualifications to perform the Services assigned to them.

### 2.2 Detailed Scope and Deliverables

2.2.1 The Parties anticipate that the Services will encompass multiple phases, including but not limited to:

(a) **Discovery and Requirements Gathering**: Titan shall conduct interviews, workshops, and on-site assessments (as needed) to identify Contoso’s business objectives, constraints, and technical environment. Titan shall produce a **Requirements Specification Document** summarizing key findings, constraints, and assumptions.

(b) **Solution Design and Architecture**: Titan shall prepare a **Solution Architecture Document**, including system topology, data flows, integration points, security considerations, and performance expectations. This document shall serve as the foundation for subsequent configuration and implementation activities.

(c) **Configuration and Customization**: Titan shall configure the agreed systems and, where necessary, develop custom components or scripts, subject to the terms of **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)**. Such configuration shall include integration with Contoso’s designated systems, subject to Contoso providing all necessary access, credentials, and approvals.

(d) **Testing and Quality Assurance**: Titan shall develop a **Test Plan** and execute functional, integration, and performance testing. Titan shall document test results and remedy material non-conformities identified during testing in accordance with a mutually agreed defect classification and remediation process.

(e) **Deployment and Go-Live Support**: Titan shall support Contoso during deployment, including cutover planning, execution support, and immediate post go-live assistance. Titan shall provide a **Deployment Report** summarizing activities executed, issues encountered, and resolutions adopted.

(f) **Training and Knowledge Transfer**: Where agreed, Titan shall provide training sessions (onsite or remote) and knowledge transfer to Contoso’s designated personnel, including delivery of training materials in electronic format.

(g) **Support and Maintenance**: If specified in a statement of work or SLA, Titan shall provide ongoing support, including incident management, patching, and minor enhancements, within the response and resolution times set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**.

2.2.2 The key Deliverables under this Agreement may include, without limitation:

- Requirements Specification Document  
- Solution Architecture Document  
- Configuration/Customization Artifacts (scripts, configuration files, etc.)  
- Test Plan and Test Results Documentation  
- Deployment Plan and Deployment Report  
- Training Materials  
- Operational Runbooks or Support Documentation  
- Periodic Status Reports and Project Plans  

2.2.3 The Parties may detail additional or more specific Services and Deliverables in one or more statements of work (**“SOWs”**). Each SOW, when executed by authorized representatives of both Parties, shall be incorporated into and governed by this Agreement. In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Agreement and an SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall prevail, unless the SOW expressly states the Parties’ intent to override specific provisions of this Agreement.

### 2.3 Performance Standards and Service Levels

2.3.1 Titan shall perform the Services in accordance with the performance standards and service levels set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** and any applicable SOW. Such standards may include, but are not limited to: (a) system uptime commitments; (b) response and resolution times for support tickets; (c) escalation procedures; and (d) reporting obligations.

2.3.2 Titan shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet agreed milestones and timelines. Unless expressly stated as “time is of the essence” for a specific milestone in the applicable SOW, failure to meet a timeline shall not constitute a material breach, provided Titan uses diligent efforts to mitigate delays and provides Contoso with reasonable notice and a recovery plan.

2.3.3 Contoso acknowledges and agrees that Titan’s ability to meet performance standards and deliverables is dependent upon Contoso’s timely provision of: (a) necessary information, decisions, and approvals; (b) access to systems, facilities, and personnel; and (c) third-party licenses or consents required for Titan to perform the Services. Delays or failures by Contoso to provide such support shall excuse Titan from related performance failures and may entitle Titan to schedule and fee adjustments pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)**.

### 2.4 Contoso Responsibilities

2.4.1 Contoso shall:

(a) designate a primary contact person and steering committee (if applicable) to coordinate with Titan;  
(b) provide timely access to relevant personnel, systems, and facilities;  
(c) ensure that any data, content, or materials supplied to Titan are accurate, complete, and lawful; and  
(d) ensure that any third-party products or services on which the Services depend are properly licensed and maintained.

2.4.2 Contoso shall be responsible for backing up its data and maintaining appropriate disaster recovery measures, except where such measures are expressly included in the Services. Titan shall have no responsibility for loss of data except to the extent caused by Titan’s gross negligence or willful misconduct and subject to the limitations set forth in **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)**.

### 2.5 Acceptance of Deliverables

2.5.1 Unless otherwise specified in an SOW, Contoso shall have ten (10) Business Days from receipt of a Deliverable (the **“Review Period”**) to review and either: (a) provide written acceptance; or (b) provide a written rejection detailing material non-conformities with the Specifications.

If Contoso provides a timely, written rejection, Titan shall use commercially reasonable efforts to correct material non-conformities and resubmit the Deliverable. The Parties shall then repeat the review procedure, with a Review Period of five (5) Business Days. Deemed acceptance shall apply to resubmissions if Contoso again fails to respond within the applicable Review Period.

- **30% (US$183,441.60)** upon delivery of the Solution Architecture Document and completion of major configuration activities as documented in a milestone acceptance certificate;

3.3.1 Any Services requested by Contoso that are outside the agreed scope of the SOW or this Agreement shall, if accepted by Titan, be charged on a time-and-materials basis at Titan’s then-current standard rates, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.3.2 Titan shall maintain records of time spent on time-and-materials work and shall provide summaries of such records with its invoices upon request.

### 3.4 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.4.1 Titan shall issue invoices to Contoso upon achievement of the relevant milestones, or monthly in arrears for time-and-materials or recurring services, as applicable.

3.4.2 **Payment Terms (Net 60)**: Contoso shall pay each undisputed invoice within **sixty (60) calendar days** from the date of the invoice, via bank transfer or other method agreed in writing. Payment shall be made in United States Dollars (USD), unless otherwise agreed.

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of: (a) one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month; or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, calculated from the due date until the date of payment in full.

3.5.2 Titan shall be entitled to suspend performance of the Services and/or withhold Deliverables if any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after written notice from Titan, without prejudice to any other remedies available under this Agreement or at law.

3.6.1 All fees are exclusive of any applicable sales, use, value-added, withholding, or similar taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for all such taxes (excluding taxes based on Titan’s net income) and shall pay or reimburse Titan for such taxes as required by law.

3.6.2 If Contoso is required by law to withhold any taxes from amounts payable to Titan, Contoso shall: (a) withhold only the minimum required amount; (b) remit such taxes to the appropriate authorities; and (c) provide Titan with official receipts or other documentation evidencing such payment.

(a) all rights and licenses granted by one Party to the other shall cease, except as expressly provided in **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** or elsewhere;

4.5.1 The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration, including without limitation **Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI**, shall survive any termination or expiration of this Agreement.
