## ARTICLE III  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

3.1 **Overview.** This Addendum defines additional and extended Services to be performed by the Service Provider in connection with the ERP and CRM solution implemented pursuant to **Article II** of the Parent SOW. The Addendum Services are intended to (a) optimize and tune the solution post go‑live, (b) design and implement advanced analytics and reporting capabilities, (c) enhance knowledge transfer and documentation, and (d) extend managed services support beyond the Parent SOW Term, all within a **medium risk** framework consistent with the Parent SOW.

3.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW.**  

   (a) The discovery, design, integration, data migration, testing, training, go‑live, and managed services phases described in **Sections 2.2.1 through 2.2.6** of the Parent SOW are presumed to be substantially completed or in steady‑state by the time Addendum Services commence.  

   (b) The Addendum Services shall leverage the Deliverables produced under the Parent SOW, including without limitation the **Requirements Specification Document, High-Level Solution Architecture Document, Detailed Design Specifications, Integration Design Document, Data Migration Plan, Go-Live Readiness Assessment, Training Materials, Post-Implementation Review Report**, and ongoing **Monthly Service Performance Reports** and **Quarterly Service Review Reports**.  

   (c) The Service Provider shall comply with the **Performance Standards** set forth in **Section 2.3** of the Parent SOW (timely, professional, workmanlike manner in accordance with generally accepted industry standards and applicable laws), which are hereby incorporated by reference and supplemented by the obligations in this Addendum.

3.3 **Workstreams.** The Addendum Services shall be organized into the following workstreams:

3.3.1 **Optimization Workstream.**  
The Service Provider shall perform optimization activities intended to improve system performance, stability, and usability of the ERP and CRM solution implemented under the Parent SOW, including:

- (a) **Performance Assessment:** Conduct an in-depth performance and usage assessment of key ERP and CRM modules (finance, procurement, inventory, sales, customer support) relying on logs, existing **Monthly Service Performance Reports** (as referenced in Section 2.2.6 of the Parent SOW), and end-user feedback.  
- (b) **Configuration Tuning:** Recommend and implement configuration changes (e.g., batch job schedules, caching strategies, indexing, workflow routing, and notification rules) to reduce processing times and increase responsiveness.  
- (c) **Process Streamlining:** Identify high-friction workflows (e.g., quote-to-order, order-to-cash, purchase-to-pay) and propose adjustments to reduce manual steps, leveraging the **Requirements Specification Document** and **Current State Assessment Report** from Phase 1 of the Parent SOW.  
- (d) **Issue Remediation:** Address recurring issues identified in **Incident and Problem Logs** and **Test Execution and Defect Reports** (Parent SOW Sections 2.2.4 and 2.2.6) by applying targeted fixes and minor enhancements within the scope and budget of this Addendum.  
- (e) **Optimization Report:** Produce an **Optimization Findings and Recommendations Report** summarizing baseline metrics, actions taken, and post‑optimization KPIs.

3.3.2 **Advanced Analytics Workstream.**  
The Service Provider shall design and implement advanced analytics capabilities, including:

- (a) **Requirements Gathering for Analytics:** Conduct focused workshops with business stakeholders and the Company’s data team to identify key performance indicators, dashboard requirements, and data sources, building on the **Requirements Specification Document** and integration points noted in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent SOW.  
- (b) **Data Model Alignment:** Align ERP and CRM data structures with the Company’s data warehouse or analytics platform, using existing **Data Mapping and Transformation Specifications** and **Integration Components and Documentation**.  
- (c) **Dashboard and Report Development:** Develop up to a specified number (to be set in a Change Order if exceeded) of interactive dashboards and standard reports for finance, sales pipeline, customer service performance, and inventory optimization.  
- (d) **Security and Access Controls:** Implement role-based security for analytics assets consistent with the Company’s security requirements and **Data Protection Laws** as referenced in **Section 1.7** and **Article XI** of the Parent SOW.  
- (e) **Analytics Runbook:** Create an **Analytics Operations Runbook** detailing data refresh schedules, error handling, and governance procedures.

3.3.3 **Knowledge Transfer and Enablement Workstream.**  
To supplement the training provided in **Phase 5 – Training, Go-Live, and Transition** of the Parent SOW, the Service Provider shall:

- (a) Develop and deliver advanced, role-based training sessions for super-users, system administrators, and the Company’s internal IT and support teams, including hands-on labs and scenario-based exercises.  
- (b) Produce a **Knowledge Transfer Pack** that consolidates key artifacts from the Parent SOW (e.g., **Configuration Workbook**, **Customization and Extension Documentation**, **Cutover Plan and Runbook**) and this Addendum into a coherent reference set.  
- (c) Facilitate shadowing and reverse-shadowing sessions in which Company personnel observe and then perform typical support tasks under supervision.  
- (d) Provide a **Training Completion and Competency Report**, including attendance, evaluation results, and recommendations for ongoing internal capability development.

3.3.4 **Extended Managed Services.**  
During the Extended Managed Services Period, the Service Provider shall:

- (a) Continue to provide ongoing application and technical support consistent with **Phase 6 – Managed Services** described in **Section 2.2.6** of the Parent SOW, subject to the Enhanced Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum;  
- (b) Maintain and update the ERP and CRM solution, including application of security patches, minor configuration changes, and support for the analytics components developed under this Addendum;  
- (c) Provide **Monthly Service Performance Reports** and **Quarterly Service Review Reports** as required under the Parent SOW, expanded to include metrics relating to the optimization and analytics components;  
- (d) Ensure that any new or updated Deliverables remain in compliance with the warranties in **Section 7.2** of the Parent SOW (professional and workmanlike performance, material conformance with Specifications, compliance with law).

3.4 **Addendum Deliverables.** Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, the Addendum Deliverables shall include, at a minimum:

- (a) **Optimization Findings and Recommendations Report**;  
- (b) Updated **Configuration Workbook** excerpt(s) reflecting changes implemented under the Optimization Workstream;  
- (c) One or more **Performance Benchmarking Reports** (baseline and post‑optimization);  
- (d) Configured **Analytics Dashboards and Reports**, with associated technical documentation;  
- (e) **Analytics Operations Runbook**;  
- (f) **Knowledge Transfer Pack**, including training materials, step-by-step guides, and FAQs;  
- (g) **Training Completion and Competency Report**;  
- (h) Updated **Incident and Problem Logs**, **Change and Release Notes**, and **Monthly Service Performance Reports** incorporating Extended Managed Services; and  
- (i) Any other deliverable expressly identified in a Change Order executed under **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW as applied through this Addendum.

3.5 **Timeline and Milestones.**  

   (a) The detailed project schedule, including milestones for each workstream, shall be agreed by the Parties within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date and documented in a project plan referenced in this Addendum.  

   (b) As a guideline, the Parties anticipate:  
   - Optimization Workstream: commencement within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date and completion within ninety (90) days thereafter;  
   - Advanced Analytics Workstream: commencement following completion of the majority of the Optimization Workstream and completion within one hundred twenty (120) days after its commencement;  
   - Knowledge Transfer and Enablement: to be scheduled to align with the completion of material optimization and analytics deliverables and concluded no later than sixty (60) days before the Addendum Expiration Date;  
   - Extended Managed Services: commencing immediately following the Parent SOW Term and continuing until the Addendum Expiration Date, unless terminated earlier per **Article IV**.

3.6 **Performance Standards and Acceptance.**  

   (a) The Service Provider shall perform Addendum Services in accordance with the **Performance Standards** set forth in **Section 2.3** of the Parent SOW.  

   (b) Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables shall follow the **Acceptance Procedures** set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW, except that where specific Acceptance Criteria for an Addendum Deliverable are agreed in writing (e.g., in the project plan or Change Order), such criteria shall govern.  

   (c) For each Addendum Deliverable, the Company shall have ten (10) Business Days (unless otherwise agreed in writing) to review and either Accept or reject the Deliverable, with detailed reasons for any rejection. The deemed Acceptance mechanism in **Section 2.6(e)** of the Parent SOW shall apply.

## ARTICLE IV  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 **Addendum Contract Value.** The total not‑to‑exceed value for the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables under this Addendum shall be **US $205,184** (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**), exclusive of applicable taxes and reimbursable expenses, and subject to adjustment only via executed Change Orders.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW Contract Value.** The Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US $2,310,799** set forth in **Section 1.6** of the Parent SOW, unless the Parties expressly agree in a Change Order to reallocate unused amounts from the Parent SOW. For clarity, the monetary cap in the Parent SOW applies to the Parent SOW only; this Addendum has its own separate not‑to‑exceed amount as stated above.

4.3 **Fee Structure.**  

   (a) **Fixed-Fee Components.** The following components of the Addendum Services shall be billed on a fixed-fee basis, included within the Addendum Contract Value:

   - Optimization Workstream: **US $72,000**  
   - Advanced Analytics Workstream: **US $88,000**  
   - Knowledge Transfer and Enablement Workstream: **US $30,000**

   (b) **Extended Managed Services Fees.** During the Extended Managed Services Period, the Company shall pay:

   - A base monthly fee of **US $6,000** for ongoing managed services as described in **Section 3.3.4**, covering up to **40 hours** of support and maintenance per month related to the ERP/CRM solution and analytics components; and  
   - An overage rate of **US $185 per hour** for any hours exceeding 40 hours in a given calendar month, subject to prior written approval by the Company for any overage exceeding ten (10) hours in such month.

   The aggregate of all Extended Managed Services fees (base and overage) shall be managed so that the Addendum Contract Value is not exceeded without a Change Order.

4.4 **Invoicing Milestones.**  

   (a) **Fixed-Fee Workstreams.** The fixed-fee amounts shall be invoiced based on milestone achievement, consistent with the philosophy of **Section 3.2** of the Parent SOW, as follows:

   - Optimization Workstream (US $72,000):  
     - 40% (US $28,800) upon commencement of the Optimization Workstream;  
     - 40% (US $28,800) upon delivery of the **Optimization Findings and Recommendations Report** and implementation of agreed optimizations;  
     - 20% (US $14,400) upon Acceptance of the Optimization Deliverables per **Section 3.6** of this Addendum.

   - Advanced Analytics Workstream (US $88,000):  
     - 40% (US $35,200) upon commencement of the Advanced Analytics Workstream;  
     - 40% (US $35,200) upon delivery of the initial configured analytics dashboards and the **Analytics Operations Runbook**;  
     - 20% (US $17,600) upon Acceptance of all Analytics Deliverables.

   - Knowledge Transfer and Enablement Workstream (US $30,000):  
     - 50% (US $15,000) upon commencement of training activities and delivery of draft training materials;  
     - 50% (US $15,000) upon completion of training sessions and delivery of the **Training Completion and Competency Report** and **Knowledge Transfer Pack**.

   (b) **Extended Managed Services.** The base monthly Extended Managed Services fees and any approved overage fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, accompanied by a detailed breakdown of hours, activities, and applicable service credits.

4.5 **Payment Terms; Net 30.** All invoices issued under this Addendum shall reference both **ADD-PHO-202502-146** and **SOW-PHO-202502-125**, and shall be payable in accordance with **Net 30** terms, consistent with **Section 3.5 (Invoicing and Payment Terms)** of the Parent SOW. The Company shall pay all undisputed amounts within thirty (30) days of receipt, and any dispute of an invoice shall follow the procedure in **Section 3.5** of the Parent SOW.

4.6 **Late Payment.** Undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest as stated in **Section 3.6 (Late Payment)** of the Parent SOW (lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law).

4.7 **Taxes.** Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent SOW.

4.8 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

   (a) The Service Provider shall be reimbursed for reasonable and pre-approved out-of-pocket travel and lodging expenses incurred in connection with onsite Addendum Services, in accordance with the expense policy and requirements set out in **Section 3.4** of the Parent SOW.  

   (b) Unless otherwise agreed in writing, reimbursable expenses under this Addendum shall be **in addition to** the Addendum Contract Value and shall be capped at **US $10,000** in the aggregate.

## ARTICLE V  
TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 **Addendum Term.** This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall remain in effect until the earlier of: (a) completion of all Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables; or (b) **November 05, 2026** (the **Addendum Expiration Date**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article V**.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

   (a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, in whole or in part, upon not less than **forty-five (45) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.  

   (b) Upon termination for convenience by the Company, the Company shall pay the Service Provider for: (i) all Addendum Services properly performed and Accepted as of the effective date of termination, (ii) any approved expenses incurred, and (iii) any non‑cancellable commitments specifically made in support of the Addendum Services, provided such commitments were pre-approved in writing by the Company.  

   (c) Upon termination for convenience by the Service Provider, the Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption and cooperate in an orderly transition of the Addendum Services, consistent with **Section 4.3** and **Section 4.6 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent SOW.

5.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

- (a) Materially breaches any provision of this Addendum or applicable provisions of the Parent SOW with respect to the Addendum Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or ten (10) days for payment-related breaches) after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  
- (b) Becomes subject to an insolvency event described in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent SOW.

5.5 **Effect of Parent SOW Termination.**  

   (a) If the Parent SOW is terminated for cause by the Company pursuant to **Section 4.4** of the Parent SOW, the Company may, at its option, terminate this Addendum in whole or in part, effective immediately upon written notice, without penalty.  

   (b) If the Parent SOW is terminated for convenience by either Party, the Parties shall confer in good faith to determine whether this Addendum shall: (i) terminate concurrently, or (ii) remain in effect as a standalone agreement, in which case the provisions of the Parent SOW required to support and interpret this Addendum shall be deemed incorporated herein by reference and shall survive solely for such purpose.  

   (c) In all cases, the limitation of liability, indemnification, confidentiality, IP, data protection, and dispute resolution provisions of the Parent SOW (as modified by this Addendum) shall survive in accordance with their terms.

5.6 **Effect of Termination of Addendum.** Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

- (a) The Service Provider shall cease performing Addendum Services, except as may be required under **Section 5.7 (Transition Assistance)**;  
- (b) The Company shall pay the Service Provider for all Addendum Services properly performed and Accepted and all approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, subject to offset as permitted under the Parent SOW;  
- (c) All rights and licenses granted to the Company that are designated as perpetual or that by their nature should survive shall remain in effect; and  
- (d) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 5.5** of the Parent SOW.

5.7 **Transition Assistance.** At the Company’s request, in connection with expiration or termination of this Addendum (other than termination by the Service Provider for the Company’s uncured material breach), the Service Provider shall provide transition assistance for up to ninety (90) days following the effective date of termination on time-and-materials terms at its then-current rates, consistent with **Section 4.6** of the Parent SOW.
