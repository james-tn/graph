## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Software and Services

2.1.1 Vanguard shall provide Contoso with a License to use Vanguard’s proprietary software solution(s) as specified in one or more Statements of Work. 2.1.2 Vanguard shall also perform the Services described in this Agreement and in each SOW, which may include:  

   (a) initial assessment and solution design;  
   
   (b) installation, configuration, and tuning of the Software (or provisioning of SaaS environments);  
   
   (c) data migration from Contoso’s existing systems to the Platform;  
   
   (d) integration of the Platform with Contoso’s existing applications and infrastructure;  
   
   (e) user acceptance testing support and remediation of identified issues;  
   
   (f) provision of training sessions, training materials, and knowledge transfer to Contoso’s personnel; and  
   
   (g) ongoing Maintenance and Support Services and minor enhancements.  

### 2.2 Detailed Scope and Deliverables

2.2.1 The specific scope of Services, detailed functional and technical requirements, implementation phases, and Deliverables will be described in one or more SOWs executed by the Parties. Each SOW will, at a minimum, specify:

   (a) the Software modules or components to be licensed;  
   
   (b) the environment in which the Software is to be deployed (e.g., SaaS, on-premises, hybrid);  
   
   (c) the detailed tasks and responsibilities of each Party;  
   
   (d) the specific Deliverables, their descriptions, and acceptance criteria;  
   
   (e) the project schedule, including milestones and estimated completion dates; and  
   
   (f) the Fees associated with such SOW and the applicable payment schedule.

2.2.2 By way of illustration and without limitation, typical Deliverables under this Agreement may include:

   (a) a solution design and architecture document, including system topology and integration points;  
   
   (b) configured Software environments, including test, staging, and production instances;  
   
   (c) integration interfaces connecting the Software to Contoso’s ERP, CRM, identity management, and other systems;  
   
   (d) data migration scripts and documented mapping of legacy data fields to the Platform’s data model;  
   
   (e) user guides and configuration documentation tailored to Contoso’s implementation; and  
   
   (f) test plans, test scripts, and user acceptance test results.

### 3.1 Contract Value

3.1.1 The total estimated contract value for the Term of this Agreement, including all anticipated Software license/subscription Fees and Services Fees, is **$2,434,488** (the “**Contract Value**”), subject to adjustment in accordance with Change Orders or additional SOWs executed by the Parties.

3.2.1 The Fees payable by Contoso to Vanguard under this Agreement shall include, as applicable:

   (a) **License or Subscription Fees** for the right to use the Software, which may be based on metrics such as number of Authorized Users, transactions, or modules licensed;

### 3.3 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.3.1 Unless otherwise stated in an applicable SOW:

   (a) License/subscription Fees shall be invoiced annually in advance;  
   
   (b) Implementation and professional Services Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears based on time and materials, or upon achievement of milestones for fixed-fee projects; and  
   
   (c) Maintenance and Support Fees shall be invoiced annually in advance.

3.3.2 All invoices shall be payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) days** from the date of invoice (**“Net 60”**). Payments shall be made in U.S. dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or other method agreed by the Parties.

3.3.3 Contoso may withhold payment of any amounts disputed in good faith, provided that:

   (a) Contoso pays all undisputed amounts in accordance with this Section;  
   
   (b) Contoso notifies Vanguard in writing within the payment period of the nature and basis of the dispute; and  
   
   (c) the Parties work diligently and in good faith to resolve the dispute in accordance with Article XIV.  

Contoso’s failure to pay any disputed amounts that are later determined to be properly due shall be subject to late charges as set forth below, calculated from the original due date.

### 3.4 Late Payment

3.4.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month, or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower, from the due date until paid in full.

3.4.2 If any undisputed Fees remain unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after written notice of late payment, Vanguard may, upon an additional ten (10) days’ written notice, suspend provision of the Services and/or access to the Software until all overdue amounts and applicable interest are paid in full, provided that Vanguard shall resume Services promptly upon receipt of such amounts.

3.5.1 Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including, without limitation, travel, lodging, meals, and other customary business expenses, provided that such expenses are consistent with Contoso’s travel and expense policies previously made available to Vanguard.

3.5.2 Vanguard shall provide documentation or receipts for reimbursable expenses in accordance with Contoso’s standard requirements, and such expenses shall be invoiced in arrears.

### 3.6 Taxes

3.6.1 All Fees are exclusive of any sales, use, value-added, goods and services, excise, or other similar taxes, duties, or charges (collectively, “**Taxes**”). Contoso shall be responsible for all Taxes imposed on or with respect to the transactions contemplated by this Agreement, other than taxes based on Vanguard’s income, property, or employees.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

4.1.1 The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **February 27, 2028** (the “**Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the “**Term**”).

4.1.2 The Parties may mutually agree in writing to renew or extend this Agreement for additional periods on terms to be negotiated in good faith.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.2.2 In the event of termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall pay Vanguard:

   (a) all Fees for Software access and Maintenance and Support Services provided through the effective date of termination (with any prepaid amounts to be prorated and refunded for periods following the effective date of termination, except where an SOW expressly states that certain Fees are non-refundable); and  
   
   (b) all Fees for Services actually performed but not yet invoiced as of the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments and approved expenses incurred by Vanguard.

4.2.3 In the event of termination for convenience by Vanguard, Vanguard shall refund to Contoso any prepaid, unearned Fees for periods following the effective date of termination.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

   (a) materially breaches this Agreement or such SOW and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof (or such longer cure period as the Parties may agree in writing, where the breach is not reasonably curable within thirty (30) days and the breaching Party has commenced and is diligently pursuing such cure); or  
   
   (b) becomes the subject of a petition in bankruptcy or any other proceeding relating to insolvency, receivership, liquidation, or assignment for the benefit of creditors that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days.

4.3.2 Notwithstanding the foregoing, Vanguard may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately upon written notice if Contoso materially breaches its payment obligations hereunder and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

   (a) all Licenses granted to Contoso under this Agreement shall immediately terminate (except to the extent expressly stated otherwise in an SOW);  
   
   (b) Contoso shall cease all use of the Software and Documentation and, at Vanguard’s election, either return or destroy any Vanguard Confidential Information and certify in writing such destruction; and  
   
   (c) each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information, in accordance with Section 5.4.

4.4.2 Termination shall not relieve either Party of the obligation to pay any Fees accrued or payable prior to the effective date of termination, or any other amounts owed under this Agreement.

4.4.3 Sections which by their nature are intended to survive, including without limitation Sections 3.4, 3.6, 4.4, and Articles V, VI (as applicable), VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI, shall survive any expiration or termination of this Agreement.

### 4.5 Transition Assistance

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement (other than termination by Vanguard for Contoso’s uncured material breach), Contoso may request reasonable transition assistance services from Vanguard to facilitate the orderly transition of Contoso’s operations from the Software and Services to Contoso or a third-party provider.  

4.5.2 Any such transition assistance shall be subject to a separate SOW or Change Order and shall be provided at Vanguard’s then-current time-and-materials rates, unless otherwise agreed.

5.2.1 Confidential Information shall not include information that:

   (a) is or becomes generally known to the public without breach of any obligation owed to the Disclosing Party;

   (d) was independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information, as demonstrated by contemporaneous written evidence.

5.3.1 The Receiving Party shall:

   (a) use the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information solely for the purposes of performing or exercising its rights under this Agreement;

5.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, or upon written request by the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party in its possession or control and, upon request, certify in writing its compliance with this obligation, except that the Receiving Party may retain copies of Confidential Information:

### 5.6 Survival

5.6.1 The obligations under this Article V shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for **five (5) years** following the expiration or termination of this Agreement; provided that trade secrets and Personal Data shall be kept confidential for so long as such information remains a trade secret or Personal Data under applicable law.

   (c) Uptime of < 95.0%: 20% credit.

12.3.2 To receive service credits, Contoso must submit a written request within thirty (30) days after the end of the month in which the SLA breach occurred, including reasonable details supporting the claim.

12.3.3 Service credits shall be applied to future invoices and shall not be redeemable for cash.
