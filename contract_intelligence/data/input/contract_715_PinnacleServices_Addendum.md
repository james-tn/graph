## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This **Article II** supplements and expands upon **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. All Services described in the Parent Agreement shall continue in full force and effect, and the **Addendum Services** defined herein constitute additional services and processing activities to be provided by Vendor under the same overarching legal and compliance framework as set out in the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 In the event of any conflict between this **Article II** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement with respect to the Addendum Services, the terms of this Addendum shall control solely for the Addendum Services. For all other Services, the Parent Agreement shall prevail.

### 2.2 Description of Addendum Services and Enhanced Processing Activities

2.2.1 **Advanced Analytics and Modeling.** In addition to the data processing and transformation activities described in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall perform advanced analytics and modeling, including:

  (a) Development and deployment of predictive models and machine learning algorithms using Client Personal Data and other Client data, for Client’s internal business purposes (e.g., customer segmentation, churn prediction, operational optimization), in compliance with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Addendum.

  (b) Creation and maintenance of analytics pipelines that process both batch and streaming data, including event logs and transaction data, subject to the Security Measures detailed in **Section 11.3** of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) Generation of advanced analytical Addendum Deliverables, such as model performance dashboards, feature importance reports, and data quality scorecards, which shall be deemed “Deliverables” under **Section 1.8** and subject to the IP and license framework in **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement and **Article VI** of this Addendum.

2.2.2 **Data Enrichment and Pseudonymization.** Vendor shall perform enhanced data enrichment and pseudonymization on Client Personal Data, including:

  (a) Integration of Client Personal Data with Client-authorized third-party datasets and reference tables under written instructions from Client, in accordance with **Section 11.2 (Processing Instructions)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) Implementation of systematic pseudonymization and tokenization of key identifiers (e.g., customer IDs, email addresses) prior to use in certain analytic workflows, with re-identification permitted only under Client’s documented instructions and subject to the Security Measures in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) Creation of enriched, pseudonymized data sets for use in Client’s internal analytics and reporting, with clear segregation between pseudonymized data and directly identifiable Client Personal Data.

2.2.3 **Multi-Region Data Replication and Resilience.** Building upon **Section 2.2.1 (Data Hosting and Storage)** and **Section 11.4 (Data Backup and Recovery)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

  (a) Provide multi-region logical replication of specified data sets designated by Client in an Addendum SOW, within the United States unless Client otherwise authorizes in writing under **Section 2.4 (Service Locations and Data Transfers)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) Implement enhanced recovery point objectives (RPO) and recovery time objectives (RTO) for the replicated Addendum Services environment, in alignment with the Addendum SLA in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

  (c) Maintain and document an expanded disaster recovery plan that covers the additional replicated environments and make such documentation available to Client upon reasonable request, subject to Vendor’s confidentiality and security requirements.

2.2.4 **Event-Stream Processing and Real-Time Interfaces.** In addition to the access and interface services described in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

  (a) Provide real-time event-stream ingestion and processing capabilities for specified event sources (e.g., clickstream, telemetry, transactional events), using secure APIs and message queues that comply with the Security Measures in **Section 11.3** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) Deliver real-time or near real-time dashboards and alerting mechanisms to Client’s designated authorized users, supporting configurable thresholds and logic, as documented in the relevant Addendum SOW.

  (c) Support integration of real-time processing outputs with Client’s downstream systems through secure APIs, webhooks, or other mutually agreed interfaces.

2.2.5 **Enhanced Support and Operational Services.** Supplementing **Section 2.2.4 (Support and Maintenance Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

  (a) Provide extended support hours for Priority 1 and Priority 2 incidents (as defined in **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement), on a 24x7 basis for Addendum Services.

  (b) Assign a named technical account manager (“TAM”) and data operations lead to coordinate ongoing operations, incident review, and change management for the Addendum Services.

  (c) Conduct quarterly service review meetings with Client to review service performance, metrics, incidents, and roadmap items related to the Addendum Services.

### 2.3 Performance Standards

2.3.1 Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, using personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and qualifications, consistent with **Section 2.3.1 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.2 Vendor shall meet or exceed the **Addendum SLA** commitments in **Article XII** of this Addendum with respect to the Addendum Services. These Addendum SLAs supplement the SLA provisions set forth in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and, where expressly stated, shall supersede such provisions for the Addendum Services.

2.3.3 Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to avoid and minimize any disruption to the Addendum Services, including by coordinating planned maintenance windows with Client and providing advance notice consistent with **Section 12.4 (Scheduled Maintenance)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.4 Relationship to Subprocessors

2.4.1 Any use of Subprocessors in connection with the Addendum Services shall be governed by **Section 2.5 (Subprocessors)** of the Parent Agreement. Vendor shall maintain and provide to Client, upon request, an updated list of Subprocessors engaged for the Addendum Services.

2.4.2 Client’s right to reasonably object to new Subprocessors under **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent Agreement applies equally to Subprocessors engaged for the Addendum Services.

### 2.5 No Sale or Sharing of Personal Data

2.5.1 Vendor’s obligations under **Section 2.6 (No Sale of Personal Data)** and **Section 11.9 (CCPA-Specific Terms)** of the Parent Agreement are hereby reaffirmed and apply fully to all Enhanced Processing Activities and Addendum Services described in this Addendum. Vendor shall not “sell” or “share” Client Personal Data (as those terms are defined under CCPA) in connection with this Addendum.

3.1.3 The fee structure for the Addendum Services may include, without limitation:

  (a) **Fixed implementation fees** for initial configuration of advanced analytics pipelines, event-stream processing, enrichment workflows, and multi-region replication;

  (b) **Recurring subscription or platform fees** (monthly or annual) for ongoing access to advanced analytics features, real-time dashboards, and replicated environments;

  (c) **Usage-based fees**, such as per-GB processed, per million events streamed, or per active analytics user, as specified in the relevant Addendum Order Form;

  (d) **Professional services fees** for custom data science work, model development, complex data integrations, or additional consulting services requested by Client in writing.

### 3.2 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.2.1 Vendor shall invoice Client for Addendum Fees in accordance with the schedule set forth in the applicable Addendum Order Form or Addendum SOW. Unless otherwise specified:

  (a) Fixed implementation and onboarding fees shall be invoiced upon completion of defined milestones; and  

  (b) Recurring and usage-based fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears.

3.2.2 Notwithstanding **Section 3.2.2 (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that all undisputed amounts under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** from the date of Vendor’s invoice (“**Net 30**”), reflecting the more active operational nature of the Addendum Services.

3.2.3 If Client disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Client shall notify Vendor in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the nature and amount of the dispute, similar to the process outlined in **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent Agreement. Client shall timely pay any undisputed portion, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve the disputed portion pursuant to **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

### 3.3 Late Payments

3.3.1 Any undisputed amounts not paid by the due date may accrue interest at a rate equal to the lesser of: (a) one percent (1%) per month; or (b) the maximum rate allowed by Applicable Law, consistent with **Section 3.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, calculated from the due date until the date of actual payment.

3.3.2 Vendor shall not suspend the Addendum Services for late payment unless:

  (a) Vendor has provided Client with at least fifteen (15) days’ prior written notice of the overdue amount; and  

  (b) Client fails to pay the undisputed outstanding amounts within such notice period, consistent with **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.

### 3.4 Taxes

3.4.1 The tax provisions under **Section 3.4 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to Addendum Fees. All Addendum Fees are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be the responsibility of Client as set forth therein.

### 3.5 Expenses and Reimbursement

3.5.1 Vendor shall be responsible for its own ordinary business expenses in providing the Addendum Services unless otherwise expressly set forth in an Addendum SOW, consistent with **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 Client shall reimburse Vendor for any pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses reasonably incurred at Client’s written request (e.g., travel for on-site workshops or training) subject to the thresholds and approval processes described in **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement has an Initial Term ending on **May 04, 2028**, as set forth in **Section 4.1.1** of the Parent Agreement. If the Parent Agreement is not renewed or is otherwise terminated prior to that date, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date as the termination of the Parent Agreement, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to extend or restate the Parent Agreement or to adopt a new master agreement that continues to govern the Addendum Services.

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is renewed for one or more Renewal Terms under **Section 4.1.2** of the Parent Agreement, this Addendum may continue in effect for the duration of such Renewal Terms, subject to the Addendum Expiration Date, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article IV.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

4.3.1 Notwithstanding any provisions to the contrary, Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part (including specific Addendum SOWs), for any reason or no reason upon **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Vendor, which is consistent with Client’s termination rights under **Section 4.2.1** of the Parent Agreement and reflects the low-risk and Client-friendly nature of the engagement.

4.3.2 Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client, consistent with **Section 4.2.2** of the Parent Agreement, and shall reasonably cooperate with Client in transitioning or winding down the Addendum Services as described in **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.5** of this Addendum.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches its obligations under this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail, consistent with **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 For avoidance of doubt, material breaches may include, without limitation:

  (a) Vendor’s material failure to meet the Addendum SLA in **Article XII** of this Addendum, resulting in substantial adverse impact on Client’s use of the Addendum Services;

  (b) Vendor’s unauthorized sale, sharing, or disclosure of Client Personal Data in connection with the Addendum Services, contrary to **Section 2.5** of this Addendum, **Section 2.6 (No Sale of Personal Data)**, and **Section 11.9** of the Parent Agreement;

  (c) Client’s failure to pay undisputed Addendum Fees that remain unpaid more than ninety (90) days after the due date; or

  (d) a Party’s violation of Applicable Law relating to data protection or privacy that materially affects the Addendum Services.

### 4.5 Effects of Termination

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) Vendor shall cease providing the Addendum Services and shall cease processing Client Personal Data under this Addendum except as necessary to perform any agreed transition, data return, or data deletion activities consistent with **Section 4.5.1** and **Section 4.5.2 (Data Return and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) Client shall pay Vendor for all Addendum Services rendered and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination; and  

  (c) all licenses granted to Vendor by Client under this Addendum with respect to Client Personal Data shall terminate, except as necessary for Vendor to comply with Applicable Law or to complete the wind-down and data return/deletion obligations.

4.5.2 Vendor shall, at Client’s written election and in accordance with **Section 4.5.2** of the Parent Agreement, within thirty (30) days following termination:

  (a) Return to Client a copy of all Client Personal Data processed solely under this Addendum in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format; and/or  

  (b) Securely delete or destroy such data and provide written certification of such deletion upon Client’s request, except to the extent retention is required by Applicable Law.

4.5.3 The survival provisions in **Section 4.5.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Addendum. Without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XVI** of this Addendum and associated payment obligations shall survive expiration or termination to the extent necessary.
