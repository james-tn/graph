## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Addendum is entered into pursuant to the framework established in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Section 2.1 of the Parent Agreement provides that Cascade shall supply Deliverables and Services as requested in Purchase Orders issued during the Term. This Addendum constitutes a detailed, transaction-specific expansion of such scope, setting out particular Additional Deliverables and Additional Services to be governed by, and integrated with, the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly modified herein, all obligations regarding performance standards, subcontracting, Contoso responsibilities, and priority of documents under Sections 2.4–2.9 of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services.

2.2 **Overview of Additional Deliverables.**  
Under this Addendum, Cascade shall provide the following **Additional Deliverables**:

2.2.1 **Enterprise Hardware Expansion.**  
(a) Provision of up to thirty-two (32) additional enterprise-grade servers, including high-density compute nodes optimised for virtualisation and containerised workloads, with associated power distribution units and rack mounting kits;  
(b) Provision of enhanced storage arrays with a minimum of 1.5 petabytes of usable storage capacity across all arrays, including tiered SSD and HDD storage pools and redundant controllers; and  
(c) Expansion of Contoso’s core network fabric with additional spine and leaf switches, including 100GbE and 40GbE uplinks, and redundant switching paths designed to ensure high availability consistent with the Service Levels defined in Article XII of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.2.2 **Software Licenses and Subscriptions.**  
(a) Additional perpetual and subscription-based licenses for Contoso’s enterprise management software, monitoring tools, and security software, including but not limited to:  
(i) up to 2,000 additional monitoring endpoints;  
(ii) up to 3,500 additional endpoint security licenses; and  
(iii) 200 additional concurrent administrative console users.  
(b) Subscription renewals and expansions aligned with the Addendum Term, ensuring that license validity and support are maintained through **27 July 2028**.

2.2.3 **Configuration and Integration Artifacts.**  
Cascade shall develop and deliver updated configuration files, deployment templates, automation scripts (including infrastructure-as-code artifacts), and API integrations necessary to incorporate the above hardware and software into Contoso’s existing environment in a manner that maintains or improves compliance with the **Specifications** and **Service Levels** described in the Parent Agreement and in this Addendum.

2.2.4 **Documentation.**  
Cascade shall provide comprehensive documentation, including updated design documents, low-level build guides, operational runbooks, dependency matrices, and troubleshooting guides, each of which shall be considered “Deliverables” under Section 1.8 of the Parent Agreement and “Work Product” under this Addendum.

2.3 **Overview of Additional Services.**  
The **Additional Services** to be provided under this Addendum shall include, without limitation:

2.3.1 **Consulting and Design Services (Extended).**  
In addition to the consulting and design services described in Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall perform capacity planning, performance modelling, and resiliency assessments tailored to the expanded infrastructure, including:  
(a) sizing studies for workload consolidation and migration;  
(b) security architecture reviews to ensure alignment with Contoso’s internal policies and applicable Data Protection Laws as referenced in Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement; and  
(c) high-availability and disaster recovery design, including DR runbooks and RPO/RTO recommendations.

2.3.2 **Implementation, Integration, and Migration Services.**  
Building on Section 2.3.2 of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall:  
(a) install and configure the Additional Deliverables in Contoso’s designated data centres and/or Managed Hosting Environment;  
(b) integrate the Additional Deliverables with Contoso’s existing network, identity, monitoring, backup, and ticketing systems;  
(c) perform controlled migration of selected production workloads to the new hardware and storage arrays, including data replication, cutover planning, and rollback procedures; and  
(d) coordinate with Contoso’s internal teams and any third-party vendors (as reasonably required) to minimise downtime and align with the Acceptance Testing procedures described in Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement and Section 2.7 of this Addendum.

2.3.3 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  
Consistent with Section 2.3.3 of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall provide:  
(a) a minimum of six (6) structured training workshops for Contoso’s operations, security, and engineering teams;  
(b) hands-on lab sessions using Contoso’s environment; and  
(c) updated training materials (slide decks, lab guides, and recorded sessions where permitted) to support self-paced learning.

2.3.4 **Enhanced Support and Managed Services.**  
In addition to the maintenance and support services described in Section 2.3.4 and Article XII of the Parent Agreement, Cascade shall provide Enhanced Support Services including:  
(a) 24x7 monitoring of the Additional Deliverables via Cascade’s network operations centre;  
(b) proactive capacity and performance reporting on a monthly basis;  
(c) quarterly health checks and patch management cycles; and  
(d) a dedicated technical account manager (TAM) for Contoso, serving as the primary escalation point and responsible for coordinating incident response in line with the Service Levels defined in Article XII of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.4 **Performance Standards.**  
Cascade shall perform all Additional Services:  
2.4.1 in accordance with **Good Industry Practice** as defined in Section 1.12 of the Parent Agreement;  
2.4.2 in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including those relating to data protection, export control, and anti-bribery, as required by Sections 2.4.2 and 7.2.3 of the Parent Agreement;  
2.4.3 using qualified and experienced personnel familiar with enterprise-class infrastructure and security requirements; and  
2.4.4 in accordance with the timelines and Milestones specified in the Project Plan and the applicable Purchase Orders, subject to adjustments agreed under **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, and subject to **Article XV (Force Majeure)** of both instruments.

2.5 **Project Timelines and Milestones (Supplement to Parent Agreement Section 2.5).**  
Unless otherwise specified in a Purchase Order under this Addendum, the following project timelines shall apply:

2.5.1 **Delivery of Additional Hardware and Software.**  
Cascade shall deliver all Additional Deliverables to the locations specified by Contoso within forty-five (45) calendar days from the date of Cascade’s acceptance of the relevant Purchase Order under this Addendum.

2.5.2 **Implementation and Migration Completion.**  
Cascade shall complete the installation, configuration, and initial migration of workloads associated with the Additional Deliverables within ninety (90) calendar days from the date of delivery of the relevant Additional Deliverables, unless extended by mutual agreement in writing pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)**.

2.5.3 **Training Completion.**  
All training workshops and knowledge-transfer activities described in Section 2.3.3 shall be completed within one hundred twenty (120) calendar days from the Addendum Effective Date, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.6 **Service Levels and Managed Hosting.**  
For any hosted or managed components under this Addendum, Cascade shall meet or exceed the Service Levels and Uptime Commitment set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as further extended in Article XII of this Addendum. The Parties agree that any **SLA Credits** due under this Addendum shall be treated as direct damages and may be set off in accordance with Section 3.9 of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Acceptance Testing (Supplementing Section 2.5.3 of Parent Agreement).**  
Following delivery and implementation of the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services:

2.7.1 **Acceptance Period.** Contoso shall have twenty (20) Business Days (the **“Addendum Acceptance Period”**) to test the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services for substantial conformity with the applicable Specifications and Service Levels.  

2.7.2 **Non-Conformity Notice.** If the Additional Deliverables or Additional Services fail to materially conform, Contoso shall notify Cascade in writing within the Addendum Acceptance Period, describing the non-conformity in reasonable detail.  

2.7.3 **Remediation.** Cascade shall, at its expense and within a reasonable period agreed by the Parties, remedy such non-conformity, and the Addendum Acceptance Period for the remediated items shall restart for a period of ten (10) Business Days.  

2.7.4 **Deemed Operational Acceptance.** The Additional Deliverables and Additional Services shall be deemed to have achieved **Operational Acceptance** upon the earlier of:  
(a) Contoso’s written confirmation of acceptance; or  
(b) the expiration of the Addendum Acceptance Period (or any re-test period) without Contoso having provided a written notice of material non-conformity.  

2.8 **Contoso Responsibilities (Supplement to Section 2.8 of Parent Agreement).**  
In addition to its obligations under Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:  
2.8.1 provide timely access to its existing infrastructure, systems, and relevant third-party systems as necessary for Cascade to perform the Additional Services;  
2.8.2 ensure that any required internal approvals (including change control and maintenance windows) are obtained in a timely manner; and  
2.8.3 provide a designated programme manager to coordinate activities under this Addendum and to act as Contoso’s primary point of contact for Cascade’s TAM.

2.9 **Priority of Documents (Supplement to Section 2.9 of Parent Agreement).**  
In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Addendum and the Parent Agreement with respect to the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services, the following order of precedence shall apply:  
(a) this Addendum;  
(b) the Parent Agreement (Ref: PUR-CAS-202510-644);  
(c) any applicable Purchase Order issued pursuant to the Parent Agreement and this Addendum; and  
(d) any other document expressly incorporated by reference.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The aggregate maximum contract value under this Addendum shall not exceed **US$3,163,350** (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**), unless increased by a further written amendment executed by both Parties. This Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the aggregate maximum contract value of **US$1,548,876** specified in Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement. The Parties acknowledge that, taken together, the cumulative maximum exposure under the Parent Agreement and this Addendum is US$4,712,226, subject always to the liability limitations set forth in Article IX of both instruments.

3.2 **Pricing and Fee Schedule (Supplementing Section 3.2 of Parent Agreement).**

3.2.1 **Hardware and Software Fees.**  
(a) Enterprise Servers and Storage: up to US$1,750,000;  
(b) Network Expansion Equipment: up to US$525,000;  
(c) Additional Software Licenses and Subscriptions: up to US$625,000.

3.2.2 **Services Fees.**  
(a) Implementation, Integration, and Migration Services: up to US$150,000;  
(b) Training and Knowledge Transfer: up to US$38,350;  
(c) Enhanced Support and Managed Services (for the Addendum Term): up to US$575,000.

3.2.3 **Payment Terms – Net 30 (Modification of Parent Agreement Section 3.3).**  
Notwithstanding Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement, which provides for **Net 45** payment terms, the Parties hereby agree that for all invoices issued under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay all undisputed invoices within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of receipt of a valid invoice (**“Net 30”**). Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended solely for the purposes of this Addendum** to the extent necessary to give effect to these Net 30 Payment Terms.

3.2.4 **Currency.**  
Unless otherwise specified in the relevant Purchase Order, all Fees under this Addendum shall be denominated and payable in **United States Dollars (USD)**, consistent with Section 3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Milestones.**

3.3.1 **Hardware and Software Deliverables.**  
Payment for Additional Deliverables shall be made as follows:  
(a) **30%** of the applicable Fees upon delivery of the relevant Additional Deliverables to Contoso’s nominated site(s);  
(b) **40%** upon completion of implementation and successful Operational Acceptance under Section 2.7; and  
(c) **30%** upon completion of associated training and transition into steady-state Enhanced Support Services.

3.3.2 **Professional Services.**  
Unless otherwise specified in a Purchase Order, fees for professional services (consulting, implementation, migration, training) shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, reflecting the actual time and materials expended in the preceding month, consistent with Section 3.3.3 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.3 **Managed Services.**  
Fees for Enhanced Support and Managed Services shall be invoiced quarterly in advance, based on the agreed annual managed services fee allocated over the Addendum Term.

3.4 **Late Payment (Supplement to Section 3.5 of Parent Agreement).**  
If Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount by the due date under the Net 30 Payment Terms:  
3.4.1 Cascade may charge interest on the overdue amount in accordance with Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement; and  
3.4.2 Cascade shall not suspend the Additional Services or withhold Additional Deliverables unless:  
(a) such payment is more than thirty (30) days overdue;  
(b) Cascade has provided Contoso with at least ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice of its intention to suspend; and  
(c) Contoso fails to cure such non-payment within such notice period.  

For avoidance of doubt, the suspension rights in Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement apply equally to this Addendum, subject to the modified Net 30 Payment Terms.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement (Disputed Amounts) shall apply to all invoices issued under this Addendum. Contoso shall notify Cascade within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of an invoice if it disputes any portion thereof, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve such disputes promptly.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement (Taxes) shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Addendum. All Fees specified herein are exclusive of VAT and other applicable indirect taxes.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Additional Services. Cascade shall obtain Contoso’s prior written approval for any individual expense exceeding GBP 500 and shall comply with Contoso’s travel and expense policies as provided from time to time.

3.8 **Set-Off.**  
Contoso’s set-off rights under Section 3.9 of the Parent Agreement, including the right to set off SLA Credits and other credits or refunds, shall apply equally to amounts payable under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall remain in full force and effect until **27 July 2028** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement currently has a Term ending **3 December 2026** (Section 4.1). The Parties intend that:  
4.2.1 if the Parent Agreement is **renewed or extended**, this Addendum shall continue in force until the Addendum Expiration Date, unless earlier terminated in accordance herewith; and  
4.2.2 if the Parent Agreement is **not renewed or is terminated** prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, this Addendum shall, subject to Section 4.4 below, terminate automatically on the effective date of termination or expiry of the Parent Agreement, except to the extent the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain services.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Supplement to Section 4.3 of Parent Agreement).**

4.3.1 **By Contoso.**  
Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience and without cause upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Cascade. This is consistent with, and does not limit, Contoso’s rights under Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.2 **By Cascade.**  
Cascade may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, consistent with Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.3 **Consequences.**  
In the event of termination for convenience, Contoso shall pay Cascade for all Additional Deliverables delivered and accepted and all Additional Services properly performed up to the effective date of termination, together with any non-cancellable third-party charges reasonably incurred in reliance on this Addendum, provided such charges were pre-approved in writing by Contoso. Cascade shall use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate any such charges.

4.4 **Termination for Cause (Supplement to Section 4.4 of Parent Agreement).**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice to the other Party if:  
4.4.1 the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement in relation to the Additional Deliverables or Additional Services and fails to remedy such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach; or  
4.4.2 any insolvency event occurs in respect of the other Party as described in Section 4.4.2 of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition Services (Supplement to Sections 4.5 and 4.6 of Parent Agreement).**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:  
4.5.1 all rights and licences granted to Contoso under this Addendum that are stated to be perpetual shall survive in accordance with their terms;  
4.5.2 each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with Section 5.5 of this Addendum and Section 5.4 of the Parent Agreement;  
4.5.3 at Contoso’s written request, Cascade shall provide **Transition Services** for up to six (6) months following the effective date of termination, at Cascade’s then-current time-and-materials rates, to support an orderly migration of services to Contoso or a third-party provider, in addition to the obligations set out in Section 4.6.3 of the Parent Agreement; and  
4.5.4 Contoso shall pay Cascade any outstanding undisputed Fees for Additional Deliverables delivered and Additional Services performed up to the effective date of termination, subject to any valid set-off or SLA Credits due.

4.6 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Addendum which by their nature or express terms are intended to survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including but not limited to Articles III (with respect to accrued payment obligations), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and Sections 4.5 and 4.6, together with the corresponding surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as identified in Section 4.7 thereof.
