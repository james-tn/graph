## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Master Agreement.**  
This Work Order is issued under, and is expressly subject to, the terms and conditions of the **Master Agreement (MSA-ZEN-202403-197, contract_197)**, including **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**. In accordance with Section 2.1 of the Master Agreement, in the event of any conflict between the terms of this Work Order and the Master Agreement, this Work Order shall prevail solely with respect to the subject matter of this Work Order, unless explicitly stated otherwise herein.

2.2 **Project Overview.**  
Under this Work Order, Vendor shall design, implement, integrate, and provide managed support for a multi-region enterprise operations platform (the **“Operations Platform”**) for Client. The Operations Platform will consist of:

- A cloud-based core application layer for order management, inventory tracking, and financial reconciliation;
- Integration with Client’s existing ERP, CRM, and HR systems via standardized APIs;
- An analytics and reporting stack, including data warehouse integration and visualization dashboards; and
- Ongoing level 2 and level 3 support services, monitoring, and periodic optimization.

This engagement builds upon the “Technology Implementation Services”, “Managed Services and Support” and “Consulting and Advisory Services” categories described in Sections 2.2.1, 2.2.2 and 2.2.3 of the Master Agreement.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Services.**  
Vendor’s Services under this Work Order shall include, at a minimum, the following activities:

2.3.1 **Requirements & Solution Design.**  
(a) Conduct detailed business and technical requirements workshops with Client’s designated stakeholders in Singapore, EMEA, and North America.  
(b) Produce a **Functional Requirements Specification (FRS)** and **Technical Design Document (TDD)** in accordance with Section 2.3 of the Master Agreement, capturing business processes, integration points, data models, and security requirements.  
(c) Present and iterate the design documents with Client for review and sign-off, including a formal design review meeting and a written acceptance memorandum.

2.3.2 **Configuration, Customization & Integration.**  
(a) Configure the Operations Platform in the designated cloud environment(s), including user roles, access control, workflows, and business rules.  
(b) Develop up to **twelve (12)** integrations between the Operations Platform and Client’s ERP, CRM, HR, and third-party logistics providers, using API-based and file-based interfaces as appropriate.  
(c) Implement data transformation, mapping, and validation logic conforming to Client’s data standards, leveraging Vendor Pre-Existing IP (as defined in Section 6.3 of the Master Agreement) where applicable.  
(d) Maintain documentation of all configurations and customizations, including environment diagrams, integration specifications, and parameter settings.

2.3.3 **Data Migration & Validation.**  
(a) Assist Client in extracting historical master and transaction data from legacy systems.  
(b) Design and execute migration scripts and jobs to load data into the Operations Platform.  
(c) Perform sample-based and automated data validation and reconciliation and produce **Data Migration Summary Reports** with statistics on records migrated, exceptions, and resolution steps.

2.3.4 **Testing, UAT Support & Go-Live Support.**  
(a) Develop and execute **System Integration Test (SIT)** test cases to validate end-to-end processes and integrations.  
(b) Support Client’s **User Acceptance Testing (UAT)** activities by providing test scenarios, expected results, and defect triage.  
(c) Log and manage defects in an agreed issue tracking tool; coordinate resolution in line with response/resolution targets consistent with Article XII of the Master Agreement.  
(d) Plan and support Go-Live activities, including cutover plan, dress rehearsals, and hypercare support during the initial four (4) weeks post-Go-Live.

2.3.5 **Managed Services & Support.**  
For the Managed Services Period, Vendor shall provide:

- **Application Support (L2/L3):** Incident resolution, problem management, minor enhancements, and configuration adjustments.  
- **Monitoring:** 24x7 automated monitoring of critical components (availability, performance, integration queues) with human triage during agreed support hours.  
- **Maintenance:** Regular patching and updates of the application and dependent components in coordination with Client.  
- **Reporting:** Monthly service performance reports, including incident metrics, SLA achievement, and improvement recommendations.

2.3.6 **Consulting, Knowledge Transfer & Training.**  
(a) Provide advisory services on process optimization, reporting best practices, and governance structures related to the Operations Platform.  
(b) Conduct at least **six (6)** training sessions for key user groups (operations, finance, IT) covering system functionality, reporting, and administrative tasks.  
(c) Deliver **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** and **Runbooks** describing operational processes and support procedures.

2.4 **Deliverables.**  
The Deliverables under this Work Order shall include, without limitation:

- **D1: Functional Requirements Specification (FRS)**  
- **D2: Technical Design Document (TDD)**  
- **D3: Integration Specifications and API Documentation**  
- **D4: Configured Operations Platform (non-production and production environments)**  
- **D5: Data Migration Scripts and Data Migration Summary Reports**  
- **D6: SIT and UAT Test Plans, Test Cases, and Defect Logs**  
- **D7: Go-Live Cutover Plan and Post-Go-Live Hypercare Report**  
- **D8: SOPs, Runbooks, and Administration Guides**  
- **D9: Training Materials (slides, recordings, handouts)**  
- **D10: Monthly Service Performance Reports (during Managed Services Period)**  

All Deliverables shall be deemed “Deliverables” and “Work Product” under Sections 1.10 and 6.4 of the Master Agreement and shall be subject to the IP provisions in Article VI of the Master Agreement and of this Work Order.

2.5 **Project Timelines and Milestones.**  
Consistent with Section 2.5 of the Master Agreement, the high-level Project schedule and Milestones are as follows (dates are indicative and may be adjusted by mutual written agreement or via Change Order):

- **Milestone 1 – Project Initiation & Requirements Sign-off**  
  - Activities: Project kickoff, detailed requirements workshops, FRS completion.

  - Target Completion: 31 March 2025.  
  - Key Deliverables: D3, D4 (non-prod), draft D8.  

- **Milestone 4 – SIT & UAT Completion**

  - Activities: Production cutover, Go-Live, hypercare support for four weeks.  
  - Target Completion: 31 October 2025.

(a) Each Deliverable will be submitted to Client for review and acceptance.  
(b) Unless otherwise specified in this Work Order, Client shall have **ten (10) Business Days** from delivery to review each Deliverable and provide written notice of acceptance or a rejection specifying material non-conformities with the agreed specifications.

(d) In the event of a valid rejection, Vendor shall correct the identified non-conformities and resubmit the Deliverable within a mutually agreed timeframe, whereupon the acceptance process shall be repeated.
