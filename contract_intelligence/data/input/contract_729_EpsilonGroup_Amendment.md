## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK (AMENDMENT)

2.1 **Relationship to Existing SOW and Parent Consulting Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Amendment is entered into **pursuant to and in supplementation of** the Existing SOW (SOW-EPS-202412-537 / contract_537) and the Parent Consulting Agreement (CSA-EPS-202412-519 / contract_519), consistent with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Existing SOW and **Section 2.7 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Consulting Agreement.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms and conditions of the Existing SOW remain in full force and effect, and all Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables described herein are subject to the Parent Consulting Agreement and the Existing SOW.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Amendment and the terms of the Existing SOW, the terms of this Amendment shall control **solely with respect to the Incremental Services, Incremental Deliverables, and Amendment Milestones** described herein. In the event of a further conflict with the Parent Consulting Agreement, the conflict rule stated in **Section 2.1.2** and **Section 16.2** of the Existing SOW shall apply.

2.2 **Description of Incremental Services.**

2.2.1 **Program Continuation and Expansion.** Under the Existing SOW, Epsilon agreed to perform services supporting Phase I & II of the “Contoso Enterprise Digital Transformation and Process Optimization Program,” as more particularly described in **Section 2.2.2** thereof. Pursuant to this Amendment, Epsilon shall provide additional services to (a) extend the design and implementation scope into ancillary functions (such as procurement, human resources, and field operations), (b) stabilize and optimize the ERP/CRM solutions deployed during Phases 4–5 under the Existing SOW, and (c) implement additional reporting, analytics, and automation capabilities.

2.2.2 **Post‑Go‑Live Stabilization and Optimization.** Epsilon shall conduct structured post‑go‑live stabilization activities, including:

(a) Root‑cause analysis of defects and performance issues arising within the first twelve (12) months after go‑live of any major release under **Phase 4** or **Phase 5** of the Existing SOW;  
(b) Development and implementation of corrective configurations, scripts, and process adjustments;  
(c) Establishment of standardized incident classification and triage workflows, in alignment with the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Existing SOW as further refined herein; and  
(d) Coaching and knowledge transfer to Contoso’s internal support teams.

2.2.3 **Enhancement Releases and Extended Functional Scope.** Epsilon shall design, build, test, and deploy at least **three (3) Enhancement Releases** during the Amendment Term, each comprising a bundle of agreed enhancements, including:

(a) Extended ERP footprint to cover procurement and vendor management, including integration of purchasing workflows with finance and inventory modules;  
(b) CRM enhancements to support field service, case routing, and multi‑channel customer engagement;  
(c) Advanced analytics dashboards and performance scorecards for Contoso’s executive leadership, leveraging the “Status Reporting & Executive Dashboards” approach referenced in **Section 2.3.1(g)** of the Existing SOW; and  
(d) Automation of selected cross‑functional processes using no‑code/low‑code tools, consistent with the digital transformation roadmap developed under **Phase 2 – Design & Roadmapping** of the Existing SOW.

2.2.4 **Operating Model and Governance Refinement.** Building on the “Target Operating Model and Process Designs” Deliverables specified in **Section 2.3.1(b)** of the Existing SOW, Epsilon shall:

(a) Refine and update RACI matrices and governance charters to reflect the expanded ERP/CRM footprint;  
(b) Define and implement a release management and change control process that aligns with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Existing SOW and this Amendment; and  
(c) Facilitate up to **twelve (12)** quarterly governance workshops over the Amendment Term to review KPIs, backlog prioritization, and risk mitigation actions.

2.2.5 **Training, Change Management, and Adoption Support.** In addition to the “Training & Change Management Materials” described in **Section 2.3.1(f)** of the Existing SOW, Epsilon shall:

(a) Develop role‑based training paths for the new functional areas introduced in the Enhancement Releases;  
(b) Conduct train‑the‑trainer sessions (onsite or remote) covering updated process flows and system capabilities;  
(c) Provide adoption metrics and feedback surveys, integrated into Contoso’s benefit realization framework as referenced in **Section 2.3.1(h)** of the Existing SOW; and  
(d) Assist Contoso in revising its internal policies and SOPs to reflect new processes and controls.

2.2.6 **Ongoing Advisory Services.** Epsilon shall provide ongoing advisory services to support Contoso’s strategic initiatives related to cloud migration, integration roadmap modernization, and data governance. Such services may be rendered on a time‑and‑materials basis, at the rates set out in **Section 3.2.1** of the Existing SOW, as updated in **Section 3.2.1** of this Amendment.

2.3 **Incremental Deliverables.**

2.3.1 **Stabilization and Optimization Deliverables.** Epsilon shall produce:

(a) Post‑Go‑Live Stabilization Plans, including prioritized issue remediation backlogs;  
(b) Monthly Stabilization Status Reports during stabilization phases;  
(c) Root Cause Analysis (RCA) documents for high‑priority defects; and  
(d) Updated configuration and technical design documentation reflecting applied fixes.

2.3.2 **Enhancement Release Deliverables.** For each Enhancement Release, Epsilon shall provide:

(a) Approved Enhancement Scope Document;  
(b) Design Specifications (functional and technical);  
(c) Test Strategy and Test Scripts;  
(d) Cutover Plans and Rollback Procedures;  
(e) Release Notes and Updated User Guides; and  
(f) Post‑Release Performance Review Report.

2.3.3 **Support Transition Deliverables.** In connection with the Support Transition Period, Epsilon shall deliver:

(a) Support Operating Model documentation, including ticket routing rules and escalation paths;  
(b) Knowledge Articles and “Run Books” for critical processes; and  
(c) A Transition Completion Report confirming handover to Contoso’s internal teams or designated third‑party providers.

2.3.4 All Incremental Deliverables shall be clearly labeled with (i) a version number, (ii) date, (iii) reference to both the Existing SOW (SOW-EPS-202412-537) and this Amendment (AMD-EPS-202412-729), and shall be delivered in electronic format consistent with **Section 2.3.2** of the Existing SOW.

2.4 **Amendment Milestones and Timelines.**

2.4.1 The Parties acknowledge that **Section 2.4 (Project Phases and Timelines)** of the Existing SOW defined the original phases (Initiation through Optimization & Benefit Realization). This Amendment refines and extends certain activities within those phases during the Amendment Term, with a focus on stabilization, enhancements, and adoption.

2.4.2 Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, the Parties agree to the following indicative Amendment Milestones (counting from the Amendment Effective Date):

(a) **Milestone A – Stabilization Kick‑Off (Month 1):** Approval of detailed stabilization and optimization plan;  
(b) **Milestone B – First Enhancement Release Go‑Live (Month 6):** Deployment and Acceptance of first Enhancement Release;  
(c) **Milestone C – Second Enhancement Release Go‑Live (Month 12):** Deployment and Acceptance of second Enhancement Release;  
(d) **Milestone D – Third Enhancement Release Go‑Live (Month 18):** Deployment and Acceptance of third Enhancement Release;  
(e) **Milestone E – Governance and Operating Model Refresh (Month 20):** Acceptance of updated operating model and governance documentation; and  
(f) **Milestone F – Transition to Steady‑State Support (Month 23–24):** Completion of Support Transition Period activities.

2.4.3 Detailed dates, dependencies, and payment triggers for the Amendment Milestones shall be established in an updated **Exhibit A‑1 (Amendment Milestone and Deliverable Schedule)**, which shall be deemed incorporated by reference into both the Existing SOW and this Amendment.

2.5 **Acceptance of Incremental Deliverables.**

2.5.1 The acceptance procedures in **Section 2.5 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Existing SOW, which modify **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Consulting Agreement, shall apply equally to all Incremental Deliverables, subject to the following clarification:

(a) The Incremental Deliverables categorized in **Section 2.3.2** as Release Notes, Test Scripts, and Cutover Plans shall be considered **Minor Deliverables**, subject to the ten (10) Business Day review period described in **Section 2.5.1(b)** of the Existing SOW.  
(b) All other Incremental Deliverables listed in **Sections 2.3.1–2.3.3** shall be considered **Major Deliverables**, subject to the fifteen (15) Business Day review period described in **Section 2.5.1(a)** of the Existing SOW.

2.5.2 Failure by Contoso to respond within the applicable review period shall result in deemed acceptance in accordance with **Section 2.5.2** of the Existing SOW and **Section 2.8.3** of the Parent Consulting Agreement.

2.6 **Company Responsibilities (Amendment‑Specific).**

In addition to the Company responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Existing SOW and **Section 2.6** of the Parent Consulting Agreement, Contoso shall:

2.6.1 Provide production and non‑production environment access necessary for Epsilon to perform stabilization and enhancement activities, subject to Contoso’s security and access control policies;

2.6.2 Assign subject‑matter experts from finance, operations, procurement, customer support, and IT to participate in requirement refinement, testing, and governance workshops;

2.6.3 Ensure timely internal decision‑making and approvals to avoid delays in the Enhancement Releases, consistent with the timelines set forth in **Section 2.4** and any Exhibit A‑1;

2.6.4 Designate a transition owner responsible for overseeing the Support Transition Period and coordinating with Contoso’s internal support teams; and

2.6.5 Promptly notify Epsilon of any material issues, incidents, or regulatory changes that could affect the Incremental Services.

2.7 **Key Personnel.**

The requirements in **Section 2.7 (Key Personnel)** of the Existing SOW remain in effect. In addition:

2.7.1 Epsilon shall designate a **Stabilization Lead**, a **Release Manager**, and a **Change Management Lead** as Key Personnel for the Incremental Services.

2.7.2 Any replacement of such Key Personnel shall follow the process described in **Section 2.7.2** of the Existing SOW and **Section 2.5.4** of the Parent Consulting Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS (AMENDMENT)

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value; Relationship to Existing SOW Contract Value.**

3.1.1 The Parties agree that the **Amendment Contract Value** for the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables under this Amendment is **US$2,015,724**.

3.1.2 The Amendment Contract Value is **in addition to** the SOW Contract Value of **US$1,379,515** referenced in **Section 1.1.9** and **Section 3.1.1** of the Existing SOW. Accordingly, the aggregate estimated fees under the Existing SOW as amended hereby shall be **US$3,395,239**, subject to adjustment through Change Orders pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Existing SOW and this Amendment.

3.1.3 The Parties acknowledge that, consistent with **Section 3.1.2** of the Existing SOW, the Amendment Contract Value is an estimate based on the assumptions and scope defined herein and may be adjusted via mutually executed Change Orders.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**

3.2.1 **Time‑and‑Materials Rates.** The blended hourly rates listed in **Section 3.2.1** of the Existing SOW shall continue to apply to time‑and‑materials engagements under this Amendment. However, the Parties agree that, commencing on the Amendment Effective Date, these rates may be adjusted annually by mutual written agreement to reflect market conditions and resource seniority. Any such adjustments shall be documented in a Change Order referencing this Amendment.

3.2.2 **Fixed and Milestone‑Based Fees.** A substantial portion of the Amendment Contract Value shall be allocated to fixed fees associated with the Amendment Milestones described in **Section 2.4** and Exhibit A‑1. Example allocation (for illustration and subject to Exhibit A‑1):

(a) Milestone A – Stabilization Kick‑Off: 10% of Amendment Contract Value;  
(b) Milestone B – First Enhancement Release: 20% of Amendment Contract Value;  
(c) Milestone C – Second Enhancement Release: 20% of Amendment Contract Value;  
(d) Milestone D – Third Enhancement Release: 20% of Amendment Contract Value;  
(e) Milestone E – Governance Refresh: 10% of Amendment Contract Value;  
(f) Milestone F – Support Transition Completion: 20% of Amendment Contract Value.

3.2.3 **Expenses.** The expense reimbursement arrangements set forth in **Section 3.2.3** of the Existing SOW (incorporating **Section 3.5** of the Parent Consulting Agreement) shall apply to the Incremental Services under this Amendment. Epsilon shall obtain Contoso’s prior written approval for any travel or expenses anticipated to exceed agreed thresholds and shall comply with Contoso’s expense policies.

3.3 **Amended Payment Terms (Net 45).**

3.3.1 **Supersession of Existing SOW Payment Term for Incremental Services.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.1 (Net 60)** of the Existing SOW and **Section 3.3.2 (Net 30)** of the Parent Consulting Agreement, the Parties agree that, **solely with respect to the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables described in this Amendment**, payment of all undisputed amounts shall be due **Net 45** days from the date of Epsilon’s invoice (the **“Amended Payment Terms”**). For avoidance of doubt, the Net 60 term applicable to the pre‑Amendment scope under the Existing SOW remains unchanged unless separately modified.

3.3.2 **Invoicing for Amendment Contract Value.**  

(a) Fixed and milestone‑based components of the Amendment Contract Value shall be invoiced upon completion and **acceptance (or deemed acceptance)** of the applicable Amendment Milestone, consistent with the acceptance procedures in **Section 2.5** of this Amendment and **Section 2.5** of the Existing SOW.  
(b) Time‑and‑materials work (including ad‑hoc advisory and out‑of‑scope efforts approved through Change Orders) and reimbursable expenses shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, as set forth in **Section 3.3.2(a)** of the Existing SOW.

3.3.3 **Payment Method.** Payments shall be made in U.S. Dollars via wire transfer or other mutually agreed electronic payment method, consistent with **Section 3.3.3** of the Existing SOW and **Section 3.3.3** of the Parent Consulting Agreement.

3.4 **Late Payments.**

3.4.1 Any undisputed amount not paid by Contoso when due under the Amended Payment Terms shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.4.1** of the Existing SOW (and **Section 3.4.1** of the Parent Consulting Agreement), from the due date until paid in full.

3.4.2 If any undisputed amount relating to the Incremental Services remains unpaid for more than forty‑five (45) days after the invoice date, Epsilon may, after giving Contoso written notice and a ten (10) Business Day cure period, suspend performance of those Incremental Services associated with such overdue invoices, consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the Existing SOW.

3.5 **Taxes and Setoff.**  

Taxes and setoff rights (or lack thereof) shall be handled in accordance with **Sections 3.5, 3.6, and 3.7** of the Existing SOW and the Parent Consulting Agreement. Nothing in this Amendment modifies those obligations except as expressly stated.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDMENT)

4.1 **Amendment Term; Relationship to Existing SOW Term.**

4.1.1 The Amendment Term shall commence on **August 30, 2026** and shall expire on **February 04, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Amendment, the Existing SOW, or the Parent Consulting Agreement.

4.1.2 The Amendment Term is a **sub‑period within** the SOW Term defined in **Section 1.1.10–1.1.12** and **Section 4.1** of the Existing SOW. To the extent the Existing SOW or the Parent Consulting Agreement is not extended or renewed beyond their respective original expiration dates, the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to determine whether and to what extent the Incremental Services may continue pursuant to any extension or survival provisions.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**

4.2.1 **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience upon **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Epsilon. Such termination shall not, by itself, terminate the entire Existing SOW unless expressly stated, but may reduce or cancel some or all Incremental Services.

4.2.2 **By Epsilon.** Epsilon may terminate this Amendment for convenience only upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, which is consistent with the client‑favorable termination structure in **Section 4.3.2** of the Existing SOW, but adjusted here to reflect the Medium risk level and extended scope.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**

Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause in accordance with the procedures and cure periods set forth in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Existing SOW and the Parent Consulting Agreement. Termination for cause of the Existing SOW or Parent Consulting Agreement shall, unless otherwise specified, automatically terminate this Amendment.

4.4 **Effect of Termination.**

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment:

(a) Epsilon shall cease performing the Incremental Services, except as reasonably requested by Contoso to provide wind‑down assistance, consistent with **Section 4.5.1(a)** of the Existing SOW;  
(b) Contoso shall pay Epsilon for all Incremental Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, including any non‑cancellable commitments made on Contoso’s behalf with Contoso’s prior approval, consistent with **Section 4.5.1(b)** of the Existing SOW; and  
(c) The Parties shall treat Incremental Deliverables and associated Confidential Information in accordance with **Article V** of the Existing SOW and this Amendment.

4.4.2 Termination of this Amendment shall not, unless expressly stated, terminate or modify the remaining obligations under the Existing SOW relating to the pre‑Amendment scope.

4.5 **Survival.**

Any provisions of this Amendment that by their nature should reasonably be understood to survive (including, without limitation, payment obligations, confidentiality, IP, indemnification, and limitation of liability provisions) shall survive expiration or termination of this Amendment, consistent with **Section 4.6** of the Existing SOW.
