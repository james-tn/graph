# AMENDMENT TO DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT  
**Contract Reference Number: AMD-MER-202501-542**

## DATA PROCESSING AMENDMENT  
(Amendment to Data Processing Agreement Ref: DPA-MER-202501-510 / contract_510)

This Data Processing Amendment (this “**Amendment**”), contract reference number **AMD-MER-202501-542**, is entered into as of **April 29, 2025** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a Texas corporation with its principal place of business at **1200 Trinity Parkway, Suite 500, Dallas, Texas 75201, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**,” the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”), and  

- **Meridian Solutions, LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company with its principal place of business at **845 Meridian Plaza, 9th Floor, Austin, Texas 78701, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Meridian**,” the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Meridian may each be referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Amendment **updates data processing terms dated January 18, 2025 (Ref: DPA-MER-202501-510, internal identifier: contract_510)**, and is **entered into pursuant to and in supplementation of** that certain **Data Processing Agreement**, reference number **DPA-MER-202501-510**, effective as of **January 18, 2025** (the “**Parent DPA**” or the “**Parent Agreement**”).

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the **Parent DPA**, reference number **DPA-MER-202501-510** (internal identifier **contract_510**) effective January 18, 2025, pursuant to which Meridian agreed to provide certain data processing, hosting, analytics, and related services to Contoso as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Data Processing)** of the Parent DPA;

WHEREAS, the Parent DPA forms part of an underlying **Master Agreement** or related commercial arrangement, with a total contract value of **US$3,604,495** on **Net 45** payment terms under **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent DPA;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to update, supplement, and in certain respects amend specific data processing, security, and service level provisions of the Parent DPA, and to introduce additional processing services, clarifications regarding international data transfers, revised service levels, and related commercial terms, in light of evolving regulatory requirements (including GDPR and CCPA) and operational needs;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend that this Amendment be read and construed together with the Parent DPA, and that, except as expressly modified herein, all terms and conditions of the Parent DPA shall remain in full force and effect;

WHEREAS, the Parties further intend that the **governing law and jurisdiction** provisions set forth in **Article XVI (General Provisions), Section 16.1 (Governing Law)** and **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution), Section 14.4 (Litigation or Arbitration)** of the Parent DPA (Texas law; courts located in Dallas County, Texas) shall continue to apply to this Amendment;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, agreements, and representations contained herein and in the Parent DPA, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE I – DEFINITIONS

1.1 **Defined Terms in Parent DPA.** Capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined in this Amendment shall have the meanings ascribed to them in **Article I (Definitions)** of the Parent DPA (Ref: DPA-MER-202501-510 / contract_510), including without limitation “Affiliate,” “Applicable Law,” “Business Day,” “Confidential Information,” “Controller,” “Data Protection Laws,” “Data Subject,” “Deliverables,” “Force Majeure Event,” “Incident / Security Incident,” “Instruction,” “Personal Data,” “Processing,” “Processor,” “Services,” “Service Levels,” “Subprocessor,” “Term,” and “Written / in Writing.”

1.2 **“Parent Agreement” or “Parent DPA.”** “Parent Agreement,” “Parent DPA,” or “Data Processing Agreement” means that certain Data Processing Agreement between the Parties, reference number **DPA-MER-202501-510** (internal identifier **contract_510**), effective as of January 18, 2025, including all schedules, exhibits, statements of work (“SOWs”), and duly executed amendments and change orders (other than this Amendment).

1.3 **“Amendment” or “Data Processing Amendment.”** “Amendment” or “Data Processing Amendment” means this Agreement, reference number **AMD-MER-202501-542**, which amends, supplements, and modifies certain provisions of the Parent DPA as expressly provided herein.

1.4 **“Expanded Services.”** “Expanded Services” means the additional or modified data processing, analytics, cross-region hosting, and reporting services described in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of this Amendment, which are supplemental to and integrated with the “Services” defined in Section 1.15 and described in **Article II** of the Parent DPA.

1.5 **“Enhanced Security Measures.”** “Enhanced Security Measures” means the additional technical and organizational security safeguards more fully described in **Section 11.2 of this Amendment**, which supplement and, where expressly stated, supersede the security measures referenced in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent DPA.

1.6 **“Additional Data Categories.”** “Additional Data Categories” means those categories of Personal Data, beyond the categories listed in **Section 2.4 (Categories of Personal Data)** of the Parent DPA, that are expressly authorized for Processing under this Amendment, as set forth in **Section 2.4 of this Amendment**.

1.7 **“Regional Hosting Zones.”** “Regional Hosting Zones” means the logically and/or physically distinct hosting regions or data centers (e.g., US-East, US-West, EU-Central) operated or utilized by Meridian for the Processing of Personal Data under the Parent DPA and this Amendment.

1.8 **“Data Impact Assessment” or “DIA.”** “Data Impact Assessment” or “DIA” means any documented privacy or data protection impact assessment, risk assessment, or similar evaluation that Contoso, as Controller, may undertake in connection with high-risk Processing activities performed by Meridian under the Parent DPA and this Amendment.

1.9 **“SLA Expansion” or “Enhanced SLA.”** “SLA Expansion” or “Enhanced SLA” means the additional or updated service level standards and related remedies set forth in **Article XII of this Amendment**, which supplement and, where expressly stated, supersede the Service Levels described in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent DPA.

1.10 **“Project Schedule.”** “Project Schedule” means the detailed description of timelines, milestones, and implementation phases for the Expanded Services as referenced in **Section 2.2** of this Amendment, as may be attached hereto as an exhibit or incorporated by written reference.

1.11 **“Security Audit Materials.”** “Security Audit Materials” means Meridian’s security policies, summaries of penetration tests, vulnerability assessments, SOC 2/ISO 27001 or similar certifications or reports, and other documentation reasonably necessary for Contoso to assess Meridian’s compliance with **Article XI** of the Parent DPA and **Article XI of this Amendment**, subject to confidentiality and security restrictions.

1.12 **“De-identified Data.”** “De-identified Data” means data originally derived from Personal Data that has been processed so that it cannot reasonably be used to identify or be linked to an identifiable natural person, and which is used in accordance with **Section 11.8 (Anonymization and Aggregation)** of the Parent DPA and **Section 11.5 of this Amendment**.

1.13 **“Regulator” or “Supervisory Authority.”** “Regulator” or “Supervisory Authority” means any governmental, data protection, privacy, or supervisory authority or regulator with jurisdiction over the Processing of Personal Data under the Parent DPA and this Amendment.

1.14 **“Processing Instructions Addendum.”** “Processing Instructions Addendum” means any written schedule, attachment, or statement of work that Contoso issues after the Amendment Effective Date, and which specifically references this Amendment and sets out additional or modified Controller’s Instructions under **Section 2.6 of the Parent DPA**.

1.15 **“Contract Value (Amendment).”** “Contract Value (Amendment)” means the total contract value of **One Hundred Forty-Seven Thousand Seven Hundred Eighty-Four U.S. Dollars (US$147,784)** applicable to the Expanded Services under this Amendment, as set forth in **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** hereof.

1.16 **“Net 60 Term” or “Net 60.”** “Net 60 Term” or “Net 60” means the payment term under which invoices issued pursuant to this Amendment are due within sixty (60) days of the invoice date, as specified in **Section 3.4** of this Amendment, which modifies the “Net 45” payment term described in **Section 3.3(c)** of the Parent DPA solely with respect to the fees governed by this Amendment.

1.17 **“Medium Risk Profile.”** “Medium Risk Profile” means the Parties’ mutual assessment that Processing activities under this Amendment present a medium risk from a data protection and commercial perspective, justifying balanced and mutually protective risk allocation provisions, including those set forth in **Articles VIII and IX** of this Amendment.

1.18 **“Data Transfer Mechanisms.”** “Data Transfer Mechanisms” means standard contractual clauses, binding corporate rules, or other lawful tools used to legitimize cross-border transfers of Personal Data in accordance with **Section 11.6 of the Parent DPA** and **Section 11.3 of this Amendment**.

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent DPA.** This **Article II** supplements and, where expressly stated, modifies **Article II (Scope of Services and Data Processing)** of the Parent DPA. Except as set forth herein, all descriptions of Services, Processing activities, categories of Data Subjects and Personal Data, duration, and limitations in **Sections 2.1 through 2.10** of the Parent DPA remain in full force and effect.

2.2 **Description of Expanded Services.**  
Subject to the terms of the Parent DPA and this Amendment, Meridian shall perform the following **Expanded Services**, which extend the “Services” defined in Section 1.15 and described in **Section 2.1 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent DPA:

  a. **Cross-Region Analytics and Reporting.** Meridian shall enhance its hosted software platform to support cross-region analytics that aggregate Personal Data from multiple **Regional Hosting Zones** (including at minimum a primary U.S. region and one EU region) for Contoso’s internal business intelligence and compliance reporting. Such analytics shall be designed and configured so as to comply with **Data Protection Laws** and the restrictions on international transfers set forth in **Section 11.6 of the Parent DPA** and **Section 11.3 of this Amendment**.

  b. **Role-Based Access Expansion.** Meridian shall implement more granular role-based access controls, including fine-grained permissions for HR, customer support, security, and compliance teams, consistent with the “principles of least privilege and role-based access control” described in **Section 2.7(d)** of the Parent DPA. This includes configuration of new roles, audit logging of privileged access, and periodic access review capabilities.

  c. **Extended Logging and Audit Trails.** Meridian shall implement extended logging for all access to and Processing of Personal Data, including the identity of the user or system accessing the data, time stamps, and a high-level description of the action performed. Such logs shall be retained for not less than **twelve (12) months** from the date of the relevant event, unless a longer period is required by Applicable Law or mutually agreed, to support Contoso’s regulatory compliance and internal investigations.

  d. **Enhanced Incident Response Support.** In addition to the obligations set forth in **Section 11.3 (Data Breach Notification)** of the Parent DPA, Meridian shall provide enhanced support during Security Incidents, including technical consultation, forensic cooperation, and assistance in drafting regulator- and Data Subject-facing communications, as further detailed in **Section 11.2(c)** of this Amendment.

  e. **Data Subject Request Workflow Support.** Meridian shall configure and, where commercially reasonable, automate workflows to enable Contoso to respond to Data Subject rights requests under GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA and other applicable laws, supplementing the assistance described in **Section 11.4 (Data Subject Rights)** of the Parent DPA. Such workflows may include search tools, export utilities, and flagged deletion capabilities.

  f. **Additional Integrations.** Meridian shall provide configuration and integration services (consistent with **Section 2.1(d)** of the Parent DPA) connecting the platform to up to **three (3) additional internal Contoso systems** (e.g., HRIS, ticketing, and CRM enhancements) in order to support the Expanded Services described above. Each integration shall be documented in a Processing Instructions Addendum or SOW referencing this Amendment.

  g. **Compliance Dashboards.** Meridian shall provide one or more configurable dashboards for Contoso’s authorized users that display key metrics related to Processing of Personal Data, including access trends, incident statistics, and regional data residency views, to facilitate Contoso’s Data Impact Assessments and ongoing governance.

2.3 **Project Schedule and Milestones.**  
Meridian shall implement the Expanded Services in accordance with a mutually agreed Project Schedule, which may be set out in an exhibit or SOW referencing this Amendment. Unless otherwise agreed in Writing:

  a. **Initiation Phase.** Within **fifteen (15) Business Days** of the Amendment Effective Date, the Parties shall finalize the Project Schedule, identify key stakeholders, and agree on technical pre-requisites.

  b. **Configuration and Integration Phase.** Meridian shall complete the initial configuration and integrations necessary to support the Expanded Services within **ninety (90) days** of the Amendment Effective Date, subject to Contoso’s timely provision of necessary access, data, and resources.

  c. **Testing and Acceptance.** Following configuration, the Parties shall conduct joint testing for a period of up to **thirty (30) days**. If material defects are identified, Meridian shall remediate such defects within a commercially reasonable timeframe. Acceptance shall be deemed to occur upon the earlier of (i) Contoso’s Written confirmation, or (ii) use of the Expanded Services in production for thirty (30) consecutive days without a Severity 1 defect remaining unresolved.

  d. **Ongoing Operations.** Upon Acceptance, the Expanded Services shall be deemed part of the production Services provided under the Parent DPA and this Amendment and shall be subject to the Enhanced SLA described in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.4 **Additional Categories of Personal Data.**  
This Amendment clarifies and, to a limited extent, expands the categories of Personal Data set forth in **Section 2.4 of the Parent DPA**. Subject always to Contoso’s Instructions and compliance with Data Protection Laws, Meridian is hereby authorized to Process the following Additional Data Categories:

  a. **Broadened HR-related Data.** In addition to the “limited HR-related data” described in **Section 2.4(e)** of the Parent DPA, Meridian may Process departmental access logs, geo-location data at the city level for access security purposes, and role-change history related to Contoso’s employees and contractors.

  b. **Support Session Metadata.** Detailed metadata regarding support sessions and recorded troubleshooting steps, excluding the recording of any audio or video content unless separately and expressly agreed in Writing.

  c. **Regulatory Classification Tags.** Tags or flags indicating the regulatory regime applicable to certain Data Subjects (e.g., EU, UK, California resident) used solely for routing and compliance purposes.

  d. **Other Categories by Written Agreement.** Any additional category of Personal Data expressly listed in a Processing Instructions Addendum referencing this Amendment and signed by both Parties.

The Parties reaffirm that they **do not intend** for Meridian to Process special categories of Personal Data as defined in **Section 2.4 of the Parent DPA**, except as may be explicitly authorized in Writing with additional safeguards.

2.5 **Limitations and Instructions.**  
The limitations and Instruction requirements set forth in **Section 2.6 (Instructions and Limitations)** of the Parent DPA are hereby reaffirmed and shall apply equally to the Expanded Services. Any new or modified Instructions relating to cross-region analytics, additional integrations, or Additional Data Categories shall be documented in a Processing Instructions Addendum and may impact fees and timelines in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent DPA and **Article XIII of this Amendment**.

2.6 **Performance Standards for Expanded Services.**  
Meridian shall perform the Expanded Services:

  a. In accordance with the “reasonable skill, care, and diligence” and industry standards described in **Section 2.7(a)** of the Parent DPA;

  b. In compliance with Applicable Law and Data Protection Laws, including GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA and similar laws, and in accordance with the Enhanced Security Measures defined in **Article XI of this Amendment**;

  c. Using appropriately trained personnel bound by confidentiality obligations, consistent with **Section 2.7(c)** and **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the Parent DPA;

  d. In a manner consistent with the Enhanced SLA, including the availability and response times described in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.7 **No Sale or Sharing; Behavioral Advertising.**  
The Parties reaffirm **Section 2.8 (No Sale or Sharing for Cross-Context Behavioral Advertising)** of the Parent DPA. The Expanded Services shall not be used by Meridian to “sell” or “share” Personal Data or to engage in cross-context behavioral advertising or profiling, except as expressly authorized by Contoso in Writing and permitted by Data Protection Laws.

2.8 **Subprocessors.**  
To the extent Subprocessors are engaged to support the Expanded Services, Meridian shall comply with **Section 2.9 and Section 11.5 (Subprocessors)** of the Parent DPA. Any material changes in Subprocessors related specifically to the Expanded Services shall be communicated to Contoso in Writing, and Contoso shall retain the objection rights set forth in **Section 11.5(d)** of the Parent DPA.

2.9 **Documentation.**  
Meridian shall maintain records of Processing activities relating to the Expanded Services consistent with **Section 2.10 (Documentation)** of the Parent DPA, and shall make such records available to Contoso upon reasonable request for purposes of demonstrating compliance with this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Relationship to Parent DPA Fees.**  
This **Article III** is specific to the Expanded Services under this Amendment and shall be read in conjunction with **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent DPA. For the avoidance of doubt, the total contract value of **US$3,604,495** referenced in **Section 3.1 of the Parent DPA** remains unchanged with respect to services governed solely by the Parent DPA. The fees under this Amendment are incremental and separate.

3.2 **Contract Value (Amendment).**  
The Parties agree that the total contract value for the Expanded Services to be performed during the Term of this Amendment (as defined in **Article IV** below) is **US$147,784** (One Hundred Forty-Seven Thousand Seven Hundred Eighty-Four U.S. Dollars) (the “**Amendment Contract Value**”).

3.3 **Fee Components.**  
The Amendment Contract Value shall be comprised of the following components:

  a. **Expanded Platform/Subscription Fees.** A recurring fee of **US$3,200 per month** for cross-region analytics, extended logging, compliance dashboards, and additional configuration capabilities, commencing upon Acceptance as defined in **Section 2.3(c)**.

  b. **Integration and Professional Services Fees.** A fixed fee of **US$48,000** for configuration, integrations, testing, and related professional services associated with the Expanded Services, billable in milestones as set out in **Section 3.5**.

  c. **Support and Incident Response Enhancements.** A recurring fee of **US$1,000 per month** for enhanced incident response services and Data Subject request workflow support.

  d. **Additional Usage Fees.** Where Contoso exceeds agreed-upon thresholds for data storage, analytical processing volume, or logging retention specific to the Expanded Services, additional fees may apply at rates to be specified in a schedule or SOW referencing this Amendment, consistent with the approach to “Additional Usage Fees” in **Section 3.2(d)** of the Parent DPA.

3.4 **Payment Terms; Modification to Net 45.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.3(c) (Net 45)** of the Parent DPA, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to invoices issued under this Amendment:

  a. **Net 60 Term.** All undisputed invoices for fees under this Amendment shall be payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 60**”).

  b. **Currency and Method.** Payments shall be made in U.S. Dollars by wire transfer or ACH to the account designated by Meridian in Writing, consistent with **Section 3.3(d)** of the Parent DPA.

3.5 **Invoicing and Milestones.**  

  a. **Integration and Professional Services.** The US$48,000 professional services fee shall be invoiced as follows: (i) 30% (US$14,400) upon execution of this Amendment; (ii) 40% (US$19,200) upon completion of configuration and integrations; and (iii) 30% (US$14,400) upon Acceptance pursuant to **Section 2.3(c)**.

  b. **Recurring Fees.** Recurring fees for Expanded Platform/Subscription and Support Enhancements (Section 3.3(a) and (c)) shall commence upon Acceptance and shall be invoiced monthly in advance.

  c. **Additional Usage.** Any Additional Usage Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, with reasonable detail to support the charges.

3.6 **Late Payment.**  
**Section 3.4 (Late Payment)** of the Parent DPA shall apply to amounts invoiced under this Amendment, provided that references therein to “payment term” shall be interpreted as the **Net 60 Term** defined in **Section 3.4(a)** of this Amendment. Meridian shall retain the right to provide written notice of overdue payment and, after a fifteen (15) day grace period, suspend the Expanded Services in accordance with **Section 4.5 (Suspension of Services)** of the Parent DPA.

3.7 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Disputed amounts under this Amendment shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent DPA. Contoso may withhold payment of the disputed portion but must pay all undisputed portions in accordance with the Net 60 Term.

3.8 **Taxes.**  
Taxes applicable to the fees under this Amendment shall be handled as set forth in **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent DPA. If any such taxes are required to be collected by Meridian, they shall be itemized on the relevant invoice.

3.9 **Expenses.**  
Reimbursable expenses relating to onsite or extraordinary services performed specifically for the Expanded Services shall be governed by **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent DPA and shall count towards, but not exceed, any reasonable expense limits mutually agreed upon in Writing.

3.10 **No Set-Off.**  
The Parties reaffirm **Section 3.8 (No Set-Off)** of the Parent DPA, subject to Applicable Law.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**  
This Amendment shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**, shall continue in full force and effect until **August 8, 2026** (the “**Amendment Expiration Date**”), which is aligned with, but slightly earlier than, the Parent DPA’s **Expiration Date of August 20, 2026** as stated in **Section 4.1 of the Parent DPA**.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Any renewal of the Expanded Services after the Amendment Expiration Date shall be subject to mutual Written agreement of the Parties and may be documented as a further amendment or new agreement. The renewal provisions in **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent DPA shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Amendment.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
The Parties reaffirm the termination for convenience rights in **Section 4.3** of the Parent DPA, subject to the following clarifications:

  a. **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience and without cause upon **thirty (30) days’** prior Written notice to Meridian.

  b. **By Meridian.** Meridian may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’** prior Written notice to Contoso, and shall cooperate reasonably to facilitate transition of the Expanded Services.

Termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent DPA, which shall remain in effect unless separately terminated.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause rights set forth in **Section 4.4 of the Parent DPA** shall apply to this Amendment. Without limiting the generality of that section, a material breach of this Amendment may constitute a material breach of the Parent DPA and vice versa, as appropriate.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent DPA.**  
If the Parent DPA is terminated or expires for any reason:

  a. This Amendment shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such termination or expiration, unless the Parties expressly agree in Writing to continue certain data processing terms under this Amendment on a standalone basis.

  b. The obligations relating to data return or deletion, confidentiality, and other post-termination responsibilities under **Sections 4.6 and 4.7** and **Article XI** of the Parent DPA, as supplemented by this Amendment, shall apply to all Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment (whether or not the Parent DPA remains in effect):

  a. Meridian shall cease performing the Expanded Services and shall Process Personal Data related to the Expanded Services only as necessary to perform data return or deletion obligations under **Section 11.6 of this Amendment** and **Section 11.7 of the Parent DPA**, or as required by Applicable Law.

  b. Any undisputed fees accrued or payable as of the effective date of termination under this Amendment shall become immediately due and payable.

  c. Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information relating specifically to the Expanded Services in accordance with **Article V of the Parent DPA** and **Article V of this Amendment**.

4.7 **Survival.**  
Without limiting **Section 4.7 and Article XVI of the Parent DPA**, the following provisions of this Amendment shall survive its termination or expiration: **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XII (Service Level Agreements) with respect to accrued credits, XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions).**

## ARTICLE V – CONFIDENTIALITY

5.1 **Reaffirmation of Parent DPA Confidentiality.**  
The Parties reaffirm and incorporate **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the Parent DPA. Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services shall be deemed Confidential Information of Contoso consistent with **Section 5.5 of the Parent DPA**.

5.2 **Clarification for Expanded Services.**  
Without limiting **Section 5.1 through 5.7** of the Parent DPA:

  a. Security Audit Materials, Project Schedule details, and Enhanced Security Measures documentation shared by Meridian shall constitute Meridian’s Confidential Information.

  b. DIAs, Processing Instructions Addenda, and any internal compliance documentation provided by Contoso shall constitute Contoso’s Confidential Information.

5.3 **Survival Period.**  
The confidentiality obligations under this Amendment shall remain in effect for a period of **five (5) years** after termination or expiration of this Amendment, consistent with **Section 5.7** of the Parent DPA; provided that obligations regarding trade secrets and Personal Data shall continue for so long as such information remains protected as trade secrets or Personal Data under Applicable Law.

## ARTICLE VI – INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

6.1 **Ownership; Reference to Parent DPA.**  
The Parties reaffirm **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent DPA. As between the Parties:

  a. Contoso retains ownership of all Personal Data and any Deliverables designated as owned by Contoso under the Parent DPA or applicable SOWs.

  b. Meridian retains ownership of its platform, tools, methodologies, and Pre-existing IP, including any enhancements or derivative works developed in connection with the Expanded Services.

6.2 **Deliverables under Expanded Services.**  
Any new Deliverables created specifically under this Amendment (e.g., configuration scripts, integration mappings, compliance dashboard designs) shall be treated as Deliverables under **Section 1.8 and Section 6.1(b)** of the Parent DPA. To the extent they are not merely configurations of Meridian’s platform:

  a. Meridian grants Contoso the license rights described in **Section 6.4 (License to Deliverables)** of the Parent DPA, for internal business use.

  b. Any Meridian Pre-existing IP incorporated into such Deliverables shall remain owned by Meridian, and Contoso’s rights shall be limited to the permitted internal use and may not include sublicensing or commercialization.

6.3 **Use of De-identified Data.**  
Meridian may create and use De-identified Data from Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services in accordance with **Section 6.2(c)** and **Section 11.8** of the Parent DPA, provided that such De-identified Data does not identify Contoso or any Data Subject, and that no attempts at re-identification are made.

6.4 **Restrictions.**  
The restrictions on use of the other Party’s IP in **Section 6.6 of the Parent DPA** are incorporated by reference and apply equally to all materials developed or shared under this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IX – LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Modified Liability Cap for Amendment.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 9.2 of the Parent DPA**, which caps each Party’s aggregate liability to the total amount of fees paid or payable in the twelve (12) months preceding the event, for Claims arising **solely and directly** from the Expanded Services under this Amendment, each Party’s aggregate liability shall be capped at **five (5) times** the amount of fees **paid or payable by Contoso to Meridian under this Amendment** during the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9.2 **Exclusions and Exceptions.**  
The exclusions in **Section 9.1 of the Parent DPA** (no indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages) remain in full force and effect. The exceptions in **Section 9.3 of the Parent DPA** (indemnification obligations, confidentiality breaches, gross negligence/willful misconduct/fraud, and certain Security Incidents) shall apply equally to this Amendment. For those exceptions related solely to this Amendment, the enhanced cap described in **Section 9.1** above shall apply, unless Applicable Law requires otherwise.

9.3 **Allocation of Risk.**  
The Parties acknowledge and agree, as in **Section 9.4 of the Parent DPA**, that the limitations of liability in this Article reflect a fair allocation of risk in light of the Medium Risk Profile and the Amendment Contract Value.

## ARTICLE X – INSURANCE

10.1 **Reaffirmation of Insurance Obligations.**  
Meridian’s insurance obligations under **Article X (Insurance)** of the Parent DPA are reaffirmed. The coverage described in **Section 10.1**, including Cyber Liability/Data Breach Insurance of not less than US$3,000,000 per claim and in the aggregate, shall apply to risks associated with the Expanded Services.

10.2 **Evidence of Coverage for Expanded Services.**  
Upon Contoso’s reasonable request, Meridian shall confirm in Writing that its insurance policies cover the enhanced Processing activities and Expanded Services described in this Amendment, and shall provide certificates of insurance in accordance with **Section 10.2 of the Parent DPA**.

Meridian’s maintenance of insurance shall not limit its obligations or liability under this Amendment, except as expressly limited by **Article IX** of the Parent DPA and this Amendment.

## ARTICLE XI – DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

11.1 **Reaffirmation of Parent DPA Article XI.**  
The Parties reaffirm **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent DPA, including, without limitation, obligations to comply with Data Protection Laws, implement security measures, notify of Security Incidents, assist with Data Subject rights, manage Subprocessors, and handle data retention and deletion.

11.2 **Enhanced Security Measures and Incident Response.**  

  a. **Enhanced Security Measures.** In addition to the baseline measures set forth in **Section 11.2 of the Parent DPA**, Meridian shall:

   - Employ multi-factor authentication for all administrative access to systems Processing Personal Data under the Expanded Services;
   - Apply encryption in transit and at rest for all Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services, except where technically infeasible and agreed in Writing;
   - Conduct at least **annual vulnerability assessments** on the systems supporting the Expanded Services and remediate material findings in a commercially reasonable timeframe;
   - Maintain network segmentation or equivalent logical separation between Regional Hosting Zones to minimize the risk of unauthorized cross-region data flows.

  b. **Security Documentation.** Meridian shall, upon reasonable request and subject to appropriate confidentiality protections, make available Security Audit Materials relevant to the Expanded Services, as part of the audit rights acknowledged in **Section 11.9 of the Parent DPA**.

  c. **Incident Response Support.** In the event of a Security Incident affecting Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services, Meridian shall:

   - Comply with the notification and cooperation requirements of **Section 11.3 of the Parent DPA**; and  
   - Provide additional technical explanation of root cause, remediation steps, and mitigation strategies reasonably necessary for Contoso’s regulator and Data Subject notifications, if any, subject to reasonable reimbursement of extraordinary costs.

11.3 **International Data Transfers for Expanded Services.**  
To the extent the Expanded Services involve transfers of Personal Data from the EU, EEA, UK, or other restricted jurisdictions to a Third Country, the Parties shall:

  a. Implement appropriate Data Transfer Mechanisms as required by **Section 11.6 of the Parent DPA**;

  b. Document any new transfer flows in a Processing Instructions Addendum referencing this Amendment;

  c. Cooperate in addressing changes in law or regulatory guidance that affect the validity of the Data Transfer Mechanisms, including updating contractual terms as reasonably necessary.

11.4 **Data Impact Assessments.**  
Meridian shall, upon Contoso’s reasonable Written request, provide information necessary for Contoso to conduct DIAs related to high-risk Processing under the Expanded Services, consistent with its obligations under Data Protection Laws and subject to Meridian’s confidentiality, security, and trade secret protections.

11.5 **Use of De-identified and Aggregated Data.**  
Meridian may continue to create and use anonymized or aggregated data in accordance with **Section 11.8 of the Parent DPA**, provided that De-identified Data derived from the Expanded Services does not re-identify or single out Contoso or any Data Subject and is used only for internal analytics, benchmarking, service improvement, or research.

11.6 **Data Retention and Deletion for Expanded Services.**  

  a. Subject to **Section 11.7 of the Parent DPA**, Meridian shall retain Personal Data processed under the Expanded Services only as long as necessary to perform such services or as required by Applicable Law.

  b. Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment or upon Contoso’s Written request, Meridian shall return and/or delete Personal Data processed solely under the Expanded Services in accordance with **Section 11.7 of the Parent DPA** and provide written certification upon request, taking into account backup retention schedules.

## ARTICLE XII – SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLA)

12.1 **Relationship to Parent SLA.**  
The service level obligations set forth in **Article XII of the Parent DPA** (including **Section 12.1 – Availability**, **Section 12.4 – Response and Resolution Times**, and **Section 12.5 – Service Credits**) remain in full force and effect. This Article sets forth **Enhanced SLA** terms specific to the Expanded Services.

12.2 **Availability for Expanded Services.**  
Meridian shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the features and functionalities constituting the Expanded Services (e.g., cross-region analytics, compliance dashboards) are available **99.9% of the time** during each calendar month, measured separately from the base platform availability where technically feasible, and subject to Scheduled Maintenance and Excluded Downtime as defined in **Sections 12.2 and 12.3 of the Parent DPA**.

12.3 **Response Times for Critical Issues (Expanded Services).**  
Without limiting **Section 12.4 of the Parent DPA**, for issues classified as **Priority 1 (Critical)** that materially affect the Expanded Services, Meridian shall:

  a. Provide an initial response within **1 hour** of receiving the support request; and  
  b. Use commercially reasonable efforts to provide a workaround or resolution within **4 hours**, consistent with the standards of **Section 12.4(a)** of the Parent DPA.

12.4 **Service Credits for Expanded Services.**  
If Meridian fails to meet the availability commitment for the Expanded Services in a given calendar month, Contoso shall be eligible for incremental service credits calculated as a percentage of the monthly fees attributable to the Expanded Services:

  - Availability 99.0% – 99.89%: **5%** credit  
  - Availability 98.0% – 98.99%: **10%** credit  
  - Availability below 98.0%: **20%** credit

These credits are in addition to any credits applicable under **Section 12.5 of the Parent DPA** and shall be applied as set forth therein. Service credits are Contoso’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for SLA failures relating specifically to the Expanded Services, without prejudice to termination rights under **Article IV**.
