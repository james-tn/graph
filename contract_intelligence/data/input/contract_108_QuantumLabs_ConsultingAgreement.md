## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 Engagement of Quantum Labs

Contoso hereby engages Quantum Labs, and Quantum Labs hereby accepts such engagement, to perform the Services and provide the Deliverables described in this Agreement and each SOW executed pursuant hereto. Each SOW shall be incorporated into and governed by this Agreement.

### 2.2 General Description of Services

Quantum Labs shall provide specialized consulting, analytics, and technology‑related professional services to support Contoso’s strategic initiatives, which may include, without limitation:

1. **Strategic Advisory Services**  
   - Evaluation and optimization of Contoso’s technology and data strategy;  
   - Development of roadmaps for advanced analytics, AI, and automation initiatives;  
   - Recommendations regarding architecture, platforms, and tools.

2. **Data & Analytics Consulting**  
   - Assessment of Contoso’s data landscape, data quality, and governance practices;  
   - Design and implementation of data models, pipelines, and analytics frameworks;  
   - Development of predictive and prescriptive models, including prototypes and proofs of concept.

3. **Technology Implementation & Integration**  
   - Configuration and integration of Vendor Technology and/or third‑party tools into Contoso’s environment (subject to applicable license terms);  
   - Assistance with APIs, data connectors, and middleware;  
   - Performance optimization and tuning.

4. **Training and Knowledge Transfer**  
   - Development of training materials, playbooks, and documentation;  
   - Conduct of workshops, seminars, and train‑the‑trainer sessions;  
   - Provision of best‑practice guidelines and operational runbooks.

5. **Ongoing Support and Advisory**  
   - Ongoing consulting support, including office hours and ad hoc consultations;  
   - Periodic review of performance metrics and program outcomes;  
   - Recommendations for continuous improvement.

### 2.3 Statements of Work

2.3.1 **SOW Structure**  
Each SOW shall, at a minimum, specify:

- (a) the Project objectives and description of the Services;  
- (b) the Deliverables and acceptance criteria;  
- (c) the Project plan, including milestones, timeline, and estimated completion dates;  
- (d) the Fees, payment schedule, and any relevant assumptions;  
- (e) the responsibilities of each Party;  
- (f) any applicable Service Levels; and  
- (g) any special terms that supplement or, where expressly stated, supersede this Agreement.

2.3.2 **Conflicts**  
In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Agreement and an SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall control, except to the extent an SOW expressly states that a specific provision of this Agreement is modified solely for purposes of that SOW.

### 2.4 Deliverables; Acceptance

2.4.1 **Provision of Deliverables**  
Quantum Labs shall deliver the Deliverables described in each SOW in accordance with the timelines and milestones set forth therein. Quantum Labs shall perform the Services using commercially reasonable efforts and in a professional and workmanlike manner in accordance with generally accepted industry standards.

2.4.2 **Review and Acceptance Testing**  
Unless otherwise specified in the applicable SOW:

- (a) Contoso shall have **ten (10) Business Days** after delivery of a Deliverable (the **“Review Period”**) to review and either (i) provide written acceptance, or (ii) provide written notice identifying in reasonable detail any material non‑conformities with the acceptance criteria specified in the SOW.  
- (b) If Contoso fails to provide written notice of acceptance or non‑conformity within the Review Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted as of the last day of the Review Period.  
- (c) Upon receipt of a timely rejection notice, Quantum Labs shall use commercially reasonable efforts to correct the identified non‑conformities and resubmit the Deliverable. Contoso shall have an additional Review Period to evaluate the corrected Deliverable.  
- (d) If Quantum Labs is unable to remedy material non‑conformities after a reasonable number of attempts (not fewer than two (2)), the Parties shall discuss in good faith an equitable adjustment in scope, Fees, or timeline.

2.4.3 **Partial Deliverables**  
Where a Deliverable is comprised of multiple components or phases, Contoso’s acceptance of any component or phase shall not be unreasonably withheld or delayed pending completion of subsequent components or phases.

### 2.5 Performance Standards

Quantum Labs shall:

- (a) assign qualified personnel with appropriate skills and experience to perform the Services;  
- (b) dedicate such time and resources as are commercially reasonable to meet agreed timelines;  
- (c) comply with all applicable laws and regulations in providing the Services; and

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Contract Value; Fee Structure

3.1.1 **Contract Value**  
The Parties acknowledge that the projected aggregate value of this Agreement, including all SOWs anticipated as of the Effective Date, is **US$186,411** (the **“Contract Value”**). Fees under this Agreement may include one or more of the following, as detailed in each SOW:

- (a) Fixed‑fee amounts for defined Deliverables or phases;  
- (b) Time‑and‑materials fees based on hourly or daily rates;  
- (c) Recurring subscription or retainer fees for ongoing Services; and  
- (d) Success‑based or performance‑based fees, where expressly stated.

### 3.2 Payment Terms

3.2.1 **Invoices**  
Quantum Labs shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the billing schedule specified in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified, Quantum Labs may invoice monthly in arrears for time‑and‑materials Services and upon achievement of milestones for fixed‑fee Deliverables.

3.2.2 **Net 60 Terms**  
All undisputed invoices shall be due and payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) days** of the invoice date (**“Net 60”**). Payment shall be made in U.S. dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or other mutually agreed method.

3.2.3 **Disputed Amounts**  
If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice, Contoso shall, within fifteen (15) days of receipt of such invoice, provide written notice describing the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute. Contoso shall timely pay all undisputed portions of the invoice. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve any disputed amounts. Failure to provide such notice within the stated period shall be deemed acceptance of the invoice.

### 3.3 Late Payments

Any undisputed amounts not paid when due under Section 3.2 shall accrue interest at the lesser of:

- (a) one and one‑half percent (1.5%) per month; or  
- (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law,

from the due date until the date of actual payment. Quantum Labs may, upon written notice, suspend performance of the Services if any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after its due date, provided that Quantum Labs shall promptly resume performance upon receipt of all overdue amounts and accrued interest.

### 3.4 Expenses

Contoso shall reimburse Quantum Labs for reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Services, including but not limited to travel, lodging, meals, and specialized tools or data sets, in accordance with Contoso’s then‑current travel and expense policies provided to Quantum Labs in advance. Quantum Labs shall provide reasonable documentation for all reimbursable expenses.

### 3.5 Taxes

Fees and expenses are exclusive of Taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for all applicable Taxes arising out of or in connection with this Agreement, excluding Taxes based on Quantum Labs’ net income. If any withholding or deduction is required by law, Contoso shall pay such additional amounts as necessary so that Quantum Labs receives the full amount it would have received absent such withholding or deduction, provided that Contoso shall furnish appropriate documentation of any Taxes withheld.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **October 20, 2028** (the **“Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the **“Term”**).

### 4.2 Renewal

The Parties may mutually agree in writing to renew this Agreement for additional periods under terms and conditions to be negotiated in good faith. Absent such written agreement, this Agreement shall terminate automatically on the Expiration Date.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

Contoso may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to Quantum Labs. Quantum Labs may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon not less than **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Contoso.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 **Material Breach**  
Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for material breach by the other Party, provided that:

- (a) the non‑breaching Party provides written notice specifying in reasonable detail the nature of the breach; and  
- (b) the breaching Party fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or such longer period as the Parties may agree in writing) after receipt of such notice.

4.4.2 **Insolvency**  
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party (i) becomes insolvent, (ii) files or has filed against it a petition in bankruptcy that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days, (iii) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, or (iv) has a receiver, trustee, or similar official appointed over a material portion of its assets.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

- (a) all SOWs shall automatically terminate, unless otherwise agreed in writing;  
- (b) Contoso shall pay Quantum Labs all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, including, in the case of termination for convenience by Contoso, any non‑cancelable commitments and reasonable wind‑down costs;  
- (c) each Party shall, upon written request and subject to Section 11.5, return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information and Client Personal Data, except as required for archival purposes or by law; and  
- (d) the licenses granted to Contoso under Article VI shall continue in accordance with their terms, subject to Contoso’s full payment of all amounts due.

### 4.6 Survival

Any provisions of this Agreement which by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including without limitation Article V (Confidentiality), Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights), Article VII (Representations and Warranties), Article VIII (Indemnification), Article IX (Limitation of Liability), Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy), Article XIV (Dispute Resolution), Article XVI (General Provisions), and any payment obligations accrued prior to termination.

“Confidential Information” means any non‑public, proprietary, or confidential information disclosed by or on behalf of a Party (**“Disclosing Party”**) to the other Party (**“Receiving Party”**), whether orally or in writing, and whether before or after the Effective Date, including but not limited to business plans, strategies, financial data, technical information, software, know‑how, trade secrets, Client Personal Data, and the terms of this Agreement. ### 5.2 Obligations

The Receiving Party shall:

- (a) use Confidential Information solely for the purposes of performing or receiving the Services under this Agreement;  
- (b) not disclose Confidential Information to any third party except as expressly permitted herein;  
- (c) restrict access to Confidential Information to its and its Affiliates’ employees, contractors, and professional advisers who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those set forth herein; and  
- (d) protect Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information of a similar nature, but in no event less than reasonable care.

### 5.3 Exceptions

The obligations in Section 5.2 shall not apply to any information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate:

- (a) was lawfully known to it without restriction prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party;  
- (b) becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party;  
- (c) was received from a third party who did not acquire or disclose such information in violation of any legal or contractual obligation; or  
- (d) was independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information.

### 5.4 Compelled Disclosure

If the Receiving Party is required by law, regulation, or court order to disclose any Confidential Information, it may do so provided that, to the extent legally permissible, it (a) gives the Disclosing Party prompt written notice of the requirement to allow the Disclosing Party to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy, and (b) discloses only that portion of the Confidential Information that is legally required.

### 5.5 Survival

The obligations in this Article V shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue for a period of **five (5) years** following the termination or expiration of this Agreement; provided that trade secrets and Client Personal Data shall be kept confidential for so long as they qualify as such under applicable law.

- (b) use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact of the Force Majeure Event on its performance; and  
- (c) resume performance as soon as reasonably practicable.

### 15.3 Suspension and Termination
