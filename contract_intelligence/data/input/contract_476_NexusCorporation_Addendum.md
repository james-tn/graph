## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum **supplements and extends** the scope of Products and Services set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. All provisions of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 2.1 through 2.9**, shall apply to the Additional Products and Additional Services described herein, except to the extent expressly modified by this Addendum.

2.1.2 For purposes of **Section 2.1 (Overview)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties acknowledge that this Addendum constitutes a specific engagement under the master framework established therein, with the commercial details for the Additional Products and Additional Services set forth in this Addendum and its Schedules.

2.2 **Overview of Additional Products and Services.**  

2.2.1 Under this Addendum, Nexus shall supply to Contoso the following key components, which are further detailed in **Schedules 1 and 2**:

  (a) **Infrastructure Hardware Expansion.** Additional enterprise-grade servers, storage arrays, high-throughput networking equipment, and security appliances to support Contoso’s expanded production workloads and disaster recovery sites, in alignment with the types of hardware described in **Section 2.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Advanced Software Modules.** Additional proprietary Nexus software and selected third-party software (collectively the “Additional Software”), including capacity management tools, real-time analytics modules, and enhanced security and compliance features, licensed on a mix of perpetual and subscription terms consistent with **Section 2.2(b)** and **Section 6.4** of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) **Professional and Implementation Services.** Extended professional Services similar to those described in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, including assessment and design, implementation and configuration, data migration, customizations and integrations, testing and acceptance support, training, and project management, but specifically scoped for the Additional Products.

  (d) **Enhanced Support and Maintenance Services.** Expanded support for the Additional Products, including 24x7 incident management, accelerated response times, patching, Upgrades, and access to a dedicated technical account manager, consistent with **Section 2.4 (Support and Maintenance Services)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (e) **Optional Managed Services.** At Contoso’s request and subject to a corresponding Change Order under **Article XIII**, Nexus may assume day-to-day operational responsibilities for certain elements of the Project Environment, including monitoring, incident response, and routine operations, under mutually agreed runbooks and procedures.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Additional Products.**  

2.3.1 **Hardware.** Nexus shall provide the hardware listed in **Schedule 1**, which shall at minimum include:

  - High-availability compute nodes designed for virtualization and container orchestration;
  - Tiered storage systems including SSD and high-capacity disks, with built-in replication and snapshot capabilities;
  - Core and edge networking devices supporting multi-gigabit throughput, QoS policies, and advanced routing;
  - Security appliances, including next-generation firewalls and intrusion detection/prevention systems.

2.3.2 Each hardware unit shall be delivered with serial numbers, firmware versions, and configuration templates in accordance with Nexus’s standard Documentation and in compliance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.3 **Software and Licenses.** Nexus shall license to Contoso the Additional Software detailed in **Schedule 1**, including:

  - Capacity and performance monitoring software;
  - Security information and event management (SIEM) integrations;
  - Data protection and backup orchestration modules;
  - Compliance reporting modules for regulatory frameworks applicable to Contoso’s industry.

Licenses shall be granted in accordance with **Section 6.4 (License to Products and Software)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 6.2** of this Addendum.

2.4 **Professional Services – Phases.** Nexus shall perform the Additional Services in the following phases, consistent with **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Phase 1 – Assessment & Design (Estimated 8–10 weeks).** Nexus shall:

  - Review Contoso’s existing infrastructure and solutions deployed under the Parent Agreement;
  - Collect updated capacity, resilience, and security requirements;
  - Develop an integrated architecture design incorporating the Additional Products;
  - Deliver a “Detailed Design Document” and “Implementation Project Plan” (Deliverables).

  (b) **Phase 2 – Implementation & Configuration (Estimated 16–20 weeks).** Nexus shall:

  - Install and configure the Additional Products in Contoso’s environments;
  - Integrate relevant systems with Contoso’s existing applications and third-party platforms;
  - Configure monitoring, alerting, backup, and failover mechanisms;
  - Conduct initial system health checks and readiness validation.

  (c) **Phase 3 – Data Migration & Cutover (Estimated 8–12 weeks).** Nexus shall:

  - Plan and execute migration of selected workloads and data from legacy or existing systems;
  - Coordinate cutover windows with Contoso’s project team;
  - Validate data integrity, performance, and resilience post-migration;
  - Provide rollback and contingency procedures in accordance with best practices.

  (d) **Phase 4 – Testing & Acceptance Support (Estimated 6–8 weeks).** Nexus shall:

  - Support Contoso’s user acceptance testing (“UAT”), performance testing, and failover tests;
  - Remediate defects, misconfigurations, or performance issues identified during testing;
  - Prepare and deliver “As-Built Documentation” and “Operational Runbooks” (Deliverables).

  (e) **Phase 5 – Training & Knowledge Transfer (Estimated 4–6 weeks).** Nexus shall:

  - Provide role-based training sessions (onsite or remote) for administrators, operators, and support staff;
  - Deliver training materials, including slide decks, lab guides, and reference documentation;
  - Conduct a formal knowledge transfer workshop prior to final Operational Acceptance.

2.5 **Project Plan and Timelines.**  

2.5.1 The Parties shall, in accordance with **Section 2.5 (Project Plan and Timelines)** of the Parent Agreement, jointly finalize an Implementation Project Plan within thirty (30) days following the Addendum Effective Date, which shall set forth detailed Milestones and dependencies.

2.5.2 Nexus shall use commercially reasonable efforts to complete the Milestones within the target dates specified in the Implementation Project Plan. Contoso acknowledges, as set forth in **Section 2.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement, that delays in providing required access, information, or approvals may impact such timelines.

2.5.3 Any material deviation from the agreed Milestones, other than those caused by Contoso or a Force Majeure Event (as defined in **Article XV** of the Parent Agreement and replicated in **Article XV** of this Addendum), shall entitle Contoso to request a corrective action plan and, if delays are significant and uncured, to exercise its rights under **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.6 **Acceptance of Deliverables and Milestones.**  

2.6.1 The acceptance procedures in **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Deliverables and Milestones under this Addendum, subject to the additional details below.

2.6.2 For each Milestone, Nexus shall provide written notice of completion and deliver the associated Deliverables. Contoso shall have:

  - **Fifteen (15) Business Days** to review and either (i) accept in writing, or (ii) reject in writing with reasonable detail describing any material non-conformities, consistent with **Section 2.8(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.3 If Contoso rejects a Deliverable, Nexus shall promptly correct the non-conformities at no additional cost, and resubmit the Deliverable. Contoso shall have ten (10) Business Days for re-review in accordance with **Section 2.8(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.4 Failure by Contoso to respond within the applicable review period shall result in deemed acceptance of the Deliverable, except where the Deliverable contains defects that could not reasonably be detected during such review period, in which case Contoso’s rights under **Article VII (Representations and Warranties)** and **Article VIII (Indemnification)** of the Parent Agreement remain unaffected.

2.7 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  

Consistent with **Section 2.7 (Contoso Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  (a) Provide Nexus with timely access to relevant systems, network segments, test environments, and personnel;

  (b) Designate a project manager who shall serve as primary point of contact and decision-maker for approvals and escalations;

  (c) Ensure that any necessary third-party consents or licenses not provided by Nexus are obtained;

  (d) Perform any tasks assigned to Contoso in the Implementation Project Plan, including data validation, business acceptance, and operational readiness tasks; and

  (e) Comply with reasonable security and operational guidelines provided by Nexus for the Project Environment.

2.8 **Enhanced Support Services and Service Levels.**  

2.8.1 Nexus shall provide Enhanced Support Services for the Additional Products as described in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XII** of this Addendum, including:

  - Target monthly availability of **99.9%** for any hosted or managed service components, as per **Section 12.1** of the Parent Agreement;
  - Incident response and resolution targets aligned with **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement (Severity 1–4 classifications).

2.8.2 Failure to meet the availability commitment for the Additional Products shall entitle Contoso to SLA Credits calculated in accordance with **Section 12.4 (Service Credits)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 12.4** of this Addendum.

2.9 **Non-Exclusivity.**  

Consistent with **Section 2.9 (Non-Exclusivity)** of the Parent Agreement, this Addendum is non-exclusive, and Contoso remains free to procure similar products or services from other vendors, subject to the confidentiality obligations set forth in **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge that the total estimated value of this Addendum (the “Addendum Fees”) is **Four Million Ninety-Six Thousand Two Hundred Forty-Five U.S. Dollars (US$4,096,245)**. This amount represents the aggregate of all anticipated Fees over the Addendum Term but does not constitute a minimum or maximum purchase commitment except as expressly specified in this Addendum or any attached Schedule.

3.1.2 This Addendum’s value is **in addition to** the estimated contract value of **US$2,797,951** referenced in **Section 3.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  

The Addendum Fees shall consist of the following components, each more fully described in **Schedule 3 (Fee and Payment Schedule)**:

  (a) **Hardware Purchase Fees.** One-time purchase prices for the Additional Products hardware listed in Schedule 1;

  (b) **Software License Fees.** License fees for the Additional Software, including (i) perpetual license fees and (ii) subscription-based license fees for the applicable term;

  (c) **Professional Services Fees.** Fees for professional and implementation Services, based on a combination of fixed-price Milestones and time-and-materials tasks as indicated in Schedule 3;

  (d) **Support and Maintenance Fees.** Annual or periodic fees for Enhanced Support Services and maintenance of the Additional Products;

  (e) **Optional Managed Services Fees.** If elected by Contoso and documented via Change Order, recurring fees for managed operations of the Project Environment.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  

3.3.1 Invoicing shall follow the framework set out in **Section 3.2 (Invoicing)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to the following specifics for this Addendum:

  (a) Hardware and perpetual software licenses shall be invoiced **upon shipment** of the relevant Additional Products, consistent with **Section 3.2(a)(i)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) Subscription-based licenses and recurring support or maintenance Services shall be invoiced **annually in advance**, consistent with **Section 3.2(a)(ii)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) Professional Services shall be invoiced (i) upon completion and acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of Milestones, for fixed-price components; and (ii) monthly in arrears for time-and-materials components, consistent with **Section 3.2(a)(iii)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.2 Payment terms for all undisputed invoices under this Addendum shall be **Net 30** (payment due thirty (30) days from the date of invoice), consistent with **Section 3.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.3 If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice, it shall follow the dispute procedure in **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion within the Net 30 term.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest as set forth in **Section 3.4 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement (the lesser of 1% per month or the maximum permitted by law). Nexus may suspend performance of the affected Additional Services or deliveries of Additional Products in accordance with Section 3.4, following written notice and subject to proportionality.

3.5 **Taxes.**  

3.5.1 All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 If Contoso is required to withhold taxes, it shall provide Nexus with appropriate documentation and cooperate in minimizing such withholding, as outlined in **Section 3.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

Reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Nexus in connection with the Additional Services (e.g., travel, lodging, per diem) shall be reimbursed by Contoso without markup, in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Expenses and Reimbursement)** of the Parent Agreement and subject to Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies.

3.7 **No Set-Off.**  

Except as expressly permitted under the Parent Agreement or agreed in writing by Nexus, Contoso shall not set off amounts due under this Addendum against other amounts, consistent with **Section 3.7 (No Set-Off)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall remain in effect until **October 19, 2027** (the “Addendum Term”).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  

Notwithstanding the Addendum Term specified above, this Addendum shall not extend the **Term** of the Parent Agreement as defined in **Section 4.1** thereof (which currently runs through **December 2, 2028**). If the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated, this Addendum shall be affected as provided in **Section 4.6** below.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Nexus, consistent with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.2 Nexus may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, consistent with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum (in whole or in part) upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement with respect to the Additional Products or Additional Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice, consistent with **Section 4.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement. Insolvency events in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement shall also justify termination.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) All outstanding unpaid invoices relating to this Addendum shall become immediately due and payable, consistent with **Section 4.6(a)(i)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) Nexus shall cease performance of the terminated Additional Services, and any time-limited or subscription-based licenses under this Addendum shall terminate as per **Section 4.6(a)(iii)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (c) Contoso shall cease use of any terminated subscription-based components and certify discontinuation upon request, as required by **Section 4.6(a)(iii)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (d) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information related to this Addendum in accordance with **Section 5.5 (Return or Destruction)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.2 In the event of termination for Nexus’s uncured material breach, Contoso shall be entitled to a pro rata refund of any prepaid Fees for Additional Services or subscriptions not yet performed or delivered as of the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.6(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.3 In the event of termination for Contoso’s convenience, Contoso shall pay Nexus for (i) all Additional Products delivered and Additional Services performed up to the effective date of termination; and (ii) any non-cancellable commitments or costs reasonably incurred by Nexus, subject to Nexus’s duty to mitigate, consistent with **Section 4.6(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5.4 **Transition Services.** At Contoso’s request, and subject to mutually agreed fees (time-and-materials, unless otherwise agreed), Nexus shall provide reasonable Transition Services for up to ninety (90) days after termination or expiration to assist Contoso in transitioning operations, data, and support either back in-house or to another provider.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Parent Agreement.**  

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated in whole, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain obligations under terms no less protective than those in the Parent Agreement.

4.6.2 If only specific SOWs or POs under the Parent Agreement are terminated, this Addendum shall remain in effect unless explicitly referenced in the termination notice, consistent with **Section 4.5 (Termination of Specific SOWs or POs)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Survival.**  

The provisions that by their nature should survive termination or expiration of this Addendum shall so survive, including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, XVI**, and Sections **3.4, 3.5, 4.5, and 4.6** of this Addendum, together with the corresponding surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as identified in **Section 4.7** thereof.
