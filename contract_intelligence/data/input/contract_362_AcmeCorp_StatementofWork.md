## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview.**  
Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Acme shall perform the SOW Services and provide the SOW Deliverables described in this Article II and **Exhibit A**. The Project covered by this SOW is a multi-year strategic transformation program intended to support Contoso’s enterprise technology modernization, data analytics capability expansion, and process optimization.

2.2 **Objectives.**  
The primary objectives of the SOW Services are to:

  2.2.1 Conduct a comprehensive **current‑state assessment** of Contoso’s core business processes, enterprise applications, and data architecture, consistent with **Section 2.1.2 (Technology consulting)** of the Parent Agreement;

  2.2.2 Design a **target operating model** and **technology roadmap** for Contoso’s core systems, including ERP, CRM, and analytics platforms, in alignment with **Section 2.1.1 (Strategic advisory services)**;

  2.2.3 Provide **implementation and configuration support** for selected software platforms, including integration with existing systems, consistent with **Section 2.1.3** of the Parent Agreement;

  2.2.4 Establish **data analytics and reporting capabilities**, including dashboards and KPIs, in line with **Section 2.1.5 (Data analytics and reporting services)**;

  2.2.5 Provide **program and project management** for the overall transformation, consistent with **Section 2.1.4** of the Parent Agreement, including governance, risk management, and stakeholder communications;

  2.2.6 Deliver **training and knowledge transfer** to Contoso personnel, as contemplated by **Section 2.1.6 (Training and knowledge transfer services)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **SOW Deliverables.**  
Without limiting the general definition of “Deliverables” in **Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement**, Acme shall provide the following key SOW Deliverables (each further detailed in **Exhibit A**):

  2.3.1 **Current-State Assessment Report.** A written assessment report in accordance with **Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement**, covering business processes, application landscape, integration points, data flows, and operational pain points.

  2.3.2 **Future-State Architecture and Roadmap.** Solution design documents, including technical architectures, integration specifications, configuration guidelines, and a three‑year roadmap, consistent with **Section 2.3.3 of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.3.3 **Detailed Project Plan and Governance Framework.** A comprehensive project plan, work breakdown structure, resource plan, and risk register as contemplated in **Section 2.3.2 of the Parent Agreement**, as well as a governance framework document defining roles, responsibilities, and reporting.

  2.3.4 **Configured Environments and Prototypes.** One or more configured non-production environments (e.g., “sandboxes”) and prototypes for ERP, CRM, and analytics solutions, as described in **Section 2.3.4 of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.3.5 **Integration Specifications and Test Artifacts.** Interface design, mapping documents, test plans, test cases, and test summary reports for system integrations.

  2.3.6 **Data Models, Dashboards, and KPI Catalog.** Logical and physical data models, analytics dashboards, and a catalog of KPIs and metrics aligned to Contoso’s business objectives, as contemplated in **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

  2.3.7 **Training Materials and User Guides.** Training decks, user manuals, standard operating procedures (SOPs), and quick‑reference guides, consistent with **Section 2.3.5** of the Parent Agreement.

  2.3.8 **Status Reports and Executive Dashboards.** Periodic written status reports and executive dashboards describing progress, issues, risks, and mitigation plans, consistent with **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Project Phases, Milestones, and Timeline.**  
The Project will be structured into the following Phases, each with indicative timelines within the SOW Term. Detailed dates will be specified in the Project Plan subject to mutual agreement by the Parties within thirty (30) days after the SOW Effective Date.

  2.4.1 **Phase 1 – Discovery & Assessment (Months 1–4).**  
  Activities: stakeholder interviews, workshops, documentation review, system walkthroughs, data profiling.  
  Milestones: (a) Stakeholder alignment workshop completed; (b) Draft Current-State Assessment Report delivered; (c) Final Current-State Assessment Report accepted in accordance with **Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.4.2 **Phase 2 – Design & Roadmap (Months 3–7).**  
  Activities: target operating model definition, solution option analysis, architecture design, roadmap planning, governance model definition.  
  Milestones: (a) Draft Future-State Architecture Document delivered; (b) Solution design review sessions completed; (c) Final Technology Roadmap and Governance Framework accepted.

  2.4.3 **Phase 3 – Implementation & Configuration (Months 6–28).**  
  Activities: environment setup, configuration of ERP and CRM modules, integration development, iterative configuration cycles, unit and system testing.  
  Milestones: (a) Initial configured environment available; (b) Completion of key module configurations; (c) Completion of integration testing; (d) User acceptance testing sign-off by Contoso (subject to **Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement**).

  2.4.4 **Phase 4 – Data Analytics Enablement (Months 10–34).**  
  Activities: data model development, ETL/ELT configuration, dashboard and report development, KPI implementation, performance tuning.  
  Milestones: (a) Data model baseline accepted; (b) First wave of dashboards deployed to test; (c) KPI catalog accepted; (d) Analytics solution ready for production.

  2.4.5 **Phase 5 – Training, Transition & Optimization (Months 24–40).**  
  Activities: knowledge transfer sessions, training delivery, cutover support, hypercare period, optimization and enhancements.  
  Milestones: (a) Completion of end‑user training; (b) Go‑live support completed; (c) Hypercare completion sign-off; (d) Final Project Closure Report accepted.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
The performance standards in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement apply to this SOW. In addition:

  2.5.1 Acme shall assign appropriately qualified and experienced **Key Personnel** to perform the SOW Services.

  2.5.2 Acme shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the milestones set forth in **Section 2.4** and the Project Plan, recognizing that delays caused by Client Dependencies or Force Majeure Events shall excuse corresponding delays.

  2.5.3 The SOW Service Levels set out in **Article XII of this SOW** shall apply to project management, status reporting, and issue response.

2.6 **Acceptance of SOW Deliverables.**  
The acceptance procedures of **Section 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** in the Parent Agreement are incorporated herein by reference and shall govern the acceptance of all SOW Deliverables, subject to the following SOW-specific provisions:

  2.6.1 Unless otherwise agreed, Contoso shall have **ten (10) Business Days** to review each Deliverable, as provided in **Section 2.6.2 of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.6.2 If Contoso fails to respond within the review period, Acme may deem the Deliverable accepted pursuant to **Section 2.6.4(b) of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.6.3 Acceptance Criteria for key Deliverables are described in **Exhibit A**. Where Exhibit A is silent, the Deliverable shall be deemed conforming if it materially meets the scope and description in this SOW and the Project Plan.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities and Dependencies.**  
In addition to the Client responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement**, Contoso shall:

  2.7.1 Provide a dedicated project sponsor and project manager with decision‑making authority, consistent with **Section 2.5.2 of the Parent Agreement**.

  2.7.2 Ensure timely access to relevant systems, including test and non-production environments, for Acme’s personnel.

  2.7.3 Ensure participation by business and IT stakeholders in workshops, design sessions, and testing activities.

  2.7.4 Review and provide consolidated feedback on Deliverables within the specified review periods. Delays in providing feedback may result in schedule adjustments and, where reasonably attributable, Fee adjustments via Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement**.

Acme may use Subcontractors in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Subcontractors)** of the Parent Agreement. Any Subcontractors materially engaged in development, configuration, or handling of Personal Data shall be identified to Contoso upon reasonable request.

The Parties shall establish a **Project Governance Committee (PGC)** that will:

  2.9.1 Meet at least monthly (or more frequently as mutually agreed) to review progress, issues, and risks;

Upon termination or expiration of this SOW:

  4.5.1 Acme’s obligations to perform SOW Services shall cease, except as reasonably necessary to effect an orderly wind‑down as described in **Section 4.5.1 of the Parent Agreement**.

  4.5.2 Contoso shall pay Acme for all SOW Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, in accordance with **Section 4.5.2 of the Parent Agreement**, including non‑cancellable commitments that cannot be mitigated.

4.6 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this SOW that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall survive, together with the surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement referenced in **Section 4.6 (Survival)** thereof.
