## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW and Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed **under and subject to** the terms and conditions of the Parent SOW (SOW-EPS-202412-537 / contract_537) and the Consulting Agreement (CSA-EPS-202412-519 / contract_519), as contemplated in **Section 2.1 (Relationship to Parent Agreement)** and **Section 16.1 (Reference to Master/Parent Agreement)** of the Parent SOW.

2.1.2 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Addendum and the terms of the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement, the following order of precedence shall apply with respect to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables only: (a) this Addendum; (b) the Parent SOW; and (c) the Consulting Agreement; **provided, however**, that the Consulting Agreement shall control with respect to any matter not specifically addressed or modified by this Addendum or the Parent SOW.

2.1.3 This Addendum does not alter or diminish the obligations of the Parties under the Parent SOW for services and deliverables outside the scope of this Addendum, except to the extent expressly stated herein.

2.2 **Project Extension Description.**  

2.2.1 The Extended Services under this Addendum shall form part of the overall Project titled **“Contoso Enterprise Digital Transformation and Process Optimization Program – Phase I & II”** as defined in **Section 1.1.5** and **Section 2.2** of the Parent SOW, and will specifically constitute a new phase, referred to herein as **“Phase III – Enterprise Rollout & Automation.”**

2.2.2 During Phase III – Enterprise Rollout & Automation, Epsilon shall provide advanced strategic consulting, implementation support, and operational enablement services to:

  (a) Scale the ERP and CRM solutions configured and piloted under Phases 4 and 5 of the Parent SOW (**Section 2.4.2, Phases 4 and 5**) across additional business units and geographies;  

  (b) Design, implement, and document end-to-end automation of key cross-functional processes (including, but not limited to, order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, and customer case resolution workflows), building upon the “Target Operating Model and Process Designs” Deliverables identified in **Section 2.3.1(b)** of the Parent SOW;  

  (c) Implement advanced analytics, KPI dashboards, and performance management frameworks extending the “Status Reporting & Executive Dashboards” Deliverables in **Section 2.3.1(g)** of the Parent SOW to an enterprise-wide audience;  

  (d) Establish a sustainable governance and operating model for ongoing digital transformation initiatives, including a Center of Excellence (“CoE”) for process improvement, technology adoption, and benefit realization, aligned with the “Benefit Realization and Post-Go-Live Review” Deliverables in **Section 2.3.1(h)** of the Parent SOW; and  

  (e) Provide targeted training, coaching, and knowledge transfer to Contoso’s internal teams to assume ownership of the configured ERP/CRM platforms, automation assets, and analytics capabilities.

2.2.3 The Extended Services shall include, without limitation, services consistent with and expanding upon the types of services described in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.2.1 through 2.2.9** of the Consulting Agreement, specifically tailored to Phase III – Enterprise Rollout & Automation. This includes:

  - Enterprise-level rollout planning and coordination;  
  - Additional solution design and configuration for new legal entities, business units, and product lines;  
  - Automation design and development leveraging workflow engines, integration platforms, and RPA tools, as applicable;  
  - Organizational change management and communication planning for global deployment;  
  - Post-rollout hypercare and operational stabilization; and  
  - Establishment of metrics and governance mechanisms for continuous improvement.

2.3 **Extended Deliverables.**  

2.3.1 In addition to the Deliverables described in **Section 2.3** of the Parent SOW, Epsilon shall develop and deliver the following **Extended Deliverables** under this Addendum:

  (a) **Enterprise Rollout Blueprint** – A detailed rollout strategy and execution plan covering additional regions, business units, and functional areas, including a cutover approach, communication strategy, and risk mitigation plan.  

  (b) **Automation Design Specifications** – Detailed technical and functional design documentation for automated workflows, integrations, and RPA bots, referencing existing configurations and architectures delivered under **Section 2.3.1(c) and (e)** of the Parent SOW.  

  (c) **Configured Automation Assets** – Implemented and tested automated workflows, scripts, integration jobs, and RPA bots deployed in Contoso’s environments, with version-controlled documentation and operational runbooks.  

  (d) **Enhanced KPI & Analytics Dashboard Suite** – Extended dashboards and reports providing consolidated visibility into process performance, system usage, and benefit realization metrics across the enlarged scope of the Project.  

  (e) **CoE Operating Model and Governance Playbook** – A comprehensive document detailing roles, responsibilities, decision rights, intake and prioritization processes, standards, and templates for managing ongoing digital initiatives.  

  (f) **Training Programs & Learning Paths** – Role-based training curricula, e-learning modules, and certification paths for Contoso personnel covering ERP/CRM capabilities, automation tooling, and CoE processes.  

  (g) **Post-Rollout Stabilization and Optimization Report** – A consolidated report summarizing rollout outcomes, issues encountered, remediation activities, optimization opportunities, and an updated benefit realization forecast.

2.3.2 Each Extended Deliverable shall include a version number, date, and reference to both this Addendum (**ADD-EPS-202412-620**) and the Parent SOW (**SOW-EPS-202412-537**) and shall be delivered in electronic format (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, or other mutually agreed format), consistent with **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent SOW.

2.3.3 To the extent any Extended Deliverable incorporates Epsilon’s Pre-Existing IP or Third-Party Materials, ownership and licensing shall be governed by **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent SOW and **Article VI** of the Consulting Agreement, as supplemented by **Article VI** of this Addendum.

2.4 **Project Phases, Timelines, and Milestones Under this Addendum.**  

2.4.1 Epsilon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to perform the Extended Services in accordance with the timelines agreed in this Addendum, subject to Contoso fulfilling its obligations under **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6** of the Consulting Agreement. Timeframes shall be treated as estimates, consistent with **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent SOW.

2.4.2 The Extended Services under this Addendum shall be organized as follows (relative to the Addendum Effective Date):

  - **Phase III-A – Detailed Planning & Design (Months 1–4)**  
    - Activities: Detailed rollout planning, stakeholder alignment, automation opportunity assessment, backlog prioritization.  
    - Key Extended Deliverables: Enterprise Rollout Blueprint; Automation Design Specifications (initial wave).  

  - **Phase III-B – Build & Configure (Months 4–12)**  
    - Activities: Configuration of additional entities and business units; development of automation assets; integration refinement; test planning.  
    - Key Extended Deliverables: Configured Automation Assets (initial waves); updated Architecture and Integration Maps referencing **Section 2.3.1(c)** of the Parent SOW; updated Test Scripts.  

  - **Phase III-C – Enterprise Rollout (Months 12–18)**  
    - Activities: Production cutover for additional regions; user training; hypercare support; incident management; change management execution.  
    - Key Extended Deliverables: Enhanced KPI & Analytics Dashboard Suite; Training Programs & Learning Paths; rollout status reports aligning with **Section 2.3.1(g)** of the Parent SOW.  

  - **Phase III-D – Optimization & CoE Establishment (Months 18–26)**  
    - Activities: Optimization sprints; defect remediation; performance tuning; CoE design and implementation; knowledge transfer.  
    - Key Extended Deliverables: CoE Operating Model and Governance Playbook; Post-Rollout Stabilization and Optimization Report; updated Benefit Realization Framework aligned with **Section 2.3.1(h)** of the Parent SOW.

2.4.3 Detailed Milestones, acceptance criteria, and associated payment triggers under this Addendum are set forth in **Exhibit A (Addendum Milestone and Deliverable Schedule)**, which shall be deemed an exhibit to the Parent SOW for purposes of **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent SOW and shall be governed thereby.

2.5 **Acceptance of Extended Deliverables.**  

2.5.1 The acceptance process for Extended Deliverables shall follow **Section 2.5 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Consulting Agreement, except as modified below.

2.5.2 For Major Extended Deliverables (including the Enterprise Rollout Blueprint, CoE Operating Model and Governance Playbook, Enhanced KPI & Analytics Dashboard Suite, and Post-Rollout Stabilization and Optimization Report), Contoso shall have **fifteen (15) Business Days** following delivery to review and either accept or provide written notice of material deficiencies, consistent with **Section 2.5.1(a)** of the Parent SOW.

2.5.3 For Minor Extended Deliverables (including interim status reports, updated test scripts, configuration documents, and minor automation assets), the **ten (10) Business Day** review period in **Section 2.5.1(b)** of the Parent SOW (which aligns with **Section 2.8.1** of the Consulting Agreement) shall apply.

2.5.4 If Contoso fails to respond within the applicable review period, the Extended Deliverable shall be deemed accepted, consistent with **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.8.3** of the Consulting Agreement.

2.5.5 Epsilon shall correct any material deficiencies identified by Contoso and resubmit the applicable Extended Deliverable for review within a mutually agreed timeframe, consistent with **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.8.2** of the Consulting Agreement.

2.6 **Company Responsibilities (Addendum-Specific).**  

In addition to the obligations set forth in **Section 2.6 (Company Responsibilities (SOW-Specific))** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6** of the Consulting Agreement, Contoso shall:

2.6.1 Designate a global program sponsor and regional leads to support Phase III – Enterprise Rollout & Automation and provide timely decisions concerning prioritization, scope, and resource allocation.

2.6.2 Provide Epsilon with access to additional systems, data, environments, and third-party vendors or partners as necessary for enterprise rollout, including any new ERP/CRM instances, integration endpoints, and automation platforms.

2.6.3 Facilitate participation of stakeholders from all impacted regions and business units in workshops, design sessions, user acceptance testing, and training programs, and ensure local change agents are available to support adoption.

2.6.4 Ensure that any required third-party licenses (for RPA tools, integration platforms, or analytics tools) are procured and remain in good standing, consistent with **Section 2.6.5** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6.5** of the Consulting Agreement.

2.6.5 Provide timely feedback on Extended Deliverables, including consolidated comments and decisions, within the review periods established in **Section 2.5** of this Addendum.

2.7 **Key Personnel.**  

2.7.1 Epsilon shall maintain a team of appropriately skilled personnel to perform the Extended Services, consistent with **Section 2.7 (Key Personnel)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.5.2 (Performance Standards)** of the Consulting Agreement.

2.7.2 Continuity of key roles (such as Program Manager, Lead Architect, and Change Management Lead) shall be maintained, and any replacement shall be subject to advance notice and qualification criteria substantially similar to those in **Section 2.7.2** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.5.4** of the Consulting Agreement.

Service Levels specific to the Extended Services under this Addendum are set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** below and shall supplement and, where expressly stated, supersede the SLA terms in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW for the Extended Services only.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total estimated contract value for the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables under this Addendum is **One Million Three Hundred Seventeen Thousand Seven Hundred Forty-Five U.S. Dollars (US$1,317,745)** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”). This amount is in addition to, and does not replace, the **SOW Contract Value of US$1,379,515** set forth in **Section 1.1.9** and **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent SOW.

3.2 **Fee Structure Under this Addendum.**  

3.2.1 **Time-and-Materials Fees.**  
Certain Extended Services, such as ad-hoc advisory work, additional optimization sprints, and unplanned support beyond the baseline scope, shall be performed on a time-and-materials (“T&M”) basis at the following hourly rates (which are consistent with, or an update to, the rates in **Section 3.2.1** of the Parent SOW):

  - Senior Partner / Program Executive: US$335/hour  
  - Principal Consultant: US$270/hour  
  - Senior Consultant: US$220/hour  
  - Consultant / Business Analyst: US$185/hour  
  - Project Manager: US$205/hour  
  - Technical Architect: US$250/hour  

These rates may be adjusted annually upon mutual written agreement, consistent with **Section 3.2.1** of the Parent SOW.

3.2.2 **Fixed and Milestone-Based Fees.**  
The majority of the Addendum Contract Value shall be structured as fixed or milestone-based fees tied to the completion and acceptance of specific Extended Deliverables and Milestones, consistent with **Section 3.2.2** of the Parent SOW. The Parties shall allocate the Addendum Contract Value approximately as follows:

  - Phase III-A (Detailed Planning & Design): US$225,000  
  - Phase III-B (Build & Configure): US$525,000  
  - Phase III-C (Enterprise Rollout): US$380,000  
  - Phase III-D (Optimization & CoE Establishment): US$187,745  

3.2.3 The specific payment amounts, Milestones, and acceptance events shall be further detailed in **Exhibit B (Addendum Fee and Milestone Payment Schedule)**, which shall be incorporated into and deemed part of this Addendum and, for purposes of interpretation, the Parent SOW.

3.2.4 **Expenses.**  
Contoso shall reimburse Epsilon for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Extended Services (including travel, lodging, meals, and related expenses) in accordance with **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.5 (Expenses and Reimbursement)** of the Consulting Agreement. Epsilon shall comply with Contoso’s expense policies as provided in advance.

3.3 **Payment Terms – Addendum-Specific (Superseding Parent SOW and Consulting Agreement for Extended Services).**  

3.3.1 **Net 45 Terms.**  
Notwithstanding the **Net 60** terms set forth in **Section 3.3.1** of the Parent SOW and the **Net 30** terms in **Section 3.3.2** of the Consulting Agreement, the Parties agree that, for all invoices issued under this Addendum and relating to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables, payment of all undisputed amounts shall be due **Net 45** days from the date of Epsilon’s invoice. This provision is **expressly intended to supersede** (a) **Section 3.3.1** of the Parent SOW and (b) **Section 3.3.2** of the Consulting Agreement, **solely** with respect to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables under this Addendum, consistent with the conflict rule in **Section 2.1.2** of this Addendum and **Section 2.1.2 / 16.2** of the Parent SOW.

3.3.2 **Invoicing.**  
(a) T&M-based Extended Services and reimbursable expenses shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, accompanied by reasonable supporting detail (e.g., timesheets, expense reports), consistent with **Section 3.3.2(a)** of the Parent SOW.  
(b) Milestone-based Fees shall be invoiced upon completion and acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the applicable Extended Deliverable or Milestone in accordance with **Section 2.5** of this Addendum and the Milestone schedule in **Exhibit B**, consistent with **Section 3.3.2(b)** of the Parent SOW.

3.3.3 **Payment Method.**  
All payments under this Addendum shall be made in U.S. Dollars by wire transfer or another electronic payment method mutually agreed upon by the Parties, consistent with **Section 3.3.3** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.3.3** of the Consulting Agreement.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

3.4.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Addendum shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.4.1** of the Parent SOW (the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law), from the due date until paid in full.

3.4.2 If any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than sixty (60) days after the invoice date, Epsilon may, after providing written notice and a ten (10) Business Day cure period, suspend performance of the Extended Services and withhold Extended Deliverables related to such overdue invoices, consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.4.2** of the Consulting Agreement.

3.5 **Taxes.**  

Taxes with respect to the Fees and expenses under this Addendum shall be handled as described in **Section 3.5** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Consulting Agreement. All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of applicable Taxes, which shall be Contoso’s responsibility except as otherwise provided by Applicable Law.

3.6 **No Setoff.**  

Except for amounts disputed in good faith, Contoso shall not set off, withhold, or reduce payments due under this Addendum based on any claim, counterclaim, or dispute, consistent with **Section 3.6** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.7 (No Setoff)** of the Consulting Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 The term of this Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (December 15, 2025) and shall continue in full force and effect until the **Addendum Expiration Date** (February 24, 2028), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Addendum, the Parent SOW, and/or the Consulting Agreement.

This Addendum is intended to fall **within** that SOW Term and, in the event the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement is not renewed or extended beyond February 20, 2028 (per **Section 4.1** of the Consulting Agreement), the Parties agree to cooperate in good faith to ensure completion or orderly wind-down of the Extended Services during the Addendum Term.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

This Addendum may be extended by mutual written agreement of the Parties, which may be documented as an amendment hereto or a new addendum, subject to the renewal or extension of the Parent SOW and the Consulting Agreement, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.2** of the Consulting Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason upon at least **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Epsilon. This right is in addition to, and shall not limit, Contoso’s termination rights under **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.3.1** of the Consulting Agreement.

4.3.2 **By Epsilon.** Epsilon may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, consistent with **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.3.2** of the Consulting Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon the other Party’s material breach of this Addendum, the Parent SOW (to the extent applicable to the Extended Services), or the Consulting Agreement, subject to the cure rights and conditions set forth in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.4** of the Consulting Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) Epsilon shall cease performing the Extended Services, except as reasonably requested by Contoso to wind down or transition the Extended Services in an orderly manner, consistent with **Section 4.5.1(a)** of the Parent SOW;  

  (b) Contoso shall pay Epsilon for all Extended Services performed and expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments made on Contoso’s behalf with Contoso’s prior written approval, consistent with **Section 4.5.1(b)** of the Parent SOW;  

  (c) The Parties shall return or destroy Confidential Information in connection with the Extended Services, in accordance with **Section 5.4** of this Addendum, **Section 5.4** of the Parent SOW, and **Section 5.5** of the Consulting Agreement; and
