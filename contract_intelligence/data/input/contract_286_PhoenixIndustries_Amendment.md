## PURCHASE AGREEMENT AMENDMENT – QUANTITIES AND PRICING

This AMENDMENT (this “**Amendment**” or this “**Amendment No. 1**”), Contract Reference Number **AMD-PHO-202404-286**, is entered into as of **August 18, 2024** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**”), by and between:

**Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a [Jurisdiction of Incorporation] corporation, with its principal place of business at **1200 Market Plaza Drive, Suite 900, Dallas, Texas 75201, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**”, the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”),  

and  

**Phoenix Industries, LLC**, a Texas limited liability company, with its principal place of business at **4550 Phoenix Ridge Parkway, Houston, Texas 77002, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Phoenix**”, the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Phoenix are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Amendment is entered into **pursuant to and in amendment of** that certain **Purchase Agreement**, Reference No. **PUR-PHO-202404-256**, dated **April 22, 2024**, between the Parties, internally identified by Contoso as **“contract_256”** (the “Parent Agreement” as defined below).

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Purchase Agreement** Reference No. **PUR-PHO-202404-256**, dated April 22, 2024, identified by Contoso as **contract_256** (the “Parent Agreement”), pursuant to which Phoenix agreed to manufacture, supply, and deliver certain **Products** and provide related **Services** to Contoso, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** and associated **Purchase Orders** and **Statements of Work** under the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement establishes the pricing framework and payment terms for Products and Services, including the estimated **Contract Value** of **US$4,759,281** (Section 3.1), **Product Pricing** (Section 3.2.1), **Service Fees** (Section 3.2.2), and **Net 30** payment terms (Section 3.3.2);

WHEREAS, the Parties desire, in accordance with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement (which requires that modifications be in writing and signed by duly authorized representatives of both Parties), to **modify quantities and pricing** for certain Products and Services contemplated under the Parent Agreement and related POs/SOWs, while maintaining the overall contractual framework established by the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties further desire to document a separate, additional **contract value** for this Amendment of **One Million Six Hundred Thirty-Three Thousand Two Hundred Four U.S. Dollars (US$1,633,204)** (the “Amendment Contract Value”) for the quantities and pricing adjustments described herein;

WHEREAS, this Amendment is intended to constitute a **Change Order** and/or contract-level pricing adjustment consistent with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement, including Sections 13.1–13.3 relating to “Change Requests,” “Evaluation,” and “Change Orders”;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement was internally classified by Contoso as **“High Risk”** and included, among other things: (a) a **no contractual cap on liability** (Article IX); (b) broad indemnification obligations on Contoso (Section 8.2); and (c) a **termination for convenience** notice period of **one hundred eighty (180) days** (Section 4.3), and the Parties now wish, solely for the purposes of this Amendment and the specific Products and Services described herein, to adopt a **Medium risk profile** with a mutual liability cap and more balanced terms, while preserving the general structure and intent of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties expressly intend that this Amendment **modifies quantities or pricing** under the Parent Agreement and relevant POs/SOWs, and shall be read in conjunction with, and as a supplement to, the Parent Agreement, such that the Parent Agreement remains in full force and effect except as specifically modified herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, representations, warranties, and agreements contained herein, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is executed under, and subject to, the Parent Agreement **PUR-PHO-202404-256 (contract_256)**. Except as expressly modified herein, all terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)**, shall apply to the Adjusted Products and Adjusted Services under this Amendment as though fully set forth herein.  

2.1.2 The Parties acknowledge and agree that this Amendment **modifies quantities or pricing** with respect to certain Products and related Services originally contemplated under the Parent Agreement and any related POs/SOWs, and shall function as a **Change Order** pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Adjusted Products.**  

2.2.1 **Product Categories.** Phoenix shall continue to manufacture, supply, and deliver to Contoso the **high-performance industrial components and assemblies** described in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement, including, without limitation:  

  (a) precision-machined parts used in Contoso’s technology-enabled industrial equipment;  
  (b) control modules and sub-assemblies for advanced automation systems;  
  (c) sensor arrays and related instrumentation; and  
  (d) standard mounting hardware and accessories necessary for system integration.  

2.2.2 **Quantity Adjustments.** Subject to the Parent Agreement and this Amendment:  

  (a) Contoso agrees to purchase, and Phoenix agrees to supply, **increased volumes** of certain precision-machined parts and sensor arrays to support Contoso’s expanded production lines in Dallas, Texas and its satellite facilities within the Territory.  

  (b) The **baseline forecast** and **minimum firm quantities** (if any) for these Adjusted Products shall be specified in POs and/or an SOW referencing this Amendment. For planning purposes only, the Parties estimate that the additional incremental volumes over the Parent Agreement baseline will correspond to an aggregate Amendment Contract Value of **US$1,633,204**.  

  (c) Where any previously issued PO or SOW under the Parent Agreement references quantities that are superseded by this Amendment, those documents shall be deemed **modified**, solely with respect to quantities and line-item pricing, as reflected in the updated POs or SOWs referencing this Amendment.

2.2.3 **Specifications.**  

  (a) Phoenix shall continue to manufacture the Adjusted Products in accordance with the Product specifications, drawings, and technical documentation provided by Contoso and accepted by Phoenix pursuant to **Section 2.2.2 (Specifications)** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (b) To the extent that quantity changes under this Amendment necessitate any modifications to specifications (for instance, alternative materials to support larger production runs or updated tolerances to meet new regulatory requirements), such changes shall be managed via the **Change Request** and **Change Order** process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

2.2.4 **Packaging and Labeling.** Packaging and labeling of Adjusted Products shall comply with **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement and any additional requirements specified in POs/SOWs referencing this Amendment (e.g., lot traceability, serialized barcodes, or updated branding for Contoso’s new product lines).

2.3 **Adjusted Services.**  

2.3.1 **Implementation and Integration Services.**  

  (a) To support deployment of the Adjusted Products, Phoenix shall provide **implementation, installation, and integration Services** as described in **Section 2.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, at the expanded volume and geographic scope required by Contoso’s updated manufacturing plans.  

  (b) The Parties anticipate that this may include: additional site surveys; integration design for two (2) additional production lines; configuration and testing of new sensor array layouts; commissioning support; and knowledge transfer to Contoso’s internal engineering teams.  

2.3.2 **Support and Maintenance Services.**  

  (a) For the Adjusted Products, Phoenix shall provide ongoing support and maintenance consistent with **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement and the **Service Levels** set forth in **Article XII** of both the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.  

  (b) In light of increased volumes and expanded criticality of the Adjusted Products, the Parties may define **tiered support bands** and appropriate service credits specifically for the incremental scope under this Amendment, all as detailed in **Article XII** herein and/or the applicable SOW.

2.3.3 **Training and Documentation.**  

In connection with the Adjusted Products, Phoenix shall make available additional training sessions, including remote and on-site training at Contoso’s Dallas facility, as reasonably requested by Contoso and subject to applicable Fees. Phoenix shall also update user manuals, technical documentation, and maintenance guides to reflect any specification changes or new configurations associated with the Adjusted Products.

2.4 **Deliverables.**  

2.4.1 Phoenix shall provide all **Deliverables** (as defined in Section 1.1.6 of the Parent Agreement) that are reasonably necessary to support the Adjusted Products and Adjusted Services. These may include, without limitation:  
  (a) updated engineering drawings and configuration files;  
  (b) revised test reports evidencing conformance with specifications and regulatory requirements;  
  (c) updated bill-of-materials (BOMs) for new or adjusted assemblies; and  
  (d) training materials tailored for the incremental scope.  

2.4.2 Unless otherwise specified in a PO or SOW referencing this Amendment, Deliverables shall be provided in the same formats and via the same secure transmission methods described in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

2.5.1 Phoenix shall perform all Services under this Amendment in a professional and workmanlike manner and in accordance with **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, including compliance with applicable laws and use of appropriately skilled personnel.

2.5.2 Due to the increased quantities and extended Amendment Term, Phoenix shall use commercially reasonable efforts to enhance its production capacity, inventory management, and logistics planning to meet Contoso’s updated delivery timelines, as further specified in POs/SOWs.

2.6 **Project Planning and Timelines.**  

2.6.1 For the larger volume rollouts contemplated by this Amendment, the Parties may agree on a written **project plan** under an SOW referencing this Amendment, in accordance with **Section 2.6.1** of the Parent Agreement. The project plan may define milestone-based deliveries, ramp-up phases, and associated Acceptance testing.

2.6.2 Phoenix shall promptly notify Contoso of any anticipated delay in fulfilling the increased quantities, consistent with **Section 2.6.2 (Delay and Notice)** of the Parent Agreement, and shall propose a mitigation plan and revised schedule.

2.7 **Acceptance.**  

Acceptance of Products, Services, and Deliverables under this Amendment shall follow the same procedures as set forth in **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise expressly specified in an SOW referencing this Amendment. The Parties may, for high-volume shipments, agree to **sampling-based acceptance procedures** or **rolling acceptance** by production batch.

2.8 **Non-Exclusivity.**  

This Amendment does not alter the **non-exclusivity** provisions of **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement; both Parties remain free to engage with third parties, subject to their respective contractual obligations.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value and Pricing Framework.**  

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **estimated aggregate value** of the additional or adjusted purchases under this Amendment is **US$1,633,204** (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**). This is an estimate and not a guaranteed minimum commitment, except to the extent expressly stated in any SOW or PO referencing this Amendment.

3.1.2 The Amendment Contract Value is **in addition to** the **Contract Value** of **US$4,759,281** stated in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise expressly provided, the Amendment Contract Value shall be aggregated with the Parent Agreement Contract Value for informational and planning purposes, but the Parties acknowledge that neither amount creates a minimum purchase obligation except where explicitly set forth in an SOW.

3.2 **Product Pricing (Adjusted Products).**  

3.2.1 The unit prices for Adjusted Products shall be set forth in a **revised fee schedule** appended to this Amendment (e.g., **Exhibit A – Adjusted Pricing Schedule**) or in POs referencing this Amendment. Such pricing shall supersede, solely with respect to the Adjusted Products, any conflicting Product pricing previously agreed under the Parent Agreement or predecessor POs/SOWs.

3.2.2 Phoenix shall apply **volume-based discounts** for certain Product families where Contoso commits to minimum annual purchase quantities under this Amendment, as documented in the relevant SOW or PO. Any such discounts shall be clearly set forth as separate line items.

3.2.3 Unless otherwise specified, all prices remain **exclusive of Taxes**, consistent with **Section 3.2.1** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall remain responsible for all applicable Taxes (other than taxes based on Phoenix’s net income).

3.3 **Service Fees (Adjusted Services).**  

3.3.1 Fees for the Adjusted Services (implementation, integration, extended support, and training) shall be specified in the applicable SOW referencing this Amendment and may be structured as:  
  (a) fixed-fee engagements for defined integration projects;  
  (b) time-and-materials-based rates for ongoing engineering support; and/or  
  (c) retainer-based pricing for premium support tiers.

3.3.2 Where payment milestones are agreed, Phoenix shall invoice upon completion of each milestone, consistent with **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement, and Contoso shall pay such milestone invoices in accordance with **Section 3.3.2 (Net 30)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 3.4** herein.

3.4 **Payment Terms – Net 30.**  

3.4.1 In accordance with **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent Agreement, all undisputed invoices issued under this Amendment shall be payable by Contoso within **thirty (30) days** after the invoice date (“**Net 30**”), by wire transfer or another mutually agreed method.

3.4.2 Phoenix shall issue invoices for:  
  (a) Products upon shipment or as otherwise specified in the applicable SOW; and  
  (b) Services in accordance with billing schedules or milestones specified in the SOW, or monthly in arrears for time-and-materials work.

3.4.3 Contoso shall have the right to dispute any portion of an invoice in good faith pursuant to **Section 3.3.3 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement. The dispute resolution process for invoicing issues shall be consistent with that Section.

3.5 **Late Payment and Remedies.**  

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Amendment shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.5.1 (Interest)** of the Parent Agreement, i.e., the lesser of (a) 1.5% per month or (b) the maximum rate allowed by applicable law.

3.5.2 Phoenix may suspend performance of obligations relating solely to this Amendment (e.g., future deliveries of Adjusted Products and performance of Adjusted Services) for non-payment in accordance with **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent Agreement, following thirty (30) days’ prior written notice and failure by Contoso to cure such non-payment in that period.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

Contoso shall reimburse Phoenix for reasonable, pre-approved expenses incurred in connection with the Adjusted Services in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement and Contoso’s applicable expense reimbursement policies.

3.7 **Set-Off.**  

Phoenix’s rights of set-off under **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to all amounts payable under this Amendment, subject to applicable law.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**  

This Amendment shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date (August 18, 2024)** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**, shall continue in effect until **June 16, 2029** (the “**Amendment Expiration Date**”), which extends beyond the Parent Term.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Term.**  

4.2.1 Notwithstanding the Parent Term in **Section 4.1** of the Parent Agreement (ending November 2, 2025), the Parties expressly agree that the **commercial commitments, pricing, and quantities** specified in this Amendment may extend beyond the Parent Term and continue in full force and effect through the Amendment Expiration Date, provided that:

  (a) the Parties shall either (i) renew or extend the Parent Agreement under **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof or (ii) execute a successor or replacement master agreement that incorporates this Amendment by reference or reaffirms its terms; and  

  (b) in the absence of a renewed or successor master agreement, the Parties agree that, solely for the purposes of the Adjusted Products and Adjusted Services, the relevant provisions of the Parent Agreement (including Articles I, II, III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX as modified herein, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall be deemed **automatically extended** through the Amendment Expiration Date, unless earlier terminated under this Article IV.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 For this Amendment only, the termination-for-convenience notice period in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement is modified to a more balanced and medium-risk allocation:

  (a) Either Party may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party.  

  (b) Termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement unless explicitly stated in the notice and permitted under the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause in the event of the other Party’s material breach of this Amendment and failure to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail, consistent with **Section 4.4.1** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 Termination rights for insolvency or bankruptcy events shall be as provided in **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent Agreement and shall apply equally to this Amendment.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason:  

  (a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate concurrently, unless the Parties explicitly agree in writing that certain provisions (e.g., pricing, volumes, IP, confidentiality, and liability allocation) shall survive and be incorporated into a successive agreement.  

  (b) For avoidance of doubt, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement shall not relieve either Party of any accrued payment obligations under this Amendment or obligations that survive under **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.7** of this Amendment.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.**  

4.6.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment, Contoso shall pay Phoenix for:  
  (a) all Adjusted Products delivered and accepted prior to the effective date of termination;  
  (b) all Adjusted Services performed and accepted prior to the effective date of termination; and  
  (c) any non-cancellable commitments reasonably incurred by Phoenix in reliance on firm orders or POs from Contoso, provided that Phoenix uses reasonable efforts to mitigate such costs.

4.6.2 Termination of this Amendment shall not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in full force and effect in accordance with its terms, except to the extent expressly stated in a written termination notice.

4.7 **Survival.**  

Provisions of this Amendment which, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration (including but not limited to payment obligations, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIV, XV, and XVI**) shall survive in accordance with their terms, together with any corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IX  
LIMITATION OF LIABILITY (MODIFIED FOR THIS AMENDMENT)

9.1 **Amendment-Specific Liability Cap.**  

Notwithstanding **Section 9.1 (NO CAP)** and **Section 9.2** of the Parent Agreement, and in recognition of the Parties’ agreement that this Amendment is categorized as **Medium Risk**, the Parties agree that, **solely with respect to claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment, the Adjusted Products, or the Adjusted Services**, the total aggregate liability of each Party to the other (including its Affiliates) for all claims, damages, and losses of any kind, whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, shall be capped at an amount equal to **two (2) times the Fees paid or payable by Contoso to Phoenix under this Amendment during the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**.

9.2 **Exclusion of Certain Damages.**  

To the maximum extent permitted by law, and solely with respect to this Amendment, **neither Party** shall be liable to the other for any **indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, or punitive damages**, or for any **consequential damages such as loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of business, or loss of data**, arising out of or in connection with this Amendment, even if such Party has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

9.3 **Exceptions to the Cap and Exclusions.**  

The limitations in **Sections 9.1 and 9.2** of this Amendment shall **not** apply to:
