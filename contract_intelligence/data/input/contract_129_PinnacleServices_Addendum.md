## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to DPA.**  
This Addendum **supplements and expands** the Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the DPA. Except as expressly modified herein, the obligations and standards governing Pinnacle’s performance of the Services under the DPA, including **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** and **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)**, shall apply equally to the Additional Services and Additional Processing Activities under this Addendum. In particular, Pinnacle shall perform the Additional Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, in compliance with Data Protection Laws, and consistent with any applicable SLAs, as further set out in **Article XII** of this Addendum and **Article XII** of the DPA.

2.2 **Description of Additional Processing Activities.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.1** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall perform the following Additional Processing Activities on behalf of Contoso, under Contoso’s Instructions as defined in **Section 1.10** and **Section 11.4** of the DPA:

2.2.1 **Real-Time Event Data Ingestion.** Pinnacle shall design, provision, and operate secure, scalable pipelines for the collection and ingestion of Event Data from Contoso’s designated applications, APIs, SDKs, and system logs. This Processing includes:

  (a) Receiving Event Data from Contoso’s systems via mutually agreed protocols (e.g., HTTPS, secure message queues, or streaming platforms);

  (b) Performing initial validation, parsing, deduplication, and structuring of Event Data;

  (c) Temporarily buffering Event Data in encrypted queues or caches to support near real-time Processing.

2.2.2 **Advanced Analytics and Segmentation.** Pinnacle shall Process Personal Data and other Client Data to generate Enhanced Analytics Deliverables, including:

  (a) Cohort and segmentation analysis based on user attributes and behaviors, in compliance with Contoso’s Instructions and Data Protection Laws;

  (b) Trend and anomaly detection in user activity and system performance metrics;

  (c) Aggregated and, where instructed, pseudonymized or anonymized reporting for internal business insights, consistent with **Section 2.2.3** of the DPA (transformation, aggregation, pseudonymization, or anonymization).

2.2.3 **Data Enrichment Services.** Pinnacle shall enrich Personal Data and other Client Data by:

  (a) Classifying records into standardized taxonomies and categories defined by Contoso;

  (b) Attaching metadata tags reflecting business rules, risk levels, or operational status;

  (c) Where expressly authorized in writing by Contoso and permitted by Data Protection Laws, cross-referencing Client Data with Contoso-approved external reference data sets, provided that such Processing does not constitute a “sale” or “sharing” of Personal Data within the meaning of the CCPA and remains compliant with **Section 11.2 (Processor / Service Provider Commitments)** of the DPA.

2.2.4 **Multi-Region Hosting and Replication.** In furtherance of **Section 11.10 (International Data Transfers)** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall implement Multi-Region Hosting for certain databases and data stores containing Personal Data, subject to Contoso’s prior written approval of the specific regions. Such Processing includes:

  (a) Replicating Personal Data across multiple data centers within the approved Territory to improve resilience and performance;

  (b) Implementing failover and disaster recovery mechanisms consistent with **Section 2.2.7** of the DPA (backups, archiving, and disaster recovery) and **Section 11.3.3** of the DPA (measures to restore availability);

  (c) Ensuring that any cross-border transfers remain subject to appropriate data transfer mechanisms and authorizations under **Section 11.10** of the DPA.

2.2.5 **User Telemetry and Behavioral Insights.** Pinnacle shall Process user telemetry data, which may include Personal Data such as unique identifiers and interaction logs, to support Contoso’s internal analysis of product usage, feature adoption, and system health. Such Processing shall:

  (a) Be limited to Contoso’s documented Instructions;

  (b) Not involve selling, sharing, or otherwise monetizing Personal Data for Pinnacle’s own purposes, in accordance with **Section 11.2.2** of the DPA;

  (c) Be subject to Contoso’s configuration choices for data minimization and retention, as further described in **Section 11.11** of the DPA and **Section 11.3** of this Addendum.

2.2.6 **Support for Data Subject Rights.** Taking into account the enhanced Processing, Pinnacle shall implement additional technical and organizational measures to facilitate Contoso’s ability to respond to Data Subject requests under **Section 11.8** of the DPA. This may include implementing tooling to locate, export, rectify, or delete Event Data and enriched Personal Data at Contoso’s direction.

2.3 **Additional Deliverables.**  
In addition to the Deliverables described in **Section 2.3** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall provide the following Deliverables under this Addendum:

2.3.1 **Updated Technical Documentation.** Detailed documentation describing:

  (a) Event Data ingestion architecture and data flow diagrams;

  (b) Multi-Region Hosting topology and failover procedures;

  (c) Data Enrichment models, classification schemes, and metadata taxonomies;

  (d) Security Documentation specific to the Additional Processing Activities, including encryption standards, key management approaches, and access control models.

2.3.2 **Enhanced Analytics Deliverables.** Periodic and ad hoc reports and dashboards, including but not limited to:

  (a) Monthly cohort retention reports, user journey analytics, and feature utilization reports;

  (b) Operational performance dashboards displaying system throughput, latency, and error rates across regions;

  (c) Data quality and completeness reports identifying missing, inconsistent, or anomalous data, to enable Contoso to take corrective actions.

2.3.3 **Operational Runbooks.** Procedures for incident response, escalation, and recovery specific to the Additional Services, aligned with the response and resolution times in **Section 12.2** of the DPA and **Section 12.2** of this Addendum.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  
Consistent with **Section 2.4** of the DPA, the Parties agree to the following indicative timelines for the Additional Services:

2.4.1 **Design and Planning Phase.** Commencing within fifteen (15) Business Days of the Addendum Effective Date, Pinnacle shall collaborate with Contoso to finalize requirements, data schemas, region selection, and SLAs for the Additional Services. This phase is expected to last approximately thirty (30) to forty-five (45) calendar days.

2.4.2 **Implementation and Pilot Phase.** Following completion of the design phase, Pinnacle shall configure the Event Data pipelines, analytics environments, and multi-region infrastructure. A pilot or limited production roll-out is targeted within sixty (60) to ninety (90) calendar days after the Addendum Effective Date, subject to timely receipt of necessary inputs and access from Contoso in accordance with **Section 2.6** of the DPA.

2.4.3 **Production Go-Live.** The Parties shall mutually agree in writing on a specific production go-live date for each major component of the Additional Services. Pinnacle shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet such dates, consistent with the performance standards in **Section 2.5** of the DPA.

2.4.4 **Ongoing Operations and Optimization.** After go-live, Pinnacle shall provide ongoing operations, monitoring, and optimization of the Additional Services throughout the Addendum Term, in alignment with the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the DPA and this Addendum.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.5** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall:

2.5.1 Ensure that the Additional Services meet or exceed the **Service Availability** and **Response and Resolution Times** set forth in **Article XII** of the DPA and **Article XII** of this Addendum;

2.5.2 Maintain and periodically update Security Documentation and controls consistent with **Section 11.3** of the DPA;

2.5.3 Use personnel who are trained on the specific architectures and technologies used for the Additional Services and who are bound by confidentiality obligations consistent with **Article V** of the DPA and this Addendum.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities for Additional Services.**  
In addition to its responsibilities in **Section 2.6** of the DPA, Contoso shall:

2.6.1 Provide Pinnacle with accurate and updated configuration information, region preferences, and data schemas necessary to implement the Additional Processing Activities;

2.6.2 Ensure that any new Instructions provided under this Addendum comply with Data Protection Laws, as required by **Section 11.4** of the DPA;

2.6.3 Notify Pinnacle promptly of any Data Subject requests that may impact the Additional Processing Activities, enabling Pinnacle to assist in accordance with **Section 11.8** of the DPA;

2.6.4 Designate appropriate technical and business contacts to participate in design reviews, acceptance testing, and governance meetings relating to the Additional Services.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **Addendum Contract Value** for the Additional Services and Additional Processing Activities under this Addendum is **US $633,788**, which is in addition to, and does not modify, the aggregate contract value of **US $2,240,501** referenced in **Section 3.1** and the recitals of the DPA. The combined total contract value for the DPA and this Addendum, if fully performed, shall therefore be **US $2,874,289**, subject to adjustments pursuant to this Article III, **Section 3.5** of the DPA (Expenses and Reimbursement), and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the DPA.

3.2 **Fee Structure for Additional Services.**  
Unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing by the Parties:

3.2.1 **Implementation Fees.** A one-time implementation fee equal to up to ten percent (10%) of the Addendum Contract Value (i.e., not to exceed **US $63,378.80**) shall be invoiced upon commencement of the implementation and pilot phase described in **Section 2.4.2** of this Addendum. This is consistent with the implementation fee structure in **Section 3.2.1** of the DPA.

3.2.2 **Recurring Service Fees.** Recurring monthly fees for hosting, Processing, analytics, and managed services under this Addendum shall be invoiced monthly in advance, based on:

  (a) The number of active Event Data sources and user accounts configured for access to Enhanced Analytics Deliverables;

  (b) The volume of data processed and stored under the Additional Services (e.g., GB of Event Data per month);

  (c) Any additional service tiers or modules (e.g., premium analytics features, expanded data retention) defined in a mutually agreed fee schedule attached to this Addendum.

3.2.3 **Usage-Based Fees.** To the extent the Parties agree, Pinnacle may charge usage-based fees for specific Event Data or analytics Processing, such as per million events Processed or per API call, consistent with **Section 3.2.3** of the DPA. Such fees shall be calculated monthly and invoiced in arrears.

3.3 **Invoicing; Payment Terms.**  

3.3.1 Pinnacle shall issue itemized invoices to Contoso for the Additional Services in accordance with this Article III and the invoicing practices in **Section 3.3** of the DPA.

3.3.2 Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 30**”), consistent with **Section 3.3.2** of the DPA. Payment shall be made in U.S. dollars via the methods agreed in the DPA.

3.4 **Late Payment; Suspension.**  
Any late payment with respect to amounts due under this Addendum shall be subject to the interest and suspension provisions set forth in **Section 3.4** of the DPA. Before suspending any portion of the Additional Services, Pinnacle shall provide Contoso with written notice and a cure period consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the DPA, ensuring that any suspension is proportionate and does not unreasonably interfere with Contoso’s compliance with Data Protection Laws.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
Reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Pinnacle in connection with the Additional Services (e.g., travel to Contoso’s sites for deployment workshops) shall be reimbursable in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the DPA.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Contoso may withhold payment of disputed amounts under this Addendum in the same manner and subject to the same conditions set forth in **Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts)** of the DPA.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
Taxes applicable to the Additional Services shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the DPA.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** and continue in full force and effect until the **Addendum Expiration Date of July 14, 2027**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or **Article IV** of the DPA.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Subject to the continued effectiveness of the DPA, this Addendum may be renewed for additional periods of one (1) year each upon the Parties’ mutual written agreement, consistent with the renewal framework set forth in **Section 4.2** of the DPA. Any renewal shall expressly reference this Addendum and may adjust pricing, scope, and other terms as mutually agreed.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing Pinnacle with at least **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice. This is without prejudice to Contoso’s broader termination rights under **Section 4.3** of the DPA.

4.3.2 **By Pinnacle.** Pinnacle may terminate this Addendum for convenience only by providing at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice and only after the first twelve (12) months of the Addendum Term, consistent with the principles set forth in **Section 4.3** of the DPA and recognizing the Client-friendly posture agreed by the Parties for this low-risk engagement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4** of the DPA, including:

  (a) Material breach of this Addendum or the DPA, not cured within the applicable cure period;

  (b) Insolvency or bankruptcy events; or  

  (c) Material violations of Data Protection Laws or material breach of **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** or **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the DPA as incorporated herein, consistent with **Section 4.4.3** of the DPA.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 This Addendum is subordinate to and dependent upon the DPA. Termination or expiration of the DPA, for any reason, shall automatically result in contemporaneous termination of this Addendum, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain obligations (e.g., data return or deletion) beyond such termination.

4.5.2 Termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the DPA, which shall continue to govern other Services and Processing activities unless otherwise terminated.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

4.6.1 Pinnacle shall cease performing the Additional Services and Additional Processing Activities, except to the limited extent necessary to complete any agreed transition or as required by Applicable Law;

4.6.2 Pinnacle shall, at Contoso’s option and in accordance with **Section 4.5.2** and **Section 11.11** of the DPA, either (a) securely return to Contoso or (b) securely delete or irreversibly anonymize all Personal Data Processed exclusively under this Addendum, subject to any legal retention obligations;

4.6.3 The Parties’ respective payment obligations for Services rendered prior to the effective date of termination shall remain due and payable, subject to any good-faith disputes pursuant to **Section 3.6** of the DPA;

4.6.4 The provisions of this Addendum which by their nature are intended to survive, including **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, and XVI**, shall so survive, consistent with **Section 16.9** of the DPA.

## ARTICLE XI  
### DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

11.1 **Roles of the Parties.**  
The Parties reaffirm that Contoso is the Controller (or Business) and Pinnacle is the Processor (or Service Provider) for all Personal Data Processed under this Addendum, in accordance with **Section 11.1** of the DPA.

11.2 **Processor / Service Provider Commitments.**  
Pinnacle shall Process Personal Data under this Addendum solely on behalf of Contoso and in accordance with Contoso’s documented Instructions, as required by **Section 11.2** and **Section 11.4** of the DPA, and shall not “sell” or “share” Personal Data or use it for any purpose other than performing the Additional Services, consistent with **Section 11.2.2** of the DPA.

11.3 **Security Measures.**  
Pinnacle shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures specific to the Additional Processing Activities, consistent with **Section 11.3** of the DPA, including:

  (a) Encryption of Event Data in transit and at rest;  

  (b) Segregated access controls for analytics and enrichment environments;  

  (c) Periodic security testing of Event Data ingestion pipelines and multi-region replication mechanisms;  

  (d) Maintenance of logs sufficient to support incident investigation and audits under **Section 11.12** of the DPA.

11.4 **Instructions and Data Minimization.**  
Contoso shall provide documented Instructions for the Additional Processing Activities, and Pinnacle shall promptly notify Contoso if it believes any Instruction violates Data Protection Laws, consistent with **Section 11.4** of the DPA. The Parties shall cooperate to ensure that only the minimum Personal Data necessary for the Additional Services is Processed.

11.5 **Data Breach Notification.**  
Any Personal Data Breach affecting Personal Data Processed under this Addendum shall be handled in accordance with **Section 11.6** of the DPA. Pinnacle shall ensure that its incident response procedures address the unique characteristics of Event Data, analytics data, and multi-region environments.

11.6 **Sub-Processors.**  
Pinnacle may engage Sub-processors for the Additional Processing Activities only in accordance with **Section 11.7** of the DPA. Upon request, Pinnacle shall identify any Sub-processors specifically involved in Event Data ingestion, analytics Processing, or multi-region hosting.

11.7 **Data Subject Requests and DPIAs.**  
Pinnacle shall, upon Contoso’s request and in accordance with **Sections 11.8 and 11.9** of the DPA, assist Contoso in responding to Data Subject requests and conducting data protection impact assessments relating to the Additional Processing Activities.

11.8 **International Transfers and Multi-Region Hosting.**  
Any international transfers of Personal Data in connection with Multi-Region Hosting shall be governed by **Section 11.10** of the DPA and any applicable data transfer mechanisms (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) executed by the Parties.

11.9 **Data Retention and Deletion.**  
Pinnacle shall retain Personal Data Processed under this Addendum only for as long as necessary to provide the Additional Services or as required by Applicable Law, consistent with **Section 11.11** of the DPA. At the end of the retention period or upon Contoso’s written request, Pinnacle shall return or delete such Personal Data as directed by Contoso.

11.10 **Audit Rights.**  
Contoso’s audit rights under **Section 11.12** of the DPA apply to the Additional Processing Activities. Pinnacle shall make available, upon request, relevant Security Documentation and third-party audit reports that cover the Additional Services.
