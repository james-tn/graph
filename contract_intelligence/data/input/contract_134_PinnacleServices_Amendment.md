- **Contoso Enterprises**, a [jurisdiction] corporation, with its principal place of business at **1450 Metropolitan Avenue, 18th Floor, New York, NY 10018, USA** (**“Contoso”**, the **“Client”** or the **“Company”**); and  
- **Pinnacle Services, Inc.**, a [jurisdiction] corporation, with its principal place of business at **2900 Market Plaza, Suite 2200, San Francisco, CA 94105, USA** (**“Pinnacle”**, the **“Vendor”** or the **“Service Provider”**).

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants set forth in this Amendment, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Amendment modifies and expands the **Scope of Services / Work** set forth in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly set forth in this **Article II**, nothing in this Amendment shall be deemed to limit or reduce the Service Provider’s obligations under **Article II** of the Parent Agreement. The Enhanced Services described in this Article II are supplemental to the Services already defined in **Sections 2.2.1 through 2.2.4** and the Deliverables listed in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Enhanced Services.**  
The Service Provider shall perform the Enhanced Services to extend and deepen the capabilities of the Platform originally contemplated in **Section 2.1 (Overview of Project)** of the Parent Agreement. The Enhanced Services shall include, without limitation:

  (a) Extension of the Platform’s data model and integrations to cover an additional **seven (7)** source systems beyond the “at least five (5) primary source systems” specified in **Section 2.2.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement, including specialized line-of-business applications and third-party data feeds.  

  (b) Development and deployment of **Extended Use Cases**, including:
   - Predictive analytics for supply chain disruptions and inventory optimization, building on the supply chain KPIs described in **Section 2.2.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
   - Advanced workforce and headcount scenario modeling extending the “headcount and HR metrics” reporting in **Section 2.2.2(c)**.  
   - Customer churn prediction and revenue-at-risk dashboards aligned with the financial performance and sales pipeline reports referenced in **Section 2.2.2(c)**.

  (c) Implementation of advanced data governance capabilities, including:
   - Data quality scoring and stewardship workflows that expand on the “data validation, reconciliations, and automated quality checks” described in **Section 2.2.2(e)**.  
   - Formal data classification and retention rules aligned with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Amendment.  

  (d) Enhanced operational and performance monitoring for the Platform, supplementing the monitoring obligations in **Section 2.2.4(a)** and the SLA reporting obligations in **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (e) Expansion of managed services to include 24x7 critical incident coverage, proactive capacity planning, and quarterly Optimization Releases, extending the “quarterly business reviews, system health assessments, and recommendations for optimization and new capabilities” described in **Section 2.2.4(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Enhanced Deliverables.**  
In addition to the Deliverables listed in **Section 2.3(a)–(j)** of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall provide the following **Enhanced Deliverables** under this Amendment:

  (a) **Extended Data Integration Specifications.** Detailed integration design, mapping, and transformation documents for the additional seven (7) source systems, aligned with the **Technical Design Documents and Integration Specifications** referenced in **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Advanced Analytics Use Case Catalog.** A catalog of Extended Use Cases, including business rationale, data sources, model specifications, acceptance criteria, and dependencies, which shall supplement the **Requirements Specification Document** referenced in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) **Predictive Models and Automation Workflows.** Deployable models, scripts, and configurations implementing predictive maintenance, churn scoring, and scenario planning, delivered as part of the **Configured and Deployed Platform** under **Section 2.3(f)** of the Parent Agreement but specifically tagged as Enhanced Deliverables under this Amendment.

  (d) **Regulatory Reporting Deliverables.** At least three (3) dedicated reporting packages designed to assist the Company with regulatory compliance, data protection reporting, and audit support, complementing the “Support audit, compliance, and security review activities” obligation in **Section 2.2.4(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (e) **Enhanced Operations Runbooks.** Updated and expanded **Operations Runbooks, Support Procedures, and Escalation Paths**, building on the Deliverable referenced in **Section 2.3(i)** of the Parent Agreement, to incorporate 24x7 coverage procedures, advanced incident playbooks, and security escalation paths.

  (f) **Quarterly Optimization Release Plans and Reports.** For each quarter during the Amended Term, detailed plans and post-implementation reports for Optimization Releases, which shall supplement the **Quarterly Health and Optimization Reports** referenced in **Section 2.3(j)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones for Enhanced Services.**  
The Enhanced Services shall be delivered in the following high-level phases, which shall be integrated into the **Project Plan and Milestone Schedule** initially created under **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement and updated through the Change Management process in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment:

  (a) **Phase A – Extended Discovery and Design (Estimated Duration: 3 months from Amendment Effective Date).**  
   - Refine requirements for Extended Use Cases.  
   - Assess additional source systems and data quality.  
   - Produce an **Amended Solution Architecture Addendum** updating the **Solution Architecture Blueprint** referenced in **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Phase B – Build and Integration (Estimated Duration: 7–9 months).**  
   - Implement additional integrations, data model extensions, and data governance rules.  
   - Build predictive models and automation workflows.

  (c) **Phase C – Testing and Rollout (Estimated Duration: 3–4 months).**  
   - Develop and execute test plans for Extended Use Cases, aligned with **Section 2.2.3(a)–(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
   - Conduct targeted end-user training sessions, supplementing the training obligations in **Section 2.2.3(d)**.

  (d) **Phase D – Enhanced Managed Services (Through Expiration Date of this Amendment).**  
   - Provide ongoing managed services, Optimization Releases, and enhanced monitoring, as further described in **Section 2.5** below and **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.5 **Performance Standards for Enhanced Services.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall perform the Enhanced Services:

  (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner with personnel possessing specialized skills in advanced analytics, data science, and cloud operations;  
  (b) in adherence to the Parent SLAs and Amendment SLAs (Article XII of this Amendment), including the 99.9% monthly availability commitment in **Section 12.1** of the Parent Agreement as supplemented herein;  
  (c) in compliance with all Applicable Laws and **Data Protection Laws** as defined in **Section 1.7** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Amendment; and  
  (d) in coordination with the Company’s project management and governance processes, including timely participation in quarterly business reviews and optimization sessions in accordance with **Section 2.2.4(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities for Enhanced Services.**  
In addition to the responsibilities defined in **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, the Company shall:

  (a) Provide timely access to additional source systems and subject matter experts relevant to the Extended Use Cases.  
  (b) Participate in model validation, UAT, and governance reviews, providing acceptance or rejection (with detailed reasons) of Enhanced Deliverables within fifteen (15) **Business Days**, consistent with the acceptance procedures in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement.  
  (c) Support integration with internal security, identity, and compliance teams for the Security Enhancements contemplated in this Amendment.

2.7 **Acceptance of Enhanced Deliverables.**  
The acceptance procedures in **Section 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Enhanced Deliverables, except that for complex predictive models and automation workflows, the Parties may mutually agree in writing (including via Change Order under **Article XIII**) on extended testing or pilot periods.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  
The total additional contract value for the Enhanced Services and Enhanced Deliverables under this Amendment shall be **One Million Sixty-Six Thousand Five Hundred Ninety-Two U.S. Dollars (US $1,066,592)** (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**), exclusive of applicable taxes. This amount is **in addition to** the Original Contract Value of **US $671,268** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. Accordingly, the aggregate potential commercial value of the Parent Agreement as amended hereby is **US $1,737,860**, subject to any further Change Orders executed under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Structure for Enhanced Services.**  
The Amendment Contract Value shall be comprised of:

  (a) **Enhanced Implementation Fees: US $616,592.**  
  This amount covers all work performed during Phases A–C described in **Section 2.4(a)–(c)** of this Amendment, including Extended Discovery and Design, Build and Integration, and Testing and Rollout of the Enhanced Services.

  (b) **Enhanced Managed Services Fees: US $450,000.**  
  This amount covers the incremental cost of enhanced managed services, including 24x7 Severity 1 coverage, Optimization Releases, and enhanced monitoring, for the period from the completion of Phase C (go-live of Enhanced Use Cases) through the **Expiration Date of this Amendment (September 28, 2028)**, as further described in **Section 3.4** below.

3.3 **Milestone-Based Payments for Enhanced Implementation.**  
The Enhanced Implementation Fees shall be invoiced upon achievement of the following **Amended Milestones**, which supplement the Milestones listed in **Section 3.3(a)–(d)** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Amendment Milestone A – Extended Requirements and Architecture**  
   - Trigger: Acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the **Amended Solution Architecture Addendum** and the **Advanced Analytics Use Case Catalog**, which supplement the **Requirements Specification Document** and **Solution Architecture Blueprint** delivered under the Parent Agreement.  
   - Amount: **US $185,000**.

  (b) **Amendment Milestone B – Extended Data Integration and Governance Complete**  
   - Trigger: Completion and acceptance of integrations for the additional seven (7) source systems, implementation of data governance enhancements, and delivery of updated **Data Model and Data Dictionary** entries for Extended Use Cases.  
   - Amount: **US $246,592**.

  (c) **Amendment Milestone C – Predictive Models and Automation Ready for UAT**  
   - Trigger: Delivery of predictive models, automation workflows, and related configurations, with successful completion of system and integration testing for Enhanced Use Cases, and readiness for user acceptance testing.  
   - Amount: **US $120,000**.

  (d) **Amendment Milestone D – Enhanced Go-Live and Hypercare Completion**  
   - Trigger: Successful go-live of the Enhanced Use Cases and completion of an additional sixty (60) day hypercare period (in addition to the ninety (90) days specified in **Section 2.2.3(f)** of the Parent Agreement) dedicated to the Enhanced Services.  
   - Amount: **US $65,000**.

3.4 **Enhanced Managed Services Fees and Schedule.**  
In addition to the managed services fees in **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement (US $6,250 per month for thirty-two (32) months and US $5,000 per month for ten (10) months), the Company shall pay the Service Provider a monthly enhanced managed services fee as follows:

  (a) From the Enhanced Go-Live date (Amendment Milestone D) through **June 30, 2027**: **US $18,000 per month**.  
  (b) From **July 1, 2027** through the **Expiration Date of this Amendment (September 28, 2028)**: **US $15,000 per month**.

These amounts are inclusive of the Amendment SLAs described in **Article XII** of this Amendment. Any adjustments to these fees shall be made through the Change Order process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

3.5 **Payment Terms (Net 60).**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.5 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement (which specifies **Net 30**), the Parties agree that all undisputed amounts invoiced under this Amendment shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** from the date of the applicable invoice (**“Net 60”**). The invoice dispute procedures in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., fifteen (15) day dispute notice, good faith resolution efforts, payment of undisputed amounts) are hereby incorporated by reference and shall apply to invoices issued under this Amendment.

3.6 **Late Payment.**  
Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement (providing for interest at one percent (1.0%) per month or the maximum rate allowed by law) shall apply to undisputed amounts invoiced under this Amendment that are not paid by the Net 60 due date. The Service Provider’s right to suspend performance as described in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to the Enhanced Services, provided that suspension of Enhanced Services shall not relieve the Company of its obligation to pay all undisputed amounts.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  
The expense and reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. To the extent Enhanced Services require additional travel or out-of-pocket expenses beyond the original Project scope, the Service Provider shall obtain the Company’s prior written approval and shall itemize such expenses in accordance with the Company’s then-current travel and expense policies.

3.8 **Taxes.**  
The tax allocation in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement (Company responsible for sales, use, VAT, etc., except taxes on Service Provider income) shall apply to fees under this Amendment as well.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amended Term.**  
This Amendment shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date (June 27, 2026)** and shall continue in effect until **September 28, 2028** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the applicable provisions of **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Term.**  
The Term of the Parent Agreement, as defined in **Section 1.17** and specified in **Section 4.1** (commencing on November 12, 2025 and ending on March 4, 2029), remains in full force and effect. This Amendment does not shorten or extend the overall Term of the Parent Agreement; instead, it defines a specific Amended Term applicable to the Enhanced Services. For the avoidance of doubt, after the Amendment Expiration Date, the Enhanced Services and associated Amendment SLAs shall cease, unless the Parties execute a further written amendment or Change Order.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly).**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.4 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement, which provides that the Company may terminate the Parent Agreement for convenience upon one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice, the Parties agree that:

  (a) The Company may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part (with respect to Enhanced Services only), for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the Service Provider.  

  (b) Upon such termination for convenience of this Amendment, the Company shall pay the Service Provider:
   - All fees earned for Enhanced Services performed and Enhanced Deliverables accepted up to the effective date of termination; and  
   - Any non-cancellable commitments reasonably incurred specifically for the Enhanced Services, provided such commitments were disclosed to and approved by the Company in advance.

The Parent Agreement (including its original Services and Deliverables) shall remain in effect unless separately terminated in accordance with **Article IV** thereof.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment and the Enhanced Services. A material breach of this Amendment (e.g., repeated SLA failures under **Article XII**, non-payment of fees under **Article III**, or failure to deliver material Enhanced Deliverables) shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement for purposes of **Section 4.3**, subject to the cure periods specified therein (thirty (30) days, or ten (10) days for non-payment).

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires in accordance with its terms:

  (a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement, without the need for separate notice.  

  (b) The Parties’ respective obligations as to confidentiality, IP ownership, indemnification, limitations of liability, data protection, and other provisions that survive termination (see **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement) shall also apply to the Enhanced Services and Enhanced Deliverables.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Amendment Only.**  
If this Amendment is terminated while the Parent Agreement remains in effect:

  (a) The Service Provider shall promptly cease performing the Enhanced Services, except for any transition or wind-down services requested by the Company, which shall be provided at the Service Provider’s then-current time and materials rates, unless termination is due to the Service Provider’s uncured material breach, in which case such transition assistance shall be provided at no additional charge (consistent with **Section 4.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement).  

  (b) The Company shall pay all amounts due and owing for Enhanced Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, subject to **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 3.5** of this Amendment.  

  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information associated with the Enhanced Services in accordance with **Section 5.4** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Amendment.

4.7 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Amendment that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI**, together with any payment obligations accrued prior to termination.
