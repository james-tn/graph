## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Master Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This SOW is executed pursuant to, and **supplements**, the Master Agreement dated **August 21, 2025 (Ref: MSA-PHO-202508-344 / contract_344)**. The Services and Deliverables described herein constitute **“Services”** and **“Deliverables”** as defined in **Sections 1.14 and 1.8** of the Master Agreement and shall be performed in accordance with **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Master Agreement.

2.1.2 In accordance with **Section 2.2(c)** of the Master Agreement, in the event of a conflict between the terms of the Master Agreement and this SOW, the terms of the Master Agreement shall govern **unless** this SOW expressly states that it is intended to modify a specific provision of the Master Agreement. For this Low-Risk Engagement, the Parties expressly agree that **Article IX of this SOW** shall modify the risk allocation of **Article IX of the Master Agreement** with respect to the Services and Deliverables under this SOW only, as more fully set out in **Section 9.5** below.

2.2 **Project Description – Implementation Services.**  

Phoenix shall provide comprehensive implementation Services to design, configure, integrate, and deploy an enhanced **enterprise data analytics and reporting Solution** for Contoso’s internal business operations. The implementation Services will include, without limitation, the following:

2.2.1 **Discovery and Requirements Analysis.**  
(a) Conduct stakeholder workshops with Contoso’s business, IT, and data governance teams to elicit functional and non-functional requirements for the Solution.  
(b) Review and assess existing Client Materials, including current data warehouse schemas, ETL scripts, dashboards, and reports.  
(c) Document business requirements, technical requirements, data sources, data quality constraints, security and access controls, and compliance considerations (including those under **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Master Agreement).  
(d) Produce a formal **Requirements Specification** Deliverable, which shall be subject to review and acceptance under **Section 2.6** of this SOW and **Section 2.6 of the Master Agreement**.

2.2.2 **Solution Architecture and Design.**  
(a) Propose a target-state reference architecture for the analytics platform, including data ingestion, transformation, storage, semantic modeling, and presentation layers.  
(b) Define integration patterns for the Client Environment and relevant Third-Party Materials.  
(c) Prepare **Architecture Diagrams** and a **Detailed Design Document** covering data models, ETL/ELT workflows, security model, and performance considerations.  
(d) Align the design with Contoso’s security requirements consistent with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** and **Section 2.8(a) (Enhanced Standards)** of the Master Agreement, while recognizing that this SOW is contractually designated as a Low-Risk Engagement.

2.2.3 **Configuration and Build.**  
(a) Configure the analytics platform, including creation of datasets, reports, dashboards, and user roles/permissions.  
(b) Develop data ingestion and transformation jobs, including validation and error-handling logic.  
(c) Implement integration with key Contoso systems (e.g., ERP, CRM, HRIS).  
(d) Develop and configure logging, monitoring, and alerting consistent with the Service Levels described in **Article XII** of this SOW and **Article XII** of the Master Agreement.

2.2.4 **Testing and Quality Assurance.**  
(a) Develop a testing strategy and test plan, including unit, system, integration, performance, and user acceptance testing.  
(b) Execute tests in coordination with Contoso, log defects, and track resolution.  
(c) Provide test summary reports and evidence of test execution.  
(d) Support Contoso in conducting **User Acceptance Testing (UAT)** against the Acceptance Criteria.

2.2.5 **Deployment and Go-Live.**  
(a) Prepare a deployment run-book and rollback plan.  
(b) Coordinate deployment to the production environment and validate post-deployment health.  
(c) Conduct a **Go-Live Review** with Contoso to confirm readiness and sign-off on the Go-Live Date.  
(d) Transition the Solution into steady-state **Managed Services** as described in **Section 2.3**.

2.2.6 **Documentation and Knowledge Transfer.**  
(a) Prepare **Documentation**, including architecture diagrams, configuration details, data flow diagrams, and operational run-books.  
(b) Deliver end-user training and administrator training sessions (on-site or remote) for designated Contoso personnel, consistent with training contemplated by **Section 2.1(f)** of the Master Agreement.  
(c) Conduct knowledge transfer workshops to ensure Contoso’s operations team can effectively interact with Phoenix’s Managed Services team.

2.3 **Managed Services – Ongoing Support and Maintenance.**  

Following successful Go-Live, Phoenix shall provide Managed Services for the Solution throughout the remainder of the SOW Term, including:

2.3.1 **Monitoring and Incident Management.**  
(a) Proactive monitoring of core Solution components and data pipelines.  
(b) Incident detection, triage, and resolution in accordance with the **Severity Levels** and response/ resolution targets set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this SOW, which are aligned with **Article XII of the Master Agreement**.  

2.3.2 **Service Request Handling.**  
(a) Handling of standard service requests, such as access requests, report changes, parameter adjustments, and configuration changes within agreed timeframes.  
(b) Performance of minor enhancements that do not materially alter the Solution architecture and are within the scope of Managed Services, as agreed in writing by the Parties.

2.3.3 **Maintenance and Patching.**  
(a) Application of patches, upgrades, and security updates to the Solution components within the maintenance windows defined in **Section 12.2** of this SOW.  
(b) Coordination with Contoso to minimize downtime and ensure compliance with **Section 12.5 (Exclusions)** of the Master Agreement.

2.3.4 **Capacity and Performance Management.**  
(a) Regular review of performance metrics and capacity utilization.  
(b) Recommendations to Contoso for scaling, optimization, or architectural adjustments (which may require Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Master Agreement).

2.4 **Milestones and Timeline.**  

Implementation Services shall be organized into the following Milestones, with target completion dates measured from the SOW Effective Date (subject to adjustment via Change Order):

- **Milestone 1 – Discovery and Requirements (Target: Month 2):**  
  Completion and acceptance of the **Requirements Specification**.

- **Milestone 2 – Architecture and Design (Target: Month 4):**  
  Completion and acceptance of the **Architecture Diagrams** and **Detailed Design Document**.

- **Milestone 3 – Configuration and Build (Target: Month 8):**  
  Completion of core configurations, data pipelines, and initial dashboards; delivery of **Build Completion Report**.

- **Milestone 4 – Testing and UAT (Target: Month 10):**  
  Completion of system, integration, and UAT, and delivery of **Test Summary Report**.

- **Milestone 5 – Go-Live and Transition to Managed Services (Target: Month 12):**  
  Successful production deployment, confirmation of Go-Live Date, and delivery of initial **Operations Run-Book** and training.

- **Milestone 6 – Annual Operations Review (Recurring, each 12-month period thereafter):**  
  Delivery of **Annual Service Performance Report** and roadmap recommendations.

2.5 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  

Consistent with **Section 2.5 (Contoso Responsibilities)** of the Master Agreement, Contoso shall:

(a) Provide timely access to relevant systems, data sources, facilities, and personnel;  
(b) Assign qualified staff to collaborate with Phoenix and make timely decisions;  
(c) Review and provide feedback on Deliverables within ten (10) Business Days or such other review periods agreed in writing;  
(d) Ensure that Client Materials are accurate and free from known malware; and

Delays or failures by Contoso to perform its responsibilities may entitle Phoenix to equitable schedule extensions and additional Fees as set forth in **Sections 2.5** and **3.7** of the Master Agreement and **Article XIII** of this SOW.

(a) Unless otherwise stated, Contoso shall have **ten (10) Business Days** after delivery to perform acceptance testing and to issue a written notice of rejection specifying any material non-conformities with the SOW or applicable specifications.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **SOW Term.**  

The **SOW Term** shall commence on the SOW Effective Date (**August 21, 2026**) and shall continue until **August 04, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** and **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Master Agreement.

(a) The Parties shall, prior to the Master Agreement’s expiration, either (i) execute an amendment or new master agreement extending or replacing the Master Agreement so as to permit this SOW to continue through its scheduled SOW Term, or (ii) agree on an orderly wind-down of this SOW in accordance with **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Master Agreement and **Section 4.5** of this SOW.

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience by Contoso)** of the Master Agreement (which provides for 180 days’ notice), Contoso may terminate this SOW, in whole or in part, **for convenience and without cause** upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Phoenix.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this SOW for cause in accordance with **Section 4.5 (Termination for Cause)** of the Master Agreement, including for material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of notice, or insolvency-related events. Termination of this SOW for cause does not automatically terminate the Master Agreement, unless expressly stated in the termination notice and permitted under the Master Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition Assistance.**  

4.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this SOW:

(a) Phoenix shall cease performance of Services under this SOW as of the effective date of termination, except to the extent Contoso requests Transition Assistance;  
(b) Contoso shall pay Phoenix all outstanding undisputed Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.6(b) of the Master Agreement**;  
(c) Phoenix shall deliver to Contoso any completed or in-progress Deliverables that Contoso has paid for or will pay for under the termination settlement; and  
(d) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 5.6 of the Master Agreement**.

4.5.2 At Contoso’s written request made within thirty (30) days after notice of termination, Phoenix shall provide Transition Assistance for up to ninety (90) days following termination, in accordance with **Section 4.7 of the Master Agreement**, at Phoenix’s then-current T&M rates (unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order or termination agreement).

4.6 **Survival.**  

The provisions of this SOW that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIV, XV, XVI, and XVII**, and all payment obligations accrued prior to termination.
