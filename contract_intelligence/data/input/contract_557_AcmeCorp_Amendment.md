## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

(a) This Amendment **modifies and supplements** the scope of Services defined in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) The Parties expressly agree that, except as specifically set forth herein, all provisions of **Article II of the Parent Agreement** (including Sections 2.1 through 2.9 concerning overview, software provision, deployment model, implementation services, Deliverables, performance standards, Contoso responsibilities, acceptance, and changes to scope) shall apply **mutatis mutandis** to the Amended Services.

2.2 **Overview of Amended Services.**  

Under this Amendment, Acme shall provide additional Services to expand Contoso’s use of the **Acme Enterprise Suite** (as defined in Section 1.18 of the Parent Agreement) beyond the core modules listed in **Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement** (CRM, ERP, Analytics and Reporting, Integration APIs). The Amended Services are aimed at supporting Contoso’s advanced analytics, supplier collaboration, and field workforce operations globally. These Services include:

  (a) Licensing and configuration of the **Additional Modules** described in Section 2.3;  
  (b) Associated implementation, integration, migration, and testing activities, as described in Sections 2.4 and 2.5;  
  (c) Enhanced training and change management support for global adoption;  
  (d) Enhanced SLA commitments for certain critical components, as detailed in Article XII; and  
  (e) Ongoing optimization and advisory services on a time-and-materials basis within the Amendment Contract Value.

2.3 **Additional Modules.**

Acme shall grant Contoso the right to use, and shall implement and configure, the following **Additional Modules**, as part of the **Software** under the Parent Agreement (subject to Article VI of the Parent Agreement and Article VI of this Amendment):

  (a) **Advanced Analytics & AI Module**  
  - Predictive analytics for sales, inventory, and supply chain operations.  
  - Machine learning models for demand forecasting and anomaly detection.  
  - Self-service dashboards integrated with the **Analytics and Reporting Module** referenced in Section 2.2(c) of the Parent Agreement.  

  (b) **Supplier Collaboration Portal Module**  
  - External supplier portal integrated with the **ERP Module** referenced in Section 2.2(b) of the Parent Agreement.  
  - Capabilities for purchase order acknowledgment, shipment notifications, and invoice collaboration.  
  - Role-based access controls for supplier users, leveraging the security roles framework designed during the **Requirements & Design Phase** described in Section 2.4(a) of the Parent Agreement.  

  (c) **Mobile Workforce Management Module**  
  - Mobile apps (iOS and Android) for field staff to access CRM and ERP data.  
  - Offline capabilities for remote operations, with synchronization to the main **Acme Enterprise Suite** instance.  
  - GPS-based job tracking and time logging, integrated with Contoso’s HRIS system via the **Integration APIs** referenced in Section 2.2(d) of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Implementation Services – Expansion Phase.**

(a) The Parties shall extend the implementation lifecycle described in **Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement** (Requirements & Design, Configuration & Development, Testing & UAT, Go-Live & Stabilization) to cover the Additional Modules.  
(b) Within thirty (30) days of the Amendment Effective Date, the Parties shall jointly update the implementation plan to reflect the following indicative timelines (subject to adjustment via the Change Management Process):

  (i) **Expansion Requirements & Design (Estimated 45–60 days)**  
  - Conduct supplemental workshops with Contoso’s business units using the Additional Modules.  
  - Produce updated solution design documents for each Additional Module, aligning with the design documentation Deliverable referenced in Section 2.5(a) of the Parent Agreement.  

  (ii) **Expansion Configuration & Development (Estimated 60–90 days)**  
  - Configure the Additional Modules, including role-based access, workflows, supplier portals, and mobile capabilities.  
  - Develop incremental integration components to connect the Additional Modules to third-party systems (e.g., HRIS, logistics platforms), leveraging the integration approach defined under the Parent Agreement.  

  (iii) **Expansion Testing & User Acceptance (Estimated 45–60 days)**  
  - Conduct system integration testing, performance testing of the Additional Modules, and end-to-end process testing across ERP, CRM, and the Additional Modules.  
  - Support Contoso’s global UAT cycles, with defect management aligned to severity levels and timelines in the SLA (Section 12.4 of the Parent Agreement).  

  (iv) **Expansion Go-Live & Stabilization (Estimated 30–45 days)**  
  - Execute data and configuration migration necessary to enable production usage of the Additional Modules.  
  - Provide “hypercare” support for at least thirty (30) days after each Additional Module’s go-live, consistent with the hypercare concept described in Section 2.4(d) of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Deliverables under this Amendment.**

In addition to the Deliverables listed in **Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement**, Acme shall provide the following Deliverables (collectively, the **“Amendment Deliverables”**):

  (a) Updated solution architecture and design documentation reflecting the Additional Modules and integrations;  
  (b) Configured instances of the Additional Modules in development, test, and production environments;  
  (c) Incremental integration components, interface specifications, and mapping documentation specifically for the Additional Modules;  
  (d) Updated end-user training materials and administrator guides incorporating the Additional Modules;  
  (e) UAT test scripts and traceability matrices for new and modified processes;  
  (f) A consolidated **Final Expansion Acceptance Report**, signed (or deemed accepted) by Contoso in accordance with Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**

(a) Acme shall perform the Amended Services in accordance with **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards) of the Parent Agreement** and shall ensure that personnel assigned to the Amended Services are appropriately trained on the Additional Modules.  
(b) Contoso acknowledges that effective performance by Acme is dependent upon Contoso’s timely provision of accurate information, resources, and decisions, as noted in Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  

In addition to the responsibilities set out in **Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement**, Contoso shall:

  (a) Provide representative users from procurement, logistics, analytics, and field operations to participate in requirements, UAT, and training;  
  (b) Ensure that any third-party systems to be integrated with the Additional Modules are accessible and appropriately documented;  
  (c) Obtain any additional consents from third-party licensors required for extended data processing or new integrations.

2.8 **Acceptance of Amendment Deliverables.**

Unless otherwise specified in a relevant SOW:

  (a) Acceptance of each Amendment Deliverable shall be governed by **Section 2.8 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement (14-day review period, notice of material non-conformity, correction, and escalation).  
  (b) The **Final Expansion Acceptance Report** shall be deemed a Deliverable for purposes of the payment milestones under **Article III of this Amendment**.

2.9 **Changes to Scope for Amended Services.**

Any changes to the scope, timeline, or Fees related to the Amended Services shall be managed in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XIII of this Amendment**, and shall require a written Change Order signed by both Parties.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  

The Parties agree that the total contract value specific to this Amendment (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**) is **US$2,577,126**. This value is incremental to, and separate from, the **Contract Value of US$4,605,619** set forth in **Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement**. For clarity, the aggregate commercial relationship between the Parties under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment totals **US$7,182,745**, subject to approved Change Orders.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  

The Amendment Contract Value is composed of the following principal components (illustrative structure):

  (a) **Additional Implementation and Configuration Fees:** US$1,050,000  
  - Covering design, configuration, integration development, testing, and go-live support of the Additional Modules.  

  (b) **Incremental Annual Subscription / License Fees for Additional Modules:** US$350,000 per year  
  - For the period from the Amendment Effective Date through the Amendment Expiration Date (pro-rated for partial years).  

  (c) **Incremental Annual Maintenance and Support Fees:** US$250,000 per year  
  - Aligning with the Maintenance and Support Services under **Article XII of the Parent Agreement**, as extended to the Additional Modules.  

  (d) **Optimization and Advisory Services Pool (Time-and-Materials):** US$327,126  
  - For post-implementation optimization, analytics model tuning, mobile UX enhancements, and supplier onboarding support, billed on a time-and-materials basis consistent with **Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.3 **Payment Milestones for Implementation (Amended Services).**

Unless otherwise specified in a SOW referencing this Amendment, the additional implementation and configuration fees (US$1,050,000) shall be invoiced as follows, modeled on but distinct from **Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement**:

  (a) **Thirty percent (30%)** (US$315,000) upon the Amendment Effective Date;  
  (b) **Thirty-five percent (35%)** (US$367,500) upon completion of the **Expansion Configuration & Development Phase** described in Section 2.4(b)(ii) of this Amendment;  
  (c) **Twenty-five percent (25%)** (US$262,500) upon completion of UAT for all Additional Modules and issuance (or deemed issuance) of the **Final Expansion Acceptance Report**;  
  (d) **Ten percent (10%)** (US$105,000) upon successful go-live of all Additional Modules in production.

3.4 **Recurring Fees.**

(a) The incremental subscription and Maintenance and Support Fees under this Amendment shall be invoiced annually in advance, in alignment with the billing cycles for the Software under **Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement**.  
(b) For partial years, such Fees shall be prorated on a monthly basis.

3.5 **Time-and-Materials Services.**

Professional services provided from the optimization and advisory services pool identified in Section 3.2(d) shall be:

  (a) Invoiced monthly in arrears based on actual hours worked;  
  (b) Charged at the hourly or daily rates set forth in the applicable SOW or Acme’s then-current rate card, consistent with **Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement**; and  
  (c) Subject to prior written approval by Contoso as required under the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Payment Terms (Net 45).**

Notwithstanding **Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement**, which stipulates **Net 30** payment terms, the Parties agree that:

  (a) All undisputed invoices issued under this Amendment shall be payable in full within **forty-five (45) calendar days** from the date of invoice (**“Net 45”**);  
  (b) This modification to payment terms applies solely to invoices issued under this Amendment and does not, by itself, amend Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement for invoices issued thereunder, unless expressly stated in a separate written amendment.

3.7 **Late Payments.**

(a) Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Amendment shall accrue interest from the due date until paid in full at the same rate as set forth in **Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement** (the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law).  
(b) Acme may, upon written notice, suspend performance of **Amended Services** under this Amendment for any undisputed amounts overdue by more than sixty (60) days, consistent with Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement. Suspension of Amended Services shall not, by itself, constitute a breach by Acme.

3.8 **Taxes.**

All Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, and Contoso’s obligations regarding Taxes, withholding, and documentation shall be governed by **Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.9 **Expenses.**

Any reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Acme in connection with on-site Amended Services shall be reimbursed in accordance with **Section 3.9 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.10 **Disputed Amounts.**

Dispute resolution for invoices under this Amendment shall follow **Section 3.10 of the Parent Agreement**, including the requirement for Contoso to notify Acme in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, with payment of undisputed portions in accordance with Section 3.6 of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**

This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **September 16, 2029** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**

(a) The **Term** of the Parent Agreement, as defined in Section 1.20 and specified in **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement, currently runs until **April 26, 2029**, subject to renewal.  
(b) The Parties expressly agree that, notwithstanding the Parent Agreement’s current Expiration Date, this Amendment shall extend the provision of the Amended Services until **September 16, 2029**, provided that:  
  (i) The Parent Agreement is renewed or extended so that it remains in force through at least the Amendment Expiration Date; or  
  (ii) If the Parent Agreement is not so renewed or extended, the Parties shall execute an additional instrument to ensure continuity of the core terms necessary to support the Amended Services (e.g., IP, confidentiality, data protection, limitation of liability).  

Until such time as any additional instrument is executed, this Amendment shall be interpreted as contingent upon the continued effectiveness of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Renewal of Amended Services.**

Any renewal of the Amended Services shall be subject to the Parties’ mutual written agreement, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience.**

(a) Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement**, which allows Contoso to terminate the Parent Agreement for convenience on 180 days’ written notice, the Parties agree that either Party may terminate this Amendment (with respect to the Amended Services only) for convenience by providing at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party.  
(b) Upon such termination for convenience:  
  (i) Contoso shall remain liable for all Fees and expenses incurred for Amended Services up to the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable commitments made by Acme in reliance on this Amendment;  
  (ii) Any prepaid Fees for Amended Services not yet performed as of the effective date of termination shall be refunded on a prorated basis.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.**

Termination for cause shall be governed by **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement**, provided that:

  (a) A material breach of this Amendment shall be treated as a material breach of the Parent Agreement for purposes of Section 4.5(a); and  
  (b) Acme’s rights to terminate for payment default or breach of License restrictions under Section 4.5(b) of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to the Fees and License grants under this Amendment.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**

Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment for any reason:

  (a) All rights to use the **Additional Modules** granted under this Amendment shall automatically terminate, and Contoso shall cease all use of the Additional Modules, while retaining rights to use other components of the Software as permitted under the Parent Agreement (if still in force);  
  (b) Sections 4.6(a)–(d) of the Parent Agreement shall apply **mutatis mutandis** with respect to any Software and Documentation provided under this Amendment;  
  (c) Contoso shall pay all outstanding and unpaid Fees and expenses related to the Amended Services accrued through the effective date of termination;  
  (d) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information relating to the Amended Services in accordance with Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Effect of Parent Agreement Termination.**

(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires and is not renewed or replaced with a substantially similar master agreement between the Parties, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same date, unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing.  
(b) For the avoidance of doubt, termination of this Amendment does not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Transition Assistance.**

Contoso may request transition assistance with respect to the Additional Modules in accordance with **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement. Fees for such assistance shall follow Acme’s then-current professional services rates unless otherwise agreed.

4.9 **Survival.**

The following provisions of this Amendment shall survive any expiration or termination of this Amendment: Articles I, III (with respect to unpaid Fees), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII (with respect to accrued credits), XIV, XVI, and this Section 4.9, together with any corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement that are stated therein to survive.
