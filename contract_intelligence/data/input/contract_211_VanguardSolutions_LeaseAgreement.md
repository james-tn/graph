## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF LEASE AND SERVICES

### 2.1 Lease Grant

2.1.1 Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Vanguard hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Vanguard, the Premises, together with the non‑exclusive right to use the common areas of the building (including lobbies, elevators, corridors, restrooms, and other shared facilities) and the building systems and infrastructure as may be reasonably necessary for the permitted use set forth in Section 2.3.

2.1.2 The lease of the Premises is for the Lease Term, commencing on the Effective Date or the Commencement Date (if different), and expiring on May 29, 2029, unless sooner terminated as provided in Article IV.

### 2.2 Area and Configuration

2.2.1 The Premises are agreed to consist of approximately 20,000 rentable square feet, or such other measurement as may be determined by the building’s standard measurement methodology, as specified in Exhibit A.

2.2.2 Vanguard shall provide Contoso with access to the Premises on a 24 hours per day, 7 days per week basis, subject to reasonable building rules and security procedures, and subject to Force Majeure Events and planned maintenance as described in Article XII.

### 2.3 Permitted Use

2.3.1 Contoso shall use the Premises solely for general office use, administrative activities, and lawful business operations consistent with a first‑class office building in New York City, and for no other purpose without Vanguard’s prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.

2.3.2 Contoso shall not use, nor permit the Premises to be used, for any purpose that:  
(a) is illegal or violates any applicable laws, ordinances, or regulations;  
(b) constitutes a nuisance or unreasonable disturbance to other tenants;  
(c) poses a material risk of damage to the building or its systems; or  
(d) violates building rules provided by Vanguard from time to time, provided such rules are applied on a non‑discriminatory basis.

### 2.4 Services Provided by Vanguard

Subject to the terms herein, Vanguard shall provide the following Services:

2.4.1 **Utilities and HVAC.** Vanguard shall furnish heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (“HVAC”) during standard building hours (8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. local time on Business Days) in accordance with building standards consistent with comparable Class A office buildings in New York City. On request, Vanguard shall provide after‑hours HVAC at the then‑applicable rate, subject to prior scheduling by Contoso. Vanguard shall also provide electrical power to the Premises for normal office use, including lighting and standard office equipment, subject to load limits described in Exhibit A.

2.4.2 **Water and Restrooms.** Vanguard shall supply hot and cold water for use in restrooms and, where applicable, pantry or kitchenette areas, consistent with building standards.

2.4.3 **Janitorial Services.** Vanguard shall provide routine janitorial and cleaning services in the Premises on Business Days (or such other schedule as reasonably determined by Vanguard) consisting of trash removal, vacuuming, restroom cleaning, and general office cleaning, all in accordance with reasonable industry standards. Specialized or above‑standard cleaning requested by Contoso shall be at Contoso’s additional expense.

2.4.4 **Building Security.** Vanguard shall maintain building security measures including, without limitation, doormen or security staff (as applicable), controlled access to building entrances during non‑business hours, and surveillance systems in common areas, in accordance with building standards. Vanguard shall not, however, be the insurer of the safety of persons or property on or about the Premises.

2.4.5 **Maintenance and Repairs.** Vanguard shall maintain and repair the base building structural elements, building systems serving the Premises (including HVAC systems, electrical distribution, plumbing, and elevators), the roof, exterior walls, and common areas. Vanguard shall perform such maintenance and repairs in a commercially reasonable manner and within reasonable timeframes, taking into account the nature and urgency of the issue.

2.4.6 **Common Areas and Amenities.** Vanguard shall maintain common areas and any amenities (e.g., shared conference rooms, fitness facilities, and lobby areas) made available to tenants of the building, subject to rules of use. Access to particular amenities may be subject to additional fees as specified in Exhibit C (Fee Schedule), if any.

### 2.5 Responsibilities of Contoso

2.5.1 **Interior Maintenance.** Contoso shall, at its expense, maintain the interior of the Premises, including but not limited to non‑structural components, fixtures, specialty installations, and equipment installed by or for Contoso, in good order and condition, normal wear and tear excepted. Contoso shall promptly notify Vanguard of any damage or condition requiring Vanguard’s repair.

2.5.2 **Alterations.** Contoso shall not make any structural alterations or modifications to the Premises without Vanguard’s prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed. Non‑structural alterations (e.g., demountable partitions, paint, flooring) not affecting building systems may be performed by Contoso with prior written notice and subject to compliance with building rules and applicable laws.

2.5.3 **Compliance with Laws.** Contoso shall comply with all applicable laws, codes, and regulations relating to its use and occupancy of the Premises, including health and safety laws, environmental regulations, and accessibility requirements, to the extent applicable to tenant‑specific operations.

2.5.4 **Furniture and Equipment.** Contoso shall provide and maintain its own furniture, fixtures, and business equipment. Installation of heavy equipment or any items exceeding building load limits requires Vanguard’s prior approval.

### 2.6 Deliverables and Documentation

2.6.1 Vanguard shall deliver to Contoso, on or before the Commencement Date, the following Deliverables:  
(a) Access credentials (keys, access cards, digital credentials) for authorized Contoso personnel;  
(b) A copy of building rules and regulations;  
(c) A summary of emergency procedures;  
(d) Baseline space plan and as‑built drawings for the Premises (to the extent available); and  
(e) A copy of the Service Levels and contact information for property management.

2.6.2 Vanguard shall provide periodic Deliverables thereafter, including monthly operating expense statements (if and as applicable), annual reconciliation statements, service incident reports as described in Article XII, and any other documentation reasonably required for Contoso to assess Vanguard’s compliance with this Agreement.

### 2.7 Performance Standards

2.7.1 Vanguard shall perform the Services with the degree of care, skill, and diligence reasonably expected of a professional commercial property manager of comparable Class A office buildings in New York City.

2.7.2 Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption to Contoso’s business operations when performing maintenance, repairs, or other Services in or near the Premises, and shall coordinate in advance with Contoso for any work likely to materially impact Contoso’s operations.

2.7.3 Vanguard shall comply with all applicable laws, ordinances, and regulations in the performance of its obligations hereunder, including Data Protection Laws with respect to any Personal Data processed in connection with building access systems, CCTV, or related services pursuant to Article XI.

### 2.8 Risk Allocation in Scope of Services

Given the medium risk level of this Agreement, the Parties acknowledge that the scope of Services and the Service Levels in Article XII have been negotiated to provide a commercially reasonable balance between cost, performance assurance, and risk allocation. The limitations and exclusions in Article IX shall apply to Vanguard’s obligations under this Article II, except as otherwise expressly provided.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Contract Value and Rent Structure

3.1.1 The total estimated Contract Value for the Term is **US $1,122,002**, inclusive of base rent and estimated additional rent (including operating expenses and Taxes recoverable from tenants), but excluding optional services and change orders.

3.1.2 **Base Rent.** Contoso shall pay to Vanguard an annual base rent, payable in equal monthly installments in advance, as follows (illustrative allocation consistent with the Contract Value):

- Year 1 (08/30/2025–08/29/2026): US $260,000 per year (US $21,666.67 per month);  
- Year 2 (08/30/2026–08/29/2027): US $270,400 per year (US $22,533.33 per month);  
- Year 3 (08/30/2027–08/29/2028): US $281,216 per year (US $23,434.67 per month);  
- Year 4 (08/30/2028–05/29/2029): Pro‑rated at an annualized rate of US $292,464 (monthly US $24,372.00) for the remaining nine (9) months.

3.1.3 **Additional Rent.** In addition to Base Rent, Contoso shall pay as “Additional Rent” its proportionate share of operating expenses, building services, and Taxes allocable to the Premises, as reasonably determined by Vanguard and in accordance with standard practice in comparable buildings. The total Contract Value is based on the then‑current estimate of such Additional Rent.

### 3.2 Payment Terms; Net 60

3.2.1 Except as otherwise set forth herein, all amounts due under this Agreement shall be payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) calendar days** after the date of Vanguard’s invoice (“Net 60”), by electronic funds transfer or other mutually agreed payment method.

3.2.2 Base Rent shall be invoiced monthly in advance. Additional Rent and other charges may be invoiced monthly or at such intervals as Vanguard reasonably determines, together with supporting documentation.

### 3.3 Invoices and Disputed Amounts

3.3.1 Vanguard shall issue invoices that itemize Base Rent, Additional Rent, and any other charges, reasonably identifying the period and nature of each charge.

3.3.2 If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion by the applicable due date and shall provide written notice of the disputed amount and the reasons for dispute within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice. The Parties shall promptly attempt to resolve such dispute in good faith in accordance with Article XIV. Any resolved amounts shall be paid within thirty (30) days after resolution.

### 3.4 Late Payment

3.4.1 Any amount not paid when due (and not subject to a good faith dispute) shall accrue interest from the date due until the date paid at the lesser of:  
(a) one percent (1.0%) per month; or  
(b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

3.4.2 Vanguard shall provide Contoso with written notice of any overdue amount and shall allow a cure period of ten (10) Business Days before imposing late payment penalties, except in the case of repeated late payments (more than three (3) times in any rolling twelve (12) month period), in which case Vanguard may assess interest without further grace period.

### 3.5 Operating Expense Reconciliation

3.5.1 Within one hundred twenty (120) days after the end of each calendar year, Vanguard shall provide Contoso with a statement of actual operating expenses and Taxes allocable to the Premises for such year, together with a reconciliation against the amounts paid by Contoso.

3.5.2 If Contoso has overpaid its share of operating expenses and Taxes for such year, Vanguard shall credit such overpayment against the next invoice(s). If Contoso has underpaid, Vanguard shall invoice the shortfall, payable in accordance with Section 3.2.

### 3.6 Taxes

3.6.1 Contoso shall be responsible for payment of all Taxes applicable to the lease of the Premises and Services hereunder, excluding taxes based on Vanguard’s net income or franchise taxes.

3.6.2 If Vanguard is required by law to collect or remit any Taxes on behalf of Contoso, Vanguard shall include such Taxes in its invoices, and Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard for such amounts in accordance with the payment terms herein.

### 3.7 Expenses and Reimbursements

3.7.1 Unless otherwise expressly stated, each Party shall bear its own internal costs incurred in connection with this Agreement.

3.7.2 Any out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred by Vanguard at Contoso’s written request (including, without limitation, overtime HVAC, additional security, or special cleaning) shall be reimbursed by Contoso at Vanguard’s actual cost plus a reasonable administrative fee (if and as specified in Exhibit C).

### 3.8 Security Deposit

3.8.1 Upon execution of this Agreement, Contoso shall pay to Vanguard a Security Deposit equal to two (2) months of the then‑current Base Rent.

3.8.2 Vanguard may apply all or any portion of the Security Deposit to cure any Event of Default by Contoso. If any portion is so applied, Contoso shall replenish the Security Deposit within thirty (30) days after written request from Vanguard.

3.8.3 Provided Contoso is not in default at the end of the Term and has satisfied all obligations hereunder, any remaining Security Deposit (without interest, unless required by law) shall be returned to Contoso within sixty (60) days after the later of (i) expiration or termination of the Agreement, or (ii) surrender of the Premises in accordance with Section 4.8.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Initial Term

4.1.1 The initial Lease Term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until **May 29, 2029**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

### 4.2 Renewal Terms

4.2.1 Upon mutual written agreement of the Parties no later than twelve (12) months prior to the expiration of the then‑current Term, this Agreement may be renewed for one (1) or more additional terms of not less than one (1) year each (“Renewal Term(s)”), on terms and conditions to be negotiated in good faith, including rent, subject to the market conditions at the time.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience at any time after the second anniversary of the Effective Date, upon not less than **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.3.2 In the event of termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall remain liable for all Rent, Taxes, and other amounts accruing through the effective date of termination, together with any agreed early termination fee set forth in Exhibit C, if any. In the event of termination for convenience by Vanguard, no early termination fee shall be payable by Contoso.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to the other Party if the other Party commits a material breach of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or, if the breach is not reasonably curable within thirty (30) days, fails to commence diligent cure within such period and complete cure within a commercially reasonable time) after receipt of written notice specifying the breach.

4.4.2 Notwithstanding the foregoing, repeated non‑payment of Rent not cured within the time prescribed in Section 4.5.1 may be grounds for immediate termination upon written notice if such defaults occur more than three (3) times in any twelve (12) month period.

### 4.5 Events of Default

4.5.1 The following shall constitute an “Event of Default” by Contoso:  
(a) Failure to pay any Rent or other material amount due hereunder within fifteen (15) days after written notice of non‑payment;  
(b) Material breach of any non‑monetary obligation under this Agreement not cured within the applicable cure period;  
(c) Use of the Premises in violation of Section 2.3 that poses an imminent risk of bodily harm or material property damage;  
(d) Assignment or subletting in violation of Section 16.9; or  
(e) Insolvency, bankruptcy, or similar proceedings, to the extent permitted by applicable law.

4.5.2 The following shall constitute an “Event of Default” by Vanguard:  
(a) Failure to provide access to the Premises, other than due to Force Majeure Events or Contoso’s default;  
(b) Material and persistent failure to provide Services in accordance with Article II and Article XII; or  
(c) Material breach of this Agreement not cured within the applicable cure period.

### 4.6 Remedies

4.6.1 Upon an Event of Default by Contoso, and subject to any applicable cure period, Vanguard may, in addition to any other remedies available at law or in equity:  
(a) Terminate this Agreement and recover possession of the Premises;  
(b) Accelerate unpaid Rent due for the remaining Term to the extent permitted by applicable law, subject to an obligation to mitigate damages;  
(c) Draw on the Security Deposit pursuant to Section 3.8; and  
(d) Seek damages as allowed under Article IX, subject to the limitations therein.

4.6.2 Upon an Event of Default by Vanguard, Contoso may, in addition to other remedies available at law or in equity:  
(a) Terminate this Agreement in whole or in part;  
(b) Seek proportionate abatements of Rent in accordance with the Service Level remedies in Article XII; and  
(c) Seek damages as allowed under Article IX.

### 4.7 Survival

4.7.1 The rights and obligations of the Parties under Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions), and any other provision that by its nature should survive, shall survive the expiration or termination of this Agreement.

### 4.8 Surrender of Premises

4.8.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason, Contoso shall vacate and surrender the Premises to Vanguard in substantially the same condition as received, ordinary wear and tear excepted, and free of Contoso’s personal property and trade fixtures, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.8.2 If Contoso fails to surrender the Premises on or before the termination date, Contoso shall be deemed to be a tenant at sufferance and shall be liable for holdover rent at a rate of one hundred fifty percent (150%) of the last applicable monthly Rent, plus all consequential damages reasonably incurred by Vanguard, subject to Article IX.

## ARTICLE XII. SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS

### 12.1 Uptime and Availability

12.1.1 Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that building systems essential for access and operation of the Premises (including elevators, HVAC during business hours, and building access control) achieve an uptime of at least **99.9%** per calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance and Force Majeure Events.

### 12.2 Response and Resolution Times

12.2.1 Vanguard shall respond to Service incidents reported by Contoso within the following target timeframes:  
(a) Critical issues (e.g., complete loss of access, major HVAC failure): initial response within 1 hour;  
(b) High priority issues (e.g., significant disruption to operations): initial response within 4 hours;  
(c) Normal issues (e.g., minor repairs, janitorial concerns): initial response within 1 Business Day.

12.2.2 Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve Service incidents within timelines consistent with their priority and industry standards, recognizing that certain issues may require longer resolution due to complexity or external dependencies.

### 12.3 Service Credits

12.3.1 If Vanguard fails to meet the uptime target in Section 12.1 for a given calendar month (excluding scheduled maintenance and Force Majeure Events), Contoso shall be entitled to a proportionate abatement of Base Rent for that month, calculated as follows:  

Service credit = (Total downtime beyond allowed threshold / total minutes in month) × monthly Base Rent.

12.3.2 Service credits shall be reflected as a credit on the next applicable invoice and shall constitute Contoso’s sole and exclusive remedy for Service Level failures, subject to Vanguard’s material breach rights under Article IV.

### 12.4 Reporting

12.4.1 Vanguard shall, upon reasonable request, provide Contoso with monthly or quarterly reports summarizing Service incidents, uptime statistics, and performance against the Service Levels, together with remedial action plans for persistent issues.
