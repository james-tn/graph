## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Master Agreement and Original SOW.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed **pursuant to**, **under**, and **as a supplement to** the Original SOW (Ref: **SOW‑ZEN‑202403‑200**, **contract_200**) and the Master Agreement (Ref: **MSA‑ZEN‑202403‑197**, **contract_197**). It shall be interpreted in conjunction with, and as part of, the SOW Documents.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified in this Addendum, all terms, conditions, obligations, rights, Service Levels, and other provisions set forth in the Original SOW and Master Agreement remain in full force and effect and apply mutatis mutandis to the Expanded Services and Additional Deliverables.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between this Addendum and the Original SOW, this Addendum shall prevail **solely with respect to the subject matter expressly addressed herein**, and the Original SOW shall otherwise control, consistent with **Section 2.1.3** and **Section 16.3** of the Original SOW and the order‑of‑precedence provisions in the Master Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Expanded Services.**  

2.2.1 Building upon the Project described in **Section 1.3** and **Section 2.2** of the Original SOW, Vendor shall provide Expanded Services that extend (a) the functional scope of the ERP/CRM implementation, (b) the geographic coverage of the solution, (c) the depth of analytics, security, and compliance capabilities, and (d) the breadth and duration of Managed Services.

2.2.2 The Expanded Services shall include, without limitation:

  a. **Advanced Analytics Enablement** across ERP, CRM, data warehouse, and customer portal ecosystems, leveraging the integration foundation described in **Section 2.3.2 (Phase 2 – Design and Build)** of the Original SOW and Deliverables D3–D5 (Configuration Workbook, Integration Design Specifications, Data Migration Strategy);  

  b. **Multi‑Region Rollout and Localization**, extending the solution beyond the initial two (2) core business regions referenced under **Section 2.3.3 (Phase 3 – Testing, UAT and Go‑Live)** of the Original SOW to up to **six (6) additional countries/regions**;  

  c. **Optimization and Continuous Improvement** through multiple Optimization Waves post‑stabilization, following Phase 4 (“Stabilization & Transition to Managed Services”) and Phase 5 (“Managed Services”) in **Section 2.3** of the Original SOW;  

  d. **Program Governance Services**, establishing a cross‑functional governance structure aligning with the high‑risk nature of the Project described in the Recitals and **Article IX** of the Original SOW; and  

  e. **Enhanced Managed Services and Premium Support**, including extended coverage, proactive monitoring, and elevated Service Levels that supplement the commitments in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of both the Master Agreement and the Original SOW.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Expanded Services.**  

2.3.1 **Advanced Analytics Enablement.**  

Vendor shall design, configure, and deploy a unified analytics and reporting framework that consumes data from the ERP, CRM, HRIS, Data Warehouse, Legacy Billing, Identity Management, and Customer Portal systems referenced in **Section 2.3.2** of the Original SOW. Specifically, Vendor shall:

  a. Develop **enterprise data models** that align with the Solution Blueprint (Deliverable D1) and Integration Design Specifications (Deliverable D4);  

  b. Implement **at least twenty‑five (25) standard dashboards** and **fifty (50) KPIs** across finance, sales, customer service, procurement, and inventory domains;  

  c. Configure role‑based access controls to analytics artifacts in compliance with the data protection measures set forth in **Article XI** of the Master Agreement and **Article XI** of the Original SOW;  

  d. Provide **training materials and workshops** for business users and analysts, supplementing Deliverable D10 (Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Pack); and  

  e. Deliver technical and functional documentation enabling ongoing support by Vendor’s Managed Services team, to be integrated into Deliverables D9 and D11 under the Original SOW.

2.3.2 **Multi‑Region Rollout and Localization.**  

Vendor shall extend the solution to additional regions as follows:

  a. For each new country/region (up to six (6) regions), develop a **Localization Package** that includes tax rules, chart of accounts variations, statutory reporting, local language variants (where required), and local regulatory compliance configuration;  

  b. Plan and execute **country‑specific deployments** following a life cycle analogous to Phases 2–4 of the Original SOW, including design, build, testing, cutover, Go‑Live, and hypercare;  

  c. Coordinate with Client’s in‑country teams to perform local UAT and regulatory sign‑offs, following the acceptance procedures referenced in **Section 2.6(e)** of the Original SOW and the Acceptance Criteria concept in **Section 1.9**;  

  d. Produce supplemental documentation for each Localization Package (e.g., localized SOPs and runbooks) to be appended to Deliverables D9 and D10 under the Original SOW; and  

  e. Maintain alignment with the **Project Plan** defined in **Section 1.7** of the Original SOW, as updated from time to time via Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Master Agreement and this Addendum.

2.3.3 **Optimization Waves and Continuous Improvement.**  

Following completion of the initial Go‑Live and stabilization phases described in **Sections 2.3.3 and 2.3.4** of the Original SOW, Vendor shall conduct up to **four (4) Optimization Waves** during the Addendum Term, focused on performance, usability, and functional enhancements. Each Optimization Wave shall:

  a. Begin with a **diagnostic assessment** leveraging data from Deliverable D11 (Monthly Service Performance Reports) and D12 (Semi‑annual Optimization & Roadmap Reports);  

  b. Prioritize improvement initiatives jointly with Client’s stakeholders via Program Governance structures (Section 2.3.4), including but not limited to query tuning, workflow optimization, and user experience refinements;  

  c. Implement prioritized changes following the change management processes in **Article XIII** of the Original SOW and this Addendum, including any necessary Change Orders for scope or fee adjustments; and  

  d. Conclude with a **Wave Completion Report** and updated roadmap, which shall be treated as an Additional Deliverable under **Section 2.4** of this Addendum.

2.3.4 **Program Governance Services.**  

Vendor shall provide Program Governance Services to ensure effective oversight of the expanded, multi‑year program. Such services shall include:

  a. Establishment of a **joint steering committee** with representatives from Client and Vendor, meeting at least quarterly to review progress, risks, issues, and financials;  

  b. Preparation and presentation of **program status reports**, including KPI dashboards, risk heatmaps, and decision logs;  

  c. Maintenance of a multi‑year **Program Roadmap** aligned with Client’s strategic objectives and incorporating the Optimization Waves and multi‑region rollout schedule;  

  d. Facilitation of escalation and dispute prevention processes consistent with **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement; and  

  e. Coordination with Client’s internal audit, compliance, and risk functions as reasonably requested, subject to the confidentiality terms of **Article V** of the Original SOW and this Addendum.

2.3.5 **Enhanced Managed Services and Premium Support.**  

In addition to the Managed Services described in **Sections 1.11, 2.2(e), 2.3.5, and Article XII** of the Original SOW, Vendor shall:

  a. Extend support coverage to a **Premium Support Window** of 24x7 for Severity 1 and 2 Incidents and **06:00–22:00 Singapore time on Business Days** for Severity 3 and 4 Incidents, as further defined in **Section 12.3** of this Addendum;  

  b. Provide **proactive monitoring and alerting** across application, integration, and database layers, with thresholds and rules agreed in the updated Service Performance Reports (D11);  

  c. Provide **capacity management and performance planning** services at least annually, with recommendations documented in enhanced Optimization & Roadmap Reports (D12);  

  d. Expand the Managed Services team size and skill mix to support additional regions and analytics capabilities, consistent with the performance standards in **Section 2.5** of the Original SOW; and  

  e. Participate in periodic **service review meetings** (monthly at a minimum) with Client to review SLA performance, incident trends, root‑cause analyses, and upcoming changes.

2.4 **Additional Deliverables.**  

Without limiting **Section 2.4 of the Original SOW**, Vendor shall produce at least the following Additional Deliverables under this Addendum (which shall constitute “Deliverables” and “Work Product” as described in **Sections 1.5, 2.4, and 6.4** of the Original SOW and **Article VI** of the Master Agreement):

- **AD1 – Analytics Solution Design & Data Model Documentation** (building on Deliverables D1 and D4);  
- **AD2 – Analytics Dashboards & KPI Catalog** (at least 25 dashboards and 50 KPIs);  
- **AD3 – Localization Design & Compliance Packs** for each new region (up to 6 regions);  
- **AD4 – Country‑Specific SOP Addenda and Runbooks** (to supplement D9 and D10);  
- **AD5 – Optimization Wave Plans and Completion Reports** (for each Optimization Wave);  
- **AD6 – Program Governance Charter and Program Roadmap**;  
- **AD7 – Enhanced Service Performance Report Templates** incorporating new SLAs and metrics under this Addendum;  
- **AD8 – Annual Capacity and Performance Assessment Reports**;  
- **AD9 – Updated Incident Handling and Escalation Procedures** aligned with Premium Support; and  
- **AD10 – Data Protection and Security Hardening Summary** reflecting enhancements implemented under Advanced Analytics Enablement and Localization.

Acceptance of Additional Deliverables shall follow the acceptance mechanisms, including Acceptance Criteria, set forth in **Section 1.9, Section 2.3, and Section 2.6(e)** of the Original SOW, as supplemented by any Project Plan or Change Order agreed under this Addendum.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

Vendor shall perform the Expanded Services:

  a. In accordance with the performance standards in **Section 2.5** of the Original SOW and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Agreement;  

  b. Using personnel with skills commensurate with the expanded multi‑region, analytics‑enabled, high‑risk Project profile; and  

  c. With adherence to the enhanced Service Levels described in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities for Expanded Services.**  

In addition to Client’s responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Original SOW and Section 2.6 of the Master Agreement, Client shall:

  a. Provide timely access to regional business stakeholders, local legal and compliance resources for each new region;  

  b. Ensure availability of subject matter experts for analytics use cases, KPI definition, and validation;  

  c. Facilitate collection, cleansing, and provision of any new or incremental data sets required for Advanced Analytics Enablement, in accordance with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Original SOW and Master Agreement;  

  d. Provide necessary infrastructure, environments, and connectivity for extended monitoring and analytics capabilities; and  

  e. Participate actively in the Program Governance structures, including attendance at steering committee meetings and timely decision‑making.

Vendor’s obligations under this Addendum are expressly conditioned on Client’s timely fulfillment of the foregoing responsibilities, consistent with **Section 2.6** of the Original SOW. Delays attributable to Client may result in schedule extensions and additional Fees through the Change Order process provided in **Article XIII** of the Original SOW and this Addendum.

TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term; Relationship to SOW Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date (8 September 2024) and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, the Original SOW, or the Master Agreement, shall continue in full force and effect until **27 March 2028** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

The Parties may mutually agree in writing to further extend the Managed Services and Premium Support beyond the Addendum Expiration Date on terms and conditions to be agreed, consistent with **Section 4.2** of the Original SOW and Section 4.2 of the Master Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party, in accordance with **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Original SOW and Master Agreement.

Any early termination for convenience initiated by Client may result in additional wind‑down and transition Fees, including reasonable costs associated with ramping down Program Governance Services, Optimization Waves, and Premium Support.

Termination for cause, including for material breach or non‑payment, shall be governed by **Section 4.4** of the Original SOW and **Section 4.4** of the Master Agreement. Any such termination may apply specifically to this Addendum, to the Original SOW as a whole, or to the Master Agreement and all SOWs, as specified in the termination notice.

4.5.1 If the Master Agreement is terminated or expires, this Addendum and the Original SOW shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue this Addendum under substantially similar terms.

- **Severity 1 (Critical):**  
  - Target response time: **15 minutes**;  
  - Target restoration/workaround time: **2 hours**.  

- **Severity 2 (High):**  
  - Target response time: **1 hour**;  
  - Target restoration/workaround time: **8 hours**.  

- **Severity 3 (Medium):**  
  - Target response time: **1 Business Hour** within the Premium Support Window;  
  - Target restoration/workaround time: **2 Business Days**.  

- **Severity 4 (Low):**  
  - Target response time: **1 Business Day**;  
  - Target restoration time: as reasonably practicable.

12.3.2 The **Premium Support Window** shall be:  
  - 24x7 coverage for Severity 1 and 2 Incidents; and  
  - **06:00–22:00 Singapore time on Business Days** for Severity 3 and 4 Incidents.  

These commitments supersede **Section 12.3.2** of the Original SOW for systems under Premium Support, while the original Service Window continues to apply to other systems.

12.4.1 For systems covered by Premium Support, if Vendor fails to meet the uptime or response commitments above, Client may be eligible for enhanced service credits as follows:

  a. **Uptime Credits:**  
     - For each full 0.05% below 99.95% monthly uptime down to 99.70%: service credit equal to 1.5% of the applicable monthly Premium Support Fees;  
     - For uptime below 99.70% in a given month: service credit equal to 30% of the applicable monthly Premium Support Fees.

  b. **Response Time Credits:**  
     - For each Severity 1 or 2 Incident where response time exceeds the target by more than 100%, Vendor shall credit Client an amount equal to 1% of the applicable monthly Premium Support Fees, capped at 10% per month for response time failures.

12.4.2 Service credits shall be applied against future Fees and shall constitute Client’s sole and exclusive remedy for failure to meet the Service Levels described in this Article XII and in the Original SOW, consistent with **Section 12.4** of the Original SOW and Section 12.4 of the Master Agreement.
