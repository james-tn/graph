# AMENDMENT TO PURCHASE AGREEMENT  
**Contract Reference No.: AMD-HOR-202507-730**

This **Amendment to Purchase Agreement** (this “**Amendment**”), Contract Reference No. **AMD-HOR-202507-730**, is made and entered into as of **July 09, 2026** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**”), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a [●] corporation, having its principal place of business at **1000 Fifth Avenue, Suite 2100, New York, NY 10018, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**”, the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”), and  

- **Horizon Partners LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company, having its principal place of business at **200 Park Avenue, 25th Floor, New York, NY 10166, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Horizon**”, the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Horizon are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Amendment **amends and supplements** that certain Purchase Agreement, Contract Reference No. **PUR-HOR-202507-662** (internal identifier **contract_662**) dated **July 21, 2025** (the “**Purchase Agreement**” or “**Parent Agreement**”), by and between the same Parties.

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the **Purchase Agreement**, Contract Reference No. **PUR-HOR-202507-662** (File: **contract_662**), effective as of **July 21, 2025** (the “Effective Date” as defined in Section 1.10 thereof), pursuant to which Horizon agreed to provide certain professional **Services** and **Deliverables** (as those terms are defined in **Article I** of the Purchase Agreement), including system integration services, configuration of a cloud-based management platform, supply of hardware units, and ongoing support and maintenance, all as more fully set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Deliverables)** of the Purchase Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Purchase Agreement established a total estimated contract value of **Four Hundred Twenty-Five Thousand Two Hundred Eighty-Five U.S. Dollars (US$425,285)** (the “Contract Value” as defined in **Section 3.1** of the Purchase Agreement), together with detailed **Fees and Payment Terms** under **Article III**, including **Net 60** Payment Terms (as defined in **Section 1.14** and **Section 3.4** of the Purchase Agreement);

WHEREAS, pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** and **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Purchase Agreement, the Parties desire to modify certain commercial terms relating to **quantities** and **pricing** of Services and hardware Deliverables, and to establish an incremental contract value and associated scope for additional units and services under the existing framework of the Purchase Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that this Amendment **modifies quantities or pricing** under the Purchase Agreement dated July 21, 2025 (Ref: **PUR-HOR-202507-662**), including adjustments to the Contract Value, Fee structure, and related payment milestones, while leaving in full force and effect all other terms and conditions of the Purchase Agreement not expressly amended herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties further desire to clarify the relationship of this Amendment to the Purchase Agreement, including that this Amendment is **entered into pursuant to** and is **subject to** the terms and conditions of the Purchase Agreement, and that capitalized terms used herein but not otherwise defined shall have the meanings ascribed to them in the Purchase Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, agreements, representations, warranties, and conditions contained herein, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES UNDER THE AMENDMENT

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.** This Amendment is entered into pursuant to, and is subject to, the terms of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly modified herein, the scope, obligations, and responsibilities of the Parties set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables. In the event of any conflict between this Amendment and the Parent Agreement with respect to the subject matter hereof, the terms of this Amendment shall prevail solely as to that subject matter.

2.2 **Overview of Incremental Services.** Under this Amendment, Horizon shall provide to Contoso the following Incremental Services, in addition to those already specified in **Sections 2.2.1–2.2.7** of the Parent Agreement:

  2.2.1 **Expanded Assessment and Design Services.**  
  (a) Horizon shall conduct a supplemental assessment of Contoso’s expanded infrastructure footprint, including newly acquired regional data centers and additional cloud environments (multi-region deployments).  
  (b) Horizon shall update and extend the solution architecture originally contemplated under **Section 2.2.1(b)** and **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement, to account for redundancy, high availability across at least three (3) availability zones, and additional integration endpoints.  
  (c) Horizon shall deliver an **Updated Solution Architecture Document** (“Updated Architecture”) and an **Incremental Technical Design Specification** (“Incremental TDS”), collectively the “Incremental Design Deliverables,” each constituting Incremental Deliverables under this Amendment.

  2.2.2 **Additional Implementation and Integration Services.**  
  (a) Building on **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement, Horizon shall implement additional integration modules to connect Contoso’s newly deployed CRM instance, expanded analytics platform, and additional third-party data sources.  
  (b) Horizon shall develop and configure at least **fifteen (15)** new integration workflows and message transformation pipelines, each documented in an **Integration Runbook Supplement**.  
  (c) Horizon shall provide extended data migration support for incremental data sets, including delta migration processes and cut-over support for two additional go-lives in distinct regions.

  2.2.3 **Configuration, Customization, and Automation Enhancements.**  
  (a) Horizon shall design and configure enhanced order processing workflows, client onboarding flows, and support-ticket triage rules, consistent with **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement, but covering additional business units.  
  (b) Horizon shall implement a **centralized automation framework**, including reusable scripts, templates, and orchestration jobs to standardize deployments across multiple environments.  
  (c) Horizon shall create at least **ten (10)** additional custom dashboards and analytics views, tailored for new business stakeholders identified by Contoso.

  2.2.4 **Incremental Hardware Units.**  
  (a) In addition to the hardware contemplated under **Section 2.2.4** and **Section 2.3(g)** of the Parent Agreement, Horizon shall supply the following Incremental Hardware Units to Contoso, on a pass-through or original supplier basis as applicable:  
   - Up to **120** additional network appliances and security gateways;  
   - Up to **300** edge devices and sensors;  
   - Up to **40** preconfigured servers or virtual appliance images; and  
   - Associated power supplies, cables, and mounting kits.  
  (b) The delivery, title, and risk of loss for such Incremental Hardware Units shall be governed by **Section 2.8 (Delivery, Title, and Risk of Loss (Hardware))** of the Parent Agreement, including FOB Destination (Incoterms 2020) to Contoso-designated locations.

  2.2.5 **Extended Testing and Acceptance Support.**  
  (a) Horizon shall prepare and execute an **Incremental Test Plan**, aligned with **Section 2.2.5** and **Section 2.6 (Acceptance Criteria and Procedure)** of the Parent Agreement, covering unit tests, system integration tests, performance/load tests, and user acceptance tests for the Incremental Services.  
  (b) Contoso’s acceptance or rejection of Incremental Deliverables shall follow the same process described in **Sections 2.6.1–2.6.4** of the Parent Agreement.  
  (c) Horizon shall support at least two (2) full UAT cycles and one (1) regression cycle for each major Incremental Deliverable grouping.

  2.2.6 **Incremental Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  
  (a) In addition to **Section 2.2.6** of the Parent Agreement, Horizon shall deliver supplemental training for newly onboarded Contoso personnel and extended teams in additional regions, including up to:  
   - Ten (10) administrator/technical training sessions; and  
   - Fifteen (15) end-user training sessions.  
  (b) Horizon shall update and provide revised training materials and system configuration documentation to reflect the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables.  
  (c) Training sessions may be conducted remotely or onsite, as mutually agreed.

  2.2.7 **Expanded Support and Maintenance Scope.**  
  (a) Pursuant to **Section 2.2.7** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, Horizon shall extend its support and maintenance obligations to cover the Incremental Deliverables and Incremental Hardware Units.  
  (b) Such support shall be provided at the Service Levels specified in the Parent Agreement and further clarified in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.3 **Timeline and Milestones for Incremental Services.**  
The Parties agree that, consistent with **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, detailed timelines, milestones, and completion dates for the Incremental Services and delivery of Incremental Deliverables shall be set forth in one or more written project schedules or SOW annexes to this Amendment (each, an “**Amendment Schedule**”). In the absence of a specific Amendment Schedule, the following high-level milestones shall apply:

  (a) **Incremental Design Deliverables** completed within ninety (90) days following the Amendment Effective Date;  
  (b) **Core Incremental Integration Workflows** implemented and available for UAT within one hundred eighty (180) days following the Amendment Effective Date;  
  (c) **Incremental Hardware Units** delivered within commercially reasonable lead times, not to exceed one hundred twenty (120) days from issuance of the applicable purchase release, subject to supplier availability and Force Majeure Events;  
  (d) **Training and Knowledge Transfer** to be completed within sixty (60) days following acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the relevant Incremental Deliverables.

Time frames are estimates, and Horizon shall not be liable for delays attributable to Contoso, third-party suppliers, or any Force Majeure Event, consistent with **Section 2.5** and **Article XV** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Responsibilities of the Parties.**  
The responsibilities described in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement (Horizon Responsibilities and Contoso Responsibilities) shall apply in full to the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables. Without limiting the foregoing:

  (a) Horizon shall provide appropriately trained personnel, a designated project manager, and subject-matter experts to oversee the execution of the Incremental Services, and shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the timelines set forth in the Amendment Schedules.  
  (b) Contoso shall provide timely access to its expanded systems, facilities, and personnel, including new data centers and cloud subscriptions, and shall designate project stakeholders to review and approve the Incremental Deliverables.

2.5 **Changes to Incremental Scope.**  
Any change to the Incremental Services, Incremental Deliverables, timelines, or related Fees under this Amendment shall be addressed via the Change Management process in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement. For clarity, a Change Order that expressly references this Amendment and Contract Reference No. AMD-HOR-202507-730 shall be deemed an amendment to this Amendment and, where applicable, to the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Incremental Contract Value.**  
In addition to the original Contract Value of **US$425,285** set forth in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that the **Incremental Contract Value** for the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables under this Amendment shall be **Two Million Three Hundred Ninety-Nine Thousand Three Hundred Eighteen U.S. Dollars (US$2,399,318)**. The Parties acknowledge that this Incremental Contract Value is an estimate based on the agreed scope and may be adjusted in accordance with duly authorized Change Orders.

3.2 **Fee Structure under the Amendment.**  
Unless otherwise specified in an Amendment Schedule, Fees under this Amendment shall be comprised of:

  (a) **Incremental Fixed Implementation Fees** for the expanded integration, configuration, and deployment Services, currently estimated at **US$1,380,000**;  
  (b) **Incremental Hardware Purchase Fees** for the Incremental Hardware Units described in **Section 2.2.4**, currently estimated at **US$740,000**, based on unit pricing agreed by the Parties and subject to adjustment based on actual quantities purchased;  
  (c) **Incremental Training and Knowledge Transfer Fees** estimated at **US$129,318**; and  
  (d) **Incremental Support and Maintenance Fees** for the expanded support scope for the Incremental Deliverables and Incremental Hardware Units, estimated at **US$150,000** over the Amendment Term, subject to the same annual uplift (if any) and invoicing structure specified in the relevant SOWs under the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Sample Incremental Fee Schedule.**  
For planning purposes, and subject to finalization in one or more Amendment Schedules, the Parties anticipate the following notional fee schedule under this Amendment:

  (a) **Incremental Implementation and Integration Services**:  
   - Design and architecture updates: US$280,000  
   - Integration development and configuration: US$1,100,000  
   Total: **US$1,380,000**

  (b) **Incremental Hardware Deliverables**:  
   - Network appliances (up to 120 units): US$420,000  
   - Edge devices and sensors (up to 300 units): US$210,000  
   - Preconfigured servers / virtual appliances (up to 40 units): US$110,000  
   Total: **US$740,000**

  (c) **Incremental Training and Knowledge Transfer**:  
   - Administrator/technical training: US$79,318  
   - End-user training: US$50,000  
   Total: **US$129,318**

  (d) **Incremental Support and Maintenance (Amendment Term)**:  
   - Annual or monthly recurring support Fees: **US$150,000** in the aggregate.

3.4 **Payment Terms; Invoicing.**  
The Payment Terms for all Fees under this Amendment shall remain **Net 60**, consistent with **Sections 1.14 and 3.4** of the Parent Agreement. In particular:

  (a) Horizon shall invoice Contoso according to milestone completion, as documented in the Amendment Schedules, or, where no milestones are specified, monthly in arrears based on Services performed and Deliverables delivered under this Amendment.  
  (b) Contoso shall pay all valid and undisputed invoices within sixty (60) days of receipt.  
  (c) The dispute resolution process for invoices in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any disputed amounts under this Amendment.

3.5 **Milestone-Based Billing.**  
Where milestone-based billing is utilized:

  (a) Horizon may invoice Contoso upon completion and acceptance (or deemed acceptance, per **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement) of each milestone or group of Incremental Deliverables;  
  (b) The Parties will identify specific percentage allocations of the Incremental Contract Value to each milestone in the applicable Amendment Schedules; and  
  (c) The provisions of **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis.

3.6 **Late Payment; Interest.**  
Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest from the due date until paid in full at the rate specified in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement (lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate allowed by Applicable Law). The accrual or payment of interest shall not limit Horizon’s rights under **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent Agreement (suspension or termination for non-payment).

3.7 **Taxes.**  
Consistent with **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement, the Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for all applicable Taxes, except for taxes based on Horizon’s net income, property, or employees. If Horizon must collect or pay any such Taxes on behalf of Contoso, it shall invoice Contoso for such Taxes, and Contoso shall pay such amounts in accordance with this Article III.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  
Out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Horizon in connection with the Incremental Services (e.g., travel, lodging, and related expenses) shall be reimbursed in accordance with **Section 3.9** of the Parent Agreement, provided such expenses are pre-approved in writing by Contoso.

3.9 **No Set-Off.**  
The no set-off obligations in **Section 3.10** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all payments due under this Amendment, except as required by Applicable Law or expressly permitted under the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**  
The term of this Amendment (the “**Amendment Term**”) shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **December 05, 2027** (the “**Amendment Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **No Automatic Renewal of Amendment.**  
Any extension or renewal of the Incremental Services or Incremental Deliverables beyond the Amendment Expiration Date shall require a written amendment or new agreement executed by both Parties pursuant to **Section 16.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
In addition to Contoso’s rights in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree:

  (a) Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience upon at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Horizon.  
  (b) Horizon may not terminate this Amendment for convenience, except with Contoso’s prior written consent (consistent with the Parent Agreement).  
  (c) Upon such termination for convenience, Contoso shall pay Horizon (i) all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred under this Amendment through the effective date of termination, and (ii) any noncancelable or committed costs incurred in reliance on this Amendment, in line with **Section 4.5.2** of the Parent Agreement, but calculated solely with respect to the Incremental Contract Value.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause if the other Party materially breaches this Amendment or the Parent Agreement with respect to the Incremental Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason:

  (a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, without separate notice, except to the extent the Parties expressly agree in writing that certain provisions or obligations of this Amendment shall survive;  
  (b) The Parties’ rights and obligations with respect to accrued Fees, expenses, Confidential Information, Intellectual Property Rights, and other surviving provisions shall be determined in accordance with **Sections 4.5 and 4.6** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XVI** of this Amendment.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Amendment.**  
Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment (whether or not the Parent Agreement remains in effect):

  (a) Each Party shall fulfill its obligations with respect to fees accrued and services performed under this Amendment prior to the effective date of termination;  
  (b) Horizon shall cooperate with Contoso to provide reasonable transition assistance related to the Incremental Services, in accordance with **Section 4.5.4 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement, on a time-and-materials basis;  
  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information related solely to the Incremental Services, in accordance with **Section 4.5.3** and **Article V** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Amendment which by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, payment obligations accrued but unpaid, Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, and XVI) shall survive termination or expiration of this Amendment, in addition to any corresponding survival provisions in the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IX  
### LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Modified Liability Regime for Amendment.**  
Notwithstanding **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement, and in view of the **Medium** risk designation of the Incremental scope under this Amendment, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to Claims arising under or relating to this Amendment:

  (a) The aggregate liability of either Party to the other Party for all Claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment, whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, shall be **capped at five (5) times the Fees paid or payable by Contoso to Horizon under this Amendment during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the Claim**; and  
  (b) This cap is intended to modify and supersede the “no overall monetary cap” approach in **Section 9.1** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to the Incremental Services and Incremental Deliverables under this Amendment.

9.2 **Exclusion of Consequential Damages.**  
To the maximum extent permitted by Applicable Law, and notwithstanding **Section 9.2** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that **neither Party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, punitive, or consequential damages** (including loss of profits, revenue, or business) arising out of or in connection with this Amendment, except to the extent such damages are specifically recoverable under an indemnification obligation for third-party Claims and are actually awarded to a third party.

9.3 **Exceptions to Limitations.**  
The limitations set forth in **Sections 9.1 and 9.2** of this Amendment shall not apply to:

  (a) A Party’s liability for death or personal injury caused by its gross negligence or willful misconduct;  
  (b) A Party’s liability for fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation;
