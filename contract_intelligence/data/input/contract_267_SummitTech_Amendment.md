## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

This Amendment (“**Amendment**”), **Contract Reference No. AMD-SUM-202406-267**, is entered into as of **3 February 2025** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**”) by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises**, a company incorporated and existing under the laws of the United Kingdom, with its principal place of business at **125 Finsbury Pavement, London EC2A 1NQ, United Kingdom** (“**Contoso**”, the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”); and  

- **Summit Tech Limited**, a company incorporated and existing under the laws of the United Kingdom, with its registered office at **48 Kingsway, London WC2B 6EY, United Kingdom** (“**Summit Tech**”, the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Summit Tech may each be referred to herein as a “**Party**” and together as the “**Parties**”.

**Parent Contract Reference:**  
This Amendment relates to and modifies that certain **Statement of Work**, Contract Reference No. **SOW-SUM-202406-236**, file identifier **contract_236**, entered into by the Parties as of **20 June 2024** (the “**Parent SOW**” or the “**Parent Agreement**”).

**Background**

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent SOW, Ref: **SOW-SUM-202406-236** (file identifier **contract_236**), effective as of 20 June 2024, under which the Vendor agreed to provide to the Client certain ERP and CRM implementation, integration, and managed support services, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent SOW;

WHEREAS, under **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent SOW, the Parties agreed that the total aggregate amount payable thereunder shall not exceed **USD $1,661,342** (Section 3.1, the “Contract Value”) and that payment for implementation phases would be tied to specified **Milestones** (Section 3.3) and ongoing support fees of USD $18,000 per month (Section 3.2.2);

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to (a) extend and expand the scope of the Services initially contemplated under **Article II** of the Parent SOW to include additional optimisation, advanced analytics, and automation workstreams, and (b) adjust and supplement certain commercial and legal terms as they apply to such extended work, including a new contract value for this Amendment of **USD $250,097**, with **Net 30** payment terms, all as set forth herein;

WHEREAS, the Parent SOW contemplates in **Section 3.1** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** that modifications to scope, timelines, and Contract Value must be agreed in a written amendment executed by both Parties, and **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent SOW requires that any amendment expressly reference **SOW-SUM-202406-236**;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend that this Amendment be read in conjunction with, and as a supplement to, the Parent SOW, and that, save as expressly modified herein, all provisions of the Parent SOW shall remain in full force and effect;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises set forth herein, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment modifies the Statement of Work dated **20 June 2024** (Ref: **SOW-SUM-202406-236**, file identifier **contract_236**). The Amendment Services are an extension of, and are to be performed in conjunction with, the Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent SOW.  

2.1.2 For clarity, the project phases enumerated in **Section 2.2** of the Parent SOW (Phases 1–7) remain in full force and effect. The Optimisation Workstreams and Enhanced Support Services described in this Article II are intended to:  
  (a) refine and optimise the solution delivered under **Phases 1–6** of the Parent SOW;  
  (b) expand the functional scope and performance of the ERP and CRM environments originally described in **Section 2.1** (Overview of Services) of the Parent SOW; and  
  (c) enhance the ongoing managed support model described in **Section 2.2.7 (Phase 7 – Ongoing Support and Maintenance)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent SOW.

2.2 **Overview of Amendment Services.**  

2.2.1 The Vendor shall provide, as part of the Amendment Services, a comprehensive set of optimisation, analytics, and automation services relating to the Client’s ERP and CRM environments that were implemented or are being implemented pursuant to the Parent SOW.  

2.2.2 The primary objectives of the Amendment Services are to:  
  (a) improve system performance, scalability, and resilience of the ERP and CRM platforms;  
  (b) introduce advanced analytics, dashboards, and reporting capabilities leveraging data generated by the systems described in **Section 2.2.3 (Implementation, Configuration, and Integration)** and **Section 2.2.4 (Testing and User Acceptance)** of the Parent SOW;  
  (c) automate high‑volume, manual processes originally identified in **Phase 1 – Discovery and Requirements Gathering** under **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent SOW but deferred from initial implementation;  
  (d) align configuration and integration patterns with best practices documented in the Solution Design Document and Configuration Documentation referenced in **Sections 2.3.2 and 2.3.5** of the Parent SOW; and  
  (e) refine the support operating model, escalation paths, and knowledge artefacts underpinning **Phase 7 – Ongoing Support and Maintenance**.

2.3 **Optimisation Workstreams.**  

The Amendment Services shall be organised into the following Optimisation Workstreams:

2.3.1 **Workstream A – Performance and Scalability Optimisation.**  
  (a) Conduct a performance assessment of the ERP and CRM environments, including review of transaction throughput, background processing, integration latency, and database performance.  
  (b) Analyse historical incident and problem records from the managed support service reports referenced in **Section 2.2.7(c)** and **Section 2.3.11** of the Parent SOW to identify recurring bottlenecks.  
  (c) Propose and implement configuration and infrastructure changes (within the scope of the systems under Vendor management) to improve response times and reduce critical incident frequency.  
  (d) Update the **Configuration Documentation and Runbooks** (as defined in **Section 2.3.5** of the Parent SOW) to reflect performance tuning adjustments (the “Updated Runbooks”).

2.3.2 **Workstream B – Advanced Analytics & Reporting.**  
  (a) Design and deliver an **Analytics Design Blueprint** that defines key performance indicators, data sources, data models, and visualisation standards for the Client’s ERP and CRM reporting requirements.  
  (b) Implement up to an agreed number (to be confirmed via PCO) of dashboards and standard reports utilising existing data models and integration flows defined under **Phase 3 – Implementation, Configuration, and Integration** in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent SOW.  
  (c) Ensure analytics solutions comply with the security controls, user roles, and access management structures implemented under **Section 2.2.3(d)** of the Parent SOW.  
  (d) Provide data lineage and governance documentation that aligns with the Specifications defined in the Requirements Specification Document and Solution Design Document referenced in **Section 2.3.1 and 2.3.2** of the Parent SOW.

2.3.3 **Workstream C – Process Automation.**  
  (a) Identify, in collaboration with Client stakeholders, up to a mutually agreed number of high‑value business processes for automation (“Automation Candidates”), prioritising those related to order‑to‑cash, procure‑to‑pay, and customer service workflows documented during **Phase 1** of the Parent SOW.  
  (b) Design and implement automation scripts, workflows, or bots (the “Automation Scripts”) to streamline the Automation Candidates within the existing ERP/CRM platforms and integrations.  
  (c) Integrate Automation Scripts into the incident and change management processes referenced in **Phase 7 – Ongoing Support and Maintenance** of the Parent SOW.  
  (d) Provide training to relevant Client users and support personnel on the use, monitoring, and first‑line troubleshooting of the Automation Scripts.

2.3.4 **Workstream D – Support Model Enhancement.**  
  (a) Review the existing Service Levels and incident management processes described in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent SOW, including **Section 12.3 (Incident Response and Resolution Times)**.  
  (b) Propose refinements to severity definitions, escalation rules, and communication protocols to reduce mean time to resolution and improve service transparency (the “Enhanced Support Services”).  
  (c) Implement a revised reporting format for the **Monthly Service and Performance Reports** referenced in **Section 2.3.11** of the Parent SOW, including additional metrics on automation availability, dashboard usage, and defect trends.  
  (d) Conduct quarterly **Value‑Realisation Reviews** during the Amendment Term to assess the impact of the Amendment Services and identify further optimisation opportunities.

2.4 **Amendment Deliverables.**  

Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, the Vendor shall produce and deliver to the Client the following Amendment Deliverables, each subject to the acceptance process in **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW (which shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Amendment):

2.4.1 Optimisation Assessment Report (covering performance and scalability);  
2.4.2 Analytics Design Blueprint;  
2.4.3 Implemented Dashboards and Standard Reports (as itemised in a PCO);  
2.4.4 Automation Candidate Register;  
2.4.5 Automation Scripts & Configuration Documentation;  
2.4.6 Updated Configuration Documentation and Updated Runbooks;  
2.4.7 Revised Support Operating Model Document (including updated severity and escalation definitions);  
2.4.8 Enhanced Monthly Service and Performance Report Templates;  
2.4.9 Value‑Realisation Review Reports (quarterly); and  
2.4.10 Any other artefacts expressly agreed in writing by the Parties in connection with the Amendment Services.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones under this Amendment.**  

2.5.1 The Parties acknowledge the importance of timely completion as emphasised in **Section 2.4 (Project Timeline and Milestones)** of the Parent SOW. For the Amendment Services, the following high-level milestones shall apply, subject to adjustment via PCO:

- **Amendment Milestone A:** Completion of Workstream A performance assessment and delivery of Optimisation Assessment Report – within ninety (90) days of the Amendment Effective Date.  
- **Amendment Milestone B:** Completion of Analytics Design Blueprint and initial dashboards – within one hundred eighty (180) days of the Amendment Effective Date.  
- **Amendment Milestone C:** Completion of initial Automation Scripts for agreed Automation Candidates – within two hundred forty (240) days of the Amendment Effective Date.  
- **Amendment Milestone D:** Implementation of Enhanced Support Services and revised reporting – within three hundred (300) days of the Amendment Effective Date.  
- **Amendment Milestone E:** Completion of first annual Value‑Realisation Review – within three hundred sixty‑five (365) days of the Amendment Effective Date.

2.5.2 Each Amendment Milestone shall be deemed achieved upon the Client’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the associated Amendment Deliverables in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent SOW.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

The Vendor shall perform the Amendment Services:

2.6.1 Consistent with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent SOW, with due skill, care, and diligence, in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with good industry practice for similar services;  

2.6.2 In compliance with all **Applicable Laws** and the Client’s reasonable written policies provided to the Vendor, including the Client’s information security and data protection policies referenced in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent SOW;  

2.6.3 Using personnel who are suitably qualified, experienced, and trained in ERP/CRM optimisation, data analytics, and process automation; and  

2.6.4 In accordance with the Service Levels and Performance Metrics specified in **Article XII** of this Amendment and **Article XII** of the Parent SOW.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities.**  

The Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW shall apply equally to the Amendment Services, including the obligation to provide timely access to relevant systems, data, and personnel, and to review and provide feedback on Amendment Deliverables within ten (10) Business Days of receipt, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total aggregate amount payable by the Client to the Vendor for all Amendment Services and Amendment Deliverables under this Amendment shall not exceed **USD $250,097** (the “**Amendment Contract Value**”), unless otherwise agreed in a further written amendment executed by both Parties.

3.1.2 For clarity, the Amendment Contract Value is **in addition to** the “Contract Value (Not‑to‑Exceed): USD $1,661,342” specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent SOW. Nothing in this Amendment shall increase the not‑to‑exceed amount of the Parent SOW; rather, this Amendment establishes a separate, additional not‑to‑exceed amount for the additional scope defined herein.

3.2 **Fee Structure under this Amendment.**  

3.2.1 **Fixed Fees for Optimisation Workstreams.** A fixed‑fee amount of **USD $180,000** for the planning, design, and implementation of Workstreams A, B, and C, as described in **Section 2.3**, inclusive of all labour, materials, and overhead costs, payable according to the milestones in **Section 3.3**.

3.2.2 **Enhanced Support Service Fees.** An additional recurring monthly fee of **USD $4,000** (above and beyond the **USD $18,000** monthly support fee specified in **Section 3.2.2** of the Parent SOW) for the Enhanced Support Services, from the date of Amendment Milestone D through the **Amendment Term**, provided that such additional monthly fees shall form part of the Amendment Contract Value.

3.2.3 **Time-and-Materials Enhancements.** Any additional enhancements, automation initiatives, or analytics extensions outside the agreed scope of this Amendment Services shall be performed on a time‑and‑materials basis (“**T&M**”) at the Vendor’s standard rates, consistent with **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent SOW, as may be updated in accordance with that section. All such T&M work must be authorised via a PCO under **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Amendment, and any associated fees shall count toward the Amendment Contract Value unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.3 **Payment Milestones for Fixed Fees.**  

The fixed fees for the Optimisation Workstreams (USD $180,000) shall be invoiced upon achievement of the following Amendment Milestones:

- **Amendment Milestone A Completion:** 25% of fixed fees – **USD $45,000**;  
- **Amendment Milestone B Completion:** 30% of fixed fees – **USD $54,000**;  
- **Amendment Milestone C Completion:** 25% of fixed fees – **USD $45,000**;  
- **Amendment Milestone D Completion:** 20% of fixed fees – **USD $36,000**.  

Each milestone shall be deemed achieved upon the Client’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the associated Amendment Deliverables in accordance with **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW.

3.4 **Invoice and Payment Terms.**  

3.4.1 The Vendor shall issue invoices for:  
  (a) fixed fees upon achievement of the applicable Amendment Milestones;  
  (b) Enhanced Support Service fees monthly in arrears; and  
  (c) any T&M work authorised under a PCO monthly in arrears.  

3.4.2 All invoices issued under this Amendment shall clearly reference (i) this **Amendment Contract Reference No. AMD-SUM-202406-267**, and (ii) the Parent SOW reference **SOW-SUM-202406-236 (contract_236)**, and shall include reasonable supporting documentation, consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent SOW.

3.4.3 Notwithstanding **Section 3.4.3 (Net 45)** of the Parent SOW, the Client shall pay all undisputed amounts relating to the Amendment Services within **thirty (30) days** of the date of a valid invoice (“**Net 30**”). For the avoidance of doubt, the Net 30 term in this Amendment shall apply only to invoices issued under **AMD-SUM-202406-267** and shall not alter the Net 45 term applicable to invoices issued solely under the Parent SOW.

3.4.4 If the Client disputes any portion of an invoice, the dispute procedure in **Section 3.4.4** of the Parent SOW shall apply mutatis mutandis.

3.5 **Late Payment.**  

Late payment interest shall accrue on undisputed overdue amounts relating to the Amendment Services in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the Parent SOW (lesser of 1% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law), from the due date until the date of actual payment. Late payment interest shall not apply to amounts disputed in good faith.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.6.1 The fee structure in this Amendment is exclusive of reasonable out‑of‑pocket travel and related expenses incurred by the Vendor in connection with on‑site work requested by the Client, which shall be reimbursed in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the Parent SOW.

3.6.2 All reimbursable expenses related to the Amendment Services shall be pre‑approved in writing by the Client and shall be invoiced with appropriate receipts or documentation.

3.7 **Taxes.**  

Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent SOW. All fees under this Amendment are exclusive of VAT or similar taxes, which shall be invoiced and paid where required by Applicable Law.
