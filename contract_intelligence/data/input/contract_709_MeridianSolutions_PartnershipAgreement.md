## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Services

2.1.1 Meridian shall provide to Contoso a comprehensive suite of professional and managed services designed to support Contoso’s enterprise technology environment, including but not limited to:

- Technology consulting and strategic advisory services;  
- Systems analysis, architecture, and design;  
- Software configuration and customization;  
- System and data integration services;  
- Implementation and deployment services;  
- Project and program management;  
- Ongoing support, monitoring, and maintenance services;  
- Training and knowledge transfer; and  
- Such other related services as may be agreed by the Parties in a SOW.

2.1.2 The specific scope of Services and Deliverables for each project or initiative shall be described in one or more SOWs executed under this Agreement. Each SOW shall reference this Agreement by its contract reference number **AGR-MER-202511-709** and shall include, as applicable: scope, Deliverables, acceptance criteria, timelines, milestones, Fees, and any project-specific Service Levels.

### 2.2 Deliverables

2.2.1 Meridian shall develop, produce, or otherwise provide the Deliverables identified in each SOW. Such Deliverables may include, without limitation:

- Requirements and design documentation;  
- Solution architecture documents;  
- Configuration workbooks and parameter settings;  
- Custom or configuration scripts, integrations, and interfaces;  
- Test plans, test cases, and test results;  
- Training materials, user guides, and runbooks;  
- Operational and support documentation; and  
- Status reports and executive summaries.

2.2.2 Each Deliverable shall conform in all material respects to the specifications, requirements, and acceptance criteria set forth in the applicable SOW. Meridian shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with generally accepted industry standards and practices, and using personnel with appropriate skills, qualifications, and experience.

### 2.3 Project Management and Governance

2.3.1 Each Party shall designate a project manager (the **“Project Manager”**) to act as the primary point of contact for coordination and management of the Services. The Project Managers shall meet (in person, telephonically, or via videoconference) at least monthly, or as otherwise agreed, to review progress, issues, risks, and upcoming milestones.

2.3.2 Meridian shall provide regular written status reports at intervals specified in the applicable SOW, which shall include, at a minimum: progress against milestones, work completed, upcoming tasks, risk and issue logs, and any material deviations from the agreed schedule or scope.

2.3.3 In the event of any material risk to schedule, budget, or quality, Meridian shall promptly notify Contoso’s Project Manager and shall propose a remediation plan, including any necessary Change Orders pursuant to Article XIII.

### 2.4 Performance Standards

2.4.1 Meridian shall perform the Services:  
(a) in accordance with this Agreement, the applicable SOW, and any mutually agreed project plan;  
(b) in compliance with Applicable Law; and  
(c) using reasonable care and skill, and consistent with generally accepted professional standards for similar services.

2.4.2 Meridian acknowledges that the Services and Deliverables may be integrated into Contoso’s production environment and that timely performance is critical. Meridian shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet all timelines and milestones set forth in the SOW. If Meridian becomes aware that it will not meet a milestone or deadline, it shall provide written notice to Contoso promptly, explaining the reasons and proposing a recovery plan.

2.4.3 Where Service Levels concerning availability, response time, or resolution time are specified in Article XII or any SOW, Meridian shall meet or exceed such Service Levels. Failure to do so may result in service credits or other remedies as set forth in Article XII.

### 2.5 Cooperation and Client Responsibilities

2.5.1 Contoso shall provide Meridian with timely access to:

- Relevant systems, networks, environments, and test data (subject to security controls);  
- Appropriate facilities and infrastructure (if on-site work is required);  
- Knowledgeable personnel for requirements gathering, design reviews, and approvals; and  
- Necessary Client Materials and information, to the extent reasonably required to perform the Services.

2.5.2 Contoso acknowledges that Meridian’s ability to perform the Services in accordance with this Agreement may depend on the timely performance of Contoso’s responsibilities and the accuracy and completeness of information supplied by Contoso. Meridian shall be relieved from its obligations to the extent its failure to perform is directly caused by Contoso’s failure to fulfill its responsibilities, provided that Meridian has notified Contoso in writing of such issues.

2.5.3 Any delays or additional costs resulting from Contoso’s failure to perform its obligations may be subject to a Change Order under Article XIII, including adjustments to timelines and Fees.

2.6.1 Meridian may use qualified subcontractors or Affiliates to perform portions of the Services, provided that: (a) Meridian remains fully responsible for the acts and omissions of such subcontractors as if they were Meridian’s own; and (b) any subcontractor with access to Confidential Information or Personal Data is bound by written obligations of confidentiality and data protection no less protective than those set forth in this Agreement.

2.6.2 Meridian shall not subcontract any material or strategic portion of the Services to an offshore or high-risk jurisdiction without Contoso’s prior written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed.

### 2.7 Acceptance of Deliverables

2.7.1 Each SOW shall specify the acceptance procedures applicable to the Deliverables, including any testing, review, and acceptance criteria. Unless otherwise set forth in the SOW:

(a) Meridian shall notify Contoso in writing when a Deliverable is ready for review (the **“Delivery Notice”**);  
(b) Contoso shall have thirty (30) days from receipt of the Delivery Notice (the **“Review Period”**) to test and review the Deliverable; and  
(c) Contoso shall either (i) provide written acceptance, or (ii) provide a written rejection specifying in reasonable detail any material non-conformities with the applicable specifications.

2.7.2 If Contoso fails to provide written acceptance or rejection within the Review Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted as of the last day of the Review Period, provided that Meridian has clearly identified the start of the Review Period.

2.7.3 In the event of rejection, Meridian shall, at no additional cost to Contoso, promptly correct the identified non-conformities and resubmit the Deliverable. The Parties shall then follow the acceptance process again for such corrected Deliverable. If after two (2) resubmissions the Deliverable still materially fails to conform, Contoso may exercise the remedies available under this Agreement, including termination for cause under Section 4.3.

3.3.1 All Fees are exclusive of any applicable sales, use, value-added, goods and services, or other similar taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for paying any such taxes imposed on the Services or Deliverables, but not for taxes based on Meridian’s net income, payroll, or property.

3.4.1 Contoso shall reimburse Meridian for reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including travel, lodging, and meals, in accordance with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies communicated to Meridian.

3.4.2 Meridian shall provide appropriate documentation and receipts for all reimbursable expenses. Contoso may refuse reimbursement for expenses that are not pre-approved or not in compliance with Contoso’s policies.

### 3.5 Late Payment and Disputed Amounts

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of (a) one percent (1%) per month, or (b) the maximum rate allowed by Applicable Law, calculated from the due date until the date payment is received.

3.5.2 If Contoso disputes any invoice in good faith, it shall notify Meridian in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the disputed invoice, specifying the amount and basis of the dispute. Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion of the invoice in accordance with Section 3.2, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve the disputed portion as promptly as practicable.

3.5.3 Meridian shall not suspend or terminate the Services for non-payment unless: (a) amounts are more than thirty (30) days past due; (b) such amounts are not subject to a good faith dispute as described above; and (c) Meridian has provided Contoso with at least ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice of its intent to suspend or terminate.

### 3.6 No Withholding; Setoff

3.6.1 Except for any amounts disputed in good faith or as expressly permitted in this Agreement, Contoso shall not withhold or set off any amount due to Meridian.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

4.1.1 This Agreement shall commence on the **Effective Date** and shall remain in full force and effect until **November 17, 2029** (the **“Initial Term”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.1.2 Thereafter, this Agreement may be renewed for additional periods as mutually agreed in writing by the Parties (each a **“Renewal Term”** and together with the Initial Term, the **“Term”**).

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement, or any individual SOW, for convenience and without cause upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.2.2 Upon termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall pay Meridian for: (a) all Services performed and Deliverables delivered and accepted up to the effective date of termination; (b) any non-cancellable commitments and reasonable wind-down costs incurred by Meridian; and (c) any agreed termination fees, if expressly specified in the applicable SOW.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement, or any affected SOW, upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement or such SOW and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or, if the breach is not reasonably curable within thirty (30) days, fails to commence and diligently pursue cure) after receiving written notice specifying the breach.

4.3.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) becomes insolvent or is unable to pay its debts as they become due;  
(b) files or has filed against it a petition in bankruptcy that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days;  
(c) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors; or  
(d) ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

(a) Meridian shall cease performing the Services (except as necessary for any agreed transition assistance under Section 4.4.3);  
(b) All outstanding and undisputed invoices shall become immediately due and payable;  
(c) Contoso shall pay Meridian for all Services performed, Deliverables delivered and accepted, and authorized expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, subject to any credits, offsets, or refunds owed to Contoso; and  
(d) Each Party shall return or, at the other Party’s written direction, securely destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information and Client Materials, subject to any retention required under Applicable Law or Section 11.5 (Data Retention).

4.4.2 Termination or expiration shall not relieve either Party of any obligations that expressly or by their nature are intended to survive, including but not limited to Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI.

4.4.3 At Contoso’s request, Meridian shall provide reasonable transition assistance for up to ninety (90) days following termination or expiration, at Meridian’s then-current T&M rates (unless otherwise agreed), to facilitate the orderly transfer of the Services to Contoso or a third party.
