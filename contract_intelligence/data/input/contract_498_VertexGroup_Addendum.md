## PREAMBLE

This ADDENDUM TO SOFTWARE LICENSE AGREEMENT (this **“Addendum”**), reference number **ADD-VER-202506-498**, is made and entered into as of **December 10, 2025** (the **“Addendum Effective Date”**), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises**, a [jurisdiction of formation] corporation, with its principal place of business at **[Contoso Address], USA** (hereinafter referred to as **“Contoso”**, the **“Client”** or the **“Company”**); and

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, Vertex and Contoso entered into the **Software License Agreement** referenced as **SLA-VER-202506-421 (contract_421)**, effective as of June 22, 2025, pursuant to which Vertex grants Contoso certain licenses to the **Software** and provides related **Services**, all as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Licensed Software)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement contemplates that the Parties may enter into additional **Statements of Work (“SOWs”)** and amendments to expand the scope of licensed **Software**, **modules**, and **users**, with Fees governed by **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, **Section 6.3 (License Grant to Software)** of the Parent Agreement provides that Contoso’s license to the Software is subject to applicable license metrics (including named users and other usage parameters) as set forth in applicable SOWs and related documentation;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to **add additional licensed users and additional Software modules** (including enhanced **Analytics and Reporting**, extended **Order Management and Billing** functionality, and supplemental **System Administration and Security** features as described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement), and to document such additional licensing under the terms of this Addendum;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement established an initial **Contract Value** of **US$2,408,736** (as set forth in the Recitals and **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement) and provided for **Net 60** payment terms (**Section 3.3**), and the Parties now wish to establish a **separate, incremental contract value** and revised payment terms for the additional licenses contemplated herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties further desire to clarify the relationship of this Addendum to the Parent Agreement, and to confirm that, except as expressly modified herein, the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and govern the Parties’ rights and obligations with respect to the additional users and modules licensed under this Addendum;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, agreements, and undertakings contained in this Addendum and in the Parent Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE III. SCOPE OF SERVICES AND ADDITIONAL LICENSES

3.1 **Overview.**  
Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services and Licensed Software)** and **Section 6.3 (License Grant to Software)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex shall provide Contoso with:

  (a) additional licenses to use the Software for the **Additional Users**;  

  (b) access to and use of the **Additional Modules** described in this Article III; and  

  (c) the **Addendum Services** required to configure, implement, and support such Additional Users and Additional Modules throughout the Addendum Term.

All such rights and services are provided subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

3.2 **Description of Additional Users.**  
(a) **Quantity and Type.** As of the Addendum Effective Date, Vertex grants to Contoso an additional **1,250 named users** of the Software, consisting of (i) 900 standard business users with access to CRM, Order Management and Billing, and Analytics and Reporting; (ii) 250 power/administrative users with elevated access to advanced reporting, configuration, and system administration; and (iii) 100 read-only or limited users with reporting and dashboard viewing capabilities only.

(b) **Role-Based Access.** The Additional Users shall be provisioned in accordance with the role-based access controls and security configurations described in **Section 2.2(d) (System Administration and Security)** and **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall define appropriate user roles, and Vertex shall assist in configuring those roles in the Software.

(c) **Usage Metrics.** The Additional Users shall be licensed on a **named-user basis**. Contoso shall not circumvent or modify user counts or usage limits (see **Section 6.4(f)** of the Parent Agreement). Replacement of named users (e.g., for personnel turnover) shall be permitted, provided that Contoso does not exceed the total number of licensed Additional Users and maintains accurate records of such replacements.

3.3 **Description of Additional Modules.**  
Under this Addendum, Vertex grants to Contoso licenses to the following Additional Modules of the Software, which expand upon and build on the modules described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Advanced Analytics and Reporting Suite.** Includes predictive analytics, machine learning–based forecasting, configurable multi-dimensional dashboards, and self-service report authoring. This extends the **Analytics and Reporting** module described in **Section 2.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Enhanced Order Management and Billing Automation.** Adds automated credit checks, multi-currency billing, contract-based recurring invoicing, and complex discounting workflows, extending the **Order Management and Billing** module described in **Section 2.2(b)**.

  (c) **Enterprise Security and Compliance Pack.** Provides enhanced **System Administration and Security** capabilities from **Section 2.2(d)** of the Parent Agreement, including advanced audit trails, data loss prevention configurations, configurable password policies, single sign-on (SSO) integrations, and privileged access monitoring.

  (d) **Customer Portal Extension.** A limited external-facing module enabling Contoso’s end customers to place orders, view invoices, and track service requests. Use is restricted to interaction with Contoso’s internal instance of the Software and subject to the same **Confidentiality** and **Data Protection** obligations as internal use.

3.4 **Addendum Services.**  
Vertex shall provide, at a minimum, the following Addendum Services, which are governed by **Article II (Scope of Services and Licensed Software)** and **Article II.3 (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Implementation and Configuration.**  
  - Configuration of Additional Modules within Contoso’s existing production and test environments;  
  - Extension of existing workflows and business rules (as referenced in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement) to support advanced billing and analytics;  
  - Set-up of role-based permissions for Additional Users and the Customer Portal Extension.

  (b) **Integration Services.**  
  Consistent with **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex shall integrate Additional Modules with Contoso’s designated ERP, HRIS, and financial systems, including:  
  - Data feeds for billing transactions into Contoso’s general ledger;  
  - Synchronization of user identities with Contoso’s enterprise directory and SSO provider;  
  - Scheduled batch interfaces for customer master data.

  (c) **Data Migration and Mapping.**  
  Vertex shall assist, as described in **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement, with mapping and migration of historical billing and analytic data from Contoso’s legacy systems into the new modules, including validation of record counts and basic consistency checks. Contoso remains responsible for data accuracy and legal compliance with respect to Client Data.

  (d) **Training.**  
  In line with **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement, Vertex shall conduct up to **ten (10) remote training sessions** and **three (3) on-site workshops** for user groups designated by Contoso, focusing on advanced analytics features, order and billing automation, and security administration.

  (e) **Support and Maintenance.**  
  The Additional Users and Additional Modules shall be supported under the same Maintenance Services framework as set forth in **Section 2.3(e)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement. Any additional support entitlements or response enhancements will be stated in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

3.5 **Deliverables and Milestones.**  
The following Deliverables (as that term is defined in **Section 1.6** and **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement) shall be provided, with acceptance governed by **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement unless otherwise specified herein:

  (a) **Environment Configuration Documents** (test and production) – due within 45 days of the Addendum Effective Date.

  (b) **Integration Design and Mapping Specifications** – due within 60 days of the Addendum Effective Date, including interface specifications for each integrated system.

  (c) **Data Migration Runbooks and Validation Reports** – delivered for each migration cycle, with Contoso having ten (10) Business Days to review and provide written acceptance or deficiency notice, consistent with **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement.

  (d) **Training Materials and User Guides** – provided at least 5 Business Days before each training session.

3.6 **Performance Standards.**  
Vertex shall perform the Addendum Services:

  (a) with the same standards of due care, skill, and diligence described in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) in accordance with the timelines set forth in this Article III, subject to delays attributable to **Force Majeure Events** or Contoso’s failure to meet its responsibilities as described in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (c) in compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including data protection laws as specified in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

3.7 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  
In addition to the responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  (a) timely provide Vertex with user lists, role definitions, and security group structures for Additional Users;  

  (b) provide Vertex with access to relevant subject matter experts and system owners for integrated systems;  

  (c) ensure that any third-party licenses or consents necessary for integration are obtained; and  

  (d) promptly review and respond to Deliverables, test results, and configuration proposals.

## ARTICLE IV. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The **Addendum Contract Value** for the Additional Users, Additional Modules, and Addendum Services described herein is **US$2,165,687**. This amount is incremental to, and separate from, the **Contract Value of US$2,408,736** stated in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Fee Structure.**  
The Fees under this Addendum shall consist of:

  (a) **License Fees for Additional Users.**  
  A total of **US$1,250,000** for the Additional Users, allocated as follows:  
  - Standard business users (900): US$750,000 total (US$833.33 per user, blended over the Addendum Term);  
  - Power/administrative users (250): US$400,000 total;  
  - Read-only users (100): US$100,000 total.

  (b) **License Fees for Additional Modules.**  
  A total of **US$615,687** for the Additional Modules, allocated across the Advanced Analytics Suite, Enhanced Order Management and Billing Automation, Enterprise Security and Compliance Pack, and Customer Portal Extension.

  (c) **Professional Services Fees.**  
  A total of **US$300,000** for Addendum Services (implementation, integration, data migration, and training), billed on a fixed-fee basis tied to milestones in **Section 4.3**.

4.3 **Payment Milestones.**  
Unless otherwise agreed in a written Change Order, the Addendum Contract Value shall be invoiced as follows:

  (a) **25% (US$541,421.75)** upon the Addendum Effective Date;  

  (b) **25% (US$541,421.75)** upon completion of environment configuration and delivery of the Environment Configuration Documents;  

  (c) **25% (US$541,421.75)** upon completion of integration build and successful completion of user acceptance testing for integrations;  

  (d) **25% (US$541,421.75)** upon final production go-live of Additional Modules and provisioning of at least 80% of Additional Users.

4.4 **Payment Terms.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, which provides for **Net 60**, the Parties agree that all undisputed Fees under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) days (“Net 45”)** from the date of Vertex’s invoice. All other invoicing and payment mechanics, including dispute procedures, shall follow **Section 3.3 and Section 3.4 (Invoice Disputes)** of the Parent Agreement, except as to the payment deadline.

4.5 **Late Payments.**  
Section 3.5 (Late Payments) of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any amounts due under this Addendum. Interest shall accrue on undisputed overdue amounts at the rates specified therein, and Vertex may suspend Addendum Services and access to Additional Modules for invoices that are more than thirty (30) days past due after notice, consistent with that Section.

4.6 **Taxes.**  
All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes, as described in **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso remains responsible for all applicable Taxes.

4.7 **Expenses.**  
Travel and related out-of-pocket expenses for on-site services shall be reimbursed by Contoso in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement. Vertex shall obtain Contoso’s pre-approval for such expenses when required by Contoso’s travel and expense policy.

## ARTICLE V. TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 **Addendum Term.**  
The Addendum Term shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (December 10, 2025)** and continue until **December 17, 2026**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article V and **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.

5.2 **Renewal.**  
The Addendum Term may be renewed for additional one (1) year periods by written agreement of the Parties, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement. Any renewal pricing for Additional Users and Additional Modules shall be agreed in writing at least sixty (60) days prior to the end of the then-current Addendum Term.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement (which permits termination for convenience upon sixty (60) days’ notice), due to the **high risk** nature of this Addendum and the up-front investments required by Vertex, the Parties agree that either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party. Such termination for convenience shall not entitle Contoso to any refund of Fees already paid or committed under executed milestones, and Contoso shall remain liable for all Fees for licenses and Services provided up to the effective date of termination, as well as any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Vertex on Contoso’s behalf.

5.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement, including for material breach not cured within the applicable cure period or insolvency-related events.

5.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
In the event the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason:

  (a) **Automatic Termination.** This Addendum shall **automatically terminate** as of the effective date of termination of the Parent Agreement, without further action by either Party.

  (b) **Wind-Down.** The rights and obligations described in **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Additional Users and Additional Modules, including cessation of license rights, return or destruction of Software and Documentation, and provision of Client Data.

  (c) **Surviving Obligations.** The provisions of the Parent Agreement that survive termination (as listed in **Section 4.7**) shall apply to this Addendum, including Articles I, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI, and associated payment obligations.

5.6 **Effect of Termination of Addendum Only.**  
If this Addendum is terminated while the Parent Agreement remains in effect, then:

  (a) all licenses granted hereunder for Additional Users and Additional Modules shall cease as of the effective date of such termination;  

  (b) the Existing Licenses under the Parent Agreement shall remain in effect, provided they are otherwise valid and not terminated;  

  (c) Contoso shall promptly cease use of and, at Vertex’s direction, remove or disable access to Additional Modules; and  

  (d) the Parties shall cooperate to ensure an orderly wind-down, including extraction of Client Data associated with Additional Modules in accordance with **Section 4.6(e)** of the Parent Agreement.
