## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relation to Parent SOW and Consulting Agreement.**  
2.1.1 This Work Order is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent SOW and the Consulting Agreement, consistent with **Section 2.1 (Relation to Parent Agreement)** and **Section 16.1 (Reference to Master/Parent Agreement)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.3 (Statements of Work)** of the Consulting Agreement.

2.1.2 The WO Services described herein are part of the broader "Services" as defined in **Section 1.17** and **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Consulting Agreement, and further elaborated in **Article II** of the Parent SOW. Nothing in this Work Order shall limit the general scope of Services described in the Parent SOW, except as expressly stated and agreed in writing in accordance with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent SOW.

2.1.3 For clarity, this Work Order **adds scope** to the Parent SOW within the Program, specifically with respect to (a) Extended Regional Rollout, (b) Advanced Analytics Enablement, and (c) Post‑Implementation Optimization, each of which build upon and rely on the Workstreams A–D defined in **Sections 2.3.1–2.3.4** of the Parent SOW.

2.2 **Overview of WO Services.**  
Under this Work Order, the Vendor shall provide the following categories of WO Services:

(a) **Extended Regional Rollout Support**, covering detailed localization, sequencing, and execution support for the EMEA Pilot, North America, APAC, and additional regions, consistent with and expanding upon the Regional Rollout concept set forth in **Section 2.6.3** of the Parent SOW;

(b) **Advanced Analytics Enablement**, including the design of dashboards, KPI automation, and benefits tracking mechanisms derived from Deliverables B4 (Process Performance KPI Definitions & Templates) and D5 (Adoption & Readiness Dashboard) of the Parent SOW;

(c) **Post‑Implementation Optimization and Continuous Improvement**, consisting of periodic reviews, optimization recommendations, training refreshers, and change reinforcement activities, building upon Deliverables D3–D6 under **Workstream D – Change Management & Training** of the Parent SOW; and

(d) **Program Management and Governance Support**, as a dedicated WO Governance Committee function to manage the above activities in line with **Section 2.8 (Governance and Key Personnel)** and the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW.

2.3 **Detailed Scope – Extended Regional Rollout.**  
2.3.1 Atlas shall provide the following Extended Regional Rollout Services:

(a) Develop region‑specific rollout plans for each of EMEA, North America, APAC, and any additional agreed regions, refining and detailing the phased approach outlined in **Section 2.6.3(a)–(c)** of the Parent SOW;

(b) For each region, prepare a **Regional Rollout Dossier** including: localization requirements, regulatory considerations, language and training localization, stakeholder maps, and sequencing of process and system changes based on the **Multi‑Year Digital Transformation Roadmap** (Deliverable A3 of the Parent SOW);

(c) Coordinate with Client’s internal project manager (per **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent SOW) and third‑party implementation partners to align the regional rollout plans with the **Program Governance Framework & Charter** (Deliverable C1) and **Detailed Implementation Workplans** (Deliverable C2) defined under **Workstream C** of the Parent SOW;

(d) Provide advisory support and on‑site or remote presence (as mutually agreed) during key regional milestones such as pilot go‑live, stabilization period, and post‑go‑live hypercare; and

(e) Prepare **Regional Lessons Learned & Stabilization Reports** capturing key findings, risk mitigations, and recommendations for subsequent regions.

2.3.2 **Key WO Deliverables – Extended Regional Rollout** shall include, at a minimum:

- **R1:** EMEA Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier  
- **R2:** North America Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier  
- **R3:** APAC Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier  
- **R4:** Regional Lessons Learned & Stabilization Reports (per region)  

2.4 **Detailed Scope – Advanced Analytics Enablement.**  
2.4.1 Building on the KPI templates and dashboards described in Deliverables B4 and D5 of the Parent SOW, Atlas shall:

(a) Design a unified **Program Analytics Framework** aligning process KPIs, adoption metrics, and benefits realization indicators with the roadmap defined in **Workstream A (Strategy & Roadmap)** and the governance model in **Workstream C** of the Parent SOW;

(b) Define data sources, integration points (on an advisory basis), and logical data models supporting automated reporting, consistent with the Vendor’s role as an advisor under **Section 2.9(d)** of the Parent SOW (clarifying that the scope does not include hands‑on configuration, coding, or implementation unless expressly agreed);

(c) Develop detailed **KPI Implementation Guides** for Client and its third‑party technology vendors, including mapping of KPIs to source systems, business rules, and calculation logic;

(d) Configure, or oversee configuration by Client and its vendors (as agreed in writing), of up to [●] standard dashboards and reports in Client’s selected analytics tooling, consistent with **Section 6.2 (Vendor IP; Tools and Methodologies)** of the Consulting Agreement; and

(e) Provide training and knowledge transfer sessions to the Client’s analytics and PMO teams in respect of the Program Analytics Framework and dashboards.

2.4.2 **Key WO Deliverables – Advanced Analytics Enablement** shall include:

- **AAn1:** Program Analytics Framework & Data Model Specification  
- **AAn2:** KPI Implementation Guides (by domain and region)  
- **AAn3:** Standard Dashboard & Report Catalogue  
- **AAn4:** Analytics Training & Knowledge Transfer Documentation  

2.5 **Detailed Scope – Post‑Implementation Optimization.**  
2.5.1 For the duration of the WO Term, and following initial go‑live in each region, Atlas shall provide Post‑Implementation Optimization Services:

(a) Conduct quarterly **Post‑Implementation Review Workshops** to evaluate performance against KPIs, adoption metrics, and governance effectiveness;

(b) Produce **Optimization Recommendations Reports** proposing adjustments to process designs (linked to Deliverable B3 of the Parent SOW), governance mechanisms (linked to Deliverable C1), and change management activities (linked to Deliverables D1–D6);

(c) Develop and refresh training materials and conduct periodic **Refresher Training Sessions**, particularly for newly on‑boarded employees or for major process adjustments, consistent with **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent SOW;

(d) Advise on integration of lessons learned into future phases of the Program, including any new SOWs or work orders executed under **Section 4.2 (Renewal or Extension)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** thereof; and

(e) Collaborate with the Client to refine the **Adoption & Readiness Dashboard** (Deliverable D5 of the Parent SOW) based on actual usage and feedback.

2.5.2 **Key WO Deliverables – Post‑Implementation Optimization** shall include:

- **O1:** Quarterly Post‑Implementation Review & Optimization Reports (per region)  
- **O2:** Updated Training Curriculum & Refresher Materials  
- **O3:** Updated Adoption & Readiness Dashboard Specifications  

2.6 **Timeline and WO Milestones.**  
2.6.1 The anticipated high‑level timeline for this Work Order shall run from the WO Effective Date through the WO Expiration Date and shall be coordinated with the phases outlined in **Section 2.6 (Timeline, Milestones, and Regional Rollout)** of the Parent SOW.

2.6.2 **Illustrative WO Milestones and Target Dates:**

- **WO Milestone 1:** Delivery and Client acceptance of EMEA Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier (Deliverable R1) – Target: **June 30, 2026**  
- **WO Milestone 2:** Delivery and Client acceptance of North America Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier (Deliverable R2) and first Analytics Framework (Deliverable AAn1) – Target: **December 31, 2026**  
- **WO Milestone 3:** Completion of APAC Regional Rollout Plan & Dossier (Deliverable R3) and KPI Implementation Guides (Deliverable AAn2) – Target: **June 30, 2027**  
- **WO Milestone 4:** Delivery of first two Quarterly Post‑Implementation Review & Optimization Reports (Deliverable O1) across at least two regions – Target: **December 31, 2027**  
- **WO Milestone 5:** Completion of Standard Dashboard Catalogue (Deliverable AAn3) and initial Refresher Training Program (Deliverables O2 and AAn4) – Target: **June 30, 2028**

2.6.3 The Parties acknowledge that target dates are illustrative and shall be finalized and, if necessary, adjusted via Change Orders in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW and this Work Order.

2.7 **Performance Standards and WO Governance.**  
2.7.1 The Vendor shall perform the WO Services in accordance with **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Consulting Agreement and **Section 2.7 (Performance Standards and Service Levels)** of the Parent SOW, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Work Order.

2.7.2 The Parties shall extend the **Project Governance Committee** described in **Section 2.8** of the Parent SOW by establishing a **WO Governance Committee**, consisting of at least two (2) representatives from each Party with authority to make decisions in respect of this Work Order. The WO Governance Committee shall:

(a) Meet at least monthly during active implementation phases and at least quarterly during stabilization phases;  
(b) Review WO Milestones, WO Deliverables, risks, issues, and proposed Change Requests relating to this Work Order; and  
(c) Coordinate with the broader Program governance to ensure consistency and avoid duplication.

2.8 **WO Specific Assumptions and Dependencies.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.9** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.7** of the Consulting Agreement, this Work Order is based on the following assumptions and dependencies:

(a) The core Deliverables identified in **Sections 2.3.1–2.3.4** of the Parent SOW (including A1–A4, B1–B4, C1–C5, D1–D6) will be completed substantially in accordance with the timelines set out in **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent SOW;

(b) Client will provide timely access to regional stakeholders, data sources, and third‑party vendors necessary for Extended Regional Rollout and Advanced Analytics Enablement;

(c) Client will maintain a stable internal project manager and PMO capability consistent with **Section 2.5.1(b)** of the Parent SOW, with adequate authority to approve Deliverables and Milestones;

(d) Vendor will not be responsible for hands‑on software configuration, coding, or integration unless expressly documented in a Change Order, consistent with **Section 2.9(d)** of the Parent SOW; and

(e) Any material change in scope, regional sequencing, or analytics tool selection will be addressed via the Change Management process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Work Order.

2.9 **Deliverables; Acceptance.**  
All WO Deliverables shall be subject to the acceptance process set out in **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent SOW and **Sections 2.5.2–2.5.4** of the Consulting Agreement, including the Client’s ten (10) Business Day review period, the Vendor’s obligation to correct notified deficiencies, and deemed acceptance provisions, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in a Change Order.
