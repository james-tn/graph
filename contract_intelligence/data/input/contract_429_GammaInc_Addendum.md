## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **General Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and constitutes an amendment and supplement thereto for purposes of **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement. The scope described herein constitutes additional **Products**, **Services**, and **Deliverables** within the meaning of **Article II** of the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified in this Addendum, all provisions of **Article II (Scope of Products, Services, and Work)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Additional Products, Additional Services, and Additional Deliverables described herein, including but not limited to the obligations regarding **Delivery and Installation (Section 2.2.3)**, **Implementation and Configuration Services (Section 2.3.1)**, **Migration and Cutover Services (Section 2.3.2)**, **Training and Knowledge Transfer (Section 2.3.3)**, **Support and Maintenance Services (Section 2.3.4)**, **Deliverables (Section 2.4)**, and **Performance Standards (Section 2.6)**.

2.2 **Edge Infrastructure Expansion.**  

2.2.1 **Scope.** Gamma shall design, supply, deliver, install, and configure a distributed edge computing infrastructure at up to **fifteen (15) Contoso branch and regional sites** within the continental United States. This infrastructure shall extend the integrated hardware and software platform implemented under the Parent Agreement and shall be architected to interoperate with the core data center environment previously deployed.

2.2.2 **Additional Products – Edge Nodes and Networking.** The Edge Infrastructure Expansion shall include, at a minimum:

a. Up to **45 edge compute nodes** (rack-mounted or micro‑form-factor servers), each equipped with enterprise-grade processors, redundant power supplies, and local solid-state storage consistent with the Specifications;

b. Up to **30 edge storage appliances**, with replication capabilities to Contoso’s central data centers provisioned under the Parent Agreement’s Core Hardware Systems;

c. Up to **30 edge network switches**, supporting secure VLAN segmentation and integration with Contoso’s WAN, as contemplated by **Section 2.2.1 (Core Hardware Systems)** of the Parent Agreement;

d. Embedded firmware and system software for each edge compute node and storage appliance, to be licensed to Contoso in accordance with **Section 6.4.2 (License to Embedded Software and Firmware)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.3 **Site Preparation and Installation.** Gamma shall:

a. Conduct remote and/or on-site surveys to validate power, cooling, rack space, and network connectivity at each designated site;

b. Coordinate shipping and physical delivery to each site, on a **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid)** basis consistent with **Section 2.2.3 (Delivery and Installation)** of the Parent Agreement;

c. Provide qualified personnel to rack, stack, cable, and power-on the Additional Products, in coordination with Contoso’s IT and facilities teams, maintaining adherence to Contoso’s safety and security policies;

d. Perform initial firmware and software configuration, including OS installation, patching, and application of security baselines as documented in the Specifications.

2.3 **Advanced Monitoring Platform.**  

2.3.1 **Deployment and Integration.** Gamma shall design and implement an Advanced Monitoring Platform that:

a. Integrates with Contoso’s existing monitoring tools referenced in **Section 2.3.1(d)** of the Parent Agreement (network monitoring, identity management, and logging solutions);

b. Provides centralized dashboards consolidating metrics from both the core data center environment (under the Parent Agreement) and the new edge infrastructure (under this Addendum);

c. Implements alerting policies based on severity levels consistent with the **Severity 1–4** definitions in **Section 12.3 (Response and Resolution Times)** of the Parent Agreement;

d. Supports role-based access control and integration with Contoso’s identity management systems to ensure least-privilege access, consistent with Contoso’s information security policies.

2.3.2 **Monitoring Deliverables.** Gamma shall deliver:

a. **Monitoring Architecture Documentation**, including logical and physical diagrams showing data flows between edge nodes, central collectors, and the monitoring platform;

b. **Dashboard Templates** for infrastructure health, capacity, performance, and SLA reporting, with at least **five (5)** core dashboard sets tailored to Contoso’s operations teams;

c. **Alert Catalogs** defining monitored metrics, thresholds, escalation paths, and notification channels, aligned with the SLA Addendum Metrics in **Article XII** of this Addendum;

d. **Runbooks and SOPs** for incident triage and escalation, which shall supplement the standard operating procedures referenced in **Section 2.4.1(c) (SOPs and runbooks)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Implementation, Migration, and Cutover Services.**  

2.4.1 Gamma shall perform a phased implementation of the Edge Infrastructure Expansion and Advanced Monitoring Platform, including:

a. **Design Phase**, during which Gamma will review Contoso’s existing architecture and documentation (as contemplated in **Section 2.3.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement) and produce a detailed High-Level Design (HLD) and Low-Level Design (LLD);

b. **Pilot Phase**, deploying the edge infrastructure at **two (2)** pilot sites to validate design assumptions, performance, and operational procedures;

c. **Rollout Phase**, extending the solution to the remaining sites, in accordance with an agreed rollout schedule;

d. **Migration and Cutover**, including workload placement and data replication configuration, consistent with the migration and cutover support described in **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.2 **Minimizing Business Disruption.** Gamma shall coordinate with Contoso to schedule maintenance windows, change freezes, and cutover activities so as to minimize business disruption, consistent with **Section 2.3.2 (Migration and Cutover Services)** of the Parent Agreement. Time-sensitive cutovers shall be designated as **Critical Milestones** for purposes of **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent Agreement and Section 2.5 of this Addendum.

2.5 **Project Plan, Milestones, and Timelines.**  

2.5.1 **Project Plan.** Within fifteen (15) Business Days of the Addendum Effective Date, Gamma shall develop and submit a Project Plan specific to this Addendum, in consistent form with the Project Plan required under **Section 2.5.1** of the Parent Agreement. The Project Plan shall:

a. Identify all Milestones, including Critical Milestones, and associated target dates;

b. Describe resource assignments, dependencies, and assumptions;

c. Include a risk register and mitigation strategies;

d. Identify any required Contoso responsibilities and prerequisites for Gamma’s performance.

2.5.2 **Milestones.** The Parties anticipate at least the following Milestones (non-exhaustive):

a. **M1 – Design Approval**: Delivery and Contoso’s approval of HLD/LLD and Specifications;

b. **M2 – Pilot Sites Operational**: Successful deployment and acceptance of edge infrastructure and monitoring at two pilot sites;

c. **M3 – Regional Rollout Complete**: Deployment and acceptance at the remaining sites;

d. **M4 – Monitoring Platform Full Production**: Advanced Monitoring Platform in full production, integrated with existing tools and operational runbooks;

e. **M5 – Final Acceptance and Knowledge Transfer Completion**, including training as described in Section 2.6 below.

2.5.3 **Time is of the Essence for Critical Milestones.** The Parties agree that for Milestones designated as Critical (including M2 and M4), time is of the essence, consistent with **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent Agreement. Repeated or material delays not excused by a Force Majeure Event or documented Contoso-caused delays may constitute a material breach for purposes of **Section 4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and Section 4.3 of this Addendum.

2.6 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  

2.6.1 Gamma shall provide comprehensive training to Contoso’s designated staff regarding the operation and support of the Edge Infrastructure Expansion and Advanced Monitoring Platform, consistent with **Section 2.3.3 (Training and Knowledge Transfer)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.2 Training shall include:

a. Up to **five (5) instructor-led training sessions**, which may be delivered on-site or remotely;

b. Admin and operator training for monitoring dashboards, alert configuration, and incident management;

c. Technical training for Contoso’s engineering staff on configuration management, patching, and capacity planning;

d. Delivery of all associated training materials in electronic format as Additional Deliverables.

2.7 **Support and Maintenance.**  

2.7.1 The Additional Products and Additional Services shall be supported by Gamma in accordance with **Section 2.3.4 (Support and Maintenance Services)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XII (Service Level Agreements) of this Addendum**.

2.7.2 Support shall include:

a. Inclusion of edge sites and monitoring components in Gamma’s helpdesk ticketing system;

b. Application of the severity levels and response times defined in **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement, as adjusted (if applicable) in this Addendum;

c. Provision of firmware, software patches, and updates necessary to maintain the stability and security of the Edge Infrastructure Expansion and Advanced Monitoring Platform.

2.8 **Performance Standards.**  

2.8.1 Gamma shall perform the Additional Services in a professional and workmanlike manner in accordance with **Section 2.6.1 (Professional and Workmanlike Manner)** and shall ensure that the Additional Products, Additional Services, and Additional Deliverables conform in all material respects to the Specifications and Acceptance Criteria, consistent with **Section 2.6.2 (Compliance with Specifications)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.8.2 In the event of any nonconformity, Gamma shall promptly re-perform or correct, at its own cost, the nonconforming work, consistent with **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4.3 **Late Payment and Suspension.** Undisputed late payments shall accrue interest and may result in suspension of performance as set forth in **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Taxes and Expenses.**  

3.5.1 Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement, and are not included in the Addendum Contract Value except as expressly stated.

3.5.2 Reimbursable expenses incurred in connection with the Additional Services shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement, provided that Contoso’s prior written approval shall be required for any travel outside of pre-approved locations or budgets.

## ARTICLE IV

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (October 4, 2024)** and shall remain in effect until **June 14, 2029** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV and the Parent Agreement.

a. Contoso shall pay Gamma for (i) all Additional Products delivered and accepted; (ii) Additional Services performed and accepted; and (iii) non-cancellable commitments reasonably incurred by Gamma in reliance on this Addendum, in each case consistent with **Section 4.5.2 (Wind-Down)** of the Parent Agreement;

4.4.2 **Impact of Parent Agreement Termination.** Termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement automatically terminates this Addendum as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to preserve certain aspects of this Addendum. In such case, the surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement (as specified in **Section 4.5.4 (Survival)**) shall continue to govern the surviving portions of this Addendum.

4.5 **Survival.**  

4.5.1 The provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIV, XV, and XVI** of this Addendum) shall survive, together with the corresponding surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as specified in **Section 4.5.4** thereof.
