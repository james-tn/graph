## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum **supplements and expands** the “Services” described in **Article II of the Parent Agreement (Scope of Services / Work)**. All obligations, performance standards, and responsibilities therein, including Epsilon’s obligations pursuant to Sections 2.3 (Performance Standards) and 2.4 (Timelines and Milestones) of the Parent Agreement, shall apply to the Additional Services described in this **Article II** unless expressly stated otherwise.

2.1.2 The Additional Services are expressly deemed “Services” as defined in Section 1.15 of the Parent Agreement, and any work product resulting therefrom is deemed “Deliverables” under Section 1.9 of the Parent Agreement, subject to the clarifications and enhancements set forth in this Addendum.

2.1.3 Epsilon shall perform the Additional Services using the same or higher standards of care described in **Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement**, including professional and workmanlike performance, compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws, adherence to security obligations in **Article XI of the Parent Agreement**, and compliance with SLAs in **Article XII of the Parent Agreement** and Article XII of this Addendum.

2.2 **New Processing Activities and Data Categories.**  

2.2.1 In addition to the purposes of Processing described in Section 2.1(b) of the Parent Agreement, Epsilon is hereby expressly authorized, on documented instructions from Contoso, to carry out the following New Processing Activities on behalf of Contoso, solely for Contoso’s business purposes and in compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws:

  a. **Behavioral and Interaction Analytics.**  
  Processing of multi-channel behavioral data (including web interactions, support interactions, system usage logs, and clickstream data) of Contoso’s employees, contractors, and customers, as applicable, for the purposes of:  
  (i) usage pattern analysis;  
  (ii) performance optimization;  
  (iii) user segmentation; and  
  (iv) identification of anomalies and possible misuse.

  b. **Operational Efficiency and Workforce Analytics.**  
  Processing of HR- and workforce-related Personal Data (limited to identifiers, job functions, department, location, and system activity metadata) for the purposes of:  
  (i) workload balancing;  
  (ii) support ticket routing;  
  (iii) training needs analysis; and  
  (iv) operational efficiency reporting.  
  No processing of sensitive employment data (e.g., health, union membership, or salary information) is authorized under this Addendum unless specifically authorized in a written SOW referencing this Addendum.

  c. **Customer Journey and Lifecycle Analytics.**  
  Processing of customer-related Personal Data (including names, contact details, transaction history, service entitlements, and interaction history) for:  
  (i) cohort analysis;  
  (ii) churn prediction;  
  (iii) product adoption metrics;  
  (iv) service quality trend analysis.

  d. **Security and Access Analytics.**  
  Processing of role-based access logs, authentication logs, and authorization decision records (generally system identifiers, IP addresses, timestamps, user role, and action performed) to:  
  (i) create access risk dashboards;  
  (ii) identify unusual access patterns; and  
  (iii) support Contoso’s security incident response processes.

2.2.2 Epsilon shall not process Personal Data under this Addendum for any purpose other than the purposes expressly described in this Article II or otherwise documented in an SOW referencing both **DPA-EPS-202404-459** and **ADD-EPS-202404-477**, consistent with **Section 11.2 (Instructions)** and **Section 11.11(a) (CCPA/CPRA-Specific Provisions)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Enhanced Analytics Services and Deliverables.**  

2.3.1 Epsilon shall provide the following **Enhanced Analytics Services** as part of the Additional Services:

  a. **Advanced Data Modeling and Normalization.**  
  Building on the aggregation and normalization activities described in Section 2.1(b)(i) of the Parent Agreement, Epsilon shall design and maintain advanced data models to support multi-dimensional analysis across Contoso’s operational, customer, and workforce datasets.

  b. **Predictive and Prescriptive Analytics.**  
  Development of statistical and machine-learning models to:  
  (i) predict operational bottlenecks;  
  (ii) flag at-risk customer segments;  
  (iii) estimate resource needs; and  
  (iv) recommend prioritized actions.  
  Epsilon shall document model assumptions, key input variables, and limitations in technical documentation delivered to Contoso.

  c. **Custom Dashboards and Reporting Suites.**  
  In addition to the dashboards described in Section 2.2(c) of the Parent Agreement, Epsilon shall deliver at least:  
  (i) one executive-level dashboard focused on aggregate KPIs;  
  (ii) three operational dashboards (e.g., security, productivity, customer success); and  
  (iii) a standard library of not fewer than twelve (12) analytic reports, as detailed in a mutually agreed SOW under this Addendum.

  d. **Automated Alerts and Workflows.**  
  Configuration of rules-based alerts and notifications based on threshold violations or model outputs, including integration with Contoso’s incident management tools as feasible and agreed in writing.

2.3.2 **Additional Deliverables** under this Addendum shall include, at a minimum:

  a. Expanded data mapping and schema documentation specifically capturing the New Processing Activities;  
  b. Model documentation and validation summaries;  
  c. Change logs for major analytic configuration updates;  
  d. User and administrator training materials, building upon Section 2.2(e) of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Project Phases, Timelines, and Milestones.**  

2.4.1 The Parties anticipate that the Additional Services will be delivered across the following **Project Phases**:

  a. **Phase A – Discovery and Design (Estimated 60–90 days).**  
  - Activities: requirements workshops; identification of data sources; privacy impact assessment; architectural design; and definition of KPIs;  
  - Deliverables: Discovery Report; Data Source Inventory; High-Level Solution Design; Draft Data Protection Impact Assessment (if applicable).

  b. **Phase B – Data Integration and Enrichment (Estimated 90–120 days).**  
  - Activities: development and testing of APIs and connectors; configuration of data transformation rules; implementation of data quality checks;  
  - Deliverables: Integration Specifications; Data Quality Rules; Initial Test Data Loads; Updated Schema Documentation.

  c. **Phase C – Model Development and Pilot (Estimated 60–120 days).**  
  - Activities: feature engineering; model training and validation; pilot dashboards; limited user rollout;  
  - Deliverables: Model Prototypes; Validation Reports; Pilot Dashboard Set; Pilot Feedback Report.

  d. **Phase D – Production Deployment (Estimated 30–60 days after successful pilot).**  
  - Activities: cutover planning; go-live; performance tuning; configuration of final SLAs and monitoring;  
  - Deliverables: Production Runbook; Final Dashboard Suite; Operational Support Plan.

  e. **Phase E – Optimization and Continuous Improvement (Remainder of Addendum Term).**  
  - Activities: periodic model recalibration; addition of new data sources; change requests;  
  - Deliverables: Quarterly Optimization Reports; Updated Documentation; Release Notes.

2.4.2 Unless otherwise specified in a SOW referencing this Addendum, milestone dates shall be **target dates**, and Epsilon shall exercise commercially reasonable efforts to meet such dates, consistent with the standard in **Section 2.3(e) of the Parent Agreement**. Epsilon shall promptly notify Contoso of any anticipated delays and propose a remediation plan.

2.5 **Service Environments and Availability.**  

2.5.1 Epsilon shall provision distinct **development, test, and production Service Environments** for the Additional Services, managed in accordance with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** and **Article XII (SLA)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.2 The **Uptime Commitment** in Section 12.1 of the Parent Agreement (99.9% monthly availability) shall apply to the production environment for the Additional Services, subject to the SLA Enhancements described in **Article XII of this Addendum**.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities (Supplement).**  

In addition to Contoso’s responsibilities under Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  a. Timely provide Epsilon with access to the data sources and systems identified in the Discovery and Design phase, subject to Contoso’s internal security policies;  
  b. Provide documented, lawful instructions for each New Processing Activity, consistent with **Section 11.2 (Instructions)** and **Section 11.3 (Compliance with Laws)** of the Parent Agreement;  
  c. Ensure that any Data Subject notices and consents required for the New Processing Activities are obtained, consistent with Section 2.5(d) and Section 7.3(a) of the Parent Agreement;  
  d. Designate subject-matter leads to review and approve KPI definitions, model objectives, and dashboard content.

2.7 **No Legal Advice.**  

Section 2.7 (No Legal Advice) of the Parent Agreement is hereby confirmed to apply in full to this Addendum. Epsilon’s assistance in privacy, security, or compliance matters related to the Additional Services is informational only, and Contoso remains responsible for its own compliance determinations.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Incremental Contract Value; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

3.1.1 In consideration of the Additional Services and Additional Deliverables under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay to Epsilon **Incremental Fees totaling US $4,137,303** over the Addendum Term (the “**Addendum Fees**”), subject to adjustments as provided herein or in any SOW executed under this Addendum.

3.1.2 The **Addendum Fees** are **in addition** to the **Contract Value of US $1,326,272** specified in the Recitals and **Article III of the Parent Agreement**. For avoidance of doubt, the Aggregate Liability Cap under **Article IX of this Addendum** is calculated with reference to Fees paid or payable under this Addendum only, unless expressly stated otherwise.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  

Unless otherwise specified in an applicable SOW, the Addendum Fees shall be comprised of:

  a. **Extended Implementation and Onboarding Fee.**  
  A one-time fee of **US $847,303** for design, integration, configuration, and initial training relating to the Additional Services, invoiced as follows:  
  - 30% upon completion of Phase A (Discovery and Design);  
  - 40% upon completion of Phase B (Data Integration and Enrichment);  
  - 30% upon completion of Phase C (Model Development and Pilot).

  b. **Enhanced Subscription / Platform Fees.**  
  Recurring Fees of **US $1,000,000 per year** for access to the Enhanced Analytics Services and associated platform capabilities, payable in equal monthly installments of **US $83,333.33**, starting on the Production Deployment milestone.

  c. **Managed Services and Optimization Fees.**  
  Recurring managed services Fees of **US $450,000 per year** for ongoing optimization, model maintenance, and support for the Additional Services, payable in equal monthly installments of **US $37,500**.

  d. **Professional Services Fees (Additional).**  
  A budgetary not-to-exceed amount of **US $1,840,000** for change requests, customizations, additional integrations, and specialized consulting services related to the Additional Services, to be billed on a time-and-materials basis at the rates agreed in the applicable SOW(s).

3.3 **Invoicing.**  

3.3.1 **Recurring Fees.**  
Epsilon shall invoice Contoso monthly in advance for recurring platform and managed services Fees described in Sections 3.2(b) and 3.2(c), consistent with **Section 3.2(a) of the Parent Agreement**.

3.3.2 **Implementation and Professional Services Fees.**  
Implementation and Professional Services Fees under Sections 3.2(a) and 3.2(d) shall be invoiced monthly in arrears based on actual work performed or as otherwise specified in the applicable SOW, consistent with **Section 3.2(b) of the Parent Agreement**.

3.4 **Payment Terms.**  

All invoices issued under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** of the invoice date (“**Net 30**”), consistent with **Section 3.2(c) of the Parent Agreement**. Payments shall be made in U.S. dollars by wire transfer or other method agreed in writing.

3.5 **Late Payments.**  

Undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at the rate specified in **Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement** (the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum allowed by law), from the date due until paid in full. Epsilon shall not assess late charges on any amounts disputed in good faith, provided Contoso timely notifies Epsilon in accordance with **Section 3.4 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  

If Contoso disputes any invoiced amount under this Addendum, Contoso shall notify Epsilon in writing within fifteen (15) days after receipt of the invoice, specifying the nature of the dispute. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve such dispute pursuant to **Article XIV of the Parent Agreement and Article XIV of this Addendum**. Contoso shall timely pay all undisputed portions of the invoice.

3.7 **Taxes and Expenses.**  

3.7.1 Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. The Addendum Fees are exclusive of applicable taxes, which shall be itemized and payable by Contoso, excluding taxes on Epsilon’s net income, property, or employees.

3.7.2 Out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Epsilon in performing on-site Additional Services shall be reimbursed by Contoso in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies.

3.8 **No Setoff.**  

Contoso shall not withhold, set off, or deduct any amounts from Addendum Fees due to Epsilon except as expressly permitted under this Addendum, the Parent Agreement, or as required by law, consistent with **Section 3.7 (No Setoff)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (February 26, 2025)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **December 03, 2027** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Addendum Term may extend beyond the **Expiration Date of the Parent Agreement (February 05, 2027)** only if the Parent Agreement is renewed or extended pursuant to **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement. If the Parent Agreement is not renewed or is otherwise terminated, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, subject to Section 4.5 of this Addendum.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

Any renewal or extension of this Addendum shall be in writing and contingent upon the renewal or extension of the Parent Agreement in accordance with **Section 4.2 of the Parent Agreement**. The Parties may agree on updated Addendum Fees and scope for any renewal period.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience and without cause upon not less than **forty-five (45) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. This provision supplements **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement**, which permits termination for convenience on not less than thirty (30) days’ notice; provided that termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement unless expressly stated in the termination notice and accepted in writing by the other Party.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach, consistent with **Section 4.4(a) of the Parent Agreement**.

4.4.2 Without limiting the foregoing, Contoso may terminate this Addendum pursuant to **Section 4.5 (Termination for Data Protection Breach)** of the Parent Agreement if the material violation relates to the New Processing Activities or Additional Services contemplated by this Addendum.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  a. Contoso shall pay Epsilon all undisputed Addendum Fees and reimbursable expenses accrued and payable up to the effective date of termination;  
  b. Epsilon shall cease performing the Additional Services and shall, at Contoso’s written instruction, return or securely delete all Personal Data processed solely in connection with the Additional Services, in accordance with **Section 11.10 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement and Section 11.4 of this Addendum;  
  c. Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information related to this Addendum pursuant to **Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in effect in accordance with its terms with respect to all other Services and Deliverables, unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing.

4.6 **Survival.**  

Sections of this Addendum which by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, and XVI) shall survive, together with any corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement specified in **Section 4.7 (Survival)** thereof.
