## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is entered into **pursuant to** and **amends and supplements** the Parent Agreement. The Parties acknowledge and agree that the general **scope of services, performance standards, and obligations** set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect, except as expressly modified by this Amendment.

2.1.2 All Services and Deliverables provided under this Amendment shall be deemed Services and Deliverables under the Parent Agreement and shall be subject to the same terms (including Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, and XVI), except where this Amendment explicitly provides otherwise.

2.2 **Incremental Hardware Procurement and Expansion.**

2.2.1 Pursuant to **Section 2.2.1 (Hardware Procurement and Supply)** of the Parent Agreement, Summit Tech shall provide additional Hardware to support an expanded data center footprint and increased compute capacity for Contoso. The additional Hardware shall include, at a minimum:

  (a) **Twenty (20) additional rack-mounted servers** with upgraded CPU (minimum 32 cores), RAM (minimum 256 GB), and storage configurations (minimum 4 TB SSD each), to be installed in Contoso’s primary San Francisco data center;

  (b) **Six (6) additional network switches** and **four (4) routers**, each configured to meet or exceed the performance, redundancy, and security specifications previously agreed under the relevant SOWs and in line with the standards described in **Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement**;

  (c) **Two (2) additional storage arrays** providing a minimum of 800 TB of usable capacity (post-RAID) and associated backup appliances to support expanded backup and disaster recovery operations; and

  (d) Required **cables, racks, PDUs, and peripheral equipment** necessary for proper installation and operation, as reasonably determined by Summit Tech and approved by Contoso in writing (email sufficient).

2.2.2 All additional Hardware shall conform to the **Specifications** as defined in **Section 1.13 (Specifications)** of the Parent Agreement and any updated technical specifications mutually agreed in writing pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement or this Amendment.

2.3 **Incremental Software and Licensing.**

2.3.1 Under **Section 2.2.2 (Software and Licensing)** of the Parent Agreement, Summit Tech shall procure and/or provide additional software licenses and subscriptions required to operate the Incremental Hardware, including:

  (a) Additional **operating system licenses** for each newly provisioned physical server;

  (b) Additional **virtualization platform licenses** sufficient to support up to 300 new virtual machines;

  (c) Increased licensing or subscriptions for monitoring, logging, and security software such that all Incremental Hardware and the new virtual machines are fully monitored and protected according to Contoso’s standards; and

  (d) Additional **database licenses** (up to eight (8) instances) as specified in a mutually agreed SOW or Change Order referencing this Amendment.

2.3.2 Summit Tech shall provide Contoso with all applicable third-party license terms in accordance with **Section 6.3 (Third-Party Products)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Implementation, Integration, and Migration Services.**

2.4.1 Consistent with **Sections 2.2.3 (Implementation and Integration Services)** and **2.2.4 (Migration and Cutover Support)** of the Parent Agreement, Summit Tech shall perform the following Incremental Services:

  (a) **Design and Planning:** Conduct a capacity and environmental assessment to integrate the Incremental Hardware into Contoso’s existing network and storage topology, including high-level and low-level design documentation;

  (b) **Installation and Configuration:** Rack, stack, cable, and configure the Incremental Hardware in Contoso’s designated data center(s) within mutually agreed timelines, and configure network settings, VLANs, and routing in accordance with Contoso’s architecture guidelines;

  (c) **Integration:** Integrate the new infrastructure components with Contoso’s existing authentication, logging, monitoring, backup, and orchestration systems, as described in **Section 2.2.3 of the Parent Agreement**;

  (d) **Migration and Cutover:** Plan and execute migration of designated workloads to the new infrastructure, including test migrations, validation, and final cutover during agreed maintenance windows, with rollback procedures, consistent with Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement; and

  (e) **Performance Validation:** Perform performance testing and tuning to validate that the Incremental Hardware and associated software meet the agreed Specifications and benchmarks.

2.5 **Managed Services Expansion.**

2.5.1 If Contoso has engaged Summit Tech under **Section 2.3 (Managed Services and Support)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that the managed services coverage shall be expanded to include the Incremental Hardware and related virtual machines. This includes:

  (a) **Monitoring and Incident Management** for all additional systems pursuant to **Section 2.3.1** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) **Maintenance and Updates** (firmware, patches, minor version upgrades) for the newly supplied Hardware and software licenses, as per **Section 2.3.2**; and

  (c) **Service Desk and Technical Support** for incidents related to the Incremental Scope, aligned with **Section 2.3.3** and the SLA parameters referenced in **Article XII**.

2.5.2 Service Levels applicable to the Incremental Scope shall be materially consistent with the uptime commitments and response/resolution times in **Sections 12.1 and 12.3** of the Parent Agreement, as further clarified in **Article XII of this Amendment**.

2.6 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**

Summit Tech shall provide **additional training and knowledge transfer** sessions specific to the Incremental Scope, in addition to the training obligations described in **Section 2.2.5 (Training and Knowledge Transfer)** of the Parent Agreement. At a minimum, Summit Tech shall:

  (a) Conduct **two (2) additional on-site or virtual training sessions** (of at least four (4) hours each) focused on operation and administration of the new infrastructure;

  (b) Provide updated **runbooks, standard operating procedures**, and configuration documentation; and

  (c) Conduct a **post-implementation workshop** with Contoso’s operations team to review lessons learned, optimization opportunities, and roadmap for future enhancements.

2.7 **Timelines and Milestones.**

2.7.1 The Parties shall agree on detailed timelines and milestones in a supplemental SOW or project plan referencing this Amendment, consistent with **Section 2.6 (Timelines and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7.2 Summit Tech shall use commercially reasonable efforts to complete the Incremental Scope according to such timelines, subject to dependencies identified under **Section 2.8 (Cooperation and Dependencies)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.8 **Acceptance.**

The **acceptance process in Section 2.7 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to Deliverables and milestones under this Amendment, including the requirement for Delivery Notices, review periods, deficiency lists, and cure opportunities.

2.9 **Exclusions.**

Any services not expressly included in this Article II or in an applicable SOW referencing this Amendment shall be deemed **out of scope** and may only be added through the Change Management procedures in **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII of this Amendment**.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amended Contract Value; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge that **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the Parent Agreement established a **Contract Value** of **US$865,668** as the aggregate not-to-exceed value for all Services and Deliverables.

3.1.2 **Effective as of the Amendment Effective Date**, Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement is **hereby amended and restated** solely with respect to the aggregate not-to-exceed value as follows:

> “3.1 **Contract Value.** The aggregate not-to-exceed contract value for all Services and Deliverables under this Agreement, as amended by Amendment Contract Reference Number AMD-SUM-202402-567, shall be **US$1,391,765** (“**Contract Value**”), unless increased by a mutually executed Change Order or further written amendment.”

3.1.3 For clarity, the **Amended Contract Value** includes both (i) the original value of US$865,668 and (ii) incremental value attributable to this Amendment and related SOWs.

3.2 **Fee Structure for Incremental Scope.**

3.2.1 The Incremental Fees shall be structured in accordance with **Section 3.2 (Fee Structure)** of the Parent Agreement and shall include:

  (a) **Fixed Fees** for defined implementation milestones, including design, installation, integration, and cutover activities related to the Incremental Hardware and software;

  (b) **T&M Fees** for consulting, optimization, and training services, at the hourly or daily rates agreed in a supplemental SOW or as otherwise agreed in writing;

  (c) **Pass-Through Costs** for Hardware, third-party software licenses, and support contracts procured on Contoso’s behalf, billed at cost plus the agreed margin (if any) as specified in the applicable SOW; and

  (d) **Managed Service Fees** for ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and support of the Incremental Hardware and related virtual machines, typically on a recurring monthly basis.

3.2.2 The Parties shall document detailed fee line items and any rate adjustments in SOW(s) or Change Orders referencing both **PUR-SUM-202402-420** and **AMD-SUM-202402-567**.

3.3 **Payment Terms; Modification of Parent Agreement.**

3.3.1 **Net 45 Terms.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.2 (Payment Terms - Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that all invoices issued by Summit Tech **on or after the Amendment Effective Date** for Incremental Fees and for any Deliverables or Services clearly identified as falling under this Amendment shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) days** from Contoso’s receipt of the invoice (**“Net 45”**).

3.3.2 The **Net 60** payment term in **Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement** shall continue to apply to invoices related solely to pre-existing SOWs and Services that are clearly outside the scope of this Amendment, unless the Parties mutually agree otherwise in writing.

3.3.3 All other provisions of **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, including invoicing mechanics and required references to Contract Reference Number **PUR-SUM-202402-420**, shall continue to apply. In addition, Summit Tech shall reference **“AMD-SUM-202402-567”** on invoices relating to the Incremental Scope.

3.4 **Late Payments.**

The late payment provisions in **Section 3.4 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any undisputed amounts due under this Amendment; provided, however, that interest shall be calculated from the due date based on the **Net 45** payment schedule specified in Section 3.3.1 above.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**

The dispute process in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to invoice disputes under this Amendment. Contoso shall identify whether a dispute relates to fees under this Amendment, the Parent Agreement, or both, and the Parties shall cooperate to resolve such disputes in good faith.

3.6 **Taxes.**

3.6.1 The provisions of **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply in full to all Incremental Fees and pass-through costs under this Amendment.

3.6.2 Summit Tech shall itemize applicable Taxes on invoices in respect of the Incremental Scope and shall identify such amounts as associated with **AMD-SUM-202402-567** for Contoso’s internal tracking.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**

The expense reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.7 (Expenses and Reimbursements)** of the Parent Agreement apply to additional out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Summit Tech in performing the Incremental Scope, subject to Contoso’s prior written approval and applicable travel and expense policies.

3.8 **No Additional Charges; Amendment-Specific Clarification.**

3.8.1 The prohibition in **Section 3.8 (No Additional Charges)** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to all Services under this Amendment. Summit Tech shall not impose any additional fees, surcharges, or expenses in connection with the Incremental Scope except as expressly authorized in this Amendment, an applicable SOW, or a Change Order.

3.8.2 Any future price increases or changes to the Amended Contract Value shall require a **written amendment or Change Order** executed in accordance with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amended Term.**

4.1.1 The Parties acknowledge that **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement sets the Term through **June 25, 2025**.

4.1.2 Solely for purposes of extending the duration of the relationship in light of the Incremental Scope and associated managed services, **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended and restated** as follows:

> “4.1 **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in effect until **November 5, 2027** (the **“Initial Term”** or **“Term”**).”

4.2 **Renewal.**

The **renewal provision in Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement remains in effect and shall apply to any renewal or extension discussions relating to Services under both the original and Incremental Scope.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

4.3.1 The termination-for-convenience rights in **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. Accordingly:

  (a) Contoso may terminate this Amendment, the Parent Agreement, or any SOW (in whole or in part) for convenience upon **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Summit Tech; and  

  (b) Summit Tech may terminate for convenience only upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** and only with respect to SOWs not yet commenced, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.3.2 If Contoso terminates only this Amendment for convenience, the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect, subject to its original terms (as otherwise amended), but the Incremental Scope and Incremental Fees shall cease as of the effective date of such termination.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

The provisions of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment. Any material breach of this Amendment shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa, if such breach materially affects the overall Services or Deliverables.

4.5 **Termination for Persistent Service Failures.**

The special termination rights in **Section 4.5 (Termination for Persistent Service Failures)** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to Service Levels applicable to the Incremental Scope. Repeated failures specific to the Incremental Scope may justify termination of the applicable SOW(s) or this Amendment, without necessarily terminating the entire Parent Agreement, as mutually agreed by the Parties.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**

Upon any expiration or termination of this Amendment:

  (a) Summit Tech shall cease performance of Services under the Incremental Scope, except for transition services as described below and in **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) Contoso shall pay Summit Tech for Incremental Scope Services actually and satisfactorily performed through the effective date of termination and for any non-cancellable third-party commitments incurred on Contoso’s behalf, subject to the mitigation obligations in **Section 4.6.2 of the Parent Agreement**;

  (c) Any perpetual licenses granted with respect to Deliverables under this Amendment shall survive as specified in **Section 4.6.3** and **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent Agreement; and

  (d) Confidential Information shall be returned or destroyed in accordance with **Section 5.4 (Return or Destruction)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Termination.**

If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall terminate automatically on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain obligations (e.g., residual support or license rights) under this Amendment.

4.8 **Transition Assistance.**

The transition assistance obligations in **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Incremental Scope. In particular, if termination is due to Summit Tech’s material breach relating to the Incremental Scope, Summit Tech shall provide reasonable transition assistance at no additional cost for at least thirty (30) days, as set forth therein.
