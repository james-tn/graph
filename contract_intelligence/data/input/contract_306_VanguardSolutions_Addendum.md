## ADDENDUM TO LEASE AGREEMENT  
(Relating to Lease Agreement Ref. **LSE‑VAN‑202508‑211 / contract_211**)

This ADDENDUM TO LEASE AGREEMENT (this “**Addendum**”) is made and entered into as of **February 7, 2026** (the “Addendum Effective Date”), by and between:

**CONTOSO ENTERPRISES, INC.**, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at 425 Market Street, Suite 1800, San Francisco, CA 94105 (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**,” the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”),  

and  

**VANGUARD SOLUTIONS, LLC**, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of New York, with its principal place of business at 200 Park Avenue, 20th Floor, New York, NY 10166 (hereinafter referred to as “**Vanguard**,” the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Vanguard may hereinafter be individually referred to as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Lease Agreement** bearing Contract Reference Number **LSE‑VAN‑202508‑211**, identified internally by Vanguard as **contract_211**, dated **August 30, 2025** (the “Parent Agreement” or the “Master Agreement”), pursuant to which Vanguard leases to Contoso, and Contoso leases from Vanguard, certain commercial office premises located at **200 Park Avenue, 14th Floor, Suite 1400, New York, NY 10166**, together with related services and rights, all as more particularly described in Article II and **Exhibit A (Premises Description)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, under **Section 1.3 (“Commencement Date”)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties recognized that the Commencement Date for occupancy of the “Premises” (as defined in Section 1.12 of the Parent Agreement) could be modified or otherwise agreed in a written addendum;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement contemplates changes to the scope of premises and services through **Article XIII (“Change Management”)**, which provides that any change that affects fees, Lease Term, or other material terms shall be documented in a written amendment or change order executed by both Parties, in accordance with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, Contoso has requested, and Vanguard has agreed, subject to the terms and conditions of this Addendum, to (i) add additional leased space within the building located at 200 Park Avenue; and (ii) make certain modifications to the configuration, service levels, rent structure, and related terms governing the use of such additional leased space;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to document such additional space and modifications by entering into this Addendum, which **adds or modifies leased space under the Lease Agreement dated August 30, 2025 (Ref: LSE‑VAN‑202508‑211 / contract_211)**, and to set forth the additional terms, conditions, and obligations of the Parties with respect thereto;

WHEREAS, the total estimated contract value associated with this Addendum for the period from the Addendum Effective Date through **August 16, 2027** (the “Addendum Expiration Date”) is **Eight Hundred Six Thousand Six Hundred Fifty Dollars (US $806,650)** (the “Addendum Contract Value”);

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF LEASED SPACE AND SERVICES

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Addendum **amends and supplements** the Parent Agreement (LSE‑VAN‑202508‑211 / contract_211) pursuant to **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** thereof. Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, all terms, conditions, rights, and obligations set forth in the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Addendum Premises as if originally included in the definition of “Premises” in **Section 1.12** of the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, the Parties acknowledge that this Addendum **adds or modifies leased space** to the building at 200 Park Avenue, while preserving the original Lease Term, Rent obligations, and other provisions of the Parent Agreement with respect to the Parent Premises, except where explicitly revised by this Addendum.

### 2.2 Addendum Premises; Area and Configuration

2.2.1 **Additional Space.** Effective as of the Addendum Effective Date, Vanguard hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Vanguard, an additional approximately **10,500 rentable square feet** of office space (the “Additional Space”) located on the **15th Floor, Suite 1500**, of 200 Park Avenue, New York, NY 10166, as more particularly described in **Exhibit A‑1 (Addendum Premises Description)** attached hereto. The exact RSF shall be determined in accordance with the building’s standard measurement methodology referenced in **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 **Use of Common Areas.** The Additional Space is leased together with the non‑exclusive right to use common areas and facilities of the building on the same basis as provided in **Section 2.1.1** and **Section 2.4.6 (Common Areas and Amenities)** of the Parent Agreement, including lobbies, elevators, corridors, restrooms, and designated amenities.

2.2.3 **Reconfiguration Rights.** Vanguard shall have the right, upon at least thirty (30) days’ prior written notice and subject to Contoso’s prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed), to relocate or reconfigure internal common areas serving the Addendum Premises or to offer alternate space on the same floor or an adjacent floor, provided that (a) the total RSF is not materially reduced; (b) the replacement space is reasonably comparable in quality; and (c) Vanguard bears all reasonable relocation and fit‑out costs.

### 2.3 Permitted Use of Addendum Premises

2.3.1 The Addendum Premises shall be used solely for **general office use, administrative activities, and lawful business operations** consistent with a first‑class office building in New York City, in accordance with **Section 2.3 (Permitted Use)** of the Parent Agreement. Any change in use is subject to Vanguard’s prior written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.

2.3.2 Without limiting the generality of Section 2.3.1, Contoso shall not:  
(a) use or permit the Addendum Premises to be used for any illegal purpose or in violation of any applicable laws, building codes, or regulations;  
(b) cause a nuisance or unreasonable disturbance to other tenants;  
(c) store or use hazardous materials except in quantities customary for office environments, in compliance with applicable laws and **Section 7.3.2** of the Parent Agreement; or  
(d) violate or cause Vanguard to violate any building rules, as updated and provided to Contoso pursuant to **Section 2.6.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.4 Services for Addendum Premises

2.4.1 **Application of Parent Services.** Vanguard shall provide to the Addendum Premises the same categories of “Services” enumerated in **Section 2.4 (Services Provided by Vanguard)** of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to Utilities and HVAC, Water and Restrooms, Janitorial Services, Building Security, Maintenance and Repairs, and Common Areas and Amenities, subject to the same standards and limitations set forth therein, except as expressly enhanced in this Addendum.

2.4.2 **Enhanced HVAC.** In addition to the HVAC obligations in **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent Agreement, Vanguard shall provide variable frequency drive (VFD)‑enabled HVAC controls in the Addendum Premises to accommodate higher‑density occupancy. Vanguard shall ensure compliance with an internal temperature range of 70‑75°F during Business Hours, except during Force Majeure Events or scheduled maintenance.

2.4.3 **After‑Hours Services.** After‑hours HVAC and security for the Addendum Premises shall be made available upon at least four (4) Business Hours’ prior notice from Contoso, at the rates set forth in **Exhibit C‑1 (Addendum Fee Schedule)** attached hereto, which supplements **Exhibit C** to the Parent Agreement. Such charges shall be deemed **Additional Rent – Addendum Premises**.

2.4.4 **Janitorial Services – Enhanced Scope.** Vanguard shall provide janitorial services to the Addendum Premises consistent with **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent Agreement, but with the following enhancements:  
(a) daily disinfecting of high‑touch surfaces in restrooms and pantry areas;  
(b) weekly deep cleaning of conference rooms and collaborative spaces; and  
(c) quarterly carpet shampooing for high‑traffic corridors, at no additional cost to Contoso unless additional frequency is requested in writing.

2.4.5 **Maintenance Response.** For the Addendum Premises, Vanguard shall meet or exceed the Response and Resolution Times specified in **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement. Critical issues affecting life safety or complete loss of access to the Addendum Premises shall be treated as “Critical issues” for purposes of **Section 12.2.1(a)**, and shall be responded to within one (1) hour, twenty‑four (24) hours per day, seven (7) days per week.

### 2.5 Responsibilities of Contoso – Addendum Premises

2.5.1 **Interior Maintenance.** Contoso shall be responsible for interior, non‑structural maintenance of the Addendum Premises in the same manner as provided in **Section 2.5.1** of the Parent Agreement. Any damage arising from Contoso’s misuse, alterations, or negligence shall be repaired at Contoso’s expense.

2.5.2 **Alterations.** Any Alterations (as described in **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent Agreement) to the Addendum Premises require Vanguard’s prior written consent for structural or system‑affecting changes and must comply with building rules and all applicable laws. For minor non‑structural alterations (e.g., cosmetic finishes, demountable partitions), Contoso shall provide Vanguard with reasonable prior written notice and final “as‑built” drawings upon completion.

2.5.3 **Furniture and Equipment.** Consistent with **Section 2.5.4** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall supply and maintain its own furniture and equipment, and shall not install any equipment in the Addendum Premises that exceeds building load limits or requires special ventilation or electrical capacity without Vanguard’s prior written consent.

### 2.6 Deliverables and Documentation – Addendum Premises

2.6.1 In addition to Vanguard’s obligations under **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement, Vanguard shall deliver the following Deliverables (as defined in **Section 1.5** of the Parent Agreement) for the Addendum Premises no later than ten (10) Business Days after the Addendum Effective Date:  
(a) updated access credentials for authorized Contoso personnel assigned to the Addendum Premises;  
(b) floor plans, as‑built drawings, and space plans for Suite 1500;  
(c) a summary of any building‑specific emergency procedures applicable to the 15th Floor; and  
(d) a copy of any special rules or protocols applicable to the Addendum Premises, if different from those applicable to the Parent Premises.

2.6.2 Vanguard shall include the Addendum Premises in its periodic operating expense statements, Service Level reports, and reconciliation statements issued pursuant to **Section 2.6.2** and **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.7 Performance Standards – Addendum Premises

2.7.1 Vanguard shall perform Services in and for the Addendum Premises with the same degree of care, skill, and diligence required under **Section 2.7.1** of the Parent Agreement, and in accordance with the enhanced Service Levels described in Article XII of this Addendum.

2.7.2 Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to schedule intrusive maintenance in the Addendum Premises outside of Contoso’s core business hours (as notified by Contoso), in alignment with **Section 2.7.2** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Addendum Contract Value and Rent Structure

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **Addendum Contract Value** for the Addendum Premises for the Addendum Term is **US $806,650**, which is separate from, and in addition to, the **US $1,122,002 Contract Value** associated with the Parent Premises under **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 **Base Rent – Addendum Premises.** Contoso shall pay Vanguard base rent for the Addendum Premises (“Addendum Base Rent”) as follows (exclusive of Additional Rent – Addendum Premises and Taxes – Addendum Premises):

- **Period 1** (02/07/2026 – 02/06/2027):  
  Annual Addendum Base Rent: US $480,000  
  Monthly Addendum Base Rent: US $40,000  

- **Period 2** (02/07/2027 – 08/16/2027):  
  Annualized Addendum Base Rent: US $504,000  
  Monthly Addendum Base Rent: US $42,000  
  Pro‑rated for six months and ten days.

3.1.3 **Additional Rent – Addendum Premises.** In addition to Addendum Base Rent, Contoso shall pay as Additional Rent – Addendum Premises a proportionate share of operating expenses, building services, and Taxes – Addendum Premises allocable to the Addendum Premises, determined in a manner consistent with **Section 3.1.3** and **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement, using the RSF of the Addendum Premises as the numerator and the total rentable area of the building as the denominator.

3.1.4 **Fee Schedule for Optional Services.** Charges for after‑hours HVAC, additional security, special cleaning, and other optional services requested specifically for the Addendum Premises shall be set forth in **Exhibit C‑1 (Addendum Fee Schedule)**, which supplements Exhibit C of the Parent Agreement and is incorporated herein by reference.

### 3.2 Payment Terms; Net 45

3.2.1 Notwithstanding **Section 3.2.1 (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, all amounts due under this Addendum, including Addendum Base Rent, Additional Rent – Addendum Premises, Taxes – Addendum Premises, and any other charges, shall be payable by Contoso within **forty‑five (45) calendar days** after the date of Vanguard’s invoice (“Net 45”), by electronic funds transfer or other payment method reasonably designated by Vanguard.

3.2.2 Vanguard shall invoice Addendum Base Rent monthly in advance, and Additional Rent – Addendum Premises and other variable charges monthly in arrears or as otherwise specified in Exhibit C‑1.

3.2.3 Contoso’s failure to pay amounts due under this Addendum shall be treated as a failure to pay Rent for purposes of **Section 4.5.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement and may constitute an Event of Default thereunder, subject to applicable notice and cure periods.

### 3.3 Invoices and Disputed Amounts

3.3.1 Vanguard shall issue invoices clearly delineating charges related to the Parent Premises and those related to the Addendum Premises. Each invoice shall identify the Contract Reference Numbers **LSE‑VAN‑202508‑211** and **ADD‑VAN‑202508‑306**, respectively.

3.3.2 The dispute process in **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent Agreement applies to this Addendum, except that Contoso shall provide written notice of any disputed amount within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt, specifically identifying any Addendum‑related charges under this Article III.

### 3.4 Late Payment

3.4.1 Amounts not paid when due under this Addendum (and not the subject of a good faith dispute) shall accrue interest as provided in **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement, from the date due until the date paid.

3.4.2 Without prejudice to **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent Agreement, if Contoso is late in making any payment under this Addendum more than three (3) times in any rolling twelve (12) month period, Vanguard may require that future Addendum Base Rent be paid one (1) month in advance as a condition to continued occupancy of the Addendum Premises.

### 3.5 Operating Expense Reconciliation – Addendum Premises

3.5.1 Vanguard shall provide Contoso with an annual reconciliation of operating expenses and Taxes – Addendum Premises as part of the year‑end statement described in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement, showing separately the amounts attributable to the Addendum Premises.

3.5.2 Any overpayment or underpayment related to the Addendum Premises shall be credited or invoiced, as applicable, in accordance with Section 3.5.2 of the Parent Agreement.

### 3.6 Taxes – Addendum Premises

3.6.1 The tax obligations in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement extend to the Addendum Premises. Vanguard shall include Taxes – Addendum Premises on its invoices as appropriate, and Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard under the Net 45 terms set forth herein.

### 3.7 Expenses and Reimbursements

3.7.1 The provisions of **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement regarding expenses and reimbursements apply equally to out‑of‑pocket costs incurred by Vanguard at Contoso’s written request for the Addendum Premises, including overtime services, special build‑outs, or supplemental systems.

### 3.8 Security Deposit

3.8.1 The Security Deposit provided under **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement shall also secure Contoso’s obligations under this Addendum. Vanguard shall have the right to apply the Security Deposit to any default by Contoso under either the Parent Agreement or this Addendum.

3.8.2 Vanguard may require an increase in the Security Deposit in proportion to the increase in aggregate Rent obligations resulting from this Addendum, and Contoso shall fund such increase within thirty (30) days of written request.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Addendum Term

4.1.1 The term of this Addendum (“Addendum Term”) shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (February 7, 2026) and shall continue in full force and effect until **August 16, 2027** (the “Addendum Expiration Date”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or Article IV of the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Addendum Term is co‑terminous with a portion of the Lease Term defined in **Section 1.11** and **Section 4.1.1** of the Parent Agreement, but shall not extend the overall Lease Term of the Parent Agreement, which expires on May 29, 2029, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

### 4.2 Renewal Options for Addendum Premises

4.2.1 Subject to the mutual written agreement of the Parties, the Addendum Premises may be included in any Renewal Term(s) contemplated by **Section 4.2** of the Parent Agreement. The Parties shall negotiate in good faith any renewal rent and conditions for the Addendum Premises no later than twelve (12) months prior to the Addendum Expiration Date.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

4.3.1 Notwithstanding **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience at any time after the first anniversary of the Addendum Effective Date by providing not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to the other Party, specifying that such notice is given pursuant to this Section 4.3.1 of the Addendum.

4.3.2 In the event of a termination for convenience by Contoso:  
(a) Contoso shall remain liable for all Addendum Base Rent, Additional Rent – Addendum Premises, Taxes – Addendum Premises, and other amounts accruing through the effective termination date;  
(b) Vanguard shall be entitled to an early termination charge, if any, as may be specified in Exhibit C‑1, which may include unamortized tenant allowances or fit‑out costs; and  
(c) the termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement as it relates to the Parent Premises.

4.3.3 In the event of termination for convenience by Vanguard, Vanguard shall not assess any early termination fee; provided, however, that Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to offer Contoso alternative space within the building if feasible, and Contoso shall cooperate reasonably in the orderly vacating of the Addendum Premises.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 A material breach of this Addendum shall constitute a material breach of the Parent Agreement and may be addressed through the termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.4** and **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, including Events of Default by either Party.

4.4.2 Without limiting **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent Agreement, it shall be an Event of Default by Contoso under this Addendum if Contoso fails to pay any material amount due hereunder within fifteen (15) days after written notice of non‑payment, or if Contoso uses the Addendum Premises in a manner that poses an imminent risk of bodily harm or material property damage.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:  
(a) Contoso shall vacate and surrender the Addendum Premises in accordance with **Section 4.8 (Surrender of Premises)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Vanguard shall have the right to re‑let the Addendum Premises to any third party; and  
(c) all payment obligations accrued prior to the termination date shall remain due and payable.

4.5.2 Termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate or modify the Parent Agreement as it applies to the Parent Premises, except to the extent expressly stated in the termination notice and mutually agreed by the Parties in writing.

### 4.6 Survival

4.6.1 The survival provisions in **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Addendum, including without limitation obligations concerning confidentiality, indemnification, limitation of liability (as modified herein), insurance, dispute resolution, and data protection.
