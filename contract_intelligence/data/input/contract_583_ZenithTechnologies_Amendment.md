## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement and Consulting Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is issued **under and subject to** (i) the Parent Agreement **SOW-ZEN-202311-530 (contract_530)** and (ii) the Consulting Agreement **CSA-ZEN-202311-523 (contract_523)**. In accordance with **Section 2.1.1** of the Parent Agreement, this Amendment provides further detail on the Services, Deliverables, timelines, Service Levels, and Fees for an extended phase of the same technology modernization and analytics programme described in **Article II of the Parent Agreement**.

2.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, this Amendment **modifies the Statement of Work dated August 24, 2024 (Ref: SOW-ZEN-202311-530, file: contract_530)** by (a) extending the term of that SOW beyond the original SOW Expiration Date of January 14, 2026, (b) providing for Extended Services through January 30, 2029, and (c) increasing the authorized Contract Value as described in Article III below.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Amendment and the Parent Agreement, the terms of this Amendment shall **govern with respect to the Extended Services only**, but the Consulting Agreement shall continue to prevail in case of conflict unless this Amendment expressly states that it overrides specific provisions of the Parent SOW.

2.2 **Enhanced Hypercare & Stabilization (Extension of Phase 6).**  

2.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that **Phase 6 – Hypercare & Stabilization** under **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement originally contemplated an approximate duration of eight (8) weeks following go-live. The Parties now agree to extend and enhance such hypercare services as follows:

  a. The Vendor shall continue to provide hypercare support for a period that may extend, in aggregate, up to **twelve (12) months** following the final go-live under the Parent SOW, subject to the schedule agreed in the Project Plan as updated from time to time by mutual written agreement.

  b. During the Enhanced Hypercare Period, the Vendor shall maintain dedicated or designated resources (remote and/or on-site as agreed) to monitor system stability, performance, and user adoption, and to remediate high-priority incidents in accordance with the **Incident Response Times** set out in **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement as supplemented by **Article XII of this Amendment**.

  c. The Vendor shall provide periodic **Hypercare Status Reports**, including defect metrics, incident trends, root cause analyses, and recommendations for remedial actions or optimizations. Such reports shall be treated as **Client-owned Deliverables** consistent with **Article VI of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 2.5** thereof (for Deliverables).

  d. The activities within the Enhanced Hypercare Period remain subject to the acceptance process defined in **Section 2.8 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 2.7 of the Consulting Agreement**, where relevant deliverables (such as post-implementation review updates) are produced.

2.3 **Managed Analytics Services (Ongoing Support and Enhancements).**  

2.3.1 In addition to the phases described in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, the Vendor shall perform **Managed Analytics Services** during the term of this Amendment, including:

  a. Continuous improvement of analytics dashboards and reports initially delivered under **Phase 4 – Data Analytics & Reporting (Section 2.3.4)** of the Parent Agreement, including iteration on KPIs, measures, data visualizations, and performance tuning.

  b. Operational monitoring of data pipelines and analytics workloads to ensure that the Deliverables defined in **Sections 2.5 (D10 – Analytics Dashboards, Reports, and Data Models)** of the Parent Agreement remain functional and aligned with business needs, subject to the Service Levels described in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW and this Amendment.

  c. Assistance with the definition and refinement of new KPIs, metrics, and analytical models requested by the Client, which may be delivered as part of scheduled Optimisation Releases or, if out-of-scope, as T&M services under **Section 3.4** of this Amendment.

  d. Limited ad hoc analytics support (e.g., answering queries, assisting power users, helping with ad hoc report creation) within a mutually agreed monthly or quarterly service envelope.

2.3.2 The specific scope, frequency, and expected effort for Managed Analytics Services shall be documented in an updated Project Plan and/or in separate service schedules agreed in writing. Work that goes beyond such agreed baseline shall be subject to the Change Management process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

2.4 **Optimisation Releases and Continuous Improvement.**  

2.4.1 The Parties shall plan and execute **Optimisation Releases** at intervals to be agreed in the Project Plan (e.g., quarterly or semi-annually). Each Optimisation Release shall:

  a. Be initiated through a backlog review and prioritization session between the Parties’ Project Managers and key stakeholders;

  b. Result in a prioritized list of enhancement items to be implemented, which may include (i) new or updated analytics dashboards (building on Deliverable D10), (ii) modifications to integration workflows (D7), (iii) refinements to data models (D5), and (iv) adjustments to training materials (D11) and runbooks (D12);

  c. Be documented as a mini-release plan, including scope, estimated effort, acceptance criteria, and target dates, which shall be consistent with the acceptance mechanism in **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement;

  d. Be subject to Acceptance by the Client (or deemed Acceptance) in accordance with **Section 2.8** of the Parent SOW, with Acceptance tied to payment milestones under **Section 3.3** of this Amendment.

2.4.2 Optimisation Releases shall, unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order, be covered under the Fixed Fee structure set out in **Section 3.3** of this Amendment, up to the Extended Contract Value.

2.5 **Additional Training & Knowledge Transfer.**  

2.5.1 Extending **Phase 5 – Training & Knowledge Transfer (Section 2.3.5)** of the Parent Agreement, the Vendor shall provide additional and advanced training sessions during the Extended Services period, including:

  a. Refresher training for existing users on updated dashboards and processes;

  b. Onboarding training for new Client personnel;

  c. Train-the-trainer sessions to enhance Client self-sufficiency.

2.5.2 Training materials, updated user guides, and revised runbooks shall be treated as **Client-owned Deliverables** in accordance with **Article VI of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 2.5** of the Parent SOW.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

2.6.1 The Vendor shall perform the Extended Services under this Amendment in accordance with:

  - **Section 2.6.1 of the Parent SOW** (Vendor’s obligations regarding skill, care, and diligence);  
  - **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Consulting Agreement; and  
  - the **Service Level Enhancements** described in Article XII of this Amendment.

2.6.2 The Vendor shall maintain continuity of key personnel where reasonably practicable and shall not reassign any critical resource without prior written notice to the Client, consistent with **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent SOW.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities.**  

The Client’s responsibilities outlined in **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6 of the Consulting Agreement** continue to apply and are specifically reaffirmed for the Extended Services, including timely provision of:

- Access to relevant systems and environments;  
- Business stakeholders for backlog prioritization and Acceptance;  
- Required approvals for Optimisation Releases and Managed Analytics work.

Delays attributable to the Client may require schedule and/or Fee adjustments, which shall be handled through Change Orders pursuant to **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Extended Contract Value and Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

3.1.1 The total **Extended Contract Value (Amendment)** authorized by this Amendment is **USD 3,199,017** (exclusive of applicable taxes and reimbursable expenses).

3.1.2 This Extended Contract Value is **in addition to** the original **Contract Value (SOW)** of **USD 3,543,473** defined in **Section 1.17 and Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. For ease of reference, the cumulative authorized Fees for the Parent SOW as amended by this Amendment are **USD 6,742,490**.

3.1.3 The Extended Contract Value forms part of the overarching contract value under the Consulting Agreement **CSA-ZEN-202311-523 (contract_523)**. To the extent that the overall contract value set forth in **Section 3.1** of the Consulting Agreement must be increased to accommodate this Amendment, the Parties agree that such increase is hereby authorized and shall be reflected in the next amendment to the Consulting Agreement, if required.

3.2 **Fee Structure (Extended Services).**  

3.2.1 Fees under this Amendment shall be structured as follows, consistent with **Section 3.2** of the Parent Agreement:

  a. **Fixed Fees** for defined Extended Services and associated milestones (e.g., scheduled Optimisation Releases, Enhanced Hypercare, additional training); and  

  b. **Time and Materials (T&M) Fees** for additional services requested by the Client and approved via Change Order (e.g., significant new functionality or major scope additions not covered by the Extended Contract Value).

3.2.2 Unless otherwise agreed in a Change Order, the majority of Managed Analytics Services and Optimisation Releases envisioned at the time of this Amendment shall be delivered under the Fixed Fee component described in Section 3.3.

3.3 **Milestone-Based Fixed Fees (Amendment).**  

3.3.1 The Parties anticipate allocating the **Extended Contract Value** to milestones as set forth below (illustrative allocation, subject to refinement in the Project Plan). All milestones described below are in addition to and do not replace the milestones defined in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement:

- **Amendment Milestone A1 – Enhanced Hypercare Initiation and Plan Accepted**  
  Amount: **USD 319,902** (10% of Extended Contract Value)  
  Trigger: Acceptance by the Client of the Enhanced Hypercare plan and updated Project Plan, including resource model and reporting cadence.

- **Amendment Milestone A2 – First Wave of Optimisation Releases Accepted**  
  Amount: **USD 639,803** (20%)  
  Trigger: Acceptance (or deemed Acceptance) of the first set of Optimisation Releases and associated Deliverables.

- **Amendment Milestone A3 – Managed Analytics Baseline Services Operational for 12 Months**  
  Amount: **USD 959,705** (30%)  
  Trigger: Completion of twelve (12) months of Managed Analytics Services with delivery of agreed reports and metrics, and Acceptance by the Client of a mid-term performance report.

- **Amendment Milestone A4 – Additional Training & Knowledge Transfer Programme Completed**  
  Amount: **USD 479,851** (15%)  
  Trigger: Completion and Acceptance of agreed training programmes and updated training materials/runbooks.

- **Amendment Milestone A5 – Final Optimisation Releases Accepted and Post-Implementation Enhancement Review**  
  Amount: **USD 799,756** (25%)  
  Trigger: Acceptance (or deemed Acceptance) of the final Optimisation Releases under this Amendment and delivery/Acceptance of a final enhancement review report.

3.3.2 Each milestone shall be considered achieved upon the Client’s Acceptance or deemed Acceptance in accordance with **Section 2.8** of the Parent SOW, as applicable to the relevant Deliverables under this Amendment.

3.4 **Time and Materials (T&M) Services.**  

3.4.1 Any additional services requested by the Client that are not included in the Extended Services baseline (for example, large-scale new integration projects, substantial redesigns of the architecture beyond the agreed roadmap) shall be billed on a T&M basis, subject to:

  a. The T&M rates and terms specified in the Consulting Agreement and/or any rate card referenced therein, as may be updated by mutual written agreement; and  

  b. Pre-approval by the Client in writing (email acceptable) and documentation in a Change Order in accordance with **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Amendment.

3.4.2 For clarity, T&M work under this Amendment is incremental to, and shall not reduce, the Extended Contract Value unless expressly agreed in writing by the Parties.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.5.1 The Client shall reimburse the Vendor for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses necessarily incurred in performing the Extended Services under this Amendment, consistent with **Section 3.5** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.6** of the Consulting Agreement.

3.5.2 All travel, lodging, and significant subsistence expenses must be approved in advance by the Client, and any single expense exceeding **USD 1,000** shall require separate written authorization, consistent with **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.6(c)** of the Consulting Agreement.

3.6 **Invoicing and Payment Terms (Net 60).**  

3.6.1 Invoices for milestone-based Fixed Fees shall be issued upon achievement and Acceptance (or deemed Acceptance) of the relevant Amendment Milestones described in Section 3.3.

3.6.2 T&M and reimbursable expense invoices shall be issued monthly in arrears and shall include reasonable supporting documentation.

3.6.3 Notwithstanding any “Net 30” terminology in the Consulting Agreement, and in alignment with **Section 3.6.3** of the Parent SOW, the Parties expressly agree that **all undisputed invoices issued under this Amendment** shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** of the invoice date (“**Net 60**”). This clause is intended to override **Section 3.4(a)** of the Consulting Agreement and any inconsistent payment term solely with respect to invoices related to this Amendment and the Extended Services.

3.7 **Late Payment.**  

Late payment provisions in **Section 3.7** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.5** of the Consulting Agreement (including the Vendor’s right to charge interest and, after notice and an opportunity to cure, to suspend non-critical Services) shall apply to invoices issued under this Amendment, provided that all references to “Net 30” in those sections shall be read as “Net 60” for purposes of this Amendment.

3.8 **Taxes and Withholding.**  

Sections **3.8 and 3.9** of the Parent SOW and **Sections 3.7 and 3.8** of the Consulting Agreement regarding taxes and withholding shall apply in full to Fees and expenses under this Amendment.

3.9 **Disputed Amounts.**  

Any disputed amounts shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.9 of the Parent SOW** and **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Consulting Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amended Term of the Parent SOW.**  

4.1.1 The Parties agree that the term of the Parent SOW **SOW-ZEN-202311-530 (contract_530)** is hereby extended beyond its original **SOW Expiration Date** of **January 14, 2026** and shall continue in full force and effect until the **Amendment Expiration Date of January 30, 2029**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent SOW, the Consulting Agreement, or this Amendment.

4.1.2 For clarity, this extension is made subject to the term of the underlying Consulting Agreement **CSA-ZEN-202311-523**, and the Parties acknowledge that any necessary extension or renewal of the Consulting Agreement shall be executed to support extension of this SOW through January 30, 2029.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.2.1 Consistent with **Section 4.3 of the Parent SOW** and the “Low Risk Profile” defined therein, either Party may terminate this Amendment (and the Extended Services) for convenience, upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.2.2 Termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not automatically terminate the underlying Parent SOW in respect of Services and Deliverables already completed or in progress prior to the effective date of such termination, except to the extent those Services are integrally linked to the Extended Services.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for material breach by the other Party, in accordance with the cure procedures set out in **Section 4.4** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.4 of the Consulting Agreement**.

4.3.2 Insolvency-related termination rights under **Section 4.4(b)** of the Consulting Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment.

4.4 **Effect of Termination of Parent SOW or Consulting Agreement.**  

4.4.1 If the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement is terminated or expires without renewal, this Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, except to the limited extent required to wind down Extended Services and provide transition support agreed in writing.

4.4.2 The effect of termination shall be as set forth in **Section 4.6** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.6** of the Consulting Agreement, including the Parties’ obligations to pay outstanding amounts and to return or destroy Confidential Information.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.**  

Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment:

  a. The Vendor shall cease performing the Extended Services, except as reasonably necessary to wind down activities and support transition;  

  b. The Client shall pay the Vendor for all Extended Services properly performed and Extended Deliverables Accepted up to the effective date of termination, together with approved expenses;  

  c. The Client shall not be obligated to pay for materially non-conforming Extended Services or Deliverables that cannot be remedied in accordance with **Section 7.4** of the Consulting Agreement; and  

  d. Provisions that by their nature are intended to survive (including payment obligations accrued, confidentiality, IP rights, warranties, indemnification, limitations of liability, and dispute resolution) shall continue in effect, consistent with **Section 4.7** of the Parent SOW and **Article XVI** of the Consulting Agreement.
