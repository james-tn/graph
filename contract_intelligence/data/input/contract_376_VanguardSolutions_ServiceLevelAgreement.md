## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Services

2.1.1 Vanguard shall provide to Contoso comprehensive managed technology services, including but not limited to:

- Hosting and operation of a cloud-based enterprise application platform used by Contoso for internal business operations and customer-facing services;
- Infrastructure management, including server provisioning, storage management, network configuration, database administration, and associated security controls;
- 24x7x365 system monitoring and incident management services;
- Preventive and corrective maintenance, including application of patches, updates, security fixes, and performance optimizations;
- Technical support, including Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 support functions as specified in **Section 2.4**;
- Professional services for initial onboarding, configuration, data migration, and integration with Contoso’s systems, as specified in applicable SOWs.

2.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that the Services are further detailed in one or more SOWs executed pursuant to this Agreement, which shall describe specific Deliverables, milestones, responsibilities of each Party, and any additional service level commitments.

### 2.2 Hosting and Infrastructure Services

2.2.1 Vanguard shall host the Software and Client Data in secure data centers or cloud infrastructure meeting industry-standard security and availability practices. Vanguard shall be responsible for:

- Provisioning and maintaining compute, storage, and network resources sufficient to support agreed performance and Service Levels;
- Implementing and maintaining firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, access controls, log monitoring, and related security controls in line with prevailing industry standards;
- Ensuring redundancy and failover capabilities for critical components to support the uptime obligations set forth in **Article XII**.

2.2.2 Vanguard may utilize third-party data centers or cloud providers (e.g., IaaS platforms) as part of the Services. Vanguard remains responsible to Contoso for the performance of such third-party providers in connection with the Services, except to the extent expressly stated otherwise in this Agreement.

### 2.3 Implementation, Configuration, and Onboarding

2.3.1 Vanguard shall provide implementation and onboarding services to enable Contoso’s use of the Services, which may include:

- Installation, configuration, and customization of the Software as reasonably necessary to meet the functional requirements agreed in an SOW;
- Data migration support, including extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) from Contoso’s legacy systems, subject to agreed data formats and quality assumptions;
- Configuration of user roles, access permissions, and integration endpoints (e.g., APIs, web services, secure file transfers) with Contoso’s systems;
- Initial performance tuning, testing, and validation to ensure the system meets agreed Service Levels prior to go-live.

2.3.2 The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to complete implementation and onboarding in accordance with the timelines specified in the applicable SOW. Contoso shall provide timely access to its systems, personnel, documentation, and data as reasonably required by Vanguard.

2.3.3 Any change to the scope, timelines, or assumptions of implementation services shall be managed under the change management process in **Article XIII**.

### 2.4 Support Services

2.4.1 Vanguard shall provide technical support services for the Services, including:

- **Help Desk / Ticketing:** Contoso’s authorized personnel may log support tickets via Vanguard’s designated ticketing system, email, or phone.
- **Support Hours:** Vanguard shall provide 24x7x365 coverage for Severity 1 (Critical) and Severity 2 (High) Incidents, and Business Day coverage (8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. Pacific Time, Monday through Friday, excluding public holidays) for lower-severity requests, unless otherwise specified in a SOW.
- **Incident Categorization:** Vanguard shall categorize Incidents based on impact and urgency as Critical, High, Medium, or Low, with corresponding response and resolution targets as set forth in **Article XII**.

2.4.2 Vanguard shall provide periodic status updates for open Incidents, communicate root cause analysis for material outages, and implement corrective and preventive measures to minimize recurrence.

### 2.5 Maintenance and Upgrades

2.5.1 Vanguard shall perform scheduled maintenance to apply updates, patches, and upgrades to the infrastructure and Software, including security patches and feature enhancements.

2.5.2 Vanguard shall use reasonable efforts to schedule routine maintenance during agreed maintenance windows (e.g., Saturdays between 12:00 a.m. and 4:00 a.m. Pacific Time) and shall provide Contoso with at least five (5) Business Days’ prior notice of any planned maintenance expected to cause material service downtime.

2.5.3 Emergency maintenance may be performed at any time without prior notice where necessary to address critical vulnerabilities or system stability issues. Vanguard shall endeavor to notify Contoso as soon as reasonably practicable before or during such emergency maintenance.

### 2.6 Service Performance Standards

2.6.1 Vanguard shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with generally accepted industry standards and practices and the Service Levels set forth in **Article XII**.

2.6.2 The Services are designed to support up to the user volumes, transaction throughput, and data volumes specified in the applicable SOW. Material increases beyond such volumes may require additional capacity and fees, to be addressed via the change management process under **Article XIII**.

2.6.3 Vanguard does not warrant that the Services will be error-free or uninterrupted, but Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize interruptions and correct reported errors in accordance with this Agreement.

### 2.7 Client Responsibilities

2.7.1 Contoso shall:

- Provide accurate, complete, and timely information, decisions, and approvals reasonably required by Vanguard to perform the Services;
- Ensure that its personnel are trained in the proper use of the Software and Services and comply with Vanguard’s reasonable usage guidelines;
- Maintain and secure its own systems, networks, and devices that access the Services, including implementing appropriate endpoint security and access controls.

2.7.2 Contoso shall be responsible for ensuring that its use of the Services and its provision of Client Data complies with Applicable Law.

### 2.8 Deliverables and Acceptance

2.8.1 For any Deliverables specified in an SOW, Vanguard shall deliver such Deliverables in accordance with the schedule set forth therein.

2.8.2 Unless otherwise stated in the SOW, Contoso shall have ten (10) Business Days from delivery of a Deliverable to review and either (a) provide written acceptance, or (b) provide a written list of material deficiencies. If Contoso fails to respond in writing within such period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

2.8.3 Vanguard shall use reasonable efforts to remedy any material deficiencies identified by Contoso and resubmit the Deliverable. The Parties shall use good faith efforts to resolve any disputes regarding acceptance.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Fees and Contract Value

3.1.1 In consideration for the Services, Contoso shall pay Vanguard fees in the aggregate amount not to exceed **USD $540,160** over the Term, subject to any adjustments as expressly permitted under this Agreement and applicable SOWs.

3.1.2 The fees will comprise a combination of:

- **Recurring Subscription Fees:** Monthly or annual fees for access to and use of the hosted Software and related Services;
- **Professional Services Fees:** Time-and-materials or fixed-fee amounts for implementation, configuration, integration, and other professional services;
- **Support and Maintenance Fees:** Fees (which may be bundled into subscription fees) for support and maintenance services.

3.1.3 Detailed pricing, including unit rates and any volume tiers, shall be set forth in the applicable SOW(s) or fee schedule attached hereto and incorporated by reference.

### 3.2 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.2.1 Vanguard shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the billing schedule set forth in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise specified, recurring subscription fees shall be invoiced **monthly in advance**, and professional services fees shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears** based on actual hours delivered.

3.2.2 All invoices shall be due and payable by Contoso within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the invoice date (“**Net 30**”), without set-off or deduction, except as expressly permitted under this Agreement.

3.2.3 Payments shall be made in United States Dollars (USD) by electronic funds transfer or other mutually agreed method to the account designated by Vanguard in writing.

### 3.3 Late Payment; Suspension

3.3.1 Any undisputed amount not received by Vanguard by the due date shall accrue interest at a rate equal to the lesser of: (a) one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month; or (b) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, in each case calculated from the date due until the date paid.

3.3.2 If any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than **sixty (60) days** after the invoice due date, Vanguard may, upon ten (10) Business Days’ written notice, suspend the provision of Services until all past-due amounts and applicable interest are paid in full. Such suspension shall not be deemed a breach by Vanguard.

3.3.3 Contoso shall be responsible for all reasonable costs and expenses (including attorneys’ fees) incurred by Vanguard in connection with the collection of undisputed overdue amounts.

### 3.4 Disputed Amounts

3.4.1 If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Contoso shall notify Vanguard in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute in reasonable detail.

3.4.2 Contoso shall timely pay the undisputed portion of the invoice. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any such dispute promptly. Any resolved amounts shall be paid within ten (10) Business Days after resolution.

### 3.5 Taxes

3.5.1 Fees under this Agreement are exclusive of any sales, use, value-added, goods and services, withholding, or other taxes, duties, or government-imposed fees (“**Taxes**”). Contoso shall be responsible for all Taxes associated with the Services, other than taxes based on Vanguard’s net income, property, or employees.

3.5.2 If Vanguard is required to collect or pay Taxes, such Taxes will be separately listed on the invoice and shall be payable by Contoso, unless Contoso provides a valid tax exemption certificate.

### 3.6 Expenses

3.6.1 With respect to any on-site or travel-related services requested by Contoso and agreed by Vanguard, Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard for reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket travel and living expenses incurred in the performance of the Services, in accordance with Vanguard’s then-current travel policy, provided Vanguard submits appropriate supporting documentation.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term

4.1.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **May 16, 2028** (the “**Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**.

### 4.2 Renewal

4.2.1 Unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the Expiration Date, the Parties may mutually agree in writing to renew this Agreement, subject to new pricing and terms to be negotiated in good faith.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience by Client

4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part, for any reason or for no reason, by providing Vanguard with at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**.

4.3.2 In the event of such termination for convenience, Contoso shall remain liable for all fees accrued up to the effective date of termination and for any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Vanguard on Contoso’s behalf, as well as any agreed early termination charges specified in an SOW.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or ten (10) days in the case of a payment default) after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail.

4.4.2 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party (a) becomes insolvent or is unable to pay its debts as they become due; (b) files or has filed against it a petition in bankruptcy that is not dismissed within sixty (60) days; (c) ceases operations; or (d) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

- All rights and licenses granted by Vanguard to Contoso under this Agreement shall immediately cease, except as expressly provided otherwise;
- Contoso shall immediately cease all access to and use of the Services and return or destroy Vanguard Confidential Information as required by **Section 5.4**;
- Contoso shall pay all undisputed fees and charges accrued and unpaid up to the effective date of termination.

4.5.2 At Contoso’s written request, made within thirty (30) days following termination or expiration, Vanguard shall provide Contoso with a one-time export of Client Data in a commonly used format (e.g., CSV, JSON) at Vanguard’s then-current professional services rates, unless otherwise specified in a SOW. After such period, Vanguard may delete Client Data in accordance with **Article XI**.

4.5.3 Termination of this Agreement shall be without prejudice to any rights or remedies that may have accrued to either Party prior to the effective date of termination. The provisions that, by their nature, are intended to survive termination (including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XVI**) shall so survive.

## ARTICLE XII  
SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLAs)

### 12.1 Uptime Commitment

12.1.1 Vanguard will use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the core production environment of the Services is available **99.9%** of the time, measured on a monthly basis (“**Uptime Commitment**”).

12.1.2 “Availability” means the ability of Contoso’s authorized users to access and use the primary production instance of the Services, excluding Scheduled Maintenance, Emergency Maintenance, and any downtime caused by factors outside Vanguard’s reasonable control.

### 12.2 Exclusions from Uptime Calculation

12.2.1 The following events shall be excluded from downtime calculations:

(a) Scheduled Maintenance and Emergency Maintenance;  
(b) Any downtime attributable to Contoso’s acts or omissions, including misuse of the Services or failure to meet its obligations;  
(c) Internet or network issues outside Vanguard’s direct control;  
(d) Force Majeure Events pursuant to **Article XV**;  
(e) Issues caused by Third-Party Services not under Vanguard’s control.

### 12.3 Incident Severity and Response Times

12.3.1 Vanguard shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the following initial response and target resolution times:

- **Severity 1 (Critical):**  
  - Examples: Complete outage of Services affecting all users; data loss or corruption.  
  - Initial Response: within 30 minutes (24x7).  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within 4 hours.

- **Severity 2 (High):**  
  - Examples: Major functionality affected, significant impact to many users, no acceptable workaround.  
  - Initial Response: within 1 hour (24x7).  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within 8 hours.

- **Severity 3 (Medium):**  
  - Examples: Partial loss of functionality; workaround available; limited user impact.  
  - Initial Response: within 4 Business Hours.  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within 3 Business Days.

- **Severity 4 (Low):**  
  - Examples: Minor issues, cosmetic defects, informational inquiries.  
  - Initial Response: within 1 Business Day.  
  - Target Resolution: to be addressed in a future release or maintenance cycle.

### 12.4 Service Credits

12.4.1 If Vanguard fails to meet the Uptime Commitment in a given calendar month, Contoso may be eligible to receive Service Credits as follows:

- Availability between 99.0% and 99.9%: Service Credit equal to 5% of the monthly recurring fees for the affected Services;  
- Availability between 98.0% and 99.0%: Service Credit equal to 10% of the monthly recurring fees for the affected Services;  
- Availability below 98.0%: Service Credit equal to 20% of the monthly recurring fees for the affected Services.

12.4.2 To receive Service Credits, Contoso must submit a written request to Vanguard within thirty (30) days following the end of the month in which the Service Levels were not met. Failure to submit such request within this timeframe shall result in forfeiture of the Service Credits for that month.

12.4.3 Service Credits shall be applied against future invoices and shall be Contoso’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for failure to meet the Uptime Commitment, without prejudice to Contoso’s other rights in the event of persistent or material failure.
