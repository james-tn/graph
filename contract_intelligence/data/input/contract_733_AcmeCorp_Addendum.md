## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is intended to **add processing activities** to those already described in **Article II of the Parent Agreement**, without limiting or reducing any of Vendor’s obligations thereunder.  

2.1.2 All Services set forth in this **Article II** shall be deemed “Services” under **Section 1.15** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, and all data processed in connection herewith shall be deemed “Personal Data” and “Client Data” as those terms are used therein.  

2.1.3 For avoidance of doubt, the obligations regarding **Performance Standards** in **Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement**, **Data Protection and Privacy** in **Article XI**, and **Service Levels** in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement also apply to the Addendum Services, except where explicitly supplemented, clarified, or enhanced in this Addendum.

2.2 **Description of Additional Processing Activities.**  

Vendor shall provide the following Additional Processing Activities to Client under this Addendum:

2.2.1 **New Data Source Integration.** Vendor shall design, implement, and maintain secure data ingestion workflows to import the following New Data Sources into the Platform (as defined in **Section 2.1(c) of the Parent Agreement**):

   a. **Customer Interaction Logs** from Client’s CRM and customer support systems, including chat transcripts, call metadata, and ticket histories (excluding audio recordings unless expressly authorized in writing);  

   b. **Marketing Engagement Data**, including email campaign metrics, website behavioral analytics (page views, click paths, session identifiers), and digital advertising attribution data;  

   c. **Product Telemetry Feeds** from Client’s SaaS products, including usage events, feature adoption metrics, and application performance indicators; and  

   d. **Limited Regulated Personal Data**, where approved in writing by Client and Vendor, such as IP addresses, device identifiers, and pseudonymous user IDs associated with identified or identifiable natural persons, to be processed in accordance with **Article XI of the Parent Agreement** and **Article XI of this Addendum.**

2.2.2 **Additional Data Enrichment and Transformation.** Vendor shall configure and operate the existing Platform (as referenced in **Section 2.1(c) of the Parent Agreement**) to:

   a. Normalize and standardize data schemas across the New Data Sources and existing datasets;  

   b. Implement deterministic and probabilistic identity resolution logic, subject to documented Client Instructions pursuant to **Section 11.2 of the Parent Agreement**;  

   c. Pseudonymize or anonymize Personal Data where required or requested by Client, consistent with **Section 11.4 (Security Measures)** of the Parent Agreement; and  

   d. Create aggregated data models for cohort analysis, funnel metrics, and customer lifecycle reporting.

2.3 **Enhanced Analytics Services.**  

In addition to the **Data Processing and Analytics** Services described in **Section 2.1(b) of the Parent Agreement**, Vendor shall provide the following Enhanced Analytics Services:

2.3.1 **Predictive Modeling and Scoring.** Vendor shall develop and deploy predictive models to generate:

   a. **Churn Propensity Scores** for Client’s end customers, based on historical interaction data and product usage patterns;  

   b. **Upsell/Cross-sell Opportunity Scores** indicating the likelihood that a customer will purchase additional products or services; and  

   c. **Engagement Risk Indicators** identifying accounts at risk of decreased usage or adoption.

2.3.2 **Segmentation and Cohort Analysis.** Vendor shall configure the Platform to support:

   a. Segmentation of customers by demographics (where provided by Client), usage behavior, and marketing response;  

   b. Time-based cohort analysis to measure retention, conversion, and feature adoption; and  

   c. Custom segment definitions as reasonably requested by Client and approved by Vendor under the Change Management process described in **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

2.3.3 **Advanced Reporting.** Vendor shall design and deliver a set of advanced dashboards and reports, which shall be Addendum Deliverables, including:

   a. Executive summary dashboards showing key performance indicators;  

   b. Operational dashboards for Client’s marketing, product, and customer success teams; and  

   c. Periodic “health reports” summarizing churn risk, growth potential, and customer lifecycle metrics.

2.4 **Addendum Deliverables.**  

Vendor shall provide the following Addendum Deliverables to Client, which shall be considered “Deliverables” as defined in **Section 1.8** and governed by **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent Agreement as modified by **Article VI of this Addendum**:

2.4.1 **Data Integration Specifications.** Technical documentation describing data schemas, mappings, transformation rules, and ingestion schedules for the New Data Sources.

2.4.2 **Model Documentation.** High-level functional descriptions of predictive models, including input features, output scores, and interpretation guidelines, excluding Vendor’s proprietary algorithms and source code, which remain Vendor’s Pre-existing IP pursuant to **Section 6.3 of the Parent Agreement.**

2.4.3 **Configured Dashboards and Reports.** A set of Platform dashboards and report templates configured in Client’s production tenant environment, aligned with the Enhanced Analytics Services defined above.

2.4.4 **Operational Runbooks.** Process documents describing standard operating procedures for data refresh, monitoring, issue escalation, and basic troubleshooting for the Additional Processing Activities.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.5.1 The Parties acknowledge the general timeline framework set forth in **Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement** and agree that, for this Addendum:

   a. Vendor shall complete initial design and data mapping for New Data Sources within **thirty (30) calendar days** following the Addendum Effective Date, subject to timely Client cooperation as required under **Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement (Client Responsibilities)**;  

   b. Vendor shall complete initial integration and ingestion workflows for at least two (2) New Data Sources within **sixty (60) calendar days** after receipt of all required technical specifications and secure access from Client;  

   c. Vendor shall deploy initial predictive modeling and segmentation dashboards into Client’s production tenant within **ninety (90) calendar days** following completion of the integration milestones set forth in subsection (b).

2.5.2 All timelines are subject to extension on a day-for-day basis for delays attributable to Client’s failure to fulfill its responsibilities under this Addendum and **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, including provision of data, access credentials, approvals, and necessary personnel.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

2.6.1 Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with the **Performance Standards set forth in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement**, including professional and workmanlike performance and compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws.

2.6.2 Vendor shall make commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that predictive models and analytics are materially consistent with the specifications agreed by the Parties; however, Vendor does not guarantee any particular business outcomes, and Client remains responsible for its own business decisions based on such analytics, consistent with **Section 7.4 (Disclaimer)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities (Addendum-Specific).**  

In addition to the responsibilities under **Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement**, Client shall:

2.7.1 Provide Vendor with a single point of contact responsible for coordination of Addendum Services, decisions, and approvals.

2.7.2 Ensure that any Regulated Personal Data included in the New Data Sources is limited to that which is necessary and proportionate for the Additional Processing Activities and that all required consents, notices, and lawful bases have been obtained, consistent with **Section 7.3 (Client Warranties)** and **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7.3 Validate and approve, in writing or via documented electronic acceptance, the data mappings, dashboards, and key model output structures prior to production deployment.

2.8 **Sub-processing and Third-Party Services.**  

Vendor may utilize Sub-processors in connection with the Additional Processing Activities, subject to **Section 11.7 of the Parent Agreement**. Any new Sub-processor engaged specifically for the Addendum Services shall be disclosed to Client upon request, and Client shall have the same rights to object and terminate affected Services as described in **Section 11.7**.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

In consideration for the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables provided under this Addendum, Client shall pay Vendor total fees up to an aggregate **Addendum Contract Value of US $1,251,311**. This amount is separate from, and in addition to, the **Contract Value of $1,851,274** set forth in **Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement**, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to reallocate existing Contract Value.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

Unless otherwise agreed in a mutually executed SOW referencing this Addendum:

3.2.1 **Implementation Fees.** A one-time implementation fee equal to **twelve percent (12%)** of the Addendum Contract Value, payable as follows:

   a. Fifty percent (50%) upon execution of this Addendum; and  
   b. Fifty percent (50%) upon completion and Client’s written acceptance of the initial production deployment of at least two (2) New Data Sources and associated dashboards.

3.2.2 **Subscription / Platform Extension Fees.** Recurring subscription fees for use of the Enhanced Analytics Services and additional Platform modules, representing **sixty percent (60%)** of the Addendum Contract Value, billed **annually in advance** over the Addendum Term in substantially equal annual installments.

3.2.3 **Professional Services Fees.** Fees for advisory, data science, customization, and training services related to this Addendum, representing the remaining **twenty-eight percent (28%)** of the Addendum Contract Value, billed monthly in arrears on a **time-and-materials basis** at Vendor’s then-current rates, consistent with the approach in **Section 3.2(c) of the Parent Agreement.**

3.3 **Payment Terms; Invoicing.**  

3.3.1 Vendor shall invoice Client for fees under this Addendum in accordance with the structure described in **Section 3.2** above and any applicable SOW.  

3.3.2 Payment of all undisputed amounts under this Addendum shall be due **sixty (60) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 60**”), which modifies, solely for invoices issued under this Addendum, the **Net 45** terms specified in **Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement**.  

3.3.3 All payments shall be made in U.S. dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or other mutually agreed method, consistent with **Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement.**

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

Any undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Addendum shall accrue interest at the lesser of (a) **one percent (1.0%) per month**, or (b) the maximum rate allowed by applicable law, in accordance with **Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement**. Vendor may, after providing at least ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice and a reasonable opportunity to cure, suspend Addendum Services for non-payment of undisputed invoices older than sixty (60) days.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

If Client disputes any portion of an invoice under this Addendum in good faith, Client shall notify Vendor in writing within **fifteen (15) days** of receipt, specifying the nature and amount of the dispute, consistent with **Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement**. The Parties shall use good faith efforts to resolve the dispute promptly, and Client shall timely pay all undisputed portions.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

All fees under this Addendum are exclusive of Taxes, and Client shall be responsible for any such Taxes in accordance with **Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement**, except for taxes based on Vendor’s net income, property, or employees.

3.7 **Expenses.**  

Reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Vendor in connection with on-site services under this Addendum shall be reimbursable by Client in accordance with **Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.8 **No Setoff.**  

Except as expressly permitted by law, Client shall not withhold, offset, recoup, or debit amounts owed under this Addendum by reason of any actual or alleged dispute or claim, consistent with **Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement.**

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (April 02, 2026)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **October 27, 2028** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

The Parties may mutually agree in writing to renew this Addendum for one or more additional terms of one (1) to three (3) years each, subject to mutually agreed fees, terms, and conditions. Any such renewal shall be documented by written amendment referencing this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason upon not less than **forty-five (45) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. This is a specific notice period for the Addendum, which is consistent with but does not modify the sixty (60) day period applicable to the Parent Agreement under **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.**

4.3.2 Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in full force and effect unless separately terminated under its own terms.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if:

   a. The other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** following receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  

   b. The other Party becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, is placed in receivership or similar proceeding, or ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course, consistent with **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement.**

4.5 **Termination for Data Protection or Security Breach.**  

Without limiting **Section 4.4** and **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement**, Client may terminate this Addendum upon written notice if:

   a. Vendor has committed a material and repeated violation of Applicable Data Protection Laws or the data protection provisions of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement (including **Article XI** of each); and  

   b. Vendor fails to cure such violation within thirty (30) days after written notice, or such violation is incapable of cure.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement **DPA-ACM-202507-602 (contract_602)** expires or is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such expiration or termination, without the need for additional notice.  

4.6.2 In such case, the rights and obligations of the Parties under this Addendum shall follow the termination provisions of **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** and **Section 4.7 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Section 4.7** below.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

   a. All Addendum-specific rights and licenses granted hereunder shall cease, except as expressly provided in **Article VI** or as necessary to allow Client to continue using Deliverables it owns under **Section 6.2**;  

   b. Client shall pay Vendor all fees and reimbursable expenses due through the effective date of termination, including amounts due for Services rendered and approved change orders under this Addendum;  

   c. Vendor shall, at Client’s written direction and in accordance with **Section 11.8 of the Parent Agreement**, return or securely delete Personal Data processed solely for the Addendum Services, except to the extent retention is required by law; and  

   d. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to ensure an orderly wind-down or transition of the Additional Processing Activities, and Client may request reasonable transition assistance (subject to mutually agreed fees and an SOW).

4.8 **Survival.**  

The rights and obligations of the Parties under the following provisions of this Addendum shall survive expiration or termination: **Articles III (Fees and Payment Terms), V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XVI (General Provisions)**, and any other provisions which by their nature should survive. The survival provisions of **Section 4.7 of the Parent Agreement** shall also apply.
