## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **General Description.**  
During the Term, Titan shall provide to Contoso a comprehensive suite of **enterprise systems integration, platform implementation, and ongoing managed support services** related primarily to Contoso’s customer relationship management (CRM), enterprise resource planning (ERP), and data analytics environments (collectively, the **“Project”**). The Services are intended to enhance Contoso’s operational efficiency, data interoperability, and reporting capabilities across its global operations.

2.2 **Core Service Components.**  
The Services to be provided by Titan under this Addendum shall include, without limitation, the following:

2.2.1 **Solution Design & Architecture.** Titan shall perform an initial discovery and assessment of Contoso’s existing CRM, ERP, and analytics systems. Based on such assessment, Titan shall develop a detailed solution design and technical architecture document, which shall: (a) describe system interfaces, data flows, and integration patterns; (b) identify required Third-Party Materials; (c) specify security and access control requirements; and (d) define scalability and performance benchmarks aligned with the Service Levels.

2.2.2 **Systems Integration & Configuration.** Titan shall configure, customize, and integrate the agreed platform components, including (as applicable) middleware, API gateways, data transformation tools, and event-driven integration mechanisms. Titan shall implement data mappings, transformations, and validations required to ensure accurate and reliable data synchronization between Contoso’s CRM, ERP, and analytics systems, in accordance with the Specifications.

2.2.3 **Data Migration & Validation.** Titan shall plan, script, and execute data migration activities from legacy systems into the new integrated environment. Data migration shall include data cleansing, de-duplication, validation, and reconciliation processes. Titan shall deliver to Contoso migration runbooks, test scripts, and validation reports, and shall obtain Contoso’s written sign-off for each migration phase prior to proceeding to subsequent phases.

2.2.4 **Customization & Development.** Where agreed in writing, Titan shall develop custom components, including but not limited to integration adapters, workflow automations, custom dashboards, and reporting templates, as part of the Work Product. Such customizations shall be documented and maintained in a configuration repository accessible to Contoso, and shall be developed in accordance with Contoso’s coding standards and security policies provided to Titan in writing.

2.2.5 **Testing & Quality Assurance.** Titan shall design and execute unit tests, system integration tests, performance tests, and, where relevant, security and penetration tests, to validate that the integrated solution meets the Specifications and Service Levels. Titan shall provide Contoso with test plans, test cases, and test results and shall remediate all material defects identified during such testing prior to production deployment. Contoso shall have the right to participate in user acceptance testing (UAT) and to approve or reject Deliverables based on conformance with the agreed acceptance criteria.

2.2.6 **Deployment & Cutover Support.** Titan shall prepare and execute a deployment and cutover plan, including rollback procedures, communication plans, and risk mitigation strategies. Titan shall provide on-site or remote support (as agreed in writing) during go-live periods, including extended hours coverage for critical milestones. Titan shall document any issues arising during cutover and track them through resolution.

2.2.7 **Managed Support & Maintenance.** Following successful deployment and acceptance, Titan shall provide ongoing managed support, which may include: (a) incident management and resolution; (b) problem management, including root cause analysis; (c) minor enhancements and configuration adjustments; (d) performance monitoring; and (e) regular health checks and optimization recommendations. Support shall be provided in accordance with the Service Levels set forth in **Article XII**.

2.2.8 **Training & Knowledge Transfer.** Titan shall provide training sessions, workshops, and documentation for Contoso’s technical and business users, covering system functionality, operational procedures, and troubleshooting. Titan shall deliver user guides, administration manuals, and runbooks, and shall conduct at least one (1) handover session per major project phase to ensure effective knowledge transfer to Contoso’s designated personnel.

2.3 **Deliverables.**  
Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, Titan shall produce and deliver, at a minimum, the following Deliverables:

- Detailed **Solution Design & Architecture Document**;  
- **Project Plan**, including milestones, dependencies, and resource assignments;  
- **Data Migration Plan**, scripts, and validation reports;  
- Configuration and customization documentation;  
- Test plans and test results for each testing phase;  
- Deployment and cutover plans;  
- Periodic status reports (weekly or as otherwise agreed);  
- Training materials and user documentation; and  
- Support and maintenance runbooks and standard operating procedures.

2.4 **Project Timelines and Milestones.**  
The Parties shall agree upon a detailed project schedule, which shall be attached to this Addendum as **Schedule A (Project Plan and Milestones)**. Key milestones shall include, at a minimum:

- Completion of solution design and architecture;  
- Completion of initial system configuration;  
- Completion of integration development;  
- Completion of data migration dry runs;  
- Completion of UAT and acceptance of core Deliverables;  
- Go-live of integrated environment; and  
- Transition to steady-state managed support.

Each milestone shall have associated acceptance criteria and target completion dates. Titan shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet or exceed all agreed milestone dates. In the event of delays caused by Contoso (including delays in providing timely information, approvals, or access), Titan shall be entitled to a reasonable extension of the applicable milestone dates, to be documented via a Change Order in accordance with **Article XIII**.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Titan shall perform the Services (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner; (b) in accordance with generally accepted industry standards and practices; (c) in compliance with the Service Levels; and (d) in accordance with the Specifications and Applicable Law. Titan shall assign appropriately qualified personnel with sufficient skill, experience, and training to perform the Services. Titan shall promptly notify Contoso if it becomes aware of any circumstance that may materially affect Titan’s ability to meet the agreed timelines, Service Levels, or quality standards.

2.6 **Dependencies and Responsibilities of Contoso.**  
Contoso shall (a) provide Titan with timely access to relevant systems, facilities, personnel, and information as reasonably necessary for Titan to perform the Services; (b) designate a project manager with authority to give approvals and make decisions on behalf of Contoso; (c) perform any Contoso responsibilities identified in this Addendum, the Project Plan, or any Change Order; and (d) use the Deliverables as instructed and in accordance with Applicable Law. Titan shall not be liable for any failure to meet Service Levels or timelines to the extent such failure is directly caused by Contoso’s material breach of its obligations under this **Section 2.6**, provided Titan has used commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate such impact and has promptly notified Contoso.

2.7 **Change Management.**  
Any changes to the scope, Deliverables, timelines, or Contract Value shall be managed in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)**. Titan shall not be obligated to perform additional services without an executed Change Order.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Fees and Contract Value.**

3.1.2 **Managed Support Fees.**  
Recurring managed support fees of **US$48,000 per calendar quarter** (US$192,000 per year), for the period commencing upon Contoso’s acceptance of the production environment and continuing through the earlier of the end of the Term or termination of this Addendum, estimated at **US$432,000** for the anticipated support period within the Term.

3.1.3 **Time & Materials (T&M) Enhancements.**  
A not-to-exceed budget of **US$344,690** for additional enhancements, change requests, and ad hoc professional services to be provided on a time-and-materials basis at Titan’s then-current rates (or as otherwise agreed in writing), subject to approval by Contoso pursuant to **Article XIII**.

3.2 **Payment Milestones.**

3.2.1 **Implementation Fee Milestones.**  
The fixed implementation fees shall be invoiced and payable as follows:

- **20% (US$210,000)** upon execution of this Addendum;  
- **30% (US$315,000)** upon completion and acceptance of the Solution Design & Architecture Document;  
- **30% (US$315,000)** upon completion and acceptance of the integrated system in UAT;  
- **20% (US$210,000)** upon successful production go-live and Contoso’s written acceptance of core Deliverables.

Managed support fees shall be invoiced **quarterly in advance** and payable in accordance with the Net 45 terms set forth in **Section 3.3**.

3.2.3 **T&M Enhancements.**  
T&M services shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears** based on actual hours worked, expenses incurred, and any other agreed charges, supported by reasonable documentation.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
All undisputed amounts invoiced by Titan under this Addendum shall be due and payable by Contoso within **forty-five (45) days** from the date of Titan’s invoice (**“Net 45”**). Payments shall be made in **U.S. Dollars (USD)** by wire transfer or other mutually agreed method to the account designated by Titan in writing.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  
Any undisputed payment not received by Titan within the Net 45 period shall be considered past due. Titan may charge interest on any past due and undisputed amounts at a rate equal to the lesser of (a) **one percent (1%) per month** (12% per annum) or (b) the maximum rate permitted under Applicable Law, calculated from the date such amount became due until the date paid. Titan shall provide Contoso with written notice of any delinquent amounts prior to assessing late fees. The assessment of late fees shall not limit Titan’s rights under **Section 4.4** or otherwise.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  
If Contoso, in good faith, disputes any portion of an invoice, Contoso shall notify Titan in writing within **fifteen (15) Business Days** of receipt of such invoice, specifying the disputed amount and the basis for the dispute. Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion in accordance with the payment terms set forth herein. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any such dispute promptly. Failure by Contoso to dispute an invoice within such fifteen (15) Business Day period shall constitute Contoso’s acceptance of such invoice.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
All fees and charges payable under this Addendum are exclusive of any sales, use, value-added, goods and services, or other similar taxes imposed by any governmental entity (excluding taxes based on Titan’s net income, property, or employees). Contoso shall be responsible for all such taxes, if any, which shall be separately itemized on Titan’s invoices. If Contoso is exempt from any such taxes, it shall provide Titan with a valid exemption certificate.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
Reasonable and properly documented out-of-pocket travel and living expenses incurred by Titan in connection with the performance of the Services and pre-approved in writing by Contoso shall be reimbursed by Contoso. Titan shall comply with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies, as provided to Titan in writing. Titan shall include copies of receipts or other reasonable supporting documentation with expense reimbursement requests.

3.8 **No Setoff.**  
Except as expressly provided in this Addendum, each Party shall pay all amounts due without setoff, counterclaim, or deduction, except to the extent that a setoff is required by Applicable Law or specifically agreed by the Parties in writing.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Effective Date and shall remain in effect until **December 31, 2026** (the **“Initial Term”**), unless earlier terminated as provided herein. Any extension of the engagement beyond the Initial Term shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
At least **ninety (90) days** prior to the expiration of the Initial Term, the Parties shall meet in good faith to discuss a renewal or extension of this Addendum. Any such renewal or extension, including any revised pricing or scope, shall be documented in a written amendment or new addendum executed by authorized representatives of both Parties.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason, upon providing the other Party with at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** of termination for convenience. During the notice period, Titan shall continue performing the Services (unless otherwise directed by Contoso) and Contoso shall pay Titan for all Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination. This Section reflects a negotiated, balanced termination right given the medium risk profile.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 **Material Breach.** Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches any provision of this Addendum or the MSA and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receiving written notice thereof (or, if such breach is not reasonably curable within thirty (30) days, within such additional reasonable period as may be necessary to cure, not to exceed sixty (60) days, provided the breaching Party has commenced and diligently pursued cure).

4.4.2 **Non-Payment.** Titan may suspend or terminate the Services if any undisputed amount due and payable remains unpaid for more than **sixty (60) days** after the applicable due date, following at least one prior written notice and an additional **fifteen (15) days** to cure. Suspension of Services shall not relieve Contoso of its payment obligations.

4.4.3 **Insolvency.** Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) becomes insolvent or is unable to pay its debts as they become due; (b) files or has filed against it a petition for bankruptcy or similar proceeding, which petition is not dismissed within sixty (60) days; or (c) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors or is subject to the appointment of a receiver or similar officer.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  

4.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum for any reason: (a) Titan shall promptly cease performing the Services, except as may be reasonably necessary to effect an orderly wind-down; (b) Contoso shall pay Titan for all Services performed and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination; and (c) each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 5.4**.

4.5.2 If Contoso terminates for Titan’s material breach under **Section 4.4.1**, and such breach is not cured within the applicable cure period, Contoso shall have no further obligation to pay fees for Services not yet performed as of the effective date of termination and shall be entitled to any other remedies available under this Addendum, the MSA, or Applicable Law, subject to the limitations set forth in **Article IX**.

4.5.3 If Titan terminates for Contoso’s material breach or non-payment under **Section 4.4**, Contoso shall pay Titan (i) for all Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination; and (ii) any non-cancellable third-party commitments that Titan has entered into in reliance on this Addendum, provided Titan has used commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate such commitments.

4.6 **Transition Assistance.**  
Upon Contoso’s request made prior to the effective date of termination or expiration, Titan shall provide reasonable transition services, including knowledge transfer and assistance to Contoso or to a successor service provider, for a period of up to **ninety (90) days**, at Titan’s then-current T&M rates (or as otherwise agreed in writing). The scope and fees for such transition assistance shall be documented in a Change Order.

4.7 **Survival.**  
The rights and obligations of the Parties under **Sections 3.4, 3.5, 3.6, 4.5, 4.6**, and **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions)** shall survive the expiration or termination of this Addendum.

12.1 **Service Availability.**

12.3 **Incident Severity Levels and Response Times.**  

12.3.1 **Severity 1 (Critical).** Production system is unavailable or severely impacted, resulting in complete loss of critical business function with no workaround.  
- Response Time: within **1 hour** of ticket submission.  
- Target Resolution or Workaround: within **4 hours**.

12.3.2 **Severity 2 (High).** Major functionality is impacted or significant performance degradation occurs, adversely affecting business operations, but system remains operational with a workaround.  
- Response Time: within **2 hours**.  
- Target Resolution: within **1 Business Day**.

12.3.3 **Severity 3 (Medium).** Non-critical functionality is impaired; workarounds exist and business operations are only moderately impacted.  
- Response Time: within **4 Business Hours**.  
- Target Resolution: within **3 Business Days**.

12.3.4 **Severity 4 (Low).** Cosmetic issues, minor defects, or informational requests that do not materially affect operations.  
- Response Time: within **1 Business Day**.  
- Target Resolution: within **10 Business Days** or in the next scheduled release.

12.4 **Service Credits.**  
If Titan fails to meet the Uptime Commitment in a given calendar month, Contoso shall be entitled to Service Credits as follows, calculated as a percentage of the monthly managed support fees:

- Uptime 99.0% to 99.89%: **5%** credit;  
- Uptime 98.0% to 98.99%: **10%** credit;  
- Uptime below 98.0%: **15%** credit.

Service Credits shall be applied to the invoice for the next billing period. Service Credits are not refunds and represent Contoso’s exclusive financial remedy for service availability failures, subject to the limitations in **Article IX**.

12.5 **SLA Exclusions.**  
The Service Levels shall not apply to performance or availability issues: (a) caused by factors outside Titan’s reasonable control, including Force Majeure Events; (b) resulting from Contoso’s misuse of the Services or failure to comply with the documentation; (c) resulting from third-party systems or networks not under Titan’s control; or (d) scheduled maintenance performed in accordance with **Section 12.2**.
