## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This SOW is executed **under and subject to** the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, Contract Reference No. CSA-EPS-202412-519, as contemplated in **Section 1.1.18 (Statement of Work)** and **Section 2.7 (Statements of Work)** thereof.

2.2 **Project Description.**  

2.2.1 The Project under this SOW is titled **“Contoso Enterprise Digital Transformation and Process Optimization Program – Phase I & II.”**  

2.2.2 Epsilon shall provide strategic consulting, operational analysis, solution design, implementation support, and change management services to support Contoso’s enterprise-wide initiative to modernize its technology platforms, optimize core business processes, and enhance organizational capabilities.  

2.2.3 The Services shall include, without limitation, services consistent with and expanding upon the types of services described in **Section 2.2.1 through 2.2.9 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, specifically tailored to the Project. This includes:

- Strategic consulting and advisory on digital transformation roadmap;  
- Assessment and documentation of current-state processes and systems in finance, operations, and customer support;  
- Design of target-state (“to-be”) processes, organizational structures, and technology architectures;  
- Development of implementation roadmaps and project plans;  
- Assistance in selection and configuration of ERP and CRM solutions;  
- Project management services for the pilot and rollout phases;  
- Training and knowledge transfer to Contoso personnel; and  
- Preparation of business cases and benefit realization frameworks.

2.3 **Deliverables.**  

2.3.1 Epsilon shall develop and deliver the following categories of Deliverables, consistent with **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) **Current-State Assessment Reports** – Including written reports, process maps, and system inventories for at least three core functional areas (Finance, Operations, Customer Support).  
(b) **Target Operating Model and Process Designs** – Detailed “to-be” process documentation, workflow diagrams, and RACI matrices for the above functions.  
(c) **Technology Roadmap & Architecture Artifacts** – High-level and detailed architecture diagrams, integration maps, and platform selection recommendations.  
(d) **Implementation Project Plan & Risk Register** – Project plan with milestones, timelines, resource plans, and a maintained risk and issue register.  
(e) **Configuration and Implementation Support Artifacts** – Configuration design documents, test scripts, and cutover plans for the selected ERP/CRM solutions.  
(f) **Training & Change Management Materials** – Training curricula, user guides, quick reference materials, and change impact assessments.  
(g) **Status Reporting & Executive Dashboards** – Bi-weekly status reports, monthly executive summaries, and key performance indicator dashboards.  
(h) **Benefit Realization and Post‑Go‑Live Review** – Business case models, benefit tracking framework, and a post‑implementation review report.

2.3.2 Each Deliverable shall include a version number, date, and reference to this SOW (SOW-EPS-202412-537) and shall be delivered in electronic format (PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel, or agreed alternative) unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.3.3 To the extent any Deliverable incorporates Epsilon’s Pre‑Existing IP or Third‑Party Materials, ownership and licensing shall be governed by **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent Agreement and Article VI of this SOW.

2.4 **Project Phases and Timelines.**  

2.4.1 The Parties agree that timeframes are estimates and will be managed consistent with **Section 2.4 (Timelines and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement. Epsilon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the timelines, and Contoso acknowledges its obligation under **Section 2.6 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement to provide timely information, access, and approvals.

2.4.2 The Project shall be organized into the following phases and indicative timelines (from the SOW Effective Date):

- **Phase 1 – Initiation & Discovery (Months 1–3)**  
  - Activities: Stakeholder interviews, data gathering, current-state assessment.  
  - Key Deliverables: Discovery Plan, Current-State Assessment Reports.  

- **Phase 2 – Design & Roadmapping (Months 3–7)**  
  - Activities: Future-state process design, technology selection, high-level architecture.  
  - Key Deliverables: Target Operating Model, Technology Roadmap, High‑Level Architecture.  

- **Phase 3 – Implementation Planning (Months 7–11)**  
  - Activities: Detailed project planning, resource planning, risk planning.  
  - Key Deliverables: Implementation Project Plan, Risk Register, Change Management Strategy.  

- **Phase 4 – Pilot Implementation (Months 11–20)**  
  - Activities: System configuration, pilot rollout for Finance and Customer Support, testing.  
  - Key Deliverables: Configuration Design Documents, Test Scripts, Pilot Go‑Live Report.  

- **Phase 5 – Full Rollout & Training (Months 20–30)**  
  - Activities: Rollout to Operations and remaining functions, user training, support transition.  
  - Key Deliverables: Training Materials, Rollout Plan, Support Transition Guide.  

- **Phase 6 – Optimization & Benefit Realization (Months 30–36)**  
  - Activities: Post‑go‑live support, optimization sprints, benefit tracking.  
  - Key Deliverables: Post‑Implementation Review Report, Benefit Realization Framework.

2.4.3 Detailed Milestones and associated dates, together with acceptance and payment triggers, are set forth in **Exhibit A (Milestone and Deliverable Schedule)** attached hereto and incorporated herein by reference.

2.5 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**  

2.5.1 The acceptance process shall follow **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, except that the Parties agree to modify the review period for major Deliverables as follows, pursuant to the last paragraph of **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement (SOW may supersede specific provisions):

2.5.2 If Contoso fails to respond within the applicable review period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted, consistent with **Section 2.8.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.3 Epsilon shall correct any material deficiencies identified by Contoso and resubmit the Deliverable for review within a mutually agreed timeframe, consistent with **Section 2.8.2** of the Parent Agreement.

In addition to the obligations set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:
