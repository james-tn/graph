### RECITALS

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Reference to Parent Agreement Scope.** The Parties acknowledge and agree that the general scope of the engagement between the Parties is set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement, including, without limitation, **Sections 2.1 (General Scope)**, **2.2 (Nature of Engagement)**, and **2.3 (Representative Deliverables and Services)**. This Addendum is intended to **extend and supplement** that scope by adding new Products, Services, and Deliverables as described in this Article II, which shall be governed by the applicable terms of the Parent Agreement, except where expressly modified herein.

2.2 **Addendum Objective.** Under this Addendum, Vendor shall design, supply, implement, and support an expanded integrated technology solution intended to:

- Increase network throughput and resiliency across Client’s U.S. data centers and primary branch locations;
- Enhance security posture through additional firewall clusters, intrusion detection appliances, and centralized logging infrastructure;
- Provide dedicated Managed Security Services and enhanced monitoring for critical infrastructure;
- Migrate specified legacy infrastructure components into the expanded environment; and
- Provide extended support and maintenance for the Addendum Products and Addendum Deliverables through the Addendum Term.

2.3 **Addendum Products and Deliverables.** Without limiting **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that this Addendum will include at least the following categories of Products and Deliverables:

2.3.1 **Network Infrastructure Expansion.**  
Vendor shall supply and configure additional network infrastructure Products (as defined in **Section 2.3.1** of the Parent Agreement), including:

- Twenty-four (24) edge network switches with multi‑gigabit capability and PoE+ support;
- Six (6) core aggregation switches with redundant power and high‑availability stacking;
- Associated optics, transceivers, and cabling kits necessary for installation.

These Products shall be delivered FOB Client’s designated locations listed in the Project Plan.

2.3.2 **Security Appliances.**  
Vendor shall provide:

- Eight (8) next‑generation firewall appliances to be deployed in active/active clusters at Client’s two primary data centers;
- Four (4) intrusion detection/prevention system (IDS/IPS) appliances for branch perimeter protection;
- One (1) centralized log aggregation and SIEM integration node.

2.3.3 **Software Subscriptions and Licenses.**  
In addition to the Licensed Software described in **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provision:

- Enterprise management platform licenses for additional 3,500 managed network ports;
- Security subscription bundles (IPS, URL filtering, anti‑malware, threat intelligence) for all new firewall and IDS/IPS appliances;
- Monitoring and analytics tools for performance and security telemetry.

Third‑party license terms communicated by Vendor shall apply in accordance with **Section 6.7 (Third‑Party IP)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.4 **Migration and Cutover Deliverables.**  
Building upon **Section 2.3.4 (Migration and Cutover Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall deliver:

- A pre‑migration assessment report identifying existing configurations, dependencies, and risks;
- A detailed migration runbook for each migration wave;
- Post‑migration validation reports documenting successful data and configuration migration and acceptance criteria results.

2.3.5 **Design and Documentation Deliverables.**  
Vendor shall provide:

- Detailed solution architecture diagrams (current state and target state);
- Low‑level design (LLD) documents capturing port mappings, VLANs, firewall policies, routing, and HA configurations;
- Operational runbooks for routine maintenance and incident response associated with Addendum Products.

2.3.6 **Training Materials.**  
Consistent with **Section 2.3.5 (Training Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall deliver updated training materials and conduct at least:

- Two (2) remote administrator training sessions (3 hours each);
- One (1) on‑site workshop (1 day) focused on firewall policy management and incident handling.

2.3.7 **Managed Security Services.**  
Vendor shall provide Managed Security Services, as a subset of the managed services contemplated in **Section 2.3.7** of the Parent Agreement, including:

- 24x7 monitoring of Addendum firewall and IDS/IPS appliances;
- Alert triage and initial incident classification;
- Monthly security posture reports outlining key events, trends, and recommendations;
- Participation, as reasonably requested, in quarterly security review meetings with Client.

2.4 **Project Phases and Timelines.**  

2.4.1 The Parties shall follow the phased engagement model described in **Section 2.4 (Project Phases and Timelines)** of the Parent Agreement, adapted for the scope of this Addendum as follows:

- **Phase 1 – Discovery and Design (Approx. 6–8 weeks from Addendum Effective Date):**  
  Vendor shall extend the discovery sessions referenced in **Phase 1** of the Parent Agreement to cover the new sites and systems. Deliverables shall include the updated solution design and Project Plan.

- **Phase 2 – Implementation and Configuration (Approx. 8–12 weeks):**  
  Vendor shall install, configure, and integrate the Addendum Products in accordance with the approved design, coordinating with Client’s change management windows.

- **Phase 3 – Testing and Acceptance (Approx. 4–6 weeks):**  
  Vendor shall support system, integration, and user acceptance testing for the new environment. Acceptance criteria shall be defined in the Project Plan and tied to milestone payments under **Article III** of this Addendum.

- **Phase 4 – Go‑Live and Stabilization (Approx. 4 weeks):**  
  Vendor shall support production cutover and stabilization for the expanded infrastructure, including incident triage and tuning of monitoring thresholds.

- **Phase 5 – Ongoing Operations and Support (Remainder of Addendum Term):**  
  Vendor shall provide ongoing support and Managed Security Services in accordance with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this Addendum and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.2 As in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet agreed timelines; delays caused by Client, its Affiliates, or third‑party suppliers, or by Force Majeure Events, shall entitle Vendor to schedule relief and, where applicable, equitable adjustments in fees in accordance with **Article XIII** of both this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards.** Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement and the Service Levels in **Article XII** of this Addendum. In the event of any inconsistency, the more Client‑protective standard shall apply to this Addendum.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities.** The Client responsibilities described in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Addendum. Without limitation, Client shall:

- Provide timely access to its facilities and systems for deployment and monitoring of Addendum Products;
- Assign a project manager to coordinate with Vendor;
- Approve designs, change windows, and test results in a timely manner.

2.7 **Subcontractors.** Vendor may use subcontractors as permitted in **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement. Vendor remains responsible for such subcontractors’ performance under this Addendum.

2.8 **Relationship to Statements of Work.** This Addendum shall be treated, for purposes of **Section 2.8 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement, as a formal SOW‑equivalent document which describes specific Products, Services, Deliverables, fees, and timelines. In the event of conflict between the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the terms of this Addendum shall control with respect to the Products and Services expressly described herein.

3.8 **Disputed Charges.**

3.9 **Taxes.**  
Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of all applicable Taxes.

The no set‑off obligations in **Section 3.9** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Addendum, except to the extent otherwise mandated by law.
