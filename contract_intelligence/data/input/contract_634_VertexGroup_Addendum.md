## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.** This Addendum is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement. Consistent with **Section 2.1 (General Scope)** and **Section 2.8 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement, this Addendum serves as an additional, detailed ordering instrument for Products, Services, and Deliverables. Except as expressly modified or supplemented herein, the scope, obligations, and standards set forth in Article II of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Additional Products and Services.

2.2 **General Scope of Additional Products and Services.** Subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Vendor shall provide to Client the Additional Products and Services described herein, which include:

- Expansion Infrastructure Products in support of Client’s extended data center and edge deployments;
- Implementation Add-On Services to integrate new capacity and optimize existing configurations;
- Extended Support Services and Enhanced Managed Services for selected components;
- Training Program Enhancements to upskill Client’s technical and operational staff; and
- Associated documentation, configurations, and project management.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Additional Products and Services.**  

2.3.1 **Expansion Infrastructure Products.**  
(a) Vendor shall supply additional hardware Products consistent with the category described in **Section 2.3.1 (Infrastructure Products)** of the Parent Agreement, including, without limitation:

  i.  Edge network switches (Layer 2/3) with enhanced throughput and PoE capabilities;  
  ii. Core network switches with redundant power and stacking support;  
  iii. Firewall and security gateway appliances to extend Client’s perimeter and internal segmentation;  
  iv. Rack-mountable servers optimized for virtualization workloads; and  
  v. Associated transceivers, cabling, rails, and mounting accessories.

(b) Specific models, quantities, unit prices, and delivery locations shall be set forth in **Schedule A (Hardware Bill of Materials)** to this Addendum, which is incorporated by reference.  

(c) Delivery, risk of loss, and installation shall follow the same principles as for Products under the Parent Agreement, with delivery schedules agreed through project planning documents approved by Client.

2.3.2 **Implementation Add-On Services.**  
(a) Vendor shall provide additional implementation, configuration, and integration Services consistent with **Section 2.3.3 (Implementation and Integration Services)** of the Parent Agreement, including:

  i. Capacity planning analysis for the additional infrastructure;  
  ii. Network design updates and high-level architecture diagrams reflecting the Expansion Infrastructure Products;  
  iii. Configuration of VLANs, routing policies, security policies, and access controls;  
  iv. Integration with Client’s existing monitoring, logging, and identity management systems; and  
  v. Performance tuning, validation testing, and updated runbooks.

(b) Vendor shall use personnel with appropriate skills and experience and shall perform such Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with the **Performance Standards** in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement.

(c) Implementation Add-On Services shall be delivered in phases aligned with the project phases described in **Section 2.4 (Project Phases and Timelines)** of the Parent Agreement (Discovery and Design, Implementation and Configuration, Testing and Acceptance, Go-Live and Stabilization).  

(d) Detailed tasks, milestones, and dependencies shall be described in **Schedule B (Implementation Plan)** to this Addendum.

2.3.3 **Extended Support Services.**  
(a) In addition to the **Support and Maintenance Services** described in **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide Extended Support Services for all Expansion Infrastructure Products and related configurations, including:

  i. Extended support hours (up to 24x7x365 for Severity 1 incidents, if specified in Schedule C);  
  ii. Priority routing of support tickets related to Expansion Infrastructure Products;  
  iii. Proactive health checks and quarterly review sessions; and  
  iv. Access to an assigned technical account manager.

(b) The Service Levels for Extended Support Services shall be as set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum, which supplements and refines **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Extended Support Services are contingent upon Client’s timely payment of applicable Fees as per **Article III**.

2.3.4 **Enhanced Managed Services.**  
(a) Where specified in **Schedule C (Support and Managed Services Schedule)**, Vendor shall provide Enhanced Managed Services which build upon the “Managed Services (if applicable)” described in **Section 2.3.7** of the Parent Agreement, including:

  i. Continuous monitoring of specified infrastructure components;  
  ii. Incident triage and initial remediation in accordance with agreed runbooks;  
  iii. Capacity and performance reporting with recommendations; and  
  iv. Configuration backup and change tracking.

(b) The scope of Enhanced Managed Services, including the specific systems covered, hours of coverage, and response commitments, shall be further detailed in Schedule C.  

(c) Vendor may engage subcontractors in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Subcontractors)** of the Parent Agreement, remaining fully responsible for their performance.

2.3.5 **Training Program Enhancements.**  
(a) Vendor shall provide additional Training Services, consistent with **Section 2.3.5 (Training Services)** of the Parent Agreement, including:

  i. Role-based training for network operations, security operations, and system administrators;  
  ii. Hands-on labs using Client’s configured environment;  
  iii. Updated training materials reflecting new architecture and operational procedures; and  
  iv. Train-the-trainer sessions to enable Client to self-deliver ongoing training.

(b) Delivery format (on-site, virtual instructor-led, or self-paced) and schedules shall be agreed in writing and documented in **Schedule D (Training Plan)**.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  
(a) The Parties anticipate that the Additional Products and Services under this Addendum will be substantially completed by **November 15, 2027** (the “Expiration Date” of this Addendum), subject to delays permitted under **Article XV (Force Majeure)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.  

(b) Milestones, including delivery, installation, testing, and go-live, are set forth in Schedule B. Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet such milestones, consistent with Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Any schedule adjustments required due to Client-caused delays, third-party dependencies, or Force Majeure Events shall be addressed via the **Change Management** process in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.5 **Acceptance.**  
Acceptance of Deliverables and completion of Implementation Add-On Services shall follow the acceptance processes and criteria set forth in the relevant schedules hereto and consistent with the **Testing and Acceptance** phase in **Section 2.4 (Phase 3)** of the Parent Agreement. Absent specific written acceptance procedures, Deliverables shall be deemed accepted ten (10) Business Days after delivery if no material, documented, and reproducible non-conformities are reported by Client within that period.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities.**  
Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to this Addendum. Without limitation, Client shall provide timely access to personnel, systems, and premises, designate a project manager with decision-making authority, and ensure accuracy of Client Data. Any failure by Client to perform its responsibilities may entitle Vendor to schedule relief and, if material, equitable fee adjustments via Change Order.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
In consideration of the Additional Products and Services provided by Vendor under this Addendum, Client shall pay Vendor fees in an aggregate amount anticipated to be **US$1,325,063** (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**), which is in addition to the Contract Value specified in Section 1.1.7 and Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement. The Parties acknowledge this amount as an estimate based on current scope and assumptions and subject to adjustment as expressly permitted herein and in the Parent Agreement, including via Change Orders under **Article XIII**.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  
The Addendum Contract Value comprises the following categories:

3.2.1 **Product Fees.** One-time purchase prices for Expansion Infrastructure Products, as detailed in Schedule A.

3.2.2 **Professional Services Fees.** Fixed or time-and-materials fees for Implementation Add-On Services and Training Program Enhancements, as detailed in Schedule B and Schedule D.

3.2.3 **Managed and Extended Support Fees.** Recurring fees for Extended Support Services and Enhanced Managed Services, including any premium service tiers, as described in Schedule C.

3.3 **Payment Schedule.**  
Unless otherwise stated in the applicable schedule:

3.3.1 **Product Fees** shall be invoiced (i) fifty percent (50%) upon Client’s issuance of a purchase authorization referencing this Addendum, and (ii) fifty percent (50%) upon shipment of the relevant Products.

3.3.2 **Professional Services Fees** for Implementation Add-On Services shall be invoiced on a **milestone** basis (e.g., design completion, installation completion, acceptance of testing) as set forth in Schedule B; Training Program Enhancements shall be invoiced upon completion of each training wave, as specified in Schedule D.

3.3.3 **Managed and Extended Support Fees** shall be invoiced in advance on a quarterly basis, unless otherwise specified in Schedule C.

3.4 **Payment Terms – Net 45.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that for invoices issued under this Addendum, all undisputed amounts shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 45**”). All other payment mechanics, including permitted methods and application of payments, remain as described in Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Late Payments.**  
Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement (Late Payments) is incorporated by reference. Interest shall accrue on any undisputed, past-due amounts at the lesser of (a) one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month, or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, from the due date until paid. Vendor may, after providing written notice consistent with Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement, suspend performance of Services under this Addendum for nonpayment.

3.6 **Disputed Charges.**  
Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement (Disputed Charges) applies to invoices under this Addendum. Client shall notify Vendor in writing within fifteen (15) days of the invoice date of any disputed amounts, specifying the basis of dispute. Undisputed portions shall be paid in accordance with the Net 45 terms above.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement (Taxes) applies to this Addendum. All Fees hereunder are exclusive of applicable taxes, which shall be borne by Client, except for taxes on Vendor’s income.

3.8 **Expenses.**  
Vendor’s reimbursement of reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses shall be governed by Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement. Any such expenses specifically associated with this Addendum shall be invoiced separately and shall not count toward the Addendum Contract Value unless expressly stated in the relevant schedule.

3.9 **No Set-Off.**  
Section 3.9 of the Parent Agreement (No Set-Off) shall apply mutatis mutandis to all Fees under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall remain in full force and effect until **November 15, 2027** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or as a result of Parent Termination.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Any renewal or extension of the Addendum Term shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties, consistent with the principles in **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement. Absent such agreement, this Addendum shall expire on the Addendum Expiration Date.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
4.3.1 **By Client.** Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience and without cause by providing Vendor at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice. Client shall remain liable for: (a) all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred or committed by Vendor through the effective date of termination; (b) any non-cancellable commitments entered into by Vendor on Client’s behalf; and (c) any early termination fees expressly set forth in the applicable schedules (e.g., for certain managed services).

4.3.2 **By Vendor.** Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience and without cause by providing Client at least **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice; provided that Vendor shall complete any critical transition or stabilization activities identified in Schedule B or Schedule C, subject to Client’s timely payment of all outstanding Fees and expenses.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination-for-cause provisions in **Section 4.5 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Addendum. In particular:

(a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum for material breach by the other Party, if such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice;  
(b) Vendor may terminate this Addendum immediately for extended nonpayment in line with Section 4.5.2 of the Parent Agreement; and  
(c) Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon insolvency or bankruptcy events of the other Party, as described in Section 4.5.3 of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Parent Termination.**  
(a) **Automatic Co-Termination.** Except as otherwise expressly agreed in writing, this Addendum shall terminate automatically, without further action by either Party, upon any termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement (“Parent Termination”).  

(b) **Survival of Accrued Rights.** Parent Termination shall not relieve either Party of its payment obligations or other accrued rights and obligations under this Addendum as of the effective date of termination.  

(c) **Transition Assistance.** If requested by Client prior to the effective date of Parent Termination, Vendor shall provide transition assistance with respect to the Additional Products and Services in accordance with the principles in **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, at Vendor’s then-current time-and-materials rates.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) Client shall pay all outstanding and undisputed invoices, as well as all Fees and expenses incurred or committed by Vendor through the effective date of termination, in accordance with Section 3.4 above and Section 4.6.1 of the Parent Agreement;  

(b) Vendor shall cease performance of the Additional Products and Services (other than any agreed transition assistance) and use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate further costs, consistent with Section 4.6.2 of the Parent Agreement;  

(c) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with Article V of this Addendum and **Section 5.4** of the Parent Agreement; and  

(d) Certain provisions of this Addendum shall survive termination as set forth in Section 16.9 below.
