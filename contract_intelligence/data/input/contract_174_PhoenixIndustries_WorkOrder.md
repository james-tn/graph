**NOW, THEREFORE**, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein and in the Parent SOW, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW.**  

2.1.1 This Work Order **adds scope** to and **supplements** the Parent SOW (Ref: SOW-PHO-202502-125, contract_125). The overall Project as defined in **Section 1.14 (Project)** of the Parent SOW is hereby expanded to include the Extended Services and Enhancement Deliverables described in this Article II.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Work Order, the Services and Deliverables, performance standards, Acceptance procedures, and Company responsibilities described in **Article II** of the Parent SOW remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Extended Services and Enhancement Deliverables, including:

- The Acceptance Procedures in **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW;  
- The Performance Standards in **Section 2.3** of the Parent SOW; and  
- The Project Management and Governance framework in **Section 2.4** of the Parent SOW.

2.2 **Overview of Extended Engagement.**  

2.2.1 The Service Provider shall provide the Company with a comprehensive set of Extended Services that build upon and extend the ERP and CRM implementation delivered under the Parent SOW. These Extended Services include:

- (a) **Advanced Analytics and Reporting Build-Out.** Design, configuration, and deployment of an Advanced Analytics Platform providing consolidated financial, operational, and customer metrics, leveraging the core ERP/CRM data implemented under the Parent SOW;  
- (b) **Additional Integrations and Interfaces.** Design, build, and deployment of Additional Integrations with the Company’s data warehouse, HR system, external partner portals, and up to three (3) new third-party systems not previously covered in the Integration Design Document delivered under **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent SOW;  
- (c) **Process Optimization and Automation.** Detailed process assessments and Optimization Services for finance, procurement, inventory, sales, and support functions, including new automation scripts, workflow enhancements, and configuration tuning;  
- (d) **Enhanced Training and Change Management.** Expanded training and change management services to drive adoption of the new analytics and optimization capabilities, supplementing the training described in **Section 2.2.5** of the Parent SOW; and  
- (e) **Extended Managed Services.** Provision of Enhanced Managed Services through the WO Term, including higher-touch support for analytics and integrations, configuration optimization, and quarterly strategic reviews, aligned with and augmenting the Managed Services in **Section 2.2.6** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.2 The Extended Services shall be delivered in phases, which are intended to run in parallel or sequentially with the ongoing Phase 6 managed services under the Parent SOW, as applicable:

- **Phase A – Analytics and Reporting Foundation (Estimated Duration: 12 Weeks)**  
- **Phase B – Integration Expansion (Estimated Duration: 16 Weeks)**  
- **Phase C – Process Optimization and Automation (Estimated Duration: 20 Weeks)**  
- **Phase D – Adoption, Training, and Change Management (Estimated Duration: 12 Weeks, overlapping with Phases A–C)**  
- **Phase E – Extended Managed Services (From completion of Phase C through Expiration of the WO Term)**

2.3 **Detailed Phase Descriptions and Deliverables.**

2.3.1 **Phase A – Analytics and Reporting Foundation.**  

(a) The Service Provider shall:  
  - Conduct stakeholder workshops to define analytics KPIs and reporting requirements for finance, procurement, inventory, sales, and customer support;  
  - Analyze the data model implemented under the Parent SOW to ensure appropriate data structures, relationships, and performance;  
  - Design the Advanced Analytics Platform architecture, including data extraction, transformation, and loading (“ETL”) mechanisms consistent with the integration design principles set out in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent SOW; and  
  - Configure baseline dashboards and reports for executive leadership, operations, finance, and sales.

(b) **Deliverables for Phase A** shall include, at a minimum:  
  - (i) **Analytics Requirements and KPI Definition Document**;  
  - (ii) **Analytics Solution Design Document**, aligned with the High-Level Solution Architecture and Detailed Design Specifications referenced in **Sections 2.2.1 and 2.2.2** of the Parent SOW;  
  - (iii) **Initial Dashboard and Report Catalogue**, including definitions, mock-ups, and data sources; and  
  - (iv) **Analytics Platform Configuration Workbook**, documenting all key configuration elements.

2.3.2 **Phase B – Integration Expansion.**  

(a) The Service Provider shall:  
  - Assess existing integration components and APIs delivered under the Parent SOW (see **Section 2.2.3(d)(i)**) for alignment with the new data and reporting requirements;  
  - Design and develop Additional Integrations with up to three (3) new third-party systems, including mapping of data elements and security considerations;  
  - Implement data validation and reconciliation processes to ensure that Additional Integrations do not compromise data integrity validated under the Data Validation and Reconciliation Report referenced in **Section 2.2.3(d)(iv)** of the Parent SOW; and  
  - Conduct integration testing and assist with user acceptance testing consistent with **Phase 4 – Testing and User Acceptance** of the Parent SOW.

(b) **Deliverables for Phase B** shall include:  
  - (i) **Extended Integration Design Document**, supplementing the Integration Design Document referenced in the Parent SOW;  
  - (ii) **Additional Integration Components and Technical Documentation**;  
  - (iii) **Integration Test Cases and Scripts** for the new interfaces; and  
  - (iv) **Integration Test and Defect Reports**, aligned with the testing documentation described in **Section 2.2.4(d)** of the Parent SOW.

2.3.3 **Phase C – Process Optimization and Automation.**  

(a) The Service Provider shall:  
  - Perform detailed process assessments for chosen business functions, leveraging the Current State Assessment Report and Requirements Specification Document delivered under **Section 2.2.1(d)** of the Parent SOW;  
  - Identify bottlenecks, manual activities, and non-value-added tasks that can be automated using the ERP/CRM platform;  
  - Configure new workflows, approval processes, and automation scripts;  
  - Conduct performance and load testing of enhanced processes consistent with **Section 2.2.4** of the Parent SOW; and  
  - Develop an Optimization Roadmap for ongoing continuous improvement through the WO Term.

(b) **Deliverables for Phase C** shall include:  
  - (i) **Process Optimization Assessment Report**, including before/after performance metrics;  
  - (ii) **Automation Design and Configuration Documentation**;  
  - (iii) **Performance and Load Test Reports** for optimized processes; and  
  - (iv) **Continuous Improvement and Optimization Roadmap**.

2.3.4 **Phase D – Adoption, Training, and Change Management.**  

(a) The Service Provider shall:  
  - Develop enhanced training materials, including video-based content and scenario-based exercises, supplementing the Training Materials referenced in **Section 2.2.5(d)(i)** of the Parent SOW;  
  - Conduct train-the-trainer sessions and targeted user training for analytics users and process owners;  
  - Provide change impact assessments and communications plans to drive adoption; and  
  - Prepare a Training Completion and Adoption Report.

(b) **Deliverables for Phase D** shall include:  
  - (i) **Enhanced Training Materials**, including updated user guides, quick reference cards, and online modules;  
  - (ii) **Change Impact and Communications Plan**; and  
  - (iii) **Training and Adoption Completion Report**.

2.3.5 **Phase E – Extended Managed Services.**  

(a) In addition to the managed services described in **Section 2.2.6** and the SLA commitments in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW, the Service Provider shall, during the Enhanced Managed Services Period:  
  - Provide dedicated support for analytics and Additional Integrations, including configuration changes and minor enhancements;  
  - Provide monthly “Optimization Clinics” with key stakeholders to review system performance, analytics usage, and enhancement opportunities;  
  - Provide quarterly strategic service review sessions, building on the Quarterly Service Review Reports under **Section 2.2.6(d)(iv)** of the Parent SOW, to evaluate the effectiveness of the optimization roadmap; and  
  - Maintain alignment with the Parent SLA Framework, as supplemented in **Article XII** of this Work Order.

(b) **Deliverables for Phase E** shall include:  
  - (i) **Monthly Optimization and Analytics Usage Reports**;  
  - (ii) **Enhanced Incident and Problem Logs** specific to analytics and Additional Integrations;  
  - (iii) **Quarterly Optimization and Strategy Review Reports**; and  
  - (iv) **Updated Service Transition Plan**, as needed.

2.4 **Timelines and Performance Standards.**  

2.4.1 The Service Provider shall perform the Extended Services in accordance with the timelines referenced in **Sections 2.3.1–2.3.5** above. Detailed project plans, including dependencies and resource assignments, shall be documented in an updated **Project Plan and Schedule** (supplementing the Project Plan delivered under **Section 2.2.1(d)(iv)** of the Parent SOW) within thirty (30) days after the Effective Date of this Work Order.

2.4.2 The Service Provider shall meet or exceed the Performance Standards set forth in **Section 2.3 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent SOW, which are hereby incorporated by reference. Any material failure to meet such Performance Standards shall be subject to the remedies available under the Parent SOW and this Work Order, including but not limited to service credits, re-performance, and, where applicable, termination rights.

2.5 **Company Responsibilities.**  

The Company shall comply with its obligations set forth in **Section 2.5 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent SOW, including timely access to personnel, systems, and information, and timely review and Acceptance of Deliverables, as adapted for the Extended Services. Failure by the Company to fulfill such responsibilities may impact timelines but shall not relieve the Service Provider of its performance obligations unless directly and demonstrably caused by such failure.

2.6 **Acceptance Procedures.**  

Acceptance of Enhancement Deliverables shall follow the Acceptance Procedures in **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW, including notice of readiness, review periods, rejection and remediation of nonconformities, and deemed Acceptance provisions. The Parties may supplement Acceptance Criteria for specific Deliverables in writing, provided such criteria are consistent with the Specifications and performance standards of the Parent SOW.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Work Order Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total not-to-exceed **WO Contract Value** for the Extended Services and Enhancement Deliverables under this Work Order shall be **US $4,083,651**, subject to adjustment only by a written Change Order executed in accordance with **Article XIII** of this Work Order and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW.

3.1.2 The WO Contract Value is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US $2,310,799** specified in **Section 1.6** of the Parent SOW. For avoidance of doubt, nothing in this Work Order shall increase the Contract Value of the Parent SOW unless expressly stated in a Change Order that references both **SOW-PHO-202502-125** and **WO-PHO-202502-174**.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

3.2.1 **Implementation/Enhancement Fees (Phases A–D).**  
The Implementation/Enhancement phases shall be billed on a fixed-fee milestone basis, similar in structure to **Section 3.2 (Implementation Fees)** of the Parent SOW, as follows:

- Phase A (Analytics and Reporting Foundation): **US $920,000**  
- Phase B (Integration Expansion): **US $1,120,000**  
- Phase C (Process Optimization and Automation): **US $1,350,000**  
- Phase D (Adoption, Training, and Change Management): **US $393,651**  

Total Implementation/Enhancement Fees: **US $3,783,651**.

3.2.2 Each phase fee shall be invoiced in the following proportions, consistent with the milestone approach under **Section 3.2** of the Parent SOW, but adapted to Client-favorable payment timing:

- (a) **30%** of the applicable phase fee upon commencement of the phase;  
- (b) **50%** upon delivery of all primary phase Deliverables (as identified in Article II for such phase); and  
- (c) **20%** upon Acceptance (or deemed Acceptance) of such phase Deliverables in accordance with **Section 2.6** of the Parent SOW.

3.2.3 **Extended Managed Services Fees (Phase E).**  

(a) For the Enhanced Managed Services Period, the Company shall pay the Service Provider a recurring **Base Monthly Extended Service Fee** of **US $25,000** per month, aligned with and in addition to the Base Monthly Service Fee of US $25,000 described in **Section 3.3(a)** of the Parent SOW.  

(b) The Base Monthly Extended Service Fee covers up to an additional **200 hours per month** of support, optimization, analytics, and integration-related services beyond the 200 hours covered in the Parent SOW’s Phase 6 managed services, thereby providing up to an aggregate of **400 hours per month** across both agreements.  

(c) Any hours beyond such additional 200 hours per month under this Work Order shall be billed on a time-and-materials basis at a blended rate of **US $175 per hour**, which is deliberately set below the US $185 per hour overage rate in **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent SOW as part of the low-risk, Client-favorable profile. Prior written approval by the Company is required for any such overage exceeding twenty (20) hours in a given month.

3.3 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.3.1 Reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket travel and lodging expenses incurred by the Service Provider in connection with on-site services performed under this Work Order shall be reimbursed by the Company at cost, subject to the following:

- (a) The Service Provider shall obtain the Company’s written approval prior to incurring any travel and lodging expenses, consistent with **Section 3.4(a)** of the Parent SOW;  
- (b) All reimbursable expenses shall comply with the Company’s travel and expense policy, as referenced in **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent SOW; and  
- (c) The Service Provider shall submit detailed documentation and receipts with each expense invoice, consistent with **Section 3.4(c)** of the Parent SOW.

3.3.2 Unless otherwise agreed in writing, expenses under this Work Order shall be **in addition to** the WO Contract Value but capped at **US $100,000** in the aggregate for this Work Order. This cap is separate from and in addition to the US $75,000 cap referenced in **Section 3.4** of the Parent SOW.

3.4 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  

3.4.1 The Service Provider shall invoice the Company in accordance with the milestones and monthly schedules described in **Sections 3.2 and 3.3** above.

3.4.2 All invoices shall:

- (a) Reference both **WO-PHO-202502-174** and the Parent SOW Contract Reference Number **SOW-PHO-202502-125**;  
- (b) Include sufficient detail to enable the Company to verify the accuracy and basis of the charges (including identification of the relevant phase, milestone, or month); and  
- (c) Be submitted to the Company’s designated accounts payable contact as specified in **Section 16.7 (Notices)** of this Work Order.

3.4.3 The Company shall pay undisputed amounts within **sixty (60) days** from the date of receipt of an undisputed invoice (“**Net 60**”). If the Company disputes any portion of an invoice, it shall notify the Service Provider in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt, describing the nature of the dispute. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve disputed amounts, and the Company shall pay all undisputed amounts when due.

3.5 **Late Payment.**  

Undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of: (a) one percent (1.0%) per month; or (b) the maximum rate permitted under applicable law, from the due date until payment is made in full. This rate is intentionally lower than the 1.5% per month rate in **Section 3.6** of the Parent SOW to reflect the low-risk, Client-favorable profile.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

All Fees and charges under this Work Order are exclusive of applicable sales, use, value-added, or similar taxes. The allocation of tax responsibility shall be as set forth in **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent SOW, which is incorporated herein by reference.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Work Order.**  

This Work Order shall commence on the Effective Date and shall remain in effect until the earlier of: (a) completion of all Extended Services and Acceptance of all Enhancement Deliverables; or (b) **March 25, 2028** (the “**WO Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Term.**  

4.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent SOW Term, as defined in **Section 1.18** and **Article IV** of the Parent SOW, currently expires on **March 17, 2026**, unless earlier terminated or renewed. Notwithstanding the Parent SOW Term, this Work Order shall continue in effect through the WO Expiration Date, provided that the Parent SOW remains in force or is extended, or that the Parties agree that the relevant provisions of the Parent SOW necessary to support this Work Order (including Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall remain in effect for the duration of this Work Order.

4.2.2 In the event of any inconsistency between the Term of this Work Order and the Parent Term, the Parties agree that:  
- (a) the termination or expiration of the Parent SOW shall, subject to Section 4.5 below, result in the corresponding termination of this Work Order; but  
- (b) the provisions of the Parent SOW that by their nature or as expressly stated therein survive termination shall continue to apply to this Work Order.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 The Company may terminate this Work Order, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to the Service Provider, which is a more Client-favorable notice period than the forty-five (45) days provided in **Section 4.3** of the Parent SOW.

4.3.2 The Service Provider may terminate this Work Order for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the Company, provided that such termination shall not affect the Parent SOW unless expressly stated.

4.3.3 Upon such termination for convenience by the Company, the Company shall pay the Service Provider for: (a) all Extended Services properly performed and Accepted; (b) all approved, reimbursable expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination; and (c) any non-cancellable commitments that were specifically incurred for this Work Order and pre-approved in writing by the Company. The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate such non-cancellable commitments.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Work Order immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

- (a) Materially breaches any provision of this Work Order or the Parent SOW as it applies hereto and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or ten (10) days for payment-related breaches) after receiving written notice thereof, consistent with **Section 4.4(a)** of the Parent SOW; or  
- (b) Becomes insolvent or is subject to bankruptcy or similar proceedings, consistent with **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent SOW.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent SOW.**  

4.5.1 If the Parent SOW is terminated for any reason, this Work Order shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination of the Parent SOW, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing that this Work Order shall continue in effect under mutually agreed successor terms.

4.5.2 Upon any expiration or termination of this Work Order (whether independent or due to termination of the Parent SOW):

- (a) The Service Provider shall promptly cease performance of the Extended Services, except as may be required for transition assistance under Section 4.6 below;  
- (b) The Company shall pay the Service Provider for all Extended Services properly performed and Accepted and all approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, subject to any offsets;  
- (c) Ownership and license rights in Enhancement Deliverables shall be as set forth in **Article VI** of this Work Order and **Article VI** of the Parent SOW; and  
- (d) Each Party shall handle Confidential Information in accordance with **Article V** of the Parent SOW and **Article V** of this Work Order.

4.6 **Transition Assistance.**  

Upon the Company’s request in connection with expiration or termination (other than termination by the Service Provider for the Company’s uncured material breach), the Service Provider shall, for up to ninety (90) days following the effective date of termination, provide reasonable transition assistance in relation to the Extended Services and Enhancement Deliverables to facilitate the orderly transfer of such services to the Company or a third-party provider. The fees for transition assistance shall be at the Service Provider’s then-current time-and-materials rates (not to exceed US $185 per hour), unless termination results from the Service Provider’s material breach, in which case the Parties shall discuss equitable adjustments, consistent with **Section 4.6** of the Parent SOW.

4.7 **Survival.**  

The provisions of this Work Order that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall survive, including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI**, as well as any accrued payment obligations.

## ARTICLE VI  
INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS

6.1 **Ownership of Enhancement Deliverables.**  

6.1.1 Consistent with **Section 6.1** of the Parent SOW, and subject to payment of all undisputed amounts due under this Work Order, the Company shall own all right, title, and interest in and to all Enhancement Deliverables (excluding Vendor Materials and Third-Party Materials), including all associated Intellectual Property rights.

6.1.2 To the maximum extent permitted under applicable law, all Enhancement Deliverables that are works of authorship shall be deemed “works made for hire,” and the Company shall be deemed the author and owner thereof.
