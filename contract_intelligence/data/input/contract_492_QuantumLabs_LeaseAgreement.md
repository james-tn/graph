## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF LEASE AND SERVICES

2.1 **Lease of Premises.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Quantum Labs hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Quantum Labs, the Premises, being approximately [X,XXX] rentable square feet of combined wet laboratory, dry laboratory, and office space located on the [__] floor(s) of **250 Innovation Way, Building B, Palo Alto, California 94301** (the “Building”), together with non-exclusive rights to use common areas of the Building, including lobbies, corridors, elevators, restrooms, and break areas, as reasonably required for access and enjoyment of the Premises.

2.2 **Use of Premises.**  
(a) **Permitted Use.** Contoso shall use the Premises solely for lawful research, development, testing, and related administrative and office activities consistent with a technology and research laboratory, and for no other purpose without the prior written consent of Quantum Labs, which consent shall not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.  

(b) **Compliance with Laws.** Contoso shall, at its sole cost and expense, comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws, regulations, ordinances, codes, and guidelines (collectively, “Applicable Laws”) relating to its use and occupancy of the Premises, including but not limited to occupational safety and health laws, environmental laws, and building codes.  

(c) **Prohibited Activities.** Contoso shall not use the Premises for any immoral or illegal purposes, nor for any use that constitutes a nuisance or unreasonably interferes with the quiet enjoyment of other tenants or occupants of the Building.

2.3 **Services Provided by Quantum Labs.** During the Term, Quantum Labs shall provide, either directly or through third-party contractors, the following Services (collectively, the “Services”) at levels consistent with first-class laboratory facilities in the geographic area:

  2.3.1 **Building Operations and Maintenance.** Quantum Labs shall operate and maintain the structural components of the Building, including foundations, roofs, exterior walls, load-bearing elements, and common building systems (e.g., plumbing, electrical risers, main HVAC systems serving multiple tenants), in good working order and in compliance with Applicable Laws.

  2.3.2 **Utilities.** Quantum Labs shall make available to the Premises:  
  (a) electricity suitable for standard office and laboratory equipment;  
  (b) water for ordinary laboratory and domestic use;  
  (c) heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (“HVAC”) sufficient to maintain commercially reasonable indoor environmental conditions during Business Hours; and  
  (d) internet/telecommunications infrastructure to the Premises demarcation point, subject to network availability and the Service Levels in Article XII. Quantum Labs may separately meter or reasonably estimate utilities usage and charge Contoso in accordance with Article III.

  2.3.3 **Janitorial and Common Area Services.** Quantum Labs shall provide routine janitorial services for common areas and, at a minimum, periodic cleaning of corridors and shared facilities. Cleaning inside the Premises will be provided as specified in Exhibit [__] (if any) or as separately agreed in writing.  

  2.3.4 **Security and Access Control.** Quantum Labs shall provide reasonable building security measures, which may include card access systems, visitor sign-in procedures, CCTV coverage of common areas, and other measures typical of similar facilities. Contoso shall comply with all security procedures and shall be responsible for the conduct of its employees, contractors, and visitors.

  2.3.5 **Shared Equipment and Facilities.** Quantum Labs shall provide Contoso with non-exclusive, scheduled access to certain shared equipment and facilities (e.g., autoclaves, freezers, imaging equipment, conference rooms) as detailed in Exhibit [__], subject to availability, reasonable usage rules, and fair allocation among tenants. Quantum Labs may charge additional usage fees as described in Article III.

2.4 **Tenant Improvements and Customization.**  
(a) **Initial Fit-Out.** Quantum Labs shall deliver the Premises in a condition consistent with a standard laboratory and office configuration, including (i) finished floors, ceilings, and walls, (ii) basic lab benches and casework, (iii) standard power and data outlets, and (iv) baseline safety features (e.g., eyewash, emergency exits) compliant with Applicable Laws. Any additional tenant-specific improvements requested by Contoso (“Tenant Improvements”) shall be agreed in a written work order specifying scope, cost, and schedule.  

(b) **Approval and Coordination.** All Tenant Improvements shall be subject to Quantum Labs’ prior written approval, not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed. Quantum Labs may require that Tenant Improvements be performed by contractors approved by Quantum Labs, especially where building systems are impacted.  

(c) **Ownership.** Unless otherwise expressly agreed in writing, all permanently affixed Tenant Improvements shall become the property of Quantum Labs upon installation and shall remain with the Premises upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, without obligation to compensate Contoso therefor.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Quantum Labs shall perform the Services:  
(a) in a professional and workmanlike manner;  
(b) using personnel with appropriate qualifications and training;  
(c) in material compliance with the Service Levels in Article XII; and  
(d) in compliance with Applicable Laws and standard industry practices for laboratory facilities.  

Continuous uptime at every moment is not guaranteed; however, Quantum Labs shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize interruptions and to promptly correct service deficiencies, subject to Force Majeure as set forth in Article XV.

2.6 **Contoso’s Obligations Regarding Premises.**  
(a) **Maintenance Within Premises.** Contoso shall be responsible for maintaining, at its own cost, all equipment, furnishings, and property owned or installed by Contoso within the Premises in good condition, reasonable wear and tear excepted.  

(b) **No Overloading.** Contoso shall not overload floors, electrical circuits, or other building systems and shall not install equipment that materially exceeds building design specifications without Quantum Labs’ prior written consent.  

(c) **Hazardous Materials.** Contoso shall handle, store, and dispose of any hazardous materials in full compliance with Applicable Laws and Quantum Labs’ safety and environmental policies. Quantum Labs may require Contoso to provide Material Safety Data Sheets and other documentation for substances used at the Premises.

2.7 **Access Rights and Hours.**  
(a) **Access.** Subject to building security procedures and any reasonable restrictions notified to Contoso in writing, Contoso shall have access to the Premises twenty-four (24) hours per day, seven (7) days per week, excluding temporary closures due to emergencies, repairs, security incidents, or Force Majeure Events.  

(b) **Business Hours Services.** Quantum Labs shall provide standard Services (including regular HVAC levels and staffing) during normal Business Hours (e.g., 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. local time, Monday through Friday, excluding holidays). After-hours or enhanced services may be subject to additional charges as specified in Article III.

2.8 **Inspections.** Quantum Labs may, upon reasonable advance notice (except in case of emergency), enter the Premises to (a) inspect, (b) perform repairs, maintenance, or upgrades, (c) show the Premises to prospective lenders, purchasers, or tenants, or (d) verify compliance with this Agreement. Quantum Labs shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption to Contoso’s operations and, where practicable, to coordinate inspections and work outside Contoso’s critical operations.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES, RENT, AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value.** The total estimated contract value for the Term is **Three Hundred Fifty-Two Thousand Ninety-Six U.S. Dollars (US $352,096)** (the “Contract Value”), which includes Base Rent and estimated Additional Charges, subject to adjustments as provided herein. The Contract Value is not a guarantee or cap on total amounts due but serves as a good faith estimate for planning and internal approval purposes.

3.2 **Base Rent.**  
(a) **Monthly Base Rent.** Contoso shall pay to Quantum Labs fixed monthly Base Rent in the amount of **US $7,500.00** (subject to annual adjustments as described in Section 3.3).  

(b) **Payment.** Base Rent shall be paid in advance, without setoff or deduction (except as expressly permitted herein), on or before the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Term. For any partial calendar month at the beginning or end of the Term, Base Rent shall be prorated on a per diem basis.

3.3 **Annual Rent Adjustments.**  
Beginning on the first anniversary of the Commencement Date and on each anniversary thereafter during the Term, the then-current monthly Base Rent shall increase by **three percent (3%)**. Quantum Labs shall provide a written schedule of adjusted Rent amounts at least thirty (30) days prior to each adjustment.

3.4 **Payment Terms; Net 30.**  
(a) **Invoices.** Quantum Labs shall issue invoices for Base Rent and Additional Charges on a monthly basis, unless otherwise specified.  

(b) **Net 30.** All undisputed amounts invoiced by Quantum Labs shall be due and payable by Contoso within thirty (30) calendar days following the invoice date (“Net 30”).  

(c) **Disputed Amounts.** If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Contoso shall (i) pay the undisputed portion by the due date, and (ii) notify Quantum Labs in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the nature of the dispute in reasonable detail. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve the dispute. Any agreed adjustment shall be reflected on a subsequent invoice or credited against future payments.

3.5 **Additional Charges and Reimbursable Expenses.**  
In addition to Base Rent, Contoso shall pay the following as Additional Charges:  

  3.5.1 **Utilities and Services.** Contoso shall pay its pro rata share or metered share (as applicable) of electricity, water, gas (if any), telecommunications, and other utilities supplied to the Premises, including any demand charges, based on metering, sub-metering, or reasonable allocation by Quantum Labs.  

  3.5.2 **Shared Equipment and Facilities.** Quantum Labs may charge reasonable usage-based fees for certain shared equipment and specialized facilities (e.g., high-end imaging, high-performance computing resources), as documented in a separate fee schedule provided to Contoso.  

  3.5.3 **Special or After-Hours Services.** Any requested enhancements or after-hours services beyond standard levels (e.g., extended HVAC, special cleaning, special security) shall be billed at Quantum Labs’ standard rates, as communicated in writing in advance.

3.6 **Late Payment Penalties.**  
If Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount when due, and such failure continues for more than ten (10) days after written notice from Quantum Labs, then:  
(a) such overdue amount shall accrue interest at the lesser of (i) one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month, or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, from the due date until the date of payment; and  
(b) Quantum Labs may, after an additional ten (10) days’ written notice, suspend non-essential Services until all overdue amounts, including interest, are paid, provided that essential building access and safety systems shall not be unreasonably restricted.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
Contoso shall be responsible for any sales, use, or similar transaction Taxes imposed on Rent or Services to the extent applicable under law, excluding Taxes based on Quantum Labs’ net income, franchise, or corporate existence. If Quantum Labs is required by law to collect such Taxes, Quantum Labs shall separately state them on the invoice.

3.8 **Security Deposit.**  
Upon execution of this Agreement, Contoso shall deposit with Quantum Labs a Security Deposit equal to **one (1) month of then-current Base Rent**. Quantum Labs shall hold the Security Deposit as security for Contoso’s obligations under this Agreement. Quantum Labs may apply all or part of the Security Deposit to cure any default by Contoso. If Quantum Labs applies any portion of the Security Deposit, Contoso shall, within thirty (30) days of written notice, replenish the Security Deposit to its original amount. Any remaining Security Deposit shall be returned to Contoso within sixty (60) days after the later of (a) the Expiration Date or earlier termination date, and (b) the date Contoso vacates and surrenders the Premises in the condition required by this Agreement, less any amounts properly applied to outstanding obligations.

3.9 **No Withholding or Setoff.**  
Except as expressly provided in this Agreement or as required by law, Contoso shall not withhold, offset, or deduct any amounts from Base Rent or Additional Charges on account of any claims, disputes, or alleged breaches by Quantum Labs. All such claims shall be asserted separately.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.**  
The Lease Term shall commence on the Effective Date, **March 12, 2024**, and shall continue in full force and effect until the Expiration Date, **December 19, 2027**, unless earlier terminated pursuant to this Article IV.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Provided that Contoso is not in material default under this Agreement beyond any applicable cure period, Contoso shall have a one-time option to renew the Term for an additional period of **three (3) years** (the “Renewal Term”) upon written notice delivered to Quantum Labs at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior to the Expiration Date. Base Rent during the Renewal Term shall be as mutually agreed in writing, but in no event shall it be less than the then-current Base Rent plus a three percent (3%) increase. If the Parties fail to agree on the Rent for the Renewal Term within ninety (90) days of Contoso’s renewal notice, the Renewal Term shall not commence and the Agreement shall expire on the original Expiration Date.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience, without cause, by providing the other Party with at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice**. During the notice period, all obligations under this Agreement shall continue in full force and effect. Termination for convenience will not relieve Contoso of its obligation to pay all amounts due through the effective termination date, including any unpaid Rent and Additional Charges.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
(a) **By Quantum Labs.** Quantum Labs may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to Contoso if:  
  (i) Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount due hereunder within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice of such failure;  
  (ii) Contoso materially breaches any other provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice thereof (or, if such breach is not reasonably curable within thirty (30) days, fails to commence and diligently pursue cure within thirty (30) days); or  
  (iii) Contoso becomes insolvent, makes a general assignment for the benefit of creditors, or is the subject of any bankruptcy, reorganization, liquidation, or similar proceeding that is not dismissed within ninety (90) days.

(b) **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Agreement upon written notice to Quantum Labs if:  
  (i) Quantum Labs materially fails to provide access to the Premises or materially fails to provide core building Services as required under this Agreement, and such failure continues for more than thirty (30) days after Contoso’s written notice;  
  (ii) Quantum Labs materially breaches any other provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice thereof (or fails to diligently pursue cure); or  
  (iii) Quantum Labs becomes insolvent, makes a general assignment for the benefit of creditors, or is the subject of bankruptcy or similar proceedings not dismissed within ninety (90) days.

4.5 **Effect of Termination or Expiration.**  
Upon expiration or earlier termination of this Agreement:  
(a) Contoso shall vacate and surrender the Premises to Quantum Labs in substantially the same condition as received, reasonable wear and tear and approved Tenant Improvements excepted, and free of Contoso’s personal property;  
(b) Contoso shall remove its personal property, trade fixtures, and any equipment not owned by Quantum Labs, repair any damage caused by such removal, and leave the Premises broom-clean;  
(c) Any licenses granted under this Agreement to access shared equipment or common facilities shall automatically cease, except as necessary to effect orderly transition during any agreed wind-down period; and  
(d) All outstanding undisputed amounts through the effective date of termination shall become immediately due and payable.

4.6 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature should survive expiration or termination shall so survive, including without limitation Sections and Articles relating to confidentiality (Article V), intellectual property (Article VI, to the extent applicable), indemnification (Article VIII), limitation of liability (Article IX), dispute resolution (Article XIV), and any payment obligations accrued prior to termination.

## ARTICLE V

5.2 **Exclusions.**  
Confidential Information does not include information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate:  
(a) is or becomes generally available to the public other than as a result of a disclosure by the Receiving Party in violation of this Agreement;  
(b) was in the Receiving Party’s lawful possession prior to disclosure by the Disclosing Party;  
(c) is lawfully obtained by the Receiving Party from a third party without breach of any confidentiality obligation; or  
(d) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information.

5.3 **Obligations of Confidentiality.**  
The Receiving Party shall:  
(a) use the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information solely for the purposes of performing its obligations or exercising its rights under this Agreement;  
(b) not disclose such Confidential Information to any third party except as permitted under Section 5.4; and  
(c) protect the Confidential Information using at least the same degree of care as it uses to protect its own confidential information of a similar nature, but in no event less than reasonable care.

5.4 **Permitted Disclosures.**  
The Receiving Party may disclose the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information:  
(a) to its and its affiliates’ directors, officers, employees, contractors, and professional advisors who have a need to know such information for the purposes of this Agreement and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Article V; and  
(b) as required by law, regulation, or court order, provided that, to the extent legally permissible, the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party prompt written notice of such requirement and cooperates reasonably in any effort to limit or challenge the required disclosure.

5.5 **Return or Destruction.**  
Upon the Disclosing Party’s written request or upon expiration or termination of this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all copies of the Disclosing Party’s Confidential Information in its possession or control, except that the Receiving Party may retain one (1) archival copy solely for legal, regulatory, or compliance purposes, subject to continued confidentiality obligations.

5.6 **Survival.**  
The obligations of confidentiality and non-use set forth in this Article V shall survive for a period of **five (5) years** following the expiration or termination of this Agreement, except with respect to trade secrets, which shall be protected for so long as they remain trade secrets under Applicable Law.

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLA)

12.1 **Uptime Commitment.**  
Quantum Labs shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that core building systems and utilities necessary for occupancy and operation of the Premises (including electricity, water, HVAC, and network connectivity to the Building demarcation point) are available at least **99.9% of the time**, measured on a calendar quarterly basis, excluding scheduled maintenance and Force Majeure Events.

12.2 **Scheduled Maintenance.**  
Quantum Labs may schedule maintenance windows that may affect service availability. Quantum Labs shall provide Contoso with at least five (5) Business Days’ advance notice for non-emergency maintenance and shall, to the extent practicable, schedule maintenance during off-peak hours.

12.3 **Response Times.**  
Quantum Labs shall respond to Contoso’s service requests as follows:  
(a) **Critical Issues** (e.g., full power outage, major water leakage, safety hazards): initial response within one (1) hour, best efforts to resolve within four (4) hours.  
(b) **High Priority Issues** (e.g., partial HVAC outage affecting lab operations): initial response within four (4) hours, best efforts to resolve within one (1) Business Day.  
(c) **Standard Issues** (e.g., minor repairs, non-urgent cleaning): initial response within two (2) Business Days, reasonable efforts to resolve within five (5) Business Days.

12.4 **SLA Credits.**  
If Quantum Labs materially fails to meet the uptime commitment in Section 12.1 for a given calendar quarter, Contoso shall be entitled, upon written request, to a service credit equal to a pro rata portion of one (1) month’s Base Rent, calculated based on the duration and severity of the outage. Service credits shall be applied against future invoices and shall be Contoso’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for SLA failures, subject to the limitations in Article IX.

12.5 **Exclusions.**  
SLA measurements and credits shall not apply to interruptions or failures caused by:  
(a) Contoso’s equipment, software, or actions;  
(b) any outages resulting from Contoso’s breach of this Agreement;  
(c) scheduled maintenance; or  
(d) Force Majeure Events under Article XV.

## ARTICLE XV  
## FORCE MAJEURE

15.1 **Definition.**  
A “Force Majeure Event” means any event or circumstance beyond the reasonable control of a Party, including but not limited to acts of God, natural disasters, fire, flood, earthquake, epidemic or pandemic, war, terrorism, civil unrest, strikes or labor disputes (excluding those involving the affected Party’s own employees), governmental orders, or failures of public utilities or telecommunications infrastructure.

15.2 **Notice.**  
The affected Party shall promptly notify the other Party in writing upon becoming aware of a Force Majeure Event, describing the nature of the event, anticipated impact, and estimated duration.

15.3 **Suspension of Obligations.**  
During the continuance of a Force Majeure Event, the affected Party shall be excused from performance of its obligations under this Agreement to the extent and for the duration that such performance is prevented or materially delayed by the Force Majeure Event, provided that:  
(a) the affected Party uses commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact; and  
(b) the affected Party resumes performance as soon as reasonably practicable.

15.4 **Extended Force Majeure.**  
If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than sixty (60) consecutive days and materially impairs Contoso’s ability to use the Premises for the Permitted Use, then either Party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days’ prior written notice, without liability other than obligations accrued prior to the effective date of termination.
