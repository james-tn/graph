## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Lease and Services Agreement** dated **April 20, 2024**, bearing Contract Reference Number **LSE-MER-202404-445** and internal identifier **“contract_445”** (the **“Parent Agreement”** or **“Lease Agreement”**), pursuant to which the Vendor leases to the Client certain premises and provides associated services as more particularly described therein;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement (Ref: **LSE-MER-202404-445**, contract_445) established, inter alia, (i) the lease of approximately **15,000 square feet of Grade A office space** and a **300 square feet dedicated server room** at **88 Raffles Quay, Singapore 048952**, as described in **Section 2.2 (Description of Premises)**, (ii) fee and payment terms including an estimated contract value of **USD $2,672,056** and **Net 60** payment terms under **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and (iii) a lease term from **April 20, 2024** through **November 13, 2028**, as set forth in **Section 4.1 (Term)**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to **modify and restate certain lease‑related terms, fee structures, risk allocation, and payment terms** contained in the Parent Agreement, including without limitation provisions in **Article II (Scope of Services and Leased Premises)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article IV (Term and Termination)**, **Article VIII (Indemnification)**, and **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)**, to reflect an adjusted risk profile and revised commercial arrangements;

WHEREAS, this Amendment is intended to (i) define a **distinct amended lease term and commercial framework** for the period from **August 16, 2024** through **June 2, 2027**, with an updated **contract value of USD $3,454,074**, (ii) update the **payment terms to Net 30**, and (iii) recalibrate the **risk level from “High” to “Medium”**, including introduction of a **monetary liability cap** and more balanced indemnification;

WHEREAS, this Amendment **modifies lease terms dated April 20, 2024 (Ref: LSE-MER-202404-445)** and is **entered into pursuant to and in supplementation of** the Parent Agreement (contract_445), and shall be read and construed together with the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained herein and in the Parent Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES, PREMISES, AND LEASE MODIFICATIONS

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Amendment **supplements, modifies, and partially restates** the provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services and Leased Premises)** of the Parent Agreement for the period from the Amendment Effective Date through the Amendment Expiration Date, and shall be read in conjunction with Sections 2.1 through 2.6 thereof.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified herein, the scope of Services and use of the Premises described in **Sections 2.1–2.6** of the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect.

### 2.2 Amended Premises Configuration

2.2.1 As of the Amendment Effective Date, the Premises (as defined in **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement) are hereby re‑configured as follows:

(a) The Client shall continue to lease **approximately 15,000 square feet** of Grade A office space at **88 Raffles Quay, Singapore 048952**, but such space shall be re‑designated as:

  - **Level 20:** approximately **9,000 square feet** (previously approximately 7,500 square feet); and  
  - **Level 21:** approximately **6,000 square feet** (previously approximately 7,500 square feet),

  reflecting a **net expansion of 1,500 square feet** on Level 20 and a **contraction of 1,500 square feet** on Level 21. The precise layout and demarcation are set out in **Annex B–1 (Amended Floor Plans)** attached to this Amendment.

(b) The **dedicated secure server room of approximately 300 square feet** (as originally described in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement) shall be relocated within Level 20 to a zone designated “20‑SR2,” with equivalent or superior raised flooring, cabling, power distribution, and cooling capacity, as certified in an updated acceptance report.

(c) The Client’s **rights to parking bays and storage spaces** (as referenced in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement) shall remain substantively unchanged, save that one (1) additional storage space shall be allocated on Level B2, subject to the building owner’s approval.

2.2.2 The Vendor’s obligations to provide **quiet enjoyment of the Premises** under **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to the Amended Premises Configuration, subject to Client’s compliance with the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

### 2.3 Amended Services

2.3.1 The Parties agree that the **Services** described in **Section 2.3 (Services to be Provided)** of the Parent Agreement shall be **expanded and clarified** as follows:

(a) **Facility Operation and Maintenance Services** (Section 2.3.1):

  - The Vendor shall now provide **enhanced preventive maintenance** for the relocated server room “20‑SR2,” including quarterly thermal imaging of power distribution units and semi‑annual testing of fire suppression systems specific to the server room.
  - The Vendor shall ensure that cleaning services in the server room are performed only by **pre‑approved, security‑cleared personnel** with appropriate training in handling IT environments.

(b) **Fit‑Out and Configuration Services** (Section 2.3.2):

  - The Vendor shall perform **Additional Fit-Out Works** to reflect the Amended Premises Configuration, including re‑partitioning, cabling rerouting, and furniture reallocation.  
  - The Vendor shall deliver updated **as‑built documentation and an acceptance report** for the reconfigured Levels 20 and 21 no later than **sixty (60) days** after the Amendment Effective Date.

(c) **Managed IT and Infrastructure Support (If Ordered)** (Section 2.3.3):

  - For the Amendment Term, the Client confirms its election to procure **managed IT and infrastructure support** as contemplated under Section 2.3.3 of the Parent Agreement.  
  - The Vendor shall provide **24x7 monitoring** of power and environmental conditions in “20‑SR2,” with automated alerts for temperature, humidity, and power thresholds, consistent with the Service Levels in Article XII of the Parent Agreement and Article XII of this Amendment.

(d) **Ancillary Services** (Section 2.3.4):

  - The Vendor shall provide **on‑site reception services** for the Client’s visitors during Business Hours at the Level 20 reception area.  
  - Additional **conference room services** and **mail handling** will be provided as detailed in **Annex A–1 (Revised Fee Schedule)**, on a usage‑based fee model.

2.3.2 Any further expansion or reduction of the Premises or Services beyond those described above shall be handled via **Change Management** under **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement** and Article XIII of this Amendment, and documented through Change Orders referring to both **LSE-MER-202404-445** and **AMD-MER-202404-460**.

### 2.4 Performance Standards

2.4.1 The Vendor’s performance obligations under **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement remain in force, and the Vendor shall continue to perform the Amended Services with due skill, care, and diligence, in accordance with best practices of reputable providers in Singapore.

2.4.2 With respect to the managed infrastructure in “20‑SR2,” the Parties reiterate and refine the Service Levels set out in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) The **99.9% monthly uptime** commitment in **Section 12.1.1** of the Parent Agreement shall apply specifically to the power and cooling availability for “20‑SR2.”

(b) Response and resolution times for **Priority 1 and Priority 2 incidents** as set forth in **Section 12.2.1** of the Parent Agreement shall be deemed incorporated here and shall apply to any outage or severe degradation affecting the dedicated server room.

2.4.3 The Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to coordinate any **Scheduled Downtime** affecting the Amended Premises Configuration with the Client’s business needs, adhering to the conditions in **Section 12.3 (Scheduled Downtime)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.5 Client Responsibilities

2.5.1 The Client shall continue to comply with its obligations under **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, including without limitation maintenance of the interior of the Premises and adherence to building rules and security policies, and shall:

(a) Provide timely feedback and approvals for Additional Fit‑Out Works;  
(b) Ensure that any Client‑appointed contractors coordinate with the Vendor to avoid interference with building systems, consistent with **Section 2.5.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.6 Service Commencement Under Amendment

2.6.1 The reconfigured Premises and Amended Services shall be deemed to commence as of the **Amendment Effective Date**, provided that the Vendor shall complete the physical reconfiguration works within **sixty (60) days** thereof, subject to Force Majeure Events and Change Orders.

2.6.2 Section 2.6 (Service Commencement and Transition) of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to transitions between the original configuration and the Amended Premises Configuration.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS (AMENDED)

### 3.1 Amended Contract Value

3.1.1 For the Amendment Term (as defined in Section 4.1.1), the Parties agree that the **total estimated contract value** is **USD $3,454,074** (the **“Amended Contract Value”**). This amount:

(a) Supersedes the prior estimate of **USD $2,672,056** set forth in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent Agreement **solely with respect to the period from the Amendment Effective Date through the Amendment Expiration Date**; and  

(b) Remains an estimate subject to variation based on actual utilization, Change Orders, and variable fees, consistent with the language in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent Agreement.

### 3.2 Fee Components (Revised)

3.2.1 **Base Rent.** The Client shall pay a revised monthly base rent for the Amended Premises Configuration, as specified in **Annex A–1 (Revised Fee Schedule)**. The allocation will reflect:

(a) A higher rate per square foot for the expanded space on Level 20 due to improved layout and server room adjacency; and  
(b) A commensurately adjusted rate for the reduced space on Level 21.

3.2.2 **Service and Maintenance Fees.** In addition to base rent, the Client shall pay:

(a) Monthly facility service fees (cleaning, security, base‑building maintenance) as restated in Annex A–1;  
(b) Monthly infrastructure maintenance fees for “20‑SR2,” including newly introduced quarterly thermal imaging and additional monitoring;  
(c) Any incremental fees relating to the election of managed IT services under **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Agreement, as detailed in Annex A–1.

3.2.3 **Utilities and Pass‑Through Charges.** The provisions of **Section 3.2.3 (Utilities and Pass‑Through Charges)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply, provided that:

(a) Server room energy usage will be monitored via dedicated sub‑metering where practicable; and  
(b) Cost allocation methodologies will be updated to reflect the Amended Premises Configuration.

3.2.4 **Ancillary Services.** Fees for ancillary services specified in **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent Agreement and expanded in this Amendment (reception, mail handling, conference rooms) shall accrue as incurred and be invoiced in accordance with Annex A–1.

### 3.3 Invoicing and Payment Terms (Superseding Section 3.3.2)

3.3.1 The Vendor shall continue to issue monthly invoices pursuant to **Section 3.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, clearly indicating the amounts attributable to base rent, Services, utilities, and ancillary charges.

3.3.2 **Payment Terms (Net 30).** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.2 (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, for all invoices issued in respect of the Amendment Term and the Amended Services, **all undisputed amounts shall be payable within thirty (30) calendar days from the date of invoice (“Net 30”)**.

3.3.3 The procedure for disputing invoices set forth in **Section 3.3.4** of the Parent Agreement (15 Business Days to notify dispute, obligation to pay undisputed portion) remains applicable.

### 3.4 Late Payment

3.4.1 The interest provisions in **Section 3.4.1** of the Parent Agreement (1.5% per month or maximum lawful rate) remain in effect and shall apply to any undisputed amount outstanding beyond the Net 30 period specified in Section 3.3.2 of this Amendment.

3.4.2 The Vendor’s rights of suspension and termination due to late payment as set forth in **Section 3.4.2** and **Section 3.4.3** of the Parent Agreement remain unchanged.

### 3.5 Taxes

3.5.1 The Tax provisions in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement are hereby reaffirmed and apply in full to the Amended Contract Value and any Fees under this Amendment.

### 3.6 Expenses and Reimbursements

3.6.1 The Parties confirm that **Section 3.6 (Expenses and Reimbursements)** of the Parent Agreement applies to any out‑of‑pocket costs for Additional Fit‑Out Works or Client‑specific requests under this Amendment, subject to prior written approval by the Client.

### 3.7 Adjustments

3.7.1 The fee adjustment mechanics and dispute procedures in **Section 3.7 (Adjustments)** of the Parent Agreement shall remain applicable, provided that annual fee adjustments shall now take into account the Amended Premises Configuration and the updated risk profile under this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDED)

### 4.1 Amendment Term

4.1.1 The **Amendment Term** shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date (August 16, 2024)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **June 2, 2027** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, this Amendment does not shorten or extend the **Lease Term (Original)** under **Section 4.1 of the Parent Agreement**, which continues through **November 13, 2028**, but rather provides a revised commercial and risk framework for the sub‑period of the Lease Term between the Amendment Effective Date and the Amendment Expiration Date.

### 4.2 Renewal

4.2.1 The renewal discussions under **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply to the Lease Agreement as a whole. Any renewal or extension beyond November 13, 2028, or any extension of the Amended commercial terms beyond June 2, 2027, shall require a separate written amendment or agreement.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 The termination-for-cause rights in **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement** (material breach, insolvency, persistent non‑payment) are incorporated into this Amendment. Any material breach of this Amendment shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement and vice versa where the breach affects obligations under both documents.

### 4.4 Termination for Convenience (Amended Notice Period)

4.4.1 Notwithstanding **Section 4.4.1** of the Parent Agreement (180 days’ notice by Client), solely with respect to the Amended Services and fee structure under this Amendment, the Client may terminate this Amendment for convenience by providing not less than **ninety (90) days’ prior written notice** to the Vendor.

4.4.2 The Vendor may terminate this Amendment for convenience by providing not less than **one hundred twenty (120) days’ prior written notice** to the Client, provided that such termination shall not take effect earlier than the **first anniversary of the Amendment Effective Date**, unless otherwise mutually agreed.

4.4.3 Upon termination of this Amendment for convenience by the Client:

(a) The Client shall remain liable for all Fees accrued under this Amendment up to the effective date of termination;  
(b) The Client shall pay any unamortized Additional Fit‑Out Works costs relating specifically to this Amendment, as set forth in Annex A–1 or the relevant fit‑out agreement; and  
(c) Transition assistance shall be provided in accordance with Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement, as adapted for the Amended Premises Configuration.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement

4.5.1 In the event the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, without the need for further action by either Party.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of this Amendment shall not, in and of itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in force for any portions of the Lease Term (Original) not governed by this Amendment, except where expressly stated.

### 4.6 Effects of Termination; Transition

4.6.1 The obligations upon expiration or termination contained in **Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement** (vacating Premises, removal of property, disposal rights, Transition Period) shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Amended Premises Configuration and Amended Services.

4.6.2 Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment (with or without simultaneous termination of the Parent Agreement), the Parties shall:

(a) Document the condition of the Amended Premises Configuration;  
(b) Determine whether any Additional Fit‑Out Works are to remain in place or be removed, and allocate costs accordingly.

## ARTICLE IX – LIMITATION OF LIABILITY (MODIFIED FROM PARENT AGREEMENT)

9.1 **Amendment Supersedes Parent Article IX for Amendment Term**

9.1.1 Notwithstanding **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement, which provides for **no monetary cap and inclusion of consequential damages** in view of the “High” risk profile, the Parties agree that for the **Amendment Term** and solely with respect to claims arising under or in connection with this Amendment or the Amended Services:

(a) A **monetary cap on liability** shall apply as set forth in Section 9.2; and  
(b) Consequential damages shall be excluded as set forth in Section 9.3.

9.1.2 For claims solely and exclusively relating to the period prior to the Amendment Effective Date or unrelated to this Amendment, the original Article IX of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply.

9.2 **Liability Cap**

9.2.1 Subject to the exceptions in Section 9.4, the **aggregate liability** of each Party to the other Party under or in connection with this Amendment (whether in contract, tort, including negligence, or otherwise) shall be **limited to an amount equal to five (5) times the total Fees actually paid by the Client to the Vendor under this Amendment during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**.

9.2.2 Where the event giving rise to the claim occurs within the first twelve (12) months of the Amendment Term, the cap shall be calculated as **five (5) times the Fees paid or payable during such shorter period**, based on invoiced or reasonably anticipated Fees.

9.3 **Exclusion of Certain Damages**

9.3.1 To the maximum extent permitted by Applicable Law, **neither Party shall be liable to the other Party for any indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages**, including but not limited to loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of data, or business interruption, arising out of or in connection with this Amendment.

9.4 **Exceptions to Limitations**

9.4.1 The exclusions and limitations in Sections 9.2 and 9.3 shall **not apply** to:

(a) A Party’s fraud or willful misconduct;  
(b) Personal injury or death caused by a Party’s negligence to the extent such limitation is not permitted by Applicable Law;  
(c) A Party’s breach of confidentiality obligations under **Article V** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment;  
(d) A Party’s breach of data protection obligations under **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment;  
(e) A Party’s indemnification obligations under **Article VIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment; or  
(f) Infringement of Intellectual Property Rights caused by a Party’s acts or omissions, to the extent such infringement is finally adjudicated.

9.4.2 For such exceptions, the limitation of liability in Section 9.2 shall not apply, but the exclusion of punitive damages in **Section 9.2.1 of the Parent Agreement** shall continue to apply.
