## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview.** Vendor shall provide enterprise digital transformation services to Client, including strategic consulting, cloud architecture, application migration, data analytics enablement, and post-implementation stabilization and support. The Services shall be performed in accordance with the terms of this Agreement and the Specifications agreed upon by the Parties.

2.2 **Project Phases.** The Project shall be executed in the following phases; each phase may contain multiple Milestones and Deliverables:

  2.2.1 **Phase 1 – Discovery and Assessment (Estimated: 12 weeks).**  
  Vendor shall:
  - Conduct stakeholder interviews, workshops, and process mapping sessions across Client’s business units.  
  - Perform an assessment of Client’s current IT landscape, including application portfolio, infrastructure, data repositories, integration patterns, and security posture.  
  - Identify dependencies, constraints, and risks related to cloud migration and data analytics.  
  - Produce a **Current State Assessment Report** and a **Gap Analysis Document** detailing gaps between existing capabilities and target-state requirements.  
  - Provide recommendations for target architecture, technology stack, and sequencing of migrations.  

  2.2.2 **Phase 2 – Target Architecture & Roadmap (Estimated: 10 weeks).**  
  Vendor shall:
  - Define a **Target Enterprise Architecture**, including reference architectures for cloud infrastructure, security, integration, and data management.  
  - Design logical and physical architectures for core systems and major workloads slated for migration.  
  - Develop a **Multi-Year Transformation Roadmap**, including prioritized workstreams, dependencies, and resource estimates.  
  - Create a **Security and Compliance Framework** aligned with Client’s policies and Data Protection Laws.  
  - Deliver an **Architecture & Roadmap Pack** to be reviewed and approved by Client.  

  2.2.3 **Phase 3 – Implementation & Migration (Estimated: 14 months).**  
  Vendor shall:
  - Implement the agreed cloud infrastructure topology using Client-approved cloud service providers.  
  - Configure and deploy core platform components (e.g., identity and access management, logging, monitoring, and backup).  
  - Migrate designated applications and data sets into the target environment, using migration runbooks approved by Client.  
  - Implement selected analytics use cases, including data ingestion pipelines, data models, and dashboards.  
  - Execute system integration activities between migrated systems and remaining on-premise systems, as required.  
  - Conduct unit testing, system integration testing, and user acceptance testing support.  

  2.2.4 **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Knowledge Transfer (Estimated: 12 weeks).**  
  Vendor shall:
  - Provide hypercare and stabilization support for migrated systems to ensure operational reliability.  
  - Transition operations to Client’s internal teams and/or third-party managed service providers, as directed by Client.  
  - Develop and deliver training sessions, documentation, and runbooks.  
  - Provide recommendations for continuous improvement and optimization.  

2.3 **Deliverables.** Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, Vendor shall provide the following Deliverables (collectively, the “Deliverables”), each of which shall be subject to Client’s review and acceptance in accordance with **Section 2.7**:

- **D1 – Project Plan and Governance Charter** (including RACI, communication plan, risk register).  
- **D2 – Current State Assessment Report**.  
- **D3 – Gap Analysis and Recommendations Document**.  
- **D4 – Target Enterprise Architecture Diagrams and Specifications**.  
- **D5 – Multi-Year Transformation Roadmap**.  
- **D6 – Security and Compliance Framework Documentation**.  
- **D7 – Migration Runbooks and Cutover Plans**.  
- **D8 – Configuration and Deployment Documentation** for platform components.  
- **D9 – Data Models, Data Pipelines, and Analytics Dashboard Specifications**.  
- **D10 – Testing Strategy, Test Plans, and Test Reports**.  
- **D11 – Operational Runbooks and Support Procedures**.  
- **D12 – Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Sessions**.
