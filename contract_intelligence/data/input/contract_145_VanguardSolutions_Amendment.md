## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK (AMENDMENT)

2.1 **Overall Relationship to Existing SOW and Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is executed under and subject to the Parent Agreement and is intended to **modify, extend, and supplement** the specific Services and Deliverables defined in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Existing SOW. Except as expressly set forth in this Amendment, all provisions of Article II of the Existing SOW shall remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Additional Services described herein.

2.1.2 In the event of a conflict between this Amendment and the Existing SOW, this Amendment shall control with respect to the Additional Services, Additional Deliverables, and matters expressly addressed herein, provided that the overall order of precedence between the Parent Agreement and the SOW, as set forth in **Section 2.1 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 16.2 of the Existing SOW**, remains unchanged (the Parent Agreement controls over the SOW and this Amendment, except where expressly modified in accordance with Section 16.3 of the Parent Agreement).

2.2 **Extension of Program Objective.**  

The Program objective described in **Section 2.2 (Project Objective)** of the Existing SOW is hereby **expanded** and **restated** for the Extended Program as follows:

a. In addition to designing, implementing, and operationalizing an enterprise-wide digital transformation and advanced analytics platform, the Extended Program shall also include:

   i. Scaling the platform to support **global operations**, multiple regions, and additional subsidiaries and business units of the Company not previously in scope under Phase A of the Existing SOW;

   ii. Implementing advanced analytics and machine learning capabilities, including predictive and prescriptive models for financial forecasting, customer personalization, supply-chain optimization, and risk management;

   iii. Establishing a **Center of Excellence (CoE)** for analytics, data governance, and process optimization, including formal operating procedures, role definitions, training curricula, and performance metrics;

   iv. Providing **managed services and ongoing support** for the analytics platform, including monitoring, incident management, change management, and periodic enhancement releases as further described in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this Amendment.

b. The objectives in **Section 2.2 of the Existing SOW** remain in effect and shall be deemed integrated with the objectives above, forming a cohesive Program that continues and expands upon the work performed under Phases A–D.

2.3 **New Phases – Overview.**  

The Extended Program will introduce **three additional phases** following, and partially overlapping with, the completion of Phases B–D set forth in **Section 2.4 of the Existing SOW**:

- **Phase E – Global Rollout & Localization**  
- **Phase F – Advanced Analytics & Automation Expansion**  
- **Phase G – Managed Services, Platform Operations & Continuous Improvement**

These new phases are intended to build on the Deliverables specified in **Section 2.6 of the Existing SOW** (including the Target-State Architecture Blueprint, Data Models, and Transition & Handover Package) and are aligned to the overall **Program** defined in **Section 1.4 of the Existing SOW**.

2.4 **Detailed Scope – Phase E: Global Rollout & Localization.**  

Phase E shall include, at a minimum, the following Additional Services and Additional Deliverables:

a. **Global Deployment Planning.** Vanguard shall develop a **Global Deployment Plan** that extends the Project Plan defined in **Section 1.8 of the Existing SOW** and further detailed in **Section 2.5 and 2.7 of the Existing SOW**, covering:

   i. Regional rollout waves (e.g., North America, EMEA, APAC, LATAM), including localized timelines and resource plans;

   ii. Regulatory and compliance considerations by region (e.g., data residency, local privacy laws to supplement **Article XI – Data Protection and Privacy** of the Existing SOW);

   iii. Local system integration mapping, building on the integration specifications described in **Section 2.5(b)(ii)** of the Existing SOW.

b. **Localization of Data Models and Dashboards.** Vanguard shall adapt the data models, dashboards, and reporting artifacts delivered under **Phase C of the Existing SOW**, to:

   i. Support multiple currencies, languages, and tax regimes;

   ii. Incorporate additional regional data sources and custom fields;

   iii. Produce region-specific KPI dashboards while maintaining a consistent global data model.

c. **Regional Training & Change Management.** Vanguard shall extend the training services described in **Section 2.5(d)** of the Existing SOW to provide:

   i. Regional training sessions (remote and, if agreed, onsite) in time zones convenient for local stakeholders;

   ii. Translated or localized training materials, where feasible and reasonably requested by the Company;

   iii. Localized adoption strategies aligned to the governance forums described in **Section 2.5(d)(iii)** of the Existing SOW.

d. **Deliverables for Phase E** shall include, without limitation:

   - Global Deployment Plan;  
   - Regional Integration Addenda;  
   - Localized Data Models and Dashboard Templates;  
   - Regional Training Material Packs;  
   - Updated Governance and Operating Model Documentation reflecting global governance structures.

2.5 **Detailed Scope – Phase F: Advanced Analytics & Automation Expansion.**  

Phase F shall focus on deepening analytics capabilities and automating critical business processes:

a. **Advanced Analytics Use Cases.** Vanguard shall develop no fewer than **fifteen (15)** additional advanced analytics use cases, beyond the twelve (12) use cases described in **Section 2.5(c)(i)** of the Existing SOW. These may include, by way of example:

   i. Multi-variate revenue and margin forecasting models;  
   ii. Customer lifetime value and next-best-offer models;  
   iii. Predictive maintenance and operations optimization models;  
   iv. Fraud detection, anomaly detection, and risk scoring.

b. **Automation & Orchestration.** Building on **Section 2.5(c)(iv) of the Existing SOW** (process optimization and automation), Vanguard shall:

   i. Identify and prioritize at least ten (10) high-impact processes for automation (e.g., financial close, order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, service ticket routing);

   ii. Design and implement automation workflows using tools agreed by the Parties, integrating with existing systems (ERP, CRM, HR, etc.);

   iii. Deliver automation runbooks and monitoring dashboards.

c. **Model Governance & MLOps.** Vanguard shall design and implement a **Model Governance Framework**, including:

   i. Standards for model development, validation, and deployment;

   ii. Versioning, monitoring, and retraining practices (MLOps);

   iii. Roles and responsibilities within the CoE developed under this Phase and Phase D of the Existing SOW.

d. **Deliverables for Phase F** shall include:

   - Advanced Analytics Use-Case Design Documents;  
   - Deployed Models and Supporting Pipelines;  
   - Automation Workflow Designs and Implementation Artifacts;  
   - Model Governance Framework Documentation;  
   - MLOps Runbooks and Operational Metrics Dashboards.

2.6 **Detailed Scope – Phase G: Managed Services, Platform Operations & Continuous Improvement.**  

Phase G shall provide ongoing support and continuous improvement services:

a. **Managed Platform Operations.** Vanguard shall provide ongoing support and operations for the analytics platform implemented under Phases B and C of the Existing SOW, including:

   i. Monitoring data pipelines, platform availability, and performance, consistent with the Enhanced Service Levels described in **Article XII of this Amendment**;

   ii. Incident management, root-cause analysis, and remediation;

   iii. Deployment of agreed enhancements and minor changes via a documented change management process aligned with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Existing SOW and this Amendment.

b. **Continuous Improvement & Backlog Management.** Vanguard shall work with the Company’s designated project manager (as described in **Section 2.10 of the Existing SOW**) to:

   i. Maintain and prioritize a backlog of enhancement requests, new use cases, and process improvement ideas;

   ii. Conduct periodic (e.g., quarterly) planning cycles to agree scope and priorities;

   iii. Deliver incremental improvements in agile sprints or releases.

c. **Operational Reporting & Governance.** Vanguard shall provide regular reporting to the governance forums described in **Section 2.5(d)(iii)** of the Existing SOW and the Program Governance Framework in **Section 2.7 of this Amendment**, including:

   i. Monthly operational performance reports;  
   ii. SLA performance summaries;  
   iii. Risk, issue, and dependency tracking updates.

d. **Deliverables for Phase G** shall include:

   - Operational Runbooks;  
   - Incident and Problem Management Procedures;  
   - Monthly Operational and SLA Reports;  
   - Backlog and Roadmap Documents;  
   - Periodic Continuous Improvement Release Notes.

2.7 **Program Governance Framework.**  

2.7.1 The project management and reporting obligations outlined in **Section 2.3(g)** and **Section 2.7 (Timeline and Milestones)** of the Existing SOW are hereby **supplemented** to establish a Program Governance Framework consisting of:

   a. A **Steering Committee** including senior executives from both Parties, meeting at least **quarterly** to review strategic alignment, budget, risks, and overall Program health;

   b. An **Operational Governance Council** (e.g., analytics steering committee, data governance council), meeting at least **monthly**, to review operational performance, backlog prioritization, and adherence to governance standards; and

   c. **Working Groups** (e.g., Data Engineering, Analytics, Change Management) meeting as needed to coordinate implementation work.

2.7.2 Vanguard shall prepare and distribute meeting agendas, materials, and minutes for Steering Committee and Operational Governance Council meetings, and shall track action items and decisions.

2.8 **Timelines and Milestones (Amended).**  

2.8.1 The timeline and milestones set forth in **Section 2.7 of the Existing SOW** are hereby extended and supplemented. In addition to Milestones M1–M6 described therein, the following milestones are added:

- **M7 – Completion of Global Deployment Plan and First Regional Go-Live (Phase E)** – targeted within twelve (12) months after the Amendment Effective Date;  
- **M8 – Completion of all Phase E Regional Rollouts** – targeted within twenty-four (24) months after the Amendment Effective Date;  
- **M9 – Deployment of at least ten (10) advanced analytics use cases and five (5) automation workflows under Phase F** – targeted within eighteen (18) months after the Amendment Effective Date;  
- **M10 – Deployment of all Phase F committed use cases and automation workflows** – targeted within thirty (30) months after the Amendment Effective Date;  
- **M11 – Initiation of Phase G Managed Services** – targeted no later than the earlier of completion of Phase F or twenty-four (24) months after the Amendment Effective Date;  
- **M12 – Completion of the Extended Program and conclusion of Managed Services** – no later than the **Revised SOW Term Expiration Date of April 14, 2028**, unless otherwise extended via Change Order.

2.8.2 The Parties shall update the **Project Plan** (as defined in **Section 1.8 of the Existing SOW**) within thirty (30) days of the Amendment Effective Date to reflect the milestones, dependencies, and resource requirements for Phases E–G.

2.9 **Acceptance of Additional Deliverables.**  

2.9.1 The Acceptance procedures set forth in **Section 2.9 of the Existing SOW** shall apply to Additional Deliverables under this Amendment, with the following clarifications:

   a. The Company shall review and accept or reasonably reject each Additional Deliverable within **ten (10) Business Days** of delivery, consistent with Section 2.9(a) of the Existing SOW;

   b. The concept of “deemed Acceptance” for lack of response, and the correction and resubmission process in Section 2.9(b)–(c) of the Existing SOW, shall apply to all Additional Deliverables;

   c. The Parties reiterate that recommendations and guidance in Deliverables are advisory, and ultimate business outcomes remain the responsibility of the Company, as noted in **Section 2.9(d) of the Existing SOW**.

2.10 **Company Responsibilities – Extended.**  

The Company responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.10 of the Existing SOW** are hereby reaffirmed and extended to the Additional Services and Additional Deliverables described in this Amendment. In particular:

a. The Company shall provide appropriate regional stakeholders, subject matter experts, and decision-makers to participate in global deployment planning, localization, and CoE establishment;

b. The Company shall ensure timely provision of information, system access, and approvals required for Phase E–G work, recognizing that Vanguard’s obligations and timelines are contingent upon such cooperation;

c. Any additional or special responsibilities of the Company related to compliance with regional data protection laws (in addition to those described in **Article XI of the Existing SOW and the Parent Agreement**) shall be documented in writing and mutually agreed.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS (AMENDMENT)

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 In addition to the **SOW Contract Value of US $1,524,969** set forth in **Section 3.1 of the Existing SOW**, the Parties hereby agree to an **additional Contract Value** for this Amendment in the amount of:

> **Three Million Two Hundred Fifty-One Thousand Five Hundred Fourteen U.S. Dollars (US $3,251,514)**

(the **“Amendment Contract Value”**).

As with **Section 3.1 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement**, this amount is **not a guaranteed maximum or minimum** and may be adjusted via **Change Orders** under **Article XIII of the Existing SOW and this Amendment**.

3.2 **Fee Structure for Additional Services.**  

The Amendment Contract Value shall be allocated as follows:

a. **Phase E – Global Rollout & Localization**  
   - **Fixed Fee Component:** **US $1,050,000**  
   - Invoicing tied to milestones:  
     i. 25% upon completion and mutual approval of the Global Deployment Plan;  
     ii. 35% upon completion of first regional go-live (Milestone M7);  
     iii. 40% upon completion of all agreed regional rollouts (Milestone M8).

b. **Phase F – Advanced Analytics & Automation Expansion**  
   - **Hybrid Model:** Fixed + Time-and-Materials (T&M)  
   - **Fixed Fee Portion:** **US $1,100,000** for design and initial deployment of at least ten (10) advanced analytics use cases and five (5) automation workflows;

   - T&M billed monthly in arrears and not to exceed the target budget without an approved Change Order.

c. **Phase G – Managed Services, Platform Operations & Continuous Improvement**  
   - **Managed Services Fee:** **US $450,000**  
   - Billed as a recurring fee (e.g., quarterly) over the Managed Services period, as reflected in the updated Project Plan and associated schedule;  
   - Scope includes core platform operations, monitoring, incident management, and agreed capacity for continuous improvement work, as described in **Section 2.6 of this Amendment**.

   c. This Amendment does **not** amend Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement globally, but only as it applies to the Incremental Services described herein.

3.4.1 Invoicing for Additional Services shall be carried out in accordance with:

   - **Section 3.4 (Invoicing)** of the Parent Agreement; and  
   - **Section 3.4 of the Existing SOW**, as supplemented hereby.

Late payment provisions in **Section 3.5 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 3.5 of the Existing SOW** shall apply to invoices issued under this Amendment. The Company shall timely notify Vanguard of any disputed portion of an invoice, pay undisputed amounts, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve such disputes.

3.7.2 Travel and out-of-pocket expenses specifically related to regional deployments and on-site workshops under Phases E–G shall be pre-approved by the Company and invoiced at cost with reasonable supporting documentation.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDMENT)

4.1 **Revised SOW Term.**  

4.1.1 **Section 4.1 (Term of SOW)** of the Existing SOW is hereby **amended and restated** solely with respect to the overall SOW Term as follows:

> “This SOW shall commence on the SOW Effective Date and shall continue in effect until **April 14, 2028** (the **‘SOW Expiration Date’**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV of the SOW or Article IV of the Parent Agreement (the **‘SOW Term’**).”

4.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, the Extended Program and Managed Services under Phases E–G shall be scheduled and performed such that completion and orderly transition occur on or before the Revised SOW Term Expiration Date, unless extended by mutual written agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  

4.2.1 The provisions of **Section 4.2 (Relationship to Parent Agreement Term)** of the Existing SOW remain in full force and effect, subject to the extended SOW Term.  

4.2.2 The Parties acknowledge that if the Parent Agreement expires on **October 6, 2026** and is not renewed or extended, the SOW (as amended) may **automatically terminate** as of that date in accordance with **Section 4.2 of the Existing SOW**, unless the Parties enter into a separate written agreement allowing this SOW to survive or replacing the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

The termination for convenience rights in **Section 4.3 of the Existing SOW** remain unchanged and shall apply to the Amended SOW, including:

a. The Company’s right to terminate for convenience upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**;

b. The Service Provider’s right to terminate for convenience upon at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice**; and

c. The Company’s obligation to pay all outstanding Fees, expenses, non-cancellable commitments, and the early termination fee (ten percent (10%) of remaining unbilled Contract Value) consistent with **Section 4.3(c) of the Parent Agreement and Existing SOW**. For purposes of calculating such fee, **both** the remaining unbilled portion of the original SOW Contract Value **and** the remaining unbilled portion of the Amendment Contract Value shall be included.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Termination for cause shall continue to be governed by **Section 4.4 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement**. A material breach of this Amendment shall constitute a material breach of the Existing SOW. The cure periods and conditions for termination remain as stated in the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition Services (Supplement).**  

4.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of the Amended SOW, the provisions of **Section 4.5 and 4.6 of the Existing SOW** shall apply, including:

   - Cessation of work, except as necessary for agreed transition assistance;  
   - Payment obligations for Services performed and expenses incurred;  
   - Return or destruction of Confidential Information pursuant to **Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement**; and  
   - Survival of license rights and other obligations in accordance with **Article VI of the Existing SOW and Article VI of the Parent Agreement**.
