## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties previously entered into the Parent Agreement, reference number **DPA-EPS-202404-459** (“contract_459”), effective as of **April 17, 2024** (the “Effective Date” of the Parent Agreement), under which Epsilon agreed to provide data hosting, processing, analytics, and related managed services to Contoso, as more fully described in **Article II – Scope of Services / Work** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established the roles of the Parties as Controller/Business (Contoso) and Processor/Service Provider (Epsilon) under **Article XI – Data Protection and Privacy**, including Epsilon’s obligations to process Personal Data only on documented instructions from Contoso (Section 11.2), to implement appropriate security measures (Section 11.5), and to notify Contoso of Data Breaches within forty‑eight (48) hours (Section 11.7);

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement contemplated that the Parties may agree to additional Services and Deliverables through Statements of Work (“SOWs”) pursuant to **Section 1.16** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, and also contemplated the amendment of its terms pursuant to **Section 16.3 (Amendments)**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to expand and further define the processing activities performed by Epsilon on behalf of Contoso, including (i) the introduction of new processing use cases, (ii) additional data sources and categories of Personal Data, (iii) cross‑jurisdictional analytics and advanced profiling capabilities, and (iv) enhanced service levels and reporting, all of which constitute “processing” within the meaning of **Section 1.13 (Processing)** and are to be performed under the data protection framework of **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement remains in full force and effect until its Expiration Date of **February 05, 2027**, and that this Addendum is intended to supplement, and not to replace, the Parent Agreement, except as expressly set forth herein;

WHEREAS, in consideration of the expanded Services under this Addendum, the Parties agree to a separate **contract value of US $3,709,456** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), together with associated Fees and payment terms that are consistent with **Article III – Fees and Payment Terms** of the Parent Agreement (including the “Net 30” payment terms set forth in **Section 3.2**);

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to set forth the additional terms and conditions applicable to such expanded processing activities, including the relationship of this Addendum to the Parent Agreement and the impact on the rights and obligations of the Parties thereunder;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, terms, and conditions set forth in this Addendum and in the Parent Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Addendum expressly supplements **Article II – Scope of Services / Work** of the Parent Agreement. The general description of Services in **Section 2.1(a)–(e)** of the Parent Agreement (data hosting and storage, data processing and analytics, configuration and integration services, support and maintenance, and professional services) remains in full force and effect and is expanded by this Addendum as follows:

  a. Epsilon shall perform additional **Enhanced Processing Activities** involving new categories of Personal Data and additional processing purposes described herein, all of which constitute “Processing” under **Section 1.13** of the Parent Agreement.

  b. Epsilon shall extend the existing configuration and integration scope under **Section 2.1(c)** of the Parent Agreement to include new data sources, cross‑regional connectors, and regional segmentation logic.

  c. Epsilon shall provide additional professional and managed services under **Section 2.1(e)** of the Parent Agreement, specifically targeted at advanced analytics and regulatory reporting.

2.2 **Enhanced Processing Activities and Use Cases.**  
Under this Addendum, Epsilon shall perform the following **Advanced Analytics Use Cases**, each of which involves Processing of Personal Data strictly in accordance with **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum:

  a. **Customer Journey Analytics.** Integration of multi‑channel interaction data (web, mobile app, call center, in‑store) to generate a unified view of Data Subjects’ engagements with Contoso, including event‑level logs, session identifiers, and marketing campaign attributes, solely for Contoso’s internal business optimization and subject to Contoso’s documented instructions under **Section 11.2** of the Parent Agreement.

  b. **Churn and Retention Modeling.** Development and deployment of predictive models to identify Data Subjects with a higher risk of disengagement, using historical purchase data, support history, and engagement metrics, in line with the legal basis established by Contoso and consistent with **Section 7.3(b)** (Contoso Warranties) of the Parent Agreement.

  c. **Behavioral Segmentation.** Profiling of Data Subjects into behavioral segments to support personalized communications, product recommendations, and service improvements, provided that such profiling is performed in compliance with GDPR requirements (e.g., Articles 21 and 22, as applicable) and subject to Contoso’s configuration of opt‑out, objection, or consent mechanisms as required by Applicable Data Protection Laws.

  d. **Operational Analytics and Forecasting.** Processing of workforce scheduling data, capacity utilization metrics, and quality indicators (which may include limited Personal Data of Contoso personnel) to optimize staffing, resource allocation, and service quality, exclusively for Contoso’s internal use.

  e. **Regulatory and Compliance Reporting Support.** Aggregation and pseudonymized analysis of Personal Data to assist Contoso in generating reports for regulatory filings, internal audits, and compliance dashboards, leveraging the security measures required under **Section 11.5** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Addendum Deliverables.**  
In addition to the Deliverables described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement, Epsilon shall provide the following **Addendum Deliverables** under this Addendum:

  a. **Regional Data Lake Architecture Documentation.** A comprehensive design document describing the logical and physical architecture of the Regional Data Lake, including segregation of data by jurisdiction, use of encryption, backup strategies, and data flow diagrams, aligned with **Section 2.2(b) (Data Mapping and Schema Documentation)** of the Parent Agreement.

  b. **Data Category Matrix.** A formal Data Category Matrix specifying, for each processing purpose described in **Section 2.2** of this Addendum: (i) categories of Personal Data (e.g., contact details, transaction history, behavioral data); (ii) categories of Data Subjects (customers, prospects, employees, contractors); (iii) jurisdictions; (iv) retention rules; and (v) relevant legal bases as determined by Contoso.

  c. **Advanced Analytics Dashboards and Models.**  
     - At least six (6) new or materially enhanced dashboards focusing on customer journey, churn risk, segment performance, and forecast accuracy; and  
     - Model documentation including data sources, feature engineering logic, performance metrics, and limitations.

  d. **Security Add‑Ons Implementation Plan.** A written implementation plan that describes the additional security measures, including advanced logging, audit trails, and monitoring capabilities, consistent with **Section 11.5** and **Section 12.3** (Incident Response and Support) of the Parent Agreement.

  e. **User and Administrator Guides (Addendum‑Specific).** Extended documentation for Contoso administrators and business users describing configuration options, data privacy settings, subject rights workflows, and data export capabilities relevant to the Enhanced Processing Activities.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree to the following target milestones under this Addendum:

  a. **Phase A – Requirements and Design (0–60 days after Addendum Effective Date).**  
     - Completion of detailed requirements workshops for each Advanced Analytics Use Case.  
     - Delivery of initial Regional Data Lake Architecture Documentation and draft Data Category Matrix.  

  b. **Phase B – Implementation and Pilot (60–180 days).**  
     - Configuration of new integrations and ingestion pipelines for at least three primary data sources across two regions.  
     - Development of initial predictive models and dashboards; completion of pilot testing for a limited user group designated by Contoso.

  c. **Phase C – Production Rollout (180–270 days).**  
     - Production deployment of the Regional Data Lake for at least two major regions (e.g., US and EU).  
     - Production deployment of three core dashboards and two predictive models, following Contoso’s acceptance.  

  d. **Phase D – Optimization and Expansion (270 days onward).**  
     - Iterative tuning of models and dashboards based on user feedback.  
     - Addition of further regions and data sources in accordance with approved Change Requests under **Article XIII**.

The milestone dates above are target dates and are subject to change in accordance with **Section 2.3(e)** and **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement. Epsilon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet such milestones and shall provide prompt notice of delays and remediation plans as required by **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
The performance standards in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement apply fully to the Addendum Services. In particular, Epsilon shall:

  a. perform the Addendum Services in a professional and workmanlike manner and in compliance with generally accepted industry standards, as required by **Section 2.3(a)** and **Section 7.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement;

  b. process Personal Data in compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws, and in accordance with **Article XI – Data Protection and Privacy** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Addendum;

  c. adhere to the security and privacy obligations set forth in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and implement the Security Add‑Ons described in **Section 2.3(d)** above;

  d. meet or exceed the Service Levels set out in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and extended by **Article XII** of this Addendum; and

  e. provide regular status reports and documentation as reasonably requested by Contoso, including monthly summaries of model performance and data incident logs relevant to the Addendum Services.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities (Addendum‑Specific).**  
In addition to its responsibilities under **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  a. designate business owners and data protection contacts for each Advanced Analytics Use Case to provide timely approvals and instructions;

  b. ensure that its privacy notices, consent mechanisms, and internal policies are updated, as necessary, to reflect the Enhanced Processing Activities under this Addendum, consistent with **Section 7.3(a)-(b)** of the Parent Agreement;

  c. promptly inform Epsilon of any material change in its legal assessment or instructions that would affect the lawfulness of processing under this Addendum; and

  d. review and approve the Data Category Matrix and any updates thereto, and provide instructions on retention periods and deletion requirements consistent with **Section 11.10** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Subprocessors.**  
Any use of Subprocessors for Addendum Services shall remain subject to **Section 2.6** and **Section 11.8** of the Parent Agreement. Epsilon shall ensure that all Subprocessors engaged for Enhanced Processing Activities are bound by written data protection terms no less protective than those in the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
In consideration of the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables, Contoso shall pay Epsilon Fees totaling **US $3,709,456** over the Term of this Addendum (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), which is separate from and in addition to the **US $1,326,272** Contract Value under **Article III** of the Parent Agreement. This Addendum Contract Value reflects the increased scope, additional processing activities, and enhanced service levels described herein.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  
Unless otherwise agreed in a schedule to this Addendum:

  a. **Implementation and Onboarding Fees.**  
     A one‑time implementation fee of **US $742,000** for design, configuration of the Regional Data Lake, initial integrations, and development of the first set of models and dashboards.

  b. **Subscription / Platform Extension Fees.**  
     Recurring Fees of **US $468,000 per contract year** (prorated for partial years) for access to and use of the enhanced analytics modules, Regional Data Lake, and Security Add‑Ons.

  c. **Managed Services and Optimization Fees.**  
     Recurring Fees of **US $280,000 per contract year** for ongoing model monitoring, tuning, data quality management, additional reporting, and extended support described in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

  d. **Professional Services Fees.**  
     Additional custom work requested by Contoso beyond the defined Addendum scope shall be billed on a time‑and‑materials basis at Epsilon’s then‑current professional services rates, subject to prior written approval by Contoso and in accordance with **Section 3.1(d)** and **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
Payment terms shall be **Net 30**, consistent with **Section 3.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement:

  a. Epsilon shall invoice Contoso for recurring Fees (subscription and managed services) **monthly in advance**, consistent with **Section 3.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise specified in a schedule to this Addendum.

  b. Implementation and Professional Services Fees shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears** based on actual work performed, consistent with **Section 3.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement, or in accordance with any milestone‑based billing agreed in writing.

  c. All undisputed invoices shall be due and payable by Contoso within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  
The late payment provisions in **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all amounts due under this Addendum. Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the rate set forth in Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement, and Epsilon shall provide notice of overdue amounts before assessing late charges.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Contoso may dispute any invoiced amount in good faith in accordance with **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement. The Parties shall work diligently and in good faith to resolve any such disputes, and Contoso shall timely pay all undisputed amounts while the dispute is being resolved.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
All Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of applicable taxes, which shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement.

3.7 **Expenses.**  
Reasonable, pre‑approved travel and other out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred by Epsilon in connection with Addendum‑specific workshops or on‑site engagements shall be reimbursed by Contoso in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

3.8 **No Setoff.**  
Except as expressly permitted under the Parent Agreement or this Addendum, or required by law, Contoso shall not withhold, set off, or deduct amounts from payments due, except in respect of bona fide disputed amounts, consistent with **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Addendum.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **January 06, 2030** (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
The Term of the Parent Agreement remains as defined in **Section 4.1** thereof (through February 05, 2027). Notwithstanding any earlier expiration of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that:

  a. The data protection, confidentiality, and related obligations of Epsilon and Contoso arising under **Articles V, XI, and XVI** of the Parent Agreement will continue to apply to any Personal Data processed under this Addendum for as long as such Personal Data is retained by Epsilon in accordance with Contoso’s instructions and Applicable Data Protection Laws.

  b. To the extent the Addendum Term extends beyond the Parent Agreement’s Expiration Date, the Parties agree that the relevant provisions of the Parent Agreement necessary to give effect to this Addendum (including but not limited to **Articles V, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI**) shall be deemed incorporated into and to survive solely for purposes of this Addendum, unless otherwise superseded by mutual written agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience and without cause, upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. This notice period reflects the increased complexity and medium‑risk nature of the Enhanced Processing Activities, while remaining commercially reasonable. For clarity, termination of this Addendum does not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement with respect to the Addendum Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail, consistent with **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Data Protection Breach.**  
Without limiting **Section 4.4** above or **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, upon written notice if Epsilon materially violates Applicable Data Protection Laws or its obligations under **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement or **Article XI** of this Addendum in connection with the Enhanced Processing Activities and, where curable, fails to cure such violation within fifteen (15) days of written notice.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  a. Contoso shall pay Epsilon all undisputed Fees and authorized expenses accrued and payable for Addendum Services up to the effective date of termination.

  b. Epsilon shall cease Processing of Personal Data solely associated with the Addendum Services (except to the extent continued Processing is required by Applicable Data Protection Laws) and shall, at Contoso’s written direction, return or securely delete such Personal Data in accordance with **Section 11.10** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 11.3** of this Addendum.

  c. Epsilon shall provide Contoso, upon request and at mutually agreed rates, with reasonable transition assistance to enable Contoso to migrate data and models to another platform or service provider.

  d. Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information related to this Addendum in accordance with **Section 5.5** of the Parent Agreement and **Article V** of this Addendum.

4.7 **Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain this Addendum (or any portion thereof) in force under separately renegotiated terms that maintain at least equivalent data protection safeguards.

4.8 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Addendum that, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration (including but not limited to **Articles III (with respect to unpaid Fees), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI**, and Section 4.2) shall survive in accordance with their terms and the corresponding survival provisions of **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement.

12.1 **Application of Parent Agreement SLA.**

12.2 **Service Level Extension for Addendum Services.**  
In addition, the Parties agree to the following Service Level Extensions for the components specifically introduced by this Addendum:

  a. **Model Execution Availability.**  
     Epsilon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the core model execution services for Advanced Analytics Use Cases are available at least **99.5%** of the time during each calendar month, excluding Scheduled Maintenance and Force Majeure Events.

  b. **Data Refresh Windows.**  
     For daily batch refreshes of analytics datasets used in Addendum dashboards, Epsilon shall target completion within **four (4) hours** of the agreed start time for at least 95% of scheduled runs in each calendar month.

  c. **Issue Response Time for Addendum‑Critical Incidents.**  
     For Priority 1 issues that specifically affect Addendum Services (e.g., analytics dashboards unavailable or material errors in model outputs), Epsilon shall provide an initial response within **one (1) hour** and will use commercially reasonable efforts to restore basic functionality as soon as practicable, taking into account impact and complexity.

If Epsilon fails to meet the Service Level Extensions in **Section 12.2**, Contoso shall be entitled to additional service credits, calculated as a percentage of the monthly Fees attributable to the Addendum Services:

  - Failure to meet 99.5% model execution availability but achieving at least 98.5%: **2%** credit;  
  - Model execution availability below 98.5%: **5%** credit;  
  - Data refresh SLA (95% completion within window) missed by more than ten (10) percentage points in a month: **3%** credit.

Such service credits shall be applied against subsequent invoices and represent Contoso’s sole monetary remedy for SLA failures specific to the Addendum Services, without prejudice to termination rights under **Article IV** of this Addendum and **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement in the case of repeated or material SLA failures.

12.4 **Exclusions.**  
The exclusions in **Section 12.5** of the Parent Agreement (downtime due to Contoso’s systems, misuse, third‑party products, Scheduled Maintenance, or Force Majeure Events) apply to the extended SLAs in this Article XII.
