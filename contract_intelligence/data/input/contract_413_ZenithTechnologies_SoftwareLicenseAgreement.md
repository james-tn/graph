4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

  (a) materially breaches any provision of this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof (or, if such breach is not capable of cure within thirty (30) days, fails to commence and diligently pursue cure within such period); or  

  (b) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, has a receiver appointed for a substantial portion of its assets, or ceases to do business in the ordinary course.

4.3.2 Zenith may terminate this Agreement or any affected SOW upon written notice if Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount due hereunder within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice of such failure.

4.4.1 Upon any termination or expiration of this Agreement:

  (a) all licenses and rights granted to Contoso in the Software and Documentation shall immediately cease;  
  (b) Contoso shall immediately cease all access to and use of the Software; and

4.5.1 Upon Contoso’s request made prior to the effective date of termination or expiration, Zenith shall provide reasonable transition assistance (such as data export assistance) for up to thirty (30) days following termination or expiration, subject to payment of Zenith’s then-current time and materials rates and reimbursement of reasonable expenses.
