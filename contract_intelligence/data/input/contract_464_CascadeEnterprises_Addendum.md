## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent SOW, Ref. No. **SOW-CAS-202409-423** (internal identifier **contract_423**) as of September 11, 2024 (the “Effective Date” under the Parent SOW), pursuant to which Cascade agreed to provide, and Contoso agreed to pay for, certain implementation, integration, and managed support services relating to a cloud-based enterprise operations platform (the “Platform”) as more fully described in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent SOW;

WHEREAS, the Parent SOW establishes, among other things, definitions in **Article I**, scope and phases of work in **Article II**, fees and payment terms including a total **Contract Value** of **US $390,646** and **Net 45** payment terms in **Article III**, and term and termination provisions in **Article IV**, with an **Expiration Date** of **May 29, 2027**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to significantly expand the scope, duration, and value of the engagement, including extension of managed services beyond the Parent SOW Expiration Date, additional project phases, enhanced Service Levels under **Article XII** of the Parent SOW, and additional deliverables and responsibilities not contemplated under the original Contract Value;

WHEREAS, the Parties further desire to **supplement, modify, and expand** certain provisions of the Parent SOW, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article IV (Term and Termination)**, and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, in order to accommodate the increased **Contract Value** under this Addendum of **US $3,287,626**, updated payment terms of **Net 30**, and a new expiration date for the services covered by this Addendum of **July 31, 2028**;

WHEREAS, this Addendum is designated as high risk in favor of the Service Provider, and therefore certain provisions herein, including without limitation **Article VIII (Indemnification)** and **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)**, are more favorable to the Service Provider than those set forth in the Parent SOW, and in the event of conflict the provisions of this Addendum shall prevail with respect to the subject matter hereof;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises and covenants contained herein and in the Parent SOW, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent SOW

2.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables described in this **Article II** are **in addition to and not in substitution for** the “Services” and “Deliverables” defined and described in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent SOW.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified in this Addendum, all provisions of **Article II** of the Parent SOW remain in full force and effect, and the Extended Services shall be performed in accordance with the same standards set forth in **Section 2.3.1** of the Parent SOW (professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with generally accepted industry standards and Applicable Law).

2.1.3 In the event of any direct conflict between this **Article II** and **Article II** of the Parent SOW, this **Article II** shall control solely with respect to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables covered by this Addendum.

### 2.2 Overview of Extended Project

2.2.1 Under this Addendum, the Service Provider shall design, implement, and support an **enterprise-wide multi-region deployment and optimization** of the Platform, building upon the initial implementation phases (Phases 1–5) described in **Sections 2.2.1–2.2.5** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.2 The Extended Services shall include, without limitation:

- Global rollout of the Platform to multiple business units and geographic regions;
- Advanced analytics and reporting enablement, including predictive modeling;
- Complex integration extensions (e.g., with additional ERP, HR, data warehouse, and third-party SaaS systems not included in the Parent SOW);
- Extended managed support and optimization through July 31, 2028;
- Enhanced governance, compliance, and audit capabilities.

2.2.3 The Extended Services will be structured into the following additional phases, which follow and overlay Phases 1–5 of the Parent SOW:

- **Phase 6 – Global Design Harmonization and Blueprinting**  
- **Phase 7 – Multi-Region Rollout Implementation**  
- **Phase 8 – Advanced Analytics and Optimization**  
- **Phase 9 – Extended Managed Services and Continuous Improvement**

### 2.3 Phase 6 – Global Design Harmonization and Blueprinting

2.3.1 The Service Provider shall review and extend the “Future State Architecture Overview” and “Detailed Solution Design Document” produced under Phases 1 and 2 of the Parent SOW (**Sections 2.2.1 and 2.2.2**) to accommodate global operations, multiple legal entities, and region-specific regulatory requirements.

2.3.2 Deliverables for Phase 6 shall include:

(a) **Global Process Harmonization Document** detailing standardized CRM, resource planning, and analytics processes across all regions, with variance catalogues for country-specific requirements;

(b) **Multi-Region Architecture Blueprint**, including network topology, data residency considerations, and High Availability Regions;

(c) **Global Data Governance and Security Model**, including revised role-based access, cross-border data transfer mechanisms, and updated audit controls consistent with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent SOW and Article XI of this Addendum;

(d) **Global Implementation Roadmap and Milestone Plan**, aligned with the Milestone concept defined in **Section 1.14** of the Parent SOW, including dependencies, cutover strategies, and regional sequencing.

2.3.3 The Company shall have fifteen (15) Business Days (superseding the ten (10) Business Days in **Section 2.2.1(c)** of the Parent SOW with respect to these specific Deliverables) from delivery of Phase 6 Deliverables to provide written acceptance or a written list of material non-conformities. Acceptance procedures shall follow **Section 2.5** of the Parent SOW.

### 2.4 Phase 7 – Multi-Region Rollout Implementation

2.4.1 The Service Provider shall configure and deploy the Platform across the agreed High Availability Regions and business units, building on **Phase 3 (Configuration, Integration, and Development)** and **Phase 4 (Testing, User Acceptance, and Go-Live)** in the Parent SOW (**Sections 2.2.3 and 2.2.4**).

2.4.2 Extended Services during Phase 7 shall include:

(a) Localization of configurations, including currencies, languages, tax logic, and regulatory fields;

(b) Implementation of additional integration interfaces with region-specific systems, following the integration principles set forth in **Section 2.3.2(e)** of the Parent SOW;

(c) Orchestration of regional data migration activities beyond the single-cutover model described in **Section 2.3.2(e)**, including staged migrations, reconciliation, and rollback protocols;

(d) Execution of region-specific UAT cycles with the Company’s stakeholders and remediation of defects according to Enhanced Service Levels.

2.4.3 Deliverables for Phase 7 shall include:

(a) **Region-Specific Configuration Runbooks**;  
(b) **Regional Data Migration Plans and Reconciliation Reports**;  
(c) **Localized Training Materials** and training sessions for up to [Y] regional cohorts (in addition to the [X] training sessions described in **Section 2.3.2(g)** of the Parent SOW);  
(d) **Regional Go-Live Completion Reports**.

### 2.5 Phase 8 – Advanced Analytics and Optimization

2.5.1 The Service Provider shall design and implement an advanced analytics layer on top of the Platform, including predictive analytics, trend forecasting, and executive dashboards.

2.5.2 Services shall include:

(a) Definition of analytics use cases in conjunction with Contoso’s business and finance stakeholders, building on the reporting capabilities referenced in **Section 2.1.2** of the Parent SOW;

(b) Implementation of data models, ETL jobs, and data mart structures necessary to support advanced analytics, consistent with the “Data Model and Integration Design” in **Section 2.2.2(b)** of the Parent SOW;

(c) Delivery of **Executive Analytics Dashboards** and self-service analytics training for designated power users;

(d) Tuning and optimization of Platform performance to meet or exceed the Enhanced Service Levels for analytics workloads.

2.5.3 Deliverables for Phase 8 shall include:

- **Analytics Solution Design and Data Model Documentation**;  
- **Catalog of Standard KPI and Metrics Definitions**;  
- **Analytics Runbook & Administration Guide**;  
- **Optimization Reports** summarizing performance enhancements and recommended continuous improvements.

### 2.6 Phase 9 – Extended Managed Services and Continuous Improvement

2.6.1 From the completion of Phase 7 regional go-lives through the Addendum Expiration Date, the Service Provider shall provide extended managed services, which shall broaden and supersede, to the extent expressly stated, the “Post-Go-Live Support and Optimization” described in **Section 2.2.5** and **Article XII** of the Parent SOW.

2.6.2 Extended managed services shall include, without limitation:

(a) 24x7 incident management for Severity 1 incidents and expanded coverage windows as set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this Addendum;

(b) Execution of Minor Enhancements as defined in **Section 1.12** above, with quarterly capacity allocations exceeding the [X] hours referenced in **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent SOW, as specified in **Section 3.3** of this Addendum;

(c) Participation in the Program Governance Committee, including quarterly strategic reviews, roadmap alignment, and reprioritization of backlogs;

(d) Periodic health checks, capacity planning assessments, and security and compliance reviews.

2.6.3 Deliverables for Phase 9 shall include:

- **Monthly Performance and Incident Reports** (expanding on those in **Section 2.2.5(b)** of the Parent SOW);  
- **Quarterly Strategic Roadmap and Optimization Reports**;  
- **Annual Architecture and Security Review Reports**;  
- **Change and Release Management Documentation**.

### 2.7 Performance Standards and Dependencies

2.7.1 The Service Provider shall meet or exceed the Enhanced Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum. For the Extended Services, to the extent of any conflict between the SLA terms in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW and **Article XII** of this Addendum, the latter shall prevail.

2.7.2 The Company acknowledges that the Service Provider’s performance is dependent upon timely access to systems, personnel, and Client Materials, as described in **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent SOW. Any delays shall be handled through the Change Management procedures in **Article XIII** of both the Parent SOW and this Addendum.

2.7.3 The Parties shall jointly establish the Program Governance Committee within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date. The PGC shall serve as the primary forum for escalations, prioritization of Extended Deliverables, and oversight of Milestones under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Addendum Contract Value and Structure

3.1.1 The total **Addendum Contract Value** for all Extended Services and Extended Deliverables under this Addendum is **US $3,287,626**, exclusive of applicable taxes.

3.1.2 This Addendum Contract Value is separate from and in addition to the **Contract Value** of **US $390,646** set forth in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent SOW. For clarity, the aggregate potential financial exposure of the Company under the Parent SOW and this Addendum is **US $3,678,272**, plus applicable taxes and reimbursable expenses.

3.1.3 The fee structure for this Addendum shall consist of:

(a) Fixed fees for Phases 6, 7, and 8 (design and implementation); and  
(b) Recurring fees and time-and-materials (“T&M”) components for Phase 9 (extended managed services and continuous improvement).

### 3.2 Fixed Fees (Phases 6–8)

3.2.1 The following fixed fees shall apply:

- **Phase 6 – Global Design Harmonization and Blueprinting**: US $650,000  
- **Phase 7 – Multi-Region Rollout Implementation**: US $1,450,000  
- **Phase 8 – Advanced Analytics and Optimization**: US $750,000  

Total Fixed Implementation Fees under this Addendum: **US $2,850,000**

3.2.2 These fixed fees are inclusive of standard project management, documentation, and remote collaboration tools, consistent with **Section 3.2.2** of the Parent SOW, but do not include travel or other reimbursable expenses as described in **Section 3.6** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.6** of this Addendum.

### 3.3 Extended Managed Services Fees (Phase 9)

3.3.1 For Phase 9, the Company shall pay a recurring managed services fee of **US $75,000 per calendar quarter**, commencing on the first full calendar quarter following completion of the final regional go-live under Phase 7 and continuing through the Addendum Expiration Date, unless earlier terminated in accordance with **Article IV**.

3.3.2 The quarterly managed services fee includes:

(a) Up to **320 hours per quarter** of Minor Enhancements and configuration changes (superseding the “[X] hours per quarter” reference in **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent SOW with respect to the Extended Services);  
(b) 24x7 coverage for Severity 1 incidents and enhanced response times as set forth in **Article XII**;  
(c) Participation in the Program Governance Committee and associated reporting.

3.3.3 Any Minor Enhancements or other work exceeding the quarterly allocation in **Section 3.3.2(a)** shall be billed on a T&M basis in accordance with a rate card to be attached as **Exhibit A** to this Addendum, or agreed under a Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII**.

3.3.4 The estimated total value of Phase 9 managed services for the Addendum Term is **US $437,626**, which, together with the fixed fees in **Section 3.2**, constitutes the Addendum Contract Value of US $3,287,626.

### 3.4 Payment Milestones

3.4.1 Fixed Implementation Fees shall be invoiced as follows:

(a) **Phase 6**:  
   - 30% upon Addendum Effective Date;  
   - 40% upon delivery of the Global Process Harmonization Document and Multi-Region Architecture Blueprint;  
   - 30% upon acceptance of all Phase 6 Deliverables.

(b) **Phase 7**:  
   - 20% upon commencement of Phase 7;  
   - 50% prorated upon completion of each regional go-live (as specified in the Global Implementation Roadmap);  
   - 30% upon completion of the final regional go-live and delivery of all Phase 7 Deliverables.

(c) **Phase 8**:  
   - 25% upon commencement of Phase 8;  
   - 50% upon delivery of Analytics Solution Design and initial dashboards;  
   - 25% upon acceptance of all Phase 8 Deliverables.

3.4.2 Managed services fees for Phase 9 shall be invoiced **quarterly in advance**, consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent SOW, but subject to the updated Net 30 terms in **Section 3.5.2** below.

### 3.5 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.5.1 The Service Provider shall issue invoices referencing both the Parent SOW Contract Reference Number (**SOW-CAS-202409-423**) and this Addendum Contract Reference Number (**ADD-CAS-202409-464**).

3.5.2 The Parties agree that, **for all invoices issued under this Addendum**, the payment terms shall be **Net 30**, and **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent SOW (Net 45) shall be deemed modified solely with respect to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables. The Company shall pay all undisputed amounts within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

3.5.3 In the event of a dispute regarding an invoice, the procedures in **Section 3.5.3** of the Parent SOW shall apply (fifteen (15) Business Days to provide notice of dispute, obligation to pay undisputed amounts, and good-faith resolution).

### 3.6 Late Payment

3.6.1 Late payment interest shall accrue as provided in **Section 3.6.1** of the Parent SOW (1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law), calculated from the due date under the Net 30 terms set forth herein.

3.6.2 The Service Provider’s right to suspend performance under **Section 3.6.2** of the Parent SOW for non-payment of undisputed amounts is expressly reaffirmed and shall apply to Extended Services under this Addendum.

### 3.7 Taxes

3.7.1 Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7** of the Parent SOW. All fees under this Addendum are exclusive of applicable taxes.

### 3.8 Expenses

3.8.1 Reimbursable expenses related to travel, lodging, per diem, and other out-of-pocket costs incurred in connection with the Extended Services shall be governed by **Section 3.8** of the Parent SOW, provided that any global travel or extended stays required for multi-region deployment under this Addendum must be pre-approved in writing by the Company.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Addendum Term

4.1.1 The Addendum Term shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until the Addendum Expiration Date, **July 31, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.1.2 The Parties may mutually agree in writing to extend the Addendum Term or enter into further addenda or successor SOWs, in accordance with **Section 4.1.2** and **Section 16.3** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.2 Relationship to Parent SOW Term

4.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent SOW has an Expiration Date of **May 29, 2027** as set forth in the introductory paragraph of the Parent SOW and **Section 1.20**. Notwithstanding such expiration, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to the Extended Services and Extended Deliverables, the Parent SOW shall be deemed **automatically extended** through the Addendum Expiration Date, subject to all other terms and termination rights therein as modified by this Addendum.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party. This modifies the sixty (60) days’ notice period set forth in **Section 4.2.1** of the Parent SOW for purposes of this Addendum.

4.3.2 Upon termination for convenience by the Company, the Company shall pay the Service Provider for:

(a) All Extended Services performed and Extended Deliverables accepted through the effective date of termination;  
(b) All non-cancellable commitments specifically incurred for the Extended Services, provided such commitments were approved in writing by the Company; and  
(c) An early termination fee equal to **fifty percent (50%)** of the remaining fixed fees that would have become due for Phases 6–8 had the Addendum not been terminated, acknowledging the high-risk, vendor-favorable nature of this Addendum.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach, consistent with **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent SOW.

4.4.2 Insolvency-related termination rights shall be as set forth in **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent SOW, and such rights apply equally to this Addendum.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent SOW

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) The Service Provider shall cease performance of Extended Services, except as necessary to perform Transition Assistance;  
(b) The Company shall pay the Service Provider all undisputed amounts due for Extended Services rendered and reimbursable expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, and any applicable early termination fee;  
(c) Rights and obligations regarding Confidential Information, Intellectual Property, Data Protection, and Indemnification shall survive as specified in this Addendum and the Parent SOW.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of the Parent SOW shall **not automatically terminate this Addendum**. If the Parent SOW is terminated for cause by the Company pursuant to **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent SOW, the Company may, at its option, concurrently terminate this Addendum upon written notice. If the Parent SOW is terminated for convenience, this Addendum shall continue in full force and effect unless expressly terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.5.3 Transition Assistance for the Extended Services shall be provided for a period of up to one hundred eighty (180) days after the effective date of termination, at the Company’s request and at the Service Provider’s then-current T&M rates, in addition to the ninety (90) days contemplated by **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.6 Survival

4.6.1 The following provisions of this Addendum shall survive expiration or termination: **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XII (for accrued service credits), XIV, XV, XVI**, and any other provisions which by their nature are intended to survive. These are in addition to the surviving provisions identified in **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent SOW.

### 12.2 Uptime Commitment

12.2.1 For the multi-region production environments governed by this Addendum, the Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the Platform is available at least **99.95%** of the time, measured on a monthly basis per High Availability Region, excluding scheduled maintenance and outages caused by the Company, third-party providers, or Force Majeure Events.

### 12.3 Incident Classification and Response Times

12.3.1 For Extended Services, incident response times shall be as follows:

- **Severity 1 (Critical, Global Impact)**: Complete outage or loss of a critical function affecting a High Availability Region or multiple regions.  
  - Response Time: within **30 minutes**, 24x7.  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within **4 hours**.

- **Severity 2 (High, Regional Impact)**: Major functionality impaired with significant business impact in at least one region.  
  - Response Time: within **2 hours** during business hours, and within **4 hours** outside business hours.  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within **1 Business Day**.

- **Severity 3 (Medium)**: Non-critical functions impaired, moderate impact.  
  - Response Time: within **1 Business Day**.  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: within **3 Business Days**.

- **Severity 4 (Low)**: Minor issues, cosmetic defects, information requests.  
  - Response Time: within **2 Business Days**.  
  - Target Resolution/Workaround: as mutually agreed.

### 12.4 SLA Credits

12.4.1 If the Service Provider fails to meet the 99.95% uptime commitment in any High Availability Region in a given calendar month, the Company shall be entitled to service credits calculated as a percentage of the applicable monthly portion of the Phase 9 managed services fee for that region:

- Uptime 99.0% – 99.949%: 5% credit  
- Uptime 98.0% – 98.99%: 10% credit  
- Uptime < 98.0%: 20% credit

12.4.2 Service credits shall be applied to the next invoice(s) and shall be the Company’s sole monetary remedy for failure to meet uptime and incident response commitments for the Extended Services, without prejudice to any other rights or remedies it may have in respect of other breaches.
