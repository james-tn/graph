## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is **executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement (Ref: SLA-ACM-202404-607 / contract_607)**, consistent with **Section 16.1 (Entire Agreement)** and **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement.  

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, all terms, conditions, rights, and obligations of the Parties under the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Addendum Services.  

2.1.3 The Addendum Services described herein form part of the **“Services”** (as defined in **Section 1.15** of the Parent Agreement) and are subject to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Services.**  

2.2.1 The Vendor shall provide to the Client the Addendum Services, which primarily consist of:  
a. Provision of licensed access to additional Authorized Users (the **Additional Licensed Users**) of the “Acme Enterprise Suite”;  
b. Provision and enablement of additional functional modules (the **Additional Modules**) of the “Acme Enterprise Suite,” as specified in **Section 2.4**;  
c. Implementation, configuration, and integration services reasonably necessary to enable the Client’s use of such Additional Modules, consistent with **Section 2.3 (Implementation and Configuration)** of the Parent Agreement; and  
d. Ongoing Maintenance and Support Services for the Additional Licensed Users and Additional Modules, consistent with **Section 2.8 (Support and Maintenance Scope)** of the Parent Agreement and the Parent SLA.

2.2.2 The Addendum Services shall be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner in accordance with generally accepted industry standards, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Additional Licensed Users.**  

2.3.1 As of the Addendum Effective Date, the Parties agree to increase the number of Authorized Users of the Software by the quantities specified in **Schedule A (Additional Licensed Users and Modules)**.  

2.3.2 The Additional Licensed Users shall be subject to the same usage rights and restrictions as “Authorized Users” under the Parent Agreement, including **Section 6.2 (License Grant to Client)** and **Section 6.3 (License Restrictions)**.  

2.3.3 Access rights for Additional Licensed Users shall commence on the applicable User Expansion Effective Date and continue for the remainder of the Addendum Term, subject to earlier termination under **Article IV** and the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Additional Modules.**  

2.4.1 The Parties agree that the following new modules of the “Acme Enterprise Suite” (each an “Additional Module”) shall be added to the Client’s licensed scope, subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum and as more particularly set forth in **Schedule A**:  
a. **Advanced Analytics Module** – providing enhanced reporting, dashboarding, and predictive analytics capabilities extending the core “reporting and analytics” functionality described in **Section 2.2(e)** of the Parent Agreement;  
b. **Supplier Portal Module** – an extension of the “inventory and supply chain management” functionality described in **Section 2.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement, enabling external suppliers to securely interact with purchase orders, shipment notifications, and inventory levels;  
c. **Automated Billing Engine Module** – expanding the “billing and invoicing” functionalities described in **Section 2.2(d)** of the Parent Agreement to include automated rating, dunning workflows, and multi-currency invoicing;  
d. **Workflow Orchestration Module** – extending “workflow and role-based access management functionalities” as described in **Section 2.2(f)** of the Parent Agreement to include cross-system workflow orchestration and advanced approvals.

2.4.2 The Vendor shall configure each Additional Module in accordance with the Client’s documented business processes, as reasonably agreed in writing by the Parties and consistent with the configuration obligations set out in **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 Unless otherwise expressly stated in this Addendum, the Additional Modules shall be deployed using the same deployment model (cloud-based multi-tenant hosted solution with secure web access) as described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Implementation and Configuration Services under this Addendum.**  

2.5.1 The Vendor shall perform the following implementation and configuration tasks in connection with the Additional Modules and Additional Licensed Users:  
a. **Requirements & Fit-Gap Analysis:** Analyze the Client’s expanded use cases and process requirements associated with the Additional Modules, including increased transaction volumes and additional user roles;  
b. **Configuration:** Configure module-specific settings, including:  
   i. Advanced Analytics dashboards, KPIs, and data models;  
   ii. Supplier Portal workflows, role-based access permissions (aligned with the role-based access management described in **Section 2.2(f)** of the Parent Agreement), and supplier account structures;  
   iii. Automated Billing Engine rating rules, invoice templates, tax calculation rules (without affecting the tax obligations outlined in **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement), and dunning schedules;  
   iv. Workflow Orchestration rules, approval chains, and system-to-system triggers.  
c. **Integration:** Integrate the Additional Modules with the Client’s existing systems identified under the Parent Agreement (e.g., ERP, HR, financial systems, identity providers), using APIs and middleware as provided in **Section 2.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement;  
d. **Data Migration/Enrichment:** Where required, perform data mapping for any incremental data structures needed by the Additional Modules and assist in test and production data loads, consistent with **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement;  
e. **User Training:** Provide training sessions (remote or on-site as agreed) to key user groups, including administrators, business owners, and supplier coordinators, consistent with the implementation and training objectives of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.2 The Parties shall cooperate to develop a project plan specifically for the Addendum Services within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date, consistent with **Section 2.4 (Project Plan and Timelines)** of the Parent Agreement. The project plan shall be annexed as **Schedule B (Addendum Project Plan)** once agreed.

2.6 **Performance Standards and Dependencies.**  

2.6.1 The Vendor shall:  
a. Perform the Addendum Services in accordance with the Parent Agreement’s **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** and this Addendum;  
b. Utilize personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and qualifications relevant to the Additional Modules and integrations;  
c. Comply with applicable laws and regulations, including Data Protection Laws as set forth in **Article XI** of both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum; and  
d. Provide Maintenance and Support Services for the Additional Modules and Additional Licensed Users under the Parent SLA.

2.6.2 The Client acknowledges that timely completion of implementation and configuration under this Addendum is contingent upon the Client fulfilling the obligations described in **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, including providing timely access to personnel, Client Data, and systems.

2.7 **Deliverables and Acceptance.**  

2.7.1 The Deliverables under this Addendum include, without limitation, configured Additional Modules, updated integration components, updated workflow configurations, data migration scripts, and training materials, all as specified in **Schedule B**.

2.7.2 Acceptance of Deliverables shall follow the acceptance procedure specified in **Section 2.7 (Deliverables and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, with the “Acceptance Period” of ten (10) Business Days to be applied to Deliverables under this Addendum unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.7.3 The Parties may define module-specific acceptance criteria in the Addendum Project Plan (Schedule B), provided that such criteria are consistent with the overall acceptance framework of the Parent Agreement.

2.8 **Support and Maintenance for Additional Users/Modules.**  

2.8.1 The Additional Modules and Additional Licensed Users shall be covered by the same Maintenance and Support Services set out in **Section 2.8 (Support and Maintenance Scope)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, including helpdesk access, incident management, and provision of Updates.

2.8.2 Any exclusions set out in **Section 2.9 (Exclusions)** of the Parent Agreement shall also apply to the Additional Modules and Additional Licensed Users unless explicitly overridden in this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (July 22, 2024)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **September 06, 2026** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement or this Addendum (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.1.2 For clarity, the Addendum Term is partially co-extensive with, but does not alter, the **Term** of the Parent Agreement as specified in **Section 4.1** thereof (April 04, 2024 to July 31, 2027). In the event of any inconsistency, **Section 4.5** of this Addendum shall prevail regarding the effect of termination of the Parent Agreement on this Addendum.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

4.2.1 The Parties may, at least **ninety (90) days** prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, negotiate in good faith any renewal or extension of this Addendum (including any revised Fees, user counts, and module scope), to be captured in a written amendment or new addendum executed pursuant to **Section 16.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 After the first **twelve (12) months** of the Addendum Term, either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience by providing at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.  

4.3.2 Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in effect unless separately terminated in accordance with **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for cause under the same conditions specified in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice; insolvency events).

4.4.2 In addition, persistent failure by the Vendor to meet service levels applicable to the Additional Modules (as per **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XII** of this Addendum) may give rise to termination rights consistent with **Section 4.5 (Termination for Persistent SLA Failures)** of the Parent Agreement, applied mutatis mutandis to the Additional Modules.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 The Parties acknowledge that this Addendum is integrally related to the Parent Agreement. Accordingly, **termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement** for any reason shall automatically result in the termination of this Addendum, effective as of the effective date of termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement, without the need for additional notice.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration of this Addendum shall not, in and of itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in full force and effect with respect to other licensed Software modules and Services, unless otherwise terminated in accordance with its terms.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  

4.6.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:  
a. All rights and licenses granted under this Addendum solely with respect to the Additional Licensed Users and Additional Modules shall immediately cease, consistent with **Section 4.6.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
b. The Client shall cease using the Additional Modules and deactivate Additional Licensed Users, except as may be necessary during any agreed transition period;  
c. The Vendor shall provide transition assistance with respect to data related to the Additional Modules, consistent with **Section 4.6.2 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to mutually agreed fees; and  
d. All outstanding unpaid Addendum Fees shall become immediately due and payable, subject to any valid disputes raised under **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Survival.**  

4.7.1 Provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XVI, and all payment obligations accrued prior to termination) shall so survive, consistent with **Section 4.7 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement.
