## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Parent SOW.**  
Subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent SOW and this Work Order, the Service Provider shall perform the **Additional Services** to further advance the Program and extend the Platform. The Parties acknowledge that:

- The general obligations and performance standards set forth in **Section 2.3 (Performance Standards)**, **Section 2.4 (Client Responsibilities)**, and **Section 2.5 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent SOW shall apply to all Additional Services and Additional Deliverables under this Work Order, unless expressly modified herein.  
- The exclusions set forth in **Section 2.6 (Exclusions)** of the Parent SOW shall also apply to this Work Order, subject to any explicit inclusions stated below.  
- The Key Personnel specified in **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW shall continue to have primary responsibility for oversight of the Additional Services, with possible allocation of supplemental resources as described in Section 2.6 below.

2.2 **Phases under this Work Order.**  
The Additional Services under this Work Order shall be organized into the following phases, which are intended to complement the existing Phases 1–6 in **Section 2.2** of the Parent SOW:

2.2.1 **Phase A – Regional Expansion (Estimated: 9 months).**  
(a) **Scope.** Cascade shall extend the existing Platform, as built under Phases 1–5 of the Parent SOW, to cover the Client’s operations in Europe and North America, including localization, data integration, and regulatory alignment.  
(b) **Key Activities.**  
   - Conduct supplemental discovery workshops with regional stakeholders to capture region-specific business requirements, in alignment with the methodologies and artefacts utilized in **Phase 1 – Discovery and Requirements (Section 2.2.1)** of the Parent SOW.  
   - Perform regional system landscape assessments for ERP, CRM, HR, and other systems to be integrated into the Platform, extending the current-state and target-state architecture defined under **Phase 2 – Design and Architecture (Section 2.2.2)** of the Parent SOW.  
   - Configure **Incremental Environments** for each new region, including separate test and staging instances where appropriate, consistent with the environment strategy implemented under **Phase 3 – Build and Configuration (Section 2.2.3)** of the Parent SOW.  
   - Develop and configure new and/or extended integration interfaces to region-specific source systems, leveraging the integration patterns established under the Parent SOW.  
(c) **Deliverables.**  
   - Regional Requirements Addendum (per region) updating the Requirements Traceability Matrix delivered under the Parent SOW.  
   - Updated Target-State Architecture and Integration Blueprint incorporating European and North American systems.  
   - Configured regional Platform environments (test, staging, production) with initial data loads and validation reports.  
   - Regional Cutover and Rollout Plans for each region.  

2.2.2 **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Enablement (Estimated: 8 months; overlapping with Phase A where feasible).**  
(a) **Scope.** Cascade shall design and implement advanced analytics features on the Platform, including machine learning models, predictive dashboards, and self-service analytics capabilities for business users, building upon the data models and ETL/ELT pipelines defined under **Phase 2 and Phase 3** of the Parent SOW.  
(b) **Key Activities.**  
   - Refine and extend the **Data Model and Dictionary** created under **Section 2.2.2(c)** of the Parent SOW to accommodate advanced analytic use cases (churn prediction, demand forecasting, anomaly detection, etc.).  
   - Implement ML pipelines in the Platform environment utilizing the ETL/ELT processes developed under **Phase 3** of the Parent SOW, ensuring adherence to the security and privacy design described in **Section 2.2.2(c) – Security and Compliance Design**.  
   - Develop advanced dashboards, predictive reports, and data exploration workspaces integrated into the Client’s existing identity and access management framework, consistent with **role-based access controls** defined in the Parent SOW.  
   - Enable self-service analytics and governed data discovery, including training materials and a governance model for data sets, metrics, and visualizations.  
(c) **Deliverables.**  
   - Advanced Analytics Solution Design Addendum, referencing and updating the Parent SOW’s Detailed Solution Design Document.  
   - ML Model Catalogue and associated documentation (including training data lineage, evaluation metrics, and operationalization procedures).  
   - Advanced Analytics Dashboards and Reports (minimum of 15 new dashboards across 5 business domains).  
   - Self-Service Analytics Governance Guide and User Enablement Materials.  

2.2.3 **Phase C – Extended Managed Services and Optimization (Remainder of WO Term).**  
(a) **Scope.** Cascade shall provide Extended Managed Services for the expanded Platform footprint, supplementing and partially superseding the services described in **Phase 6 – Managed Services and Support (Section 2.2.6)** of the Parent SOW, for the WO Term.  
(b) **Key Activities.**  
   - Provide Level 2 and Level 3 support for all regions and advanced analytics components, in accordance with the **Enhanced Service Levels** described in **Article XII** of this Work Order.  
   - Perform proactive monitoring, performance tuning, and capacity planning for both the original and expanded regions, with quarterly optimization workshops.  
   - Implement minor enhancements and configuration changes requested by the Client, provided such changes fall within the defined minor-enhancement thresholds agreed by the Parties (e.g., under a predefined monthly effort cap).  
   - Produce monthly consolidated **Service Performance Reports** and quarterly **Capacity and Optimization Recommendations**, which shall be deemed Additional Deliverables under this Work Order and integrated with the reporting obligations described in **Section 2.2.6(c)** of the Parent SOW.  

2.3 **Timelines.**  
The Parties anticipate the following high-level timeline, which shall be refined in a joint project plan within thirty (30) days of the Effective Date:

- Phase A – Regional Expansion: Commencing no later than 1 March 2026, estimated completion by 30 November 2026.  
- Phase B – Advanced Analytics: Commencing no later than 1 May 2026, estimated completion by 31 December 2026.  
- Phase C – Extended Managed Services: Commencing upon the earlier of (i) completion of regional go-live for Europe, or (ii) 1 January 2027, and continuing through the Expiration Date (27 September 2027).

Delays attributable to the Client’s failure to perform its responsibilities under **Section 2.4 of the Parent SOW** shall entitle the Service Provider to equitable extensions and potential fee adjustments, consistent with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW.

2.4 **Additional Deliverables and Acceptance.**  
(a) The Additional Deliverables shall be subject to the acceptance process defined in **Section 2.5 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent SOW. The review period shall be ten (10) Business Days unless otherwise agreed in writing.  
(b) For avoidance of doubt, the following are Additional Deliverables under this Work Order:  
   - Updated Requirements Traceability Matrix for new regions;  
   - Updated Architecture diagrams and Integration Blueprints;  
   - Regional rollout and cutover documentation;  
   - Advanced analytics design documents, ML model catalogue, dashboards, and self-service governance materials;  
   - Enhanced runbooks and configuration guides reflecting regional and advanced analytics components;  
   - Extended Managed Services reporting (monthly and quarterly).  
(c) If the Client identifies material non-conformities, the Service Provider shall remedy them in accordance with **Section 2.5.4** of the Parent SOW and resubmit the Deliverable for acceptance.  

2.5 **Client Responsibilities (Supplement).**  
In addition to the responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.4 of the Parent SOW**, the Client shall:

2.5.1 Appoint regional coordinators in Europe and North America to facilitate requirements gathering, testing, and rollout.  

2.5.2 Ensure that all necessary consents and licenses for integration with regional third‑party systems are obtained, consistent with **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent SOW.  

2.5.3 Provide timely access to sample and production data for ML model training, subject to applicable data protection laws and the data minimization principles referenced in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent SOW and this Work Order.  

2.6 **Key Personnel and Resourcing.**  
The Key Personnel defined in **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW shall continue to oversee the Additional Services. Cascade shall also assign additional region-specific leads and data scientists as required. Any changes to Key Personnel shall be managed in accordance with **Section 2.7** of the Parent SOW.

2.7 **Exclusions under this Work Order.**  
Unless expressly stated herein or agreed in a signed WO Change Order:

2.7.1 The Service Provider is not responsible for end‑user device support, local office network issues, or on-site support outside existing project locations, consistent with **Section 2.6(b)** of the Parent SOW.  

2.7.2 Major functional enhancements that constitute new business capabilities not directly related to the Platform’s analytics and workflow scope shall be out of scope, consistent with **Section 2.6(d)** of the Parent SOW.  

2.7.3 Any third‑party software license or infrastructure cost for additional analytics or ML tools shall be borne by the Client, as further reflected in **Section 3.5 (Expenses and Third-Party Costs)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 3.5** of this Work Order.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **WO Contract Value.**  
The total maximum contract value for this Work Order shall be **US$1,469,701** (one million four hundred sixty-nine thousand seven hundred one United States dollars), exclusive of Taxes. This WO Contract Value is in addition to, and does not amend, the **Contract Value of US$1,017,121** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent SOW, except as the Parties may otherwise agree in a duly executed Change Order.

3.2 **Fee Structure and WO Milestones.**  
3.2.1 **Fixed Fees – Phase A (Regional Expansion).**  
- Total Fixed Fee for Phase A: **US$620,000**.  
- Milestones and payment triggers (aligned with the Parent SOW’s **Section 3.2.1** structure):  
  - **Milestone A1 – Regional Requirements & Architecture Finalization:** 20% of Phase A Fee (US$124,000) upon Client’s acceptance of the regional requirements addenda and updated architecture/integration blueprint.  
  - **Milestone A2 – Environment Configuration Completion:** 40% of Phase A Fee (US$248,000) upon acceptance of configured regional test and staging environments and completion of initial data loads and validation reports.  
  - **Milestone A3 – Production Go-Live per Region:** 40% of Phase A Fee (US$248,000) upon completion and acceptance of production go-live and cutover for both Europe and North America (acceptance may be split proportionately per region as agreed in writing).

3.2.2 **Fixed Fees – Phase B (Advanced Analytics Enablement).**  
- Total Fixed Fee for Phase B: **US$529,701**.  
- Milestones and payment triggers:  
  - **Milestone B1 – Advanced Analytics Design:** 25% of Phase B Fee (US$132,425.25) upon acceptance of the Advanced Analytics Solution Design Addendum and updated Data Model and Dictionary.  
  - **Milestone B2 – ML Model Implementation & Validation:** 35% of Phase B Fee (US$185,395.35) upon acceptance of core ML models (as defined in the Design Addendum) and model validation reports.  
  - **Milestone B3 – Dashboards & Self-Service Enablement:** 40% of Phase B Fee (US$211,880.40) upon acceptance of the advanced dashboards, self-service governance framework, and user enablement materials.

3.2.3 **Extended Managed Services – Phase C.**  
(a) **Monthly Extended Managed Services Fee.**  
- The Extended Managed Services fee for Phase C shall be **US$27,000 per month** from the commencement of Extended Managed Services (as defined in Section 2.2.3) through the Expiration Date, subject to **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of this Work Order.  

(b) **Estimated Total for Phase C.**  
- Based on an estimated service period of twenty (20) months, the aggregate Extended Managed Services fees are estimated at **US$540,000**, such that the sum of Phases A, B, and C does not exceed the WO Contract Value of US$1,469,701 without a signed WO Change Order.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  
3.3.1 **Invoicing for Fixed Fees.**  
In deviation from the 30%/70% split in **Section 3.2.1** of the Parent SOW, the Parties agree that the Fixed Fees under this Work Order shall be invoiced upon completion and Client acceptance of each WO Milestone as described in **Sections 3.2.1 and 3.2.2**.

3.3.2 **Invoicing for Extended Managed Services.**  
Extended Managed Services Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, consistent with **Section 3.2.2(b)** of the Parent SOW.

3.3.3 **Payment Terms (Net 30).**  
All invoices issued under this Work Order shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 30**”), without set-off or deduction except as expressly permitted under the Parent SOW and this Work Order, consistent with **Section 3.3.3** of the Parent SOW.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  
The late payment provisions set forth in **Section 3.4 of the Parent SOW** (including interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, and rights of suspension of Services) shall apply to undisputed amounts under this Work Order.

3.5 **Expenses and Third-Party Costs.**  
The provisions of **Section 3.5 of the Parent SOW** shall apply to this Work Order. In particular:

3.5.1 The Client shall reimburse the Service Provider for reasonable, pre-approved travel and lodging expenses associated with on-site activities under Phases A and B (e.g., regional workshops and go-live support).  

3.5.2 At the Client’s request, the Service Provider may procure third‑party analytics tools or cloud services; such costs shall be invoiced at cost plus a five percent (5%) administrative fee unless otherwise agreed.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent SOW and apply equally to this Work Order.

3.7 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Any disputed invoice amounts shall be addressed under **Section 3.7 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent SOW, which is incorporated herein by reference and shall apply mutatis mutandis to all invoices under this Work Order.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **WO Term.**  
The Term of this Work Order shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until **27 September 2027** (the “**WO Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or as a result of termination or expiry of the Parent SOW pursuant to **Article IV of the Parent SOW**.

4.2 **No Automatic Renewal.**  
This Work Order shall not automatically renew. Any extension or renewal shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties, including applicable renewal fees and service levels, and shall be documented either in an amendment to this Work Order or a new work order executed pursuant to the Parent SOW.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly).**  
4.3.1 The Client may terminate this Work Order for convenience, in whole or in part, upon **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to the Service Provider.  

4.3.2 The Service Provider may terminate this Work Order for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to the Client, provided that such termination shall not relieve the Service Provider of its obligations regarding Transition Activities and cooperation obligations set forth in Section 4.5.

For clarity, these convenience termination rights apply solely to this Work Order and do not amend **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent SOW, which shall continue to govern the Parent SOW.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Work Order upon written notice if the other Party:

4.4.1 Materially breaches this Work Order (or the Parent SOW as it relates to the Additional Services) and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof, consistent with **Section 4.4.1** of the Parent SOW; or  

4.4.2 Becomes insolvent or is subject to insolvency events as described in **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent SOW.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of this Work Order.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Work Order for any reason:

4.5.1 The Client shall pay the Service Provider all accrued and unpaid Fees for Additional Services performed and Additional Deliverables delivered up to the effective date of termination, plus all approved expenses, and, in the case of termination for convenience by the Client, any reasonable and demonstrable wind-down costs incurred by the Service Provider in respect of this Work Order only.  

4.5.2 The Service Provider shall cooperate with the Client in good faith to transition the Additional Services to the Client or a third party designated by the Client, in accordance with **Section 4.5.3 of the Parent SOW**, at rates to be mutually agreed but not exceeding the then-current time and materials rates applicable under this Work Order.  

4.5.3 Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information as required under **Section 5.4** of the Parent SOW, as supplemented by **Article V** of this Work Order.  

4.5.4 Any licenses granted under **Article VI** of the Parent SOW, as extended or clarified by **Article VI** of this Work Order, shall continue in force in accordance with their terms, subject to Sections 6.2 and 6.3 of the Parent SOW.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Parent SOW.**  
4.6.1 This Work Order is subordinate to the Parent SOW. If the Parent SOW is terminated for any reason, this Work Order shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain support or transition services beyond that date under separate terms.  

4.6.2 The termination or expiration of this Work Order shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent SOW, which shall remain in effect in accordance with its terms unless separately terminated.

4.7 **Survival.**  
Those provisions of this Work Order that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XVI, and this Section 4.7) shall so survive, in addition to the surviving provisions set forth in **Section 4.6 (Survival)** of the Parent SOW.
