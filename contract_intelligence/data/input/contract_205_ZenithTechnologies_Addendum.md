## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Original SOW and Master Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Addendum is **entered into pursuant to**, and shall be read and construed together with, the Original SOW (Ref: **SOW-ZEN-202403-200**, **contract_200**) and the Master Agreement (Ref: **MSA-ZEN-202403-197**, **contract_197**). Except as expressly modified or supplemented by this Addendum, all terms and conditions of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement remain in full force and effect and apply to the Addendum Services.

2.1.2 Notwithstanding Section 2.1.3 of the Original SOW, in the event of a conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Addendum and the terms of the Original SOW or the Master Agreement, the following order of precedence shall apply solely with respect to the subject matter of this Addendum: (a) this Addendum (ADD-ZEN-202403-205); (b) the Original SOW (SOW-ZEN-202403-200, contract_200); and (c) the Master Agreement (MSA-ZEN-202403-197, contract_197).

2.1.3 For clarity, all references to “SOW,” “this SOW,” “Project,” “Services,” and “Deliverables” in the Original SOW shall, as of the Addendum Effective Date, be deemed to include and encompass the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables, unless this Addendum expressly provides otherwise.

2.2 **Supplemental Project Plan and Phasing.**

2.2.1 Within twenty (20) Business Days following the Addendum Effective Date, Vendor shall prepare and submit to Client for review and approval a **Supplemental Project Plan** that:

  a. Extends the existing Project Plan referenced in Sections 1.7 and 2.3 of the Original SOW;  
  b. Identifies additional phases, Milestones, and tasks necessary to implement the Addendum Services;  
  c. Defines updated dependencies between the original Phases 1–5 and the new phases; and  
  d. Aligns with the extended term contemplated in Section 4.1 of this Addendum and the enhanced Service Levels in Article XII.

2.2.2 The Supplemental Project Plan shall, at a minimum, include:

  - Additional Milestones AD-M1 through AD-M8 tied to the Addendum Deliverables described in Section 2.4;  
  - Specific timelines and go-live dates for each Expansion Region;  
  - Resource allocation for Enhanced Managed Services; and  
  - Dependencies relating to Client Responsibilities (as described in Section 2.6 of the Original SOW and supplemented in Section 2.5 of this Addendum).

2.2.3 Approval of the Supplemental Project Plan shall be subject to the acceptance procedures described in Section 2.6(e) of the Original SOW and Section 2.5.2 of the Master Agreement.

2.3 **Expanded Scope: Additional Regions and Functions.**

2.3.1 **Expansion Regions Rollout.** The Parties agree that the Project, as defined in Section 1.3 of the Original SOW, shall be expanded to include implementation and support for at least **four (4) additional business regions** beyond the “two (2) core business regions” referenced in Section 2.3.3 of the Original SOW. For each Expansion Region, Vendor shall:

  a. Perform localized requirements gathering, gap analysis, and design adaptations consistent with **Phase 1 – Initiation and Discovery** set forth in Section 2.3.1 of the Original SOW;  
  b. Configure and/or adapt the ERP and CRM modules in accordance with local regulatory, tax, and operational requirements;  
  c. Implement integrations with region-specific systems (e.g., local payroll, tax reporting, logistics providers), applying the framework of **Phase 2 – Design and Build** in Section 2.3.2 of the Original SOW;  
  d. Conduct testing, UAT support, and Go-Live activities for each Expansion Region in line with Section 2.3.3 of the Original SOW; and  
  e. Provide hypercare, stabilization, and transition to Managed Services consistent with Section 2.3.4 of the Original SOW.

2.3.2 **Advanced Analytics and Reporting.** Vendor shall design and deliver:

  a. A centralized analytics layer integrating transaction and customer data across all regions;  
  b. A suite of standardized executive dashboards and KPIs for Finance, Sales, and Service, building upon Deliverables D11 and D12 (monthly Service Performance Reports and semi-annual Optimization & Roadmap Reports) in Section 2.4 of the Original SOW; and  
  c. At least ten (10) custom analytics reports per Expansion Region, configured within the ERP/CRM environment or Client’s data warehouse.

2.3.3 **Automation and Optimization Services.** Vendor shall perform periodic optimization services, including:

  a. Implementation of workflow automation for at least five (5) cross-functional business processes (e.g., quote-to-cash, procure-to-pay, case-to-resolution);  
  b. Performance tuning and capacity planning services, including quarterly health checks and remediation plans; and  
  c. Root-cause analysis for recurring high-severity Incidents, with concrete remediation actions.

2.3.4 **Security and Compliance Enhancements.** In alignment with **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement, Vendor shall:

  a. Implement role-based access controls and segregation-of-duties rules tailored to each Expansion Region;  
  b. Integrate audit logging and monitoring for privileged access; and  
  c. Provide annual security and compliance posture reports as part of the Deliverables.

2.3.5 **Enhanced Managed Services.** Building on **Phase 5 – Managed Services** in Section 2.3.5 of the Original SOW and the Service Levels in **Article XII** of both the Master Agreement and the Original SOW, Vendor shall:

  a. Extend Managed Services to cover all Expansion Regions and newly implemented modules;  
  b. Provide 24x7 proactive monitoring of critical system components;  
  c. Implement automated incident detection, alerting, and self-healing scripts where feasible; and  
  d. Provide quarterly trend analyses and recommendations for continuous improvement.

2.4 **Addendum Deliverables.**

Without limiting the Deliverables D1–D12 specified in Section 2.4 of the Original SOW, Vendor shall produce the following **Addendum Deliverables** under this Addendum:

- **AD-D1 – Supplemental Project Plan and Regional Rollout Roadmap** (updating D2 from the Original SOW);  
- **AD-D2 – Regional Localization Design Packs** for each Expansion Region (including regulatory, tax, and language adaptations);  
- **AD-D3 – Extended Integration Specifications** for region-specific systems and additional interfaces (supplementing D4);  
- **AD-D4 – Advanced Analytics and Dashboard Catalog**, including data models, KPIs, and report definitions;  
- **AD-D5 – Automation Design and Implementation Guide**, detailing automated workflows and scripts;  
- **AD-D6 – Security and Compliance Enhancement Report**, including access control matrices and audit configurations;  
- **AD-D7 – Enhanced Managed Services Operating Manual**, detailing expanded Service Window coverage, escalation paths, and automation tools;  
- **AD-D8 – Quarterly Optimization & Performance Review Reports**, supplementing Deliverable D12.

Unless expressly stated otherwise, the Acceptance Criteria and acceptance procedures for Addendum Deliverables shall follow those established for the relevant analogous Deliverables in the Original SOW, including **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** and any Acceptance Criteria defined in the Project Plan and referenced in Section 1.9 of the Original SOW.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities – Supplement.**

In addition to Client’s responsibilities under Section 2.6 of the Original SOW, Client shall:

  a. Provide regional subject matter experts for each Expansion Region for discovery, design validation, and UAT;  
  b. Obtain and maintain any additional third-party licenses required for regional integrations not procured by Vendor;  
  c. Ensure that global and regional data privacy, tax, and regulatory requirements are communicated in writing to Vendor in a timely manner; and  
  d. Provide timely approval or rejection (with reasons) of Addendum Deliverables in accordance with the acceptance procedures described in Section 2.6(e) of the Original SOW.

Vendor’s obligations with respect to the Addendum Services are contingent upon Client’s timely fulfillment of these responsibilities, and any delays may be handled via the Change Order process described in **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement.

2.6 **Performance Standards – Supplement.**

Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with the performance standards described in **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Original SOW and **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Agreement, and shall meet or exceed the enhanced Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term; Expiration.**

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (13 October 2024) and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **4 December 2026** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

4.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that the term of the Original SOW, as set forth in Section 4.1 thereof, currently runs from **16 June 2024** through **15 June 2026** (the “SOW Expiration Date”). The Parties further agree that the Original SOW is hereby **extended** solely in respect of the Addendum Services and Enhanced Managed Services described herein so that such Services may continue through the Addendum Expiration Date, subject to the provisions of this Article IV.

4.2 **Renewal for Enhanced Managed Services.**

The Parties may, by mutual written agreement executed no later than sixty (60) days prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, extend the Enhanced Managed Services portion of this Addendum on terms and conditions to be agreed, consistent with Section 4.2 of the Original SOW.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, **for convenience** by providing at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party, consistent with Section 4.3 of the Original SOW and **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Master Agreement. Client acknowledges that early termination for convenience may result in additional wind-down, transition, and demobilization Fees, particularly with respect to Enhanced Managed Services, to be agreed in good faith and documented in a Change Order or termination addendum.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with the procedures and grounds stated in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement, including without limitation material breach of this Addendum (which shall be deemed a material breach of the Original SOW), persistent Service Level failures, or non-payment of undisputed Fees.

4.4.2 Any termination notice under this Section 4.4 may specify that such termination applies to (i) this Addendum alone, (ii) the Original SOW (including this Addendum), and/or (iii) the Master Agreement and all SOWs thereunder, subject to the rights and limitations set forth in the Master Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination or Expiration.**

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason, Client shall pay Vendor all Fees and reimbursable expenses due and payable for Addendum Services performed and Addendum Deliverables delivered (or in progress) up to the effective date of termination, consistent with Section 4.5.1 of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement.

4.5.2 At Client’s written request, Vendor shall provide **Transition Assistance Services** in accordance with **Section 4.5.2 (Transition Assistance)** of the Original SOW and the Master Agreement, at Vendor’s then-current rates, under a mutually agreed Change Order or separate SOW.

4.5.3 Sections that, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration of this Addendum (including but not limited to payment obligations, confidentiality, IP rights, limitations of liability, data protection obligations, and dispute resolution) shall survive in accordance with Section 4.6 of the Original SOW and Section 16.11 thereof.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Original SOW or Master Agreement.**

4.6.1 If the Master Agreement is terminated or expires, this Addendum and the Original SOW shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue some or all Services under substantially the same terms as the Master Agreement.

4.6.2 If the Original SOW is terminated or expires (other than by expiration of the Addendum Term), this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, except to the extent that the Parties expressly agree otherwise in writing.
