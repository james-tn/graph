2.2 **Description of Additional Modules and Users.**  

(a) Subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Vendor shall provide Client with Additional Licenses to the following **Additional Modules**, which form part of the “Software” under **Section 1.17** of the Parent Agreement:

   1. **Advanced Analytics Suite** – an enhanced analytics and reporting module expanding upon any analytics referenced in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement, providing multi-dimensional dashboards, predictive analytics, and scheduled reporting.  
   2. **Workflow Automation Plus** – extended workflow capabilities, including advanced rule sets, conditional routing, and cross-system automation.  
   3. **Integration Connectors – Premium Pack** – additional pre-built connectors to third-party CRM, ERP, and HRIS systems, supplementing the integration connectors described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Vendor further grants Client Additional Licenses for the following **Additional Users**, which increase Client’s license metrics under **Section 3.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement:

   1. **Two hundred (200) additional named users** for the Core Software platform.  
   2. **Fifty (50) additional concurrent users** for high-activity operational teams.  

(c) The detailed listing of Additional Modules, applicable license model (named vs. concurrent), quantities, and per-unit pricing is set forth in **Schedule 1 (Additional License Metrics and Fees)**, which is incorporated into and made part of this Addendum.

2.3 **Nature of Services.**  

(a) Consistent with **Section 2.1 (Overview of Services)** and **Section 2.3 (Implementation Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall perform the following Services in connection with the Additional Licenses:

   1. **Configuration and Enablement.** Configuration of the Additional Modules in Client’s existing production and staging environments, including role-based access controls and data source mappings.  
   2. **Integration Services.** Implementation of up to three (3) additional integrations with Client’s designated third-party systems using Vendor’s standard APIs and the Integration Connectors – Premium Pack, consistent with **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  
   3. **Data Mapping and Validation Support.** Support in mapping Client’s data to the Additional Modules, with Client maintaining primary responsibility for data cleansing, validation, and backup pursuant to **Section 2.7(d)** of the Parent Agreement.  
   4. **Training.** Delivery of up to four (4) remote training sessions (two hours each) for end-user and administrator enablement relating to the Additional Modules and Additional Users, in line with **Section 2.4 (Training)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Any services beyond those described above, including but not limited to custom development, additional integration points, or extended training, shall be considered **Additional Services** under **Section 3.2(d)** of the Parent Agreement and shall require a separate SOW or Change Order.

2.4 **Project Plan and Timelines.**  

(a) Within fifteen (15) Business Days following the Addendum Effective Date, Vendor shall collaborate with Client (consistent with **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement) to develop a high-level implementation plan for the Additional Modules and Additional Users, including:

   1. A deployment schedule targeting production enablement of all Additional Modules within ninety (90) days of the Addendum Effective Date, subject to Client’s timely cooperation.  
   2. Milestones for completion of integration configuration, user acceptance testing, and training.  

(b) Client acknowledges that timeframes are estimates only unless expressly stated as binding milestones, as reiterated in **Section 2.6(c)** of the Parent Agreement; provided, however, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve the agreed milestones.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

(a) Vendor shall perform all Services under this Addendum in a **professional and workmanlike manner** and in accordance with generally accepted industry standards, consistent with **Section 2.6(b)** and **Section 7.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) The Additional Modules, to the extent provided as a hosted or SaaS solution, shall be subject to the same **Service Levels** and **uptime commitment of 99.9%** as set forth in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, including response times specified in **Section 12.3** and service credit remedies specified in **Section 12.4**.

(a) Client’s obligations in **Section 2.7 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to the Services provided under this Addendum. Without limiting the foregoing, Client shall:  

   1. Provide Vendor with timely access to Client’s systems, environments, and personnel needed for configuration and integration of the Additional Modules.

4.1 **Addendum Term.**

(b) Notwithstanding the Addendum Expiration Date, the Parent Agreement shall remain in effect according to its own terms, and this Addendum shall not be construed to shorten or extend the Parent Agreement’s Term.

(a) Any renewal of the Additional Licenses following the Addendum Expiration Date shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties, which may take the form of a new addendum or renewal Order Form under **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** and **Section 3.8 (Fee Adjustments and Renewals)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Vendor may adjust fees for any renewal term in accordance with **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement, subject to providing the required sixty (60) days’ written notice.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience.**  

(a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, without cause, upon **not less than thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to the other Party.  

(b) Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement or any other Order Form or SOW then in effect, unless expressly stated otherwise in the notice of termination and agreed by the Parties in writing.  

(c) Except as may be provided in a specific pricing schedule, termination for convenience under this Addendum shall not entitle Client to any refund of fees already paid, consistent with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.**  

(a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement with respect to the Additional Licenses and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail, consistent with **Section 4.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party experiences an insolvency event as described in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Addendum.**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) All Additional Licenses granted under this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination or the Addendum Expiration Date (as applicable), and Client shall promptly cease all use of the Additional Modules and any incremental capacity associated with the Additional Users, in accordance with **Section 4.5(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Client shall promptly pay Vendor all outstanding fees and expenses due under this Addendum through the effective date of termination, as required by **Section 4.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Each Party shall, upon written request, return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information specific to the Additional Modules in accordance with **Section 4.5(c)** and **Section 5.4** of the Parent Agreement.  

(d) If termination is due to Vendor’s uncured material breach of this Addendum, Vendor shall refund to Client any prepaid, unused fees under this Addendum covering the period after the effective date of termination on a pro rata basis, consistent with **Section 4.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

In the event the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason:

(a) This Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, without the need for further action by either Party.  

(b) The rights and obligations of the Parties under this Addendum shall be subject to **Section 4.5 (Effect of Termination or Expiration)** and **Section 4.6 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement, including the termination of all Licenses under **Article VI**.  

(c) Any survival provisions in this Addendum shall remain in effect to the extent they are consistent with and not duplicative of the survival provisions in the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Survival.**  

Any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, payment obligations, confidentiality, IP provisions, limitations of liability, and dispute resolution) shall so survive, in addition to the provisions of the Parent Agreement that survive under **Section 4.6** thereof.
