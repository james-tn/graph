### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Agreement, Reference No. **SLA-ZEN-202502-413** (“**contract_413**”), pursuant to which Zenith granted Contoso a license to access and use Zenith’s proprietary enterprise software platform known as **ZenithOps Suite** and to receive associated implementation, hosting, maintenance, and support services, all on the terms and conditions set forth therein;

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND LICENSE EXTENSION

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This **Article II** supplements and, where expressly stated, modifies **Article II (Scope of Services and License)** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to the Additional Authorized Users and Additional Modules described herein.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, all terms of **Article II** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables.

2.1.3 For the avoidance of doubt, the core license grant in **Section 2.2 (Software License Grant)** of the Parent Agreement continues to govern Contoso’s use of the existing Software and the ZenithOps Suite, and this Addendum merely expands the scope to cover additional named Authorized Users and additional modules as set forth below.

### 2.2 Overview of Additional Scope

2.2.1 Under this Addendum, Zenith shall:

  (a) provide Contoso with additional subscription-based licenses to the Software for Additional Authorized Users, increasing the number of named Authorized Users beyond the original 250 users specified in **Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement**;  

  (b) grant Contoso subscription-based access to certain Additional Modules of the ZenithOps Suite, including but not limited to:

  - ZenithOps **Advanced Analytics Pro** Module;  
  - ZenithOps **Workflow Orchestrator** Extended Module;  
  - ZenithOps **Integration Hub Plus**; and  
  - ZenithOps **Security & Compliance Dashboard**;

  (c) perform Addendum Services to configure and integrate the Additional Modules and Additional Authorized Users within Contoso’s existing ZenithOps environment, including role configuration, permissions, data model extensions, dashboard customization, and integration mapping; and  

  (d) provide Maintenance and Support Services (as defined in Section 1.13 of the Parent Agreement) for the Additional Modules and Additional Authorized Users in accordance with **Article XII of the Parent Agreement**, as supplemented in this Addendum.

### 2.3 Expansion of Authorized Users

2.3.1 **Amendment to Section 2.2.2 of Parent Agreement (User Limit).**  
Notwithstanding the limit of **250 named Authorized Users** stated in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that, as of the Addendum Effective Date and for the Addendum Term, the total number of named Authorized Users authorized to access and use the Software shall be expanded as follows:

  (a) Existing Authorized Users under Parent Agreement: 250 users;  
  (b) Additional Authorized Users under this Addendum: 1,250 users;  
  (c) Total Expanded User Entitlement: **1,500 named Authorized Users**.

2.3.2 Each Additional Authorized User shall be subject to the same restrictions and obligations set forth in **Section 2.6 (Restrictions on Use)** of the Parent Agreement, and Contoso shall remain fully responsible for compliance by all Additional Authorized Users.

2.3.3 **User Tiers.**  
The Additional Authorized Users shall be allocated across User Tiers as follows:

  (a) **Standard Users**: up to 900 Additional Authorized Users, with access to core ZenithOps Suite functions and read-only capabilities in the Advanced Analytics Pro Module;  

  (b) **Power Users**: up to 300 Additional Authorized Users, with access to both read and write capabilities in the Advanced Analytics Pro Module and Workflow Orchestrator Extended Module;  

  (c) **Administrators**: up to 50 Additional Authorized Users (inclusive of any pre-existing Administrators), with rights to configure Additional Modules, manage integrations in Integration Hub Plus, and assign roles, all subject to Zenith’s security model.

2.3.4 The allocation of User Tiers may be adjusted by mutual written agreement of the Parties through the change management process set forth in **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and reiterated in **Article XIII of this Addendum**.

### 2.4 Additional Modules and Functional Scope

2.4.1 **Advanced Analytics Pro Module.**  
Zenith shall enable and configure the **Advanced Analytics Pro** module for Contoso’s production and non-production environments. Key capabilities include:

  (a) enhanced data visualization and dashboarding capabilities;  
  (b) support for complex queries, trend analytics, and predictive scoring;  
  (c) role-based access controls aligned with Contoso’s organizational hierarchy; and  
  (d) integration with Contoso’s CRM and ERP systems as described in Section 2.4.4.

2.4.2 **Workflow Orchestrator Extended Module.**  
Zenith shall activate the **Workflow Orchestrator Extended** module, enabling:

  (a) multi-step, cross-departmental workflow automation;  
  (b) conditional routing, parallel tasks, and escalations;  
  (c) SLA tracking at the workflow step level; and  
  (d) integration with email and collaboration tools to support task notifications.

2.4.3 **Integration Hub Plus.**  
Zenith shall provision **Integration Hub Plus** to support high-volume, near real-time integrations between the Software and Contoso’s designated systems, including but not limited to CRM, ERP, identity management, and data warehouse platforms. Addendum Deliverables include:

  (a) configuration of at least four (4) new integration endpoints;  
  (b) implementation of monitoring and alerting for integration failures; and  
  (c) documentation of integration mappings and data transformation rules.

2.4.4 **Security & Compliance Dashboard.**  
Zenith shall deploy a **Security & Compliance Dashboard** to provide Contoso with:

  (a) visibility into user access, role assignments, and authentication events;  
  (b) configurable compliance reports to support audits; and  
  (c) policy-driven alerts based on user activities and data access patterns.

### 2.5 Addendum Services – Implementation and Configuration

2.5.1 Zenith shall perform the Addendum Services using qualified personnel in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.2 The Addendum Services shall be organized into phases, similar to those described in **Section 2.4.1 of the Parent Agreement**, but tailored to this Addendum:

  (a) **Phase A – Detailed Design (estimated 3 weeks):** validation of Additional Module requirements, user segmentation into User Tiers, integration design for Integration Hub Plus, and security requirements for the Security & Compliance Dashboard;  

  (b) **Phase B – Configuration & Build (estimated 7 weeks):** configuration of Additional Modules, creation of custom reports and dashboards, integration development and unit testing, initial role matrix configuration, and preparation of training materials;  

  (c) **Phase C – User Acceptance Testing & Pilot (estimated 4 weeks):** support for Contoso-led testing for a pilot group of up to 200 Additional Authorized Users; remediation of identified Errors (as defined in Section 1.8 of the Parent Agreement); and refinement of security configurations;  

  (d) **Phase D – Full Rollout & Stabilization (estimated 4 weeks):** staged enablement of Additional Modules for all Additional Authorized Users, monitoring of performance and usage, and transition to steady-state support under the SLA Commitments.

2.5.3 The timelines in Section 2.5.2 are estimates only and may be adjusted under the change management procedures in **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII of this Addendum**.

2.5.4 Contoso’s obligations in support of the Addendum Services shall include, without limitation:

  (a) providing timely access to systems, APIs, and relevant technical resources as required for Integration Hub Plus;  

  (b) designating business and technical stakeholders to review and approve Addendum Deliverables during the Acceptance Period described in Section 2.6 below;  

  (c) ensuring that necessary data fields and test data sets are made available for development, testing, and UAT; and  

  (d) managing internal change management communications to end users.

### 2.6 Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables

2.6.1 Each Addendum Deliverable shall be subject to an Acceptance Period identical in length and process to that described in **Section 2.7 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, namely, ten (10) Business Days from delivery, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.6.2 If Contoso fails to provide written notice of material non-conformity within the Acceptance Period, or uses the Addendum Deliverable in a production environment, the Addendum Deliverable shall be deemed accepted under the same standards as **Section 2.7.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.3 Any disputes over acceptance shall be escalated in accordance with **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIV of this Addendum**.
