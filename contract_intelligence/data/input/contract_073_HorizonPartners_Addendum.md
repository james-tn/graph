## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Parent DPA.**  

2.1.1 Pursuant to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** and **Section 2.2 (Data Processing Activities)** of the Parent DPA, Contoso now issues additional Documented Instructions and requests that Horizon perform the Additional Processing Activities described in this Article II. These Extended Services are in addition to, and shall be performed in conjunction with, the Services described in **Section 2.1(a)–(f)** of the Parent DPA, including cloud data hosting and storage, data processing and analytics, application services (SaaS), implementation and integration services, support and maintenance, and professional services and consultancy.

2.1.2 This Addendum is intended to further detail the purposes and nature of Processing under **Section 11.2 (Processing on Documented Instructions)** of the Parent DPA and to document certain specific Processing activities for compliance with Data Protection Laws, including GDPR and CCPA/CPRA as referenced in the **Recitals** and **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent DPA.

2.2 **Description of Additional Processing Activities.**

Without limiting the Services described in the Parent DPA, Horizon shall perform the following Additional Processing Activities:

2.2.1 **Advanced Analytics and Segmentation.**  
Horizon will provide advanced analytics services that extend the activities described in **Section 2.1(b) (Data Processing and Analytics)** of the Parent DPA, including:

  (a) building and managing advanced customer and user segmentation models, including behavioral clustering, propensity scoring, and lifecycle stage modeling based on Transaction-Level Data, usage metrics, and log and device data defined in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent DPA;

  (b) enabling Contoso to run on‑demand and scheduled segmentation queries via the Horizon platform (as referenced in **Section 2.1(c) (Application Services)** of the Parent DPA), with configurable attributes and filters;

  (c) generating Aggregated Output for marketing, product, and support use cases, provided that Horizon shall not use Personal Data for any purpose inconsistent with **Section 2.6 (Restrictions on Use of Personal Data)** of the Parent DPA.

2.2.2 **Model Management Services.**  
Horizon shall design, deploy, and operate Model Management Services, including:

  (a) ingesting Contoso’s historical and real‑time datasets, including user identifiers, transaction details, and usage metrics as contemplated in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent DPA, to train models that predict churn risk, likelihood to convert, upsell propensity, and support ticket deflection;

  (b) implementing model training, validation, and monitoring pipelines in Horizon’s hosted environments described in **Section 2.1(a) (Cloud Data Hosting and Storage)** of the Parent DPA, ensuring appropriate logical segregation and access controls consistent with **Section 11.4 (Security Measures)**;

  (c) exposing model outputs (e.g., scores and probabilities) to Contoso via APIs and dashboards within the Horizon platform, which shall be treated as Deliverables as defined in **Section 1.8 (Deliverables)** of the Parent DPA and subject to the intellectual property and licensing provisions of **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent DPA and **Article VI** of this Addendum;

  (d) implementing Data Minimization Measures so that only the minimum Personal Data necessary for model performance is used, consistent with Data Protection Laws and **Section 2.6** of the Parent DPA.

2.2.3 **HR Analytics Services.**  
Subject to Contoso’s compliance with **Section 2.5 (Contoso Responsibilities)** and **Section 7.3 (Contoso Representations and Warranties)** of the Parent DPA, Horizon shall Process HR‑related Personal Data (already referenced at a high level in **Section 2.2(b) – “HR‑related data (role, department, work contact details)”** of the Parent DPA) for workforce analytics, including:

  (a) analyzing anonymized or pseudonymized workforce data to provide insights into headcount trends, department-level attrition rates, and skills distribution;

  (b) creating dashboards and reports that assist Contoso’s HR and leadership teams in capacity planning, diversity metrics tracking (without Processing “special categories” of data under GDPR unless expressly authorized in writing and subject to additional safeguards), and workforce optimization;

  (c) ensuring that no automated decision‑making producing legal or similarly significant effects on employees is conducted by Horizon without prior written instruction from Contoso and additional safeguards, in line with GDPR requirements and **Section 11.3 (Compliance with Data Protection Laws)** of the Parent DPA.

2.2.4 **Identity Resolution Services.**  
Horizon will perform Identity Resolution Services by:

  (a) receiving multiple identifiers (e.g., email addresses, device IDs, CRM IDs, HR system IDs) as contemplated in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent DPA and building matching rules or probabilistic models to unify profiles across systems;

  (b) maintaining a secure, logically segregated identity graph within Horizon’s hosted environment, ensuring security and access controls under **Section 11.4** of the Parent DPA;

  (c) allowing Contoso to leverage unified profiles for analytics and reporting through the Horizon platform, subject to **Section 2.6 (Restrictions on Use of Personal Data)** of the Parent DPA and the CCPA/CPRA restrictions outlined in **Section 11.11 (CCPA/CPRA Service Provider Status)**.

2.2.5 **Extended Logging and Observability.**  
To support Enhanced SLA Metrics and security monitoring:

  (a) Horizon will collect additional system logs, telemetry, and audit trails relating to access, data flows, and model execution events within the Extended Services;

  (b) such logs may contain Personal Data (e.g., user identifiers and IP addresses) and shall be treated as Personal Data under **Section 1.11 (“Personal Data”)** of the Parent DPA and secured under **Article XI** thereof;

  (c) Contoso may request summarized or Aggregated Output from such logs for security and compliance reporting.

2.3 **Extended Categories of Data Subjects and Personal Data.**

2.3.1 **Data Subjects.** In addition to the Data Subjects identified in **Section 2.2(a)** of the Parent DPA, the Additional Processing Activities may involve:

  (a) more granular Processing of Contoso’s employees and contractors (for HR Analytics Services), limited to professional context data and expressly excluding “special categories” of data as defined under GDPR unless otherwise agreed in a written amendment;

  (b) prospective customers and trial users whose data is collected by Contoso’s marketing systems and provided to Horizon for propensity scoring and segmentation.

2.3.2 **Categories of Personal Data.** In addition to the categories listed in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent DPA, the Extended Personal Data Categories may include, solely as necessary for the Additional Processing Activities:

  (a) extended behavioral event streams (e.g., clickstream data, navigation paths, feature usage);

  (b) hashed identifiers or salted tokens for identity resolution;

  (c) role, tenure, and hierarchy metadata for HR Analytics Services, excluding sensitive HR data unless specifically approved in an SOW and subject to additional safeguards;

  (d) model outputs such as scores, labels, and predictive classifications, which shall be treated as Personal Data to the extent they can be associated with identified or identifiable individuals.

2.4 **Performance Standards and Interplay with Parent DPA.**

2.4.1 Horizon shall perform the Additional Processing Activities:

  (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with **Section 2.3** of the Parent DPA;

  (b) in compliance with **Applicable Law**, including Data Protection Laws, consistent with **Sections 2.3(b)** and **11.3** of the Parent DPA;

  (c) using appropriately trained personnel bound by confidentiality obligations no less protective than those described in **Article V (Confidentiality)** of the Parent DPA;

  (d) in accordance with any applicable SOW executed pursuant to the Master Agreement and referencing this Addendum.

2.4.2 Where this Addendum specifies standards that are more protective of Contoso or Data Subjects than those in the Parent DPA, such more protective standards shall prevail as between the Parties for the scope of the Additional Processing Activities.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.**

2.5.1 The Parties shall document specific implementation phases, milestones, and acceptance criteria for the Additional Processing Activities in one or more SOWs referencing this Addendum and the Parent DPA. By way of guidance:

  (a) initial configuration of Model Management Services and Identity Resolution Services shall, unless otherwise agreed in an SOW, be targeted for completion within one hundred twenty (120) days following Contoso’s provision of required access, data, and resources;

  (b) HR Analytics dashboards and reports shall be delivered in draft form within sixty (60) days following availability of required HR data feeds, with a review and refinement period not to exceed thirty (30) days, consistent with the refinement approach in **Section 2.4(b)** of the Parent DPA;

  (c) delays caused by Contoso’s failure to provide timely inputs or approvals shall extend timelines on a day‑for‑day basis, provided Horizon has notified Contoso in writing as contemplated in **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent DPA.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities – Additional Obligations.**

In addition to the responsibilities in **Section 2.5** of the Parent DPA, Contoso shall:

  (a) ensure that any new categories of Personal Data provided for HR Analytics Services and Identity Resolution Services are collected and disclosed in compliance with Data Protection Laws and with appropriate notices and legal bases, consistent with **Section 7.3** of the Parent DPA;

  (b) clearly identify any data fields that may constitute “special categories” of data under GDPR or “sensitive personal information” under CCPA/CPRA and not transmit such data to Horizon unless explicitly authorized in writing and subject to additional safeguards;

  (c) review and approve in writing any material changes to model use cases that may involve automated decision‑making with legal or similarly significant effects on individuals.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
In consideration for the Additional Processing Activities and Extended Services to be performed by Horizon under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay to Horizon fees in an aggregate amount not to exceed **USD $2,419,932** over the Addendum Term, unless the Parties mutually agree in writing (including via SOW amendment) to additional services and fees. This Addendum Contract Value is in addition to, and does not modify, the contract value of **USD $1,825,695** set forth in **Article III** of the Parent DPA, except as expressly stated herein.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  
The Addendum Fees may include:

  (a) **Extended Subscription Fees.** Recurring monthly or annual fees for access to additional modules and functionality of the Horizon platform related to Model Management Services, HR Analytics Services, and Identity Resolution Services, calculated based on the number of enabled modules, data volume, or processing capacity attributable to the Extended Services;

  (b) **Implementation and Configuration Fees.** One‑time or phased fees for initial setup, integration, model design, and configuration activities specific to the Additional Processing Activities, consistent with the professional services fee structure outlined in **Section 3.1(b)** of the Parent DPA;

  (c) **Usage‑Based Extended Fees.** Charges based on incremental usage related to the Extended Services, such as additional storage, model inference calls, or identity resolution transactions, calculated and invoiced in a manner consistent with **Section 3.1(c)** and **3.2(c)** of the Parent DPA.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**

3.3.1 Horizon shall invoice Addendum Fees in accordance with the following, unless otherwise specified in an SOW:

  (a) Extended Subscription Fees shall be invoiced in advance on a quarterly or annual basis, aligned with the existing billing cycle under the Parent DPA where commercially feasible;

  (b) Implementation and Configuration Fees may be invoiced on a milestone basis (e.g., project kickoff, completion of initial model deployment, acceptance of HR analytics dashboards) or on a time‑and‑materials basis monthly in arrears, as specified in the relevant SOW;

  (c) Usage‑Based Extended Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears based on actual measured Extended Services usage for the preceding month.

3.3.2 Notwithstanding **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms) – “Net 30”** of the Parent DPA, the Parties hereby agree that the payment terms for invoices issued under this Addendum shall be **Net 45**, such that all undisputed Addendum Fee invoices shall be due and payable by Contoso within **forty‑five (45) days** of the invoice date. This specific change to payment terms applies solely to this Addendum and does not amend the Parent DPA payment terms for services outside the scope of this Addendum, unless expressly agreed by the Parties in writing.

3.4 **Late Payment; Interest.**  
In the event any undisputed Addendum Fee amount is not received by Horizon by the applicable due date, Horizon may, consistent with **Section 3.4** of the Parent DPA:

  (a) charge interest on the overdue amount at the lesser of (i) one and one‑half percent (1.5%) per month, or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, calculated from the due date until payment is received; and

  (b) after providing written notice and a ten (10) Business Day cure period, suspend the provision of the Extended Services until all undisputed overdue amounts are paid in full. Such suspension shall be carried out in a manner that is proportionate and does not unreasonably impact Services under the Parent DPA that are fully paid, where technically feasible.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  
The dispute mechanism in **Section 3.5** of the Parent DPA shall apply equally to invoices under this Addendum. Contoso shall notify Horizon in writing prior to the due date of any disputed portion of an Addendum invoice, specifying the basis of dispute, and shall pay all undisputed amounts. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve such disputes promptly.

3.6 **Taxes, Expenses, and Reimbursement.**  
The provisions of **Sections 3.6 (Taxes)** and **3.7 (Expenses and Reimbursement)** of the Parent DPA shall apply mutatis mutandis to all Addendum Fees. Any additional out‑of‑pocket expenses related specifically to the Additional Processing Activities (e.g., specialized data transfer costs or third‑party tools approved by Contoso) shall be pre‑approved in writing by Contoso and reimbursed at cost.

3.7 **No Withholding.**  
The provisions of **Section 3.8 (No Withholding)** of the Parent DPA shall apply to all payment obligations under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent DPA, shall remain in full force and effect until **March 09, 2031** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent DPA Term.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the Term of the Parent DPA, as set forth in **Section 4.1** of the Parent DPA, currently expires on **January 09, 2030**. The Parties intend that:

  (a) the Addendum shall not extend the Term of the Parent DPA; and  

  (b) if the Parent DPA is not renewed or extended beyond January 09, 2030, the Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination or expiration of the Parent DPA, notwithstanding any later Addendum Expiration Date, except as necessary for wind‑down and data return/deletion obligations.

4.3 **Renewal.**  
Any renewal or extension of this Addendum shall be subject to the Parties’ mutual written agreement and shall be coordinated with any renewal of the Parent DPA under **Section 4.2** thereof. Unless expressly stated in such renewal documentation, this Addendum shall not automatically renew.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent DPA which contemplates a sixty (60) day notice period, and in light of the low risk classification and the client‑friendly nature of this Addendum, either Party may terminate this Addendum (in whole or in respect of specific Additional Processing Activities) for convenience upon providing the other Party with not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice**, without terminating the Parent DPA, unless the notice expressly states otherwise.

4.5 **Termination for Cause and Regulatory Reasons.**  
The termination rights and procedures in **Sections 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** and **4.5 (Termination for Regulatory or Data Protection Reasons)** of the Parent DPA shall apply to this Addendum. For clarity:

  (a) a material breach by Horizon relating specifically to the Additional Processing Activities (including breach of **Article XI** of the Parent DPA as applied to such activities) may justify termination of this Addendum alone or, at Contoso’s election, the Parent DPA as well, in accordance with Section 4.4 of the Parent DPA; and  

  (b) if a Regulator issues a binding order materially restricting or prohibiting the Additional Processing Activities, Contoso may terminate this Addendum or the affected portion of the Extended Services upon written notice, consistent with **Section 4.5** of the Parent DPA.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Addendum.**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) Horizon shall cease performing the Additional Processing Activities, except to the extent necessary to provide transition assistance under **Section 4.7** of the Parent DPA and **Section 4.7** below or as required by Applicable Law;

  (b) the data return and deletion obligations in **Section 4.6(b)** and **Section 11.8 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent DPA shall apply to Personal Data Processed solely for the Additional Processing Activities, subject to any retention obligations imposed by Applicable Law;

  (c) Contoso shall pay Horizon all Addendum Fees due and payable for Extended Services rendered up to the effective date of termination, in accordance with **Section 4.6(c)** of the Parent DPA;

  (d) the provisions of this Addendum which by their nature should survive termination, including but not limited to confidentiality, intellectual property, indemnification, limitation of liability, and data protection obligations, shall survive in accordance with **Section 16.9** of the Parent DPA and **Article XVI** of this Addendum.

4.7 **Transition Assistance.**  
Upon Contoso’s written request made prior to or within thirty (30) days after termination or expiration of this Addendum, Horizon shall provide reasonable transition assistance related to the Additional Processing Activities, consistent with **Section 4.7** of the Parent DPA. In the event termination is due to Horizon’s uncured material breach specifically relating to the Additional Processing Activities, such assistance shall be provided at no additional cost to Contoso, to the extent commercially reasonable.
