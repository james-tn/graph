## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Purchase Agreement, reference number **PUR-GAM-202403-298** (file identifier **contract_298**), effective as of March 18, 2024 (the **“Parent Effective Date”**), pursuant to which Gamma agreed to sell and deliver certain **Products**, perform **Services**, and provide **Deliverables**, as such terms are defined in **Article I (Definitions)** and described in **Article II (Scope of Products, Services, and Work)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established an estimated aggregate contract value of **US $4,081,691** under **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)**, subject to adjustment via additional Statements of Work and approved Change Orders under **Article XIII (Change Management)**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to increase and reconfigure certain **hardware quantities**, associated **support and maintenance Services**, and **pricing structures** originally contemplated under the Parent Agreement and related Statements of Work and Purchase Orders, in order to support an expanded phase of Contoso’s data center modernization initiative described in **Section 2.1 (General Scope)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, this Amendment is intended to **modify quantities and pricing** under the Purchase Agreement **dated March 18, 2024 (Ref: PUR-GAM-202403-298 / contract_298)**, in accordance with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement, which requires that any amendment be in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both Parties;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge and agree that this Amendment **supplements** and **amends** certain provisions of the Parent Agreement, including **Article II (Scope of Products, Services, and Work)** and **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and that, except as expressly modified herein, all terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement shall continue in full force and effect;

WHEREAS, the Parties have agreed that the incremental contract value associated with this Amendment is **One Million Nine Hundred Fifty-Four Thousand Twenty-Seven US Dollars (US $1,954,027)** (the **“Amendment Value”**), which is in addition to the Contract Value defined in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth in this Amendment, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
REFERENCE TO MASTER/PARENT AGREEMENT AND SCOPE OF PRODUCTS, SERVICES, AND WORK

### 2.1 Reference to Master/Parent Agreement

2.1.1 **Reference and Incorporation.** This Amendment **is executed under and subject to the terms of Purchase Agreement reference number PUR-GAM-202403-298** (file identifier **contract_298**). Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms, conditions, rights, obligations, and definitions of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and are incorporated herein by this reference.

2.1.2 **Hierarchy.** In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between the terms of this Amendment and the Parent Agreement, the terms of this Amendment shall control solely with respect to the subject matter expressly addressed herein (i.e., the Incremental Products, Incremental Services, Incremental Deliverables, and related Fees and timelines). For all other matters, the Parent Agreement shall prevail.

### 2.2 General Scope

2.2.1 **Extension of Article II of Parent Agreement.** This Amendment supplements and extends **Article II (Scope of Products, Services, and Work)** of the Parent Agreement by adding Incremental Products and Incremental Services to support Contoso’s **Expanded Data Center Footprint** and increased capacity, performance, and resiliency requirements.

2.2.2 **Relationship to Existing SOWs and POs.** All Products, Services, and Deliverables under this Amendment shall be ordered pursuant to one or more **Statements of Work (SOWs)** and/or **Purchase Orders (POs)** that:  
(a) reference both the Parent Agreement (Ref: **PUR-GAM-202403-298 / contract_298**) and this Amendment (Ref: **AMD-GAM-202403-385**); and  
(b) specify that the quantities and pricing therein are governed by the unit pricing, discounts, and fee structures set forth in **Article III** of this Amendment.

2.2.3 **Nature of Expansion.** The Parties anticipate that the Incremental Products and Incremental Services will include, without limitation:  
(a) Additional rack-mounted and blade servers, storage arrays, and network switches consistent with **Section 2.2.1 (Core Hardware Systems)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Additional embedded and system software licenses consistent with **Section 2.2.2 (Embedded and System Software)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(c) Additional installation, configuration, migration, and cutover Services as described in **Sections 2.3.1 and 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement; and  
(d) Expanded support and maintenance Services under **Section 2.3.4** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.3 Incremental Products

2.3.1 **Quantities and Categories.** The Incremental Products to be supplied under this Amendment shall include, at a minimum, the following categories, with specific models, quantities, and unit prices set forth in **Exhibit A (Amendment Fee and Quantity Schedule)**:

- Enterprise-grade rack-mounted servers for application workloads and virtualization clusters;  
- High-capacity storage arrays (including SSD and hybrid configurations) to support expanded backup and analytics workloads;  
- Additional network switches, including top-of-rack and aggregation switches, to interconnect the Incremental Products with Contoso’s existing infrastructure;  
- Related peripherals (rails, power distribution units, cabling, transceivers) necessary for deployment; and  
- Embedded firmware and system software required for the operation of the foregoing hardware.

2.3.2 **Specifications.** Each Incremental Product shall conform in all material respects to the **Specifications** agreed by the Parties and documented in the relevant SOW or PO, consistent with **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement. Gamma shall ensure that all Incremental Products are compatible with, and operable within, the architecture previously defined under the Parent Agreement’s project scope.

2.3.3 **Delivery and Installation.** Delivery and installation of Incremental Products shall follow the delivery terms set forth in **Section 2.2.3 (Delivery and Installation)** of the Parent Agreement, including **DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) Contoso’s designated facility (Incoterms 2020)**, unless otherwise agreed in writing in the applicable SOW. Gamma shall coordinate shipment and installation so as not to unreasonably disrupt Contoso’s ongoing operations.

### 2.4 Incremental Services and Deliverables

2.4.1 **Implementation and Configuration.** Gamma shall provide Incremental Services to design, deploy, and configure the Incremental Products in Contoso’s Expanded Data Center Footprint, consistent with **Section 2.3.1 (Implementation and Configuration Services)** of the Parent Agreement. Such Services shall include:  
(a) Assessment of existing capacity and performance baselines;  
(b) Integration of Incremental Products with existing systems deployed under the Parent Agreement;  
(c) Configuration of networking, storage, and compute layers to ensure high availability and optimal performance; and  
(d) Validation testing to confirm compliance with agreed acceptance criteria.

2.4.2 **Migration and Cutover.** Where specified in an SOW under this Amendment, Gamma shall provide additional **Migration and Cutover Services** consistent with **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement, including workload balancing, data migration, and controlled cutover activities, scheduled during approved maintenance windows.

2.4.3 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.** Gamma shall provide incremental training Sessions to Contoso’s personnel, augmenting the training provided under the Parent Agreement, in accordance with **Section 2.3.3** thereof. This may include advanced training on new hardware platforms, updated firmware features, and revised operational runbooks that reflect the Expanded Data Center Footprint.

2.4.4 **Support and Maintenance.** The Incremental Services include expanded support and maintenance coverage, which shall be subject to the Service Levels defined in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by any **Additional Uptime Commitments** agreed in writing. Gamma shall update its helpdesk and ticketing arrangements to recognize the incremental asset inventory and associated SLAs.

2.4.5 **Incremental Deliverables.** As part of the Incremental Services, Gamma shall provide Incremental Deliverables that are consistent with **Section 2.4 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
(a) Updated system architecture diagrams reflecting Incremental Products;  
(b) Revised configuration documentation and inventory lists;  
(c) Updated SOPs, runbooks, and backup/recovery procedures covering the new environment;  
(d) Additional project status reports and completion reports specific to the Incremental rollout; and  
(e) Test plans and test reports demonstrating that Incremental Products and Incremental Services meet the agreed Specifications and acceptance criteria.

### 2.5 Timelines and Milestones under the Amendment

2.5.1 **Integrated Project Plan.** Within fifteen (15) **Business Days** following the Amendment Effective Date or execution of the first SOW under this Amendment (whichever is later), Gamma shall deliver to Contoso an **Integrated Project Plan** that incorporates and aligns the original project plan required under **Section 2.5.1** of the Parent Agreement with the new milestones required for the Incremental Products and Incremental Services.

2.5.2 **Critical Milestones.** Any milestones designated as **“Critical” or “Time-Sensitive”** under SOWs referencing this Amendment shall be subject to the same **“time is of the essence”** standard described in **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent Agreement. Repeated or material delays (not excused under **Article XV (Force Majeure)** of the Parent Agreement) may constitute a material breach as referenced in **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.3 **Performance Standards.** Gamma shall perform all Incremental Services in a professional and workmanlike manner and in compliance with the **Performance Standards** set forth in **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement. Any nonconforming Incremental Products or Incremental Deliverables shall be repaired, replaced, or re-performed at Gamma’s cost in accordance with **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Value and Relationship to Parent Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the estimated aggregate value of the Incremental Products, Incremental Services, and Incremental Deliverables under this Amendment is **US $1,954,027 (the “Amendment Value”)**, which is **in addition to** the **US $4,081,691 Contract Value** defined in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 The **Combined Contract Value** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment is therefore estimated at **US $6,035,718**, subject to future adjustments via additional SOWs or Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

3.2 **Fee Structure under the Amendment.**

3.2.1 **Products.** Fees for Incremental Products shall be as set forth in **Exhibit A (Amendment Fee and Quantity Schedule)** to this Amendment and/or in the applicable SOW or PO referencing this Amendment. Product Fees may be itemized on a unit-price basis, and may include:  
(a) hardware list prices;  
(b) volume-based discounts tied to aggregate hardware units (including those under the Parent Agreement); and  
(c) bundled support pricing based on the total asset base.

3.2.2 **Services.** Fees for Incremental Services shall be specified in the applicable SOW and may be structured as:  
(a) fixed-price milestones for implementation, configuration, and migration work;  
(b) time-and-materials (T&M) services subject to daily or hourly rates consistent with the Parent Agreement; and/or  
(c) a combination of fixed-price and T&M, with any T&M caps explicitly stated in the SOW.

3.2.3 **Support and Maintenance.** Recurring Fees for expanded support and maintenance services (including any Additional Uptime Commitment) shall be reflected as annual or monthly charges, billed in advance or arrears as specified in the SOW, and shall be integrated into the SLA framework of **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Invoicing under the Amendment.**

3.3.1 **Invoice References.** Each invoice issued under this Amendment shall:  
(a) reference the Parent Agreement by its contract reference number **PUR-GAM-202403-298** and identifier **contract_298**, and  
(b) reference this Amendment by **AMD-GAM-202403-385**,  
(c) identify the applicable SOW or PO, and  
(d) describe the Incremental Products and Incremental Services provided.

3.3.2 **Product Invoices.** Consistent with **Section 3.3.1 (Product Invoices)** of the Parent Agreement, Gamma may invoice for Incremental Products upon delivery to the designated location, together with proof of delivery.

3.3.3 **Services Invoices.**  
(a) For fixed-price or milestone-based Incremental Services, Gamma may invoice upon completion and acceptance of the corresponding milestone, following any acceptance procedures in the applicable SOW; and  
(b) For T&M Incremental Services, Gamma may invoice monthly in arrears with reasonable supporting documentation, as contemplated in **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Payment Terms.**

3.4.1 **Net 60.** Payment terms for all undisputed amounts under this Amendment shall be **Net 60 (sixty) days** from the date of receipt of a properly issued invoice, consistent with **Section 3.4.1 (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4.2 **Disputed Amounts.** Dispute procedures for any invoice under this Amendment shall follow **Section 3.4.2 (Disputed Invoices)** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall pay undisputed portions and the Parties shall cooperate to resolve disputes in good faith.

3.5 **Late Payment and Suspension.** The **late payment interest** and **suspension rights** set forth in **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement apply to Fees under this Amendment. Gamma’s right to suspend performance extends to Incremental Services and shipments of Incremental Products, subject to the good faith dispute provisions in **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Taxes and Expenses.**  

3.6.1 Taxes on the Fees under this Amendment shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.6.2 Reimbursement of pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses related to Incremental Services shall be governed by **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement, and such expenses shall be billed under invoices referencing this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.** The **Amendment Term** shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and continue until **July 22, 2028** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.** Notwithstanding the **Expiration Date of January 29, 2026** set forth in **Section 4.1** of the Parent Agreement:

4.2.1 The Parties agree that the Amendment Term shall not extend the overall Term of the Parent Agreement unless the Parent Agreement is separately renewed or extended under **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof.  

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is not renewed beyond January 29, 2026, the Parties shall either:  
(a) execute an extension or renewal of the Parent Agreement sufficient to cover the Amendment Term; or  
(b) negotiate in good faith a successor agreement incorporating the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

4.2.3 In the absence of such extension or successor agreement, the Parties shall wind down activities under this Amendment in accordance with **Section 4.5 (Effect of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to any agreed transitional services to protect Contoso’s operational continuity.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part (including any SOW referencing this Amendment), for convenience and without cause upon not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Gamma. This is more client-favorable than the sixty (60) days’ notice in **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement and shall apply specifically to this Amendment.

4.3.2 **By Gamma.** Gamma may terminate this Amendment (but not the entire Parent Agreement, unless otherwise permitted) for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, provided that such termination shall not relieve Gamma of its obligations with respect to Incremental Products and Incremental Services already delivered or committed to under non-cancellable SOWs.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause under the circumstances and cure periods described in **Section 4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement, including material breach that is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

4.5.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain certain obligations under this Amendment (such as ongoing support) under a successor agreement.

4.5.2 Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment (whether or not coincident with the Parent Agreement), the Parties shall follow the wind-down, payment, and return-of-materials provisions set forth in **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, including payment for Incremental Products delivered, Incremental Services performed, and non-cancellable commitments incurred in reliance on this Amendment.

4.6 **Survival.** Provisions of this Amendment that, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration (including payment obligations, confidentiality, IP rights, limitations of liability, and dispute resolution) shall survive, consistent with **Section 4.5.4** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IX  
LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Exclusion of Certain Damages.** To the maximum extent permitted by Applicable Law, and consistent with **Section 9.1** of the Parent Agreement, neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect, incidental, special, exemplary, or consequential damages, or for loss of profits, revenue, data, or goodwill, arising out of or related to this Amendment.

9.2 **Amendment-Specific Liability Cap.**  

Notwithstanding **Section 9.2** of the Parent Agreement, and in recognition of the **low risk** allocation agreed by the Parties, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to claims arising under this Amendment, each Party’s aggregate liability to the other Party shall not exceed an amount equal to **five (5) times** the total Fees actually paid or payable by Contoso to Gamma under this Amendment during the **twelve (12) month period** immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

9.3 **Application with Parent Cap.** For clarity, the Amendment-specific cap in **Section 9.2** above operates alongside, and not in addition to, the overall limitation in **Section 9.2** of the Parent Agreement. To the extent a claim spans both the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, the Parties shall discuss in good faith a reasonable allocation of the combined cap, but in no event shall the aggregate liability exceed the greater of:  
(a) the cap in **Section 9.2** of the Parent Agreement, or  
(b) the Amendment-specific cap set forth above.

9.4 **Exceptions.** The exceptions in **Section 9.3** of the Parent Agreement (indemnification, breach of confidentiality, gross negligence or willful misconduct, and amounts owed for Products and Services) apply equally to this Amendment.

9.5 **Allocation of Risk.** The Parties acknowledge that the Fees and other terms in this Amendment reflect the **allocation of risk** between the Parties and that the limitations of liability in this Article IX are an essential element of the Parties’ bargain, consistent with **Section 9.4** of the Parent Agreement.
