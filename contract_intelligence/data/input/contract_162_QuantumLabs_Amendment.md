## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Consulting Services Agreement** dated **August 25, 2024** (the **“Effective Date”**), identified as **Consulting Services Agreement – Contract Reference Number CSA-QUA-202408-108** and also identified internally by the Client as **“contract_108”** (the **“Consulting Agreement”** or **“Parent Agreement”**);

WHEREAS, pursuant to **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement, no amendment or modification of the Parent Agreement shall be binding unless it is in writing and signed by duly authorized representatives of both Parties;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement currently has a **Term** (as defined in Section 1.17 and Section 4.1 of the Parent Agreement) commencing on August 25, 2024 and continuing until **October 20, 2028**, and provides for consulting and related professional services under various **Statements of Work (SOWs)** in accordance with **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement recites an initial anticipated **Contract Value** of **One Hundred Eighty-Six Thousand Four Hundred Eleven U.S. Dollars (US$186,411)** under **Section 3.1.1 (Contract Value)**, and the Parties now wish to significantly expand the scope, value, and duration of their relationship;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire, in light of a **medium risk profile** for this expanded engagement, to adjust certain provisions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article IV (Term and Termination)**, and **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)**, and to supplement the Parent Agreement with additional terms regarding Service Levels, Data Protection, and Change Management specifically applicable to the expanded scope described herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend that this Amendment:  
- **(a)** establishes an additional **contract value ceiling** of **Three Million Seven Hundred Ninety-Eight Thousand Two Hundred Twenty U.S. Dollars (US$3,798,220)** associated with the expanded Services described herein (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**),  
- **(b)** modifies the payment terms applicable to Services rendered under this Amendment from **Net 60** (as stated in Section 3.2.2 of the Parent Agreement) to **Net 30**,  
- **(c)** introduces a **liability cap equal to three (3) times the Fees paid by Contoso in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim** with respect to Services and Deliverables provided under this Amendment, and  
- **(d)** extends certain commercial and operational terms through an **Amendment-specific expiration date of May 03, 2029**, subject to and as more fully described herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and agreements herein contained, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 **Amendment of Article II of Parent Agreement**  
This Amendment **supplements and partially modifies** the scope of services described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly set forth herein, Article II of the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and shall apply to the Amendment Services. The categories of Services set forth in Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement (Strategic Advisory Services, Data & Analytics Consulting, Technology Implementation & Integration, Training and Knowledge Transfer, and Ongoing Support and Advisory) are hereby extended and expanded as described below.

2.1.2 **Hierarchy**  
In the event of any conflict between the provisions of this **Article II of the Amendment** and **Article II of the Parent Agreement**, the provisions of this Amendment shall control solely with respect to the Amendment Services.

### 2.2 Description of Amendment Services

2.2.1 **Program Overview**  
Under this Amendment, Quantum Labs shall provide a multi-year, enterprise-scale analytics and AI enablement program for Contoso, consisting of:

- Expansion of advanced analytics and AI models across multiple business units;  
- Design and implementation of a unified data platform and governance framework;  
- Deployment and optimization of Vendor Technology and third-party tools;  
- Ongoing advisory, support, training, and knowledge transfer; and  
- Performance monitoring against jointly defined KPIs.

2.2.2 **Workstreams**  
The Amendment Services shall, at a minimum, comprise the following workstreams:

**(a) Strategic Analytics & AI Roadmap (Extension of Section 2.2(1))**  
Quantum Labs will update and extend the strategic advisory work provided under the Parent Agreement by:

- Developing a three- to five-year enterprise analytics roadmap aligned with Contoso’s corporate strategy;  
- Identifying priority use cases across finance, operations, marketing, and risk;  
- Recommending technology stack options, including Vendor Technology and third-party platforms; and  
- Producing executive-ready presentations, decision memos, and investment cases (each being a Deliverable).

**(b) Enterprise Data Platform & Governance (Extension of Section 2.2(2))**  
Building upon the data & analytics consulting previously described in Section 2.2(2) of the Parent Agreement, Quantum Labs shall:

- Design a consolidated data architecture, including a data lakehouse, standardized data models, and data pipelines;  
- Implement data ingestion, transformation, and quality-monitoring workflows;  
- Define and document data governance policies, stewardship roles, and data cataloging practices; and  
- Deliver technical design documents, runbooks, and data dictionaries as Deliverables.

**(c) Advanced Model Development & MLOps (Extension of Section 2.2(2)-(3))**  
Quantum Labs shall design and implement predictive and prescriptive models, including but not limited to:

- Demand forecasting, customer churn prediction, pricing optimization, and fraud detection;  
- Model deployment pipelines, monitoring, and automated retraining using MLOps best practices;  
- Integration of models into Contoso’s production systems through APIs and middleware;  
- Delivery of model documentation, performance reports, and implementation guides as Deliverables.

**(d) Training, Change Enablement, and Adoption (Extension of Section 2.2(4))**  
Quantum Labs shall provide extensive training and change management support, including:

- Development of curricula for technical and non-technical user groups;  
- Delivery of workshops, seminars, and “train-the-trainer” sessions;  
- Creation of user guides, FAQs, and learning assets as Deliverables; and  
- Ongoing office hours and advisory sessions to support adoption.

**(e) Operational Support & Continuous Improvement (Extension of Section 2.2(5))**  
Quantum Labs will provide ongoing support and advisory services, including:

- Level 2 and Level 3 support for analytics platform issues;  
- Quarterly health checks and maturity assessments;  
- Continuous improvement recommendations and backlog prioritization; and  
- Joint review of KPIs and performance metrics on at least a quarterly basis.

### 2.3 Deliverables, Milestones, and Timelines

2.3.1 **Deliverables Framework**  
The Parties reaffirm that **Section 2.3 (Statements of Work)** and **Section 2.4 (Deliverables; Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement shall govern Deliverables and acceptance, except as expressly modified below.

2.3.2 **Milestone-Based Deliverables**  
Each Amendment SOW shall identify specific Deliverables to be produced within each workstream, along with:

- Detailed descriptions and technical specifications;  
- Acceptance criteria and success metrics;  
- Milestone dates and dependencies; and  
- Resource assumptions and responsibilities.

2.3.3 **Timelines**  
The Parties anticipate that the Amendment Services shall proceed in phases over the Amendment Term. Initial indicative phases include:

- **Phase 1 (Months 1–6)**: Strategy refinement, target architecture, and foundational platform components;  
- **Phase 2 (Months 7–18)**: Model development, pilot deployments, and initial production rollouts;  
- **Phase 3 (Months 19–36)**: Scale-up, enterprise integration, and advanced training;  
- **Phase 4 (Months 37–48)**: Optimization, transition planning, and exit/hand-over.

Specific timelines shall be crystallized in the applicable Amendment SOW and may be adjusted through the Change Management process set out in **Article XIII**.

2.3.4 **Acceptance Period**  
The ten (10) Business Day **Review Period** in Section 2.4.2 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to Deliverables under this Amendment, except that the Parties may agree in an Amendment SOW to a longer or shorter review period for particularly complex or time-sensitive Deliverables.

### 2.4 Performance Standards

Quantum Labs shall perform the Amendment Services:

- In accordance with the professional and workmanlike standard set out in **Section 2.4.1** and **Section 7.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- Using personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and domain expertise;  
- In compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including **Data Protection Laws** as defined in Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement and further addressed in **Article XI** of this Amendment; and  
- In a manner designed to support the Service Levels described in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

### 2.5 Contoso Responsibilities

Contoso’s obligations in **Section 2.6 (Contoso Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Amendment Services. In addition, Contoso shall:

- Designate executive sponsors and product owners for each major workstream;  
- Provide timely access to relevant systems, datasets, subject-matter experts, and third-party vendors;  
- Ensure that required business decisions, prioritizations, and approvals are made within agreed timeframes; and  
- Provide reasonably adequate internal communication and change-management support to facilitate adoption of Deliverables.

Quantum Labs shall not be responsible for delays, cost overruns, or diminished performance of Deliverables to the extent caused by Contoso’s failure to meet these responsibilities, and any such impacts shall be addressed through the Change Management process in **Article XIII**.

### 2.6 Subcontractors

The provisions of **Section 2.7 (Subcontractors)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply in full to the Amendment Services. Quantum Labs may use Subcontractors for specialized tasks, provided that it remains responsible for their performance.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Amendment Contract Value and Structure

This amount is an estimate and may be exceeded upon mutual written agreement pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)**.

Consistent with **Section 3.1.2 (Fee Types)** of the Parent Agreement, Fees under this Amendment may include:

- Time-and-materials fees based on agreed daily or hourly rates;  
- Recurring subscription or retainer fees for ongoing support; and  
- Performance-based or success-based fees tied to agreed KPIs (e.g., model performance, adoption metrics), where expressly agreed in an Amendment SOW.

### 3.2 Payment Terms (Modification of Section 3.2.2)

3.2.1 **Invoices**  
Quantum Labs shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the billing schedule set forth in each Amendment SOW. Unless otherwise specified:

- Time-and-materials services may be invoiced **monthly in arrears**;  
- Fixed-fee Deliverables may be invoiced upon completion of milestones; and  
- Recurring services may be invoiced monthly or quarterly in advance as stated in the applicable Amendment SOW.

3.2.2 **Net 30 Terms – Amendment Override**  
**Section 3.2.2 (Net 60 Terms)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended solely for the Services and Deliverables provided under this Amendment** as follows:

> For all undisputed invoices issued under this Amendment and any Amendment SOW, Contoso shall pay the invoiced amounts within **thirty (30) days** of the invoice date (**“Net 30”**).  

The Net 60 payment term in Section 3.2.2 of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply to all Services and Deliverables that are not governed by this Amendment.

3.2.3 **Disputed Amounts**  
The dispute process in **Section 3.2.3 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply, except that the Parties shall use reasonable efforts to resolve billing disputes under this Amendment within **twenty (20) days** of notice. Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts by their due date.

### 3.3 Late Payments

Undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Amendment shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.3 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement. Quantum Labs may suspend or withhold performance of the Amendment Services for overdue undisputed amounts that remain unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after written notice, as described in Section 3.3.

### 3.4 Expenses and Reimbursement

The expense reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.4 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all reimbursable expenses associated with the Amendment Services, including but not limited to reasonable travel, lodging, meals, and necessary specialized tools or datasets, subject to Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies.

### 3.5 Taxes

Fees and expenses under this Amendment are exclusive of **Taxes**, as defined in Section 1.20 and addressed in **Section 3.5 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement, which applies in full.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Amendment Term

The term of this Amendment (the **“Amendment Term”**) shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date (April 21, 2025)** and shall continue until the **Amendment Expiration Date (May 03, 2029)**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with:

- **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**, as modified by this Amendment; and  
- The specific termination provisions set forth herein.

### 4.2 Relationship to Parent Agreement Term

4.2.1 **Extension for Amendment Services**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement, which sets an Expiration Date of October 20, 2028, the Parties agree that:

- The Parent Agreement is hereby **supplemented** to permit Quantum Labs to provide, and Contoso to receive, the Amendment Services through the **Amendment Expiration Date**; and  
- All intellectual property, confidentiality, and payment obligations related to the Amendment Services shall continue to be governed by the Parent Agreement (as amended hereby) through the Amendment Expiration Date and thereafter to the extent such provisions survive termination.

For clarity, this Amendment does not, by itself, extend the Parent Agreement for any services other than the Amendment Services, but the Parent Agreement shall be deemed in full force and effect for all purposes necessary to give effect to this Amendment through the Amendment Expiration Date.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

The **termination for convenience** provisions in **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement** are modified with respect to the Amendment Services as follows:

- Contoso may terminate this Amendment or any Amendment SOW for convenience upon **not less than sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Quantum Labs, which is a shorter period than the 180 days’ notice under the Parent Agreement, reflecting a balanced risk allocation.  
- Quantum Labs may terminate this Amendment or any Amendment SOW for convenience upon **not less than ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, consistent with the existing notice period for Quantum Labs in Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

Termination for convenience of this Amendment shall not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement with respect to services not governed by this Amendment unless expressly stated in such notice.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

The **Termination for Cause** provisions in **Section 4.4 (Material Breach; Insolvency)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment and all Amendment SOWs. Either Party may terminate this Amendment (in whole or in part) for material breach upon written notice and the breaching Party’s failure to cure within thirty (30) days after receipt of such notice, as provided in Section 4.4.1.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment for any reason:

- (a) Quantum Labs shall cease providing the Amendment Services, except as necessary to fulfill wind-down obligations;  
- (b) Contoso shall pay Quantum Labs all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred for Amendment Services through the effective date of termination (including non-cancelable commitments and reasonable wind-down costs), consistent with **Section 4.5(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- (c) The provisions regarding return or destruction of Confidential Information and Client Personal Data in **Section 4.5(c)** and **Article V and XI** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all materials processed or generated under this Amendment; and  
- (d) Any licenses granted under **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement (as modified by this Amendment) shall continue in accordance with their terms, subject to Contoso’s full payment of all amounts due.

### 4.6 Termination of Parent Agreement

If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety pursuant to **Article IV**, then, unless otherwise agreed in writing:

- This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination of the Parent Agreement; and  
- The effect of termination shall be as described in **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 4.5 of this Amendment**.

### 4.7 Survival

Any provisions of this Amendment that by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including without limitation Articles **V (Confidentiality)**, **VI (Intellectual Property Rights)**, **VII (Representations and Warranties)**, **VIII (Indemnification)**, **IX (Limitation of Liability)**, **XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, **XIV (Dispute Resolution)**, and payment obligations accrued prior to termination.

## ARTICLE IX. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### 9.1 Modification of Parent Agreement’s No-Cap Regime (Medium-Risk Adjustment)

The Parties acknowledge that **Article IX of the Parent Agreement** currently provides for **no contractual cap on liability**, reflecting a high-risk engagement. In light of the Parties’ desire to adopt a **medium-risk allocation** for the expanded scope and increased Amendment Contract Value, Article IX is hereby modified **solely with respect to the Amendment Services** as follows.

### 9.2 Liability Cap Applicable to Amendment Services

Notwithstanding **Section 9.1 of the Parent Agreement**, the total aggregate liability of either Party to the other arising out of or relating to this Amendment, the Amendment Services, and any Amendment SOW, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), or otherwise, shall **not exceed an amount equal to three (3) times the total Fees paid (and payable, but excluding Taxes and expenses) by Contoso to Quantum Labs for the Amendment Services during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim** (the **“Amendment Liability Cap”**).

The no-cap liability regime in Section 9.1 of the Parent Agreement shall remain in effect for Services and Deliverables not governed by this Amendment.

### 9.3 Exclusion of Certain Damages

The exclusion of consequential and other indirect damages set forth in **Section 9.2 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply to the Amendment Services, subject to the same exceptions enumerated in that Section, including:

- (a) Breach of confidentiality under **Article V** or data protection obligations under **Article XI**;  
- (b) Indemnification obligations under **Article VIII**; and  
- (c) Quantum Labs’ gross negligence or willful misconduct.

### 9.4 Exceptions to Liability Cap

The Amendment Liability Cap in Section 9.2 shall **not** apply to:

- A Party’s breach of its confidentiality obligations under **Article V**;  
- A Party’s breach of its data protection obligations under **Article XI**;  
- A Party’s indemnification obligations under **Article VIII**; or  
- A Party’s liability for gross negligence or willful misconduct.

In such cases, liability shall be governed by the uncapped regime of **Article IX of the Parent Agreement**.
