### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties expressly acknowledge and agree that, except as expressly amended by this Amendment, the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, including without limitation **Articles V–XVI** (Confidentiality, IP, Indemnification, Limitation of Liability, Insurance, Data Protection, SLA, Change Management, Dispute Resolution, Force Majeure, and General Provisions), shall continue in full force and effect and shall apply to this Amendment;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements set forth in this Amendment, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF AMENDED SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This **Article II** is intended to **supplement and modify** the provisions of **Article II (Scope of Lease and Services)** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to the Amended Premises Services and Additional Capital Improvements described herein. Except as expressly stated, the definitions and general obligations set forth in **Sections 2.1–2.5** of the Parent Agreement continue to apply.

2.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, Phoenix’s obligations regarding facility management, maintenance, janitorial, security, landscaping, and utility services described in **Section 2.2.1 and 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement remain in effect and are hereby expanded as provided below for the Amendment Term.

2.2 **Scope of Amended Premises Services.**

2.2.1 **Expanded Operational Management.** During the Amendment Term, Phoenix shall provide an enhanced scope of operational management for the Premises, which shall include:

(a) **Enhanced Building Operations.** In addition to the building operations referenced in **Section 2.2.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement, Phoenix shall implement advanced energy management controls, enhanced after-hours HVAC scheduling, and integration with Contoso’s building automation platform (where compatible) to support extended business operations for Contoso’s high-availability functions.

(b) **Extended Maintenance Coverage.** Phoenix shall extend maintenance and repair coverage, as referenced in **Section 2.2.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement, to include prioritized preventive maintenance for critical electrical and mechanical components designated by Contoso as “mission critical” under the Project Plan. Phoenix shall schedule such maintenance to minimize disruption and shall maintain a rolling 12‑month maintenance calendar shared electronically with Contoso.

(c) **Enhanced Security Services.** Phoenix shall augment the “Security Services” described in **Section 2.2.1(d)** of the Parent Agreement by:  
   (i) installing and managing additional CCTV coverage for key ingress/egress points and designated secure areas;  
   (ii) implementing multi-factor access control for sensitive zones, subject to Contoso’s written specifications; and  
   (iii) coordinating with Contoso’s corporate security team on incident response procedures and emergency drills.

(d) **Specialized Environmental Controls.** For any areas of the Premises designated by Contoso for equipment staging, light industrial testing, or temperature-sensitive storage, Phoenix shall provide specialized HVAC zoning, humidity control, and monitoring consistent with the Service Standards in **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 **Operational Hours and Access.** The non-exclusive right to use Common Areas and access the Premises granted under **Section 2.1.1** of the Parent Agreement remains in effect. Phoenix agrees to maintain secure access to the Premises on a 24x7 basis for authorized Contoso personnel and pre-approved contractors, subject to building rules and Emergency Conditions.

2.3 **Additional Capital Improvements.**

2.3.1 **Scope of Improvements.** In addition to the Tenant Improvements contemplated in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement and any **Exhibit B – Tenant Improvement Work Letter**, Phoenix shall perform the following Additional Capital Improvements under this Amendment:

(a) **Electrical Infrastructure Upgrades.** Expansion of the electrical capacity serving the Premises, including additional dedicated circuits and panel upgrades required to support Contoso’s increased equipment load, ensuring compliance with Applicable Law and relevant building codes.

(b) **Redundant Connectivity Pathways.** Construction and provisioning of additional risers, conduits, and protected pathways for data and communications cabling, to facilitate Contoso’s redundant connectivity infrastructure and minimize network single points of failure.

(c) **Extended Security Infrastructure.** Installation of additional access control readers, CCTV cameras, recording storage, and related security system components, integrated with existing building systems, subject to Contoso’s security protocols and Acceptable Use requirements.

(d) **Resilience Enhancements.** Installation of surge protection, backup power interfaces (where generator capacity exists), and other measures to enhance the resilience of critical building systems servicing the Premises, as detailed in the Project Plan.

2.3.2 **Ownership of Improvements.** Unless otherwise expressly stated in a written change order, all Additional Capital Improvements permanently affixed to the Premises shall be deemed “Improvements” as defined in **Section 1.11** of the Parent Agreement and shall become the property of Phoenix upon installation, subject to Contoso’s rights of use during the Lease Term and Amendment Term.

2.3.3 **Cost Allocation.** Costs associated with the Additional Capital Improvements shall be included in or amortized through the **Amendment Contract Value (US$2,788,557)** and the Amended Rent Schedule described in **Article III** of this Amendment. To the extent any extraordinary or scope-increasing change is requested by Contoso after execution of this Amendment, such changes shall be handled via the **Change Management** process in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement as supplemented by **Article XIII** of this Amendment.

2.4 **Timelines and Project Plan.**

2.4.1 **Project Plan Adoption.** Within thirty (30) days following the Amendment Effective Date, the Parties shall develop and mutually agree upon a written **Project Plan**, which shall include:

(a) specific milestones for completion of the Additional Capital Improvements;  
(b) anticipated start and completion dates for each workstream;  
(c) dependencies and constraints; and  
(d) acceptance criteria and test protocols where applicable.

The Project Plan shall be incorporated by reference into this Amendment and may be updated by mutual written agreement in accordance with **Article XIII (Change Management)** of this Amendment.

2.4.2 **Performance Standards.** Phoenix shall perform the Amended Services and Additional Capital Improvements (i) in a professional, workmanlike manner consistent with the standards in **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent Agreement, (ii) in compliance with Applicable Law, and (iii) using personnel with appropriate training and qualifications.

2.4.3 **Delays and Force Majeure.** In the event of delays caused by Force Majeure Events (as defined in **Section 15.1** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XV** of this Amendment) or by Contoso’s acts or omissions (including delayed approvals or access), Phoenix’s schedule obligations shall be equitably extended, and any associated cost impacts shall be addressed pursuant to **Article XIII** of this Amendment.

2.5 **Contoso Obligations regarding Use and Coordination.**

2.5.1 **Access and Cooperation.** Contoso shall provide Phoenix and its contractors with reasonable access to the Premises as necessary to perform the Additional Capital Improvements and Amended Services, subject to standard security and safety protocols. Contoso shall use commercially reasonable efforts to coordinate its internal projects to avoid materially impeding Phoenix’s performance.

2.5.2 **Compliance with Work Rules and Law.** The obligations in **Section 2.3.1 (Compliance with Law and Work Rules)** of the Parent Agreement are hereby reaffirmed. Contoso shall ensure that its increased operational activities under this Amendment do not violate Applicable Law or create hazardous conditions, and shall promptly notify Phoenix of any operational changes that may affect building systems.

2.5.3 **Change in Use.** To the extent Contoso intends to materially modify the use of any portion of the Premises beyond the uses contemplated in **Section 2.1.2** of the Parent Agreement (general office, light industrial, warehousing), Contoso shall obtain Phoenix’s prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld) and, where such change increases risk or operating costs, the Parties shall address any additional fees through **Article XIII (Change Management)** of this Amendment.

2.6 **Supplemental Deliverables.**

Phoenix shall provide, in addition to the Deliverables specified in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, the following Supplemental Deliverables:

(a) updated building operations and maintenance reports reflecting the Additional Capital Improvements and Amended Services, provided **monthly** during the primary execution phases and **quarterly** thereafter;  
(b) as-built drawings and updated system diagrams related to electrical, security, and HVAC upgrades;  
(c) commissioning reports and test results for resilience and environmental controls; and  
(d) upon reasonable request, summaries of relevant Regulatory Data pertaining to environmental and safety compliance.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES, RENT, AND PAYMENT TERMS (AMENDMENT)

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**

3.1.1 **Aggregate Value.** The total contract value associated with this Amendment (inclusive of the lease-related financial consideration for the Amended Premises Services, the amortized cost of the Additional Capital Improvements, and the enhanced Services described in **Article II**) is **Two Million Seven Hundred Eighty-Eight Thousand Five Hundred Fifty-Seven U.S. Dollars (US$2,788,557)** (the “Amendment Contract Value”).

3.1.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Value.** The Amendment Contract Value shall be **incremental** to the total estimated contract value of **US$1,679,296** specified in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent Agreement, except to the extent the Parties specifically agree that certain fees already included in the Parent Agreement are superseded by the Amended Rent Schedule. In the event of conflict, the Amended Rent Schedule shall control with respect to the scope covered by this Amendment.

3.2 **Amended Base Rent and Service Fees.**

3.2.1 **Amended Rent Schedule.** The Parties agree that for the Amendment Term, Phoenix shall invoice Contoso in accordance with an **Amended Rent Schedule** (superseding the Parent Agreement’s **Exhibit C** solely as to the Amended Premises Services), which shall comprise:

(a) **Monthly Base Rent Component.** A fixed monthly Base Rent amount allocated such that the aggregate Base Rent over the Amendment Term contributes to the Amendment Contract Value in line with the financial model agreed by the Parties (the exact numeric schedule to be set forth in a revised Rent Schedule attached as **Exhibit C-1** to this Amendment).

(b) **Monthly Capital Recovery Component.** A fixed monthly charge representing the amortized cost of the Additional Capital Improvements over the Amendment Term, calculated on a straight-line basis, with no prepayment penalty if the Amendment is terminated early in accordance with Article IV, subject to early termination fee provisions.

(c) **Enhanced Service Fee Component.** A fixed monthly fee for the enhanced operational and security services described in **Article II** of this Amendment, including SLA Enhancements under **Article XII**.

3.2.2 **Operating Expenses and Taxes.** The allocation and reconciliation of Operating Expenses and Taxes under **Sections 3.3.1–3.3.3** of the Parent Agreement remain in effect, provided that any incremental Operating Expenses reasonably attributable to the Additional Capital Improvements or increased usage under this Amendment may be included in Operating Expenses and allocated to Contoso in accordance with the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Invoicing under the Amendment.**

3.3.1 **Regular Invoicing.** Phoenix shall issue invoices **monthly in advance** for recurring Base Rent, Capital Recovery, and Enhanced Service Fee components, and **monthly in arrears** for variable or usage-based charges, consistent with **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent Agreement, modified as to payment terms below.

3.3.2 **Detail Requirements.** Each invoice shall (a) identify this Amendment by reference to **AMD-PHO-202506-719**; (b) specify the billing period; (c) separate charges associated with the Parent Agreement from charges associated with this Amendment; and (d) include sufficient detail to permit Contoso to verify the accuracy of the charges.

3.4 **Payment Terms (Net 60).**

3.4.1 **Net 60 Terms.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.4.1 (Net 45)** of the Parent Agreement, all **undisputed amounts** invoiced by Phoenix under this Amendment shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** from the invoice date (“Net 60”). For avoidance of doubt, this modification applies solely to amounts billed under this Amendment and does not alter the Net 45 terms applicable to any other amounts under the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.4.2 **Method of Payment.** Payments shall be made in U.S. Dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or check, as directed in Phoenix’s invoice, consistent with **Section 3.4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4.3 **Disputed Amounts.** The provisions of **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent Agreement regarding disputed amounts shall apply mutatis mutandis to charges under this Amendment, except that any reference to the payment due date shall be construed with reference to the Net 60 Terms.

3.5 **Late Payments and Finance Charges.**

3.5.1 **Late Payment Charges.** Any undisputed amount not received by Phoenix within the Net 60 period shall accrue interest at the lesser of (a) **one and one-quarter percent (1.25%) per month**, or (b) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, from the due date until paid, superseding **Section 3.5.1** of the Parent Agreement solely for amounts due under this Amendment.

3.5.2 **Suspension Rights.** Without limiting any rights under **Article IV (Term and Termination)** or **Article VIII (Indemnification)**, if Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount due under this Amendment within thirty (30) days after written notice of non-payment (in addition to the Net 60 period), Phoenix may, upon written notice, suspend non-critical portions of the Amended Services, provided that such suspension shall not relieve Contoso of its payment obligations hereunder.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**

3.6.1 **Pass-Through Costs.** Phoenix may pass through to Contoso, without markup, documented third-party costs reasonably incurred in connection with requested additional services or scope changes under this Amendment, consistent with **Section 3.6.2** of the Parent Agreement.

3.6.2 **Reimbursement Terms.** Contoso shall reimburse Phoenix for reimbursable expenses within sixty (60) days of receipt of an invoice containing reasonable supporting documentation, subject to the dispute process in **Section 3.4.3** of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDMENT)

4.1 **Amendment Term; Expiration.**

4.1.1 **Amendment Term.** The term of this Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date (**December 21, 2025**) and shall continue in full force and effect until **August 18, 2027** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**), unless earlier terminated pursuant to this **Article IV** or the termination provisions of the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 **Relationship to Lease Term.** This Amendment does not, by itself, alter or reduce the **Lease Term** or **Expiration Date (July 21, 2028)** specified in **Sections 1.13 and 1.20** and **Section 4.1.1** of the Parent Agreement, except that the Parties acknowledge that certain fee structures and enhanced services under this Amendment will terminate or adjust as of the Amendment Expiration Date.

4.2 **Renewal; Extension.**

4.2.1 **Negotiated Renewal.** Any extension or renewal of this Amendment beyond the Amendment Expiration Date shall be subject to mutual written agreement of the Parties, which may be documented as a subsequent amendment referencing both **LSE-PHO-202506-622 (contract_622)** and **AMD-PHO-202506-719**.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience under this Amendment.**

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** In addition to Contoso’s termination rights under **Section 4.2.1** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to Phoenix, provided that:

(a) Contoso is not then in uncured material default under the Parent Agreement or this Amendment; and  
(b) Contoso pays all amounts accrued and unpaid under this Amendment through the effective date of termination, plus any agreed early termination fees related to unamortized Additional Capital Improvements (if expressly stated in the Amended Rent Schedule).

4.3.2 **By Phoenix.** In addition to Phoenix’s rights under **Section 4.2.2** and **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, Phoenix may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, provided Phoenix cooperates to minimize disruption and, where reasonably practicable, offers to revert to the original service scope under the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

4.4.1 **By Phoenix.** Phoenix may terminate this Amendment upon written notice if:

(a) Contoso fails to pay any material undisputed amount due under this Amendment within thirty (30) days after written notice of non-payment (in addition to the Net 60 period), consistent with **Section 4.3.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement; or  
(b) Contoso materially breaches any obligation under this Amendment and fails to cure within thirty (30) days after written notice (or such longer period as reasonably necessary to cure, if diligently pursued), consistent with **Section 4.3.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 **By Contoso.** Contoso may terminate this Amendment if Phoenix materially fails to perform the Amended Services or Additional Capital Improvements in accordance with this Amendment or Applicable Law and fails to cure such breach within the cure period specified in **Section 4.3.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement, as applied to this Amendment.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

4.5.1 **Automatic Termination.** If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall **automatically terminate** as of the same effective date of termination or expiration, without the need for additional notice, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue certain obligations independently.

4.5.2 **Survival of Payment and Risk Provisions.** Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment (whether or not the Parent Agreement also terminates), Contoso shall remain liable for (a) all unpaid amounts accrued prior to termination; and (b) any obligations that by their nature survive, including but not limited to **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI** of this Amendment.

4.6 **Surrender of Premises and Reversion of Services.**  

Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment, but not the Parent Agreement, the enhanced services and fee structure under this Amendment shall cease, and the scope of services and fees shall revert to those set forth in the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise agreed in writing.
