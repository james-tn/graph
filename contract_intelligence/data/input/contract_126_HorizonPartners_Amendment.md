# AMENDMENT TO CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT  
**Contract Reference No.: AMD-HOR-202401-126**

---

## PREAMBLE

This AMENDMENT TO CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT (this “**Amendment**”), Contract Reference No. **AMD-HOR-202401-126**, is made and entered into as of **August 31, 2024** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**”), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation, with its principal place of business at **1000 Innovation Way, Wilmington, Delaware 19801** (hereinafter, “**Contoso**,” the “**Client**,” or the “**Company**”); and  

- **Horizon Partners LLC**, a Delaware limited liability company, with its principal place of business at **200 Skyline Avenue, Suite 500, Newark, Delaware 19711** (hereinafter, “**Horizon**,” the “**Vendor**,” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Horizon are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Amendment relates to, **amends**, and **supplements** that certain **Consulting Services Agreement**, Reference No. **CSA-HOR-202401-121** (internal identifier: **contract_121**) dated **January 19, 2024** (the “**Consulting Agreement**” or the “**Parent Agreement**”), by and between Contoso and Horizon.

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Consulting Services Agreement, Reference No. **CSA-HOR-202401-121**, dated January 19, 2024 (file identifier: **contract_121**), pursuant to which Horizon agreed to provide certain professional consulting services to Contoso, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and related provisions of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement currently contemplates an estimated total contract value of **US $703,673** as set forth in **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)**, and a Term commencing on the Effective Date and expiring on **June 2, 2025**, as set forth in **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to expand and extend the scope of services, deliverables, and duration of their engagement, and to significantly increase the overall contract value to reflect additional projects, workstreams, and ongoing advisory services, under an enhanced and higher-risk profile engagement;

WHEREAS, in connection with the foregoing, the Parties wish to modify and/or supplement certain terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to provisions relating to scope of services, fees, payment terms, term and termination, intellectual property, limitation of liability, and risk allocation, all as more fully set forth in this Amendment;

WHEREAS, **Section 16.2 (Entire Agreement; Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement expressly provides that any amendment or modification to the Parent Agreement shall be in writing and signed by duly authorized representatives of both Parties;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and agreements contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties, intending to be legally bound, hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Amendment changes terms of the Consulting Agreement dated January 19, 2024 (Ref: **CSA-HOR-202401-121**, contract_121). The Services described herein are **Extended Services** and are provided **under and subject to** the Parent Agreement, as expressly modified by this Amendment. **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement remains in force, except as supplemented or expanded by this Article II.

2.2 **Overall Objective and Program Context.**  
The Parties agree that, during the Amended Term, Horizon shall support Contoso in executing a multi-year **Program** focused on:

- Scaling Contoso’s enterprise technology solutions business;  
- Implementing the strategic and operational recommendations previously identified during the Diagnostic, Assessment, and Strategy phases described in **Section 2.3(a)–(c)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- Modernizing Contoso’s technology platforms, operating model, and processes; and  
- Embedding sustainable change through targeted organizational change management and capability-building initiatives.

The Extended Services are intended to build upon and operationalize the Current State Assessment Report, Strategic Transformation Roadmap, and related Deliverables contemplated in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Program Workstreams and Deliverables.**  
Without limiting any future Statement of Work (“SOW”) executed under the Parent Agreement as amended, the Extended Services shall encompass the following **Program Workstreams**, each of which will include detailed Deliverables and milestones to be specified in one or more SOWs:

2.3.1 **Workstream A: Enterprise Strategy Refresh (Months 1–6 from Amendment Effective Date).**

(a) Activities:  
- Re-validate and update the strategic assumptions and Prioritized Opportunity Matrix delivered under the Parent Agreement (**Section 2.3(a)**);  
- Integrate market, competitive, and regulatory changes since January 19, 2024;  
- Conduct executive workshops to align on a revised 3–5 year enterprise strategy.

(b) Key Deliverables:  
- **Updated Current State Assessment Addendum**, integrating post‑January 2024 developments;  
- **Revised Strategic Transformation Roadmap v2.0**, superseding and expanding the original roadmap described in **Section 2.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- **Enterprise Strategy and Investment Thesis Deck** for board-level approval.

2.3.2 **Workstream B: Digital Platform Modernization (Months 3–24).**

(a) Activities:  
- Detailed architecture design and implementation support for modernization of core customer‑facing and back‑office platforms;  
- Vendor selection advisory for key technology components;  
- Support for data migration, integration, and testing initiatives.

(b) Key Deliverables (Milestone Deliverables):  
- **Target Architecture Blueprint** and integration patterns;  
- **Platform Modernization Implementation Plan** with phased releases;  
- **Quarterly Technology Health Reports** including KPIs, risk indicators, and remediation actions.

2.3.3 **Workstream C: Operating Model & Process Redesign (Months 4–24).**

(a) Activities:  
- Design and implementation support for a revised operating model for sales, delivery, and support facing Contoso’s enterprise technology customers;  
- Process mapping and re-engineering of high‑value processes;  
- Design of governance frameworks, RACI matrices, and performance scorecards.

(b) Key Deliverables:  
- **Operating Model Design Package**;  
- **End‑to‑End Process Maps and SOPs** for prioritized processes;  
- **Performance Management Framework** including KPIs, dashboards, and reporting templates.

2.3.4 **Workstream D: Risk, Compliance & Data Protection Alignment (Months 2–18).**

(a) Activities:  
- Align Program activities with Data Protection Laws defined in **Section 1.8** and Article XI of the Parent Agreement;  
- Conduct cybersecurity, privacy, and regulatory compliance assessments;  
- Define and support implementation of mitigation plans and controls.

(b) Key Deliverables:  
- **Risk & Compliance Assessment Report**;  
- **Data Protection and Privacy Controls Playbook**, ensuring alignment with GDPR and CCPA obligations described in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment;  
- **Remediation Plan** with prioritized actions and owners.

2.3.5 **Workstream E: Change Management Office (Throughout Amended Term).**

(a) Activities:  
- Establish a Change Management Office (“CMO”) to coordinate stakeholder communications, training, and adoption support described in **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement;  
- Develop and manage a unified change management calendar;  
- Deliver leadership and manager training modules.

(b) Key Deliverables:  
- **Change Management Office Charter**;  
- **Integrated Change Management Plan v2.0**, superseding the prior Change Management Plan under **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement to the extent expressly identified in a SOW;  
- **Stakeholder Engagement and Adoption Scorecards**, updated at least quarterly.

2.4 **Program Governance and Performance Standards.**

2.4.1 **Program Governance Committee.**  
The Parties shall establish a **Program Governance Committee** comprising at least (a) Contoso’s executive sponsor, CIO (or equivalent), and Program Director; and (b) Horizon’s Managing Partner (or delegate), Engagement Partner, and Program Manager. The Committee shall:

- Meet at least monthly to review progress against the Strategic Transformation Roadmap v2.0;  
- Approve or recommend material scope changes to be documented via Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment;  
- Serve as a senior escalation body consistent with **Article XIV (Dispute Resolution)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.2 **Performance Standards.**  
The performance standards in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement (professional and workmanlike performance, industry standards, compliance with laws, and use of appropriately skilled personnel) are hereby reaffirmed and shall apply fully to the Extended Services. In addition, for the Extended Services:

(a) Horizon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet all Milestone Deliverable dates documented in applicable SOWs.  

(b) For Milestone Deliverables identified as “critical” in an SOW, time shall continue to be of the essence (consistent with **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement), provided Contoso has fulfilled its dependencies and responsibilities in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement and any SOW.  

(c) Acceptance of Deliverables shall follow the acceptance mechanisms in **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement, unless an SOW expressly specifies a different acceptance procedure.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities.**  
Contoso’s responsibilities under **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement (provision of Client Materials, data, systems access, timely decisions, and internal policy communication) are reaffirmed and extended to all Extended Services. The Parties acknowledge that failure by Contoso to meet these responsibilities may result in delays or increased costs addressed through **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

2.6 **Subcontracting.**  
Horizon’s right to subcontract Services, as set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement, shall apply equally to the Extended Services, subject to the same confidentiality and data protection obligations and Client’s rights to notice or consent as may be set forth in applicable SOWs.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amended Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 **Supersession of Section 3.1.**  
The Parties hereby agree that **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the Parent Agreement is **amended and restated in its entirety** as follows:

> “3.1 **Contract Value.**  
> The Parties acknowledge that the estimated total contract value for the engagement under this Agreement, inclusive of all anticipated Statements of Work and Fees (as amended by Amendment Reference No. AMD-HOR-202401-126), is **Four Million Five Hundred Ninety-Three Thousand Eight Hundred Thirty-Nine U.S. Dollars (US $4,593,839)** (the “**Contract Value**”). The Contract Value is an estimate only and not a guaranteed minimum; actual Fees payable shall be as set forth in executed Statements of Work and approved Change Orders.”

3.1.2 For clarity, the prior estimated contract value of **US $703,673** in the Parent Agreement is replaced by the **Amended Contract Value** set forth above and is no longer operative.

3.2 **Fee Structure and Schedules.**  
The fee structure in **Section 3.2 (Fee Structure)** of the Parent Agreement remains generally applicable. For the Extended Services, the Parties anticipate a mix of fixed-fee and time-and-materials components, including, by way of example and subject to applicable SOWs:

(a) **Program Strategy & Governance (Workstream A and governance portions of E):**  
- Estimated fixed fee: **US $750,000**  
- Illustrative milestone schedule (subject to SOW):  
  - 30% upon SOW execution;  
  - 40% upon delivery of Revised Strategic Transformation Roadmap v2.0;  
  - 30% upon acceptance of Enterprise Strategy and Investment Thesis Deck.

(b) **Digital Platform Modernization (Workstream B):**  
- Core advisory and design services: Time-and-materials (T&M) basis, with blended hourly or daily rates to be specified in SOWs (e.g., Partner, Senior Consultant, Consultant, Specialist).  
- Estimated T&M budget: **US $2,250,000** over 24 months, invoiced monthly in arrears.  

(c) **Operating Model & Process Redesign (Workstream C):**  
- Combination of fixed-fee phases (design) and T&M phases (implementation).  
- Estimated fees: **US $900,000**.

(d) **Risk, Compliance & Data Protection Alignment (Workstream D):**  
- Primarily T&M, with specific fixed-fee assessments to be agreed in SOWs.  
- Estimated fees: **US $393,839**.

(e) **Change Management Office (Workstream E):**  
- T&M for ongoing change management support and capability building, estimated at **US $300,000** over the Amended Term.

3.3 **Payment Terms and Invoicing.**

3.3.1 **Net 45 Payment Term Reaffirmed.**  
The Parties reaffirm and restate that all properly issued and undisputed invoices shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) days** from the date of Contoso’s receipt thereof (“**Net 45**”), as set forth in **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement. This Net 45 term applies to all Fees and reimbursable expenses under the Extended Services.

3.3.2 **Invoicing.**  
Horizon shall invoice Contoso:

(a) Monthly in arrears for T&M services and reimbursable expenses, consistent with **Section 3.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement; and  

(b) Pursuant to milestone schedules set forth in applicable SOWs for fixed-fee components.  

Invoices shall clearly identify the relevant SOW, Workstream, and Milestone Deliverables.

3.4 **Disputed Amounts.**  
The dispute mechanisms in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all invoices under this Amendment. Contoso shall notify Horizon of any disputed amounts within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt, specifying the basis of the dispute, and shall timely pay all undisputed portions.

3.5 **Late Payment.**  
The late payment provisions in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement, including interest accrual at up to one percent (1%) per month (or the maximum permitted by law) and Horizon’s right to suspend Services after thirty (30) days’ prior written notice, are reaffirmed and apply to Fees under this Amendment.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
The expenses provisions in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement (pre-approval, itemized expense reports, no markup, commercially reasonable efforts to minimize costs) shall apply to the Extended Services. Contoso shall reimburse all reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Horizon in performing the Extended Services, including travel and lodging for on-site Program activities.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
**Section 3.8 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement is reaffirmed. All Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, and Contoso shall be responsible for applicable Taxes other than those based on Horizon’s net income, property, or payroll.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amended Term.**  

4.1.1 **Amendment to Section 4.1.**  
The Parties agree that **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby **amended and restated** solely with respect to the Expiration Date as follows:

> “4.1 **Term.**  
> This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date (January 19, 2024) and, unless earlier terminated pursuant to this **Article IV**, shall continue in effect until **October 8, 2026** (the “**Expiration Date**”) (the “**Term**”).”

4.1.2 For clarity, the Parties acknowledge that the original Expiration Date of June 2, 2025 is superseded by the new Expiration Date of **October 8, 2026**, resulting in an **Amended Term**.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience – Extended Notice.**  

4.2.1 **Amendment to Section 4.3 (Client).**  
Notwithstanding the Client-friendly provision in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement (30-day notice for Client termination), the Parties agree that, solely with respect to the Extended Services and any SOWs executed after the Amendment Effective Date:

(a) Contoso may terminate the Agreement, any SOW, or any portion of the Extended Services for convenience **only upon one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to Horizon.  

(b) During the 180-day notice period, Contoso shall continue to pay all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred in accordance with the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

4.2.2 **Vendor Termination for Convenience.**  
Horizon’s right to terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days’ prior written notice, as set forth in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement, remains in effect; provided, however, that Horizon shall continue to use reasonable efforts to avoid termination during periods when critical milestones are due under an active SOW, except where continuing performance would result in material unmitigable risk or loss to Horizon.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause rights in **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement remain unchanged and continue to apply to the Extended Services. Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of notice, or upon insolvency events described therein.

4.4 **Effect of Termination.**  
The consequences of termination in **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement are reaffirmed and shall apply to the Extended Services, including:

- Horizon’s obligation to cease performance (subject to any agreed transition assistance);  
- Contoso’s obligation to pay for Services performed and approved expenses incurred through the effective date of termination; and  
- Horizon’s obligation, upon Contoso’s request, to provide transition services for up to sixty (60) days (or longer if agreed) at the rates specified in the applicable SOW.

4.5 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Termination.**  
If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires in accordance with **Article IV** thereof, then, unless expressly agreed otherwise in writing:

(a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement;  

(b) Any Extended Services shall cease, subject to any transition assistance obligations; and  

(c) The rights and obligations of the Parties with respect to accrued Fees, IP ownership, confidentiality, data protection, and other provisions intended to survive shall be as set forth in **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement and the survival provisions of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IX. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Supersession of Article IX of Parent Agreement.**  
In light of the Parties’ express agreement to a **High‑Risk Profile** engagement, the Parties hereby agree that **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement is **amended and restated in its entirety** as follows, solely with respect to the relationship between Contoso and Horizon under the Parent Agreement as amended by this Amendment:

9.2 **No Cap on Liability.**  
There shall be **no contractual cap on liability** for either Party’s aggregate liability to the other Party arising out of or relating to the Parent Agreement as amended by this Amendment. Any caps previously set forth in the Parent Agreement, including in **Section 9.2**, are hereby removed and are of no further force or effect.

9.3 **Inclusion of Consequential and Similar Damages.**  
To the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, the Parties agree that neither Party shall be entitled to exclude the other Party’s liability for indirect, incidental, consequential, special, exemplary, or punitive damages, or for loss of profits, revenue, goodwill, or business opportunity. Any exclusions previously set forth in **Section 9.1** of the Parent Agreement are hereby removed, and each Party may seek recovery of such damages to the extent awarded by a court or tribunal of competent jurisdiction or agreed in settlement.

9.4 **Carve-Outs Unaffected.**  
Nothing in this Article IX shall limit or restrict any liability that cannot be limited under applicable law, including but not limited to liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence, fraud, or fraudulent misrepresentation.

9.5 **Allocation of Risk.**  
The Parties expressly acknowledge that this uncapped liability framework and the inclusion of consequential and similar damages are an integral part of the bargain reflected in the Amended Contract Value and other risk allocations herein.
