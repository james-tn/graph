## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Amendment modifies quantities or pricing under the Parent Agreement dated February 1, 2024 (Ref: **PUR-ZEN-202402-231**, internal identifier **contract_231**). It is entered into pursuant to and in strict conformity with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** and the change management framework in **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 Except as expressly set forth in this Amendment, all provisions of the Parent Agreement (including **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** and applicable SOWs) remain unchanged and in full force and effect and shall apply to the Amended Equipment, Amended Services, and Deliverables described herein.

2.1.3 The Parties acknowledge that this Amendment constitutes a “Change Order” within the meaning of **Section 1.3 (“Change Order”)** and **Section 13.2 (Change Orders)** of the Parent Agreement, documenting agreed changes to scope, fees, and, where applicable, timelines.

### 2.2 Overview of Amended Scope

2.2.1 Under **Section 2.1.2** of the Parent Agreement, the initial scope of work includes, among other things:

- design and supply of enterprise-grade network routers, switches, and secure gateway devices;  
- installation, configuration, and integration of such Equipment;  
- deployment and configuration of Zenith’s proprietary network management software; and  
- ongoing maintenance, monitoring, and support services.

2.2.2 Under this Amendment, the Parties agree to:

(a) increase the quantities of certain network routers, switches, and secure gateway devices originally contemplated under the Parent Agreement;  
(b) add incremental managed support capacity and extended-hours coverage for certain critical locations; and  
(c) adjust related pricing and payment terms as described in **Article III**.

2.2.3 The Amended Services are intended to support Contoso’s expanded data center footprint and additional regional offices, while maintaining the same or better Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.3 Amended Equipment Quantities

2.3.1 The Parties agree that, as of the Amendment Effective Date, the following incremental quantities of Equipment shall be added to the applicable purchase schedules or SOW(s) under the Parent Agreement (exact part numbers and technical specifications to be detailed in an Amendment SOW):

- **Core Network Routers**: Additional quantity: **15** units (incremental), to be deployed across two primary data centers and three regional hubs.  
- **Aggregation Switches**: Additional quantity: **40** units (incremental), including 10 high-density switches for data center use and 30 mid-range switches for branch offices.  
- **Secure Gateway Devices (Next-Generation Firewalls)**: Additional quantity: **25** units (incremental), including high-availability pairs for two core locations.  
- **Out-of-Band Management Devices**: Additional quantity: **10** units (incremental), for remote management and resiliency.

2.3.2 The Parties acknowledge that the above quantities supplement, and do not replace or reduce, any quantities previously ordered under the Parent Agreement, except to the extent expressly stated otherwise in a specific Amendment SOW.

2.3.3 All Amended Equipment shall be deemed “Equipment” under **Section 1.8** and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement and shall be subject to the same warranties set forth in **Section 7.2 (Zenith Warranties)** of the Parent Agreement, including the ninety (90) day Warranty Period defined therein, unless otherwise expressly extended or modified in an Amendment SOW.

### 2.4 Amended Services

2.4.1 In connection with the Amended Equipment, Zenith shall provide the following incremental Services:

(a) **Design and Planning Services**: Additional design sessions to adjust network topology, capacity planning, and security zoning to incorporate the Amended Equipment into Contoso’s existing infrastructure.  

(b) **Installation and Configuration Services**: On-site and remote installation, configuration, and integration of the Amended Equipment within Contoso’s data centers and regional locations, including updating configuration templates and applying Contoso-specific security baselines.  

(c) **Cutover and Migration Support**: Extended cutover and migration support to minimize downtime during deployment of the Amended Equipment, including coordination with Contoso’s change management process and rollback planning.  

(d) **Managed Support and Monitoring Services**: Incremental expansion of existing managed support services, including 24x7 monitoring of the additional devices, enhanced incident response capacity, and coverage of the Amended Equipment under existing Service Levels described in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and any applicable SOW.  

(e) **Knowledge Transfer and Documentation**: Updated configuration runbooks, network diagrams, and operational playbooks reflecting the Amended Equipment, along with knowledge transfer sessions for Contoso’s technical personnel.

2.4.2 The Amended Services will be performed in accordance with **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, including obligations to:

- perform all Services in a professional and workmanlike manner;  
- follow generally accepted industry standards and best practices; and  
- allocate sufficient resources to meet agreed timelines and Service Levels.

2.4.3 The Parties acknowledge that certain dependencies may rely on Contoso’s timely performance of its obligations, including providing site access, change approvals, and test windows, consistent with **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.5 Deliverables, Milestones, and Acceptance

2.5.1 Zenith shall produce and deliver, as part of the Amended Services, the following additional Deliverables (which supplement those described in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement):

(a) Updated **infrastructure design documents** reflecting the incremental capacity;  
(b) Revised **configuration runbooks** covering the Amended Equipment;  
(c) **Integration test plans and results** demonstrating successful deployment;  
(d) **Security hardening guidelines** tailored to the extended footprint; and  
(e) **Operational playbooks** for incident response and maintenance of the new devices.

2.5.2 Detailed milestones and delivery timelines for the Amended Equipment and Amended Services shall be specified in the relevant Amendment SOW(s). By way of example, an indicative schedule may include:

- Design completion: within sixty (60) days of the Amendment Effective Date;  
- Equipment delivery to primary sites: within ninety (90) days of the Amendment Effective Date;  
- Completion of installation and initial acceptance: within one hundred fifty (150) days of the Amendment Effective Date.

2.5.3 The acceptance procedures for Deliverables and Amended Services shall follow the process set forth in **Sections 2.3.2–2.3.4** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise stated in an Amendment SOW, Contoso shall have thirty (30) days after delivery to accept or reject any Deliverable in writing.

### 2.6 Service Levels and Managed Services

2.6.1 To the extent any Amended Services constitute “Managed Services” under **Section 12.1** of the Parent Agreement, the uptime commitment of **99.9%** monthly uptime percentage shall apply to the expanded managed environment, inclusive of the Amended Equipment, subject to the exclusions and definitions described in the applicable SOW.

2.6.2 Response and resolution times for incidents involving the Amended Equipment shall follow the Service Levels described in **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement (Severity 1–4 categories), unless explicitly varied in an Amendment SOW.

2.6.3 Service credits (if any) arising from Service Level failures related to the Amended Services shall be calculated in accordance with **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement and any relevant SOW, based on the monthly Fees associated with the affected Amended Services.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Amendment Contract Value and Pricing

3.1.1 The Parties agree that the **Amendment Contract Value** associated with the Amended Equipment and Amended Services under this Amendment is **US$616,225** (exclusive of Taxes as defined in **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement), broken down approximately as follows:

- Amended Equipment (hardware only): US$472,000  
- Implementation and professional services: US$102,725  
- Incremental support and maintenance fees through September 4, 2027: US$41,500

3.1.2 For clarity, as of the Amendment Effective Date, the **Revised Contract Value** under the Parent Agreement, as modified by this Amendment, is **US$3,910,058**, subject to further change by future SOWs or amendments.

3.1.3 Unless otherwise specified in this Amendment or an Amendment SOW, all unit prices, discounts, and rate cards applicable to the Amended Equipment and Amended Services shall be consistent with the pricing principles and structures used in the Parent Agreement and related SOWs.

### 3.2 Payment Terms

3.2.1 Notwithstanding **Section 3.2.2 (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that the payment terms applicable to all Fees invoiced under this Amendment shall be **net forty-five (45) days** from the date of Contoso’s receipt of a correct and undisputed invoice (“**Net 45**”), reflecting the low risk profile of this engagement and Contoso’s internal financial controls.

3.2.2 For Amended Equipment, Zenith shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the following schedule, which modifies the general schedule in **Section 3.2.1 (Equipment)** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to the Amended Equipment:

(a) **Forty percent (40%)** of the Amended Equipment fees upon execution of this Amendment;  
(b) **Fifty percent (50%)** of the Amended Equipment fees upon delivery of the Amended Equipment to Contoso’s designated sites; and  
(c) **Ten percent (10%)** of the Amended Equipment fees upon successful completion of installation and initial acceptance of such Amended Equipment in accordance with the acceptance criteria defined in the relevant Amendment SOW.

3.2.3 For the implementation and professional services comprising the Amended Services, Zenith shall invoice Contoso based on the achievement of milestones consistent with **Section 3.2.1 (Implementation and Professional Services)** of the Parent Agreement, for example:

- **30%** upon project kickoff and completion of detailed design;  
- **40%** upon completion of configuration, integration testing, and site readiness; and  
- **30%** upon final acceptance of the Amended Services and related Deliverables.

3.2.4 For incremental support and maintenance services, Zenith shall invoice Contoso **annually in advance** for the applicable period of coverage, unless the Parties mutually agree to a different invoicing cadence in writing.

### 3.3 Taxes, Expenses, and Late Payments

3.3.1 Taxes applicable to the Fees under this Amendment shall be governed by **Section 3.3 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. All Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be itemized on Zenith’s invoices and borne by Contoso as provided in the Parent Agreement.

3.3.2 Reimbursement of expenses shall follow the rules set forth in **Section 3.5 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement. Any travel or out-of-pocket expenses incurred specifically in connection with the Amended Services shall require Contoso’s prior approval and shall comply with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies.

3.3.3 Any undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Amendment shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.4 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement, from the applicable due date until the date of payment.

3.3.4 Invoice disputes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall notify Zenith of any disputed amount within fifteen (15) days of receipt, pay undisputed amounts in accordance with the Net 45 terms, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any disputes.

### 4.1 Amendment Term; Relation to Parent Agreement Term

4.1.1 This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall continue in effect until **September 4, 2027** (the “**Amendment Expiration Date**”).

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Contoso’s right to terminate the Parent Agreement, in whole or in part, for convenience upon thirty (30) days’ prior written notice as set forth in **Section 4.2.1** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Amendment and to any Amended Equipment or Amended Services identified herein or in any Amendment SOW. Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, without penalty, on thirty (30) days’ prior written notice.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause in accordance with **Section 4.3.1 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement, in the event of a material breach of this Amendment by the other Party that is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice.

4.3.2 The insolvency-related termination rights in **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination

4.4.1 Upon the expiration or termination of this Amendment for any reason:

(a) Contoso shall pay Zenith for all Amended Equipment delivered and accepted, and for all Amended Services performed and Deliverables accepted, up to the effective date of termination, in accordance with **Section 4.4.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Zenith shall promptly refund any prepaid but unearned Fees associated solely with the Amended Services, as required by **Section 4.4.1(b)** of the Parent Agreement; and  
(c) Each Party shall return or, at the disclosing Party’s written request, destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information relating to this Amendment, consistent with **Section 4.4.1(c)** and **Section 5.4 (Compelled Disclosure; Return/Destruction)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 In the event of termination by Contoso for cause pursuant to **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, as applied to this Amendment, Contoso shall have no obligation to pay for any Amended Services or Deliverables that are non-conforming or not yet accepted.

4.4.3 Transition assistance obligations set forth in **Section 4.4.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Amended Services, and Zenith shall provide reasonable assistance, at its then-current time and materials rates, to transition responsibility for any Amended Services to Contoso or a third party.

### 4.5 Survival

4.5.1 Provisions of this Amendment which by their nature are intended to survive expiration or termination (including, without limitation, **Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI**, and this **Section 4.5**) shall survive any expiration or termination of this Amendment, together with the corresponding survival provisions of the Parent Agreement.
