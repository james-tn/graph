## ARTICLE III  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

3.1 **General Description of Additional Services.**  
Pursuant to **Section 2.1 (General Description of Services)** and **Section 2.2 (Specific Data Processing Activities)** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall provide the following **Additional Services** to Contoso under this Addendum:

  3.1.1 **Advanced Analytics Platform Enablement.**  
  Pinnacle shall design, configure, and operate an advanced analytics environment that ingests and Processes Client Data, including Incremental Personal Data Categories, to enable customer segmentation, behavioral analysis, and predictive modeling. This includes:

  - Integration with Contoso’s existing data sources and APIs referenced under **Section 2.2.1** of the DPA;  
  - Implementation of ETL (extract-transform-load) pipelines;  
  - Provision of multi-tenant dashboards for authorized Contoso users; and  
  - Deployment of pre-configured analytical models tailored to Contoso’s business use cases.

  3.1.2 **Data Enrichment and Profiling Services.**  
  Pinnacle shall Process Personal Data to perform enrichment and profiling operations, in accordance with Contoso’s documented Instructions under **Section 11.4 (Instructions)** of the DPA, including:

  - Creation and maintenance of customer preference profiles using clickstream data and historical transaction data;  
  - Pseudonymization of direct identifiers where feasible, consistent with **Section 11.3.1 (Pseudonymization)** of the DPA;  
  - Aggregation and anonymization of Personal Data for high-level trend reporting, as contemplated by **Section 2.2.3** of the DPA.

  3.1.3 **Multi-Region Hosting and Geo-Redundant Storage.**  
  In addition to the hosting and storage services described in **Section 2.1(a)** and **Section 2.2.2** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall:

  - Provision geographically redundant hosting environments in at least two distinct data center regions (e.g., US-East and US-West) within approved Territories;  
  - Configure continuous data replication with Recovery Point Objectives (RPO) and Recovery Time Objectives (RTO) aligned with the Enhanced SLA in **Article XII** of this Addendum;  
  - Maintain geo-redundant backup storage with extended retention periods consistent with **Section 2.2.7** and **Section 11.11 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the DPA.

  3.1.4 **Event-Based Notification and Monitoring.**  
  Supplementing **Section 2.2.6** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall implement advanced monitoring and event-based notification systems to:

  - Detect anomalies in data processing operations;  
  - Alert Contoso to potential security-related events (without prejudice to the obligations in **Section 11.6 (Data Breach Notification)** of the DPA);  
  - Provide Contoso with access to real-time or near real-time operational dashboards indicating system health, performance metrics, and processing queues.

3.2 **Additional Deliverables.**  
The following **Additional Deliverables** will be produced and delivered by Pinnacle during the Addendum Term:

  3.2.1 **Advanced Analytics Dashboards.**  
  A suite of configurable dashboards providing analytics on customer behavior, segmentation, and conversion metrics, accessible via secure portals as contemplated in **Section 2.2.4** of the DPA. Dashboards shall include at minimum:

  - Overview dashboard (traffic, conversions, engagement);  
  - Segmentation dashboard (customer cohorts, lifecycle stages);  
  - Attribution dashboard (sources, campaigns, channels).

  3.2.2 **Data Models and Configuration Artifacts.**  
  Structured data models, schema definitions, model feature sets, and integration specifications (as further to **Section 2.3.2** of the DPA) necessary for Contoso to understand and utilize the analytical outputs.

  3.2.3 **Periodic Analytics Reports.**  
  In addition to “Periodic Reports” under **Section 2.3.3** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall deliver:

  - Monthly analytics summary reports;  
  - Quarterly trend and performance review reports;  
  - Annual benchmarking report for Contoso’s key KPIs.

3.3 **Incremental Personal Data Categories and Data Subjects.**  
Without limiting the definition of Personal Data in **Section 1.11** of the DPA, the Additional Processing Activities may involve the following incremental categories:

  3.3.1 **Behavioral Data:** web and application clickstream logs, page dwell times, navigation paths, and interaction events associated with pseudonymous identifiers.  

  3.3.2 **Device and Technical Data:** device identifiers, browser types, operating system versions, IP address ranges, and related telemetry, processed as necessary for service delivery, security monitoring, and analytics.  

  3.3.3 **Preference and Profile Data:** user preferences, opt-in/opt-out flags, communication channel preferences, and inferred interests derived from behavior, stored and processed consistent with Contoso’s Instructions and Data Protection Laws.

3.4 **Performance Standards.**  
In addition to **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall perform the Additional Services:

  3.4.1 Using commercially reasonable efforts to maintain data pipeline latency such that 95% of data records are available for analytical use within **two (2) hours** of initial ingestion, under normal operating conditions.  

  3.4.2 Ensuring that scheduled enrichment jobs complete within the windows agreed in writing between the Parties, subject to Usage Metrics and volume constraints.  

  3.4.3 Providing periodic service reviews at least twice per calendar year to review performance, SLA compliance, security posture, and potential optimizations, in alignment with the collaborative obligations set forth in **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the DPA.

3.5 **Timelines and Milestones for Additional Services.**  
Building on **Section 2.4 (Timelines and Milestones)** of the DPA:

  3.5.1 **Additional Setup and Onboarding.**  
  Pinnacle shall commence implementation of the Additional Services within **twenty (20) Business Days** following the Addendum Effective Date, subject to Contoso providing necessary inputs and access.  

  3.5.2 **Go-Live for Additional Services.**  
  The Parties shall target a go-live date for the advanced analytics platform no later than **ninety (90) days** following the Addendum Effective Date, recognizing that this date may be adjusted by mutual written agreement pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of this Addendum.  

  3.5.3 **Ongoing Enhancement Cycle.**  
  Pinnacle shall, during the Addendum Term, implement reasonably necessary updates and enhancements to the Additional Services to maintain compatibility, security, and performance, consistent with **Section 2.4.3** of the DPA and subject to Change Requests under **Article XIII**.

3.6 **Client Responsibilities for Additional Services.**  
In addition to the obligations set forth in **Section 2.6** of the DPA, Contoso shall:

  3.6.1 Provide accurate and up-to-date data source connection details, API credentials, and schema definitions required for integration;  

  3.6.2 Ensure that its Instructions for data enrichment and profiling are consistent with Data Protection Laws, including any requirements to obtain Data Subject consent;  

  3.6.3 Review and validate analytics outputs and segmentation criteria for fitness for its intended business purposes;  

  3.6.4 Notify Pinnacle promptly of any observed anomalies or suspected errors in the analytics environment.

3.7 **Sub-processing for Additional Services.**  
Any Sub-processors engaged by Pinnacle specifically for the Additional Services shall be subject to **Section 11.7 (Sub-processors)** of the DPA. Pinnacle shall, upon Contoso’s request, identify any additional Sub-processors utilized for the analytics and enrichment services and comply with the objection process thereunder.

## ARTICLE IV  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

4.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The Parties acknowledge and agree that:

  4.1.1 The aggregate contract value for the **Additional Services** under this Addendum is **US $2,602,971** (the “Addendum Contract Value”).  

  4.1.2 This Addendum Contract Value is **in addition to** the contract value of **US $2,240,501** specified in **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the DPA, resulting in a combined potential contract value of **US $4,843,472**, subject to adjustments pursuant to **Section 3.5** and **Article XIII** of the DPA and this Addendum.

4.2 **Fee Structure.**  
Unless otherwise agreed in a mutually executed statement of work referencing this Addendum:

  4.2.1 **Implementation Fees.**  
  A one-time implementation fee equal to **ten percent (10%)** of the Addendum Contract Value (**US $260,297.10**) shall be invoiced upon commencement of the Additional setup and onboarding described in **Section 3.5.1**.  

  4.2.2 **Recurring Service Fees.**  
  Recurring fees for the Additional Services (analytics, enrichment, and multi-region hosting) shall be invoiced monthly in advance, based on:

  - Number of active analytics users;  
  - Volume of Personal Data processed (GB per month); and  
  - Number of configured analytics dashboards and enrichment jobs.

  4.2.3 **Usage-Based Fees.**  
  To the extent the Parties agree to usage-based pricing (e.g., per API call, per report generated, or per incremental data model), such fees shall be calculated monthly and invoiced in arrears, consistent with **Section 3.2.3** of the DPA.

4.3 **Payment Terms.**  

  4.3.1 **Net 45 Payment Terms.**  
  Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.2 (Net 30)** of the DPA, Contoso shall pay all undisputed Addendum Fees within **forty-five (45) calendar days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 45**”).  

  4.3.2 **Invoicing.**  
  Pinnacle shall issue itemized invoices that distinguish Addendum Fees from fees under the Parent Agreement. Each invoice shall reference both **DPA-PIN-202411-069** and **ADD-PIN-202411-167** for cross-referencing.

4.4 **Late Payment.**  
The late payment provisions of **Section 3.4** of the DPA shall apply to Addendum Fees, with the following clarifications:

  4.4.1 Pinnacle may assess interest on overdue, undisputed Addendum Fees at the rate defined in **Section 3.4.1** of the DPA, calculated from the Net 45 due date.  

  4.4.2 Before suspending the Additional Services for non-payment, Pinnacle shall provide written notice and a cure period of at least **fifteen (15) Business Days**, as per **Section 3.4.2** of the DPA, and such suspension shall be proportionate and limited to the Additional Services where practicable.

4.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
The reimbursement provisions of **Section 3.5** of the DPA apply to the Additional Services. Pinnacle shall obtain Contoso’s written approval prior to incurring extraordinary expenses (e.g., dedicated networking, third-party data enrichment services requested by Contoso).

4.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Contoso may withhold payment of disputed portions of invoices relating to Addendum Fees in accordance with **Section 3.6** of the DPA, provided that it specifies the Addendum reference number and the basis for the dispute.

4.7 **Taxes.**  
Taxes applicable to the Additional Services shall be handled as described in **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the DPA. Pinnacle shall separately itemize any Taxes applicable specifically to the Addendum Fees.

## ARTICLE V  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

5.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue in full force and effect until **April 21, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article V or **Article IV** of the DPA.

5.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
Notwithstanding the foregoing:

  5.2.1 This Addendum may not extend beyond the Term of the Parent Agreement. If the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated pursuant to **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the DPA, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such expiration or termination, subject to the survival of obligations set forth herein and in the DPA.  

  5.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is renewed pursuant to **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the DPA, the Parties may mutually agree in writing to extend the Addendum Term accordingly.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

  5.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason, by providing Pinnacle with at least **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice, aligned with **Section 4.3** of the DPA.  

  5.3.2 Pinnacle may terminate this Addendum for convenience only by providing at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice and only after the initial twelve (12) months of the Addendum Term, mirroring the restrictions in **Section 4.3** of the DPA.

5.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause under the same conditions set forth in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the DPA, including:

  5.4.1 Material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement, not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice describing the breach;  

  5.4.2 Insolvency or bankruptcy events affecting a Party;  

  5.4.3 Immediate termination rights for Contoso in the event of Pinnacle’s material violation of Data Protection Laws or material breach of **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the DPA or this Addendum, consistent with **Section 4.4.3** of the DPA.

5.5 **Effect of Termination.**  

  5.5.1 Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum, Pinnacle shall promptly cease the Additional Processing Activities, except to the limited extent required to fulfill post-termination obligations or by Applicable Law, consistent with **Section 4.5.1** of the DPA.  

  5.5.2 Within thirty (30) days of termination or expiration of this Addendum, Pinnacle shall, at Contoso’s option, either return or delete/encrypt the Incremental Personal Data and Additional Deliverables in accordance with **Sections 4.5.2 and 11.11** of the DPA.  

  5.5.3 Termination of this Addendum shall not relieve either Party of its obligation to pay Addendum Fees for Additional Services performed prior to the effective date of termination, nor affect any rights or obligations intended to survive termination, as set forth in **Section 16.9 (Survival)** of the DPA and **Article XVI** of this Addendum.

  10.3.1 A Party’s indemnification obligations under **Article IX** of this Addendum and **Article VIII** of the DPA;  

  10.3.2 A Party’s breach of its confidentiality obligations under **Article VI** of this Addendum and **Article V** of the DPA (excluding consequential or incidental components of damages);  

  10.3.3 A Party’s gross negligence or willful misconduct;  

  10.3.4 Pinnacle’s liability for unauthorized or unlawful Processing of Personal Data resulting from Pinnacle’s failure to implement the Enhanced Security Measures required under **Article XI** of this Addendum, but only to the extent such failure is the direct cause of the loss, consistent with **Section 9.3.4** of the DPA.

10.4 **Allocation of Risk.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the limitations of liability in this Article X, together with those in **Article IX** of the DPA, reflect a fair allocation of risk appropriate to the **Medium** risk level of this Addendum and are a fundamental basis for the Parties’ decision to enter into this Addendum.

## ARTICLE XII  
### DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

12.1 **Applicability of DPA.**  
The data protection obligations in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the DPA apply fully to all Additional Processing Activities under this Addendum.

12.2 **Enhanced Security Measures.**  
In addition to the measures in **Section 11.3** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall implement the following Enhanced Security Measures for the Additional Services:

  12.2.1 **Advanced Monitoring and Logging:** fine-grained logging of access to analytics dashboards and data pipelines, with retention periods aligned with audit requirements.  

  12.2.2 **Geo-Redundant Backups:** maintenance of encrypted backups in at least two geographically distinct locations within approved Territories.  

  12.2.3 **Segregation of Duties:** separation of roles for system administrators, data engineers, and security personnel to reduce insider risk.

12.3 **Data Breach Notification.**  
The breach notification obligations in **Section 11.6** of the DPA apply to any Personal Data Breach affecting data Processed under this Addendum. Pinnacle’s initial notification shall, where feasible, identify whether the breach affects Incremental Personal Data Categories.

12.4 **Data Subject Requests and Profiling.**  
Pinnacle shall assist Contoso, upon request, in responding to Data Subject requests concerning profiling and automated decision-making that may result from the analytics and enrichment services, in accordance with **Section 11.8** of the DPA.

12.5 **International Data Transfers and Territory Expansion.**  
Any international data transfers or Territory Expansion associated with the Additional Services shall comply with **Section 11.10** of the DPA and any additional data transfer mechanisms agreed by the Parties (e.g., updated Standard Contractual Clauses).

12.6 **Data Retention and Deletion.**  
Data retention and deletion for Incremental Personal Data Categories shall follow the principles in **Section 11.11** of the DPA, with specific retention periods and anonymization schedules to be documented in writing between the Parties.

12.7 **Audit Rights.**  
Contoso’s audit rights under **Section 11.12** of the DPA extend to the systems and processes used for the Additional Services, subject to the same limitations and conditions.

## ARTICLE XIII  
### SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLA)

13.1 **Enhanced Availability.**  
In addition to the availability commitments in **Section 12.1** of the DPA, Pinnacle shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the analytics dashboards and APIs used for the Additional Services are available **99.9%** of the time each calendar month, excluding Permitted Downtime as defined in **Section 12.1** of the DPA.

13.2 **Response and Resolution Times.**  
The incident response and resolution targets in **Section 12.2** of the DPA shall apply, with Severity definitions expanded to include incidents affecting the analytics environment. Pinnacle shall designate analytics-related incidents as no lower than Severity 2 if they materially impact Contoso’s ability to access analytics data.

13.3 **SLA Credits for Additional Services.**  
If Pinnacle fails to meet the Enhanced Availability for the Additional Services in a given calendar month, Contoso shall be entitled to service credits calculated as a percentage of the monthly Addendum recurring fees for the affected analytics services, using the same credit tiers in **Section 12.3** of the DPA.

13.4 **SLA Reporting.**  
Upon request, Pinnacle shall provide monthly SLA reports specific to the Additional Services, including uptime, incident logs, and performance metrics, in addition to the reporting obligations in **Section 12.4** of the DPA.
