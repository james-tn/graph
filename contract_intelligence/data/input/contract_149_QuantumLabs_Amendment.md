## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Amendment **amends, supplements, and partially supersedes** specific terms of the Parent Agreement **solely** with respect to the Program and any Program SOWs. Except as expressly modified herein, the provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply to the Services and Deliverables provided under the Program.

2.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, Sections **2.1 through 2.8** of the Parent Agreement remain in effect for all services, provided that, solely for the Program:  
- (a) the detailed scope described in this Article II shall be deemed an SOW-level description for purposes of Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement; and  
- (b) the acceptance procedures in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement are **supplemented** by the additional provisions set forth in Section 2.6 of this Amendment.

### 2.2 Program Overview

2.2.1 Quantum Labs shall provide an integrated suite of consulting, analytics, implementation, and managed services to support Contoso’s multi-phase transformation initiatives (the **“Program”**). This Program builds upon the existing strategic advisory, data & analytics, and technology implementation services contemplated in **Section 2.2 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 The Program will focus on the following workstreams, which shall be further detailed in Program SOWs:

- **(a) Strategic Analytics Roadmap Expansion**  
  Enhancement of existing analytics roadmaps to enterprise-wide scale, including multi-domain data integration, advanced AI/ML use cases, and governance frameworks, consistent with the strategic advisory services described in Section 2.2(1) of the Parent Agreement.

- **(b) Enterprise Data Platform & Integration Layer**  
  Design, implementation, and optimization of an enterprise data platform within the Program Environment, including pipelines, data models, performance tuning, and integration with Contoso’s existing tools, consistent with the data & analytics consulting and technology integration services described in Sections 2.2(2) and 2.2(3) of the Parent Agreement.

- **(c) Applied AI & Automation Use Cases**  
  Development of prototypes, pilots, and production-ready models leveraging Quantum Labs’ Vendor Technology and, where agreed, third-party tools, including configuration, testing, and documentation of AI models, with clear delineation between Deliverables and Vendor Technology as defined in **Sections 1.8, 1.18, 6.1, and 6.2** of the Parent Agreement.

- **(d) Managed Services & Support**  
  Provision of ongoing support, monitoring, and improvements of the solutions implemented under the Program, including service desk support, incident management, and performance reporting, aligning with the ongoing support and advisory services referenced in Section 2.2(5) of the Parent Agreement and subject to the Enhanced Service Levels in Article XII of this Amendment.

- **(e) Training, Enablement, and Operational Runbooks**  
  Development and delivery of training materials, workshops, and Operational Runbooks to enable Contoso teams to operate and extend the implemented solutions, consistent with Section 2.2(4) of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.3 Deliverables

2.3.1 Without limiting any more detailed description in a Program SOW, the Deliverables under this Program shall include, at a minimum:

- (a) **Program Architecture Documents** – Detailed solution architecture specifications, integration diagrams, and technology stack documentation for the enterprise data and analytics platform.

- (b) **Data Models and Pipelines** – Logical and physical data models, ETL/ELT workflows, and configuration scripts or templates required to ingest, transform, and expose data as specified in Program SOWs.

- (c) **AI/ML Model Artifacts** – Model design documents, training pipelines, parameter configurations, performance reports, and deployment instructions for production-intended use cases.

- (d) **Dashboards and Analytics Reports** – Configured dashboards, KPIs, and analytic reports tailored to Contoso’s business requirements.

- (e) **Operational Runbooks** – Comprehensive runbooks describing standard operating procedures, monitoring expectations, incident response steps, and escalation paths.

- (f) **Training Materials & Workshops** – Slide decks, user guides, quick reference sheets, recorded sessions, and agendas for training workshops conducted by Quantum Labs.

2.3.2 Each Program SOW shall classify Deliverables as either (i) **Client‑Owned Deliverables** or (ii) Deliverables owned by Quantum Labs but licensed to Contoso under Section 6.3 of the Parent Agreement and Section 6.2 of this Amendment.

### 2.4 Timelines and Milestones

2.4.1 The Program shall be executed in phases, with Milestones expressly defined in each Program SOW. Unless otherwise agreed in a Program SOW, key phases shall include:

- **Phase 1 – Discovery & Design (approx. 3–4 months)**  
  Requirements gathering, current-state assessment, target-state architecture, and prioritized roadmap.

- **Phase 2 – Build & Validate (approx. 6–9 months)**  
  Implementation of data pipelines, platform components, and initial AI use cases, followed by unit and integration testing.

- **Phase 3 – Pilot & Production Rollout (approx. 6–9 months)**  
  Pilot deployments, performance tuning, and controlled production rollout.

- **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Managed Services (through Amendment Expiration Date)**  
  Ongoing optimization, support, and incremental enhancements governed by Enhanced Service Levels.

2.4.2 Milestone completion dates shall be estimates subject to mutual adjustment via Change Request (Program) under Article XIII of this Amendment and Article XIII of the Parent Agreement, particularly where delays are caused by Contoso’s responsibilities under Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement or by Force Majeure Events.

### 2.5 Performance Standards

2.5.1 For the Program, Quantum Labs shall comply with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement and shall additionally:

- (a) Assign a dedicated **Program Delivery Lead** and technical lead with experience commensurate with the scope and complexity of the Program.

- (b) Provide a written **Program Governance Plan**, including steering committee cadence, reporting templates, and escalation paths.

- (c) Provide monthly status reports summarizing progress against Milestones, identified risks, issues, and mitigation actions.

2.5.2 If Quantum Labs fails to perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with Section 7.2(a) of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may invoke the acceptance rights in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement and Section 2.6 of this Amendment.

### 2.6 Deliverable Review and Acceptance (Supplement to Parent Agreement §2.4)

2.6.1 The Deliverable acceptance process in **Section 2.4 (Deliverables; Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Program Deliverables, subject to the following clarifications:

- (a) The **Review Period** for each major Deliverable (as designated in a Program SOW) shall be **fifteen (15) Business Days**, instead of the default ten (10) Business Days in Section 2.4.2(a) of the Parent Agreement. For all other Deliverables, the ten (10) Business Day Review Period shall continue to apply.

- (b) Contoso’s rejection notice shall, where reasonably practicable, categorize non‑conformities by severity (critical, major, minor) to facilitate remediation.

- (c) Quantum Labs shall use commercially reasonable efforts to remedy identified material non‑conformities within a mutually agreed timeframe, generally not exceeding twenty (20) Business Days per remediation cycle, subject to complexity.

2.6.2 If Quantum Labs is unable to remedy material non‑conformities after two (2) remediation cycles, the Parties shall meet and confer in good faith to determine (i) a fee reduction, (ii) a scope modification, or (iii) termination of the affected Deliverable or workstream, without prejudice to Contoso’s rights under Article IV of this Amendment and Article IV of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Amendment Contract Value

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the projected aggregate value of the Program under this Amendment, excluding Taxes and reimbursable expenses, is **Four Million Nine Hundred Fifty-Three Thousand Six Hundred Thirty-Eight U.S. Dollars (US$4,953,638)** (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**).

3.1.2 The Amendment Contract Value is an upper limit for the Program, and Quantum Labs shall not exceed this amount in aggregate Fees for the Program without Contoso’s prior written approval via a Program-level Change Order under Article XIII.

3.1.3 For clarity, the Amendment Contract Value is **in addition to** the Original Contract Value specified in **Section 3.1.1 of the Parent Agreement**. The total potential exposure across the Parent Agreement and this Amendment shall be the sum of the Original Contract Value and the Amendment Contract Value, subject to approved changes.

### 3.2 Fee Structure and Milestones

3.2.1 Fees for the Program may consist of:

- (a) **Fixed‑Fee Components** tied to major Milestones (e.g., completion of Phase 1, completion of initial production deployment);  
- (b) **Time‑and‑Materials Components** for flexible advisory services, change requests, or incremental enhancements; and  
- (c) **Recurring Managed Services Fees** for ongoing support, maintenance, and monitoring, as detailed in each Program SOW.

3.2.2 An illustrative allocation of the Amendment Contract Value (subject to refinement in Program SOWs) is:

- Phase 1 (Discovery & Design): US$550,000  
- Phase 2 (Build & Validate): US$2,100,000  
- Phase 3 (Pilot & Rollout): US$1,650,000  
- Phase 4 (Managed Services & Optimization): US$653,638  

3.2.3 Unless otherwise set forth in a Program SOW:

- Fixed‑fee amounts shall be invoiced **upon achievement of the corresponding Milestone**, as acknowledged in writing (including email) by Contoso’s designated project lead.  
- Time‑and‑materials fees shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears** based on actual hours worked, using the rates agreed in the relevant Program SOW.  
- Recurring managed services fees shall be invoiced **monthly in advance**.

### 3.3 Payment Terms; Net 60

3.3.1 Payment terms for all invoices issued under this Amendment shall be **Net 60**, in alignment with **Section 3.2.2 (Net 60 Terms)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.2 The dispute process for invoices in **Section 3.2.3 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any disputed amounts under this Amendment; provided that, in the interest of a low-risk engagement for Contoso, Quantum Labs agrees that any amounts reasonably documented as disputed by Contoso shall not accrue late payment interest until the dispute is resolved.

### 3.4 Late Payments

3.4.1 Interest on any undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue as provided in **Section 3.3 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement, provided that Quantum Labs shall provide at least one written reminder notice and a ten (10) day cure period before suspending performance of the Program Services.

### 3.5 Expenses and Reimbursement

3.5.1 Contoso shall reimburse Quantum Labs for reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Program, in accordance with **Section 3.4 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 The Parties agree that, for the Program, Quantum Labs shall use reasonable efforts to minimize travel and related expenses by leveraging remote collaboration and virtual meetings where practical.

### 3.6 Taxes

3.6.1 Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. All Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Amendment Term

4.1.1 This Amendment shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date** (May 24, 2025) and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or Article IV of the Parent Agreement, shall continue in full force and effect until **September 13, 2026** (the **“Amendment Expiration Date”**).

4.1.2 For avoidance of doubt, the **Term** of the Parent Agreement as set forth in **Section 4.1 (Term)** (ending October 20, 2028) remains unchanged, except as this Amendment-specific term applies to the Program.

### 4.2 Renewal

4.2.1 The Parties may mutually agree in writing to extend or renew the Program beyond the Amendment Expiration Date, by executing a further amendment or Program SOW referencing both the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly)

4.3.1 **By Contoso.** Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement (which provides for 180 days’ notice by Contoso), Contoso may terminate this Amendment or any Program SOW **for convenience upon thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Quantum Labs. This shorter notice period reflects the low-risk engagement profile applicable to this Amendment.

4.3.2 **By Quantum Labs.** Quantum Labs may terminate this Amendment or any Program SOW for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, which is longer than the ninety (90) day period otherwise required under Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement and is intended to provide additional protection to Contoso.

4.3.3 Termination for convenience of this Amendment shall not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement; however, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement shall have the effect described in Section 4.6 below.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 The termination for cause provisions of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment and all Program SOWs, provided that, before exercising any such termination rights in relation to the Program, the non‑breaching Party shall provide a written notice of default describing the alleged breach and a **minimum thirty (30) day cure period** (or longer if reasonably required, not to exceed sixty (60) days, for breaches that by their nature require more time to cure).

### 4.5 Effect of Termination

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment:

- (a) All Program SOWs shall automatically terminate, unless otherwise agreed in writing.  
- (b) Contoso shall pay Quantum Labs all Fees and reimbursable expenses incurred through the effective date of termination in accordance with Section 4.5(b) of the Parent Agreement, provided that no early termination fee or penalty shall apply to a termination for convenience by Contoso under this Amendment.  
- (c) Quantum Labs shall reasonably cooperate with Contoso to transition the Program, including providing available documentation and, if requested, limited transition assistance on a time‑and‑materials basis at agreed rates.  
- (d) Sections regarding confidentiality, IP ownership, indemnification, limitation of liability, data protection, dispute resolution, and other provisions which by their nature should survive, shall survive, in accordance with **Section 4.6 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 16.9** of this Amendment.

### 4.6 Relationship to Parent Agreement Termination

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, except as necessary to give effect to the survival provisions of this Amendment and the Parent Agreement.

4.6.2 In case of any conflict between the termination provisions of this Amendment and those of the Parent Agreement, the more **Client‑protective** provision (favoring Contoso) shall govern with respect to the Program, consistent with the low-risk engagement profile.

## ARTICLE IX. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### 9.1 Modification of Parent Agreement’s No-Cap Provision

9.1.1 The Parties expressly agree that, **solely with respect to the Program and this Amendment**, the **no contractual cap** on liability set forth in **Section 9.1 (No Cap; High‑Risk Engagement)** of the Parent Agreement **shall not apply**.

9.1.2 Instead, the Parties hereby adopt the following **low-risk, client-friendly** liability framework for the Program:

### 9.2 Liability Cap

9.2.1 **Aggregate Cap.** Except as provided in Section 9.3 below, the total aggregate liability of each Party arising out of or relating to this Amendment and the Program, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), or otherwise, shall not exceed an amount equal to **one (1) times the total Fees actually paid by Contoso to Quantum Labs under this Amendment during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim** (the **“Liability Cap”**).

### 9.3 Exclusions from Cap

9.3.1 The Liability Cap shall not apply to:

- (a) A Party’s obligations to indemnify the other Party for third-party claims under **Article VIII (Indemnification)** of this Amendment and **Article VIII of the Parent Agreement**;  
- (b) A Party’s breach of its confidentiality obligations under **Article V** of this Amendment and **Article V of the Parent Agreement**, or of data protection obligations under **Article XI** of this Amendment and **Article XI of the Parent Agreement**;  
- (c) A Party’s gross negligence or willful misconduct;  
- (d) Contoso’s payment obligations for Fees properly due and payable under this Amendment.

### 9.4 Exclusion of Certain Damages

9.4.1 Subject to Section 9.3, and to the maximum extent permitted by applicable law, neither Party shall be liable to the other for any **indirect, incidental, special, punitive, or consequential damages**, or for any loss of profits, revenue, or data, arising out of or relating to this Amendment, even if such Party has been advised of the possibility of such damages.

### 9.5 Basis of Bargain

9.5.1 The Parties acknowledge that this Article IX reflects a deliberate allocation of risk for the **low-risk** engagement embodied by this Amendment and that the Fees and Amendment Contract Value have been set in reliance on this allocation.

- Severity 2 (High): **2 hours**  
- Severity 3 (Medium): **1 Business Day**

- Uptime ≥ 98.0% but < 99.0%: **15%** credit;  
- Uptime < 98.0%: **25%** credit.

12.4.2 SLA Credits shall be applied against subsequent invoices and shall be in addition to, and not in lieu of, Contoso’s other rights and remedies for persistent or material failures, subject to the limitations in Article IX.
