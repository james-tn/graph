## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Purchase Agreement** dated **April 19, 2024**, with Contract Reference Number **PUR-DEL-202404-343** and internal identifier **contract_343** (the “**Parent Agreement**”), pursuant to which Vendor agreed to provide certain technology solutions, software tools, implementation, customization, and related professional services to Client, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established, inter alia, (i) a total **Contract Value** of **Eight Hundred Forty-Four Thousand Five Hundred Twenty-Five U.S. Dollars (US$844,525)** under **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)**, (ii) a fee structure comprising implementation, software license/subscription, support and maintenance, and additional professional services fees under **Section 3.2 (Fee Structure)**, and (iii) payment terms, service levels, and change management mechanisms under **Articles III, XII, and XIII** respectively;

WHEREAS, subsequent to the execution of the Parent Agreement, the Parties have identified a business need to **increase the scope and quantities of licensed software modules, support coverage, and professional services**, thereby impacting the Contract Value, certain pricing arrangements, and related quantities originally agreed under **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** and **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to **supplement and amend** certain provisions of the Parent Agreement, specifically with respect to quantities, pricing, fee structure, service levels related to the additional scope, and certain associated commercial terms, while **preserving all other terms, conditions, and obligations** of the Parent Agreement, except as expressly modified herein;

WHEREAS, this Amendment is intended to constitute a written amendment to the Parent Agreement in accordance with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** thereof, which requires that no amendment or modification shall be effective unless in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both Parties;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, agreements, and promises contained in this Amendment and the Parent Agreement, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is entered into **pursuant to** and **expressly amends and supplements** the Parent Agreement (Ref: **PUR-DEL-202404-343 / contract_343**), in accordance with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** thereof.

2.1.2 Except as expressly set forth in this Amendment, the **scope of Services and Deliverables** as described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement shall remain unchanged and shall continue to govern the Parties’ rights and obligations. The Additional Services and Additional Deliverables defined herein are **incremental** and shall be treated as Services and Deliverables under the Parent Agreement for all purposes, including those set forth in **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XII (Service Level Agreements), and XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Additional Services.**  

2.2.1 Under this Amendment, Vendor shall provide to Client, and Client shall purchase from Vendor, the following Additional Services, which expand upon the categories already enumerated in **Section 2.2 (Nature of Services)** of the Parent Agreement:

  (a) **Expanded Software Provisioning and Licensing.** An increase in the quantity and scope of licenses to Vendor’s proprietary software platform(s), including:
  - Increase of the **Core transaction processing platform** licensed users and processing capacity by one hundred fifty percent (150%) over the baseline quantities assumed in the Parent Agreement;
  - Addition of **two (2)** new **Analytics and reporting modules** focused on real-time operational dashboards and predictive analytics;
  - Deployment of an expanded **integration middleware and APIs** layer to support up to **ten (10)** additional Third-Party Products beyond what was originally contemplated, consistent with **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement; and
  - Provision of a dedicated **multi-region production environment** within the existing administrative and configuration consoles, subject to the same security and access controls referenced in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) **Additional Implementation and Configuration Services.** Vendor shall deliver additional implementation services necessary to deploy the Expanded License Quantities and Additional Deliverables within Client’s environment, including:
  - Extended **requirements gathering and solution design** workshops focused on the newly added modules and interfaces;
  - Further **configuration and parameterization** of the software platform to support increased transaction volumes and additional business units of Client;
  - Expanded **data migration mapping and support** for legacy data sources not previously covered under the Parent Agreement;
  - Design and configuration of up to **five (5)** new **interfaces to Third-Party Products**; and
  - **Regression testing, performance testing, and quality assurance** to confirm conformity with updated Specifications as defined in the Parent Agreement.

  (c) **Customization, Development, and Enhancements.** Vendor shall perform additional custom development activities, including:
  - Creation of up to **eight (8)** additional custom reports and dashboards;
  - Development of **workflow automation** for specific Client processes identified during workshops;
  - Custom integration of advanced **security and audit logging** features aligned with Client’s compliance requirements; and
  - Documentation of enhancements and configuration baselines, integrated into the Deliverables defined in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement.

  (d) **Enhanced Support and Maintenance Services.** In addition to the support services described in **Section 2.2.4 (Support and Maintenance)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:
  - Provide an **elevated support tier** with prioritized response for Severity 1 and Severity 2 incidents, as further defined in Section 12.2 of the Parent Agreement and supplemented in **Article XII** of this Amendment;
  - Extend helpdesk availability to include **limited weekend coverage** during defined rollout periods; and
  - Offer **proactive monitoring and advisory services** related to capacity planning and performance optimization.

  (e) **Additional Training and Knowledge Transfer.** Vendor shall conduct:
  - Up to **six (6)** additional training workshops (onsite or remote as mutually agreed);
  - Creation of **module-specific user guides** and **quick reference cards** for the newly introduced features; and
  - Hands-on sessions with Client’s technical team focusing on maintaining and extending the newly implemented functionalities.

2.3 **Additional Deliverables.**  

2.3.1 The Additional Deliverables under this Amendment shall include, without limitation:

  (a) Updated **solution design documents** reflecting the expanded scope, including architecture diagrams and configuration matrices;

  (b) **Configured software instances** for additional environments (e.g., expanded staging and performance testing environments) and the new modules described in Section 2.2.2(a);

  (c) **Custom-developed components, scripts, integration interfaces**, and workflows arising from the Additional Services;

  (d) Additional **test plans, test cases, and test reports** covering regression, performance, and user acceptance testing in relation to the expanded solution;

  (e) Updated **user documentation, operational runbooks, and technical manuals** covering the Extended License Quantities and new functionalities; and

  (f) Enhanced **project status reports, implementation plans, and training materials** that integrate with the project governance framework under the Parent Agreement.

2.3.2 Each Additional Deliverable shall be deemed accepted in accordance with the acceptance mechanism set forth in **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement (i.e., written acceptance or deemed acceptance after ten (10) Business Days absent written notice of material non-conformity).

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.4.1 The Parties shall agree in writing (email confirmation being sufficient for project-level coordination, but without altering contractual obligations unless memorialized in a Change Order) on a detailed **implementation plan** for the Additional Services, which shall:

  (a) Align key milestones (e.g., additional design completion, extended SIT/UAT cycles, and additional go-live events) with the framework established in **Section 2.4 (Timelines and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) Include a phased rollout approach for the Expanded License Quantities across Client’s business units and geographies; and

  (c) Specify dependency milestones for Client responsibilities, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.2 Any material adjustment to timelines or milestone dates resulting from Client-caused delays, change requests, or unforeseen technical constraints shall be addressed via the **Change Management** process under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Amendment.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities.**  

2.5.1 In addition to the responsibilities enumerated in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Client shall:

  (a) Provide additional subject matter experts and business stakeholders for the newly affected business units;

  (b) Ensure timely provisioning of any additional infrastructure or Third-Party Products required for the Expanded License Quantities;

  (c) Facilitate access to extended datasets and integration end-points necessary for the additional interfaces and analytics modules; and

  (d) Participate actively in testing, acceptance, and training activities associated with the Additional Services.

2.5.2 Delays or failures by Client to fulfill the above responsibilities may result in schedule impacts and additional Fees in accordance with **Section 2.5** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

2.6.1 Vendor shall perform the Additional Services in accordance with the performance standards and service quality requirements described in **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, including adherence to generally accepted professional standards in the information technology services industry.

2.6.2 The Service Levels in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Amendment, shall apply to the Additional Services and Additional Deliverables.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amended Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The Parties hereby acknowledge and agree that, in consideration for the Additional Services and Additional Deliverables described in **Article II** herein, the overall contract value associated with this Amendment is **Two Million Three Hundred Forty-Nine Thousand Seven Hundred Forty-Seven U.S. Dollars (US$2,349,747)** (the “**Amendment Contract Value**”).

3.1.2 For clarity:

  (a) The **Contract Value (Parent)** remains **US$844,525**, as stated in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) The **combined estimated contract value** for the Parent Agreement **and** this Amendment is therefore **US$3,194,272** (US$844,525 + US$2,349,747), exclusive of applicable taxes and reimbursable expenses.

3.2 **Fee Structure Under this Amendment.**  

3.2.1 The Fees payable under this Amendment shall consist of:

  (a) **Additional One-Time Implementation and Customization Fees:**  
  A one-time fee of **US$1,150,000** for the incremental implementation, configuration, customization, and integration services described herein, to be invoiced and paid in accordance with **Section 3.3.1** below.

  (b) **Incremental Software License / Subscription Fees:**  
  Additional recurring software license/subscription fees totaling **US$900,000** over the Term of this Amendment, calculated on the basis of:
  - An incremental annual license fee of **US$360,000** for contract years overlapping with this Amendment’s Term; and
  - Pro-rated amounts for partial contract years, if applicable, aligned with the Parent Agreement’s license billing cycle.

  (c) **Incremental Support and Maintenance Fees:**  
  Additional support and maintenance fees totaling **US$299,747** over the Term of this Amendment, which include:
  - Enhanced support tiers and additional weekend coverage; and
  - Proactive monitoring and advisory services.

  (d) **Additional Professional Services (Time-and-Materials):**  
  Any further professional services requested by Client and not expressly included in this Amendment shall be billed on a time-and-materials basis at Vendor’s prevailing rates, as set forth in applicable SOWs or Change Orders, consistent with **Section 3.2.4** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Milestones (Amendment Fees).**  

3.3.1 **Additional Implementation Fee Milestones (US$1,150,000)**  

Unless otherwise agreed in a written Change Order:

  (a) **20%** (US$230,000) payable within thirty (30) days following the Amendment Effective Date;

  (b) **30%** (US$345,000) payable upon completion and Client’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the extended solution design phase for the Additional Services;

  (c) **30%** (US$345,000) payable upon completion and Client’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the extended system integration testing (SIT) covering the Additional Deliverables; and

  (d) **20%** (US$230,000) payable upon go-live of the primary additional production rollout for the Expanded License Quantities.

3.3.2 **Incremental License / Subscription Fees (US$900,000)**  

  (a) Incremental license fees shall be **invoiced annually in advance**, synchronized with the existing license invoicing schedule under **Section 3.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) If the Amendment Effective Date falls mid-license year, the first invoice may be prorated for the remainder of that year, followed by full-year invoices for subsequent years within the Term of this Amendment.

3.3.3 **Incremental Support and Maintenance Fees (US$299,747)**  

  (a) Incremental support and maintenance fees shall be **invoiced annually in advance**, in accordance with the approach in **Section 3.3(f)** of the Parent Agreement, adjusted to reflect the enhanced support tier.

  (b) Any materially expanded support scope requested by Client beyond that described herein shall be handled through a Change Order and may result in additional Fees.

3.3.4 **Additional Time-and-Materials Services and Expenses**

  (a) Additional time-and-materials services shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears**, as provided in **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) Reimbursable expenses shall be invoiced monthly in arrears and shall be subject to Client’s travel and expense policies in accordance with **Section 3.8 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Payment Terms.**  

3.4.1 All Fees under this Amendment are payable **Net 30** from the invoice date, consistent with **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4.2 Any disputes regarding invoices shall follow the procedure in **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement (i.e., written notice within fifteen (15) Business Days, payment of undisputed amounts, etc.).

3.5 **Late Payment.**  

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest as specified in **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement (1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law).

3.5.2 Vendor may suspend Additional Services and access to any Expanded License Quantities for persistent non-payment in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement, provided Vendor issues prior written notice as required therein.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

3.6.1 All Fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.7 **Price Adjustments.**  

3.7.1 Vendor may adjust recurring incremental license and support fees as permitted by **Section 3.7 (Price Adjustments)** of the Parent Agreement (up to 5% annually or CPI-based), with such adjustments applying equally to the incremental Fees under this Amendment, subject to the same notice requirements.

3.8 **Expenses.**  

3.8.1 Client shall reimburse Vendor for reasonable, properly documented travel and out-of-pocket expenses incurred in performing the Additional Services, consistent with **Section 3.8 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term of Amendment.**  

4.1.1 This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall remain in full force and effect until **December 08, 2026** (the “**Amendment Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Amendment or the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Term of this Amendment is **coextensive with and subject to** the Term of the Parent Agreement under **Section 4.1 (Term)** thereof; accordingly, if the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated prior to the Amendment Expiration Date, this Amendment shall automatically **expire or terminate** concurrently, subject to Sections 4.4 and 4.5 below.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

4.2.1 To the extent the Parent Agreement is renewed under **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof, the Parties may, by mutual written agreement, extend all or part of this Amendment to coincide with such renewal, including through a further amendment or Change Order specifying any revised scope and pricing.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 **By Client.**  
Client may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part (with respect to the incremental scope), for convenience and without cause upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Vendor. In such event, Client shall pay Vendor for:

  (a) All Additional Services performed and Additional Deliverables delivered through the effective date of termination;

  (b) All non-cancellable third-party commitments directly attributable to this Amendment; and

  (c) Any reasonable wind-down costs associated with the Additional Services, provided such costs are properly documented.

4.3.2 **By Vendor.**  
Vendor may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Client, provided that Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption to Client and to assist with transition, subject to Client’s payment of applicable Fees and any additional transition services fees.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment for cause in accordance with the termination rights and cure periods set forth in **Section 4.5 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement, provided that a material breach of this Amendment shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa to the extent the breach is intertwined with the Additional Services.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 Termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement for any reason shall automatically result in the concurrent termination of this Amendment, without the need for separate notice, except to the extent that:

  (a) Certain payment obligations, confidentiality obligations, IP provisions, and other surviving clauses under this Amendment shall continue, as described in Section 4.6 below; and

  (b) The Parties agree in writing to continue specific portions of this Amendment as part of a successor arrangement.

4.6 **Effect of Termination; Survival.**  

4.6.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment for any reason:

  (a) All rights and licenses granted with respect to the Expanded License Quantities shall cease, except where explicitly made perpetual by written agreement;

  (b) Client shall cease using any Additional Deliverables and Expanded License Quantities not expressly preserved in a surviving license grant;

  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information in accordance with **Section 4.6(c)** and **Article V** of the Parent Agreement, as applicable; and

  (d) Client shall pay all accrued but unpaid Fees and reimbursable expenses associated with this Amendment through the effective date of termination.

4.6.2 The provisions of this Amendment that by their nature should survive termination or expiration, including but not limited to payment obligations, confidentiality, IP ownership and restrictions, limitation of liability, indemnification, dispute resolution, and governing law, shall survive in accordance with **Section 4.6(e)** and **Section 16.11 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented herein.
