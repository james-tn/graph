## RECITALS  

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent Agreement, Contract Reference No. **LSE-MER-202510-574** (file identifier **contract_574**), effective as of **October 17, 2025**, pursuant to which Vendor agreed to lease certain **Leased Property** and provide **Services** and **Deliverables** to Client, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Leased Property)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established an **Initial Term** through **September 29, 2029** (the “Expiration Date” under **Section 1.10** and **Section 4.1**) and contemplated an aggregate estimated contract value of **US$1,596,968** over such Initial Term pursuant to **Section 3.1 (Fees and Contract Value)**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to (i) extend and modify certain lease terms, including extension of the term beyond the original Expiration Date, (ii) adjust the scope of Leased Property and related **Services** and **Service Levels** under **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, (iii) revise the contract value for the extended period, and (iv) amend certain related provisions of the Parent Agreement, all as set forth herein;

WHEREAS, this Amendment **modifies lease terms dated October 17, 2025 (Ref: LSE-MER-202510-574)**, including specific modifications to **Article II (Scope of Services and Leased Property)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, in accordance with **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties desire to set forth their agreement in this written Amendment, duly signed by authorized representatives of both Parties;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that, except as expressly amended or supplemented hereby, all terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect, and that this Amendment and the Parent Agreement shall be read and construed together as a single, integrated agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and undertakings contained in this Amendment and in the Parent Agreement, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES, LEASED PROPERTY, AND RELATIONSHIP TO PARENT AGREEMENT  

2.1 **Reference to Parent Agreement Scope.**  
The Parties acknowledge and reaffirm **Article II (Scope of Services and Leased Property)** of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 2.1 (General Scope)**, 2.2 (Types of Leased Property), 2.3 (Services), and 2.6 (Client Obligations and Use of Leased Property). This Amendment does not replace Article II of the Parent Agreement; rather, it **supplements and modifies** such provisions solely as they relate to the Extended Leased Property and Extended Services described herein.

2.2 **Purpose of Amendment to Scope.**  
This Amendment is intended to (a) extend the lease term for certain existing Leased Property that would otherwise have expired by or before **September 29, 2029**, (b) add additional equipment, workspace, and related infrastructure as Extended Leased Property, and (c) provide additional and enhanced Services, including maintenance, managed services, and workspace services, to support Client’s continued and expanded operations at Vendor-managed facilities and Client premises through **March 28, 2030**, the new Expiration Date for the Amended Term.

2.3 **Extended Leased Property.**  
Subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment:

  2.3.1 Vendor shall continue to lease to Client, on substantially the same basis as set forth in the Parent Agreement, all Leased Property that remains in service as of **September 29, 2029**, and which is identified by the Parties in one or more Amended Order Documents executed on or before such date.

  2.3.2 Vendor shall additionally provide the following categories of Extended Leased Property (which supplement the categories in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement):

  - Additional commercial office furniture, fixtures, and fittings for expansion floors at Client’s Wilmington facility;  
  - Incremental information technology equipment, including high-capacity storage arrays, redundant core switches, and upgraded server platforms;  
  - Enhanced access control and security systems, including expanded CCTV coverage and biometric access devices at Vendor-managed facilities utilized by Client;  
  - Additional dedicated workspace and premises within facilities operated by Vendor, to accommodate up to an additional [●] seats for Client’s operations.

  2.3.3 The specific identity, quantity, configuration, and physical location of the Extended Leased Property, as well as any installation milestones, shall be detailed in one or more Amended Order Documents that reference both **LSE-MER-202510-574** and **AMD-MER-202510-686**, and shall be deemed incorporated into the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

2.4 **Extended Services.**  
In addition to the Services described in **Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement**, Vendor shall provide the following Extended Services:

  2.4.1 **Enhanced Installation and Deployment.** Vendor shall perform phased installation, configuration, and integration of the Extended Leased Property, ensuring compatibility with existing Leased Property and Client systems. Vendor shall coordinate installation windows to minimize disruption to Client’s business operations.

  2.4.2 **Expanded Maintenance and Support.** Vendor shall extend preventative and corrective maintenance obligations for all Extended Leased Property and the previously deployed Leased Property that remains in use during the Amended Term. Maintenance shall include adherence to updated manufacturer guidelines, application of security firmware updates, and proactive replacement of components nearing end-of-life.

  2.4.3 **Advanced Managed Services.** Vendor shall expand managed services to include:
  - 24x7 infrastructure monitoring and alerting for critical IT systems;  
  - Enhanced incident management aligned with the SLA Enhancements in **Article XII** of this Amendment;  
  - Capacity planning and reporting for compute, storage, and network utilization;  
  - Backup and disaster recovery readiness verifications on a quarterly basis.

  2.4.4 **Workspace Services Expansion.** For any additional workspace or premises leased under this Amendment, Vendor shall provide building services consistent with **Section 2.3.4 (Workspace Services)** of the Parent Agreement, with any incremental building rules or security requirements documented in the applicable Amended Order Document.

  2.4.5 **Onboarding and Training for Extended Scope.** Vendor shall provide additional onboarding and training for Client’s designated personnel who will use or administer the Extended Leased Property and Extended Services, covering any new safety, security, and operational procedures.

  2.4.6 **Documentation and Enhanced Reporting.** Vendor shall supplement documentation and reporting obligations under **Section 2.3.6 of the Parent Agreement** by providing at least **monthly** reports during the Amended Term summarizing:
  - Service availability and SLA attainment;  
  - Incidents and resolutions;  
  - Changes or upgrades implemented; and  
  - Capacity and utilization metrics for key systems.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Vendor’s obligations under **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards) of the Parent Agreement** remain in full force and effect and are hereby confirmed for the Extended Services. In addition, Vendor shall:

  - Meet or exceed the SLA Enhancements set forth in **Article XII** of this Amendment;  
  - Use commercially reasonable efforts to coordinate with Client’s internal change and release management processes; and  
  - Maintain continuity of key technical and account management personnel, recognizing the heightened risk profile and business criticality of the Extended Services.

2.6 **Client Obligations.**  
The obligations of Client set forth in **Section 2.6 (Client Obligations and Use of Leased Property) of the Parent Agreement** apply to all Extended Leased Property and Extended Services, including:

  - Providing reasonable access to premises, systems, and personnel;  
  - Complying with Use Restrictions, including not subleasing or encumbering the Extended Leased Property;  
  - Maintaining the Client-supplied environment to meet power, cooling, and connectivity requirements; and  
  - Promptly reporting damage, malfunctions, or loss in accordance with **Section 2.6.4** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **Title to Extended Leased Property.**  
Consistent with **Section 2.7 (Title to Leased Property)** of the Parent Agreement, title and ownership of all Extended Leased Property shall remain with Vendor or its lessors at all times. No provision of this Amendment shall be construed to transfer to Client any right, title, or interest in or to any Leased Property or Extended Leased Property, other than the limited right to use such property in accordance with the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

2.8 **Acceptance of Extended Leased Property and Deliverables.**  
The acceptance procedures of **Section 2.8 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Extended Leased Property and any new Deliverables. Unless otherwise specified in an Amended Order Document, Client shall have thirty (30) days following delivery or implementation to test and either accept or provide written notice of material non-conformance. Use in production shall constitute acceptance.

2.9 **Subcontractors.**  
Vendor may utilize subcontractors in accordance with **Section 2.9 of the Parent Agreement** for performance of Extended Services, provided Vendor remains fully responsible for such subcontractors’ acts and omissions and for ensuring adequate confidentiality and data protection measures.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS  

3.1 **Additional Contract Value and Fee Structure.**  
In consideration of the Extended Leased Property and Extended Services provided under this Amendment, Client shall pay Vendor fees such that the aggregate estimated contract value for the extension period governed by this Amendment is **US$1,102,282** (the “**Additional Contract Value**”). This value is separate from, and in addition to, the **US$1,596,968** contract value referenced in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement for the original Initial Term.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  
Fees under this Amendment will include, without limitation:

  3.2.1 **Extended Lease Charges.** Monthly or periodic lease charges for Extended Leased Property, including any extensions of prior leases beyond September 29, 2029.

  3.2.2 **One-Time Extension and Upgrade Fees.** One-time fees associated with:
  - Installation and configuration of new Extended Leased Property;  
  - Upgrades and migrations of existing Leased Property;  
  - Additional onboarding and training.

  3.2.3 **Recurring Service and Maintenance Fees.** Recurring charges for Extended Services, including enhanced managed services and workspace services.

  3.2.4 **Change-Related Fees.** Fees arising from Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

Specific amounts, billing frequencies, and milestones shall be detailed in one or more Amended Order Documents.

3.3 **Payment Terms; Net 45.**  
Vendor shall invoice Client in accordance with the schedule set forth in the applicable Amended Order Document(s). Unless otherwise expressly agreed:

  - Invoices for recurring fees shall be issued monthly in arrears;  
  - Invoices for one-time fees may be issued in advance upon execution of the relevant Amended Order Document or upon completion of the applicable milestone.

Client shall pay all **undisputed** amounts within **forty-five (45) calendar days** of receipt of a valid invoice, consistent with **Section 3.2 (Net 45)** of the Parent Agreement. These Net 45 Payment Terms are reaffirmed and apply fully to this Amendment.

3.4 **Disputed Amounts.**  
The dispute procedure set forth in **Section 3.3 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all invoices issued under this Amendment. Client must notify Vendor in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt of an invoice of any disputed portion, specifying the basis for dispute. Client shall timely pay the undisputed portion, and the Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve the dispute.

3.5 **Late Payment.**  
Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the rate set forth in **Section 3.4 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement (the lesser of 1% per month or the maximum permitted by law) from the due date until paid. Vendor may, upon written notice and subject to the cure opportunity set forth in **Section 3.4** and **Section 4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement as modified by this Amendment, suspend Extended Services and access to Extended Leased Property for non-payment.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  
Vendor’s reimbursable expenses under this Amendment, including travel and lodging related to Extended Services, shall be governed by **Section 3.5 (Expenses and Reimbursements)** of the Parent Agreement. Client shall reimburse pre-approved, reasonable, out-of-pocket expenses in accordance with Client’s then-current policies, provided written approval is obtained for any trip exceeding USD $1,000.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
Client shall be responsible for all **Taxes** associated with the Additional Contract Value and any other fees under this Amendment, as set forth in **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. Vendor shall separately itemize Taxes on invoices where applicable.

3.8 **No Set-Off.**  
Subject to the dispute provisions above, Client shall have no right of set-off or withholding beyond what is expressly permitted in **Section 3.7 (No Set-Off)** of the Parent Agreement, which is hereby reaffirmed and extended to apply to all amounts due under this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION  

4.1 **Amended Term.**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that, solely with respect to the Extended Leased Property and Extended Services covered by this Amendment, the term shall extend beyond the original Expiration Date and continue in effect until **March 28, 2030** (the “**Amended Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement, as modified by this Amendment.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
The Parent Agreement’s Initial Term (through September 29, 2029) remains in effect for all existing Leased Property and Services not extended under this Amendment. To the extent any existing Leased Property or Services are expressly extended or modified via an Amended Order Document referencing this Amendment, the corresponding term for such items shall be governed by the Amended Term.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
The Parties acknowledge that **Section 4.2 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement provides for a 30-day notice period for Client and 60 days for Vendor, tied to a “Low” risk level. In light of the **High Risk Level** under this Amendment, the Parties agree as follows, **solely with respect to Extended Leased Property and Extended Services**:

  4.3.1 Client may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to Vendor.  

  4.3.2 Vendor may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing **ninety (90) days’ prior written notice** to Client.  

During any such notice period, Vendor shall continue to provide, and Client shall continue to pay for, all Extended Leased Property and Extended Services in accordance with this Amendment and the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause provisions set forth in **Section 4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply in full to this Amendment. Either Party may terminate this Amendment and the applicable Amended Order Documents for material breach not cured within thirty (30) days, or in the event of insolvency as described in **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

  4.5.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the same effective date of termination or expiration, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain certain Extended Leased Property or Extended Services under a standalone arrangement.

  4.5.2 Upon termination or expiration of this Amendment, the obligations in **Section 4.4 (Effect of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Extended Leased Property and Extended Services, including:
  - Cessation of Services;  
  - Return and de-installation of Extended Leased Property;  
  - Payment of all accrued and outstanding fees; and  
  - Survival of provisions that by their nature should survive.

4.6 **Return of Extended Leased Property.**  
Client shall make Extended Leased Property available for Vendor to de-install and remove within thirty (30) days of the effective date of termination or expiration, in accordance with **Section 4.4.2 of the Parent Agreement**, except that Client shall be responsible for reasonable direct de-installation and shipping costs where termination is due to Client’s uncured material breach.

4.7 **Transition Assistance.**  
Vendor shall provide Transition Assistance in accordance with **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, for up to sixty (60) days following termination or expiration of this Amendment (other than termination by Vendor for Client’s uncured material breach). Fees for such assistance shall be at Vendor’s then-current time-and-materials rates unless otherwise provided in an Amended Order Document.

4.8 **Survival.**  
Without limiting **Section 4.4.4 of the Parent Agreement**, Articles **V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, and XVI** of this Amendment shall survive termination or expiration of this Amendment to the extent necessary to give effect to the Parties’ rights and obligations.

## ARTICLE IX  
## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY  

9.1 **Modification of Parent Limitation of Liability.**  
The Parties acknowledge that **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement establishes a liability cap of three (3) times the fees paid or payable in the prior twelve (12) months and excludes certain categories of damages. In recognition of the High Risk Level, **solely as between the Parties with respect to the Extended Leased Property, Extended Services, and this Amendment**, the Parties agree as follows:

9.2 **No Aggregate Cap.**  
THERE SHALL BE **NO AGGREGATE MONETARY CAP ON LIABILITY** FOR EITHER PARTY WITH RESPECT TO CLAIMS ARISING OUT OF OR RELATING TO THIS AMENDMENT, THE EXTENDED LEASED PROPERTY, OR THE EXTENDED SERVICES. FOR THE AVOIDANCE OF DOUBT, THE CAP IN **SECTION 9.2** OF THE PARENT AGREEMENT SHALL NOT APPLY TO CLAIMS SOLELY ARISING UNDER OR RELATING TO THIS AMENDMENT.

9.3 **Consequential and Other Damages.**  
SUBJECT TO APPLICABLE LAW, AND NOTWITHSTANDING ANYTHING TO THE CONTRARY IN THE PARENT AGREEMENT, THE PARTIES AGREE THAT **CONSEQUENTIAL, INCIDENTAL, INDIRECT, SPECIAL, EXEMPLARY, AND PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOSS OF PROFITS, LOSS OF DATA, AND BUSINESS INTERRUPTION, SHALL BE RECOVERABLE TO THE EXTENT ACTUALLY INCURRED AND PROVEN**, EXCEPT THAT:

  - NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR PUNITIVE DAMAGES EXCEPT TO THE EXTENT AWARDED BY A COURT OF COMPETENT JURISDICTION IN CONNECTION WITH A THIRD-PARTY CLAIM SUBJECT TO INDEMNIFICATION; AND

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLA ENHANCEMENTS)  

12.1 **Relationship to Parent Service Levels.**

12.2 **Availability Targets.**  
For critical managed components of the Extended Leased Property, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve a minimum **monthly service availability of 99.95%**, measured as a percentage of total minutes in a calendar month, excluding scheduled and emergency maintenance, Client-caused outages, and Force Majeure Events.

12.3 **Incident Response and Resolution (Enhanced).**  
The incident categories in **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement are refined for Extended Services as follows:

  - **Priority 1 (Critical)** – Outage or severe degradation impacting business-critical systems or a substantial portion of Client’s users:  
    - Initial Response: within **30 minutes**  
    - Target Workaround: within **2 hours**  
    - Target Resolution: within **8 hours**

  - **Priority 2 (High)** – Significant functionality loss with a viable workaround:  
    - Initial Response: within **2 hours**  
    - Target Resolution: within **12 hours**

  - **Priority 3 (Medium)** – Minor functional impact or performance degradation:  
    - Initial Response: within **1 Business Day**  
    - Target Resolution: within **3 Business Days**

  - **Priority 4 (Low)** – Cosmetic issues or general inquiries:  
    - Initial Response: within **2 Business Days**  
    - Target Resolution: per agreed maintenance cycles.

12.4 **SLA Credits.**  
If Vendor fails to meet the availability target or response/resolution time targets for Extended Services in a given calendar month, Client may be eligible for **service credits** equal to a percentage of the monthly fees for the affected Extended Services, as follows:

  - Availability between 99.0% and 99.95%: credit of **5%** of monthly Extended Services fees;  
  - Availability between 98.0% and 99.0%: credit of **10%**;  
  - Availability below 98.0%: credit of **15%**.

Service credits shall be applied to future invoices and constitute Client’s sole and exclusive monetary remedy for SLA failures, without prejudice to Client’s other contractual rights, including termination rights for repeated material failures under **Article IV** of this Amendment and Article IV of the Parent Agreement.

12.5 **SLA Exclusions.**  
The exclusions in **Section 12.5** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to Extended Services, including downtime resulting from Client’s misuse, third-party failures outside Vendor’s control, Force Majeure Events, and scheduled maintenance performed in accordance with the agreed notice requirements.
