## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relation to Parent Agreement.**  
This SOW is executed under and subject to the Parent Agreement and constitutes a Statement of Work as described in **Section 2.3 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement. The Services described herein form part of the “Services” defined in **Section 1.17** and further elaborated in **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement. Nothing in this SOW shall be construed to limit or modify the general scope of services described in the Parent Agreement, except as expressly stated with reference to a specific section of the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of the Program.**  
Under this SOW, the Vendor shall provide a comprehensive **Global Digital Transformation and Process Optimization Program** for the Client, consisting of strategic advisory, process design, implementation readiness, and organizational change management across multiple regions. The Program is intended to:

- Translate Contoso’s corporate growth strategy into an actionable digital transformation roadmap;  
- Assess and optimize end‑to‑end business processes in key domains (e.g., Order‑to‑Cash, Procure‑to‑Pay, Record‑to‑Report, and Customer Lifecycle Management);  
- Support the Client in preparing for and governing a large‑scale implementation of new and existing enterprise systems, including cloud‑based platforms; and  
- Provide change management and training services to enable adoption and realization of targeted benefits.

The Services under this SOW align with the general categories set forth in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement, including strategic business consulting (Section 2.2.1), technology consulting (Section 2.2.2), business process optimization (Section 2.2.3), implementation support (Section 2.2.4), change management and training (Section 2.2.5), and data analytics and reporting services (Section 2.2.6).

2.3 **Workstreams and Detailed Scope.**

2.3.1 **Workstream A – Strategy & Roadmap.**  
The Vendor shall:

(a) Conduct executive interviews and stakeholder workshops to validate the Client’s strategic objectives and constraints;  
(b) Perform market and competitive analysis (consistent with **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement), focusing on digital trends and best practices in the Client’s industry;  
(c) Develop a current‑state capability assessment across key functional areas and regions;  
(d) Design a multi‑year digital transformation roadmap, including prioritized initiatives, dependencies, indicative timelines, and high‑level cost/benefit analysis;  
(e) Prepare an executive presentation and supporting documentation summarizing the proposed roadmap and recommendations; and  
(f) Present the roadmap to the Client’s executive steering committee and incorporate feedback into a final version.

Key Deliverables for Workstream A shall include:

- A1: **Current‑State Assessment Report**  
- A2: **Target State Capability Model**  
- A3: **Multi‑Year Digital Transformation Roadmap**  
- A4: **Executive Presentation & Summary**  

2.3.2 **Workstream B – Process Optimization.**  
The Vendor shall:

(a) Select, in consultation with the Client’s project manager (per **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent Agreement), target end‑to‑end processes for detailed review;  
(b) Perform process discovery, mapping, and documentation using industry‑standard notation and the Vendor’s Tools and Methodologies (per **Section 6.2** of the Parent Agreement);  
(c) Conduct gap analysis to identify inefficiencies, control weaknesses, and opportunities for automation and standardization;  
(d) Design optimized future‑state process flows, including defined roles, responsibilities, and control points;  
(e) Develop process KPIs and reporting templates for monitoring performance; and  
(f) Provide prioritized recommendations for process and technology improvements aligned with the transformation roadmap.

Key Deliverables for Workstream B shall include:

- B1: **Detailed Current‑State Process Maps (per domain)**  
- B2: **Gap Analysis & Improvement Opportunities Report**  
- B3: **Future‑State Process Designs & RACI Matrices**  
- B4: **Process Performance KPI Definitions & Templates**  

2.3.3 **Workstream C – Implementation Readiness & Governance.**  
The Vendor shall:

(a) Define implementation governance structures, including steering committee, program management office (PMO), and workstream governance;  
(b) Develop detailed implementation workplans for prioritized initiatives, including dependencies, critical path, and resource estimates;  
(c) Provide advisory support on solution architecture and integration strategy consistent with **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement;  
(d) Prepare risk and issue management frameworks and registers;  
(e) Coordinate with the Client’s third‑party technology vendors (as implementation partners) to align scope, timelines, and responsibilities; and  
(f) Provide go‑live readiness assessment criteria and checklists.

Key Deliverables for Workstream C shall include:

- C1: **Program Governance Framework & Charter**  
- C2: **Detailed Implementation Workplans (Gantt and narrative)**  
- C3: **High‑Level Solution Architecture & Integration Advisory Report**  
- C4: **Risk & Issue Management Framework and Initial Register**  
- C5: **Go‑Live Readiness Criteria & Checklists**  

2.3.4 **Workstream D – Change Management & Training.**  
The Vendor shall:

(a) Develop a change management strategy and plan, including stakeholder mapping, communication strategy, and adoption metrics;  
(b) Design and produce training curricula and materials (as part of “training materials, user guides, and knowledge‑transfer documentation” under **Section 2.5.1(e)** of the Parent Agreement);  
(c) Deliver “train‑the‑trainer” sessions to designated Client representatives;  
(d) Facilitate change workshops and communication sessions with impacted business units;  
(e) Provide adoption and readiness dashboards; and  
(f) Conduct a post‑implementation review focusing on change adoption effectiveness.

Key Deliverables for Workstream D shall include:

- D1: **Change Management Strategy & Plan**  
- D2: **Stakeholder Analysis & Communications Plan**  
- D3: **Training Curriculum & Materials**  
- D4: **Train‑the‑Trainer Sessions & Attendance Records**  
- D5: **Adoption & Readiness Dashboard**  
- D6: **Post‑Implementation Change Effectiveness Report**  

2.4 **Deliverables; Acceptance.**  

2.4.1 The Deliverables identified in Sections 2.3.1–2.3.4 shall be produced in accordance with the detailed schedule set forth in Section 2.6 below and shall constitute “Deliverables” within the meaning of **Section 1.9** and **Section 2.5 (Deliverables; Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Timeline, Milestones, and Regional Rollout.**

2.6.1 The anticipated high‑level timeline for the Program is as follows (subject to adjustment via the Change Order process in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this SOW):
