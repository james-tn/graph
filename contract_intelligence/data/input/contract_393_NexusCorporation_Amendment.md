## ARTICLE II  
### SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Amendment is entered into pursuant to, and **amends and supplements**, the Parent Agreement (Ref. No. PUR-NEX-202402-337 / contract_337). The Parties agree that, except as expressly modified herein:

- The **framework, definitions, obligations, and responsibilities** set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Incremental Products and Incremental Services purchased under this Amendment; and  
- The specific scope, timelines, and commercial details of the Incremental Products and Incremental Services shall be governed by this Amendment and any SOWs or POs executed hereunder that expressly reference this Amendment.

2.2 **Overview of Incremental Products and Services.**  
(a) Under this Amendment, Nexus shall supply to Contoso the Incremental Products described in **Schedule A**, which are anticipated to include, without limitation:

  - Additional enterprise-grade **hardware**, including servers, storage arrays, and networking equipment consistent with Section 2.2(a) of the Parent Agreement;  
  - Additional **software** license quantities (both perpetual and subscription-based), consistent with Section 2.2(b) of the Parent Agreement, including increased user counts, core counts, and device-based licenses; and  
  - Any associated accessories, firmware, and embedded Third-Party Materials, subject to the third-party license terms disclosed to Contoso pursuant to Section 6.4(b) of the Parent Agreement.

(b) Nexus shall also provide Incremental Services, including, but not limited to:

  - **Assessment & Design Services**, as contemplated in Section 2.3(i) of the Parent Agreement, to account for the expanded infrastructure capacity and performance requirements associated with the Incremental Products;  
  - **Implementation & Configuration**, as contemplated in Section 2.3(ii), including installation and configuration of the Incremental Products, integration with existing Contoso environments, and adaptation of existing runbooks and operating procedures;  
  - **Data Migration** and/or data replication activities, as described in Section 2.3(iii), to support migration of workloads to newly provisioned hardware and software environments;  
  - **Customizations & Integrations**, per Section 2.3(iv), as needed to integrate Incremental Products with Contoso’s internal applications or third-party platforms already in scope under the Parent Agreement;  
  - **Testing & Acceptance Support**, consistent with Sections 2.3(v) and 2.8, including user acceptance testing (UAT), performance benchmarking, and remediation of material non-conformities;  
  - **Training**, per Section 2.3(vi), for administrators and end users impacted by the increased environment footprint; and  
  - **Project Management**, per Section 2.3(vii), to plan, track, and coordinate the extended rollout schedule.

2.3 **Revised Quantities and Pricing Scope.**  
(a) The Parties agree that certain quantities and pricing originally anticipated under the Parent Agreement are hereby superseded, as they relate to the categories of Products and Services listed in **Schedule A** and **Schedule B**. Specifically:

  - Hardware unit counts for specified server, storage, and network models are increased as reflected in Schedule A;  
  - Subscription-based software license counts (e.g., per-user and per-core subscriptions) are increased, and their three- or five-year subscription terms may be extended through dates that are consistent with the Amendment Term; and  
  - Professional services hours or fixed-fee service packages are adjusted to support the expanded implementation scope.

(b) Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in the Parent Agreement, all items described in Schedule A and Schedule B shall be deemed **Incremental Products** or **Incremental Services** under this Amendment and shall not require a separate Change Order under Article XIII of the Parent Agreement solely by virtue of being added hereunder.

2.4 **Performance Standards and Service Levels.**  
(a) Nexus shall perform all Incremental Services in a professional and workmanlike manner in accordance with Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement and generally accepted industry standards.

(b) The **Parent SLA** (Article XII of the Parent Agreement) shall apply to the Incremental Products and Incremental Services to the same extent as to any previously implemented Products and Services. For any hosted or managed components supplied pursuant to this Amendment, Nexus shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain **99.9% availability** in each calendar month, as set forth in Section 12.1 of the Parent Agreement.

(c) Response and resolution times for incidents affecting Incremental Products or Incremental Services shall follow the severity levels defined in Section 12.3 of the Parent Agreement (Severity 1 through Severity 4). Service credits for downtime or service level failures shall be calculated with reference to the portion of the monthly Fees attributable to the affected elements of the Incremental Products and Incremental Services.

2.5 **Project Plan and Timelines (Amended).**  
(a) In accordance with Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement, Nexus shall, in collaboration with Contoso, prepare a **Project Plan (Amended)** specific to this Amendment, including:

  - Detailed milestones for procurement, delivery, installation, configuration, and acceptance of Incremental Products;  
  - Dependencies on Contoso’s tasks, including provision of facilities, power, cooling, and network connectivity;  
  - Resource assignments on both sides; and  
  - Target dates for completion of each Project phase.

(b) Nexus shall use commercially reasonable efforts to perform the Incremental Services in accordance with the Amended Project Plan. Contoso acknowledges that delays in providing information, approvals, or access may affect timelines, consistent with Section 2.5(b) of the Parent Agreement.

(c) If Nexus reasonably anticipates that a milestone may not be met, Nexus shall notify Contoso in writing, provide an explanation, and propose a revised schedule. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to mitigate schedule impact, in alignment with Section 2.5(c) of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**  
Acceptance of any Deliverables (as defined in Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement) produced under this Amendment shall be governed by Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement. In the absence of more specific criteria in an applicable SOW, Deliverables shall be deemed accepted if not rejected in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days after delivery, with a further ten (10) Business Day period for review of any corrected Deliverables as specified in Section 2.8(b).

2.7 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  
Contoso’s obligations under Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement shall apply in full to the Incremental Products and Incremental Services. Contoso shall, among other things:

- Provide timely site access and secure storage for hardware deliveries;  
- Assign a project manager to coordinate internal stakeholders;  
- Provide required test data and test cases for acceptance activities; and  
- Obtain any necessary third-party consents not otherwise provided by Nexus.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value and Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) The Parties acknowledge that the **Amendment Contract Value** associated with the Incremental Products and Incremental Services is **US$1,852,943**, exclusive of Taxes. This amount represents the total estimated aggregate of all anticipated Fees under this Amendment.

(b) The Amendment Contract Value is in addition to, and does not reduce, the total estimated contract value of **US$2,797,951** stated in the Recitals and Section 3.1(a) of the Parent Agreement. For purposes of any aggregate liability calculations under **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, the Fees actually paid under this Amendment shall be aggregated with Fees paid under the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise expressly stated.

3.2 **Fee Schedule and Revised Pricing.**  
(a) The specific Fees applicable to the Incremental Products and Incremental Services, including unit prices, extended subscription terms, and service fees, are set forth in **Schedule B (Revised Pricing and Fee Schedule)**.

(b) To the extent that any unit prices or discount levels in Schedule B conflict with previously agreed pricing in an SOW or PO under the Parent Agreement for the same Product or Service type, **Schedule B shall control for all purchases made under this Amendment** after the Amendment Effective Date.

(c) For clarity, nothing in this Amendment shall be construed as retroactively modifying the pricing of Products and Services already delivered, accepted, and invoiced under the Parent Agreement prior to the Amendment Effective Date.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
(a) **Net 30.** The Parties confirm that the payment terms applicable to all undisputed Fees under this Amendment shall be **Net 30**, consistent with Section 3.3(a) of the Parent Agreement. Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts within thirty (30) days from the date of the applicable invoice.

(b) **Invoicing.** Nexus shall invoice Contoso in accordance with Section 3.2 of the Parent Agreement, as adapted to the scope of this Amendment, such that:

  - Hardware and perpetual software licenses under this Amendment shall be invoiced upon shipment;  
  - Subscription-based license Fees and recurring support or maintenance Services introduced or extended under this Amendment shall be invoiced annually in advance, or as otherwise specified in Schedule B; and  
  - Incremental professional Services shall be invoiced monthly in arrears on a time-and-materials basis, or upon completion of milestones where so indicated in Schedule B or an applicable SOW.

(c) Each invoice issued under this Amendment shall reference both **PUR-NEX-202402-337 (contract_337)** and **AMD-NEX-202402-393**, and shall separately identify the line items associated with this Amendment to facilitate tracking of the Amendment Contract Value.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  
Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement (Late Payment) shall apply to all undisputed amounts outstanding under this Amendment. Undisputed amounts not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of (i) one percent (1%) per month, or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, calculated from the due date until payment, provided that Nexus first provides written notice of non-payment.

3.5 **Taxes.**  
The provisions of Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement (Taxes) shall apply to all Fees payable under this Amendment. All Fees are exclusive of Taxes, and Contoso shall be responsible for paying any such properly invoiced Taxes, except for taxes based on Nexus’s net income.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
To the extent Nexus incurs reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses in performing Incremental Services (e.g., travel, lodging, per diem), such expenses shall be reimbursed by Contoso at actual cost without markup, consistent with Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement and subject to Contoso’s travel and expense policies provided in advance.

3.7 **No Set-Off.**  
Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement (No Set-Off) shall apply to payments under this Amendment. Contoso shall not set off, deduct, or withhold any amounts from payments due under this Amendment except as expressly permitted under the Parent Agreement or as agreed in writing by Nexus.

## ARTICLE IV  
### TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**  
This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall remain in effect until **July 24, 2029**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV and **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**. For the avoidance of doubt, certain subscription-based licenses and ongoing support obligations may have specific end dates described in Schedule B that fall within or are coterminous with the Amendment Term.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
(a) The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement currently has a Term ending **December 2, 2028** (Section 4.1). To the extent that the Amendment Term extends beyond the Parent Agreement Term, the Parties hereby agree that the Parent Agreement is deemed **renewed or extended solely for the limited purpose of supporting the obligations described in this Amendment** through **July 24, 2029**, unless otherwise modified by a separate amendment to the Parent Agreement.

(b) In the event of any conflict between the Term of the Parent Agreement and the Amendment Term, the Amendment Term shall control with respect to the Incremental Products and Incremental Services described herein.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
(a) Contoso may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Nexus, consistent with Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement.

(b) Nexus may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, also consistent with Section 4.3.

(c) Termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement, but may result in termination or reduction of specific SOWs or POs associated with Incremental Products and Incremental Services, as specified in the termination notice or as mutually agreed.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Each Party may terminate this Amendment (and any affected SOWs or POs referencing this Amendment) for cause, in accordance with Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement, in the event of the other Party’s material breach that remains uncured after thirty (30) days’ written notice, or in the event of insolvency or bankruptcy events described in Section 4.4(b).

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
(a) In the event the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, **unless the Parties expressly agree in writing** that this Amendment shall survive in whole or in part.

(b) Upon such termination, the effects described in Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment, including acceleration of outstanding invoices and cessation of Services. Any prepaid Fees attributable to undelivered Incremental Services or unused subscription-based licenses may be refunded or credited in accordance with Section 4.6(c) of the Parent Agreement, where such termination results from Nexus’s uncured material breach.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.**  
(a) Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment:

  - All outstanding unpaid invoices under this Amendment shall become immediately due and payable;  
  - Nexus shall cease performance of Incremental Services and shall not be obligated to deliver further Incremental Products;  
  - Contoso shall cease use of any subscription-based licenses specifically added under this Amendment that are terminated, and shall certify such cessation upon request; and  
  - Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information relating to this Amendment, in accordance with Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement.

(b) Termination of this Amendment shall not relieve either Party of obligations accrued prior to the effective date of termination.

4.7 **Survival.**  
The provisions of this Amendment that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and Sections 3.4, 3.5, 4.5, and 4.7) shall so survive, along with the corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement as referenced herein.
