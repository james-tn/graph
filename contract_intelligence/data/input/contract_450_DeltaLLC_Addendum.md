## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Addendum is executed under and forms part of the Parent Agreement. Pursuant to Section 2.1 (**General Scope**) of the Parent Agreement, which contemplates multiple Purchase Orders and Statements of Work during the Term, the Parties agree that this Addendum operates as (a) a specialized Statement of Work for additional products and services, and (b) an amendment expanding the Deliverables and Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Unless explicitly stated otherwise, all obligations, standards, and procedures in Article II of the Parent Agreement apply mutatis mutandis to the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services described herein.

2.2 **Additional Deliverables.**  
In addition to the Deliverables specified in Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement, the Vendor shall provide the following **Additional Deliverables**, which constitute "Deliverables" under the Parent Agreement:

2.2.1 **Advanced Sourcing Module.**  
An extension to the **Core Platform Software** described in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement, enabling:
  - Multi-round e-auctions and RFx (RFI/RFP/RFQ) processes;
  - Complex evaluation criteria, scoring, and weighting;
  - Automated award recommendations and scenario analysis;
  - Audit trails and approval workflows aligned with Client’s procurement policies.  
This module shall integrate seamlessly with the existing Platform environment configured for the Client, using the **Integration Components** described in Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 **Supplier Collaboration Module.**  
A web-based portal and APIs to permit Client’s suppliers within the Territory to:
  - Maintain their profiles, certifications, and compliance documentation;
  - Submit quotations and bids in response to sourcing events;
  - Acknowledge purchase orders generated by the Platform;
  - Collaborate on delivery schedules, shipment tracking, and invoice queries.  
The module will be configured to support role-based access controls consistent with the Client’s existing Platform configuration under Section 2.3.2 of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.3 **Enhanced Analytics Module.**  
A set of advanced analytics, including:
  - Pre-configured KPI dashboards (e.g., spend under management, supplier risk, cycle times);
  - Predictive analytics for supplier performance and demand forecasting;
  - Custom report builder tools enabling Client power users to define new analytics queries.  
This module shall leverage the data sources integrated under the Parent Agreement and may require additional data mapping activities as part of the Additional Services.

2.2.4 **Integration Enhancements.**  
Custom integration adaptors and enhancements to existing **Integration Components** (Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement) to support:
  - Bi-directional integration with an additional ERP instance used by Client in the EMEA region;
  - Real-time synchronization with a third-party risk scoring provider (a Third-Party Material under Section 1.18 and Section 6.6 of the Parent Agreement);
  - Extended interfaces to Client’s data warehouse and reporting tools.

2.2.5 **Optional Hardware Appliances (if applicable).**  
Where agreed in writing in a separate Schedule to this Addendum, additional **Hardware Appliances** as contemplated in Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement may be supplied to enhance local processing capabilities in certain Client data centers. Delivery terms shall be FCA in accordance with Section 2.8.1 of the Parent Agreement unless otherwise specified.

2.2.6 **Documentation and Training Materials.**  
Updated user guides, technical documentation, configuration guides, and training materials specific to the Additional Deliverables, complementing the materials set out in Sections 2.2.3 and 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement. Documentation may be provided electronically and will be integrated into the Client’s existing knowledge base.

2.3 **Additional Services.**  
The Vendor shall perform the following **Additional Services** in accordance with the professional standards set out in Section 2.6 (Performance Standards) of the Parent Agreement:

2.3.1 **Solution Design and Planning.**  
Workshops with Client stakeholders to gather detailed requirements specific to the Advanced Sourcing Module, Supplier Collaboration Module, and Enhanced Analytics Module; development of an updated solution architecture aligning with the Platform already implemented pursuant to the Parent Agreement.

2.3.2 **Implementation and Configuration of Additional Modules.**  
Configuration of workflows, user roles, templates, and approval chains within the Additional Deliverables, harmonized with existing Platform configuration, including:
  - Definition of sourcing templates and scoring models;
  - Supplier onboarding workflows and tiering schemes;
  - Data models for analytics, including KPI definitions and thresholds.

2.3.3 **Integration and Data Migration Enhancements.**  
Performance of incremental integration and data migration services to:
  - Incorporate additional vendor master data, contract records, and historical transactions required for the Enhanced Analytics Module;
  - Configure and test new integration interfaces described in Section 2.2.4;
  - Migrate relevant historical sourcing event data and supplier performance metrics.

2.3.4 **Testing and Acceptance Support.**  
Support Client in user acceptance testing (UAT) for the Additional Deliverables in accordance with Section 2.8 (Delivery, Testing and Acceptance) of the Parent Agreement, including:
  - Provision of test scripts and acceptance criteria;
  - Support during UAT cycles and remediation of reproducible material defects;
  - Resubmission of Deliverables for acceptance where necessary.

2.3.5 **Training Services.**  
Delivery of training sessions for Client’s procurement, supplier management, and analytics teams, including:
  - Train-the-trainer workshops for power users;
  - Targeted training for supplier-facing Client personnel;
  - Optional supplier training webinars, where agreed in a Purchase Order under this Addendum.

2.3.6 **Support and Maintenance.**  
Ongoing support and maintenance for the Additional Deliverables shall be provided in accordance with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by Article XII of this Addendum. The Additional Deliverables shall be treated as part of the Platform for purposes of updates and upgrades under Section 2.2.6 of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.7 **Professional Services.**  
At the Client’s request and subject to additional fees as set out in Article III, Vendor may provide further professional services, including:
  - Custom analytics development;
  - Additional integration endpoints;
  - Advisory services for procurement transformation initiatives.

2.4 **Implementation Plan – Addendum.**  
The Parties shall agree a detailed **Implementation Plan – Addendum** which will set out:
  - Phase A (0–60 days from Addendum Effective Date): detailed design and blueprinting for Additional Deliverables;
  - Phase B (60–150 days): configuration and integration development, including unit testing;
  - Phase C (150–210 days): UAT, performance testing, and pilot rollout;
  - Phase D (210–270 days): full production deployment for all covered business units.  
The Parties reaffirm that, consistent with Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, time for performance shall not be of the essence unless expressly agreed in a written Schedule; and Vendor shall not be liable for delays caused by Client’s acts or omissions, including failure to provide timely approvals, information, or access.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities.**  
Client’s responsibilities under Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement apply equally to this Addendum. Without limitation, Client shall:
  - Provide access to additional systems and data sources required for the Enhanced Analytics Module;
  - Obtain any additional third-party consents required to integrate with new ERP instances or risk-scoring providers;
  - Allocate sufficient internal resources for UAT and change management related to the Additional Deliverables.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  
Vendor shall perform the Additional Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement. Client acknowledges, as in Section 2.6, that complex software may contain inherent defects and that Vendor does not warrant error-free or uninterrupted operation; however, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to correct reproducible material errors in accordance with the Addendum Service Levels.

2.7 **Subcontracting.**  
Vendor may use subcontractors for all or part of the Additional Services pursuant to Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement, remaining fully responsible for their acts and omissions.

2.8 **Delivery, Testing and Acceptance.**  
Delivery, testing, and acceptance of Additional Deliverables shall follow the mechanism set forth in Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement, including:
  - Electronic delivery as the default (Section 2.8.1);
  - An acceptance period, typically thirty (30) days, for major Deliverables (Section 2.8.2);
  - Acceptance criteria and deemed acceptance provisions (Section 2.8.3);
  - Rejection and remediation process for non-conforming Deliverables (Section 2.8.4).

Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement (**Late Payment**) applies mutatis mutandis. ## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **21 July 2026** (the **"Addendum Expiration Date"**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
The Addendum may be renewed or extended by mutual written agreement of the Parties. Any renewal will be on terms to be agreed, including scope, pricing, and duration, and shall be documented in a written amendment referencing **ADD-DEL-202403-450** and **PUR-DEL-202403-307**.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience by Client.**  
Client may terminate this Addendum for convenience in whole or in part by providing Vendor with at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**, consistent with Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience by Client) of the Parent Agreement. Client shall remain liable for all fees incurred up to the effective date of termination and for any non-cancellable commitments entered into by Vendor in reliance on this Addendum. Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not entitle Client to a refund of prepaid fees, except where expressly agreed in writing.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience by Vendor.**  
Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience by providing Client with at least ninety (90) days’ prior written notice, consistent with Section 4.4 (Termination for Convenience by Vendor) of the Parent Agreement, and shall cooperate, where reasonably practicable, in providing Transition Assistance akin to Section 4.7.2.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause, in whole or in part, under the conditions outlined in Section 4.5 (Material Breach and Insolvency) of the Parent Agreement, including material breach not cured within thirty (30) days and insolvency events.

4.6 **Termination for Non-Payment.**  
Vendor may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if Client fails to pay any undisputed amount relating to this Addendum within sixty (60) days of its due date and fails to cure such non-payment within ten (10) Business Days, in accordance with Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

4.7.1 **Effect on Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:
  - All licenses and rights granted to Client under this Addendum in respect of the Additional Deliverables shall automatically terminate, except as expressly provided in Article VI and Section 4.7.2 below;
  - Client shall immediately cease use of the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services, except to the extent expressly permitted under any surviving license rights;
  - Client shall pay all outstanding fees and charges due under this Addendum up to the effective date of termination, including any early termination charges expressly agreed in writing.

4.7.2 **Data Export and Transition Assistance.**  
Upon Client’s written request made within thirty (30) days after the effective date of expiration or termination, Vendor shall make commercially reasonable efforts to provide a final export of Client’s data processed in connection with the Additional Deliverables, in a standard industry format, subject to payment of reasonable professional services fees, consistent with Section 4.7.2 and Article XI of the Parent Agreement.

4.7.3 **Termination or Expiration of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date without the need for further action by either Party. In such event:
  - The provisions of this Article IV and any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive (including Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, XVI and payment obligations) shall survive;
  - Any continuing use of the Additional Deliverables or Additional Services by Client shall be subject to a separate written agreement, if agreed by Vendor in its sole discretion.

4.8 **Survival.**  
Sections and Articles of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive expiration or termination (including, without limitation, Articles III, V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XIV, XV, XVI and any accrued but unpaid payment obligations) shall survive expiration or termination of this Addendum.
