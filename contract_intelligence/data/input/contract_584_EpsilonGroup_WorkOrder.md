## ARTICLE II  
# SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Work Order is executed under, and is expressly subject to, the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Article V (Confidentiality)**, **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)**, **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, and **Article XIII (Change Management)**.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict between the terms of the Parent Agreement and the terms of this Work Order, the Parent Agreement shall prevail, **except** where this Work Order expressly states that a particular provision of the Parent Agreement is superseded and references such provision by article or section number.

2.2 **Overview of Services.**  

Epsilon shall provide Contoso with a comprehensive program of consulting and implementation support services focused on **enterprise data and analytics enablement, digital process optimisation, and strategic technology roadmap execution** (collectively, the **“Program”**). The Program shall be structured into the following primary workstreams:

- **Workstream A – Enterprise Data & Analytics Strategy and Implementation**  
- **Workstream B – Digital Process Optimisation & Workflow Redesign**  
- **Workstream C – Enterprise Software Implementation Support & Integration**  
- **Workstream D – Training, Change Management, and Ongoing Advisory Support**

The foregoing Services are within the general categories described in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement (strategic consulting, business process analysis, project and program management, data analytics, implementation support, and training), and this Work Order provides the detailed scope and deliverable framework required by Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Services.**

2.3.1 **Workstream A – Enterprise Data & Analytics Strategy and Implementation.**  
Epsilon shall assist Contoso in designing, validating, and implementing an enterprise-wide data and analytics framework to support Contoso’s software platform operations and decision-making processes. Services shall include, at a minimum:

- Current-state assessment of Contoso’s data architecture, data governance practices, and reporting capabilities, including stakeholder interviews and technical discovery.
- Definition of a target-state data model, including logical and physical data models, semantic layers, and integration patterns.
- Development of a **Data & Analytics Strategy Blueprint** Deliverable, including roadmap, prioritized use cases, and recommended technology stack.
- Design and implementation support for a minimum of three (3) high-priority analytics use cases, including data ingestion workflows, transformation logic, and dashboard/report design.
- Recommendations regarding alignment with Data Protection Laws (in coordination with Article XI of the Parent Agreement) and data minimisation principles.

2.3.2 **Workstream B – Digital Process Optimisation & Workflow Redesign.**  
Epsilon shall provide process analysis and optimisation services for Contoso’s internal processes related to product development, customer onboarding, and operations. Services shall include:

- Documentation of “as-is” processes using standard process mapping techniques.
- Quantitative and qualitative assessment of process performance, bottlenecks, and control gaps.
- Design of “to-be” optimised processes and workflows, including responsibility matrices (RACI), process KPIs, and control points.
- Preparation of **Process Design Documentation** Deliverables for at least five (5) core end-to-end processes.
- Implementation support for pilot rollouts of redesigned processes, including change impact analysis and feedback incorporation.

2.3.3 **Workstream C – Enterprise Software Implementation Support & Integration.**  
In line with Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement regarding implementation support for enterprise software platforms, Epsilon shall provide:

- Integration and configuration advisory support for Contoso’s selected analytics and workflow platforms (including but not limited to data warehouse, BI tools, and workflow engines).
- Development of high-level and low-level integration design documents, including API design, data mapping, and technical specifications.
- Testing support, including test plan development, test case creation, and defect triage for integration components.
- Periodic status reporting and risk/issue tracking, with escalation aligned with Article XIV (Dispute Resolution) of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.4 **Workstream D – Training, Change Management, and Ongoing Advisory Support.**  
Epsilon shall assist Contoso in preparing its workforce for the changes resulting from the Program:

- Creation of **Training Materials** Deliverables, including user guides, quick reference cards, and training slide decks.
- Delivery of at least eight (8) structured training sessions (virtual or in-person as agreed) for relevant Contoso personnel.
- Development of a change management plan, including communications strategy, stakeholder engagement, and adoption metrics.
- Ongoing advisory sessions (monthly or as otherwise agreed) to review performance, identify improvements, and support Contoso’s leadership in decision-making.

2.4 **Deliverables and Milestones.**

2.4.1 **Deliverables.**  
Without limiting the definition of Deliverables in Section 1.1.8 of the Parent Agreement, the key Deliverables under this Work Order shall include, at a minimum:

- **D1:** Data & Analytics Strategy Blueprint (Workstream A)  
- **D2:** Use Case Implementation Design and Configuration Guides (Workstream A)  
- **D3:** “As-Is” Process Maps and Assessment Report (Workstream B)  
- **D4:** “To-Be” Process Design Documentation, including KPIs (Workstream B)  
- **D5:** Integration Architecture & Design Documents (Workstream C)  
- **D6:** Test Plans and Test Reports (Workstream C)  
- **D7:** Training Materials Package (Workstream D)  
- **D8:** Change Management and Communications Plan (Workstream D)  
- **D9:** Monthly Executive Status Reports (all Workstreams)  

2.4.2 **Milestones and Target Dates.**  
Indicative milestones (subject to adjustment via Change Order):

- **M1 – Program Initiation & Discovery Completed:** within sixty (60) days of Effective Date.  
- **M2 – Data & Analytics Strategy Blueprint (D1) Delivered:** within one hundred twenty (120) days of Effective Date.  
- **M3 – Core “To-Be” Process Designs (D4) Approved:** within one hundred fifty (150) days of Effective Date.  
- **M4 – Integration Design Documents (D5) Delivered:** within one hundred eighty (180) days of Effective Date.  
- **M5 – Initial Use Case Implementations & Pilot Processes Operational:** within nine (9) months of Effective Date.  
- **M6 – Completion of Training Sessions & Delivery of Training Materials (D7):** within twelve (12) months of Effective Date.  

Unless expressly stated otherwise in this Work Order, **time shall not be of the essence**, in accordance with Section 2.4.2 of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**  
Acceptance shall follow the procedure in Section 2.4.3 of the Parent Agreement, with the following clarifications:
