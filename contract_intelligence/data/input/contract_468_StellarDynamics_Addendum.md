## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum **adds products and services dated April 02, 2025 (Ref: PUR-STE-202504-389)** and is entered into pursuant to, and shall be read together with, the Parent Agreement. The Parties expressly acknowledge that **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement establishes a master framework under which additional engagements may be defined by SOWs, Orders, or amendments.  

2.1.2 This Addendum shall constitute a **specific, binding engagement** under such framework, and, for the avoidance of doubt, functions as a combined **Order / SOW / Change Order** in relation to the additional Products, Services, and Deliverables described herein, without limiting the Parties’ ability to execute further SOWs or Orders under the Parent Agreement.

2.2 **Overview of Additional Engagement.**  

2.2.1 The Vendor shall, in accordance with the standards set forth in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, supply the Additional Products, perform the Additional Services, and deliver the Additional Deliverables described in this **Article II** and in **Schedules 1–3**.  

2.2.2 The overarching objectives of this Addendum are to:  

  (a) Expand the Company’s **server, storage, and network infrastructure** beyond the baseline design delivered under the Parent Agreement, in order to support increased production workload, geographic redundancy, and enhanced disaster recovery capabilities;  

  (b) Provide additional **software and subscription services**, including advanced monitoring, security analytics, backup, and capacity planning tools, complementing those contemplated by **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (c) Extend the **integration** of the infrastructure and services with the Company’s existing systems (including identity management, ticketing, and monitoring platforms), consistent with **Section 2.2.3** and **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (d) Implement **Enhanced Managed Services** (as defined in Section 1.10) for the Service Expansion Environment, in alignment with and incremental to the service levels defined in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (e) Provide additional **training, knowledge transfer, runbooks, and documentation** tailored to the expanded environment, complementing the Deliverables described in **Sections 2.3.5 and 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Additional Products and Infrastructure Components.**  

2.3.1 The Additional Products to be supplied by the Vendor under this Addendum shall include, without limitation, the following categories (each detailed in **Schedule 1 (Additional Products and Bill of Materials)**):  

  (a) **Compute Resources.** Additional enterprise-grade servers, including high-availability nodes for (i) production cluster expansion, (ii) disaster recovery sites, and (iii) development and testing environments;  

  (b) **Storage Systems.** High-performance storage arrays and backup appliances with integrated deduplication and replication capabilities, engineered to interoperate with the Company’s existing storage infrastructure implemented under the Parent Agreement;  

  (c) **Network and Security.** Core and edge switches, firewalls, and network appliances necessary to support increased bandwidth, segmentation, and secure remote access to the Service Expansion Environment;  

  (d) **Software Licenses and Subscriptions.** Additional licenses and subscription services for virtualization, advanced monitoring, security analytics, and backup platforms, including increments to user counts, CPU/host counts, and data volume tiers; and  

  (e) **Ancillary Components.** Rack infrastructure, cabling, power distribution, and related hardware necessary to install and operate the foregoing components.  

2.3.2 The Vendor shall ensure that all Additional Products are compatible with, and properly integrated into, the environment designed and delivered under the Parent Agreement, including the **Infrastructure Design Documentation** referenced in **Section 2.3.1** thereof.

2.4 **Additional Deliverables.**  

The Vendor shall provide, at a minimum, the following Additional Deliverables, each to satisfy the acceptance process described in **Section 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, as further adapted in **Section 2.8** of this Addendum:

  (a) **Updated Design Documentation.** High-level and low-level design documents reflecting the Service Expansion Environment, including updated network diagrams, capacity planning, security controls, and redundancy requirements;  

  (b) **Configuration and Integration Artifacts.** Configured templates, scripts, APIs, and connectors that enable the Additional Products to integrate with the Company’s identity management system, ticketing platform, monitoring tools, and backup infrastructure;  

  (c) **Testing and Validation Materials.** Test plans, test scripts, and test results demonstrating that the expanded environment meets defined performance, availability, and security requirements, derived from and extending the criteria under the Parent Agreement;  

  (d) **Operational Runbooks and SOPs.** Enhanced standard operating procedures, runbooks, and escalation matrices, specifically tailored to the additional clusters, nodes, and services deployed under this Addendum; and  

  (e) **Training Materials and Sessions.** Additional training content and workshops for the Company’s technical staff, focusing on the new features, capacity management strategies, and incident handling procedures relevant to the Service Expansion Environment.

2.5 **Project Plan and Milestones.**  

2.5.1 The Parties have agreed a **Project Plan (Addendum)** attached as **Schedule 2**, which specifies key tasks, responsibilities, Milestones, and target completion dates, and is intended to supplement the general Project Plan concept in **Section 1.16** and **Section 2.5 (Timelines and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement.  

2.5.2 Unless otherwise explicitly agreed in a Change Order (Addendum):  

  (a) The Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet all Milestone dates, with time being of the essence solely in respect of Milestones expressly designated as “critical” in **Schedule 2**;  

  (b) The Vendor shall promptly notify the Company in writing of any circumstances likely to delay a Milestone, providing a revised timetable and proposed remedial measures, consistent with **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (c) The Company shall provide timely access to relevant systems, facilities, and personnel reasonably required to meet the Milestones, and any delay arising from the Company’s failure to do so shall entitle the Vendor to a reasonable extension of time, in accordance with **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Enhanced Managed Services Scope.**  

2.6.1 The Vendor shall provide **Enhanced Managed Services** for the Service Expansion Environment, including:  

  (a) Continuous monitoring (24x7) of key infrastructure components, including capacity thresholds, performance baselines, and security events;  

  (b) Incident response in accordance with the priority definitions and response times in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Addendum;  

  (c) Proactive capacity management, including monthly utilization reports, trend analysis, and recommendations for scaling, resource re-balancing, and performance optimization; and  

  (d) Quarterly service reviews with the Company’s designated representatives to review Service Level performance, incidents, capacity trends, and planned improvements.  

2.6.2 The Enhanced Managed Services shall be governed by the Service Levels (Addendum) set out in **Article XII**, which shall be read together with **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of any conflict, the more stringent Service Level requirement (for the specific service component in question) shall apply unless expressly stated otherwise.

2.7 **Dependencies and Assumptions.**  

The Parties acknowledge the following dependencies and assumptions, which are material to the scope of this Addendum:

  (a) The baseline infrastructure, as designed and implemented under the Parent Agreement, shall remain materially in place and supported in accordance with that Agreement;  

  (b) The Company shall maintain sufficient licensing for third-party Products that are required to integrate with the Additional Products;  

  (c) The Company will provide appropriate network connectivity, power, and physical access to facilities to enable installation and maintenance of the Additional Products; and  

  (d) Any material deviations from these assumptions shall be addressed via the change management process described in **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

2.8 **Acceptance of Additional Deliverables.**  

2.8.1 The acceptance procedure in **Section 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Additional Deliverables and Milestones under this Addendum, provided that:  

  (a) The Company shall have **fifteen (15) Business Days** (or such other period specified in **Schedule 2**) from receipt of a Deliverable to conduct acceptance tests and notify the Vendor of acceptance or rejection;  

  (b) If rejected, the Vendor shall promptly remedy any non-conformities at no additional charge and re-submit the Deliverable, repeating the acceptance process until acceptance or termination in accordance with **Article IV**; and  

  (c) For avoidance of doubt, the deemed acceptance mechanism in **Section 2.6.4** of the Parent Agreement (reminder plus an additional five (5) Business Days) shall also apply to Deliverables under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total estimated contract value of this Addendum is **USD $2,458,955** (two million four hundred fifty-eight thousand nine hundred fifty-five United States dollars), exclusive of any applicable taxes, unless otherwise expressly stated.  

3.1.2 This amount is **incremental** to the total estimated contract value of **USD $4,351,035** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. For clarity, the aggregate estimated value of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum is **USD $6,810,0‑‑ (approx.)**, subject to actual Orders and approved Change Orders.

3.2 **Fee Structure (Addendum).**  

The Additional Fees under this Addendum shall be composed of the following components:

  (a) **Additional Product Fees.** One-time fees for the Additional Products listed in **Schedule 1**;  

  (b) **Additional Implementation and Integration Fees.** Fixed-price or time-and-materials fees for implementation, migration, and integration Services related to the Service Expansion Environment, as set out in **Schedule 3 (Fee Schedule)**;  

  (c) **Enhanced Managed Services Fees.** Recurring monthly or annual fees for the Enhanced Managed Services defined in **Section 2.6**, based on the capacity and scope of the Service Expansion Environment; and  

  (d) **Additional Training and Consulting Fees.** Fees for extra training sessions, workshops, and advisory services requested by the Company beyond the baseline training contemplated in **Section 2.3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Terms; Invoicing.**  

3.3.1 Notwithstanding **Section 3.3.2 (Net 45 days)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that, for all invoices issued under this Addendum, the payment term shall be **Net 30 days** from the date of invoice. This amendment of payment terms is made solely for this Addendum and shall not, by itself, modify the payment terms applicable to other SOWs or Orders under the Parent Agreement unless expressly stated.  

3.3.2 Unless otherwise specified in **Schedule 3**:  

  (a) The Vendor shall invoice fifty percent (50%) of the Additional Product Fees upon Company’s written acceptance of the corresponding Order for the Additional Products, and the remaining fifty percent (50%) upon delivery of such Products to the Company’s designated site, consistent with **Section 3.3.1 (Product Fees)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) Additional Implementation and Integration Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, based on Milestones achieved or time and materials incurred, as documented in the Project Plan (Addendum) and approved timesheets;  

  (c) Enhanced Managed Services Fees shall be invoiced monthly in advance (unless the Parties agree in writing to annual in-advance billing) and shall be subject to adjustment in accordance with any approved Change Order (Addendum) that alters the scope of the Service Expansion Environment; and  

  (d) Additional Training and Consulting Fees shall be invoiced upon completion of the relevant sessions, or monthly in arrears for time-and-materials engagements.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  

3.4.1 If the Company fails to pay any **undisputed** portion of an invoice by the due date (Net 30), the Vendor may charge interest on such overdue amount at the same rate described in **Section 3.4.1** of the Parent Agreement (two percent (2%) per annum above the Bank of England base rate), calculated daily from the due date until actual payment.  

3.4.2 The notice and suspension rights set out in **Section 3.4.2** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to invoices under this Addendum, provided that the Vendor shall not suspend **critical** services required to ensure business continuity and data integrity.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

The dispute mechanism in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all invoices under this Addendum. The Company shall notify the Vendor of any disputed amount within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of an invoice, pay any undisputed portion on time, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve the dispute promptly.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

The tax provisions in **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement apply to the Additional Fees. The Vendor shall clearly itemize all applicable value-added, sales, use, or similar taxes on invoices issued under this Addendum.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.7.1 The expense reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to Additional Services under this Addendum.  

3.7.2 The Company shall reimburse pre-approved, reasonable, and properly documented out-of-pocket expenses incurred by the Vendor in connection with on-site implementation or consulting services under this Addendum, in accordance with the Company’s travel and expense policy communicated in writing to the Vendor.

3.8 **Set-Off.**  

The set-off provisions in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Addendum. For clarity, the Company shall not set off amounts due under this Addendum against amounts allegedly owed by the Vendor, except as permitted under that section or by law.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall remain in full force and effect until **31 July 2029** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  

4.2.1 The Parent Agreement currently has an Expiration Date of **13 June 2029**, as set forth in **Section 4.1** thereof. The Parties acknowledge that the Term of this Addendum extends slightly beyond such date to **31 July 2029**, solely for the purposes of winding down and finalizing the Additional Services and Enhanced Managed Services.  

4.2.2 The Parties hereby agree that the Parent Agreement is deemed **automatically extended, solely with respect to the rights and obligations necessary to perform and enforce this Addendum**, through the Addendum Expiration Date, unless otherwise mutually agreed in writing or terminated earlier in accordance with **Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum (in whole, or in part at the Company’s option with respect to certain Additional Services or Enhanced Managed Services) for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. This notice period is intended to be consistent with, but does not limit, the termination for convenience rights in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.  

4.3.2 In the event of termination for convenience by the Company:  

  (a) The Company shall pay the Vendor for (i) all Additional Products delivered and accepted, (ii) all Additional Services performed and Additional Deliverables accepted up to the effective date of termination, and (iii) any non-cancellable commitments reasonably and demonstrably incurred by the Vendor in reliance on this Addendum, provided the Vendor uses commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate such commitments, in line with **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (b) Any prepaid Enhanced Managed Services Fees for periods post-termination shall be refunded on a pro-rata basis.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum in whole or in part with immediate effect upon written notice if the other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement as it relates to this Addendum, which is either incapable of remedy or not remedied within thirty (30) days after the non-breaching Party provides written notice specifying the breach and requiring its remedy, consistent with **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement.  

4.4.2 Material breaches giving rise to termination may include, without limitation:  

  (a) The Company’s failure to pay undisputed Additional Fees that remain unpaid for more than sixty (60) days after the due date;  

  (b) The Vendor’s repeated failure to meet critical Service Levels (Addendum) in a manner that constitutes a material breach of the Service Level obligations set out in **Article XII** of this Addendum and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (c) Any material breach of the confidentiality or data protection obligations under **Article V** or **Article XI** of this Addendum or **Articles V and XI** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination Linked to Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate at the same effective date of termination, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain certain obligations (such as wind-down services, data return, or confidentiality obligations) for a defined period.  

4.5.2 Termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in effect with respect to other SOWs, Orders, or engagements, unless otherwise expressly agreed.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**  

Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) All outstanding unpaid invoices relating to this Addendum shall become immediately due and payable, subject to any good-faith disputes in accordance with **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement;  

  (b) The Vendor shall cease performance of the terminated Additional Services and Enhanced Managed Services and deliver to the Company all completed and in-progress Additional Deliverables for which the Company has paid;  

  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information related to this Addendum pursuant to **Section 5.5** of the Parent Agreement, subject to any legal retention requirements; and  

  (d) The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to transition responsibilities, as appropriate, to the Company or a successor service provider, on terms to be agreed, with reasonable fees for any post-termination assistance.

4.7 **Survival.**  

Any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination or expiration (including, without limitation, **Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and Section 4.6**) shall survive, together with the surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement as identified in **Section 4.7** thereof.

  (c) **Priority 3 (Medium).** Response within four (4) hours; target resolution or workaround within two (2) Business Days;  

  (d) **Priority 4 (Low).** Response within one (1) Business Day; resolution scheduled for next planned maintenance cycle.

12.4.2 The Company must notify the Vendor in writing of any claim for service credits within thirty (30) days after the end of the month in which the Service Level failure occurred, consistent with **Section 12.4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

12.5 **Exclusive Monetary Remedy.**
