# AMENDMENT TO PURCHASE AGREEMENT  
**Contract Reference No.: AMD-BET-202404-708**  

This **AMENDMENT** (this “Amendment”), bearing reference number **AMD-BET-202404-708**, is made and entered into as of **June 20, 2024** (the “Amendment Effective Date”), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises**, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Washington, with its principal place of business at **1234 Innovation Way, Seattle, WA 98101, USA** (“Contoso”, the “Client” or the “Company”); and  

- **Beta Ltd**, a limited company organized and existing under the laws of England and Wales, with its registered office at **75 Kingsbridge Road, London EC2M 5TD, United Kingdom** (“Beta”, the “Vendor” or the “Service Provider”).

Contoso and Beta are hereinafter individually referred to as a “Party” and collectively as the “Parties.”

---

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Purchase Agreement**, bearing reference number **PUR-BET-202404-591** and internal identifier **contract_591**, dated **April 21, 2024** (the “Parent Agreement” or “Master Agreement”), pursuant to which the Service Provider agreed to supply, implement, configure, and support an enterprise data analytics and reporting solution (the “Solution”) for the Company, as more fully described in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, under the Parent Agreement, the total **Contract Value** was defined in **Section 1.6** and **Section 3.1** as **Three Million Three Hundred Five Thousand Six Hundred Thirty‑Nine U.S. Dollars (US $3,305,639)**, composed of the fee components set forth in **Section 3.2**, including a One‑Time Implementation Fee (Section 3.2.1), fixed License and Subscription Fees (Section 3.2.2), Support and Maintenance Services fees (Section 3.2.3), and a Contingency and Reserved Services Pool (Section 3.2.4);

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to **modify quantities and pricing** under the Parent Agreement, including adjusting the scope and volume of the Solution and Services as described in **Article II** and the fees set forth in **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, and to reflect a revised total contract value of **US $4,821,900**;

WHEREAS, the Parties further desire to clarify certain commercial terms related to fees, payment, Service Levels, and change management, while affirming that all other terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect, as modified by this Amendment;

WHEREAS, this Amendment is intended to be a written amendment and modification of the Parent Agreement pursuant to **Section 16.4 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement, and is entered into pursuant to, and in strict conformity with, the terms and conditions of **Purchase Agreement reference number PUR-BET-202404-591 (contract_591)**;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual promises, covenants, and undertakings contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.** This Amendment is expressly intended to supplement and modify the Scope of Services/Work set forth in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement. Except to the extent modified herein, all provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 2.3 (Project Phases and Timelines), 2.4 (Performance Standards), 2.5 (Company Responsibilities), and 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)**, shall remain in full force and effect and shall apply equally to the Expansion Scope under this Amendment.

2.2 **Purpose of Expansion.** The Parties acknowledge that the Company’s use of the Solution has expanded beyond the assumptions reflected in the original Contract Value of **US $3,305,639** under **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. In particular, the Company requires:
   (a) increased user counts and analytic capacity to support additional business units and geographies;  
   (b) additional custom integrations with new ERP, CRM, and data warehouse systems beyond those initially identified during **Phase 1 – Initiation and Design** and **Phase 2 – Build and Integration** under **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement; and  
   (c) enhanced reporting, training, and support capabilities to meet operational needs and internal governance requirements.

2.3 **Additional Licenses and Capacity.**  
2.3.1 The Service Provider shall grant to the Company, subject to the license terms and restrictions in **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent Agreement, Additional Licenses consisting of:
   (a) up to an additional 250 named end users of the Solution across North America;  
   (b) increased data ingestion capacity of up to 4 TB of additional monthly processed data; and  
   (c) access to two (2) additional advanced analytics modules, including (i) predictive forecasting; and (ii) anomaly detection and fraud analytics.

2.3.2 The Additional Licenses are strictly subject to the same limitations set forth in **Section 6.3 (License Grant to Company)** and **Section 6.4 (Restrictions)** of the Parent Agreement, including the Company’s obligations not to sublicense, lease, resell, or reverse engineer the Solution.

2.4 **Additional Integrations and Customizations.**  
2.4.1 The Service Provider shall design, develop, configure, test, and deploy additional custom integrations as part of the Deliverables under this Amendment, including:
   (a) integration with an additional ERP instance deployed in Canada;  
   (b) integration with a new CRM platform used by the Company’s commercial division; and  
   (c) synchronization with a secondary internal data warehouse used by the Company’s finance department.

2.4.2 The Service Provider shall also provide additional configuration and customization services consistent with **Section 2.2.3 (Configuration and Customization)** of the Parent Agreement, including:
   (a) at least three (3) additional executive-level dashboards tailored to new business segments;  
   (b) at least eight (8) additional operational dashboards; and  
   (c) at least fifteen (15) additional standard or custom reports, as documented in updates to the Project Plan.

2.5 **Enhanced Support Services.**  
2.5.1 The Service Provider shall continue to provide support and maintenance services as set forth in **Section 2.2.6 (Support and Maintenance)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement and, in addition, shall provide Enhanced Support Services consisting of:
   (a) extended support hours to include 24x7 coverage for Severity 1 incidents as defined in **Section 12.3** of the Parent Agreement;  
   (b) quarterly service review meetings with the Company’s designated representatives; and  
   (c) a dedicated technical account manager to coordinate escalations and oversee service quality.

2.5.2 The Enhanced Support Services shall be subject to the Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, as modified in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.6 **Timelines and Project Phases for Expansion.**  
2.6.1 The Parties agree that the Expansion Scope shall be implemented through additional phases that align with the framework set forth in **Section 2.3 (Project Phases and Timelines)** of the Parent Agreement, as follows:
   (a) **Expansion Design Phase (Estimated 4–6 Weeks):** Finalization of revised Project Plan; identification of new data sources and integration requirements; definition of new dashboards and reports.  
   (b) **Expansion Build and Integration Phase (Estimated 8–12 Weeks):** Development, configuration, and testing of Additional Licenses provisioning, new integrations, and customizations.  
   (c) **Expansion UAT and Go‑Live Phase (Estimated 4–6 Weeks):** User acceptance testing of the expanded Solution, followed by staged deployment and post‑go‑live stabilization.

2.6.2 The Company shall fulfill all responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.5 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement in connection with the Expansion Scope, including timely provision of data, access, and approvals.

2.7 **Performance Standards and Acceptance.**  
2.7.1 The Service Provider shall perform the Services under this Amendment in accordance with the performance standards in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, i.e., in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with industry standards, and in conformity with the mutually agreed Specifications and Project Plan.

2.7.2 Acceptance of Deliverables under this Amendment shall follow the process in **Section 2.6 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement. Each new or modified Deliverable shall be deemed accepted upon the earliest of:  
   (a) the Company’s written acceptance; or  
   (b) fifteen (15) Business Days after delivery, provided no written notice of material non‑conformance is given within such period.

2.8 **No Diminution of Existing Obligations.** Nothing in this Amendment shall be construed to reduce or limit any commitments, warranties, Service Levels, or other obligations of the Service Provider under the Parent Agreement, except to the extent expressly modified herein, and the Service Provider remains obligated to provide all Deliverables and Services described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, as expanded by this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Adjusted Contract Value.**  
3.1.1 The Parties agree that, in lieu of the original Contract Value of **US $3,305,639** set forth in **Section 1.6 and Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement, the total contract value for the Parent Agreement as amended by this Amendment shall be **US $4,821,900** (the “Adjusted Contract Value”), exclusive of Taxes.

3.1.2 The Adjusted Contract Value includes:  
   (a) all original fees set forth in **Section 3.2** of the Parent Agreement; plus  
   (b) incremental fees for Additional Licenses, Expansion Scope services, Enhanced Support Services, and extended term as described in this Article III.

3.2 **Fee Components under this Amendment.**  
In addition to the fee components described in **Section 3.2** of the Parent Agreement, the following incremental components are hereby added:

3.2.1 **Additional Implementation and Integration Fees.** An additional **US $600,000** for expansion design, implementation, integration, configuration, and testing activities described in **Article II** of this Amendment. Such additional implementation fee shall be invoiced as follows:  
   (a) 40% (US $240,000) upon the Amendment Effective Date;  
   (b) 30% (US $180,000) upon completion of the Expansion Build and Integration Phase, as reasonably determined by the Service Provider; and  
   (c) 30% (US $180,000) upon successful go‑live of the Expansion Scope in production.

3.2.2 **Additional License and Subscription Fees.** Additional license and subscription fees totaling **US $750,000** for the Additional Licenses and advanced analytics modules described in **Section 2.3**, payable in equal **annual installments** over the remaining Term and Extension Term. These fees are incremental to the fixed License and Subscription Fees of **US $1,600,000** specified in **Section 3.2.2** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2.3 **Enhanced Support and Maintenance Fees.** Incremental Enhanced Support Services fees of **US $166,261** for the extended support and technical account management described in **Section 2.5**, payable in equal annual installments over the remaining Term and Extension Term, in addition to the **US $300,000** support and maintenance fees in **Section 3.2.3** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2.4 **Revised Contingency and Reserved Services Pool.** The Contingency and Reserved Services Pool described in **Section 3.2.4** of the Parent Agreement is increased from **US $155,639** to **US $255,639**, to support Expansion Scope change requests and optional services, to be consumed and invoiced in accordance with **Section 3.3.4** and **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2.5 **Total Incremental Amount.** The incremental fees described in Sections 3.2.1 through 3.2.4, together with the original Contract Value, yield the Adjusted Contract Value of **US $4,821,900**.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  
3.3.1 Except as expressly modified in this Amendment, **Section 3.3 (Invoicing and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement shall remain in full force and effect and shall apply to all fees under this Amendment.

3.3.2 All fees due under this Amendment shall be payable in accordance with the **Net 45** payment terms set forth in **Section 3.3.5 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, i.e., within forty‑five (45) days from the date of the applicable invoice.

3.3.3 Reserved services and any additional Services requested pursuant to the Change Order process under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, based on actual hours incurred at the rate card set forth in **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement, as may be updated by mutual written agreement.

3.4 **Late Payment; Suspension.** The provisions of **Section 3.5 (Late Payment; Suspension)** and **Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall govern any late payments or invoice disputes relating to fees under this Amendment.

3.5 **Taxes; Expenses.**  
3.5.1 All fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.5.2 Reimbursement of reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Expansion Scope shall be governed by **Section 3.8 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Extended Term; Alignment with Expiration Date.**  
4.1.1 Notwithstanding **Section 1.17 (Term)** and **Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that the overall Term of the Parent Agreement, as amended hereby, shall be extended so that it continues in full force and effect until **February 26, 2029** (the “Extended Expiration Date”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with the Parent Agreement or this Amendment.

4.1.2 The Extension Term created by this Amendment is intended to align the revised commercial structure and Adjusted Contract Value with the Company’s extended usage requirements for the Solution.

4.2 **Renewal.** The automatic renewal provisions of **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Agreement as amended, such that, absent timely notice of non‑renewal, the Agreement may renew for successive one-year periods following the Extended Expiration Date, subject to fee adjustments as permitted under the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client‑Friendly Adjustment).**  
4.3.1 The Parties agree that, solely with respect to this Amendment and the incremental fees hereunder, **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience (Favoring Vendor))** of the Parent Agreement is modified as follows:

   (a) The Company may terminate the Expansion Scope and the incremental fees introduced by this Amendment, in whole or in part, for convenience upon at least **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to the Service Provider, without affecting the Company’s rights with respect to the unmodified portion of the Parent Agreement, unless the Company expressly elects to terminate the Agreement in its entirety.  

   (b) In the event of such termination for convenience by the Company of only the incremental scope and fees under this Amendment, the early termination charge described in **Section 4.6(d)** of the Parent Agreement shall not apply to the incremental license and support fees attributable solely to this Amendment. For the avoidance of doubt, the early termination charge in **Section 4.6(d)** shall continue to apply with respect to fees under the Parent Agreement that are not associated with this Amendment.

4.3.2 The Service Provider’s right to terminate for convenience as set forth in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement remains unchanged and applies equally to this Amendment.

4.4 **Termination for Cause and Non‑Payment.**  
4.4.1 The provisions of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** and **Section 4.5 (Termination for Non‑Payment)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. A material breach of this Amendment shall constitute a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa.

4.5 **Effect of Termination.**  
4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate, and all Additional Licenses, Expansion Scope Services, and Enhanced Support Services shall cease, subject to any rights or obligations that expressly survive.  

4.5.2 The effect of termination provisions in **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement, including payment of accrued fees and early termination charges (as applicable), shall apply to the Adjusted Contract Value, except as modified in **Section 4.3.1(b)** of this Amendment.

4.6 **Transition Assistance.** The transition assistance obligations described in **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to the expanded Solution and Company Data processed under this Amendment, on the same terms and conditions.

## ARTICLE IX  
## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.2 **Aggregate Cap.**  
For any and all claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment (excluding claims arising solely under the unmodified portions of the Parent Agreement), each Party’s aggregate liability to the other Party, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), or otherwise, shall be capped at an amount equal to **two (2) times the fees actually paid or payable by the Company to the Service Provider under this Amendment during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**.

9.3 **Exclusion of Consequential Damages (Amendment‑Specific).**  
With respect to this Amendment only, neither Party shall be liable to the other for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, exemplary, or punitive damages (including loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of data, or business interruption), whether in contract, tort, or otherwise, even if such Party has been advised of the possibility of such damages, except to the extent such limitation is prohibited by applicable law.

9.4 **Exceptions.**  
The limitations set forth in this Article IX shall not apply to:  
   (a) a Party’s liability for death or personal injury caused by its negligence;  
   (b) a Party’s fraud or fraudulent misrepresentation;  
   (c) the Company’s payment obligations for fees due under this Amendment; or  
   (d) a Party’s indemnification obligations under **Article VIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, to the extent such liabilities cannot be limited under applicable law.

9.5 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
For clarity, the limitations on liability and exclusion of consequential damages in this Article IX apply solely to claims arising from or relating to this Amendment and its incremental scope and fees. The original risk allocation set forth in **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement, including the lack of a contractual cap and inclusion of consequential damages, shall continue to apply to claims arising solely under the unmodified provisions of the Parent Agreement.
