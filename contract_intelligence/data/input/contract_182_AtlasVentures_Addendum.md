## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW and Parent Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed **under and subject to** the terms and conditions of the Parent SOW and the Parent Agreement. In accordance with **Section 2.1.1 and Section 2.1.2 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 2.2.2 and Section 16.11 of the Parent Agreement**, this Addendum: (a) references the Parent SOW (Ref. **SOW-ATL-202507-175**, contract_175); (b) is deemed incorporated into and governed by the Parent SOW and the Parent Agreement; and (c) shall be subordinate to the Parent Agreement in the event of any conflict, except where this Addendum expressly states that it overrides a specific provision of the Parent SOW or Parent Agreement with respect to the Addendum Services.

2.1.2 For avoidance of doubt, this Addendum **supplements the Statement of Work dated March 23, 2026 (Ref: SOW-ATL-202507-175)** by extending the Program beyond the original SOW Term and by adding new Services, Deliverables, and fees. Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, all terms of the Parent SOW remain in full force and effect and apply to the Addendum Services.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Services.**

2.2.1 Under this Addendum, the Service Provider shall provide an extended suite of consulting, implementation, and managed services designed to build upon the Deliverables and Program Phases described in **Article II of the Parent SOW**, including:

- Post-implementation enablement and adoption services following Phase 4 – Optimization, Stabilization & Transition of the Parent SOW (Section 2.3.4);
- Implementation of advanced data and analytics capabilities, augmenting the **Data & Analytics Blueprint** Deliverable described in **Section 2.4.6 of the Parent SOW**;
- Establishment of an ongoing **Managed Services Phase** for governance, analytics operations, reporting, and continuous improvement; and
- Integration of the transformation outcomes into Contoso’s broader enterprise strategy and operations.

2.2.2 The Addendum Services are consistent with and extend the categories listed in **Sections 2.3.1 through 2.3.5 of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 2.2 of the Parent SOW**, including strategic advisory, operational consulting, project management, implementation support, knowledge transfer, and training.

2.3 **Addendum Phases.**  

The Addendum Services shall be organized into four (4) major phases, which are an extension of and addition to the Program Phases defined in **Section 2.3 of the Parent SOW**:

2.3.1 **Phase A – Post-Transformation Enablement (Months 1–6 from Addendum Effective Date).**  

(a) **Objectives.** Enable sustained adoption of the new operating models, processes, and tools delivered under Phases 2–4 of the Parent SOW, including reinforcement of change management and embedding of Program Artifacts into daily operations.  

(b) **Key Activities.**  
- Conduct enablement workshops with at least three (3) functional groups previously identified under **Section 2.4.8 of the Parent SOW** (e.g., Sales, Operations, IT);  
- Refine training curricula and materials delivered under Phase 3 of the Parent SOW to incorporate feedback from early adopters;  
- Provide coaching to Contoso’s internal champions, extending the knowledge transfer contemplated in **Section 2.3.4(d) and Section 2.3.4(e) of the Parent SOW**;  
- Update the **Program Governance Handbook** (Section 2.4.5 of the Parent SOW) to reflect post-launch governance structures; and  
- Align enablement activities with the **Benefit Realization Tracking Framework** (Section 2.4.9 of the Parent SOW).

(c) **Deliverables.**  
- Updated Training Materials and Playbooks;  
- Enablement Workshop Decks and Recordings;  
- Revised Program Governance Handbook (Post-Transformation Edition);  
- Updated Benefit Realization Tracking Templates.

2.3.2 **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Implementation (Months 4–18).**  

(a) **Objectives.** Operationalize and implement advanced analytics and reporting capabilities envisioned in the **Data & Analytics Blueprint** (Section 2.4.6 of the Parent SOW), including integration of data sources, dashboards, and predictive models.

(b) **Key Activities.**  
- Finalize technical design of data models and analytics architecture, consistent with the transformation roadmap defined under **Section 2.3.2 of the Parent SOW**;  
- Implement data pipelines, ETL/ELT processes, and integration with core systems identified in the technology assessment under **Section 2.3.1(c) of the Parent SOW**;  
- Develop and deploy executive dashboards aligned with the **Program KPIs** defined in **Section 2.7 of the Parent SOW**;  
- Configure metric definitions to align with process cycle time, conversion rates, and adoption rates;  
- Pilot predictive analytics use cases for GTM performance and operational optimization; and  
- Provide technical and business user training for analytics tools.

(c) **Deliverables.**  
- Data Integration Design and Data Dictionary;  
- Configured Data Models and ETL/ELT Jobs;  
- At least six (6) Executive and Operational Dashboards;  
- Predictive Analytics Use Case Specifications and Models;  
- Analytics User Training Materials and Runbooks.

2.3.3 **Phase C – Optimization and Continuous Improvement (Months 19–30).**  

(a) **Objectives.** Ensure sustained performance of the transformed processes and analytics capabilities, refine KPIs, and drive continuous improvement consistent with the **Optimization Recommendations Report** and **Final Program Report** referenced in **Section 2.3.4 of the Parent SOW**.

(b) **Key Activities.**  
- Quarterly performance reviews analyzing Program KPIs and newly implemented analytics;  
- Process refinement workshops with functional stakeholders;  
- Backlog management for enhancement requests;  
- Implementation of prioritized enhancements to processes, dashboards, and training materials;  
- Update of the Benefit Realization Tracking Framework based on actual performance data.

(c) **Deliverables.**  
- Quarterly Performance Review Reports;  
- Updated Process Documentation and SOPs;  
- Enhancement Release Notes and Configuration Documents;  
- Updated Benefit Realization Tracking Framework Reports.

2.3.4 **Phase D – Managed Services Phase (Remainder of Addendum Term).**  

(a) **Objectives.** Provide ongoing managed services for analytics operations, performance monitoring, and limited operational support for transformed processes, subject to the Service Levels in **Article XII**.

(b) **Key Activities.**  
- Monitoring and maintenance of analytics pipelines and dashboards;  
- Handling Support Tickets for issues, enhancements, and user questions;  
- Periodic data quality checks and remediation;  
- Limited configuration updates aligned with agreed change windows;  
- Ongoing advisory sessions (e.g., monthly) with Contoso’s leadership to align analytics and process insights with strategic objectives.

(c) **Deliverables.**  
- Monthly Managed Services Reports (including SLA metrics);  
- Ticket Logs and Resolutions;  
- Data Quality Assessment Reports;  
- Quarterly Advisory Briefings.

2.4 **Addendum Deliverables.**  

The Addendum Deliverables described in Sections 2.3.1–2.3.4 above are in addition to the Deliverables specified in **Section 2.4 of the Parent SOW**. All such Addendum Deliverables shall be subject to the acceptance procedures set forth in **Section 2.10 of the Parent SOW**, including Contoso’s right to review and accept or reject Deliverables with written notice of material non-conformities.

2.5 **Timelines and Dependencies.**  

2.5.1 The Addendum Phases described above are expected to commence and complete within the Addendum Term, with approximate relative durations as indicated. The start and end dates of each Phase may be adjusted by mutual written agreement, or via Change Order, consistent with **Section 2.5.3 of the Parent SOW** and **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement**.

2.5.2 The Parties acknowledge that timely completion of Addendum Deliverables is dependent upon Contoso fulfilling its responsibilities under **Section 2.9 of the Parent SOW** (Company Responsibilities), including timely access to information, systems, and personnel, and timely feedback on Deliverables.

2.6 **Performance Standards and Personnel.**  

2.6.1 Atlas shall perform the Addendum Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with the standards set forth in **Section 2.6.1 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 2.5.1 of the Parent Agreement**, and with the practices of a reputable management consulting and managed services provider.

2.6.2 Atlas shall assign personnel with appropriate skills and experience. Replacement of key personnel assigned under this Addendum shall follow the process and approval requirements described in **Section 2.6.2 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 2.5.2 of the Parent Agreement**.

2.7 **Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables.**  

Acceptance shall follow **Section 2.10 of the Parent SOW**, with the same review periods and correction rights. Any deviations (e.g., extended review periods for complex analytics Deliverables) shall be mutually agreed in writing and specified in the relevant Milestone description.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**

3.1.1 The total contract value for this Addendum is **US $3,059,996** (the “Addendum Fees”). This amount is **incremental to and separate from** the **US $4,152,445** contract value defined as SOW Fees in **Section 3.1.1 of the Parent SOW** and the **US $133,135** contract value referenced in **Section 3.1 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.1.2 The Addendum Fees shall consist of the following components:

(a) Fixed Fees for Enablement (Phase A) and Analytics Implementation (Phase B);  
(b) Mixed Fixed and Time & Materials (“T&M”) Fees for Optimization (Phase C); and  
(c) Recurring Managed Services Fees for Phase D, subject to an annual not-to-exceed amount.

3.2 **Fee Schedule and Milestones.**

3.2.1 **Phase A – Post-Transformation Enablement (Fixed Fee: US $450,000).**  
- Milestone A.1 – Completion of initial enablement workshops and delivery of updated training materials: **US $150,000**;  
- Milestone A.2 – Delivery of revised Program Governance Handbook and enablement coaching sessions: **US $150,000**;  
- Milestone A.3 – Delivery of updated Benefit Realization Tracking Templates and final enablement summary report: **US $150,000**.

3.2.2 **Phase B – Advanced Analytics Implementation (Fixed Fee: US $1,250,000).**  
- Milestone B.1 – Delivery of Data Integration Design and initial data model configuration: **US $300,000**;  
- Milestone B.2 – Deployment of at least three (3) Executive Dashboards and pilot ETL/ELT processes: **US $350,000**;  
- Milestone B.3 – Deployment of full set of dashboards, predictive models for at least two (2) use cases, and completion of analytics training: **US $400,000**;  
- Milestone B.4 – Stabilization period close-out and acceptance of analytics platform and documentation: **US $200,000**.

3.2.3 **Phase C – Optimization and Continuous Improvement (Mixed: Approx. US $750,000).**  
- Fixed components for quarterly reviews, updated process documentation, and enhancement releases: **US $400,000** (payable in equal quarterly installments over the Phase C period);  
- T&M components for additional enhancement work, advisory sessions, and ad hoc support: not to exceed **US $350,000**, billed monthly in arrears at the then-current rate card mutually agreed in writing and attached to this Addendum as **Schedule A-1** (which supplements the rate card referenced in **Section 3.2.3 of the Parent SOW**).

3.2.4 **Phase D – Managed Services Phase (Recurring Fees: Approx. US $609,996).**  
- Monthly Managed Services Fee: **US $12,708.25**, payable for the Managed Services period, such that the aggregate Phase D fees do not exceed **US $609,996** over the Addendum Term, unless increased by a mutually executed Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII** of this Addendum and **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement**.

3.2.5 **Not-to-Exceed Amount.**  
The aggregate Fixed Fees, T&M Fees, and Managed Services Fees payable under this Addendum shall not exceed **US $3,059,996** without a mutually executed Change Order, consistent with the not-to-exceed approach stated in **Section 3.2.5 of the Parent SOW**.

3.3 **Payment Terms; Net 30.**

3.3.1 **Net 30.**  
Consistent with **Section 3.3.1 of the Parent SOW**, and notwithstanding **Section 3.3.1 (Net 45)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties agree that all undisputed invoices issued under this Addendum shall be payable within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of receipt by Contoso of a valid invoice (“Net 30”).

3.3.2 **Invoicing.**  
Atlas shall invoice Contoso as follows:

(a) Fixed-Fee components (Phases A and B, and fixed part of Phase C) upon completion and accepted delivery (or deemed acceptance) of the corresponding Milestones or scheduled installments;  
(b) T&M components (Phase C) monthly in arrears, with detailed time entries, consistent with **Section 3.3.2(b) of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.2.1 of the Parent Agreement**;  
(c) Managed Services Fees (Phase D) monthly in advance, unless otherwise agreed in writing; and  
(d) Reimbursable expenses in accordance with **Section 3.5 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement**, as further addressed in Section 3.4 below.

3.3.3 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Contoso may dispute invoices in good faith in accordance with **Section 3.3.3 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.5.2 of the Parent Agreement**. Contoso shall pay any undisputed portion in accordance with Section 3.3.1, and the Parties shall cooperate in resolving disputes promptly.

3.4 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.4.1 Subject to Contoso’s prior written approval (including by email) and the Company’s then-current T&E policies (provided in writing), Contoso shall reimburse Atlas for reasonable, necessary, and pre-approved travel and out-of-pocket expenses incurred in performing the Addendum Services, consistent with **Section 3.5.1 of the Parent SOW** and **Sections 3.4.1–3.4.2 of the Parent Agreement**.

3.4.2 Atlas shall submit expense documentation concurrently with the applicable invoice, including receipts and itemized descriptions, in accordance with **Section 3.5.2 of the Parent SOW**.

3.5 **Late Payment.**  

Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest beginning on the day after the due date at the rate set forth in **Section 3.4 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.5.1 of the Parent Agreement** (1% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is lower), until paid in full.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement**. Atlas shall separately state any applicable sales, use, or similar taxes on its invoices. Contoso shall be responsible for such taxes (excluding taxes on Atlas’s net income).

3.7 **No Set-Off.**  

Subject to applicable law and **Section 3.7 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement**, neither Party may set off amounts owed under this Addendum against amounts owed under other agreements, except as expressly agreed in writing.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (February 27, 2027)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **February 21, 2031**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW Term.**  

4.2.1 The Parent SOW’s SOW Term (March 23, 2026 to January 17, 2028, as set forth in **Section 1.1.7 of the Parent SOW**) overlaps with and precedes portions of the Addendum Term. During any period in which both the Parent SOW and this Addendum are in effect, the Addendum Services shall be treated as an extension of the Program described in the Parent SOW.

4.2.2 Upon expiration of the Parent SOW, the Addendum shall continue in force for its remaining Term, and, for purposes of governing the Addendum Services, references in the Parent SOW to the “SOW Term” shall be deemed to include the Addendum Term, solely to the extent necessary to interpret ongoing rights and obligations hereunder.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **forty-five (45) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. This notice period is intended to reflect a **Medium** risk allocation and is intermediate between the SOW-specific thirty (30) day notice in **Section 4.3.1 of the Parent SOW** and the default provisions of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.2 During the notice period, the Parties shall cooperate to wind down or transition the Addendum Services in an orderly manner. Contoso shall remain responsible for payment of (i) all Fees for Addendum Services performed, (ii) approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, and (iii) any Transition Services requested by Contoso, consistent with **Section 4.3.2 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement**, including without limitation for a material breach of this Addendum that remains uncured thirty (30) days after written notice specifying the breach. For recurring SLA failures under the Managed Services Phase, the cure and termination rights in **Section 12.4** of this Addendum shall also apply.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Transition Services.**  

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) Atlas shall cease performance of Addendum Services (except for any Transition Services requested under Section 4.5.2);  
(b) Contoso shall pay Atlas all undisputed Addendum Fees and approved expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.5.1(b) of the Parent SOW** and **Section 4.5.1(b) of the Parent Agreement**; and  
(c) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information relating to this Addendum in accordance with **Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement**, subject to legal retention requirements.

4.5.2 At Contoso’s written request, Atlas shall provide Transition Services (e.g., knowledge transfer, documentation handover, limited support to a successor provider) for up to sixty (60) days after termination or expiration. Transition Services shall be provided on a T&M basis at the rates set forth in Schedule A-1 (or as otherwise agreed) and shall be subject to the same confidentiality and IP provisions applicable during the Addendum Term.

4.6 **Termination of Parent Agreement or Parent SOW.**  

4.6.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety under **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination, unless otherwise agreed in writing by the Parties.

4.6.2 If only the Parent SOW (SOW-ATL-202507-175 / contract_175) is terminated while the Parent Agreement remains in effect, this Addendum may continue in effect solely with respect to the Addendum Services if expressly agreed in writing by the Parties, in which case the Parent Agreement shall continue to govern this Addendum in all respects.

4.6.3 In any such termination scenario, the surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement and Parent SOW referenced in **Section 16.10 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 4.7 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Addendum, including payment obligations, confidentiality, IP, indemnification, and limitations of liability.
