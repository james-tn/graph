## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

(a) This Amendment modifies lease terms dated **November 25, 2025 (Ref: LSE-EPS-202511-377)** under the Parent Agreement and is entered into pursuant to and in accordance with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** thereof.  

(b) Except as expressly set forth in this Amendment, all provisions of **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and continue to govern the Leased Assets and Services. This Amendment specifically (i) expands the scope of Leased Assets, (ii) enhances certain Services, and (iii) aligns Service Levels and term with Contoso’s updated operational requirements during the Amended Term.

2.2 **Description of Amendment Services.**  

(a) **General Description.** During the Amended Term, Epsilon shall continue to lease to Contoso the Leased Assets covered by the Parent Agreement and shall additionally provide the **Additional Leased Assets** and **Enhanced Services** specified in this Amendment and any Amendment SOWs. Such Amendment Services shall be provided in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) **Additional Leased Assets.** Without limiting **Section 1.7** above, the Additional Leased Assets may include, without limitation:

  (i) Additional information technology hardware (servers, high-density storage arrays, network routers/switches, and secure printing devices) to support expansion of Contoso facilities;  

  (ii) Enhanced ergonomic office fixtures and furniture (adjustable desks, ergonomic chairs, collaborative workstations, conference room audio-visual systems) for new and existing Contoso locations;  

  (iii) Supplemental specialized machinery or testing devices as may be described in one or more Amendment SOWs to support Contoso’s increased production capacity;  

  (iv) Ancillary components and accessories (uninterruptible power supplies, server racks, structured cabling, KVM switches, mounting hardware, and related accessories).

(c) **Enhanced Services.** Epsilon shall provide the following Enhanced Services in addition to the Services under **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement:

  (i) **Proactive Monitoring and Diagnostics.** Continuous remote monitoring of critical Leased Assets and Additional Leased Assets, including proactive detection of hardware failures, capacity thresholds, and environmental conditions.  

  (ii) **Extended Support Hours.** For designated critical assets, Epsilon shall offer 24x7 incident intake and triage, with technical support available in accordance with the response times set out in **Article XII** of this Amendment.  

  (iii) **Lifecycle Management.** Coordination of planned refresh, replacement, or upgrade of Leased Assets and Additional Leased Assets during the Amended Term, including advance planning and scheduling with Contoso’s designated contact.  

  (iv) **Enhanced Reporting.** Quarterly asset and performance reports, including age, utilization, incident statistics, and recommended lifecycle actions, in addition to any reporting obligations under **Section 2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Deliverables.**  

(a) Epsilon shall deliver to Contoso, as part of the Amendment Services, the following **Deliverables** (as defined in **Section 1.5** of the Parent Agreement), to the extent applicable:

  (i) Updated asset inventory records reflecting all Additional Leased Assets, including serial numbers, asset tags, locations, and lease start/end dates;  

  (ii) Installation and configuration documentation for newly deployed hardware, including network diagrams, configuration baselines, and integration notes;  

  (iii) Service and maintenance logs, including incident tickets, resolution details, and preventive maintenance activities performed;  

  (iv) Quarterly performance reports and lifecycle recommendations as described in **Section 2.2(c)(iv)** above.

(b) All Deliverables shall be provided in electronic form unless otherwise agreed in writing and shall constitute “Deliverables” under **Section 1.5** and “Work Product” under **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement, subject to the ownership and license provisions thereof and of **Article VI** of this Amendment.

2.4 **Timelines and Deployment.**  

(a) Epsilon shall deploy and make ready for use the Additional Leased Assets in accordance with deployment schedules set forth in the applicable Amendment SOWs, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Timelines and Delays)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) Time shall not be of the essence unless a particular milestone is explicitly identified as “critical” in an Amendment SOW; in that case, the remedies set forth in **Section 2.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XII** of this Amendment shall apply.  

(c) Epsilon shall notify Contoso promptly in writing of any anticipated material delays and shall propose revised schedules, mitigation steps, or temporary workarounds, consistent with **Section 2.5(b)–(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  

(a) Epsilon shall perform all Amendment Services in accordance with:

  (i) The performance standards set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (ii) The **Amendment SLAs** set forth in **Article XII** of this Amendment, which, for the covered assets and services, shall supplement and, where expressly stated, supersede the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) For avoidance of doubt, failure to meet the Amendment SLAs consistently may constitute “chronic failure” under **Section 12.5** of the Parent Agreement and shall be deemed a material breach under **Section 4.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement, subject to applicable cure periods.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities.**  

In addition to the responsibilities set out in **Section 2.7 (Contoso Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

(a) Provide timely access to new or expanded Contoso sites for delivery, installation, and maintenance of Additional Leased Assets;

(b) Designate a primary technical and operational contact for coordination of lifecycle management and quarterly performance reviews;

(c) Ensure that network, power, and environmental conditions at its facilities meet the minimum requirements specified by Epsilon or the equipment manufacturer, and promptly remediate any identified deficiencies; and  

(d) Promptly notify Epsilon of any anticipated changes in facility utilization or capacity needs that may materially affect the Amendment Services.

2.7 **SOWs Under Amendment.**  

(a) Each Amendment SOW shall, at a minimum, specify: (i) the Additional Leased Assets to be provided; (ii) the Enhanced Services to be performed; (iii) the lease period for the applicable assets (if different from the Amended Term); (iv) the Amendment Fees and payment schedule; (v) the locations of the Additional Leased Assets; and (vi) any special terms or deviations from this Amendment or the Parent Agreement.  

(b) In the event of a conflict between this Amendment and an Amendment SOW, this Amendment shall control unless such Amendment SOW expressly states that it is intended to supersede a specific provision of this Amendment, in which case the Parent Agreement’s **Section 2.1** conflict hierarchy remains applicable.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  

(a) The aggregate maximum contract value attributable solely to this Amendment (the “**Amendment Contract Value**”) is **US$3,502,437**, subject to adjustment by mutual written agreement of the Parties through executed Amendment SOWs.  

(b) The Parties acknowledge that the **Parent Contract Value** under **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement is **US$4,044,494**. Unless otherwise agreed in writing, the Amendment Contract Value is **in addition to** and does **not reduce or replace** the Parent Contract Value; accordingly, the combined potential exposure for total Fees under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, if fully utilized, may equal up to **US$7,546,931**.

3.2 **Fee Structure for Amendment.**  

(a) **Recurring Lease Fees.** Recurring lease fees for Additional Leased Assets shall be specified in the applicable Amendment SOW and shall generally be calculated on a monthly basis, reflective of asset type, value, and term. These recurring Fees constitute “Fees” under **Section 1.9** of the Parent Agreement.  

(b) **Service and Maintenance Fees.** Fees for Enhanced Services (e.g., proactive monitoring, extended support, lifecycle management, and enhanced reporting) shall be set forth in the applicable Amendment SOW and may be structured as (i) fixed monthly service fees, (ii) time and materials at agreed hourly rates, or (iii) a combination thereof.  

(c) **Usage-Based Fees.** If certain Amendment Services are usage-based (e.g., per-page printing, per-hour machinery runtime, or per-ticket premium support), such metrics and associated rates shall be defined in the Amendment SOW, consistent with **Section 3.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Payment Terms (Modification of Section 3.3(b) – Payment Terms).**  

Notwithstanding **Section 3.3(b) (Net 45)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that, solely with respect to Amendment Fees under this Amendment:

(a) **Invoicing.** Epsilon shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the schedule in the applicable Amendment SOW.

(b) **Net 30.** Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of Epsilon’s invoice (“**Net 30**”). Accordingly, for Fees governed by this Amendment, the “Net 45” term in **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby superseded and replaced with **Net 30**.

(c) **Method of Payment.** Payment methods shall remain as set forth in **Section 3.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

(a) The interest and suspension rights described in **Section 3.4 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to Amendment Fees, except that references to “undisputed amounts not paid when due” shall be construed with reference to the Net 30 term established herein.  

(b) Epsilon may, in accordance with **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement and after providing written notice and cure opportunities, suspend Amendment Services and access to Additional Leased Assets for failure to pay undisputed Amendment Fees.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

The procedures set forth in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to any disputed Amendment Fees, except that the 15-day dispute notice window shall be measured from the invoice date of any invoice issued under this Amendment or an Amendment SOW.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

Taxes payable with respect to the Amendment Fees shall be governed by **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement. For clarity, all Amendment Fees are exclusive of Taxes, which Contoso shall bear as applicable.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

Any reimbursable costs and expenses reasonably incurred by Epsilon in connection with Amendment Services shall be subject to **Section 3.7 (Expenses and Reimbursement)** of the Parent Agreement, unless an Amendment SOW provides different or more specific reimbursement terms.

3.8 **Set-Off.**  

The set-off restriction in **Section 3.8 (Set-Off)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to Amendment Fees, unless the Parties expressly agree in an Amendment SOW to a limited set-off right with respect to specific service credits or other agreed adjustments.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amended Term (Modification of Section 4.1 – Term).**  

(a) The overall Term of the Parent Agreement, as defined in **Section 1.18** and **Section 4.1**, remains effective until **May 10, 2027**, unless earlier terminated or extended.  

(b) For the Amendment Services and Additional Leased Assets covered by this Amendment, the Parties agree that the lease and service period shall commence on the **Amendment Effective Date** (**October 18, 2026**) and continue until the **Amendment Expiration Date** (**February 03, 2028**), subject to earlier termination in accordance with the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.  

(c) To the extent that the Amendment Expiration Date extends beyond the Parent Agreement’s Expiration Date, the Parties agree that, with respect solely to the assets and services governed by this Amendment, the Parent Agreement shall be deemed extended for such limited purpose, consistent with the concept of SOW continuity described in **Section 4.2(b) (SOW Continuity)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal.**  

(a) The renewal provisions of **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Amendment, provided that any renewal of the Amendment Term beyond the Amendment Expiration Date shall require an express written agreement referencing both **LSE-EPS-202511-377** and **AMD-EPS-202511-390**.  

(b) The Parties may agree in writing to (i) extend the Amendment Services under a new SOW while leaving other aspects of the Parent Agreement unchanged, or (ii) consolidate this Amendment into a future master amendment or successor lease agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Modifying Period Only).**  

(a) **Section 4.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement permits either Party to terminate the Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon sixty (60) days’ prior written notice. The Parties agree that, with respect to this Amendment and any Amendment SOWs, **either Party may terminate for convenience upon not less than thirty (30) days’ and not more than sixty (60) days’ prior written notice**.  

(b) The payment obligations in **Section 4.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement (payment of Fees due up to termination, plus non-cancellable third-party costs) shall apply to Amendment Fees in the event of termination for convenience by Contoso.  

(c) Upon termination for convenience initiated by Epsilon (other than due to Contoso’s breach), Epsilon shall cooperate with Contoso to ensure orderly return or transition of Additional Leased Assets, consistent with **Section 4.6(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

(a) **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement applies fully to this Amendment. A material breach of Amendment-specific obligations (including chronic SLA failures under **Article XII** of this Amendment) shall constitute a material breach of the Agreement.  

(b) Termination of this Amendment or an Amendment SOW for cause shall not automatically terminate the Parent Agreement or other SOWs unless the nature of the breach prevents continued performance under the Agreement as a whole, consistent with **Section 4.5 (Partial Termination)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement on Amendment.**  

(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain this Amendment as a standalone agreement (in which case they shall document the applicable terms, including any necessary modifications to dependency provisions).  

(b) Upon termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, the provisions of **Section 4.6 (Effects of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Additional Leased Assets and Amendment Services, including cessation of services, return of Leased Assets, final payments, and survival of certain provisions, as supplemented by **Section 16.10** of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE XII  
## SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLAs)

For critical incidents affecting assets governed by this Amendment, the following targets apply (modifying **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement where stricter):

(a) **Critical Incidents:**  

- Initial response: within **30 minutes** of notification (24x7);
