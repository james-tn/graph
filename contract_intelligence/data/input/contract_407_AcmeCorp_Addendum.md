## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overall Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) This Addendum **supplements the Statement of Work dated August 15, 2025 (Ref: SOW-ACM-202501-313, contract_313)** and is entered into pursuant to and in express supplementation of the Parent SOW and the Consulting Agreement.  
(b) Except as expressly modified in this Addendum, the Services, Deliverables, responsibilities, and assumptions described in **Article II of the Parent SOW** remain in full force and effect and are hereby extended to encompass the Extended Services described below.  
(c) The Extended Services constitute an extension of the **Project** and **Program Phases** defined in **Sections 1.4 and 1.5 of the Parent SOW**, and are to be managed under the same Project Governance Committee established in **Section 1.7 and Section 2.10 of the Parent SOW**, with expanded reporting obligations as set forth in **Section 2.6 of this Addendum**.

2.2 **Objectives of the Extended Services.**  
The primary objectives of the Extended Services are to:

  (i) optimize and enhance the solution designed and implemented under **Phases 2 and 3 of the Parent SOW (Sections 2.2.2 and 2.2.3)**, including performance tuning, configuration refinement, and additional integrations with Third-Party Systems as defined in **Section 1.19 of the Parent SOW**;  
  (ii) extend testing, validation, and hypercare support beyond the initial Project timeline defined in **Section 2.3(a) of the Parent SOW**, ensuring stabilization, resilience, and scalability of the implemented solution;  
  (iii) expand analytics, reporting, and governance capabilities, including delivery of advanced dashboards and KPIs for executive stakeholders, building on the advisory services described in **Section 2.2.6 (Phase 6 – Advisory and Governance Support)** of the Parent SOW; and  
  (iv) provide ongoing knowledge transfer, training refreshers, and operational runbooks that leverage and update **Deliverables 8–10** under **Sections 2.2.5 and 2.2.6 of the Parent SOW**.

2.3 **Extended Program Phases and Deliverables.**  
The Extended Services shall be organized into the following phases, which shall be deemed a continuation and expansion of the **Program Phases** defined in **Section 1.5 of the Parent SOW**:

2.3.1 **Phase 7 – Post-Implementation Optimization.**  
(a) **Activities.** In addition to the Implementation and Integration work described in **Section 2.2.3 of the Parent SOW**, Acme shall:

  - Conduct a series of performance and scalability assessments of the implemented solution, including load testing, capacity planning, and bottleneck analysis;  
  - Refine configuration settings in the enterprise applications and integration middleware based on real-world usage and telemetry data;  
  - Implement minor functional enhancements and workflow optimizations approved by Contoso via the Change Management process described in **Article XIII of the Parent SOW** and **Article XIII of this Addendum**;  
  - Conduct architecture and code reviews to validate alignment with Contoso’s target-state architecture as defined in **Deliverable 2 – Target-State Architecture Document** in **Section 2.2.2 of the Parent SOW**.

(b) **Deliverable 11 – Optimization Assessment and Recommendations Report.**  
A detailed report summarizing findings from performance assessments, configuration reviews, and optimization activities, including prioritized recommendations and implementation status.

(c) **Deliverable 12 – Updated Configuration Workbooks and Runbooks.**  
Updated versions of **Deliverable 4 (Configuration Workbooks)** and **Deliverable 5 (Technical Runbooks)** as defined in **Section 2.2.3 of the Parent SOW**, reflecting all approved optimization changes.

2.3.2 **Phase 8 – Extended Integrations and Data Analytics.**  
(a) **Activities.** Building on the integration and data flow work described in **Sections 2.2.2(a) and 2.2.3(a) of the Parent SOW**, Acme shall:

  - Design and implement additional, mutually agreed integrations with designated Third-Party Systems, including vendor APIs, customer analytics platforms, and internal data warehouses;  
  - Develop and deploy advanced analytics dashboards and reports for key business stakeholders (e.g., order management, customer analytics, internal reporting), leveraging data models defined in **Deliverable 3 – Detailed Design and Integration Specifications** of the Parent SOW;  
  - Implement data quality rules, monitoring dashboards, and remediation workflows to ensure accuracy and completeness of data flowing through the integrated ecosystem.

(b) **Deliverable 13 – Extended Integration Specifications and Mapping Documents.**  
Detailed technical specifications, data mapping matrices, and API contracts for all new integrations implemented under this Phase.

(c) **Deliverable 14 – Analytics Dashboards and Reporting Catalog.**  
A catalog and sample set of analytics dashboards and reports, including documentation of metrics, dimensions, KPIs, and user access controls.

2.3.3 **Phase 9 – Extended Testing, Hypercare and Stabilization.**  
(a) **Activities.** In addition to the Testing and Validation Support described in **Section 2.2.4 of the Parent SOW**, Acme shall:

  - Design and execute extended regression test cycles covering new enhancements and integrations delivered in Phases 7 and 8;  
  - Establish and operate a **Hypercare Period** of intensive post-go-live support, including rapid triage, defect resolution, and daily stand-up meetings with Contoso’s IT and business teams;  
  - Maintain an expanded **Test Execution Summary and Defect Log**, updating the **Deliverable 7** defined in **Section 2.2.4(b)–(c) of the Parent SOW** to cover Extended Services.

(b) **Deliverable 15 – Extended Test Strategy, Plans, and Results.**  
Updated and extended test strategy and test plans, including regression coverage, defect metrics, and exit criteria for stabilization.

(c) **Deliverable 16 – Hypercare Support Summary Report.**  
At the end of the Hypercare Period, Acme shall produce a summary report outlining incidents handled, root causes, fixes implemented, and recommendations for long-term support processes.

2.3.4 **Phase 10 – Continuous Advisory, Governance and Training Refresh.**  
(a) **Activities.** Supplementing the advisory services described in **Section 2.2.6 of the Parent SOW**, Acme shall:

  - Participate in ongoing governance meetings under the **Project Governance Committee** structure in **Section 2.10 of the Parent SOW**, with a minimum quarterly cadence and additional ad hoc sessions as reasonably requested;  
  - Provide executive dashboards and governance materials (“governance decks”) highlighting progress against KPIs, risk trends from the **Risk Register**, and roadmap updates;  
  - Conduct periodic training refreshes and onboarding sessions for new Contoso personnel, updating **Deliverable 8 – Training Materials and User Guides** and **Deliverable 9 – Training Completion Report** described in **Section 2.2.5 of the Parent SOW**;  
  - Provide targeted advisory sessions on best practices, technology roadmaps, and process optimization.

(b) **Deliverable 17 – Quarterly Governance and KPI Dashboards.**  
Quarterly governance materials, including KPI dashboards, risk summaries, and financial status tracking against both the Parent SOW Contract Value and the Addendum Contract Value.

(c) **Deliverable 18 – Updated Training Assets and Training Logs.**  
Updated training materials and a consolidated training log documenting attendance, content delivered, and feedback.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones.**  
(a) The Extended Services shall commence as of the Addendum Effective Date and shall run through the Addendum Term, subject to adjustments via Change Orders.  
(b) The Parties anticipate the following **indicative milestones** (which shall be deemed additional Milestones for purposes of **Section 1.6 and Section 2.3 of the Parent SOW**):

  - **Milestone A – Completion of Phase 7 Optimization Deliverables (11 and 12):** within 180 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
  - **Milestone B – Completion of Phase 8 Integrations and Analytics (Deliverables 13 and 14):** within 360 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
  - **Milestone C – Completion of Phase 9 Extended Testing and Hypercare (Deliverables 15 and 16):** within 540 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
  - **Milestone D – Completion of initial one-year cycle of Phase 10 Governance and Training (initial Deliverables 17 and 18):** within 720 days of the Addendum Effective Date.

(c) Consistent with **Section 2.3(b) and Section 2.5(c) of the Parent SOW**, Acme shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve these milestones, and any delays resulting from Contoso’s failure to provide timely access, decisions, or approvals shall excuse Acme’s performance to the extent impacted.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
The performance standards set forth in **Section 2.4 of the Parent SOW** shall apply to the Extended Services. Without limiting the foregoing:

  (i) Acme shall perform the Extended Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with generally accepted industry standards and the warranties in **Section 7.2 of the Parent SOW**; and  
  (ii) Acme shall assign appropriately skilled Key Personnel (as defined in **Section 1.9 of the Parent SOW**) and shall not reassign such Key Personnel without Contoso’s prior written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld, delayed, or conditioned.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities; Dependencies.**  
In addition to Contoso’s responsibilities under **Section 2.5 of the Parent SOW**, Contoso shall:

  - Provide timely access to production and non-production environments as required for optimization and integration activities;  
  - Ensure availability of subject-matter experts and decision-makers for design workshops, testing sign-offs, and governance meetings;  
  - Review and provide written feedback on Enhancement Deliverables within ten (10) Business Days of delivery, consistent with **Section 2.8 of the Parent SOW**;  
  - Obtain or maintain any necessary licenses for Third-Party Systems not expressly agreed in writing to be procured by Acme, consistent with **Sections 2.5(d) and 6.5 of the Parent SOW**.

2.7 **Out-of-Scope Services.**  
Any services not expressly described in **Section 2.3** above shall be considered **Out-of-Scope Services**, consistent with **Section 1.11 and Section 2.6 of the Parent SOW**, and shall require a duly executed Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII of the Parent SOW** and **Article XIII of this Addendum** before Acme is obligated to perform such services.

2.8 **Deliverable Format, Delivery and Acceptance.**  
(a) Enhancement Deliverables shall be provided in the formats described in **Section 2.7(a) of the Parent SOW** (e.g., MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, PDF) and via secure electronic means.  
(b) Acceptance of Enhancement Deliverables shall be governed by **Section 2.8 of the Parent SOW**, including deemed acceptance after ten (10) Business Days absent written notice of material nonconformity.  
(c) In the event of nonconformity, Acme shall correct and resubmit the Enhancement Deliverable at no additional cost, and the acceptance process shall repeat as set forth in **Section 2.8(c) of the Parent SOW**.

- **Milestone B (Deliverables 13 & 14 – Phase 8 Integrations & Analytics):** US $1,125,000  
- **Milestone C (Deliverables 15 & 16 – Phase 9 Testing & Hypercare):** US $530,000  

Total Fixed Fees under this Addendum: **US $2,530,000**

  - Senior Consultant: US $305/hour  
  - Consultant: US $235/hour  
  - Project Manager: US $270/hour

Disputes regarding any invoice under this Addendum shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.5 of the Parent SOW**. Contoso shall promptly notify Acme of any disputed amount, and the Parties shall work in good faith to resolve such dispute while Contoso timely pays all undisputed portions of the invoice.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
(a) Reasonable, documented, and pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses (including travel, lodging, and meals) incurred by Acme specifically for Extended Services shall be reimbursable by Contoso, in accordance with **Section 3.6 of the Parent SOW** and Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies.

(c) Unless explicitly stated otherwise in a Change Order, reimbursable expenses are **in addition to** the Addendum Contract Value.

3.7 **Taxes; Withholding.**  
Taxes and withholding obligations relating to Fees under this Addendum shall be governed by **Sections 3.7 and 3.8 of the Parent SOW** and the Consulting Agreement. All Fees are exclusive of Taxes (other than taxes based on Acme’s net income).

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (January 03, 2026)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **October 15, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or **Article IV of the Parent SOW**.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW Term.**  
The Parties acknowledge that **Section 4.1 of the Parent SOW** establishes a SOW Term from August 15, 2025, to August 22, 2027. By execution of this Addendum, the Parties agree that:

  (a) The Parent SOW Term is **extended solely for purposes of the Extended Services described in this Addendum** through the Addendum Expiration Date of October 15, 2028; and  
  (b) All surviving rights and obligations under the Parent SOW (e.g., confidentiality, IP, indemnification) shall continue to apply during and after the Addendum Term in accordance with **Section 16.13 of the Parent SOW**.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
(a) The 180-day termination-for-convenience notice period applicable to the Parent SOW under **Section 4.3 of the Parent SOW** is hereby **modified for this Addendum** to provide a more balanced approach for this medium-risk engagement.  
(b) Either Party may terminate this Addendum (but not, by virtue of this provision alone, the Parent SOW or the Consulting Agreement) for convenience, without cause, upon not less than **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.  
(c) For clarity, the 180-day notice period in **Section 4.3 of the Parent SOW** shall continue to apply to termination for convenience of the **Parent SOW** itself, except to the extent specifically amended in a separate written agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Termination for cause under this Addendum shall follow the framework in **Section 4.4 of the Parent SOW**, including:

  - Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent SOW as it relates to the Extended Services and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or such longer period as may be agreed in writing) after receipt of written notice specifying the breach;  
  - Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party becomes insolvent, is placed into receivership, or initiates or becomes subject to bankruptcy or similar proceedings.

4.5 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of this Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

  (a) Acme shall cease performance of Extended Services, except as reasonably necessary to wind down and complete any in-progress tasks agreed to in writing;  
  (b) Contoso shall pay Acme for (i) all Extended Services performed and approved expenses incurred through the effective date of termination, and (ii) any non-cancellable commitments incurred with Contoso’s prior written consent, in accordance with **Section 4.5(b) of the Parent SOW**;  
  (c) Each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information relating to the Extended Services in accordance with **Section 5.5 of the Parent SOW**, subject to permissible archival copies; and  
  (d) Rights and licenses to Deliverables conditioned on payment shall become effective only upon full and final payment of undisputed Fees related to such Deliverables, consistent with **Section 4.5(d) of the Parent SOW**.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent SOW or Consulting Agreement.**  
(a) In the event the **Parent SOW** or the **Consulting Agreement** is terminated in its entirety, this Addendum shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue the Extended Services under substantially similar terms.  
(b) Any such continuation shall specify which provisions of the Parent SOW and the Consulting Agreement survive and continue to govern this Addendum, consistent with **Section 4.6 of the Parent SOW**.

4.7 **No Separate Termination Fee.**  
Except as expressly specified in this Addendum or any applicable Change Order, there shall be no separate termination fee for terminating the Extended Services, provided that Contoso remains responsible for all accrued and committed amounts in accordance with **Section 4.7 of the Parent SOW** and **Section 4.5** above.

4.8 **Transition Assistance.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum (other than termination for cause by Acme due to Contoso’s uncured material breach), Acme shall, upon Contoso’s request, provide transition assistance for the Extended Services on a T&M basis at Acme’s then-current rates, consistent with **Section 4.8 of the Parent SOW**.
