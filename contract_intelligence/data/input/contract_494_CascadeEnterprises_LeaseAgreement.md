## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Lease of Premises.** Subject to the terms and conditions of this Agreement, Cascade hereby leases to Contoso, and Contoso hereby leases from Cascade, the Premises for the Lease Term, together with the non-exclusive right, in common with other tenants and occupants of the building, to use the common areas, elevators, stairwells, corridors, lobbies, restrooms, and other shared facilities provided by Cascade.

2.2 **Use of Premises.** 

2.2.1 Contoso shall use the Premises solely for general commercial office purposes, including administrative, managerial, research and development, and other lawful professional business activities, and for no other purpose without Cascade’s prior written consent, such consent not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned or delayed.

2.2.2 Contoso shall not use, nor permit any third party to use, the Premises for any illegal or immoral purpose, for retail use, heavy industrial purposes, or for any activity that would cause excessive noise, vibration, pollution, or nuisance to other tenants or occupants.

2.3 **Services to be Provided by Cascade.** During the Lease Term, Cascade shall provide, or cause to be provided, the following Services in a professional and workmanlike manner and in accordance with applicable industry standards for comparable Grade A office premises in Singapore:

2.3.1 **Building Operations and Maintenance.** Cascade shall operate, maintain, repair, and, where necessary, replace the base building systems serving the Premises and common areas, including structural elements, main electrical systems, plumbing, HVAC plant, fire protection systems, elevators, and security systems, except for damage arising from the acts or omissions of Contoso or its personnel, in which case the provisions of Article VIII (Indemnification) may apply.

2.3.2 **Cleaning Services.** Cascade shall provide daily cleaning of common areas and routine cleaning of the Premises during normal business hours, including removal of normal office waste and cleaning of restrooms, corridors, and lobby areas. Any enhanced or specialized cleaning services requested by Contoso (e.g., data center deep cleaning) shall be subject to additional charges, as set forth in Article III.

2.3.3 **Security Services.** Cascade shall provide 24/7 security coverage for the Building’s main entrances, lobbies, and critical access points, including CCTV surveillance, access control systems, and security personnel. Contoso shall be responsible for the security of its own assets and confidential information within the Premises and may, at its own cost and subject to Cascade’s reasonable approval, install additional security systems within the Premises.

2.3.4 **Utilities.** Cascade shall ensure the availability of electricity, water, and air-conditioning to the Premises during normal business hours as defined in the Building’s operating rules (which shall be furnished by Cascade to Contoso upon request). Air-conditioning outside such hours may be provided upon Contoso’s prior request, subject to additional charges as per the current building tariffs.

2.3.5 **Repairs within Premises.** Cascade shall be responsible for repairs and maintenance of the base building systems and structural elements, while Contoso shall be responsible for routine repairs and maintenance of its own fixtures, fittings, furniture, cabling, IT equipment, and any tenant-specific installations. Where work is required to be carried out by Cascade’s nominated contractors, Cascade shall provide such services at pre-agreed rates or as otherwise agreed in writing.

2.4 **Fit-Out and Alterations.**

2.4.1 Cascade shall, to the extent specifically agreed in writing and within the agreed budget, perform initial fit-out works to the Premises to deliver a functional open-plan office layout with meeting rooms, pantry area, and cabling as described in a mutually agreed fit-out plan. Such fit-out works, if included in the Contract Value, shall be detailed in a separate schedule to this Agreement.

2.4.2 Contoso may at its sole cost (unless otherwise agreed) carry out additional fit-out works or alterations within the Premises, subject to: (a) prior written approval by Cascade (such approval not to be unreasonably withheld); (b) compliance with building rules, codes, and applicable laws; (c) use of qualified contractors approved by Cascade; and (d) restoration obligations upon expiry or termination, as set forth in Section 4.5.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**

2.5.1 Cascade shall perform the Services with all due skill, care, and diligence, using adequately trained, experienced, and qualified personnel, and in accordance with the standards of a prudent building owner and manager of comparable commercial properties in Singapore.

2.5.2 Cascade shall comply, and ensure that its subcontractors comply, with all applicable laws, regulations, building codes, fire and safety requirements, and official guidelines relevant to the operation and maintenance of the Building and the provision of Services, including any health and safety requirements.

2.5.3 Cascade shall meet or exceed the Service Levels set forth in Article XII. In the event of any failure to meet the Service Levels, the remedies specified in Article XII shall apply, without prejudice to Contoso’s other rights and remedies under this Agreement, subject always to Article IX (Limitation of Liability).

2.6 **Access and Cooperation.**

2.6.1 Cascade shall provide Contoso, its employees, contractors, and authorized visitors with reasonable and secure access to the Premises during normal business hours and, where permitted under building rules, outside such hours, subject to security protocols and access card regulations.

2.6.2 Contoso shall cooperate with Cascade by providing access to the Premises when reasonably required for inspection, maintenance, repair works, or compliance with legal requirements, provided that Cascade shall give reasonable prior notice (except in emergencies) and shall use commercially reasonable efforts to minimize disruption to Contoso’s business operations.

2.7 **Deliverables.** Cascade shall provide to Contoso the following Deliverables, to the extent applicable:

(a) Fit-out plans, drawings, and as-built drawings for works carried out by or on behalf of Cascade;  
(b) Maintenance schedules and logs for building systems serving the Premises;  
(c) Records of security access and incident reports relevant to the Premises, subject to privacy and security policies;  
(d) Utility consumption reports as reasonably requested by Contoso; and  
(e) Any other reports or documentation as may be expressly specified in this Agreement or reasonably requested by Contoso and agreed by Cascade in writing.

2.8 **Limitations on Services.** Unless expressly agreed in this Agreement, Cascade shall not be responsible for:

(a) Contoso’s IT infrastructure, servers, data networks, or software;  
(b) insurance of Contoso’s property, equipment, or business interruption losses;  
(c) specialized environmental controls required for data centers or labs beyond standard office specifications; or  
(d) any services or obligations expressly allocated to Contoso elsewhere in this Agreement.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value.** The Parties acknowledge that, subject to the terms of this Agreement, the total estimated contract value for the Lease Term, including Base Rent, Service Charges, and any included fit-out contributions, shall be **USD 1,524,919** (the **“Contract Value”**). This amount may be adjusted for additional services, legally mandated increases (e.g., taxes), or mutually agreed variations.

3.2 **Base Rent.**

3.2.1 Contoso shall pay to Cascade a monthly Base Rent in the amount specified in the rent schedule to be attached as Schedule 1 (the **“Rent Schedule”**), which shall be consistent with the Contract Value.

3.2.2 Base Rent shall be payable monthly in advance, on or before the first (1st) Business Day of each calendar month during the Lease Term. For any partial month at the beginning or end of the Lease Term, Rent shall be prorated on a daily basis based on a 30-day month.

3.3 **Service Charges and Utilities.**

3.3.1 In addition to Base Rent, Contoso shall pay Service Charges on a monthly basis, representing its fair proportion of the building’s operating expenses, maintenance, security, cleaning, and other shared costs, in accordance with Cascade’s standard methodology for allocation among tenants.

3.3.2 Utility charges (e.g., electricity, water) may be charged on a metered, sub-metered, or pro-rata basis, as applicable, and as indicated in the Rent Schedule or a separate utilities schedule.

3.3.3 Charges for air-conditioning outside normal business hours shall be invoiced as additional services at the prevailing rates notified by Cascade to Contoso.

3.4 **Payment Terms (Net 30).**

3.4.1 Unless otherwise stated, all invoices issued by Cascade under this Agreement, including invoices for Base Rent, Service Charges, Utilities, and additional Services, shall be due and payable by Contoso within **thirty (30) calendar days** of the invoice date (**“Net 30”**).

3.4.2 All payments shall be made in USD (or such other currency as the Parties may mutually agree) by bank transfer to the account designated by Cascade in the relevant invoice, free from any set-off, deduction or withholding, except as required by law.

3.5 **Late Payment.**

3.5.1 If Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount by the due date, Cascade may charge interest on the overdue amount from the due date until the date payment is received in full at a rate equal to the lesser of: (a) one percent (1%) per month; or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law.

3.5.2 Cascade shall provide a written reminder notice with a grace period of at least ten (10) Business Days prior to commencing any remedial action or exercising any rights of suspension of Services or rights of re-entry, except in the case of persistent or material non-payment.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**

3.6.1 If Contoso disputes in good faith any part of an invoice, it shall notify Cascade in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the amount disputed and reasons therefor.

3.6.2 Contoso shall pay all undisputed portions of the invoice by the due date. The Parties shall use good faith efforts to resolve any invoice disputes as promptly as practicable. Any resolved disputed amounts shall be paid within ten (10) Business Days after resolution.

3.7 **Taxes.**

3.7.1 All Fees are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be added to each invoice where applicable. Contoso shall be responsible for any applicable GST or other indirect taxes on the supply of the Premises and Services under this Agreement.

3.7.2 If any withholding tax is required by law, Contoso shall be entitled to deduct such tax from the payment and shall provide Cascade with sufficient documentation to enable Cascade to claim any available credit or refund.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  

3.8.1 To the extent Cascade incurs out-of-pocket expenses specifically requested by Contoso and not included in the Contract Value (e.g., special works, expedited works, additional security), such expenses shall be reimbursed by Contoso at cost plus any agreed administrative charge, subject to Contoso’s prior written approval.

3.8.2 Cascade shall provide reasonable supporting documentation for any reimbursable expenses, such as invoices or receipts, upon Contoso’s request.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Initial Term.** The Lease Term shall commence on the Commencement Date (which shall be the Effective Date unless otherwise agreed) and shall continue until the Expiration Date of **July 24, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.2 **Renewal.**

4.2.1 Provided that Contoso is not in material breach of this Agreement at the date of notice and at the expiry of the Term, Contoso shall have the option to request a renewal of the Lease for an additional term of three (3) years (the **“Renewal Term”**), on mutually agreeable rental and commercial terms then prevailing for comparable premises in the building.

4.2.2 Contoso shall exercise such renewal option by giving written notice to Cascade no later than nine (9) months prior to the Expiration Date. Renewal shall be subject to a new written agreement or an amendment to this Agreement duly executed by both Parties.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly).**

4.3.1 Contoso may terminate this Agreement for convenience, in whole or in part, at any time after the first twelve (12) months of the Term by providing Cascade with at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice.

4.3.2 In the event of termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall be liable for: (a) all Rent and charges due up to the effective termination date; and (b) any documented, non-recoverable capital costs incurred by Cascade specifically for Contoso’s fit-out and amortized over the initial Term, limited to the unamortized portion as at the termination date, unless otherwise waived by Cascade.

4.3.3 Cascade may terminate for convenience only with Contoso’s written consent, except in circumstances where (i) the Building is to be redeveloped or demolished; or (ii) major works require vacation of the Premises. In such cases, Cascade shall provide at least six (6) months’ prior written notice and shall use commercially reasonable efforts to provide alternative comparable premises on similar terms.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) commits a material breach of any provision of this Agreement which is incapable of remedy; or  
(b) commits a material breach of any provision of this Agreement that is capable of remedy and fails to remedy such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach and requiring it to be remedied.

4.4.2 Without limitation, repeated non-payment of Rent or Service Charges, unauthorized use of the Premises, serious safety violations, or misuse causing substantial damage shall constitute material breaches.

4.4.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon written notice if the other Party becomes insolvent, enters into liquidation or administration, or ceases to carry on business, subject to any mandatory provisions of applicable insolvency laws.

4.5 **Effects of Termination or Expiration.**

4.5.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

(a) Contoso shall vacate the Premises and return possession to Cascade in substantially the same condition as at the Commencement Date, fair wear and tear excepted, and subject to any agreed fit-out works to remain;  
(b) Contoso shall remove its furniture, equipment, and personal property, and, at Cascade’s written request, remove any Contoso-specific alterations and reinstate the Premises to its previous configuration, reasonable costs to be borne by Contoso; and  
(c) Cascade shall promptly refund any prepaid Rent, Service Charges or deposits relating to periods after the date of termination, less any legitimate deductions for outstanding amounts or repairs attributable to Contoso.

4.5.2 Termination or expiration shall not affect any rights, obligations, or liabilities of either Party that have accrued prior to the effective date of termination or expiration, nor any provisions that are expressly stated to survive.

(c) was already in the Receiving Party’s possession without restriction before receipt from the Disclosing Party; or

5.6 **Survival.** The provisions of this Article V shall survive for a period of **five (5) years** following the expiration or termination of this Agreement, except with respect to trade secrets and personal data, which shall be kept confidential for so long as they remain protected as such under applicable law.

15.4 **Mitigation.** The affected Party shall use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects of the Force Majeure Event and to resume performance of its obligations as soon as reasonably practicable.
