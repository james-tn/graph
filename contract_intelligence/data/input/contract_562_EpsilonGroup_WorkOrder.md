## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Work Order is a “Statement of Work” as contemplated by **Section 1.1.18** and **Section 2.7 (Statements of Work)** of the Parent Agreement and is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement.  

2.1.2 In accordance with **Section 2.7 (last paragraph)** of the Parent Agreement, in the event of any conflict between the terms of the Parent Agreement and the terms of this Work Order, the terms of the Parent Agreement shall control unless this Work Order expressly states that it is intended to supersede a specific provision of the Parent Agreement, in which case this Work Order shall control solely with respect to the Services under this Work Order.

2.2 **Overall Description of the Program.**  

2.2.1 Under this Work Order, Epsilon shall provide Contoso with a comprehensive set of professional consulting, advisory, design, implementation, and change management services in support of Contoso’s **NextGen Operations Transformation Program** across specified Operational Domains.  

2.2.2 Consistent with **Section 2.2 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, the Services hereunder will include, without limitation:  
  (a) strategic consulting;  
  (b) assessment and analysis of current-state processes;  
  (c) design of target-state (“to-be”) processes and operating models;  
  (d) development of implementation roadmaps;  
  (e) technology selection and configuration support;  
  (f) project management and PMO services;  
  (g) development and delivery of training and knowledge transfer; and  
  (h) preparation of business cases and benefit realization plans.

2.3 **Program Phases and Key Deliverables.**  

The Program shall be executed in four primary Phases, each with specific Program Deliverables. Unless otherwise agreed by Change Order, the scope of each Phase is as follows:

2.3.1 **Phase 1 – Assessment & Strategy (Target Duration: 16 Weeks).**  
Epsilon will:  
  (a) Conduct detailed current-state assessments of the in-scope Operational Domains (order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, record-to-report, customer support), including process mapping, stakeholder interviews, and system inventory;  
  (b) Analyze process performance metrics, pain points, and root causes;  
  (c) Benchmark Contoso’s operations against industry best practices;  
  (d) Prepare a **Current-State Assessment Report** and **Opportunities Catalog**;  
  (e) Develop a **Future-State Vision and Transformation Strategy** aligned with Contoso’s strategic objectives.  

**Key Deliverables – Phase 1** (Program Deliverables):  
  - D1.1: Current-State Process Maps and Documentation  
  - D1.2: Assessment Report with Findings, Root Cause Analyses, and Improvement Opportunities  
  - D1.3: Future-State Vision Deck and Executive Summary  
  - D1.4: High-Level Transformation Roadmap (2–3 Year Horizon)  

2.3.2 **Phase 2 – Detailed Design & Roadmap (Target Duration: 20 Weeks).**  
Building on Phase 1, Epsilon will:  
  (a) Design detailed target-state process flows and operating models for in-scope Operational Domains;  
  (b) Define detailed functional and non-functional requirements for enabling technology solutions (ERP/CRM/Workflow/Analytics), consistent with **Section 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement**;  
  (c) Develop an integrated **Program Roadmap**, including release waves, dependencies, and resource plans;  
  (d) Define a **Change Management and Communications Plan**;  
  (e) Establish detailed **KPIs and Benefit Realization Framework**.  

**Key Deliverables – Phase 2:**  
  - D2.1: Target Operating Model Documentation  
  - D2.2: Detailed Process Design and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)  
  - D2.3: Requirements Specifications for Technology Enablement  
  - D2.4: Integrated Program Roadmap and Resource Plan  
  - D2.5: Change Management & Communications Plan  
  - D2.6: KPI Catalog and Benefit Realization Plan  

2.3.3 **Phase 3 – Implementation & Rollout (Target Duration: 52 Weeks).**  
In this Phase, Epsilon will:  
  (a) Provide project management and PMO services as contemplated by **Section 2.2.6** of the Parent Agreement;  
  (b) Support Contoso in vendor selection and/or configuration of identified technologies;  
  (c) Support solution design workshops, configuration reviews, and user acceptance testing;  
  (d) Develop training content and deliver training as contemplated by **Section 2.2.7** and **Section 2.3.5** of the Parent Agreement;  
  (e) Support rollout, including cutover planning and go-live support across prioritized Operational Domains;  
  (f) Provide weekly status reports, risk and issue logs, and program dashboards as contemplated by **Section 2.3.6**.  

**Key Deliverables – Phase 3:**  
  - D3.1: PMO Charter, Governance Playbook, and RACI Matrices  
  - D3.2: Detailed Project Plans and Sprint Backlogs  
  - D3.3: Configuration and Design Review Documents  
  - D3.4: UAT Test Plans, Scripts, and Summary Results  
  - D3.5: Training Materials, User Guides, and Quick Reference Cards  
  - D3.6: Go-Live Cutover Plan and Hypercare Support Plan  

2.3.4 **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Optimization (Target Duration: 24 Weeks).**  
Following the initial rollouts, Epsilon will:  
  (a) Provide post-go-live stabilization support, including issue triage, root cause analysis, and remediation coordination;  
  (b) Conduct periodic performance reviews against KPIs and benefit realization targets;  
  (c) Identify and prioritize incremental enhancements;  
  (d) Institutionalize continuous improvement practices and governance.  

**Key Deliverables – Phase 4:**  
  - D4.1: Stabilization Status Reports and Issue Logs  
  - D4.2: KPI and Benefits Realization Reports  
  - D4.3: Optimization Backlog and Release Plans  
  - D4.4: Continuous Improvement Framework Documentation  

2.4 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**  

2.4.1 Acceptance of Program Deliverables under this Work Order shall follow the procedures outlined in **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, except as supplemented by this Section.  

2.4.2 Unless otherwise specified in this Work Order, the Company shall have ten (10) Business Days following delivery of each Deliverable to review and either (a) accept the Deliverable in writing, or (b) provide written notice of material deficiencies with reference to the Acceptance Criteria.  

2.4.3 For major Milestone Deliverables (D1.2, D2.4, D3.5, D4.2), the Parties may agree in the integrated Program Roadmap to extend the review period up to fifteen (15) Business Days.  

2.4.4 If Contoso fails to respond within the applicable review period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted in accordance with **Section 2.8.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Program Governance and Key Personnel.**  

2.5.1 **Governance Structure.** The Parties shall establish a Program Governance Structure including:  
  (a) an Executive Steering Committee with senior stakeholders from both Parties;  
  (b) a joint PMO led by Epsilon’s Program Director and Contoso’s Program Sponsor; and  
  (c) functional workstream leads.  

2.5.2 **Key Personnel – Epsilon.** Epsilon shall assign the following Key Personnel (names may be designated in an attachment):

  - Lead Enterprise Architect  
  - PMO Lead  
  - Change Management Lead  

Epsilon shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure continuity of Key Personnel in accordance with **Section 2.5.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement. Any replacement of Key Personnel shall be with personnel of comparable qualifications and experience, subject to prior written notice to Contoso.

2.5.3 **Key Personnel – Contoso.** Contoso shall designate:  
  - Program Sponsor  
  - Program Manager  
  - Business Process Owners for each Operational Domain  
  - Change Champion Network Lead  

2.6 **Company Responsibilities.**  

Consistent with **Section 2.6 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:  
  (a) Provide timely access to relevant systems, data, facilities, and personnel;  
  (b) Make available appropriate subject matter experts for discovery and design workshops;  
  (c) Review Deliverables and provide approvals or feedback within specified timeframes;

Unless otherwise agreed via Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement and Article XIII of this Work Order**, the following are Out-of-Scope Services:
