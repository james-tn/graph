## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND WORK

2.1 **General Engagement.** During the Term, the Company hereby engages the Service Provider, and the Service Provider hereby accepts such engagement, to perform the Services and deliver the Deliverables described in this Article II and in one or more Statements of Work, in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

2.2 **Statements of Work.**

2.2.1 The specific details of each engagement, including the description of Services, Deliverables, timelines, Milestones, Service Levels (if applicable), and associated Fees, shall be set forth in one or more SOWs substantially in the form approved by the Parties.

2.2.2 Each SOW shall reference this Agreement, be numbered sequentially, and be signed by duly authorized representatives of both Parties. Upon execution, each SOW shall be deemed incorporated into and governed by this Agreement. In the event of any conflict between the terms of this Agreement and an SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall prevail unless the SOW expressly states that it overrides a specific provision of this Agreement.

2.3 **Description of Services.** The Services to be provided by the Service Provider may include, without limitation:

2.3.1 **Strategic Advisory Services.**  
(a) Assessment of the Company’s current business operations, technology platforms, competitive landscape, and market positioning;  
(b) Development of strategic recommendations related to digital transformation, product roadmap, market expansion, and organizational structure;  
(c) Conducting executive workshops and strategy sessions with the Company’s senior leadership.

2.3.2 **Operational and Process Consulting.**  
(a) Analysis of existing processes, workflows, and operating models;  
(b) Design of optimized processes and operating models to improve efficiency, productivity, and customer satisfaction;  
(c) Development of implementation plans, including change management, training, and communication strategies.

2.3.3 **Go-To-Market and Commercial Strategy.**  
(a) Market research and segmentation analysis;  
(b) Pricing, packaging, and positioning recommendations;  
(c) Design of sales and channel strategies, lead generation programs, and performance metrics.

2.3.4 **Project Management and Implementation Support.**  
(a) Planning and coordination of project activities related to the implementation of agreed strategic initiatives;  
(b) Tracking project progress, risks, and dependencies;  
(c) Reporting to the Company’s project governance bodies, including steering committees and executive sponsors.

2.3.5 **Knowledge Transfer and Training.**  
(a) Development and delivery of training materials and workshops for the Company’s staff;  
(b) Documentation of key methodologies and processes;  
(c) Support for transition of responsibilities to internal teams upon completion of relevant phases.

2.4 **Deliverables.** Without limiting the foregoing, and subject to refinement in applicable SOWs, the Service Provider shall provide the following types of Deliverables:

2.4.1 **Assessment Reports.** Comprehensive written reports (in electronic format) summarizing findings from assessments of the Company’s operations, technology, or markets, including identified issues, risks, and opportunities.

2.4.2 **Strategic Roadmaps.** Time-phased roadmaps outlining recommended initiatives, timelines, resource requirements, and dependencies for achieving the Company’s strategic objectives.

2.4.3 **Process Documentation.** Detailed process maps, workflows, standard operating procedures, and related documentation in a format reasonably acceptable to the Company.

2.4.4 **Presentation Materials.** Executive-level slide decks and other presentation materials used in workshops, steering committee meetings, and executive briefings.

2.4.5 **Implementation Tools.** Checklists, templates, and planning tools (excluding Vendor Tools as defined in Section 1.1.20 and subject to licenses granted under Section 6.2) to assist in execution and monitoring of strategic initiatives.

2.4.6 **Training Materials.** Training guides, manuals, and reference documents to support the Company’s personnel in adopting new processes or tools.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**

2.5.1 The Service Provider shall perform the Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards for reputable management consulting firms and in accordance with the skill and care ordinarily exercised by professionals in similar services.

2.5.2 The Service Provider shall assign personnel with appropriate skills, qualifications, and experience to perform the Services. Any substitution of key personnel identified in an SOW shall be subject to the Company’s prior written approval (not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed).

2.5.3 The Service Provider shall comply with all reasonable policies and procedures of the Company that have been provided to the Service Provider in writing and that relate to the performance of the Services, including without limitation security, confidentiality, and workplace conduct policies.

2.6 **Timelines and Milestones.**

2.6.1 Each SOW shall specify the timeline for performance of the Services, including key Milestones and delivery dates for Deliverables.

2.6.2 The Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to perform the Services and deliver the Deliverables in accordance with the timelines set forth in the applicable SOW. If the Service Provider becomes aware of any circumstance that is likely to cause a material delay, it shall promptly notify the Company in writing, explain the cause of delay, and propose a mitigation plan.

2.6.3 The Parties acknowledge that timelines may be adjusted by mutual agreement in writing, including via a Change Order pursuant to Article XIII, particularly where dependencies on the Company’s resources, decisions, or third-party actions impact the schedule.

2.7 **Company Responsibilities.**

2.7.1 The Company shall provide timely access to relevant information, systems, personnel, and facilities as reasonably necessary for the Service Provider to perform the Services.

2.7.2 The Company shall review and provide feedback on Deliverables within the timeframes specified in each SOW or, if no timeframe is specified, within ten (10) Business Days of receipt, failing which the Deliverable may be deemed accepted for purposes of payment under Article III, subject to the Company’s rights under any warranties in Article VII.

2.7.3 The Company acknowledges that the Service Provider’s performance is dependent upon the Company’s timely cooperation, decisions, and instructions, and the Service Provider shall not be liable for delays or failures to meet timelines to the extent caused by the Company’s acts or omissions.

2.8 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**

2.8.1 Each SOW may define acceptance criteria and procedures for the Deliverables. Absent such specific criteria, a Deliverable shall be deemed accepted upon the earlier of: (a) written confirmation of acceptance by the Company; or (b) the expiration of ten (10) Business Days from delivery without written notice of a material non-conformity.

2.8.2 If the Company reasonably determines that a Deliverable does not materially conform to the applicable SOW, it shall provide the Service Provider with a written notice describing the non-conformities in reasonable detail. The Service Provider shall, at no additional cost, use commercially reasonable efforts to correct such non-conformities and re-deliver the Deliverable within a mutually agreed period.

2.8.3 If after a reasonable number of correction attempts (not less than two (2)), the Deliverable still fails to materially conform, the Company may, as its exclusive remedy with respect to such Deliverable, either (a) accept the Deliverable with an equitable reduction in the associated Fees, or (b) reject the Deliverable and receive a refund of Fees paid specifically for such Deliverable.
