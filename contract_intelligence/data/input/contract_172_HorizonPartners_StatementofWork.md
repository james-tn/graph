## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This SOW is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement, including **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**. The Services described herein are a subset of, and fully consistent with, the Services described in **Section 2.2 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, specifically focusing on detailed operating model implementation support for selected business units of Client.

2.2 **Project Overview and Phases.**  
Under this SOW, Vendor shall provide a structured consulting engagement titled **“Digital Operating Model Implementation Support & Optimization for Core Enterprise Functions”** (the “Project”), consisting of the following four phases, which refine and extend the high-level timeline in **Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement**:

2.2.1 **Phase 1 – Targeted Discovery & Assessment (Estimated 12 weeks).**  
Building upon the initial strategic assessment contemplated in **Section 2.2.1** and the Initial Assessment Report in **Section 2.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall perform a **deep-dive assessment** of three designated functional areas of Client’s business: (a) Sales Operations; (b) Customer Support; and (c) Technology Delivery (collectively, the “Focus Areas”). Activities will include:

- Conducting up to **30 stakeholder interviews** across executive, managerial, and operational levels;
- Reviewing existing process documentation, SOPs, and IT system configurations;
- Analyzing key performance data and KPIs for the Focus Areas;
- Identifying gaps between current state and the strategic roadmap previously developed under CSA-HOR-202408-169, where applicable.

Deliverables for Phase 1 shall include:

- **Phase 1 Assessment Addendum** supplementing the “Initial Assessment Report” described in **Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement**, tailored to the Focus Areas;
- **Risk and Dependency Matrix** specific to the operating model changes contemplated under this SOW;
- **Preliminary Implementation Workplan** outlining workstreams, dependencies, and estimated resource requirements for Phases 2–4.

2.2.2 **Phase 2 – Detailed Design & Planning (Estimated 5–7 months).**  
Consistent with **Section 2.2.2 (Operating Model and Process Design)** and the “Process Documentation” Deliverables in **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

- Design detailed **to‑be process flows** for the Focus Areas, including:
  - Lead-to-Order and Order-to-Cash processes for Sales Operations;
  - Incident, Request, and Problem Management workflows for Customer Support;
  - Agile/DevOps delivery processes for Technology Delivery;
- Develop **RACI matrices** for each core process;
- Document **governance structures**, including steering committees, process councils, and decision rights;
- Align the **technology architecture** to support the to‑be processes, building on the “Technology Recommendations” described in **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent Agreement.

Deliverables for Phase 2 shall include:

- **Target Operating Model Blueprint** for each Focus Area;
- **Process Map Package** (Visio or equivalent) with narrative descriptions;
- **Governance and Roles Charter**;
- **Technology Alignment Report**, referencing the ERP, CRM, analytics, and cloud platforms evaluated under the Parent Agreement.

2.2.3 **Phase 3 – Implementation Support & Change Enablement (Estimated 9–12 months).**  
In alignment with **Section 2.2.4 (Program Management and Change Enablement)** and **Section 2.3.5 (Program Management Artifacts)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide:

- **Program management support**, including:
  - Development and maintenance of integrated project plans for up to four transformation workstreams;
  - Bi-weekly status reports;  
  - Maintenance of risk, issue, and decision logs;
- **Change management services**, including:
  - Preparation and delivery of up to **10 stakeholder workshops**;
  - Creation of **training materials** and communication collateral, building upon **Section 2.3.6 (Training and Communication Materials)** of the Parent Agreement;
- **Implementation advisory** regarding configuration, integration, and testing of new or updated systems in the Focus Areas.

Deliverables for Phase 3 shall include:

- **Integrated Program Plan** (MS Project or equivalent) with baseline and updated timelines;
- **Steering Committee Pack Templates** and at least **10 completed packs**;
- **Training Decks and Job Aids** for key roles in each Focus Area;
- **Implementation Progress Reports** at least monthly.

2.2.4 **Phase 4 – Performance Measurement, Optimization & Handover (Remainder of SOW Term).**  
Aligned with **Section 2.2.5 (Performance Measurement and Optimization)** and **Section 2.4(d)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

- Define and refine **KPIs and dashboards** for the Focus Areas, including operational, financial, and customer metrics;
- Conduct **quarterly performance reviews** and propose optimization initiatives;
- Develop and document a **Handover Playbook** enabling Client to independently sustain the new operating model.

Deliverables for Phase 4 shall include:

- **KPI Framework and Dashboard Specifications** (for use in Client’s BI tools);
- **Quarterly Optimization Reports** (minimum of 4, subject to SOW Term);
- **Final Handover Documentation**, including updated process maps, governance artifacts, and a capabilities maturity assessment.

2.3 **Deliverables and Acceptance.**  

2.3.1 **Deliverables.**  
The Deliverables under this SOW include, without limitation, the following:

- Phase 1 Assessment Addendum and Risk/Dependency Matrix;  
- Target Operating Model Blueprints (3 Focus Areas);  
- Comprehensive Process Map Package and Governance/Roles Charter;  
- Technology Alignment Report;  
- Integrated Program Plan, Steering Committee Packs, and Program Management Artifacts;
