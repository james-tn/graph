## ADDENDUM TO LEASE AGREEMENT (ADDITIONAL / MODIFIED LEASED SPACE)

This Addendum (the **“Addendum”**), bearing Contract Reference Number **ADD-VAN-202405-653**, is made and entered into as of **November 15, 2024** (the **“Addendum Effective Date”**),

**BETWEEN:**

1. **Contoso Enterprises**, a company duly incorporated and existing under the laws of England and Wales, with its registered office at 250 Kingsway, London WC2B 6AA, United Kingdom (hereinafter referred to as the **“Client”** or the **“Company”**, which expression shall, where the context so admits, include its successors and permitted assigns);  

**AND**

2. **Vanguard Solutions**, a company duly incorporated and existing under the laws of England and Wales, with its registered office at 14 Bishopsgate, London EC2N 4AG, United Kingdom (hereinafter referred to as the **“Vendor”**, the **“Lessor”**, or the **“Service Provider”**, which expression shall, where the context so admits, include its successors and permitted assigns).

The Client and the Vendor are hereinafter individually referred to as a **“Party”** and collectively as the **“Parties”**.

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Lease Agreement** dated **May 25, 2024**, bearing **Contract Reference Number LSE-VAN-202405-613** and internal identifier **contract_613** (the **“Parent Agreement”** or **“Lease”**), pursuant to which Vendor agreed to lease to Client, and Client agreed to lease from Vendor, certain commercial office space located at **14 Bishopsgate, London EC2N 4AG, United Kingdom**, comprising approximately **2,500 square metres** of net usable area on the **6th and 7th floors** (as defined therein as the **“Premises”**, see Section 1.11 and **Schedule 1 (Description of Premises)** of the Parent Agreement);

WHEREAS, under **Article II (Scope of Lease and Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor agreed to provide, inter alia, building operations and maintenance, cleaning, security, utilities management, and related services (collectively, the **“Services”**, as defined in Section 1.14 thereof) in respect of the Premises, and the Parties agreed the applicable **Service Levels** under **Article XII** and **Schedule 4 (Service Levels)**;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established a **Contract Value** of **USD $3,426,925** over the Term (Section 1.5), and a Lease Term running from the Effective Date to the **Expiration Date of December 01, 2028** (Section 1.18 and Article IV);

WHEREAS, Client now desires to lease **additional office premises and/or to modify the configuration of the existing leased space** within the same building at 14 Bishopsgate, and Vendor is willing to grant such additional or modified space to Client, together with corresponding Services and Service Levels, subject to and in accordance with the terms and conditions of this Addendum and the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend that this Addendum **adds or modifies leased space** under the Parent Agreement dated May 25, 2024 (Ref: **LSE-VAN-202405-613**, internal identifier **contract_613**) and that it is **entered into pursuant to and in supplementation of** the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, and agreements contained herein, and other good and valuable consideration (the sufficiency and receipt of which are hereby acknowledged), the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF LEASE AND SERVICES (ADDITIONAL / MODIFIED PREMISES)

2.1 **Reference to Parent Scope.** This Addendum is executed under and supplements **Article II (Scope of Lease and Services)** of the Parent Agreement. Unless expressly modified herein, all obligations regarding Premises condition, Services, performance standards, access, and client responsibilities set forth in Article II of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Additional Premises.

2.2 **Grant of Lease – Additional Premises.**  
(a) Subject to the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Vendor hereby leases to Client, and Client hereby takes from Vendor, the **Additional Premises** described in **Schedule A to this Addendum (Description of Additional Premises)**, together with the non‑exclusive right to use the Common Areas (as defined in Section 2.1 of the Parent Agreement) in connection therewith.  
(b) As of the Addendum Commencement Date, the definition of **“Premises”** in Section 1.11 of the Parent Agreement is hereby amended such that references to “Premises” shall be construed to include the Additional Premises, and the total net usable area and floor configuration shall be as set forth in Schedule A to this Addendum.  
(c) The **Adjusted Premises** shall be treated as a single integrated premises for purposes of **Articles II, III, VII, VIII, IX, X, XII, XIII, XIV, XV, and XVI** of the Parent Agreement, save where this Addendum expressly provides otherwise.

2.3 **Premises Condition for Additional Premises.**  
(a) Vendor shall deliver the Additional Premises to Client on the Addendum Commencement Date in a condition no less than **Category A** as defined in Section 2.2(a) of the Parent Agreement (including raised flooring, suspended ceilings, basic lighting and power distribution, HVAC suitable for normal office use during Working Hours, and compliant fire detection and suppression systems).  
(b) Vendor shall, at its own cost (unless otherwise specified in a separate signed fit‑out agreement), ensure that the Additional Premises comply with all applicable building, fire, and health and safety regulations as at the Addendum Commencement Date, consistent with Section 2.2(b) of the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Any **additional fit‑out, cabling, partitioning, or other customization** of the Additional Premises requested by Client shall be subject to Vendor’s prior written consent (not to be unreasonably withheld or delayed) and may be subject to additional charges agreed in writing, in accordance with Section 2.2(c) and Article XIII of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Services for Additional Premises.**  
Vendor shall provide, in relation to the Additional Premises, the same categories of Services as set out in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement, including without limitation:

- **Building Operations and Maintenance** of structural elements, external walls, and core building systems serving the Additional Premises;  
- **Cleaning Services**, ensuring that the Additional Premises are cleaned to a professional standard at least once daily on Business Days, commensurate with cleaning standards applicable to the original Premises;  
- **Security Services**, including access control, CCTV coverage of entrances/exits serving the Additional Premises, and manned reception as required;  
- **Utilities Management**, ensuring supply of electricity, water, and HVAC to the Additional Premises during Working Hours, with sub‑metering and cost allocation in accordance with Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement as supplemented by this Addendum; and  
- **Waste Management** and **Maintenance and Repairs** for building systems and Common Areas serving the Additional Premises, in accordance with Sections 2.3(e) and 2.3(f) of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Reconfiguration of Existing Premises (if applicable).**  
Where the Parties agree to **reconfigure or re‑allocate** existing portions of the Premises (for example, surrender of part of the 7th floor area in exchange for additional space on the 8th floor):

(a) Such reconfiguration shall be detailed in **Schedule A** and, if necessary, in a further **Change Order** under Article XIII of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) The reconfigured layout shall be reflected in an updated **floor plan** and **Handover Report** under Section 2.6(a) of the Parent Agreement, which shall be signed by both Parties and appended to this Addendum;  
(c) Any resulting adjustment in the area of the Premises and related **Rent**, **Service Charges**, or **utilities allocation** shall be set out in Article III and **Schedule B (Addendum Fee Schedule)** to this Addendum; and  
(d) Any surrender of space shall not be treated as a termination of the Parent Agreement but as a contractual realignment of the Premises, unless expressly stated otherwise in this Addendum.

2.6 **Deliverables and Documentation – Additional Premises.**  
Without limiting Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall prepare and deliver the following additional Deliverables in relation to the Additional Premises:

(a) A **Supplemental Handover Report** for the Additional Premises, documenting condition at the Addendum Commencement Date, signed by both Parties;  
(b) An updated or supplemental **Maintenance Schedule** covering building systems and equipment specific to the Additional Premises;  
(c) Inclusion of Additional Premises in quarterly **Service Level Reports** required under Section 2.6(c) and Article XII of the Parent Agreement, with separate identification of incidents specifically affecting the Additional Premises where material; and  
(d) Updated **Incident and Repair Logs** to reflect incidents or repairs affecting the Additional Premises.

2.7 **Permitted Use.**  
Client’s use of the Additional Premises shall be the same as the **Permitted Use** defined in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, namely general commercial office purposes (administrative, managerial, professional activities), and subject to the same prohibitions on industrial, retail, residential, illegal, hazardous, or non‑compliant uses.

2.8 **Client Responsibilities – Additional Premises.**  
Client’s responsibilities under Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement (including maintaining the interior in good order, complying with Building Rules, obtaining necessary licences, refraining from unauthorized structural alterations, reporting damage, and avoiding nuisance or hazardous materials) shall apply equally to the Additional Premises.

2.9 **Access.**  
Vendor shall provide Client with access to the Additional Premises in accordance with Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement, i.e., **24/7 access** subject to security procedures and emergency conditions. Extended services outside Working Hours (such as after‑hours HVAC for the Additional Premises) may be charged at agreed additional rates, as provided in Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement and Section 3.4 of this Addendum.

2.10 **Compliance with Laws and Building Rules.**  
Both Parties shall continue to comply with applicable laws and regulations under Section 2.9 of the Parent Agreement, and Client shall ensure its employees and visitors in the Additional Premises comply with the **Building Rules** set out in Schedule 2 of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES, RENT AND PAYMENT TERMS (ADDITIONAL PREMISES)

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value and Fee Structure.**  
(a) In consideration for the lease of the Additional Premises and provision of associated Services, Client shall pay Vendor the **Addendum Contract Value** of **USD $480,135** over the Addendum Term, subject to adjustments agreed in accordance with this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.  
(b) The fee structure for the Additional Premises shall consist of:

  (i) **Base Rent – Additional Premises.** An annual base rent in respect of the Additional Premises, as specified in **Schedule B (Addendum Fee Schedule)**, which over the Addendum Term forms part of the Addendum Contract Value;  

  (ii) **Service Charge – Additional Premises.** An annual service charge for building operations, maintenance, cleaning, and security relating to the Additional Premises, in addition to any Service Charge applicable under **Schedule 3 (Fee Schedule)** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  (iii) **Additional Services Fees.** Fees for any additional services requested by Client in relation to the Additional Premises (e.g., specialist fit‑out, enhanced cleaning, extended HVAC hours) and agreed in writing, at Vendor’s then‑current rates or as otherwise agreed in a Change Order.

3.2 **Payment Terms – Net 30 (Amendment of Parent Terms).**  
(a) Notwithstanding **Section 3.2(b) (Net 60)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby agree that **all invoices issued by Vendor under this Addendum** shall be payable in accordance with **Net 30** payment terms: Client shall pay all undisputed amounts within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of the invoice.  
(b) For the avoidance of doubt, the Net 60 payment terms remain in effect under the Parent Agreement for amounts not related to this Addendum unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to harmonise payment terms.  
(c) All payments under this Addendum shall be made in the same currency specified in **Schedule 3 (Fee Schedule)** of the Parent Agreement (unless otherwise set out in Schedule B to this Addendum) by electronic funds transfer to the bank account designated in writing by Vendor.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Milestones.**  
(a) Vendor shall issue invoices for Base Rent and Service Charges relating to the Additional Premises **quarterly in advance**, consistent with Section 3.2(a) of the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise specified in Schedule B.  
(b) The first instalment of Rent and Service Charges for the Additional Premises shall be invoiced on or around the **Addendum Commencement Date** and shall cover the period from the Addendum Commencement Date to the end of the then‑current quarter.  
(c) Subsequent instalments shall be invoiced on or about the first day of each calendar quarter.  
(d) Any one‑off charges related to fit‑out, reconfiguration, or additional services specific to the Additional Premises shall be invoiced in accordance with the relevant written agreements or Change Orders.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  
(a) Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement (Late Payment) shall apply to this Addendum, except that all references to the payment due date shall be construed by reference to the **Net 30** terms specified in Section 3.2 of this Addendum.  
(b) Accordingly, if Client fails to pay any undisputed amount under this Addendum by the due date, Vendor may charge interest at the rate specified in Section 3.4(a) of the Parent Agreement (4% per annum above the Bank of England base rate).  
(c) Vendor shall not charge interest on bona fide disputed amounts, provided Client has notified Vendor in accordance with Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement, as applied here.  
(d) Vendor’s right to suspend non‑essential Services under Section 3.4(c) of the Parent Agreement shall extend to Services provided under this Addendum, provided such suspension is proportionate and does not pose a health or safety risk.

3.5 **Utilities and Operating Expenses – Additional Premises.**  
(a) Utilities serving the Additional Premises shall be billed to Client on the same basis as set out in Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement, i.e., sub‑metered and invoiced at cost plus an administrative fee not exceeding five percent (5%), or allocated on a fair and reasonable pro rata basis where sub‑metering is not feasible.  
(b) Operating Expenses relating to the Additional Premises (e.g., security, cleaning of Common Areas serving the Additional Premises, building management, and common area utilities) shall be included within the Service Charge defined in Section 3.1(b) of this Addendum and described in Schedule B.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Section 3.6 of the Parent Agreement (Disputed Amounts) shall apply to invoices issued under this Addendum, save that the timelines for payment shall reflect the **Net 30** terms described in Section 3.2 above.

3.7 **Security Deposit.**  
(a) Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement (Security Deposit) remains in full force and effect.  
(b) The Parties agree that, in light of the Additional Premises and associated Addendum Contract Value, Vendor may request an **increase in the Security Deposit**, up to a maximum of an additional **one (1) month’s Base Rent** attributable to the Additional Premises, to be detailed in **Schedule B**.  
(c) Any additional Security Deposit shall be governed by the same rules as set out in Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement (segregated account, limited application, replenishment, and return).

3.8 **Taxes.**  
Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement (Taxes) shall apply to all payments due under this Addendum. All amounts are exclusive of VAT and other applicable Taxes, for which Client shall remain responsible.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION (ADDENDUM)

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
(a) The Parent Agreement Term (Section 4.1) remains unchanged and continues until the Expiration Date of **December 01, 2028**, unless earlier terminated.  
(b) The lease of the Additional Premises under this Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Commencement Date**, which shall be the later of: (i) the Addendum Effective Date; and (ii) the date on which Vendor grants Client access to and legal possession of the Additional Premises in Category A condition (as evidenced by the Supplemental Handover Report).  
(c) The Addendum Term shall continue until the **Addendum Expiration Date of September 07, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or Article IV of the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Renewal Option.**  
(a) The renewal option in Section 4.2 of the Parent Agreement (one‑time three‑year renewal) shall, unless otherwise agreed in writing, apply to the **Adjusted Premises**, including the Additional Premises, provided that Client is not in material breach at the time of exercising the renewal option.  
(b) The commercial terms (including Rent) applicable to the Additional Premises during any renewal term shall be agreed by the Parties as part of the overall renewal negotiations, reflecting then‑current market conditions, as envisaged in Section 4.2(a) of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience – Client‑Friendly Terms.**  
(a) In addition to the termination rights in Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement, either Party may terminate this Addendum **with respect to the Additional Premises only**, for convenience, by providing the other Party with not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice**.  
(b) Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement with respect to the original Premises, unless expressly stated in the notice and accepted by the other Party.  
(c) In the event of termination for convenience by Client under this Addendum:

  (i) Client shall pay all amounts due under this Addendum up to the effective date of termination;  
  (ii) Client shall not be liable for any early termination fee, save for any amortised or non‑refundable amounts specifically identified in Schedule B (e.g., landlord‑funded fit‑out for the Additional Premises), which may be subject to a fair and proportionate pro rata reimbursement; and  
  (iii) Vendor shall use reasonable endeavours to mitigate any loss, for example by re‑letting the Additional Premises.

(d) In the event of termination for convenience by Vendor under this Addendum:

  (i) Vendor shall refund any prepaid amounts relating to the period after the effective date of termination for the Additional Premises; and  
  (ii) Vendor shall use reasonable endeavours to support Client’s transition to alternative space within the building or at another Vendor‑managed site, where commercially and operationally feasible.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
(a) Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement (Termination for Cause) shall apply equally to material breaches of this Addendum.  
(b) If a Party materially breaches this Addendum (including non‑payment of amounts due hereunder) and fails to cure such breach within the applicable cure periods set forth in Section 4.4(a)–(c) of the Parent Agreement, the non‑breaching Party may, at its option, terminate:  

  (i) this Addendum only; or  
  (ii) this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, where the breach is so fundamental as to frustrate the purpose of the overall lease relationship.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date.  
(b) Upon such termination or expiry, the Parties’ respective obligations regarding vacating the Premises, inspections, payment of outstanding amounts, and survival of certain provisions shall be governed by **Section 4.5 (Effects of Termination or Expiry)** of the Parent Agreement, applied to the **Adjusted Premises**, including the Additional Premises.  
(c) Any specific fee amortisation or fit‑out recovery arrangements applicable solely to the Additional Premises shall be handled in accordance with Schedule B and any relevant fit‑out agreements, subject to the client‑friendly principles in Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement and Section 4.3 of this Addendum.

4.6 **Holdover – Additional Premises.**  
The holdover provisions in Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement (month‑to‑month basis, 125% holdover rent) shall apply equally to Client’s occupancy of the Additional Premises after the Addendum Expiration Date or earlier termination, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## ARTICLE IX  
LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

9.1 **Exclusion of Certain Damages.**  
Section 9.1 of the Parent Agreement (exclusion of indirect, consequential, special damages) shall apply equally to this Addendum.

9.2 **Cap on Liability – Modified for Addendum.**  
Notwithstanding Section 9.2 of the Parent Agreement (cap at two (2) times the total fees paid during the preceding twelve months), the Parties agree that, with respect to claims arising **solely under this Addendum**:

(a) Each Party’s total aggregate liability to the other arising out of or in connection with this Addendum (whether in contract, tort, or otherwise) shall not exceed an amount equal to **three (3) times the total fees actually paid by Client to Vendor under this Addendum during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim**; and  
(b) With respect to claims involving both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the combined cap shall not exceed the sum of (i) the cap under Section 9.2 of the Parent Agreement, plus (ii) the cap under Section 9.2(a) of this Addendum, provided that double recovery for the same loss is prohibited.

9.3 **Exceptions to Limitations.**  
The exceptions in Section 9.3 of the Parent Agreement (death/personal injury, fraud, undisputed payment obligations, certain indemnities, and specified breaches of confidentiality/data protection) shall apply equally to the liability cap in Section 9.2 of this Addendum. To the extent permitted by law, such liabilities may remain uncapped or capped only as allowed by the Parent Agreement.

9.4 **Mitigation.**  
The duty to mitigate losses set out in Section 9.4 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to each Party’s claims under this Addendum.
