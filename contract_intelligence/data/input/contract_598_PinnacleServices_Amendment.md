## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent SOW.**  
This Amendment **modifies and expands** the scope of Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent SOW. Except as expressly provided herein:

- The definitions of **Services**, **Deliverables**, **Milestones**, **Specifications**, and **Service Levels** in the Parent SOW shall apply to the Extended Services and Enhanced Deliverables.
- The obligations regarding performance standards in **Section 2.6 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent SOW remain in effect and are **supplemented** (not reduced) by the additional obligations in this Article II.

2.2 **Overview of Extended Services.**  
In addition to the Services already provided under the Parent SOW, Vendor shall perform the following Extended Services for Client:

2.2.1 **Enterprise Platform Expansion.**  
(a) Extend the unified enterprise application platform described in **Section 2.1(a)** of the Parent SOW to cover at least three (3) Additional Business Units, including Contoso’s regional operations in Asia-Pacific and Europe;  
(b) Design and implement multi-entity, multi-currency capabilities and segregated data domains to enable secure and compliant operation for Additional Business Units;  
(c) Update and expand the **Solution Design Document ("SDD")** referenced in **Section 2.2.1(b)** of the Parent SOW to reflect the expanded architecture, including cross-region data flows and resiliency patterns;  
(d) Conduct additional stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops with newly onboarded business units and update the Requirements Documentation referenced in **Section 2.3(a)** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.2 **Advanced Application Enhancements.**  
(a) Develop and deploy advanced analytics modules, including but not limited to predictive reporting, near-real-time dashboards, and KPI scorecards for senior management;  
(b) Implement additional APIs and integration components to link with new external systems not previously in scope under **Section 2.2.2(b)** of the Parent SOW (including third-party risk scoring platforms, payment gateways, and regulatory reporting systems);  
(c) Enhance role-based access control, fine-grained authorization, and audit logging beyond the baseline defined in **Section 2.2.2(c)** of the Parent SOW, to accommodate cross-jurisdiction compliance requirements;  
(d) Produce updated technical documentation, configuration guides, and release notes for each new feature and release introduced under this Amendment, consistent with **Section 2.2.2(d)** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.3 **Extended Data Migration & Ongoing Data Integration.**  
(a) Perform additional data profiling, mapping, and migration from newly identified legacy systems owned by Additional Business Units, building upon the data migration activities described in **Section 2.2.3 of the Parent SOW**;  
(b) Establish ongoing ETL and data synchronization pipelines to support near real-time updates between the enterprise platform and external data sources;  
(c) Maintain and update data reconciliation reports, including new reconciliation views for Additional Business Units, and align them with the acceptance procedures in **Section 2.5 (Acceptance Criteria and Procedures)** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.4 **Performance, Scalability, and Security Hardening.**  
(a) Conduct dedicated performance and load testing beyond the baseline testing set forth in **Section 2.2.4(a)** of the Parent SOW, targeting at least a 2x increase in user concurrency and transaction throughput;  
(b) Implement infrastructure scaling strategies (horizontal and vertical) for High Availability Components, including auto-scaling policies and disaster recovery improvements;  
(c) Perform advanced security assessments, including penetration testing, configuration hardening, and remediation activities aligned with Client’s internal security policies and applicable Data Protection Laws referenced in **Section 1.7 of the Parent SOW**;  
(d) Document all performance and security test results, including remediation plans, in updated **Test Summary Reports** as contemplated in **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent SOW.

2.2.5 **Enhanced Managed Services & Application Support.**  
In addition to the managed services described in **Section 2.2.6 of the Parent SOW**, Vendor shall:  
(a) Increase support coverage to a **24x7 follow-the-sun model** for Priority 1 and Priority 2 incidents as defined in **Section 12.3 of the Parent SOW**, and to extended local business hours (at least 12x5) for Priority 3 and 4 incidents;  
(b) Provide dedicated Level 3 engineering resources for complex problem management, root-cause analysis, and continuous improvement;  
(c) Maintain separate support queues and SLA measurements for Additional Business Units and produce consolidated monthly operational reports that incorporate the expanded SLA metrics, building on **Section 2.2.6(e)** and **Section 12.4** of the Parent SOW;  
(d) Implement proactive monitoring and anomaly detection using advanced tooling, with thresholds and alerting configured in consultation with Client’s IT team.

2.2.6 **Regulatory Compliance Enablement.**  
(a) Design and configure the platform to facilitate compliance with industry-specific regulations applicable to Client (e.g., financial services regulations, where applicable), in addition to the general Data Protection Laws referenced in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent SOW;  
(b) Provide configurable workflows and audit trails to support regulatory reporting, approvals, and attestations;  
(c) Assist Client in providing documentation and technical responses to regulator inquiries directly related to the Extended Services and Enhanced Deliverables.

2.2.7 **Training, Knowledge Transfer & Change Adoption.**  
(a) In addition to training described in **Section 2.2.8 of the Parent SOW**, provide tailored training programs for Additional Business Units, including train-the-trainer sessions and role-based online training;  
(b) Develop enhanced e-learning modules, recorded video tutorials, and localized quick reference guides (where reasonably practicable);  
(c) Conduct periodic refresher training sessions at least twice per year during the Amendment Term.

2.3 **Enhanced Deliverables.**  
Without limiting **Section 2.3 of the Parent SOW**, the Vendor shall deliver at a minimum:

(a) Updated and expanded **Requirements Documentation and Business Process Maps** for all Additional Business Units;  
(b) Revised **Solution Design Document (SDD)** and Technical Architecture Diagrams reflecting multi-region, multi-entity operations;  
(c) New and updated **Configured Application Modules and Integration Components**, including advanced analytics, new APIs, and regulatory reporting modules;  
(d) Additional **Data Migration Scripts, Mapping Documents, and Reconciliation Reports** for new legacy sources;  
(e) Enhanced **Test Plans, Performance/Load Test Cases, Test Summary Reports, and Defect Logs** specifically addressing performance, scalability, and security;  
(f) Updated **Deployment and Cutover Plans** for each Additional Business Unit, including rollback and contingency procedures;  
(g) Enhanced **Operational Runbooks and Support Procedures**, including defined escalation paths, incident handling playbooks, and on-call rotations;  
(h) Expanded **Training Materials** and detailed **Records of Training Sessions** for legacy and new users;  
(i) **Monthly Operational and SLA Performance Reports** that reflect the Enhanced SLAs defined in this Amendment; and  
(j) Any other documents, code repositories, and artifacts agreed in writing by the Parties via Change Order in accordance with **Article XIII of the Parent SOW**.

2.4 **Supplemental Milestones & Timelines.**  
The following Supplemental Milestones shall **supplement and, where explicitly stated, supersede** the Milestones set forth in **Section 2.4 of the Parent SOW** with respect to the Extended Services:

(a) **Supplemental Milestone A – Multi-Entity Design Completion:**  
- Completion of updated requirements and design for Additional Business Units and multi-entity architecture;  
- Target completion: within **120 days** of the Amendment Effective Date.

(b) **Supplemental Milestone B – Extended Platform Configuration & Initial Enhancements:**  
- Completion of configuration and development of advanced analytics, additional APIs, and initial integration for at least one Additional Business Unit;  
- Target completion: within **240 days** of the Amendment Effective Date.

(c) **Supplemental Milestone C – Multi-Region UAT & Phase 3 Go-Live:**  
- Completion of user acceptance testing for Extended Services and successful production go-live for Additional Business Units in at least two regions;  
- Target completion: within **360 days** of the Amendment Effective Date.

(d) **Supplemental Milestone D – Performance & Security Hardening Completion:**  
- Completion of performance load testing, security hardening, and remediation actions;  
- Target completion: within **420 days** of the Amendment Effective Date.

(e) **Supplemental Milestone E – Transition to Enhanced Steady-State Support:**  
- Formal transition to the Enhanced Managed Services environment, with Enhanced SLAs in effect;  
- Target completion: no later than **18 months** after the Amendment Effective Date.

The acceptance of Enhanced Deliverables tied to these Supplemental Milestones shall be conducted in accordance with **Section 2.5 (Acceptance Criteria and Procedures)** of the Parent SOW, which remains fully applicable to this Amendment.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Vendor shall perform the Extended Services:

(a) In a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with **Section 2.6(a)** of the Parent SOW and in accordance with enhanced best practices appropriate for high-risk, multi-region enterprise platforms;  
(b) In compliance with the Enhanced SLAs in **Article XII** of this Amendment, which build upon and refine **Article XII of the Parent SOW**;  
(c) In compliance with all Applicable Law, including the Data Protection Laws referenced in **Section 1.7 of the Parent SOW**; and  
(d) With appropriate due care to avoid disruption to Client’s business, including coordination of major releases outside peak hours and advance communication of maintenance windows, in a manner consistent with **Section 2.6(d)** of the Parent SOW.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities (Supplement).**  
In addition to Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.7 of the Parent SOW**, Client shall:

(a) Ensure that Additional Business Units provide timely access to stakeholders, systems, and local regulatory requirements;  
(b) Assign local coordinators for each Additional Business Unit to assist with requirements, testing, and change management;  
(c) Provide baseline performance targets and acceptable risk thresholds for mission critical components; and  
(d) Ensure that Client’s internal IT and security teams cooperate reasonably with Vendor in connection with performance tests, penetration testing, and remediation activities.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amended Contract Value.**  
The Parties agree that, in addition to the original **Contract Value of USD $2,907,957** specified in **Section 3.1 of the Parent SOW**, the Extended Services and Enhanced Deliverables under this Amendment shall be provided for an additional amount of **USD $4,897,094** (the "**Amendment Contract Value**).  

For clarity:

- The **aggregate** total value of the engagement under the Parent SOW **as amended** hereby shall be **USD $7,805,051**.  
- This Amendment Contract Value is separate from and **in addition to** any amounts contemplated in the **Contingency and Optional Services Pool** of **USD $157,957** referenced in **Section 3.2(c) of the Parent SOW**.

3.2 **Fee Structure Under this Amendment.**  
The Amendment Contract Value shall be comprised of:

(a) **Fixed-Price Expansion & Enhancement Fees:** **USD $2,600,000**, covering design, development, data migration for Additional Business Units, performance and security hardening, and associated testing and deployment activities relating to the Supplemental Milestones;  

(b) **Enhanced Managed Services & Support Fees:** **USD $2,000,000**, representing additional recurring fees for increased coverage (24x7), expanded user base, and Enhanced SLAs over the Amendment Term; and  

(c) **Strategic Advisory & Compliance Services Pool:** **USD $297,094**, to be utilized for mutually agreed regulatory compliance advisory services, advanced architecture consulting, and emergent scope relating to new regulations, documented via Change Orders.

3.3 **Milestone-Based Payments (Expansion & Enhancements).**  
The fixed-price expansion and enhancement Fees in Section 3.2(a) shall be invoiced upon completion and Client’s acceptance (per **Section 2.5 of the Parent SOW**) of the following Supplemental Milestones:

(a) **Supplemental Milestone A – Multi-Entity Design Completion:**  
- 20% of expansion & enhancement Fees = **USD $520,000**;

(b) **Supplemental Milestone B – Extended Platform Configuration & Initial Enhancements:**  
- 30% of expansion & enhancement Fees = **USD $780,000**;

(c) **Supplemental Milestone C – Multi-Region UAT & Phase 3 Go-Live:**  
- 30% of expansion & enhancement Fees = **USD $780,000**;

(d) **Supplemental Milestone D – Performance & Security Hardening Completion:**  
- 20% of expansion & enhancement Fees = **USD $520,000**.

3.4 **Enhanced Recurring Managed Services Fees.**  
(a) The Enhanced Managed Services & Support Fees in Section 3.2(b) shall be invoiced in equal quarterly installments of **USD $250,000** over the applicable Amendment Term for Enhanced Managed Services, commencing on the date of the Supplemental Milestone E (Transition to Enhanced Steady-State Support) or such other date as mutually agreed in writing.  
(b) These Enhanced Managed Services Fees are **in addition to** and independent from the **Managed Services & Support Fees** of **USD $1,000,000** provided in **Section 3.2(b) of the Parent SOW**.  
(c) The service credits contemplated in **Section 12.5 of the Parent SOW**, as modified in **Article XII of this Amendment**, shall apply against the Enhanced Managed Services & Support Fees only in accordance with the terms set forth herein.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
(a) Section 3.5 of the Parent SOW shall continue to apply to all reimbursable expenses under this Amendment.  
(b) For the Extended Services, Vendor may incur reasonable, pre-approved travel and living expenses for on-site work in locations where Additional Business Units operate, and such expenses shall be reimbursable at cost, subject to Client’s then-current travel and expense policies.  
(c) Vendor shall include references to **AMD-PIN-202401-598** on all expense reimbursement invoices related solely to this Amendment.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
Taxes shall be handled consistent with **Section 3.6 of the Parent SOW**, which remains in full force and effect. For the avoidance of doubt, the Amendment Contract Value is exclusive of any applicable taxes.

3.7 **Invoicing and Payment Terms – Net 60.**  
(a) Vendor shall issue invoices in accordance with the milestone and recurring structures set out in **Sections 3.3 and 3.4** of this Amendment.  
(b) **Payment Terms for this Amendment shall be “Net 60”**: Client shall pay all undisputed amounts within **sixty (60) days** from the date of invoice. This modifies **Section 3.7(b) (Net 30)** of the Parent SOW **solely** with respect to invoices issued under this Amendment (Ref: **AMD-PIN-202401-598**).  
(c) Invoices shall reference both the Parent SOW Contract Reference Number **SOW-PIN-202401-521** and this Amendment’s Contract Reference Number **AMD-PIN-202401-598**, identify the relevant Milestone or billing period, and include sufficient detail to support the amounts invoiced.

3.8 **Disputed Invoices.**  
The provisions of **Section 3.8 (Disputed Invoices)** of the Parent SOW shall apply to invoices issued under this Amendment, except that the timeframe for Client to notify Vendor of any dispute remains fifteen (15) Business Days from receipt of the invoice.

3.9 **Late Payment.**  
(a) The interest and suspension rights set forth in **Section 3.9** of the Parent SOW shall apply to undisputed amounts invoiced under this Amendment.  
(b) For the avoidance of doubt, interest shall accrue starting from the due date under the **Net 60** term.

3.10 **No Set-Off.**  
The prohibition on set-off in **Section 3.10** of the Parent SOW shall apply to this Amendment, subject to amounts properly disputed per Section 3.8 above.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.**  
This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date (**April 25, 2024**) and shall remain in effect until **October 18, 2026** (the "**Amendment Expiration Date**"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent SOW Term.**  
(a) The Term of the Parent SOW as defined in **Section 4.1** (through January 20, 2027) remains unchanged.  
(b) The Parties acknowledge that the Amendment Expiration Date is earlier than the Parent SOW’s Expiration Date. Upon the Amendment Expiration Date, all Extended Services and Enhanced SLAs shall cease, except to the extent incorporated into a subsequent amendment or Change Order, but the underlying Parent SOW shall continue in force in accordance with its terms.

4.3 **Renewal of Amendment.**  
Any extension or renewal of this Amendment shall require the Parties’ mutual written agreement at least **ninety (90) days** prior to the Amendment Expiration Date and shall be documented as a further amendment or Change Order referencing both **SOW-PIN-202401-521** and **AMD-PIN-202401-598**.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience (Amendment).**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent SOW (60 days’ notice), either Party may terminate this Amendment for convenience, without cause, by providing the other Party with not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice, explicitly referencing this Amendment. For clarity, such termination for convenience shall:

(a) Apply only to this Amendment and the Extended Services, unless the notice expressly states that the Parent SOW is also terminated;  
(b) Entitle Vendor to payment for all Extended Services performed and Enhanced Deliverables accepted up to the effective date of termination, plus any non-cancellable commitments properly incurred in reliance on this Amendment; and  
(c) Not affect either Party’s rights and obligations under the Parent SOW unless expressly stated.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.**  
The termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.4 of the Parent SOW** shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Amendment. A material breach of this Amendment shall constitute a materialrench of the Parent SOW and vice versa, if the breach affects obligations that are common or interdependent.

4.6 **Termination for Persistent SLA Failures (Enhanced SLAs).**  
The rights in **Section 4.5 of the Parent SOW (Termination for Persistent SLA Failures)** shall also apply to the Enhanced SLAs set forth in **Article XII of this Amendment**. Persistent failures of the Enhanced SLAs shall give Client the right, upon 30 days’ written notice, to terminate this Amendment in whole or in part, and, at Client’s option, the associated managed services components of the Parent SOW.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Parent SOW on this Amendment.**  
If the Parent SOW is terminated or expires for any reason:

(a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate concurrently without any further action by the Parties;  
(b) Vendor shall cease performance of the Extended Services, except as reasonably required to perform agreed Transition Assistance Services;  
(c) All unpaid but accrued fees for Extended Services and Enhanced Managed Services, and all non-cancellable commitments, shall become immediately due and payable, subject to the dispute provisions; and  
(d) The post-termination obligations and survival provisions of **Section 4.6 and 4.8 of the Parent SOW**, and of this Amendment, shall apply.

4.8 **Transition Assistance Services.**  
Vendor shall provide Transition Assistance Services with respect to the Extended Services for up to ninety (90) days following termination or expiration of this Amendment, consistent with **Section 4.7 of the Parent SOW**, at the Vendor’s then-current time and materials rates or such other rates as may be agreed in writing.

4.9 **Survival.**  
In addition to the provisions of **Section 4.8 of the Parent SOW**, the following Articles and Sections of this Amendment shall survive expiration or termination of this Amendment: **Article III (to the extent of accrued payment obligations), Article V (Confidentiality), Article VI (IP), Article VII (Representations and Warranties) to the extent of any ongoing commitments, Article VIII (Indemnification), Article IX (Limitation of Liability), Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy), Article XIV (Dispute Resolution), and Article XVI (General Provisions)**.

## ARTICLE XII – SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENTS (SLAs)

12.1 **Enhanced Availability Commitment.**  
For hosted or managed components under Vendor’s control related to the Extended Services, Vendor shall target system availability of at least **99.95%** per calendar month, excluding scheduled maintenance and Force Majeure Events, measured at the primary production environment level.

12.2 **Scheduled Maintenance.**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Provide at least **seven (7) Business Days’** prior notice for planned maintenance that may impact availability;  
(b) Schedule such maintenance during off-peak hours for each affected region and Additional Business Unit; and  
(c) Ensure that total scheduled maintenance does not exceed eight (8) hours per calendar month per environment, absent Client’s prior written approval.

12.3 **Incident Response and Resolution Times (Enhanced).**  
The incident categories from **Section 12.3 of the Parent SOW** apply, with the following enhanced target times:

- **Priority 1 (Critical)** – System outage or major functionality unavailable for mission-critical processes:  
  - Response: within **30 minutes** (24x7)  
  - Target resolution or workaround: within **2 hours**  

- **Priority 2 (High)** – Significant degradation or functionality impacted, workaround available but business materially affected:  
  - Response: within **1 hour** (24x7)  
  - Target resolution: within **8 hours**  

- **Priority 3 (Medium)** – Non-critical issue, minor impairment:  
  - Response: within **4 Business Hours**  
  - Target resolution: within **2 Business Days**  

- **Priority 4 (Low)** – Cosmetic issues or general inquiries:  
  - Response: within **1 Business Day**  
  - Target resolution: in next scheduled release or as mutually agreed.

12.4 **SLA Measurement and Reporting.**  
Vendor shall provide **monthly SLA reports** that include:

(a) Uptime statistics for each environment relevant to the Extended Services;  
(b) Incident logs with timestamps, priorities, response and resolution times;  
(c) Root cause analyses (RCAs) for all Priority 1 incidents and repeated Priority 2 incidents;  
(d) Trend analyses and recommendations for continuous improvement.

12.5 **Remedies for Enhanced SLA Breaches.**  
(a) If Vendor fails to meet the **99.95% availability** commitment in a given calendar month, Client shall be entitled to **service credits** against the Enhanced Managed Services Fees as follows:  
   - For each 0.05% shortfall in availability below 99.95% (down to 99.5%), a service credit equal to **3%** of the monthly Enhanced Managed Services Fees;  
   - For availability below 99.5%, an additional service credit of **20%** of the monthly Enhanced Managed Services Fees.

(b) If Vendor fails to meet Priority 1 or Priority 2 response/resolution targets in more than three (3) incidents in any calendar month, Client shall be entitled to an additional service credit of **5%** of the monthly Enhanced Managed Services Fees for that month.

(c) Service credits shall be applied against future invoices, and in the absence of future invoices, shall be refunded in cash within sixty (60) days after written demand.

(d) Service credits shall be Client’s sole and exclusive **monetary** remedy for Enhanced SLA failures, without prejudice to Client’s rights of termination under **Section 4.6 of this Amendment** and **Section 4.5 of the Parent SOW**.
