12.3.1 The Vendor shall respond to support requests in accordance with the following target response times, measured from the time of the Client’s submission of a support ticket:

- **Severity 1 (Critical)** – Complete loss of production service or severe business impact with no workaround: initial response within one (1) hour;  
- **Severity 2 (High)** – Significant degradation of service with limited workaround: initial response within four (4) hours;  
- **Severity 3 (Medium)** – Partial, non-critical functionality impacted: initial response within one (1) Business Day;  
- **Severity 4 (Low)** – General questions, enhancement requests: initial response within two (2) Business Days.

12.3.2 The Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve issues in a timely manner, taking into account the severity and complexity of the issue.

### 12.4 Service Credits

12.4.1 If the Vendor fails to meet the Uptime Commitment in any calendar month, the Client shall be eligible for service credits as follows:

- Uptime between 99.0% and 99.9%: 5% of the monthly subscription Fees for the affected Software;  
- Uptime between 98.0% and 99.0%: 10% of the monthly subscription Fees for the affected Software;  
- Uptime below 98.0%: 20% of the monthly subscription Fees for the affected Software.

12.4.2 The Client must request service credits in writing within thirty (30) days following the end of the month in which the failure occurred. Service credits shall be applied to future invoices and shall not be redeemable for cash. Service credits are the Client’s sole and exclusive remedy for failure to meet the Uptime Commitment, subject to other remedies expressly provided under this Agreement.
