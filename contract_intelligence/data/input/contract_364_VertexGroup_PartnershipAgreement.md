## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

### 2.1 General Description of Services
2.1.1 Vertex shall provide to Contoso a comprehensive suite of technology consulting, software development, systems integration, and managed services designed to support Contoso’s enterprise platforms, including but not limited to customer relationship management (CRM), enterprise resource planning (ERP), data analytics, and related infrastructure.

2.1.2 The Services shall be further described in one or more SOWs executed under this Agreement. Each SOW shall specify the scope of Services, Deliverables, acceptance criteria, dependencies, resource commitments, and any specific Service Levels, in addition to those set out in Article XII.

### 2.2 Specific Service Categories
Without limiting the generality of Section 2.1, the Services may include, but are not limited to, the following categories:

2.2.1 **Consulting and Advisory Services.**  
Vertex shall provide strategic and operational consulting services, including requirements gathering, business process analysis, solution architecture design, technology roadmap development, and project governance advisory. Vertex shall work collaboratively with Contoso’s designated stakeholders to align the proposed solutions with Contoso’s business objectives.

2.2.2 **Software Development and Customization.**  
Vertex shall design, develop, configure, and customize software applications, modules, and interfaces in accordance with the specifications set forth in the applicable SOW. This may include custom code development, system integrations (APIs, middleware, data connectors), user interface design, and configuration of off-the-shelf platforms. All development work shall be performed in accordance with Good Industry Practice, Vertex’s secure development lifecycle practices, and any coding standards agreed in writing by the Parties.

2.2.3 **Implementation and Integration Services.**  
Vertex shall provide implementation services for new systems or enhancements, including installation, configuration, data migration, integration with existing systems, testing, and deployment. Vertex shall coordinate with Contoso’s internal IT team and any Third-Party vendors to ensure seamless integration and minimal disruption to Contoso’s operations.

2.2.4 **Managed Services and Support.**  
Vertex may provide ongoing managed services, including application maintenance, incident management, problem resolution, performance monitoring, and patching, as specified in the applicable SOW. Vertex shall provide support in accordance with the Service Levels defined in Article XII, including tiered support, response and resolution times, and escalation procedures.

2.2.5 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  
Where specified in an SOW, Vertex shall provide training to Contoso’s personnel, including “train-the-trainer” sessions, user guides, operational run-books, and other materials reasonably necessary to enable Contoso to use and administer the Deliverables effectively.

### 2.3 Deliverables
2.3.1 Each SOW shall identify the Deliverables to be provided by Vertex. Deliverables may include, without limitation: software modules, configuration files, technical design documents, architectural diagrams, test plans and results, operational procedures, user documentation, and training materials.

2.3.2 Unless otherwise stated in an SOW, Deliverables shall be provided in English and in commonly used electronic formats (e.g., PDF, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, source code in agreed programming languages and repository formats).

### 2.4 Performance Standards
2.4.1 Vertex shall perform the Services and deliver the Deliverables:

- (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with Good Industry Practice;  
- (b) using personnel who are appropriately skilled, qualified, and experienced;  
- (c) in compliance with all Applicable Laws and any reasonable written policies of Contoso that are communicated to Vertex in advance (including, without limitation, information security, health and safety, and site access policies); and  
- (d) in accordance with the timelines, milestones, and Service Levels set forth in this Agreement and the applicable SOW.

2.4.2 Vertex shall assign a project manager for each SOW, who shall be responsible for day-to-day coordination, reporting, and communication with Contoso. Contoso shall designate a corresponding project manager or primary point of contact.

### 2.5 Timelines and Milestones
2.5.1 Each SOW shall include a detailed project plan or schedule, identifying key milestones, deliverable due dates, dependencies, and resource allocations. Vertex shall use commercially reasonable efforts to achieve each milestone and deliver Deliverables by the agreed dates.

2.5.2 Contoso acknowledges that timely performance by Vertex is dependent upon Contoso’s timely cooperation, including provision of necessary information, access to systems and personnel, and prompt review and feedback on Deliverables. Any delays attributable to Contoso or its third-party suppliers shall entitle Vertex to reasonable extensions of the relevant timelines and may result in additional fees in accordance with Section 14.4.

### 2.6 Acceptance of Deliverables
2.6.1 Unless otherwise provided in an SOW, upon delivery of a Deliverable, Contoso shall have a period of **ten (10) Business Days** (the “Acceptance Period”) to review and test the Deliverable to determine whether it materially conforms to the acceptance criteria set forth in the applicable SOW.

2.6.2 If Contoso determines, acting reasonably, that the Deliverable does not materially conform to the acceptance criteria, Contoso shall notify Vertex in writing before the expiry of the Acceptance Period, specifying in reasonable detail the nature of the non-conformity.

2.6.3 Upon receipt of such notice, Vertex shall, at its own cost, promptly correct the non-conformities and re-submit the Deliverable for acceptance, whereupon the Acceptance Period shall re-commence for a further ten (10) Business Days.

2.6.4 If Contoso does not notify Vertex of any non-conformity within the applicable Acceptance Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted. Use of the Deliverable in production by Contoso for a period exceeding thirty (30) days shall also constitute acceptance.

### 2.7 Subcontracting
2.7.1 Vertex may, in its discretion, subcontract any portion of the Services to third parties, provided that Vertex shall remain fully responsible and liable for the acts and omissions of any subcontractors as if they were the acts and omissions of Vertex.

2.7.2 Upon Contoso’s written request, Vertex shall provide details of subcontractors engaged in the provision of Services that involve access to Contoso’s systems or Personal Data. Vertex shall ensure that all such subcontractors are bound by written agreements that impose confidentiality, security, and data protection obligations no less protective than those set out in this Agreement.

### 2.8 Relationship Framework
2.8.1 The Parties agree that, during the Term, Vertex shall be treated as a preferred strategic vendor for the categories of Services described herein. Contoso may, however, procure similar or competing services from third parties, unless otherwise expressly stated in an SOW.

2.8.2 Nothing in this Agreement shall obligate Contoso to issue a minimum number of SOWs or to purchase any minimum amount of Services, except as may be expressly set forth in a particular SOW.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Fees and Contract Value
3.1.1 In consideration for the Services and Deliverables provided under this Agreement, Contoso shall pay to Vertex fees in accordance with the fee schedules and pricing models set forth in the applicable SOWs, which may include fixed fees, time-and-materials rates, milestone-based payments, and/or recurring fees.

3.1.2 The aggregate fees payable under all SOWs executed pursuant to this Agreement are anticipated to be approximately **US$3,826,889**, subject to adjustments for changes in scope, additional SOWs, or other fee modifications in accordance with this Agreement.

### 3.2 Invoicing and Payment Terms
3.2.1 Vertex shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the billing schedule specified in each SOW. In the absence of such specification, Vertex may invoice monthly in arrears for Services performed and expenses incurred.

3.2.2 All invoices shall be payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) days** from the date of invoice (“**Net 60**”), without any setoff or deduction, except as required by Applicable Law or as expressly permitted under this Agreement.

3.2.3 All amounts shall be invoiced and paid in United States Dollars (US$) unless otherwise specified in the applicable SOW. Payments shall be made by electronic funds transfer to the bank account designated in writing by Vertex.

### 3.3 Late Payment
3.3.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the rate of **1.5% per month**, or the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, whichever is lower, from the due date until the date of actual payment.

3.3.2 If any undisputed amount remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after written notice of non-payment from Vertex, Vertex may, without prejudice to its other rights and remedies, suspend performance of the Services (including access to hosted services or Deliverables) until all overdue amounts and applicable interest are paid in full. Suspension under this Section 3.3.2 shall not constitute a breach by Vertex.

### 3.4 Disputed Amounts
3.4.1 If Contoso disputes any portion of an invoice in good faith, Contoso shall notify Vertex in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of the invoice date, specifying the nature and amount of the dispute. Contoso shall pay the undisputed portion of the invoice in accordance with Section 3.2.

3.4.2 The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any invoice disputes promptly. Upon resolution, Contoso shall pay any agreed amounts due within ten (10) Business Days.

### 3.5 Taxes
3.5.1 All fees and charges under this Agreement are exclusive of any applicable taxes, including value-added tax (VAT), sales tax, use tax, withholding tax, or other similar charges. Contoso shall be responsible for payment of all such taxes, except for taxes based on Vertex’s net income, property, or employees.

3.5.2 If Contoso is required by Applicable Law to withhold any taxes from payments to Vertex, Contoso shall (a) withhold only such amounts as required by law; (b) timely remit such taxes to the appropriate authority; and (c) provide Vertex with official receipts or other documentation evidencing such payment, sufficient to enable Vertex to claim a tax credit or refund.

### 3.6 Expenses and Reimbursement
3.6.1 Contoso shall reimburse Vertex for reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including travel, accommodation, and subsistence costs, in accordance with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies, provided that such policies have been provided to Vertex in advance in writing.

3.6.2 Vertex shall provide appropriate documentation or receipts for reimbursable expenses. Expenses shall be invoiced on a monthly basis or as otherwise specified in the relevant SOW.

### 3.7 Fee Adjustments
3.7.1 For multi-year SOWs, the Parties may agree in the SOW on annual fee adjustments, which may be linked to a recognized inflation index (such as the UK Consumer Price Index) or fixed percentage increases.

3.7.2 Any changes to the scope of Services that impact the fees shall be handled in accordance with the change management procedures set forth in Article XIII.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term
4.1.1 This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **16 April 2028** (the “**Initial Term**”).

4.1.2 Upon expiration of the Initial Term, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one (1) year periods (each, a “Renewal Term”) unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal to the other Party at least ninety (90) days prior to the end of the then-current Term.

4.1.3 The Initial Term and any Renewal Terms are collectively referred to as the “**Term**”.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience
4.2.1 Contoso may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience, in whole or in part, by providing Vertex with at least **one hundred eighty (180) days** prior written notice.

4.2.2 Vertex may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for convenience, in whole or in part, by providing Contoso with at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior written notice.

4.2.3 In the event of termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall pay Vertex for: (a) all Services performed and Deliverables delivered (and accepted or deemed accepted) up to the effective date of termination; (b) any non-cancellable commitments incurred by Vertex; and (c) any reasonable wind-down costs mutually agreed by the Parties.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause
4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW for cause, upon written notice, if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement or such SOW and fails to cure such breach within sixty (60) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail.

4.3.2 Notwithstanding Section 4.3.1, either Party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) becomes insolvent; (b) enters into liquidation, administration, or receivership; (c) makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors; or (d) ceases, or threatens to cease, to carry on business in the ordinary course.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination
4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

- (a) all rights and licenses granted to Contoso under this Agreement shall cease, except as expressly stated otherwise;  
- (b) Vertex shall promptly cease performance of the Services (except as necessary to effect an orderly transition);  
- (c) each Party shall, upon request, return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information in accordance with Section 5.5; and  
- (d) Contoso shall pay all amounts due and outstanding for Services performed and approved expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination.

4.4.2 At Contoso’s written request, Vertex shall provide reasonable transition assistance for up to ninety (90) days after termination (or such other period as the Parties may agree in writing), at Vertex’s then-current time-and-materials rates, to facilitate the orderly transfer of the Services to Contoso or a third-party provider.

### 4.5 Survival
4.5.1 The provisions of this Agreement that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration shall so survive, including, without limitation, Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), XVI (General Provisions), and any payment obligations accrued as of the effective date of termination.
