## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **General Scope.**  
Gamma shall provide to Contoso, on a non-exclusive basis, the Software, Deliverables, and Services described in this Agreement and in one or more SOWs or purchase orders issued by Contoso and accepted by Gamma. The Parties agree that this Agreement establishes the master terms and conditions under which Contoso may procure from Gamma enterprise software licenses (or subscriptions), associated hardware appliances (if any), implementation and integration Services, and ongoing maintenance and support.

2.2 **Software Provision.**  
(a) Gamma shall provide Contoso with the right to use Gamma’s enterprise platform (the “Platform”), together with associated modules and tools, as specified in the applicable SOWs. The Platform may be provided either as (i) on-premises Software installed on Contoso’s infrastructure; or (ii) a hosted, cloud-based service operated by Gamma or its subcontractors, as specified in the SOW.  

(b) Gamma shall deliver all necessary license keys, access credentials, technical documentation, and installation guides reasonably required for Contoso to install, configure, and use the Software in accordance with this Agreement.

2.3 **Hardware (If Applicable).**  
To the extent specified in an SOW, Gamma shall supply hardware appliances, servers, or other physical components that are pre-configured or optimized for use with the Platform. Such hardware shall be new (or, if expressly agreed, manufacturer-refurbished), free from material defects in materials and workmanship, and accompanied by the manufacturer’s standard warranties. Title and risk of loss in hardware shall pass to Contoso upon delivery at the agreed delivery point, unless otherwise stated in the applicable SOW or purchase order.

2.4 **Implementation and Integration Services.**  
(a) Gamma shall plan, design, configure, install, and integrate the Platform and Deliverables into Contoso’s technical environment in accordance with the project plan, timelines, and Milestones set forth in the relevant SOW.  

(b) Implementation Services include, as applicable:  
   (i) requirements gathering and analysis;  
   (ii) solution and architecture design;  
   (iii) system configuration and parameterization;  
   (iv) development of integrations and interfaces with Contoso’s existing systems;  
   (v) data migration planning and support;  
   (vi) system testing, user acceptance testing (UAT) support, and performance tuning;  
   (vii) go-live planning and cutover support.

(c) Gamma shall assign personnel with appropriate skills and experience to perform the Services and shall use commercially reasonable efforts to maintain continuity of key personnel. Gamma shall be solely responsible for the supervision, control, and compensation of its personnel.

2.5 **Training.**  
Upon Contoso’s request and as described in the applicable SOW, Gamma shall provide training to Contoso’s personnel on the use, administration, and maintenance of the Platform and Deliverables. Training may be delivered on-site or remotely and shall be provided in English unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2.6 **Maintenance and Support.**  
(a) During the Term, and subject to Contoso’s payment of applicable support fees, Gamma shall provide maintenance and support Services in accordance with the Service Levels set forth in Article XII and any applicable SLA schedule.  

(b) Maintenance and support shall include:  
   (i) access to a help desk or support portal during the support hours referenced in Article XII;  
   (ii) correction of reproducible errors or defects;  
   (iii) provision of updates, upgrades, patches, and bug fixes to the Software as they become generally available;  
   (iv) reasonable assistance in resolving issues related to the operation of the Platform in Contoso’s environment.

2.7 **Performance Standards.**  
Gamma shall perform the Services:  
(a) in a professional and workmanlike manner, in accordance with generally accepted industry standards;  
(b) in compliance with Applicable Law; and  
(c) in accordance with the timelines, Milestones, and Service Levels specified in the applicable SOW.  

Gamma acknowledges that time is of the essence in respect of critical Milestones designated as such in an SOW. If Gamma anticipates any delay that may impact any Milestone or go-live date, Gamma shall promptly notify Contoso in writing, explaining the reasons and proposing a remedial plan.

2.8 **Acceptance of Deliverables.**  
(a) Each SOW shall specify acceptance criteria for Deliverables and any applicable acceptance testing procedures. In the absence of such specific criteria, Deliverables shall be considered accepted if they materially conform to the relevant specifications in the SOW.  

(b) Upon delivery of a Deliverable, Gamma shall notify Contoso in writing and Contoso shall have a reasonable period, not less than ten (10) Business Days (the “Acceptance Period”), to review and test the Deliverable. If Contoso reasonably determines that the Deliverable does not conform to the acceptance criteria, Contoso shall provide Gamma with written notice of rejection specifying the non-conformities.  

(c) Gamma shall, at its own cost, promptly correct the non-conformities and resubmit the Deliverable for re-testing. The Parties shall repeat the acceptance process as necessary. If a Deliverable fails acceptance after three (3) attempts, Contoso may, at its option and without prejudice to other remedies:  
   (i) accept the Deliverable subject to an equitable price reduction; or  
   (ii) reject the Deliverable and terminate the relevant portion of the SOW, with a right to a refund of amounts paid for such Deliverable.

2.9 **Client Responsibilities.**  
Contoso shall:  
(a) provide Gamma with timely access to relevant personnel, systems, and information reasonably required by Gamma to perform the Services;  
(b) designate a project manager to coordinate interactions with Gamma;  
(c) be responsible for obtaining and maintaining any third-party licenses, consents, or approvals for non-Gamma systems with which the Platform will interface, unless otherwise expressly agreed in the SOW; and  
(d) perform any tasks expressly identified in an SOW as Contoso’s responsibilities.  

Gamma shall not be liable for delays or failures to meet Milestones or Service Levels to the extent caused by Contoso’s failure to fulfill its responsibilities, provided that Gamma has given timely notice of such dependencies.

2.10 **Statements of Work.**  
Each SOW executed under this Agreement shall:  
(a) reference this Agreement by its title and Contract Reference No. PUR-GAM-202510-133;  
(b) describe the scope of Services and Deliverables, timelines, Milestones, fees, and payment terms (to the extent they differ from Article III); and  
(c) be signed by duly authorized representatives of both Parties.  

In the event of any conflict between this Agreement and an SOW, the terms of this Agreement shall prevail unless the SOW expressly references the specific provision of this Agreement to be modified and states the Parties’ intent to supersede it.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value.**  
The aggregate total fees payable by Contoso to Gamma under this Agreement shall not exceed **US$3,839,415** (the “Contract Value”), unless increased by written amendment or by additional SOWs expressly approved by Contoso. The Contract Value shall encompass all license/subscription fees, implementation Services, hardware (if any), and maintenance/support fees, as specified in the SOWs.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  
Unless otherwise provided in an SOW, the fees shall be structured as follows:

(a) **Software License/Subscription Fees.**  
Annual or multi-year license/subscription fees for the Platform and associated modules, invoiced in accordance with the schedule set forth in the relevant SOW.

(b) **Implementation/Professional Services Fees.**  
Fixed-price or time-and-materials fees for implementation, integration, and other professional Services, as specified in the applicable SOW.

(c) **Maintenance and Support Fees.**  
Annual maintenance fees equal to a percentage (as specified in the SOW) of the then-current license/subscription fees, invoiced annually in advance.

(d) **Hardware Fees (If Applicable).**  
One-time hardware purchase fees as specified in the SOW or relevant purchase order.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
(a) Unless otherwise stated in an SOW, Gamma shall invoice Contoso upon completion of the relevant Milestones or, in the case of recurring fees, at the start of the applicable billing period.  

(b) All undisputed amounts shall be due and payable within **forty-five (45) calendar days** from the date of receipt of a correct and undisputed invoice (“Net 45”).  

(c) Invoices shall be sent electronically to the billing contact designated by Contoso and shall include: (i) the Contract Reference No. PUR-GAM-202510-133; (ii) the relevant SOW or purchase order number; (iii) a description of the Services or Deliverables; and (iv) any applicable taxes itemized separately.

3.4 **Milestone-Based Payments.**  
Where the SOW provides for Milestone-based payments, the Parties agree that:  
(a) Gamma shall be entitled to invoice upon Contoso’s acceptance of the Milestone (in accordance with Section 2.8); and  
(b) Contoso shall not unreasonably withhold or delay acceptance of a Milestone that conforms in all material respects to the agreed criteria.

3.5 **Taxes.**  
All fees are exclusive of any applicable sales, use, value-added, goods and services, or similar taxes. Contoso shall be responsible for such taxes (except for taxes based on Gamma’s income, property, or employment of personnel), which shall be separately stated on Gamma’s invoices. The Parties shall reasonably cooperate in seeking any lawful exemptions or reductions in taxes.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
Gamma shall be responsible for all expenses incurred in performing the Services, unless the Parties expressly agree in an SOW that certain travel or out-of-pocket expenses are reimbursable. Any reimbursable expenses shall:  
(a) be pre-approved in writing by Contoso;  
(b) comply with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policies, as notified to Gamma; and  
(c) be supported by appropriate receipts and documentation.

3.7 **Late Payments.**  
If Contoso fails to pay any undisputed amount by the due date, Gamma may charge interest on the overdue amount at a rate of the lesser of (i) 1.0% per month; or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, calculated from the due date until payment is received. Gamma shall provide written notice of overdue amounts and grant a cure period of at least ten (10) Business Days before charging interest.

3.8 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Contoso may withhold payment of any portion of an invoice that Contoso reasonably and in good faith disputes, provided that Contoso:  
(a) pays the undisputed portion of the invoice when due; and  
(b) notifies Gamma in writing within fifteen (15) days of receipt of the invoice, specifying the nature of the dispute.  

The Parties shall use good faith efforts to resolve the dispute promptly. Any disputed amount resolved in Gamma’s favor shall be paid by Contoso within fifteen (15) days of resolution, without interest if resolved within thirty (30) days from the original due date.

3.9 **No Set-Off.**  
Except as expressly permitted under this Agreement or required by law, neither Party shall set off or deduct any amounts from sums owed to the other Party, without the other Party’s prior written consent.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Term.**  
This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **31 March 2030** (the “Term”).

4.2 **Renewal.**  
The Parties may mutually agree in writing to renew this Agreement for additional periods upon mutually agreed terms and conditions. Any renewal must be documented by a written amendment or new agreement executed by both Parties prior to the expiration of the then-current Term.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
Contoso may terminate this Agreement, in whole or in part (including any SOW), for convenience at any time upon not less than **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Gamma. Gamma may terminate this Agreement for convenience only upon not less than **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Contoso, and only if (a) no active SOWs remain in effect, or (b) Contoso consents in writing to termination of active SOWs. This provision is intended to be client-friendly in light of the low risk profile.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Agreement or any SOW immediately upon written notice if the other Party:  
(a) materially breaches this Agreement or the applicable SOW and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail (or within such longer cure period as is reasonably necessary, provided that the breaching Party commences cure within such thirty (30)-day period and diligently pursues cure); or  
(b) becomes insolvent, makes an assignment for the benefit of creditors, is the subject of any bankruptcy, winding up, or similar proceeding which is not dismissed within sixty (60) days, or ceases to conduct business in the ordinary course.

4.5 **Termination for Persistent SLA Failures.**  
In addition to Section 4.4, Contoso may terminate this Agreement or an affected SOW upon thirty (30) days’ written notice if Gamma fails to meet critical Service Levels (as defined in Article XII) in three (3) or more consecutive months or in any four (4) months in a rolling twelve (12)-month period, and fails to implement a mutually agreed remediation plan.

4.6 **Effect of Termination.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:  
(a) all SOWs shall automatically terminate unless the Parties expressly agree that a particular SOW will survive;  
(b) Contoso shall pay Gamma for all Services properly performed and Deliverables properly delivered and accepted as of the effective date of termination, subject to any rights of set-off or credits arising from Gamma’s breach;  
(c) any prepaid fees for Services or maintenance/support not yet rendered shall be refunded to Contoso on a pro-rata basis;  
(d) Gamma shall promptly return to Contoso, or at Contoso’s written direction securely destroy, all Confidential Information and Contoso Data in its possession or control, in accordance with Article XI; and  
(e) all licenses granted to Contoso under Article VI that are designated as perpetual shall survive in accordance with their terms, provided that Contoso has paid the applicable license fees.

4.7 **Transition Assistance.**  
Upon Contoso’s request made at or before termination, Gamma shall provide reasonable transition assistance, for a period not exceeding ninety (90) days following termination, to facilitate the orderly transfer of Services to Contoso or a third-party provider. Such assistance shall be provided at Gamma’s then-current time-and-materials rates, unless termination is due to Gamma’s uncured material breach, in which case transition assistance shall be provided at no additional charge for a reasonable period (not less than thirty (30) days).

4.8 **Survival.**  
The following provisions shall survive expiration or termination of this Agreement, together with any other provisions which by their nature are intended to survive: Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties) with respect to ongoing warranties, VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance) to the extent of obligations for past acts, XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), XVI (General Provisions), and Sections 3.5, 3.7, 3.8, 4.6, 4.7.

12.3 **Scheduled Maintenance.**  
Gamma may perform scheduled maintenance during standard maintenance windows, which shall be notified to Contoso at least five (5) Business Days in advance. Scheduled maintenance shall not exceed eight (8) hours per month and shall not count against the Uptime Commitment.

- **Severity 1 (Critical):** Platform unavailable or severely degraded with no workaround – response within one (1) hour, 24x7.  
- **Severity 2 (High):** Major functionality impaired; workaround available – response within four (4) Business Hours.  
- **Severity 3 (Medium):** Non-critical issue or minor functionality affected – response within one (1) Business Day.  
- **Severity 4 (Low):** General inquiries, cosmetic issues – response within three (3) Business Days.

If Gamma fails to meet the Uptime Commitment or other critical Service Levels in a given calendar month, Contoso shall be entitled to service credits, to be applied against future invoices, calculated as follows:

- Uptime between 98.0% and 99.0%: credit equal to 10% of the monthly fee for the affected service.  
- Uptime below 98.0%: credit equal to 20% of the monthly fee for the affected service.

Service credits are Contoso’s primary remedy for SLA breaches but shall not limit Contoso’s rights under Sections 4.5 and 9.3 for repeated or material failures.

The Party affected by a Force Majeure Event shall:

(b) use reasonable efforts to mitigate the effects of the Force Majeure Event; and  
(c) resume performance as soon as reasonably practicable.

15.3 **Suspension of Obligations.**  
During the continuance of a Force Majeure Event, the affected Party’s obligations under this Agreement shall be suspended to the extent and for the duration that such obligations are impacted by the Force Majeure Event. The affected Party shall not be liable for any failure or delay in performance caused by the Force Majeure Event, provided it complies with this Article XV.

15.4 **Extended Force Majeure.**  
If a Force Majeure Event continues for more than sixty (60) consecutive days and materially prevents the performance of a material part of the Services, either Party may terminate the affected SOW or, if the Force Majeure Event renders the entire Agreement impracticable, this Agreement upon thirty (30) days’ written notice, without penalty.
