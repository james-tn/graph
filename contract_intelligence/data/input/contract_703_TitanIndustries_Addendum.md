## PREAMBLE

This **Addendum to Lease Agreement** (this “**Addendum**”), bearing reference number **ADD-TIT-202406-703**, is made and entered into as of **August 16, 2024** (the “**Addendum Effective Date**”), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises**, a [jurisdiction of organization to be inserted] corporation, having its principal place of business at **1200 Contoso Way, Suite 500, Seattle, Washington 98101** (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**”, the “**Client**” or the “**Company**”); and  

- **Titan Industries**, a [jurisdiction of organization to be inserted] corporation, having its principal place of business at **4500 Titan Park Drive, Building A, Tacoma, Washington 98421** (hereinafter referred to as “**Titan**”, the “**Vendor**” or the “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Titan are sometimes referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

This Addendum is entered into with reference to and in connection with that certain **Lease Agreement**, bearing reference number **LSE-TIT-202406-646** and internal identifier **contract_646**, dated **June 27, 2024** (the “Parent Agreement” or “Lease Agreement”).

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the **Lease Agreement LSE-TIT-202406-646 (contract_646)** dated **June 27, 2024** (the “Parent Agreement”), pursuant to which Titan leases to Contoso, and Contoso leases from Titan, certain premises premises consisting of approximately **75,000 rentable square feet** of combined office and warehouse/light industrial space located at **4500 Titan Park Drive, Tacoma, Washington 98421** (as defined therein, the “Premises”), and Titan provides certain related facility services, all on the terms and conditions set forth therein;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement provides, among other things, for (i) **Base Rent** and **Operating Expenses** as set forth in **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** and **Exhibit B (Rent Schedule)** of the Parent Agreement, (ii) a **Term** commencing on the Commencement Date and ending on the **Expiration Date of January 08, 2027** (as set forth in **Sections 1.16, 1.17 and Article IV (Term and Termination)** of the Parent Agreement), and (iii) Titan’s provision of **Services** (as defined in **Section 1.14** of the Parent Agreement) under **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement is expressly defined as a **medium risk** arrangement in its Recitals, and the Parties intend this Addendum, which adds or modifies leased space and related services, to maintain a similarly balanced, medium-risk allocation of responsibilities, consistent with **Articles VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability) and X (Insurance)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, Contoso now desires to (a) lease additional premises within the same Building, and (b) modify certain provisions in the Parent Agreement relating to the Premises, Services, and payment terms, and Titan is willing to grant such additional leasehold rights and provide associated Services on the terms and subject to the conditions set forth in this Addendum;

WHEREAS, **this Addendum adds or modifies leased space dated June 27, 2024 (Ref: LSE-TIT-202406-646)**, supplements and amends certain terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, and shall be read and construed together with the Parent Agreement;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE I  
DEFINITIONS

The definitions set forth in **Article I of the Parent Agreement** are incorporated herein by reference, to the extent not expressly modified by this Addendum.

1.3 **“Addendum Effective Date”** has the meaning set forth in the Preamble above, namely **August 16, 2024**.

1.4 **“Parent Agreement” or “Master Agreement”** means that certain **Lease Agreement** between the Parties dated **June 27, 2024**, bearing reference number **LSE-TIT-202406-646** and internal identifier **contract_646**, including all schedules, exhibits, amendments, and addenda thereto, as may be further amended from time to time.

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK; MODIFICATION OF PREMISES

2.1 **Reference to Parent Agreement Scope.** The Parties acknowledge the detailed scope of Services set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement, including **Sections 2.1 through 2.8**, which govern Titan’s obligations with respect to building operations, maintenance, utilities, janitorial and common area services, security, parking, tenant improvements, service performance standards, additional services, operational coordination, and compliance and safety. Except as expressly modified herein, such provisions apply equally to the Additional Premises and Expanded Premises.

2.2 **Modification of Definition of “Premises.”**  
Effective as of the Addendum Commencement Date, **Section 1.13 (Premises)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby amended and restated in its entirety to read as follows (additions emphasized for clarity in this Addendum):

> **“Premises”** means approximately **115,000 rentable square feet** of combined office and warehouse/light industrial space located on the first, second, **and third floors and mezzanine level** of the Building, consisting of:  
> (a) approximately 75,000 rentable square feet of combined office and warehouse/light industrial space located on the first and second floors of the Building, and  
> (b) an additional approximately 40,000 rentable square feet of office, collaboration, and light industrial flex space located on the third floor and mezzanine level of the Building,  
> all as more particularly described and depicted in **Exhibit A (Premises Description and Floor Plan)** and **Exhibit A‑1 (Additional Premises Description and Floor Plan)**, together with the non-exclusive right to use, in common with others, the Building common areas.

2.3 **Use of Additional Premises.**  
(a) The permitted use of the Additional Premises shall be consistent with **Section 2.2 (Use of Premises)** of the Parent Agreement. Accordingly, Contoso shall use the Additional Premises primarily for general office operations, collaborative workspace, conference and training facilities, warehousing, light assembly, logistics, and related corporate activities consistent with a first-class commercial and industrial facility in Tacoma, Washington. No other use shall be permitted without Titan’s prior written consent, not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.  

(b) The Parties acknowledge that portions of the Additional Premises may be configured as higher-density office and collaboration space, which may require enhanced HVAC balancing, supplemental electrical distribution, and data cabling. Titan shall cooperate reasonably with Contoso regarding such tenant improvements pursuant to **Section 2.4 (Tenant Improvements and Fit-Out)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 2.5** of this Addendum.

2.4 **Extension of Landlord’s Services to Additional Premises.**  
(a) Titan’s obligations under **Section 2.3 (Landlord’s Services)** of the Parent Agreement (including building operations and maintenance, utilities, janitorial and common area services, security, and parking) are hereby expressly extended to the Additional Premises, such that Titan shall operate, maintain, repair, and replace, as needed, the structural elements and base building systems serving the Additional Premises consistent with the standards applied to the original Premises.  

(b) Titan shall ensure that the Additional Premises are supplied with electricity, water, sewer, and telecommunications infrastructure to the connection points at the Additional Premises, consistent with **Section 2.3.2 (Utilities)** of the Parent Agreement. Where practicable, utilities serving the Additional Premises may be separately metered or sub-metered, and allocated in accordance with **Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 3.3** of this Addendum.  

(c) Titan’s security and access control measures described in **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent Agreement shall include access control to the Additional Premises, including badge access points and common area monitoring, provided that Contoso remains responsible for interior security within the Additional Premises.

2.5 **Tenant Improvements and Fit-Out for Additional Premises.**  
(a) Consistent with **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall be responsible for interior tenant improvements within the Additional Premises, including office build-out, conference rooms, furniture layout, dedicated server or equipment rooms, and related items.  

(b) Titan’s prior written approval (not to be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed) shall be required for any structural modifications or changes impacting base building systems in the Additional Premises. Titan’s design and construction standards under the Parent Agreement shall apply to all such work.  

(c) Unless otherwise agreed in writing, all permanent fixtures and improvements affixed to the Additional Premises (other than Contoso’s trade fixtures and movable equipment) shall, at the expiration or earlier termination of the Parent Agreement or this Addendum, become the property of Titan without compensation, subject to **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement** and **Section 4.4** of this Addendum regarding removal and restoration.

2.6 **Service Performance Standards for Additional Premises.**  
(a) Titan shall perform all Services for the Additional Premises in accordance with the **Service Levels (SLAs)** set forth in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to the critical system availability (99.9% uptime for critical building systems) and response/resolution times described in **Section 12.2**.  

(b) Titan acknowledges that the Additional Premises may be used for critical corporate operations and training events. Titan shall make commercially reasonable efforts to schedule disruptive maintenance affecting the Additional Premises outside Contoso’s core business hours, consistent with **Section 2.7 (Operational Coordination)** of the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Any persistent SLA failures relating to Services in the Additional Premises shall be subject to the same service credit and remedy framework as set forth in **Section 12.4 of the Parent Agreement**, and shall be considered in evaluating material breach for purposes of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.3** of this Addendum.

2.7 **Additional Services Under This Addendum.**  
In addition to the Services described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, Titan shall, upon request by Contoso and subject to mutually agreed work orders or change orders pursuant to **Article XIII of the Parent Agreement** and **Article XIII of this Addendum**, provide the following incremental Services in relation to the Additional Premises:

  (a) **After-Hours HVAC and Lighting** for evening or weekend training sessions or project work, billable as additional Services under **Section 3.7(b) of the Parent Agreement**;  

  (b) **Enhanced Janitorial Services** within the Additional Premises (beyond the Building common areas covered under **Section 2.3.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement), including daily cleaning of collaboration areas and training rooms;  

  (c) **Dedicated Security Services** for high-traffic training events or executive briefings, including temporary security personnel and enhanced visitor management, as requested by Contoso; and  

  (d) **Move Coordination and Space Planning Support**, including provision of updated CAD or BIM floor plans and coordination of internal moves within the Expanded Premises.

2.8 **Deliverables Under This Addendum.**  
By no later than thirty (30) days following the Addendum Commencement Date, Titan shall provide the following Deliverables, which shall be deemed **“Deliverables”** under **Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement**:

  (a) **Updated Floor Plans and Stack Diagram** depicting the Expanded Premises, clearly identifying the Additional Premises and all access points;  

  (b) **Updated Preventive Maintenance Schedule** for base building systems serving the Additional Premises;  

  (c) **Commissioning or Functional Test Reports** (if any new base building systems are installed or materially modified to serve the Additional Premises); and  

  (d) **Updated Emergency Egress and Life-Safety Information** applicable to occupants of the Additional Premises.

2.9 **Compliance and Safety.**  
The Parties’ respective obligations under **Section 2.8 (Compliance and Safety)** of the Parent Agreement are hereby reiterated and extended to the Additional Premises. Contoso shall ensure that its use of the Additional Premises complies with all Applicable Laws, including fire and occupancy codes, and Titan shall maintain the Building’s common areas and base building systems serving the Additional Premises in compliance with Applicable Law.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **General; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This **Article III** supplements and, where expressly stated, modifies **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement with respect to the Additional Premises. For the avoidance of doubt, all amounts payable under this Addendum are in addition to, and not in substitution for, the amounts payable under the Parent Agreement, except as explicitly stated otherwise.

3.2 **Addendum Base Rent; Contract Value.**  
(a) The Parties acknowledge and agree that the **Addendum Contract Value** for the Additional Premises over the Addendum Term is approximately **US $3,667,344**, inclusive of Addendum Base Rent and estimated Operating Expenses and other recurring charges associated with the Additional Premises.  

(b) The **initial annual Addendum Base Rent** for the Additional Premises, and any applicable annual escalations (e.g., 3% per Lease Year or as otherwise agreed), shall be set forth in **Exhibit B-1 (Addendum Rent Schedule)** attached hereto.  

(c) Addendum Base Rent shall be payable in equal monthly installments, in advance, on or before the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Addendum Term, consistent with **Section 3.2(b) of the Parent Agreement**. If the Addendum Commencement Date is not the first day of a calendar month, Addendum Base Rent for such partial month shall be prorated on a 30-day basis and paid on or before the Addendum Commencement Date.

3.3 **Operating Expenses and Taxes Allocation.**  
(a) In addition to Addendum Base Rent, Contoso shall pay as additional rent its proportionate share of Operating Expenses and Taxes attributable to the Expanded Premises, as provided in **Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement**, recalculated to reflect the increase in total rentable square footage leased by Contoso.  

(b) Titan shall update the calculation of Contoso’s proportionate share of Operating Expenses and Taxes (ratio of 115,000 rentable square feet to the total rentable square footage of the Building) and provide Contoso with a written summary of such updated ratio and the resulting monthly estimated Operating Expenses and Tax charges.  

(c) Titan may invoice Contoso for estimated Operating Expenses and Taxes attributable to the Additional Premises on a monthly basis, subject to annual reconciliation as provided in **Section 3.3(c) of the Parent Agreement**. Any overpayments or underpayments shall be credited or payable within thirty (30) days after reconciliation.

3.4 **Utilities and Additional Services Charges.**  
(a) Contoso shall remain responsible for utilities serving the Expanded Premises that are separately metered to Contoso, as provided in **Section 3.4(a) of the Parent Agreement**.  

(b) For utilities not separately metered, Titan may allocate utility costs to the Expanded Premises using reasonable and equitable methods, including but not limited to square footage, usage estimates, or sub-metering, consistent with **Section 3.4(b) of the Parent Agreement**.  

(c) Charges for Additional Services requested by Contoso under **Section 2.7 of this Addendum** (e.g., after-hours HVAC, enhanced janitorial, dedicated security) shall be invoiced either as separate line items or as part of Titan’s regular monthly invoice, and shall be payable in accordance with the **Net 30** Payment Terms described in **Section 3.5**.

3.5 **Payment Terms; Invoicing.**  
(a) **Payment Terms – Net 30.** Consistent with **Section 3.5(a) of the Parent Agreement**, all undisputed amounts due under this Addendum, including Addendum Base Rent, Operating Expenses, Taxes, and additional Service charges, shall be payable within **thirty (30) days** of Contoso’s receipt of a correct and undisputed invoice from Titan.  

(b) **Invoicing Procedures.** Titan shall invoice Contoso monthly in advance for Addendum Base Rent and estimated Operating Expenses and Taxes attributable to the Additional Premises, and in arrears for variable and additional charges (e.g., after-hours HVAC, special janitorial, reconciled Operating Expenses). Invoices shall clearly distinguish between amounts owed under the Parent Agreement and amounts owed under this Addendum.  

(c) **Disputed Amounts.** In the event of a good-faith dispute regarding any invoiced amount under this Addendum, the procedures in **Section 3.6(b) of the Parent Agreement** (requiring payment of undisputed amounts and timely written notice of the dispute) shall apply.

3.6 **Late Payment; Interest.**  
Undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Addendum shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.6(a) of the Parent Agreement**, at the lesser of (i) one percent (1%) per month, or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, from the due date until paid.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  
(a) Except as expressly provided in this Addendum or the Parent Agreement, each Party shall bear its own costs relating to the negotiation and performance of this Addendum.  

(b) Titan shall be entitled to reimbursement of reasonable, documented costs incurred in providing any extraordinary or non-standard work or services related to the Additional Premises, including changes or improvements requested by Contoso, consistent with **Section 3.7(b) of the Parent Agreement**.  

(c) Titan shall obtain Contoso’s prior written approval (email sufficient) before incurring any pass-through costs for Additional Services for the Additional Premises that exceed any applicable threshold amounts agreed in **Exhibit B** of the Parent Agreement or **Exhibit B-1** of this Addendum, except in emergencies required to protect health, safety, or prevent significant property damage.

3.8 **Set-Off.**  
The limitation on set-off rights in **Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply equally to amounts payable under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
Subject to earlier termination as provided herein and in the Parent Agreement, the Addendum Term shall commence on the Addendum Commencement Date and continue until the **Addendum Expiration Date of March 25, 2026**.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
(a) The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement has an **Expiration Date of January 08, 2027** (Section 1.17 of the Parent Agreement). This Addendum, however, has its own Addendum Expiration Date of March 25, 2026.  

(b) Unless otherwise agreed in a subsequent amendment, Contoso’s right to occupy the Additional Premises shall automatically terminate on the Addendum Expiration Date. From and after such date, (i) the definition of “Premises” in the Parent Agreement shall revert to the original Premises of approximately 75,000 rentable square feet, and (ii) all obligations relating to the Additional Premises (including Addendum Base Rent) shall cease, except for those provisions that expressly survive termination.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
(a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party; provided that such termination shall not be effective earlier than **six (6) months** after the Addendum Commencement Date, unless otherwise agreed in writing.  

(b) If Contoso terminates this Addendum for convenience, Contoso shall pay all amounts due and payable under this Addendum up to the effective date of termination, but shall not owe any early termination penalty beyond such amounts, except for any specifically agreed early termination fee set forth in **Exhibit B-1**.  

(c) If Titan terminates this Addendum for convenience, Titan shall refund any pre-paid amounts relating to periods after the effective date of termination and shall cooperate with Contoso to effect an orderly relocation or consolidation of operations into the remaining Premises under the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
(a) Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement with respect to the Additional Premises and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after written notice, consistent with **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement**.  

(b) Material breaches by Contoso under this Addendum include, without limitation, failure to pay undisputed Addendum Base Rent or other charges within applicable cure periods, unauthorized use of the Additional Premises, or violation of Applicable Law materially affecting Titan or other tenants.  

(c) Material breaches by Titan under this Addendum include, without limitation, failure to provide essential Services to the Additional Premises resulting in extended loss of use of the Additional Premises, or failure to maintain base building systems serving the Additional Premises in compliance with Applicable Law, in each case subject to the cure provisions above.

4.5 **Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) Upon expiration or earlier termination of this Addendum (whether by lapse of the Addendum Term or pursuant to Section 4.3 or 4.4), Contoso shall vacate and surrender the Additional Premises in accordance with **Section 4.5 (Effect of Termination; Surrender)** of the Parent Agreement, including removal of movable equipment and trade fixtures and repair of any damage caused by such removal.  

(b) Unless otherwise expressly stated, termination or expiration of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate or otherwise affect the Parent Agreement with respect to the original Premises, which shall remain in full force and effect in accordance with its terms.  

(c) If the Parent Agreement is terminated in its entirety for any reason (including for convenience under **Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement** or for cause under **Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement**), this Addendum shall automatically terminate concurrently, and the Parties’ rights and obligations with respect to the Premises and Additional Premises shall be governed by **Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement** and this Section 4.5.

4.6 **Survival.**  
Any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive expiration or termination (including payment obligations accrued prior to termination, confidentiality, indemnification, limitation of liability, and dispute resolution) shall so survive, consistent with **Section 4.6 and Article XVI of the Parent Agreement**.

## ARTICLE VIII
