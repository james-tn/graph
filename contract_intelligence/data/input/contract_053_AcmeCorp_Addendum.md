## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum **adds products and services dated July 09, 2024 (Ref: PUR-ACM-202407-048)** and is entered into pursuant to, and in supplement of, the Parent Agreement. The Additional Products and Services described herein are to be provided by Acme under the terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement, as amended by this Addendum.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, the scope of Products and Services described in **Article II – Scope of Services and Work** of the Parent Agreement remain unchanged and continue to apply to all Products, Services, and Deliverables, including the Additional Products and Services.

2.2 **Additional Products – Enhanced Modules.**  

2.2.1 **Enhanced Modules Overview.** In addition to the Acme enterprise software modules for finance, supply chain, sales, customer support, and analytics described in **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement, Acme shall provide Contoso with the following Enhanced Modules as part of the **Acme Suite**:

   a. **Advanced Analytics and Predictive Insights Module** – providing advanced data visualisation, machine learning-based forecasting, and KPI dashboards across finance, operations, and sales.  
   b. **Global Tax and Compliance Module** – enabling automated tax calculation, indirect tax reporting, and compliance with multi-jurisdictional tax regimes applicable to Contoso’s global operations.  
   c. **Field Service Management Module** – supporting dispatch, scheduling, mobile workforce management, inventory visibility for field technicians, and customer appointment booking.  
   d. **Supplier Collaboration Portal Module** – enabling secure supplier onboarding, purchase order collaboration, invoice submission, shipment tracking, and supplier performance analytics.

2.2.2 **License Nature and Quantity.**  
The Enhanced Modules shall be licensed to Contoso in accordance with **Section 6.3.1 (Software Licence)** of the Parent Agreement, as follows:

   a. A non-exclusive, non-transferable (subject to Section 16.8 of the Parent Agreement), worldwide licence for the Addendum Term to use the Enhanced Modules for Contoso’s internal business purposes;  
   b. Up to **2,000 named internal users** across all Enhanced Modules and up to **500 external supplier users** for the Supplier Collaboration Portal;  
   c. Any additional user quantities or modules beyond those set forth herein shall be subject to a mutually agreed Change Order under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement.

2.2.3 **Hosting Model.**  
The Advanced Analytics and Supplier Collaboration Portal Modules shall be provided as **Hosted Components**, subject to the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and Article XII of this Addendum. The Global Tax and Field Service Management Modules may be deployed in Contoso’s on-premises or hybrid environment consistent with **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Extended Hardware Components.**  

2.3.1 To support the Enhanced Modules, Acme shall supply the following Extended Hardware Components, consistent with **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement:

   a. Two (2) additional high-availability database server appliances;  
   b. Four (4) application server appliances for the Field Service Management and Global Tax modules;  
   c. Appropriate load balancers and network edge devices required to support the Supplier Collaboration Portal.

2.3.2 All Extended Hardware Components shall be new or equivalent to new in performance and covered by manufacturer warranties as further described in **Article VII** of the Parent Agreement. Delivery terms shall be DAP (Delivered at Place) to Contoso’s designated data centres in the United Kingdom and continental Europe, unless otherwise specified in a Change Order.

2.4 **Implementation Wave 2 – Services and Deliverables.**  

2.4.1 **Discovery and Design Expansion.**  
In addition to the discovery, requirements analysis, and solution design obligations of Acme in **Section 2.3(a) and (b)** of the Parent Agreement, Acme shall perform additional discovery workshops with Contoso’s global tax, field service, procurement, and analytics stakeholders to capture:

   a. region-specific compliance and reporting requirements;  
   b. field service workflows (dispatch, escalation, parts logistics);  
   c. supplier onboarding and collaboration workflows;  
   d. analytic KPIs and data sources spanning the existing Acme Suite modules and third-party systems.

Acme shall update and extend the **solution design document** referenced in Section 2.3(b) of the Parent Agreement to include architecture diagrams, integration points, and configuration specifications specific to the Enhanced Modules.

2.4.2 **Configuration, Customisation, and Integration.**  
In addition to the obligations set forth in **Section 2.3(c) and (d)** of the Parent Agreement:

   a. Acme shall configure the Enhanced Modules according to the extended solution design, including custom tax rules, field service territories, appointment types, supplier workflows, and analytic dashboards;  
   b. Acme shall integrate the Enhanced Modules with Contoso’s existing HR, CRM, identity management, and external tax calculation engines via APIs or middleware, documenting all integration endpoints;  
   c. Custom development work (including any custom connectors, scripts, or reports) shall be treated as **Client-Specific Deliverables** subject to **Section 6.2.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 **Data Migration and Cleansing.**  
Extending **Section 2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement, Acme shall:

   a. Assist in mapping and migrating appropriate historical field service data, supplier records, tax configuration data, and analytic data from Contoso’s legacy systems;  
   b. Provide data validation scripts and tools to support Contoso’s responsibility for data quality and validation;  
   c. Conduct at least two (2) test migrations prior to production cutover for each Enhanced Module.

2.5 **Testing, Acceptance, and Milestones.**  

2.5.1 The testing and acceptance process described in **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Addendum Deliverables and Milestone Deliverables. For each Enhanced Module, the Parties shall define specific acceptance criteria, including functional tests, performance benchmarks, and integration validations, in a Milestone plan appended to this Addendum or a related SOW.

2.5.2 Contoso shall have **fifteen (15) Business Days** from delivery of each Milestone Deliverable to review and either (i) provide written acceptance or (ii) provide a written list of deficiencies, consistent with **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5.3 Acme shall promptly correct any deficiencies identified by Contoso and resubmit the affected Deliverable, in accordance with **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent Agreement, at no additional cost to Contoso, unless the defects result from Contoso’s failure to comply with its obligations.

2.5.4 **Milestone Summary (Illustrative):**

   - Milestone 1: Extended Solution Design Approved – within 60 days of Addendum Effective Date.  
   - Milestone 2: Completion of Configuration and Integration for Advanced Analytics and Supplier Portal – within 150 days.  
   - Milestone 3: Completion of Configuration and Integration for Global Tax and Field Service – within 210 days.  
   - Milestone 4: Successful UAT Completion and Go-Live – within 270 days.  
   - Milestone 5: Post-Go-Live Optimisation Phase Completion – within 360 days.

2.6 **Training and Optimisation Services.**  

2.6.1 In addition to the training obligations in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Acme shall provide:

   a. Role-based training for tax, field service, procurement, and analytics super-users and administrators;  
   b. Creation and delivery of at least ten (10) module-specific training videos and associated quick reference guides;  
   c. “Train-the-trainer” sessions for Contoso’s internal training team.

2.6.2 As part of Optimisation Services, Acme shall:

   a. Conduct quarterly optimisation workshops during the first twelve (12) months following the go-live of each Enhanced Module;  
   b. Analyse system usage and performance metrics and provide written recommendations for improvement;  
   c. Assist with configuration tweaks, report adjustments, and minor enhancements within the original scope at no additional professional services Fees, provided that such work does not exceed the included optimisation hours set forth in Article III.

2.7 **Support and Maintenance for Enhanced Modules.**  

2.7.1 The support and maintenance framework set forth in **Section 2.6** and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement shall apply fully to the Enhanced Modules and Hosted Components. Acme shall provide:

   a. Helpdesk support specifically for the Enhanced Modules during Business Hours as defined in Section 2.6(a) of the Parent Agreement;  
   b. Patches, bug fixes, and minor updates for the Enhanced Modules in accordance with Section 2.6(b);  
   c. Routine maintenance windows and notice as per Section 2.6(c) and **Section 12.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7.2 Any major upgrades or feature additions to the Enhanced Modules requested by Contoso shall be subject to additional Fees and a Change Order under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and Article XIII of this Addendum.

2.8 **Cooperation and Dependencies.**  

2.8.1 The cooperation and dependency framework set forth in **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Addendum. Without limiting that section, Contoso shall:

   a. Make available subject matter experts in global tax, field service, supplier management, and analytics for workshops and UAT;  
   b. Provide timely access to legacy systems and data sources required for integrations and data migration;  
   c. Ensure that its infrastructure and network meet Acme’s minimum requirements for Hosted Components and Extended Hardware Components.

2.8.2 Delay or failure by Contoso to perform its obligations that materially impacts Acme’s performance shall entitle Acme, after reasonable notice and consultation, to a reasonable extension of time and, where justified, a mutually agreed adjustment to Fees under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement, provided that Acme uses commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate such delays.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **25 February 2028**, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article IV or **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.

4.1.2 The Addendum Term may be renewed by mutual written agreement of the Parties, consistent with the renewal concept set forth in **Section 4.2** of the Parent Agreement, provided that any renewal shall be documented in writing and specify updated Fees and scope.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.2.1 Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience at any time upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Acme, consistent with the Contoso-favourable right in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement.  

4.2.2 Acme may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, provided such termination shall not be effective prior to completion of any then-active critical Milestones agreed in writing by the Parties, consistent with **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement, unless Contoso consents otherwise in writing.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for material breach or insolvency in accordance with **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement. For the avoidance of doubt, a material breach of this Addendum shall constitute a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa to the extent the breach affects obligations under both documents.

4.4 **Effect of Termination of Addendum.**  

4.4.1 Upon expiry or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

   a. All outstanding, undisputed Addendum invoices shall become immediately due and payable;  
   b. Acme shall submit a final invoice for Addendum Services performed and Addendum Products delivered up to the effective date of termination;  
   c. Licenses granted in respect of the Enhanced Modules shall terminate, unless expressly stated to survive in a written SOW associated with this Addendum;  
   d. The Parties shall cooperate to wind down Implementation Wave 2 in an orderly manner.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason:

   a. This Addendum shall automatically terminate effective on the same date, without the need for additional notice;  
   b. The Parties’ respective rights and obligations under this Addendum shall be governed by **Section 4.5 (Effect of Termination)** and **Section 4.6 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by this Article IV;  
   c. Acme shall provide **Transition Assistance** for up to ninety (90) days following termination, consistent with **Section 4.5(e)** of the Parent Agreement, on a time-and-materials basis at agreed rates, to facilitate the transition of the Enhanced Modules and associated data to Contoso or a successor provider.

4.6 **Survival.**  

4.6.1 The survival provisions set forth in **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement shall apply equally to this Addendum. Without limitation, the following provisions of this Addendum shall survive expiration or termination: Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions), together with any payment obligations accrued prior to termination.
