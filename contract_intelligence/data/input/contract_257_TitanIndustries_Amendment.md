## PREAMBLE

This **AMENDMENT TO LEASE AGREEMENT** (this **“Amendment”** or **“Amended Lease”**), Contract Reference Number **AMD-TIT-202401-257**, is made and entered into as of **October 18, 2024** (the **“Amendment Effective Date”**), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises**, a corporation duly organized and existing under the laws of its jurisdiction of incorporation, with its principal place of business at **100 Marina Boulevard, #20-01, Singapore 018985** (**“Contoso”, “Client” or “Company”**); and  

- **Titan Industries**, a corporation duly organized and existing under the laws of its jurisdiction of incorporation, with its principal place of business at **25 HarbourFront Place, #18-10, Singapore 099613** (**“Titan”, “Vendor” or “Service Provider”**).

Contoso and Titan are hereinafter sometimes referred to individually as a **“Party”** and collectively as the **“Parties”**.

This Amendment **modifies lease terms dated January 7, 2024 (Ref: LSE-TIT-202401-254, identifier: contract_254)** pursuant to that certain **Lease Agreement**, Contract Reference Number **LSE-TIT-202401-254** (the **“Parent Agreement”** or **“Master Lease”**), entered into between the same Parties and effective as of **January 7, 2024** (the **“Original Effective Date”**).

This Amendment is a **High Risk** document, and, consistent with the risk allocation already reflected in **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement, is intended to be **vendor‑favorable** and to supplement and, where expressly stated, modify the terms of the Parent Agreement.

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent Agreement (file name: **contract_254**), Lease Agreement Contract Reference Number **LSE-TIT-202401-254**, pursuant to which Titan leases to Contoso certain premises located at **Titan Industrial Complex, 45 Pioneer Crescent, #05-01 to #05-10, Singapore 628123**, comprising approximately **5,000 square meters** (as defined therein as the **“Lease Premises”**), together with related services as more fully set forth in **Article II – Scope of Services / Work** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, under **Section 1.9 (Lease Term)** and **Section 4.1 (Lease Term)** of the Parent Agreement, the Lease Term commenced on **January 7, 2024** and is scheduled to expire on **September 29, 2027** (the **“Expiration Date”**), subject to earlier termination in accordance with **Article IV** thereof;

WHEREAS, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement establishes, among other things, the **Base Rent** of **US$35,000 per calendar month**, subject to annual escalation of **3%**, the **Contract Value** of **US$1,386,859**, and detailed **Net 30** payment terms, security deposit requirements, and provisions relating to Additional Rent and Chargeable Services;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire (i) to expand and clarify the scope of the Lease Premises and certain Services provided by Titan under **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, (ii) to adjust the term, rent, and contract value, (iii) to align various operational and service‑level provisions with Contoso’s evolving use of the Premises, and (iv) otherwise to amend and supplement certain provisions of the Parent Agreement, all on the terms and conditions set forth in this Amendment;

WHEREAS, the Parties acknowledge that **Article XVI, Section 16.2 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement requires that any amendment or modification of the Parent Agreement be in writing, signed by authorized representatives of both Parties, and expressly reference the Parent Agreement and Contract Reference Number **LSE-TIT-202401-254**;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and agreements contained in this Amendment, and intending to be legally bound, the Parties agree as follows:

## ARTICLE III – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK (AMENDMENT)

3.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement – Article II.**  
This Article III amends and supplements **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of conflict between this Article III and Article II of the Parent Agreement, this Article III shall govern with respect to the Extended Premises and Enhanced Services.

3.2 **Extended Premises Description.**  
(a) The Lease Premises described in **Section 1.8** and **Section 2.1** of the Parent Agreement (approximately **5,000 square meters** at **45 Pioneer Crescent, #05-01 to #05-10, Singapore 628123**) are hereby confirmed and shall continue in effect.  
(b) As of the Amendment Effective Date, the Parties may, from time to time, agree in writing (including in email confirmed by both Parties or in a signed change order) to designate additional areas within the Titan Industrial Complex as part of the **Extended Premises**, including but not limited to:  
   (i) Additional storage areas contiguous to the existing Premises;  
   (ii) Shared plant rooms or equipment rooms providing dedicated infrastructure to Contoso;  
   (iii) Additional parking allocations designated for Contoso’s employees and visitors; and  
   (iv) Any mezzanine or sub‑divided areas within the existing floorplate allocated for Contoso’s exclusive use.  
(c) Any such additional areas shall be documented in an updated **Schedule A (Premises Description)** attached to this Amendment and shall be treated as part of the Premises for all purposes under the Parent Agreement as amended.

3.3 **Purpose and Permitted Use (Supplement to Section 2.2).**  
(a) The permitted use defined under **Section 2.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement (office, light industrial, research and development, storage, and ancillary business operations) is expanded to expressly include:  
   (i) Limited-scale testing of prototype hardware and equipment, provided that such activities remain within the thresholds and safety standards prescribed by applicable law and Titan’s building rules; and  
   (ii) Operation of a small-scale logistics and staging area for Contoso’s internal distribution and deployment of goods, subject to compliance with fire safety and loading regulations.  
(b) Contoso’s obligations under **Section 2.2(b)** and **Section 7.3** of the Parent Agreement (lawful use, no hazardous or nuisance activities) remain in full force and are expressly reaffirmed. Titan retains the discretion, as in the Parent Agreement, to withhold or condition consent to any change of use that may increase risk, regulatory burden, or insurance premiums.

3.4 **Enhanced Services – Property Management and Operations (Supplement to Section 2.4).**  
During the Amended Lease Term, Titan shall provide the following **Enhanced Services**, in addition to and as an integral part of the Services under **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) **Enhanced Building Operations and Maintenance.**  
  (i) Titan shall increase the frequency of routine inspections of critical building systems serving the Premises, including HVAC, electrical distribution, fire protection, and security systems, to ensure alignment with higher utilization by Contoso.  
  (ii) Titan shall implement a formalized preventative maintenance program, with maintenance calendars and logs to be made available to Contoso upon request as **Deliverables** within the meaning of **Section 1.5** and **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) **Optional Internal Cleaning Services.**  
  (i) Whereas **Section 2.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement places responsibility for internal cleaning on Contoso unless otherwise agreed, Titan shall, upon written request from Contoso, provide internal cleaning for designated zones of the Premises as **Chargeable Services** under **Section 2.9** and **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement.  
  (ii) The scope, frequency, and rates for such internal cleaning shall be documented in a supplemental schedule or change order referencing this Amendment.

(c) **Enhanced Security Services.**  
  (i) In addition to building‑level security under **Section 2.4(c)**, Titan shall coordinate and manage, as Chargeable Services, additional access control hardware for Contoso’s internal use (e.g., card readers, CCTV coverage limited to Contoso’s exclusive areas), subject to Titan’s building policies.  
  (ii) Operational logs and incident reports related to security events in or affecting the Premises shall be treated as **Deliverables** under **Section 2.8(b)** of the Parent Agreement and shall be made available to Contoso upon request, subject to confidentiality restrictions and data protection laws.

(d) **Utilities Coordination and Capacity Planning.**  
  (i) Supplementing **Section 2.4(d)**, Titan shall provide capacity planning support to assess expected load for electricity, water, and any other utility services in connection with Contoso’s expanded operations.  
  (ii) Titan shall advise Contoso in writing of any required upgrades or limitations, and any associated costs shall be treated as Chargeable Services or capital contributions as agreed in a separate change order.

(e) **Regulatory and Compliance Support.**  
  (i) In addition to the building-wide permits under **Section 2.4(e)**, Titan shall provide reasonable assistance to Contoso in obtaining permits or clearances directly related to the configuration of the Premises (e.g., layout approvals, fire safety egress changes), without assuming legal responsibility for Contoso’s business‑specific regulatory requirements.  
  (ii) Any out‑of‑pocket costs incurred by Titan in providing such assistance shall be reimbursed by Contoso on a Net 30 basis.

3.5 **Service Performance Standards (Supplement to Section 2.5 and Article XII).**  
(a) Titan reaffirms its obligation under **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement to perform Services with reasonable skill, care, and diligence consistent with good industry practice, subject to the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) As further detailed in **Article XII of this Amendment**, Titan shall adhere to enhanced uptime commitments, response times, and reporting obligations corresponding to the Extended Premises and Enhanced Services.  
(c) The Parties agree that where this Amendment imposes additional performance commitments, such commitments are in lieu of, and not additive to, any comparable commitments under the Parent Agreement for the same systems or scope, to avoid double counting of obligations.

3.6 **Deliverables (Supplement to Section 2.8).**  
In addition to the Deliverables identified in **Section 2.8** of the Parent Agreement, Titan shall provide:

(a) **Quarterly Facility Status Reports** summarizing:  
  (i) Material repairs in the Premises or Common Areas affecting Contoso;  
  (ii) Key service incidents and their resolution times;  
  (iii) Planned maintenance activities for the subsequent quarter;  
  (iv) Any regulatory inspections impacting the Premises.

(b) **Annual Compliance Summary** addressing building-level regulatory compliance that materially affects Contoso’s use of the Premises.

(c) **Transition Period Reports** during the Transition Period summarizing the implementation status of Enhanced Services and any operational adjustments required of Contoso.

3.7 **Contoso’s Obligations (Supplement to Section 2.7).**  
(a) Contoso shall provide reasonable cooperation and access to Titan’s personnel and contractors for purposes of implementing the Enhanced Services, including access to internal equipment rooms where necessary.  
(b) Contoso shall promptly notify Titan of any material changes in its operations that may impact building systems (for example, substantial increases in power usage or changes to occupancy patterns) to allow Titan to assess potential impacts and implement corresponding adjustments.

## ARTICLE IV – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS (AMENDMENT)

4.1 **Relationship to Article III of Parent Agreement.**  
This Article IV amends and supplements **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of conflict, this Article IV shall govern with respect to the Amended Contract Value, Revised Base Rent and any financial terms specifically covered herein.

4.2 **Amended Contract Value.**  
The Parties acknowledge and agree that, as a result of this Amendment, the total estimated contract value for the Amended Lease Term is **US$2,870,829** (the **“Amended Contract Value”**), which supersedes the **Contract Value of US$1,386,859** stated in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. This Amended Contract Value reflects:  
(a) The Revised Base Rent described below;  
(b) Updated estimates of Additional Rent (operating expenses, utilities, taxes, etc.); and  
(c) The projected cost of Enhanced Services anticipated over the Amended Lease Term, excluding Taxes, discretionary Chargeable Services, and any further changes agreed via Change Requests.

4.3 **Revised Base Rent (Modification of Section 3.2).**  
(a) **Replacement of Section 3.2(a).** As of the Amendment Effective Date, **Section 3.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby deleted in its entirety and replaced with the following:  

> “Contoso shall pay to Titan base rent in the amount specified in the applicable amendment or change order in effect from time to time (the ‘Base Rent’), payable in advance on or before the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Lease Term.”

(b) **Current Revised Base Rent.** As between the Parties, and for the period from the Amendment Effective Date through the Amended Lease Term, the **Revised Base Rent** shall be **US$72,500 per calendar month**, payable in advance on or before the first (1st) day of each calendar month, subject to the 3% annual escalation mechanism set forth in **Section 3.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement, which remains in full force and effect and shall apply to the Revised Base Rent.

4.4 **Operating Expenses and Additional Rent (Confirmation of Section 3.3).**  
(a) **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement (Operating Expenses and Additional Rent) is hereby reaffirmed. Contoso shall continue to pay its proportionate share of building operating expenses, property taxes, and other charges in accordance with that section.  
(b) To reflect any expansion of the Extended Premises, Titan is authorized to adjust Contoso’s proportionate share of operating expenses by reference to actual occupied floor area or other reasonable allocation metrics, with such adjustments reflected in Titan’s invoices.

4.5 **Chargeable Services (Confirmation and Extension of Section 3.4).**  
(a) All Enhanced Services that are not expressly included in the Revised Base Rent shall be invoiced as Chargeable Services under **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) Titan shall provide a summary of Chargeable Services rendered in each invoice, reasonably itemized by type of service and time period.

4.6 **Payment Terms – Net 30 (Confirmation of Section 3.7).**  
(a) The **Net 30** payment terms set forth in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement remain unchanged and apply fully to all amounts invoiced under this Amendment.  
(b) Titan shall ensure that invoices referencing Chargeable Services, Enhanced Services, or adjustments to Additional Rent specify both Reference Numbers **LSE-TIT-202401-254** and **AMD-TIT-202401-257**.

4.7 **Late Payment; Interest (Reaffirmation of Section 3.8).**  
(a) The late payment interest provisions of **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement (1.5% per month, or maximum permitted by law) continue to apply to any undisputed amounts due under this Amendment.  
(b) Titan’s right to suspend non‑essential Services under **Section 3.8(b)** of the Parent Agreement is expressly extended to the Enhanced Services, subject to the same limitations that such suspension shall not prevent lawful occupancy by Contoso.

4.8 **Security Deposit (Confirmation of Section 3.9).**  
(a) The security deposit obligations in **Section 3.9** of the Parent Agreement remain in force.  
(b) If the Revised Base Rent results in the existing security deposit being less than three (3) months of the then‑current estimated Rent, Titan may, upon written notice, require Contoso to top up the security deposit to comply with **Section 3.9(b)**.

4.9 **Taxes (Confirmation of Section 3.10).**  
All amounts payable under this Amendment remain exclusive of Taxes, which shall be payable by Contoso as set forth in **Section 3.10** of the Parent Agreement.

4.10 **Disputed Amounts (Confirmation of Section 3.11).**  
The dispute process for invoices under **Section 3.11** of the Parent Agreement continues to apply, and Contoso shall timely pay all undisputed portions of invoices under this Amendment while the Parties resolve bona fide disputes in good faith.

## ARTICLE V – TERM AND TERMINATION (AMENDMENT)

5.1 **Amended Lease Term (Modification of Section 4.1).**  
(a) **Section 4.1 (Lease Term)** of the Parent Agreement is modified solely to adjust the expiration date with respect to the obligations governed by this Amendment.  
(b) The **Amended Lease Term** for the Extended Premises and Enhanced Services shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall expire on **October 23, 2027**, unless earlier terminated pursuant to this Amendment or the Parent Agreement. For the avoidance of doubt, this does not shorten the existing Lease Term but extends Titan’s obligations for the Enhanced Services and Revised Base Rent beyond the original Expiration Date of **September 29, 2027**, for the period from September 30, 2027 through October 23, 2027.

5.2 **Renewal Options (Supplement to Section 4.2).**  
(a) The renewal option in **Section 4.2** of the Parent Agreement remains available, subject to the timelines and conditions specified therein.  
(b) Any renewal term agreed pursuant to Section 4.2 shall incorporate the then-current Revised Base Rent (subject to negotiation), applicable Service Levels, and financial terms set out in this Amendment or any subsequent amendments.

5.3 **Termination for Convenience (Confirmation of Section 4.3).**  
(a) The termination for convenience rights in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement (180 days’ notice by either Party, with early termination charge for Contoso) remain in full force and apply to this Amendment, including the Extended Premises and Enhanced Services.  
(b) For clarity, if Contoso terminates for convenience, the early termination charge equal to three (3) months of then-current Base Rent under **Section 4.3(b)(ii)** of the Parent Agreement shall be calculated with reference to the Revised Base Rent in effect at the time of termination.

5.4 **Termination for Cause (Confirmation of Section 4.4).**  
(a) The termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to this Amendment.  
(b) Titan’s right to terminate immediately for non‑payment, insolvency, or illegal activities under **Section 4.4(b)** extends to non‑payment of sums due under this Amendment and to any illegal or hazardous activities carried out within the Extended Premises.

5.5 **Effect of Termination or Expiration (Supplement to Section 4.5).**  
(a) Upon termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement or this Amendment (whichever occurs first), Contoso shall surrender the Extended Premises in accordance with **Section 4.5(a)–(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) All amounts owed by Contoso under this Amendment up to the date of termination or expiration (including any early termination charges, costs for Enhanced Services rendered, and any restoration costs specific to the Extended Premises) shall become immediately due and payable, consistent with **Section 4.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Sections of this Amendment that, by their nature, should survive termination or expiration (including Articles IV, V, VII, VIII, IX, X, XI, XII, XIV, XV, and XVI of this Amendment) shall survive in parallel with the survival provisions stated in **Section 4.5(e)** of the Parent Agreement.

5.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
(a) If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same effective date without further action by either Party, except to the extent that specific obligations are stated herein to survive such termination.  
(b) In such event, Contoso shall remain responsible for all payment obligations incurred under this Amendment prior to the effective date of termination or expiration.

5.7 **Holdover (Application of Section 4.6).**  
The holdover provisions in **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement (month‑to‑month tenancy at 150% of last monthly Rent) shall apply to any holdover occupancy of the Extended Premises, with Rent calculated using the Revised Base Rent, plus applicable Additional Rent and charges for Enhanced Services.

The restrictions in **Section 6.4** and the “no implied rights” principle in **Section 6.5** of the Parent Agreement continue to apply to any IP created or used in connection with this Amendment. (b) For periods in which the Extended Premises or Enhanced Services are materially affected, the Parties may mutually agree in writing to attribute Service Credits specifically to the portion of Revised Base Rent corresponding to the affected areas or services.

The exclusions listed in **Section 12.5** of the Parent Agreement apply to SLA obligations under this Amendment. Additionally, Titan shall not be responsible for SLA failures attributable to:  
(a) Contoso’s failure to comply with capacity limitations or environmental specifications for the Extended Premises; or  
(b) Any Contoso-controlled equipment or software that interferes with building systems.
