## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

This **Addendum to Data Processing Agreement** (this “**Addendum**” or “**Addendum DPA**”), bearing **Contract Reference Number: ADD-VER-202509-695**, is entered into as of **March 25, 2026** (the “**Addendum Effective Date**”), by and between:

- **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a Delaware corporation, with its principal place of business at 4100 Contoso Way, New York, NY 10005, USA (“**Contoso**”, “**Client**” or “**Company**”); and  

- **Vertex Group LLC**, a New York limited liability company, with its principal place of business at 980 Vertex Plaza, Suite 2400, New York, NY 10019, USA (“**Vertex**”, “**Vendor**” or “**Service Provider**”).

Contoso and Vertex are each referred to herein individually as a “**Party**” and collectively as the “**Parties**.”

WHEREAS, the Parties have previously entered into that certain **Data Processing Agreement**, Contract Reference Number **DPA-VER-202509-641**, dated **September 29, 2025**, identified internally by Company as **“contract_641”** (the “**Parent Agreement**” or “**DPA**”);

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement sets forth the general terms and conditions under which Service Provider provides data processing, hosting, analytics, integration, and managed services, and under which Service Provider acts as processor/service provider with respect to certain **Client Data** and **Personal Data** as those terms are defined in **Article I (Definitions)** of the Parent Agreement, including Sections **1.6 (“Client Data”)** and **1.15 (“Personal Data”)**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to expand and **add additional processing activities** to the scope of Services previously agreed, including additional data ingestion sources, advanced analytics use cases, and extended retention and hosting arrangements, all in connection with the processing of Personal Data and Client Data;

WHEREAS, **this Addendum adds processing activities dated September 29, 2025 (Ref: DPA-VER-202509-641)**, and is intended to **supplement and amend** certain provisions of the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, solely in respect of the additional processing activities described herein;

WHEREAS, the Parties have categorized the engagement under the Parent Agreement as “**High Risk**” from a data protection, operational, and business continuity perspective (as expressly noted in the recitals to the Parent Agreement), and intend that this Addendum shall likewise be treated as **High Risk**, with the same **uncapped liability framework** set forth in **Article IX (Limitation of Liability)** of the Parent Agreement, and similarly protective, Vendor‑favorable terms;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth in this Addendum and the Parent Agreement, and other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is **entered into pursuant to** and **supplements** the Parent Agreement, including **Article II – Scope of Services / Work**. Except as expressly modified in this Addendum, all terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect and apply to the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables.  

2.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that **Section 2.1 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement already authorizes Service Provider to perform data processing, hosting, analytics, integration, and related managed services. This Addendum **adds processing activities** by expanding the categories of Client Data, Personal Data, and use cases that will be processed under the Services, and by specifying additional Deliverables and performance expectations specific to the Addendum Services.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Services.**  

Under this Addendum, Service Provider shall perform the following Additional Processing Activities and Addendum Services, in each case as more fully detailed in subsequent subsections and in applicable Addendum Order Forms:

(a) **Expanded Data Ingestion & Integration.**  
Service Provider shall onboard and integrate additional Client systems and third-party feeds into its platform, including, without limitation:

- Web and mobile interaction logs for Company’s customer-facing applications;
- Customer support and ticketing data from Company’s helpdesk platform;
- Collaboration and productivity data (e.g., calendar, messaging metadata) for employee analytics, limited to metadata fields as approved by Company;
- Third-party enrichment data (e.g., firmographic and demographic attributes) obtained and lawfully licensed by Company from external data providers.

(b) **Advanced Analytics Use Cases.**  
Service Provider shall design, implement, and operate new machine learning and analytics use cases, including:

- **Customer Churn Prediction** models;
- **Propensity-to-Buy and Cross-Sell** scoring;
- **Operational Incident Anomaly Detection** for Company’s internal systems;
- **Employee Productivity and Resource Allocation** dashboards, subject to Data Protection Laws and Company HR policies.

(c) **Regional Processing Zones & Data Residency.**  
Service Provider shall configure Regional Processing Zones to host certain Client Data and Personal Data (e.g., EU and UK resident data) in specific jurisdictions or regions, as specified in applicable Addendum Order Forms, consistent with **Section 11.9 (Cross-Border Transfers)** of the Parent Agreement.

(d) **Extended Retention & Archival Services.**  
Subject to **Section 11.6 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement, Service Provider shall provide configurable retention and archival features for specified Data Domains for longer time horizons (e.g., 7–10 years) as expressly requested by Company in writing, subject to Company’s compliance with Applicable Law.

(e) **Enhanced Monitoring, Runbooks, and Incident Response Playbooks.**  
Building on **Section 2.2(d) (Playbooks and incident run-sheets)** of the Parent Agreement, Service Provider shall create and maintain enhanced runbooks and playbooks tailored to the Additional Processing Activities, including region-specific incident response workflows and escalation matrices.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Additional Processing Activities.**  

2.3.1 **Data Domains and Categories.**  
Without limiting the definition of Client Data and Personal Data in the Parent Agreement, the Additional Processing Activities shall include Processing of the following data categories:

- Customer identifiers, contact details, and digital identifiers (IP address, device IDs, cookie IDs);
- Transaction and behavioral data (purchase history, clickstream, support interactions);
- Employee identifiers (business email, employee ID, department, role) and limited activity metadata (meeting counts, email volumes, support tasks), excluding content of communications unless expressly authorized and compliant with Applicable Law;
- Enrichment attributes obtained from licensed third-party data providers, as provided by Company.

2.3.2 **Purposes of Processing.**  
The purposes of Processing under this Addendum are aligned with the lawful purposes defined in the Parent Agreement and shall include:

- Providing predictive and descriptive analytics to improve customer experience, cross-sell and upsell strategies, and churn reduction;
- Enabling operational monitoring and anomaly detection to improve system reliability and incident response;
- Providing aggregated, role-based internal analytics for workforce planning and productivity insights, subject to Company’s HR and labor law obligations;
- Supporting regulatory, risk, and audit reporting for Company, via dashboards and reports generated from Client Data.

2.3.3 **Regional Processing Zones.**  
To comply with **Data Protection Laws** including GDPR and UK GDPR and **Section 11.9 (Cross-Border Transfers)** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) Service Provider shall, where specified by Company in an Addendum Order Form, host and process EU and UK resident data primarily within EU/EEA or UK data centers operated by Service Provider or its Subprocessors.  

(b) Cross-border transfers from such regions to other locations (including the United States) shall be governed by appropriate safeguards, such as standard contractual clauses or other legally recognized transfer mechanisms, consistent with the Parent Agreement.  

(c) Company acknowledges that certain operational or support activities may involve remote access to systems or data from outside the EU/UK, provided appropriate safeguards and access controls are in place in accordance with **Section 11.5 (Security Measures)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Addendum Deliverables.**  

In addition to Deliverables described in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement, Service Provider shall provide at least the following Addendum Deliverables:

(a) **Analytics Models & Documentation.**  
- At least three (3) distinct churn prediction models with supporting documentation (assumptions, variables, limitations) for different product lines;
- One (1) global propensity-to-buy model and one (1) cross-sell recommendation model;
- Documented ML pipelines including data preprocessing, feature engineering, and retraining schedules.

(b) **Dashboards & Reports.**  
- Monthly and quarterly **Customer Journey and Churn Risk Dashboards**;  
- Quarterly **Employee Productivity and Workload Distribution Dashboards** (aggregated at team or department level, to avoid undue individual profiling except where specifically permitted by Company and Applicable Law);  
- Operational anomaly detection dashboards, including trend analyses.

(c) **Integration Runbooks & Technical Documentation.**  
- Implementation runbooks for each new data source, including mapping, transformation rules, and data quality checks;  
- API specification documents for newly exposed or extended APIs used for data ingestion or extraction.

(d) **Incident Response Playbooks.**  
- Region-specific incident playbooks addressing Security Incidents affecting Additional Processing Activities;  
- Escalation matrices and roles, including contact points at Company’s security and privacy teams.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.5.1 **Implementation Phases.**  
The Parties shall agree in applicable Addendum Order Forms on implementation milestones for each Additional Processing Activity, which, unless otherwise specified, shall follow these general phases:

- **Phase 1 – Discovery & Design (30–60 calendar days):** Data mapping, requirements documentation, architecture design;  
- **Phase 2 – Build & Integration (60–120 calendar days):** Pipeline implementation, model development, configuration;  
- **Phase 3 – UAT & Stabilization (30–60 calendar days):** User acceptance testing, tuning, and go‑live;  
- **Phase 4 – BAU Operations:** Ongoing operations, support, and model maintenance.

2.5.2 **Dependence on Company Responsibilities.**  
Consistent with **Section 2.4 (Company Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, Service Provider’s ability to meet milestones under this Addendum shall be dependent on timely provision by Company of:

- Access to source systems, sample data, and test environments;  
- Necessary approvals, sign-offs, and prioritization;  
- Up-to-date consents, authorizations, and privacy notices as required for the Additional Processing Activities.

Any delay by Company in fulfilling these responsibilities shall entitle Service Provider to a corresponding extension of time and adjustment of milestones without penalty.

2.6 **Performance Standards.**  

2.6.1 Service Provider shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.3 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, mutatis mutandis.  

2.6.2 For avoidance of doubt, Service Provider does not guarantee that ML models will achieve specific levels of accuracy or business outcomes; Company acknowledges that model performance may vary and that Service Provider’s obligation is to use commercially reasonable efforts and sound industry practices in model development and maintenance.

2.7 **Subprocessors and Third-Party Services.**  

Any Subprocessors or third-party tools engaged specifically for the Additional Processing Activities shall be subject to **Section 2.6 (Subprocessors and Third-Party Services)** and **Section 11.3 (Subprocessing)** of the Parent Agreement. Service Provider will, upon reasonable request, identify any additional Subprocessors engaged for services under this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total contract value for the Addendum Services during the Addendum Term shall be **Four Million Seven Hundred Eleven Thousand Three Hundred Seventy-Seven Dollars (US $4,711,377)** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”).  

3.1.2 The Addendum Contract Value is **in addition** to the **Contract Value** of **US $4,287,004** set forth in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement. For the avoidance of doubt, fees due under this Addendum do not reduce or replace fees due under the Parent Agreement; they are cumulative.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

The Addendum Contract Value comprises the following components:

(a) **Addendum Base Subscription Fees.**  
Recurring fees for access to additional platform features, Regional Processing Zones, and Addendum Services (“**Addendum Base Subscription Fees**”), billed **annually in advance**, in accordance with the invoicing timing under **Section 3.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement, but subject to **Net 60** payment terms as described in Section 3.4 of this Addendum.

(b) **Addendum Usage-Based Fees.**  
Variable fees for incremental storage, compute, and data transfer attributable to Additional Processing Activities, calculated based on the add-on rate card or pricing in applicable Addendum Order Forms. These fees are in addition to any “Usage-Based Fees” specified in **Section 3.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement.

(c) **Addendum Professional Services Fees.**  
Fees for implementation, data science, ML engineering, and related professional services specific to the Additional Processing Activities, charged on a time-and-materials or fixed-fee basis as specified in Addendum Order Forms and invoiced monthly in arrears, consistent with **Section 3.2(c)** and **Section 3.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

(d) **Travel and Expenses.**  
Company shall reimburse Service Provider for reasonable, documented out-of-pocket travel and living expenses incurred in connection with on-site workshops or implementation sessions requested by Company under this Addendum, consistent with **Section 3.2(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Invoicing under Addendum.**  

3.3.1 Unless otherwise specified in an Addendum Order Form:

(a) Addendum Base Subscription Fees shall be invoiced annually in advance on or around the Addendum Effective Date and each anniversary thereof during the Addendum Term;  

(b) Addendum Usage-Based Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears;  

(c) Addendum Professional Services Fees shall be invoiced monthly in arrears, based on actual hours worked or milestones achieved; and  

(d) Travel and expenses shall be invoiced monthly in arrears with supporting receipts.

3.4 **Payment Terms (Net 60).**  

Notwithstanding **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms – Net 45)** of the Parent Agreement, for all invoices issued under this Addendum, **Company shall pay all undisputed amounts within sixty (60) days from the invoice date (“Net 60”)** by wire transfer or other agreed method. All other aspects of Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement (currency, payment method, etc.) remain applicable.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  

Section **3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement applies to all invoices under this Addendum, except that: Company shall provide written notice of good-faith dispute within fifteen (15) days of receiving the relevant invoice, and shall pay all undisputed amounts by the Net 60 due date. The Parties will cooperate in good faith to resolve such disputes, and amounts determined payable shall be due within fifteen (15) days of resolution.

3.6 **Late Payments; Interest.**  

For any undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Addendum, Section **3.6 (Late Payments; Interest)** of the Parent Agreement applies, including interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, and Service Provider’s right to suspend the Addendum Services upon ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice if overdue amounts remain unpaid.

3.7 **Taxes.**  

Section **3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all fees under this Addendum. The Addendum Contract Value and any related charges are exclusive of Taxes, which shall be borne by Company, subject to the same terms and gross-up obligations.

3.8 **No Setoff.**  

Company shall have no right of setoff against fees owed under this Addendum, except as expressly permitted by **Section 3.8 (No Setoff)** of the Parent Agreement and required by a final, non-appealable court order.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term and Expiration.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (March 25, 2026) and shall continue in full force and effect until **November 7, 2028** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.1.2 The Addendum Term may extend beyond the **Expiration Date (April 16, 2027)** of the Parent Agreement only if the Parties expressly amend or extend the Parent Agreement to cover such period. In the absence of such extension, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement shall have the effect described in Section 4.5 below.

4.2 **Termination for Convenience.**  

Section **4.2 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Addendum, except that termination of this Addendum for convenience shall be permitted **upon one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** by either Party, provided that:

(a) Termination of the Parent Agreement for convenience by either Party shall automatically terminate this Addendum on the effective date of termination of the Parent Agreement; and  

(b) Termination of this Addendum for convenience by Company shall not relieve Company of its obligations to pay all committed minimum recurring charges and non-cancellable components of the Addendum Contract Value, as expressly stated in applicable Addendum Order Forms.

4.3 **Termination for Cause.**  

Section **4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Addendum. A material breach of this Addendum that remains uncured after the applicable cure period shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement and vice versa, to the extent the breach relates to obligations incorporated from the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Addendum.**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) Company shall pay Service Provider all fees accrued and outstanding under this Addendum as of the effective date of termination, including any early termination charges relating to Addendum-specific commitments;  

(b) Except as expressly stated otherwise in this Addendum, all rights and licenses granted under this Addendum shall terminate, provided that Company’s rights under the Parent Agreement shall remain in effect as to Deliverables and Services under the Parent Agreement, to the extent the Parent Agreement remains in force;  

(c) Service Provider shall provide, upon Company’s written request within thirty (30) days of termination of this Addendum, a one-time export of Client Data associated specifically with the Additional Processing Activities, in a commonly used electronic format, consistent with **Section 4.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement. Non-standard transition assistance may be provided at Service Provider’s then-current professional services rates;  

(d) Service Provider shall delete or anonymize Client Data and Personal Data processed solely under this Addendum in accordance with **Section 11.6 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement and Section 11.4 of this Addendum, except to the extent retention is required by Applicable Law or necessary to protect legal interests.

4.5 **Effect of Termination or Expiration of Parent Agreement.**  

4.5.1 This Addendum is subordinate to and dependent upon the Parent Agreement. Accordingly, termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement, for any reason, shall automatically and immediately result in termination of this Addendum, without the need for further action by either Party.  

4.5.2 In such event, the provisions of Section 4.4 of this Addendum shall apply, and any surviving provisions of the Parent Agreement (pursuant to **Section 4.5 (Survival)** thereof) shall continue to govern the rights and obligations of the Parties with respect to Client Data and Personal Data previously processed under this Addendum.

4.6 **Survival.**  

Any clauses in this Addendum which by their nature should survive termination or expiration shall survive, including Articles **III** (to the extent of accrued fee obligations), **V**, **VI**, **VII**, **VIII**, **IX**, **XI**, **XIV**, **XV**, and **XVI**, and Sections **2.7**, **4.4**, and **4.5**, in each case to the extent consistent with the survival provisions of the Parent Agreement.
