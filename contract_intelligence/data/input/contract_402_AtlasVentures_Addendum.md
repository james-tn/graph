## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent Agreement, Reference No. **CSA‑ATL‑202406‑248** (contract_248), effective as of June 07, 2024, under which Atlas agreed to provide, and Contoso agreed to procure, consulting, advisory, professional, and related services as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement;

## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relation to Parent Agreement and Parent SOW.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and the Parent SOW. It constitutes an amendment and supplementation to the Parent SOW as contemplated by **Section 16.4 (Amendments)** of the Parent SOW and **Section 16.3 (Amendments)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.1.2 The Addendum Services form part of the “Services” defined in **Section 1.17** of the Parent Agreement and further elaborated in **Article II (Scope of Services)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article II** of the Parent SOW. Except as explicitly modified herein, nothing in this Addendum shall be construed to limit the general scope of the Services already defined in the Parent SOW.

2.1.3 In the event of any conflict between the terms of this **Article II** and **Article II** of the Parent SOW, the terms of this Addendum shall prevail **solely with respect to the Addendum Services**, and the Parent SOW shall continue to govern all other Services.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Services.**  

2.2.1 Under this Addendum, Vendor shall provide an extended **Post‑Implementation Optimization, Analytics, and Managed Transformation Support Program** (the “Addendum Program”) designed to complement the Global Digital Transformation and Process Optimization Program defined in **Section 2.2** of the Parent SOW. The Addendum Program shall:

(a) Extend advisory and optimization support beyond the original SOW Expiration Date of December 28, 2026, through the **Addendum Expiration Date** of **April 13, 2029**;  
(b) Introduce structured **Optimization Waves** in EMEA, North America, and APAC, building on the Regional Rollout defined in **Section 2.6.3** of the Parent SOW;  
(c) Enhance change adoption, process stabilization, and benefits realization in the domains originally identified in the Parent SOW (e.g., Order‑to‑Cash, Procure‑to‑Pay, Record‑to‑Report, Customer Lifecycle Management);  
(d) Provide value‑tracking, KPI monitoring, and advanced analytics services leveraging the **Process Performance KPI Definitions & Templates (Deliverable B4)** and **Adoption & Readiness Dashboard (Deliverable D5)** defined in **Sections 2.3.2 and 2.3.4** of the Parent SOW; and  
(e) Introduce **managed governance support** for the Project Governance Committee established in **Section 2.8** of the Parent SOW, including recurring reporting, risk management, and change control support.

2.2.2 The Addendum Program is aligned with the categories set forth in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement (strategic business consulting, business process optimization, implementation support, change management and training, and data analytics and reporting) but is more narrowly focused on post‑implementation optimization and ongoing support rather than initial design and planning.

2.3 **Detailed Addendum Scope and Workstreams.**  

2.3.1 **Workstream E – Post‑Implementation Stabilization & Optimization.**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Conduct structured stability assessments across the core processes designed under **Workstream B (Process Optimization)** of the Parent SOW, using the Future‑State Process Designs & RACI Matrices (Deliverable B3) as baselines;  
(b) Facilitate workshops with Client stakeholders in each region to identify pain points, residual process gaps, control weaknesses, and additional automation opportunities;  
(c) Prioritize optimization initiatives based on impact, feasibility, and alignment with the **Multi‑Year Digital Transformation Roadmap (Deliverable A3)**;  
(d) Design and document incremental process improvements, including updated process maps, control points, and RACI adjustments, ensuring consistency with the **Program Governance Framework & Charter (Deliverable C1)**;  
(e) Support Client in implementing and operationalizing the incremental improvements (advisory only, consistent with **Section 2.9(d)** of the Parent SOW confirming Vendor’s non‑implementation role); and  
(f) Provide periodic stabilization reports summarizing changes implemented, risk reduction, and performance improvements.

**Key Addendum Deliverables for Workstream E** shall include:

- E1: **Regional Stability & Optimization Assessment Reports (per major region)**  
- E2: **Updated Future‑State Process Maps & Control Matrices**  
- E3: **Optimization Backlog & Prioritization Matrix**  
- E4: **Quarterly Stabilization & Improvement Summary Reports**

2.3.2 **Workstream F – Benefits Realization & Advanced Analytics.**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Define or refine measurable value drivers and KPIs using the KPI templates created under Deliverable B4 of the Parent SOW;  
(b) Design and implement (advisory) a KPI measurement framework and benefits realization model for the Program;  
(c) Provide advanced analytics, dashboards, and periodic performance review packs, leveraging the **Adoption & Readiness Dashboard (Deliverable D5)** and relevant Client data, subject to **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum;  
(d) Facilitate quarterly benefits realization review sessions with the Project Governance Committee, providing insights and recommendations; and  
(e) Prepare an annual **Value Realization Report** summarizing realized and projected benefits vs. the roadmap defined under Workstream A of the Parent SOW.

**Key Addendum Deliverables for Workstream F** shall include:

- F1: **Benefits Realization Framework & KPI Catalog**  
- F2: **Standardized Analytics & Dashboard Specifications**  
- F3: **Quarterly Performance & Benefits Review Packs**  
- F4: **Annual Value Realization Report**

2.3.3 **Workstream G – Managed Governance, Change & Training Support.**  
Vendor shall:

(a) Provide ongoing governance support to the **Project Governance Committee** established under **Section 2.8.1** of the Parent SOW, including preparation of agendas, status reports, risk and issue logs, and decision registers;  
(b) Maintain and refine the **Risk & Issue Management Framework and Initial Register (Deliverable C4)** for the duration of the Addendum Term;  
(c) Provide targeted change management and training refresh sessions, building on the **Change Management Strategy & Plan (Deliverable D1)**, **Stakeholder Analysis & Communications Plan (Deliverable D2)**, and **Training Curriculum & Materials (Deliverable D3)** from the Parent SOW;  
(d) Conduct periodic train‑the‑trainer refresh sessions and maintain updated attendance records, consistent with **Deliverable D4 (Train‑the‑Trainer Sessions & Attendance Records)**; and  
(e) Assist Client in managing and prioritizing **Change Requests** related to process and governance changes, ensuring alignment with the Change Management process in **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum.

**Key Addendum Deliverables for Workstream G** shall include:

- G1: **Governance Meeting Packs & Decision Registers (ongoing)**  
- G2: **Updated Risk & Issue Registers (rolling)**  
- G3: **Change Management & Training Refresh Materials**  
- G4: **Train‑the‑Trainer Refresh Session Reports**

2.4 **Deliverables; Acceptance.**  

2.4.1 The Addendum Deliverables identified in Sections 2.3.1–2.3.3 shall constitute “Deliverables” within the meaning of **Section 1.9** and **Section 2.5 (Deliverables; Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, as applied through **Section 2.4** of the Parent SOW.

2.4.2 Unless otherwise expressly stated in this Addendum, the acceptance process for Addendum Deliverables shall be as specified in **Sections 2.5.2–2.5.4** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent SOW, including the Client’s ten (10) Business Day review period, the Vendor’s obligation to correct notified deficiencies, and the deemed acceptance provision.

2.4.3 Where an Addendum Deliverable is explicitly identified as an iterative or periodic deliverable (e.g., Quarterly Reports, Governance Meeting Packs), each such instance shall be deemed a separate Deliverable for purposes of acceptance, invoicing, and warranties.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities (Addendum‑Specific).**  

In addition to the responsibilities specified in **Section 2.5** of the Parent SOW and **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, the Client shall:

(a) Provide timely access to updated Client Materials and system data necessary for analytics and benefits measurement, subject to **Article XI** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum;  
(b) Designate regional process owners and benefit owners to participate in Optimization Waves and benefits realization reviews;  
(c) Maintain internal project governance structures and decision‑making mechanisms, including the continued operation of the Project Governance Committee; and  
(d) Ensure timely validation and approval (or reasonable rejection) of Addendum Deliverables and associated Milestones to avoid delays, recognizing the impact of Client delays as set out in **Section 2.5.2** of the Parent SOW.

2.6 **Timeline, Milestones, and Optimization Waves.**  

2.6.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent SOW currently anticipates completion of core Program activities by **December 15, 2026** (Milestone 6 in **Section 2.6.2** of the Parent SOW). The Addendum Services shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and continue through **April 13, 2029**, with a focus on post‑implementation optimization and benefits realization.

2.6.2 **Illustrative Milestones under this Addendum** (subject to refinement via the Change Management process in **Article XIII** of this Addendum and the Parent SOW):

2.6.3 The exact sequencing, content, and timing of Optimization Waves may be refined by mutual agreement through Change Orders pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum.

2.7 **Performance Standards and Service Levels.**  

2.7.1 Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, the SLAs in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, and the additional Addendum SLAs set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum.

If performance falls below the applicable service levels, the remedies set forth in **Article XII** of this Addendum shall apply, without prejudice to the limitations in **Article IX**.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (September 30, 2025)** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent SOW, shall continue in full force and effect until **April 13, 2029** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”) (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.1.2 The Addendum Term overlaps and extends beyond the SOW Term defined in **Section 4.1** of the Parent SOW (March 28, 2025 – December 28, 2026). The Parties acknowledge that (i) the original SOW Term continues to apply to the base Program, and (ii) this Addendum extends the Parties’ relationship with respect to the Addendum Services through the Addendum Expiration Date.

4.2 **Renewal or Extension.**  
Any extension or renewal of this Addendum beyond the Addendum Expiration Date shall be subject to the mutual written agreement of the Parties, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.2** of the Parent SOW, and shall be documented in a further amendment or addendum.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client‑Friendly).**  

4.3.1 **By Client.** Client may terminate this Addendum for convenience, in whole or in part, upon providing at least **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Vendor. This is more favorable to Client than the one hundred eighty (180) day notice period applicable to SOW‑level termination under **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent SOW and shall govern solely with respect to this Addendum.

4.3.2 **By Vendor.** Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon providing at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client. This extended notice period provides Client additional time to secure alternative service providers for the Addendum Services.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause in accordance with **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.4** of the Parent SOW, including termination for material breach not cured within thirty (30) days of written notice, or upon insolvency‑related events.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement or Parent SOW.**  

4.5.1 In accordance with **Section 4.5.1** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, upon expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement, this Addendum and the Parent SOW shall automatically terminate unless the Parties expressly agree in writing that certain Services will continue under alternative terms.

4.5.2 In the event the Parent SOW is terminated in accordance with **Article IV** thereof while the Parent Agreement remains in effect, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date, unless expressly preserved by written agreement of the Parties.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  

4.6.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum, Vendor shall cease performance of Addendum Services, except as may be necessary to provide transition assistance pursuant to **Section 4.7** of the Parent SOW and **Section 4.6.2** below.

4.6.2 Client shall pay Vendor:

(a) all Addendum Fees and expenses accrued but unpaid up to the effective date of termination;  
(b) all Addendum Fees for work in progress performed up to the effective date of termination (including T&M work not yet invoiced); and  
(c) any mutually agreed early termination or wind‑down charges explicitly stated in this Addendum or in any applicable Change Order, provided that such charges shall be reasonable and commensurate with Vendor’s non‑recoverable costs.

4.6.3 For convenience terminations initiated by Client under **Section 4.3.1**, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate its costs and losses.

4.7 **Transition Assistance (Addendum).**
