## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS, SERVICES, AND DELIVERABLES

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Addendum **adds products or services** to the scope described in **Article II of the Parent Agreement**. Unless expressly modified herein, all terms and conditions of **Article II (Scope of Services and Deliverables)**, **Section 2.4 (Timelines and Milestones)**, **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)**, and **Section 2.6 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply, mutatis mutandis, to the Additional Deliverables and Additional Services.

2.1.2 As between the Parties, the Parent Agreement shall continue to govern all pre-existing Deliverables and Services. This Addendum extends the existing relationship by adding additional hardware capacity, enhanced edge and server functionality, managed Hosted Services, and ongoing optimization Services, as set forth herein.

### 2.2 Additional Hardware Deliverables

2.2.1 **Expanded Edge Gateway Fleet.** In addition to the Equipment referenced in **Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement**, Vendor shall manufacture, configure, and supply to Company an additional fleet of industrial-grade edge computing gateway units (the “**Additional Edge Units**”), consisting of:

(a) Up to **250** additional edge gateway units, each equipped with redundant power supplies and industrial-temperature components suitable for deployment in harsh environments;  
(b) Integrated TPM (Trusted Platform Module) chips and secure boot capabilities consistent with the security features described in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement; and  
(c) Optional cellular (5G) modules for remote sites, as specified in applicable Work Orders.

2.2.2 **High-Performance Server Appliances.** Vendor shall supply additional rack-mounted server appliances (the “**Additional Server Appliances**”) to host expanded processing workloads resulting from Company’s internal growth:

(a) Up to **40** additional 2U or 4U server appliances, each pre-configured with Vendor’s proprietary system software stack;  
(b) Redundant, hot-swappable storage arrays and network interface cards to support high availability requirements; and  
(c) Hardware configurations aligned with performance parameters set forth in mutually agreed Specifications attached as **Schedule A** to this Addendum.

2.2.3 **Redundant Components and Spares.** In line with **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall maintain and supply a pool of spare edge units, power modules, and network components sufficient to support a minimum **N+1** redundancy model across Company’s designated production regions, as further detailed in Work Orders.

### 2.3 Additional Embedded Software, Managed and Hosted Services

2.3.1 **Advanced Embedded Software Features.** Vendor shall provide updated and enhanced Embedded Software (as defined in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement) for both existing and Additional Edge Units and Additional Server Appliances, including:

(a) Advanced telemetry and analytics agents capable of streaming high-volume metrics to Company’s central analytics platform;  
(b) Enhanced security modules enabling certificate-based mutual TLS, role-based access controls, and secure remote command execution;  
(c) Over-the-air (OTA) fleet management and firmware update capabilities with staged rollout, rollback, and audit logging functions.

2.3.2 **Managed Hosted Services.** Vendor shall provide expanded Hosted Services (as defined in **Section 12.1.1** of the Parent Agreement), including:

(a) A multi-tenant cloud-based management console for monitoring device health, configuration, and performance metrics for all Additional Edge Units and Additional Server Appliances;  
(b) Proactive incident detection using rule-based and machine-learning-based anomaly detection to trigger alerts and automated remediation workflows;  
(c) Role-based dashboards and reporting tools tailored for Company’s operations, IT, and security teams.

2.3.3 **Technical Integration Services.** Vendor shall perform Additional Services to integrate the Additional Deliverables with Company’s existing systems, including:

(a) Design and implementation of new configuration templates and scripts (consistent with **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement) supporting multi-region deployments;  
(b) Integration with Company’s identity and access management systems, ticketing systems, and SIEM tools;  
(c) Designing and executing non-production pilot deployments, followed by staged rollouts to production environments.

### 2.4 Implementation, Optimization, and Training Services

2.4.1 **Implementation Services.** Building upon **Section 2.3.1 (Implementation and Deployment Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

(a) Develop a detailed implementation roadmap for the Additional Edge Units and Additional Server Appliances, including capacity planning, region-by-region deployment waves, and dependencies on Company resources;  
(b) Provide on-site or remote support for physical installation, logical configuration, and initial operational validation;  
(c) Coordinate with Company’s designated project managers to ensure minimal disruption to ongoing operations.

2.4.2 **Optimization Services.** Vendor shall provide continuous optimization Services during the Addendum Term, including:

(a) Quarterly performance assessments of deployed Additional Deliverables, including resource utilization, latency, and throughput benchmarks;  
(b) Recommendations for configuration changes and architectural adjustments to improve performance, resiliency, and cost-efficiency;  
(c) Implementation, upon Company’s approval, of optimization recommendations, which may be delivered under time-and-materials or fixed-fee arrangements as specified in applicable Work Orders.

2.4.3 **Training Services.** In addition to the training contemplated by **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:

(a) Conduct advanced-level training sessions focused on the management console, optimization tools, and troubleshooting workflows specific to the Additional Deliverables;  
(b) Provide updated and supplemental training materials, including updated operator guides and troubleshooting playbooks, in electronic format;  
(c) Offer periodic refresher training, at least annually, for Company’s designated personnel.

### 2.5 Timelines, Milestones, and Acceptance

2.5.1 **Deployment Timelines.** The Parties anticipate that initial deployment of the Additional Edge Units and Additional Server Appliances will commence within **ninety (90) days** of the Addendum Effective Date, subject to timely issuance of Work Orders and reasonably necessary Company cooperation, and will continue in phases throughout the Addendum Term.

2.5.2 **Milestones.** The Parties shall establish specific project milestones in Work Orders issued under this Addendum, which may include:

(a) Completion of pilot deployments in designated regions;  
(b) Completion of each subsequent regional deployment wave;  
(c) Completion of integration with Company’s monitoring, incident management, and security infrastructure;  
(d) Completion of optimization assessments and implementation phases.

2.5.3 **Acceptance.** Acceptance of Additional Deliverables and Additional Services shall be conducted in accordance with **Section 2.6 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise expressly set forth in a Work Order. Unless otherwise stated, Company shall have a **fifteen (15) Business Day** acceptance period after completion of each milestone.

2.5.4 **Non-Conforming Deliverables.** In the event of material non-conformity, Vendor shall remedy such non-conformity in accordance with **Section 2.6.2** and the warranties in **Article VII of the Parent Agreement** and **Article VII of this Addendum**.

### 2.6 Performance Standards

2.6.1 Vendor shall perform the Additional Services in accordance with the performance standards specified in **Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement**, the Service Levels set forth in **Article XII of the Parent Agreement**, and any additional Service Levels specified in **Article XII of this Addendum**.

2.6.2 Vendor shall ensure that the Additional Deliverables, when properly installed and used in accordance with the Documentation and Specifications, materially conform to the applicable Specifications.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Term of Addendum

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **April 12, 2028**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the applicable provisions of **Article IV of the Parent Agreement**.

(a) The Parent Agreement is hereby **extended solely for purposes of governing this Addendum and all Work Orders issued hereunder** through the Addendum Term; and  
(b) All terms of the Parent Agreement that by their nature or by express language were intended to survive expiration shall also survive and apply to this Addendum throughout the Addendum Term.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Notwithstanding **Section 4.2.1 of the Parent Agreement** (which permits termination for convenience upon one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice), either Party may terminate this **Addendum** for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.

4.2.2 In the event of termination for convenience of this Addendum by Company:

(a) Company shall pay Vendor for all Additional Deliverables accepted and Additional Services properly performed up to the effective date of termination;  
(b) Company shall pay any non-cancellable third-party commitments incurred by Vendor specifically in reliance on this Addendum, subject to Vendor’s duty to mitigate; and  
(c) Any early termination charges specifically set forth in a Work Order under this Addendum shall apply.

4.2.3 Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, unless expressly stated in the notice of termination and agreed by the Parties in writing.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice in the event of a material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement, to the extent such breach materially affects the Additional Deliverables or Additional Services, and such breach is not cured within thirty (30) days after written notice, consistent with **Section 4.3.1 of the Parent Agreement**.

4.3.2 The insolvency or similar events described in **Section 4.3.2 of the Parent Agreement** shall constitute grounds for immediate termination of this Addendum as well.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination; Relationship with Parent Agreement

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) All rights and licenses granted under this Addendum that are term-limited shall terminate, consistent with **Section 4.4.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Company shall cease use of any term-limited Additional Hosted Services and any Additional Deliverables subject to term-limited licenses;  
(c) Company shall pay all undisputed Fees accrued but unpaid for Additional Deliverables and Additional Services through the effective date of termination; and  
(d) Each Party shall return or destroy Confidential Information related to this Addendum as required by **Section 5.5** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.2 Termination of the **Parent Agreement** for any reason will automatically result in termination of this Addendum as of the same effective date, unless the Parties mutually agree in writing to continue certain Services under a new agreement.

4.4.3 Termination of this Addendum shall not affect the Parties’ rights and obligations with respect to the Parent Agreement (including other schedules, statements of work, or addenda) except to the extent expressly stated in a written amendment.
