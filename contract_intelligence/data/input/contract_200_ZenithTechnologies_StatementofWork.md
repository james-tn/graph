## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Master Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This SOW is executed **pursuant to** and **under** the Master Agreement dated **24 March 2024** (Ref: **MSA-ZEN-202403-197**, **contract_197**) and is subject in all respects to the terms and conditions set forth in the Master Agreement.  

2.1.2 In accordance with **Section 2.1 (Master Framework)** of the Master Agreement, this SOW sets forth the specific details of the Services, Deliverables, timelines, responsibilities, Service Levels, and Fees for the Project described herein.  

2.1.3 In the event of a conflict between the terms of this SOW and the Master Agreement, and unless explicitly stated otherwise, **the SOW shall prevail solely with respect to the subject matter of this SOW**, as stated in Section 2.1 of the Master Agreement.

2.2 **Overall Project Description.**  

Vendor shall perform a comprehensive multi-phase technology implementation and managed services engagement, including:

  a. **Solution Design & Architecture** – Requirements gathering, business process analysis, solution blueprinting, and technical architecture design for Client’s target ERP and CRM landscape;  

  b. **Configuration, Customization & Integration** – Configuration of ERP and CRM modules, development of custom components and integrations to Client’s existing systems, and migration of data from legacy systems;  

  c. **Testing, Deployment & Go-Live Support** – Unit, system, and integration testing, User Acceptance Testing (UAT) support, performance testing, and production Go-Live preparation;  

  d. **Training & Knowledge Transfer** – Development and delivery of end-user and administrator training, documentation, runbooks, and handover materials; and  

  e. **Managed Services & Support** – Post Go-Live application management, incident handling, optimization, and periodic reporting in line with the Service Levels and incident handling framework referenced in **Article XII** of the Master Agreement.

These categories of Services align with and further specify the service categories described in **Sections 2.2.1, 2.2.2, and 2.2.3** of the Master Agreement.

2.3 **Project Phases and Timelines.**  

The Project will be executed in the following phases, with indicative timelines measured from the SOW Effective Date. Detailed dates and dependencies shall be set forth in the Project Plan, which will be jointly developed and agreed within twenty (20) Business Days after the SOW Effective Date.

  2.3.1 **Phase 1 – Initiation and Discovery (Weeks 1–6)**  
  - Conduct stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops;  
  - Document “as-is” and “to-be” business processes;  
  - Identify scope boundaries, integrations, and data migration requirements;  
  - Produce the **Solution Blueprint Document** and **High-Level Project Plan** (Deliverables D1 and D2).

  2.3.2 **Phase 2 – Design and Build (Weeks 7–24)**  
  - Develop detailed functional and technical design documents;  
  - Configure ERP core modules (Finance, Procurement, Inventory) and CRM modules (Sales, Service);  
  - Build integrations with at least five (5) specified systems (HRIS, Data Warehouse, Legacy Billing, Identity Management, and Customer Portal);  
  - Develop data migration scripts and validation routines;  
  - Deliver the **Configuration Workbook**, **Integration Design Specifications**, and **Data Migration Strategy** (Deliverables D3, D4, D5).

  2.3.3 **Phase 3 – Testing, UAT and Go-Live (Weeks 25–36)**  
  - Prepare test plans and test scripts;  
  - Conduct system integration testing and performance testing;  
  - Support Client’s UAT cycles, including defect logging and remediation;  
  - Execute cutover rehearsals and final cutover;  
  - Achieve **Go-Live** for at least two (2) core business regions;  
  - Deliver **Test Summary Reports**, **Defect Logs**, and **Cutover Runbook** (Deliverables D6, D7, D8).

  2.3.4 **Phase 4 – Stabilization & Transition to Managed Services (Weeks 37–48)**  
  - Provide hypercare support for four (4) weeks post Go-Live;  
  - Tune performance and resolve high-severity defects;  
  - Transition primary support responsibilities to the Managed Services team;  
  - Deliver **Operational Runbooks**, **Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)**, and **Support Transition Documentation** (Deliverables D9, D10).

  2.3.5 **Phase 5 – Managed Services (Month 12 – SOW Expiration)**  
  - Provide Level 1, 2, and 3 support in accordance with the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Master Agreement and Section 12.3 of this SOW;  
  - Provide periodic health checks, reporting, performance optimization, and minor enhancements;  
  - Deliver monthly **Service Performance Reports** and semi-annual **Optimization & Roadmap Reports** (Deliverables D11, D12).

The Parties acknowledge that, in accordance with Section 2.5.1 of the Master Agreement, timelines may be affected by Client dependencies, third-party delays, and other factors. Any material changes to the schedule shall be governed by the change management process under **Article XIII** of the Master Agreement and Article XIII of this SOW.

2.4 **Deliverables.**  

Without limiting the generality of Section 2.3 above, Vendor shall produce at least the following Deliverables:

- **D1** – Solution Blueprint Document;  
- **D2** – High-Level Project Plan;  
- **D3** – Configuration Workbook (ERP and CRM);  
- **D4** – Integration Design Specifications and API Catalog;  
- **D5** – Data Migration Strategy and Mapping Specification;  
- **D6** – Test Plans, Test Scripts and Test Summary Reports;  
- **D7** – UAT Defect Logs and Resolution Plan;  
- **D8** – Cutover Plan and Cutover Runbook;  
- **D9** – Operational Runbooks and SOPs;  
- **D10** – Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Pack;  
- **D11** – Monthly Service Performance Reports (including uptime, incident statistics, SLA adherence);  
- **D12** – Semi-annual Optimization & Roadmap Reports.

The Parties acknowledge and agree that the Deliverables constitute “Deliverables” and “Work Product” as described in Sections 1.10 and 6.4 of the Master Agreement and may involve Vendor Pre-Existing IP and Third-Party Materials as described in Sections 6.3 and 6.6 of the Master Agreement.

2.5.1 Vendor shall perform the Services in accordance with the performance standards set forth in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Master Agreement, including performance in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards and utilizing personnel with the requisite skills, experience, and qualifications.

Client’s obligations in connection with this SOW shall include, in addition to those expressly set forth in **Section 2.6** of the Master Agreement:

Vendor shall assign the following Key Personnel to this Project:
