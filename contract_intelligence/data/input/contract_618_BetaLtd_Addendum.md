## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.** This Addendum **adds products and services** to, and supplements, the scope of the Solution and Services described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement (Ref: **PUR‑BET‑202404‑591**, **contract_591**). Except as expressly provided herein, the roles, responsibilities, performance standards, and acceptance procedures set forth in **Sections 2.1 through 2.7 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply to the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables.

2.2 **Addendum Deliverables.** Without limiting the generality of Section 2.1 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall provide the following **Addendum Deliverables**:

2.2.1 **Advanced Analytics Module.**  
The Service Provider shall deliver and configure an Advanced Analytics Module that extends the **Core Analytics Platform** referenced in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement. This module shall include, at a minimum:

  (a) Predictive modeling capabilities (e.g., regression, classification, time‑series forecasting) configurable by authorized Company data scientists and analysts;

  (b) A library of at least **ten (10)** pre‑built predictive models (e.g., demand forecasting, churn prediction, anomaly detection, revenue forecasting) tailored to the Company’s key use cases as defined jointly during the Project Addendum Plan phase;

  (c) Automated feature engineering capabilities and model performance dashboards integrated with the existing executive‑level and operational dashboards referenced in Section 2.2.3 of the Parent Agreement;

  (d) Role‑based access controls aligned with the security model established during Phase 1 of the Parent Agreement (Section 2.3.1), ensuring only authorized personnel can create, deploy, or modify models; and

  (e) Documentation and training material specific to the Advanced Analytics Module.

2.2.2 **Enhanced Data Connectors and Integrations.**  
In addition to the **Custom Integrations** described in Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall design, develop, test, and deploy **Enhanced Data Connectors**, including:

  (a) At least **four (4)** new API‑based connectors to third‑party SaaS platforms designated by the Company in writing;

  (b) At least **two (2)** high‑volume streaming connectors capable of ingesting IoT or event‑driven data at rates to be defined in the Project Addendum Plan;

  (c) Batch ingestion processes for multiple new internal data sources, including but not limited to manufacturing, supply chain, or HR systems as identified by the Company;

  (d) Data quality validation routines and monitoring dashboards; and

  (e) Updated documentation and data flow diagrams reflecting these Enhanced Data Connectors.

2.2.3 **Additional Configuration and Customization.**  
Building upon Section 2.2.3 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall:

  (a) Configure at least **two (2)** additional executive‑level dashboards, **eight (8)** additional operational dashboards, and **fifteen (15)** additional standard or ad‑hoc reports for Extended Users and new business units;

  (b) Implement advanced alerting and notification rules tied to the outputs of the Advanced Analytics Module;

  (c) Provide configuration to support regional reporting for additional geographies within North America or other territories as agreed in writing; and

  (d) Align all new configurations with the Specifications, Service Levels, and security standards in the Parent Agreement.

2.2.4 **Supplemental Documentation.**  
In addition to the Documentation obligations in Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall provide:

  (a) Updated user manuals, administrator guides, and technical documentation specifically addressing the Advanced Analytics Module, Enhanced Data Connectors, and Premium Support workflows;

  (b) A technical runbook describing operational procedures, incident handling, and escalation paths for the Addendum Deliverables; and

  (c) Version‑controlled documentation made available to the Company via an online portal or a mutually agreed repository.

2.2.5 **Additional Training Services.**  
Supplementing the training commitments in Section 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall provide:

  (a) Up to **five (5)** additional virtual training sessions for end users, focusing on use of the Advanced Analytics Module and new dashboards;

  (b) Up to **three (3)** additional administrator or power‑user training sessions, including content on data governance, model lifecycle management, and advanced configuration;

  (c) At least **one (1)** on‑site workshop (subject to travel and expense provisions under Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement and Article III of this Addendum) for Company’s analytics leadership and architects; and

  (d) Training materials that may be reused internally by the Company for onboarding new users.

2.2.6 **Premium Support Services.**  
In addition to the **Support and Maintenance** set forth in Section 2.2.6 and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, the Service Provider shall provide Premium Support Services as described in Article XII of this Addendum, including:

  (a) Extended support hours to provide 24x7 coverage for Severity 1 (Critical) incidents;

  (b) Designated technical account management (“TAM”) resources;

  (c) Quarterly service reviews; and

  (d) Enhanced SLA targets and additional service credits as described in Section 12.4 of this Addendum.

2.3 **Project Phases and Timelines for Addendum.**  
The Parties shall use the phased approach in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement as a framework and shall incorporate the Addendum Deliverables into a **Project Addendum Plan (Schedule 1)**, which shall include at least the following phases:

  (a) **Phase A – Addendum Initiation and Design (Estimated 4–6 Weeks).** Requirements definition for the Advanced Analytics Module and Enhanced Data Connectors, alignment with existing data model, and identification of Extended Users and new use cases.

  (b) **Phase B – Build, Integration, and Configuration (Estimated 10–14 Weeks).** Development and configuration of the Advanced Analytics Module, Enhanced Data Connectors, and additional dashboards and reports.

  (c) **Phase C – UAT and Model Validation (Estimated 4–6 Weeks).** Joint user acceptance testing in accordance with Section 2.3.3 of the Parent Agreement, with additional focus on validating predictive models’ performance.

  (d) **Phase D – Production Deployment and Stabilization (Estimated 4–6 Weeks).** Deployment into the Solution Environment, cut‑over activities, stabilization, and transition into Premium Support, consistent with Sections 2.3.4 and 2.3.5 of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Performance Standards.**  
The Service Provider shall perform all Addendum Services:

  (a) In a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards, as required by Section 2.4(a) of the Parent Agreement;

  (b) In compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including Data Protection Laws (as defined in Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement);

  (c) In conformity with the Specifications and Project Addendum Plan; and  

  (d) Using personnel with appropriate skills, training, and experience.

2.5 **Company Responsibilities.**  
The Company shall support the successful implementation of this Addendum by fulfilling the responsibilities set forth in Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement (Company Responsibilities), including:

  (a) Providing timely access to necessary systems, data sources, and personnel for designing and deploying the Enhanced Data Connectors;

  (b) Designating product owners or business leads for the new analytics use cases; and

  (c) Providing timely feedback during UAT and reasonable support for change management and adoption.

2.6 **Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables.**  
The acceptance process set forth in Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Addendum Deliverables. Unless otherwise specified in the Project Addendum Plan:

  (a) A Deliverable shall be deemed accepted upon the earlier of: (i) the Company’s written acceptance; or (ii) fifteen (15) Business Days after delivery if no written rejection is delivered;

  (b) If the Company rejects a Deliverable, it shall provide written notice specifying material non‑conformities with reasonable detail, and the Service Provider shall use commercially reasonable efforts to correct and resubmit the Deliverable; and

  (c) The Parties shall repeat this process until acceptance is achieved.

2.7 **High‑Risk Allocation.**  
The Parties reaffirm the high‑risk allocation noted in Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement and, in light of the **uncapped liability** regime in Article IX of the Parent Agreement, agree that **this Addendum is likewise high‑risk and vendor‑favorable**, including broad Company indemnities (as expanded in Article VIII of this Addendum), restrictive IP rights (Article VI), and extended termination notice periods (Article IV).

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The total **Addendum Contract Value** under this Addendum shall be **US $4,888,767** (Four Million Eight Hundred Eighty‑Eight Thousand Seven Hundred Sixty‑Seven U.S. Dollars), exclusive of any applicable **Taxes** consistent with Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  
The Addendum Contract Value shall consist of the following components (the “Addendum Fees”):

3.2.1 **One‑Time Advanced Implementation Fee.**  
An amount of **US $1,950,000** for design, implementation, integration, configuration, model development, and launch activities relating to the Advanced Analytics Module, Enhanced Data Connectors, and additional dashboards and reports described in Article II.

3.2.2 **Additional License and Subscription Fees (Fixed for Addendum Term).**  
An amount of **US $2,300,000**, payable in **equal annual installments** over the Addendum Term, for the Company’s right to access and use the Advanced Analytics Module, additional capacity, and any associated Vendor Materials, in accordance with Article VI of the Parent Agreement and Article VI of this Addendum.

3.2.3 **Premium Support Services Fees.**  
An amount of **US $450,000** for Premium Support Services, payable in **equal annual installments** over the Addendum Term and in addition to the Support and Maintenance Services fees set forth in Section 3.2.3 of the Parent Agreement.

3.2.4 **Additional Professional Services Contingency Pool.**  
An amount of **US $188,767** allocated for optional professional services under this Addendum (e.g., incremental model development, additional integrations, or advanced training), to be consumed at the rates set forth in Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement and any updated rate card agreed in writing.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms (Net 45).**  
Consistent with Section 3.3 and Section 3.3.5 of the Parent Agreement:

3.3.1 **Implementation Fees.**  
The Advanced Implementation Fee under Section 3.2.1 shall be invoiced as follows:

  (a) Forty percent (40%) upon the Addendum Effective Date;  
  (b) Thirty percent (30%) upon completion of Phase B – Build, Integration, and Configuration, as reasonably determined by the Service Provider; and  
  (c) Thirty percent (30%) upon go‑live of the Advanced Analytics Module in production.

3.3.2 **Additional License and Subscription Fees.**  
The additional annual license and subscription fees shall be invoiced **annually in advance** on or about each anniversary of the Addendum Effective Date, except that the first annual installment shall be invoiced on the Addendum Effective Date. These fees are in addition to the license and subscription fees described in Section 3.2.2 of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.3 **Premium Support Services Fees.**  
Premium Support Services fees shall be invoiced **annually in advance** together with the additional license and subscription fees under Section 3.3.2 above.

3.3.4 **Additional Professional Services Pool.**  
Fees for services drawn against the additional professional services contingency pool under Section 3.2.4 shall be invoiced **monthly in arrears**, based on actual hours incurred at the applicable rate card (Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement or as otherwise agreed). Any unused portion of this pool as of the Addendum Expiration Date shall be **forfeited without refund**, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

3.3.5 **Payment Terms (Net 45).**  
All undisputed invoices issued under this Addendum shall be **payable in U.S. Dollars within forty‑five (45) days** from the date of the invoice (Net 45), in accordance with Section 3.3.5 of the Parent Agreement.

3.4 **Late Payment; Suspension.**  
Section 3.5 (Late Payment; Suspension) and Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts) of the Parent Agreement are incorporated herein by reference. The Service Provider may, upon written notice, suspend provision of the Addendum Services or access to the Advanced Analytics Module if any undisputed amount remains unpaid beyond the applicable period, in the same manner as for the Solution and Services under the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Taxes.**  
All Addendum Fees are **exclusive of Taxes**. The Company shall be responsible for all Taxes in accordance with Section 3.7 (Taxes) of the Parent Agreement. If withholding is required, the Company shall provide appropriate documentation and reasonable assistance, consistent with the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Expenses.**  
Any reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred by the Service Provider’s personnel in connection with on‑site Addendum Services shall be reimbursed in accordance with Section 3.8 (Expenses) of the Parent Agreement, including the requirement for the Company’s written approval for any single trip expected to exceed US $3,000.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** and shall continue in full force and effect until **March 24, 2027** (the “Addendum Expiration Date”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement’s Term, as defined in Section 1.17 and Section 4.1 of the Parent Agreement, currently runs until **April 24, 2028**, and may renew pursuant to Section 4.2 thereof. This Addendum is intended to **co‑exist within** the Term of the Parent Agreement. If the Parent Agreement is not renewed, this Addendum shall **automatically terminate** no later than the termination or expiration date of the Parent Agreement, regardless of the Addendum Expiration Date.

4.3 **Renewal of Addendum.**  
Unless either Party provides written notice of non‑renewal of this Addendum at least **ninety (90) days** prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, the Parties may, by mutual written agreement, renew this Addendum for one or more periods aligned with any renewal of the Parent Agreement, subject to updated fees and terms as agreed in writing.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience.**  
The Parties reaffirm the termination for convenience rights set out in Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement, which are **Vendor‑favorable**. Accordingly:

  (a) The Company may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice** to the Service Provider; and  

  (b) The Service Provider may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon at least **ninety (90) days’ prior written notice** to the Company.

Any termination for convenience under this Addendum shall be treated as a termination for convenience under Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement for purposes of fees and early termination charges, mutatis mutandis.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause, consistent with Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement, in the event of the other Party’s material breach of this Addendum or the Parent Agreement as it relates to this Addendum, subject to a sixty (60) day cure period following written notice.

4.6 **Termination for Non‑Payment.**  
The Service Provider may terminate this Addendum for non‑payment on similar terms to Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement (Termination for Non‑Payment), including the requirement for prior written notice and applicable cure periods.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement **expires or is terminated for any reason**, then:

  (a) This Addendum shall automatically and simultaneously terminate without the need for separate notice;  

  (b) All rights and licenses granted under this Addendum shall cease at the same time as those under the Parent Agreement;  

  (c) The Company shall immediately cease use of the Advanced Analytics Module and any Addendum‑specific Vendor Materials; and  

  (d) All payment, confidentiality, IP, indemnity, and limitation of liability provisions applicable to accrued obligations shall survive in accordance with the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

4.8 **Effect of Termination; Early Termination Charge.**  
Upon any expiration or termination of this Addendum:

  (a) The Company shall pay all undisputed Addendum Fees accrued up to the effective date of termination;  

  (b) If the Company terminates for convenience, the Company shall pay an **early termination charge** equal to the lesser of (i) thirty percent (30%) of the remaining license, subscription, and Premium Support fees that would have been payable for the remainder of the Addendum Term; or (ii) **US $750,000** (which is an Addendum‑specific cap, more Vendor‑favorable than the Parent Agreement cap); and  

  (c) Sections of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination (including Articles III (to the extent of accrued obligations), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, and XVI) shall survive, together with the applicable provisions of the Parent Agreement.

4.9 **Transition Assistance.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum (other than for the Service Provider’s uncured material breach), the Service Provider shall provide transition assistance relating to the Addendum Deliverables on the same terms and at the same time and materials rates as set forth in Section 4.7 of the Parent Agreement, for a period of up to ninety (90) days unless otherwise agreed.
