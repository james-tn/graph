- **Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a corporation organized and existing under the laws of the State of Delaware, with its principal place of business at **1000 Innovation Parkway, Suite 500, Dallas, Texas 75201, USA** (hereinafter referred to as **“Contoso”, “Client”** or **“Company”**); and  

- **Epsilon Group, LLC**, a limited liability company organized and existing under the laws of the State of Texas, with its principal place of business at **2500 Epsilon Plaza, 9th Floor, Austin, Texas 78701, USA** (hereinafter referred to as **“Epsilon”, “Vendor”** or **“Service Provider”**).

This Addendum is entered into **pursuant to and in supplementation of** that certain **Data Processing Agreement**, reference number **DPA-EPS-202502-471**, identified by Client as **“contract_471”** (the **“Parent Agreement”** or **“DPA”**), dated **February 16, 2025**, between the Parties.

## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties previously entered into the Parent Agreement, reference number **DPA-EPS-202502-471 (contract_471)**, effective as of **February 16, 2025** (the **“Parent Effective Date”**), pursuant to which Vendor agreed to provide data processing, hosting, analytics, reporting, and related services to Client, as more particularly described in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement establishes the roles of the Parties as “controller/business” (Client) and “processor/service provider” (Vendor) under **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** and provides a legal framework for the Processing (as defined therein) of **Client Data** and **Personal Data**;

WHEREAS, the Parties now desire to expand and further define the Processing activities and related services to be performed by Vendor under the Parent Agreement, including additional data categories, processing purposes, and service components which were not fully described in **Section 2.2 (Description of Services)** and associated SOWs of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parties intend that **this Addendum adds processing activities dated February 16, 2025 (Ref: DPA-EPS-202502-471)**, and clarifies and supplements certain terms, while leaving in full force and effect all other provisions of the Parent Agreement not expressly modified herein;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement specifies a total contract value of **US$4,758,418** under **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)**, while the Parties now agree that the additional Processing activities and services described in this Addendum shall have an incremental contract value of **One Million Ninety-Six Thousand Four Hundred Ninety-Nine U.S. Dollars (US$1,096,499)** (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**), payable on a **Net 60** basis, which terms are intended to be consistent with and supplement **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement characterizes the overall risk associated with the Services as **Low**, but the Parties, in light of the expanded scope and increased data volumes contemplated herein, agree that the risk level applicable to the additional Processing activities under this Addendum shall be **Medium**, and therefore intend to adopt **balanced, mutually protective terms** consistent with such risk profile;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants, promises, agreements and other good and valuable consideration set forth herein and in the Parent Agreement, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This **Article II** supplements and expands upon **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Except as expressly modified herein, the description of Services, obligations, and performance standards in the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Addendum Activities.

2.1.2 Pursuant to **Section 2.1 (Appointment of Vendor as Processor)** and **Section 2.4 (Client Instructions)** of the Parent Agreement, Client hereby provides additional documented instructions for Vendor’s Processing of Personal Data as set forth in this Addendum. Vendor acknowledges that it shall Process Personal Data solely in accordance with these instructions and **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by **Article XI** of this Addendum.

2.2 **Overview of Addendum Activities.**  

Vendor shall perform the following additional Processing activities and services (collectively, the **“Addendum Activities”**) for Client:

  a. Expansion of data ingestion to include Additional Data Categories and Third‑Party Data Sources not previously configured under existing SOWs;  
  b. Implementation of Enhanced Analytics Services, including predictive modeling, customer journey analytics, and multi‑channel attribution;  
  c. Provision of Extended Hosting Services, including increased storage capacity, warm/cold data tiers, and extended backup retention;  
  d. Implementation of additional platform capabilities and role‑based access controls to accommodate new user groups within Client’s organization; and  
  e. Provision of specialized support and advisory services relating to data governance, data quality, and regulatory compliance with respect to the Addendum Activities.

2.3 **Additional Data Categories and Sources.**

2.3.1 **Additional Data Categories.** In addition to the Client Data already in scope under the Parent Agreement, Client authorizes Vendor to Process, on Client’s behalf, the following Additional Data Categories, which may constitute Personal Data depending on context and Applicable Data Protection Laws:

  a. **Enhanced Customer Profile Data:** enriched demographic attributes (e.g., age bands, household income ranges, occupation codes), lifestyle and interest indicators, and segmentation identifiers;  
  b. **Behavioral and Interaction Data:** website and mobile application interaction logs (e.g., page views, clickstream paths, session identifiers, time‑on‑page metrics), email open and click events, and campaign engagement metrics, subject to appropriate pseudonymization measures;  
  c. **Device and Technical Data:** device identifiers, browser types, operating system versions, IP‑derived geolocation at city/region level, and connection metadata;  
  d. **Customer Support Interaction Data:** non‑sensitive transcripts or summaries of customer support interactions, redacted of sensitive content by Client prior to transmission; and  
  e. **Marketing Attribution Data:** identifiers, codes, or tags associated with marketing campaigns, advertising channels, and conversion events.

2.3.2 **Third‑Party Data Sources.** Vendor is authorized to ingest and Process Client Data, including the Additional Data Categories described in **Section 2.3.1**, from the following categories of Third‑Party Data Sources, as designated by Client in the applicable SOW:

  a. Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platforms;  
  b. Marketing automation and email service providers;  
  c. Advertising platforms and demand‑side platforms;  
  d. Web analytics tools and tag management systems; and  
  e. Other enterprise systems expressly identified in writing by Client.

Client shall ensure it has appropriate rights and consents to permit Vendor to obtain and Process Client Data from these Third‑Party Data Sources as contemplated herein, consistent with **Section 7.3 (Client Warranties)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Enhanced Analytics Services.**

2.4.1 Building upon the **Data Processing and Analytics** described in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide Enhanced Analytics Services, which may include:

  a. **Predictive Modeling:** development and operation of models to generate churn propensity scores, engagement likelihood scores, and product recommendation indicators, using pseudonymized identifiers wherever practicable;  
  b. **Customer Journey Analytics:** analysis of multi‑touch interaction paths across channels and devices, to identify common journeys, drop‑off points, and conversion drivers;  
  c. **Segmentation and Cohort Analysis:** creation and maintenance of advanced customer segments and cohorts, including time‑based and behavior‑based segmentation;  
  d. **Attribution Modeling:** design and calculation of multi‑touch attribution models that allocate value to marketing touchpoints based on configured business rules; and  
  e. **Custom Analytical Dashboards:** configuration of dashboards and reports, as additional **Deliverables** under **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement, tailored to the new datasets and models implemented pursuant to this Addendum.

2.4.2 Vendor shall perform the Enhanced Analytics Services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement and in compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws, including implementing pseudonymization, minimization, and aggregation where appropriate.

2.5 **Extended Hosting Services and Capacity.**

2.5.1 Vendor shall extend its **Data Hosting and Storage** obligations under **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement to include:

  a. Increased storage capacity to accommodate projected data volume growth arising from the Additional Data Categories and Third‑Party Data Sources;  
  b. Implementation of **tiered storage** such that recent data (e.g., last 13 months) is maintained in a “warm” storage tier optimized for query performance, and older data is moved to a “cold” storage tier optimized for cost and regulatory retention requirements; and  
  c. Extended backup retention, with daily backups retained for a minimum of 35 days and weekly backups retained for a minimum of 12 months, consistent with Client’s data retention policies where communicated in writing.

2.5.2 Vendor shall ensure that Extended Hosting Services are subject to the same or stronger technical and organizational security measures as described in **Section 11.4 (Security Measures)** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.6 **Implementation Phase, Milestones, and Timelines.**

2.6.1 The Parties acknowledge that the Parent Agreement contemplates phases and timelines in **Section 2.6 (Timeline and Milestones)**. For the Addendum Activities, the Parties agree to the following **indicative Implementation Phase** milestones, which shall be further detailed in applicable SOW(s):

  a. **Phase A – Detailed Design (30–45 days from Addendum Effective Date):**  
     i. Data inventory and mapping for Additional Data Categories and Third‑Party Data Sources;  
     ii. Design of data models, schemas, and transformation logic;  
     iii. Design of initial predictive models and attribution frameworks.

  b. **Phase B – Build and Integration (45–60 days):**  
     i. Configuration of new ingestion pipelines and connectors;  
     ii. Implementation of data quality and validation rules tailored to Additional Data Categories;  
     iii. Development and initial training of predictive models and analytics logic.

  c. **Phase C – Testing and Validation (30 days):**  
     i. Functional and performance testing of ingestion, processing, and analytics;  
     ii. User acceptance testing by Client of key dashboards and outputs;  
     iii. Security and privacy validation consistent with **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement.

  d. **Phase D – Go‑Live and Stabilization (30 days):**  
     i. Production deployment of Addendum Activities;  
     ii. Monitoring, issue resolution, and performance tuning;  
     iii. Transition to Operational Phase and handover documentation.

2.6.2 Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet the foregoing milestones, subject to Client’s timely cooperation in providing necessary Client Data, access, and approvals, as contemplated by **Section 2.7 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement. Delays caused by Client shall extend deadlines on a day‑for‑day basis.

2.7 **Operational Phase and Performance Standards.**

2.7.1 Following successful completion of the Implementation Phase and Client’s acceptance of the relevant Deliverables, Vendor shall provide ongoing Operational Phase services, including:

  a. Regular execution and monitoring of ingestion, transformation, and analytics workflows;  
  b. Periodic re‑training of predictive models as reasonably necessary to maintain accuracy;  
  c. Ongoing maintenance and refinement of dashboards and reports; and  
  d. Operational support in accordance with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XII** of this Addendum.

2.7.2 Vendor shall comply with the **Performance Standards** set forth in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, including professional and workmanlike performance, adherence to industry standards, and compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws.

2.8 **Subprocessors.**  

To the extent Vendor engages new or additional Subprocessors to support the Addendum Activities, Vendor shall comply with **Section 11.9 (Subprocessors)** of the Parent Agreement. Client’s right to object to new Subprocessors on reasonable data protection grounds shall apply equally to Subprocessors used exclusively for the Addendum Activities.

2.9 **No Sale of Personal Data.**  

The prohibition on sale or sharing of Personal Data in **Section 2.9 (No Sale of Personal Data)** and **Section 11.10 (CCPA‑Specific Terms)** of the Parent Agreement applies fully to the Addendum Activities. Vendor shall not use any Personal Data Processed under this Addendum for its own commercial purposes or for any purpose other than providing the Services and Addendum Activities to Client.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

The total contract value for the Addendum Activities and related Deliverables under this Addendum is **One Million Ninety‑Six Thousand Four Hundred Ninety‑Nine U.S. Dollars (US$1,096,499)** (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**). This amount is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **US$4,758,418** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

Consistent with **Section 3.2 (Fee Structure)** of the Parent Agreement, the Addendum Contract Value shall be comprised of the following components:

  a. **Addendum Implementation Fees.** One‑time fees for design, configuration, integration, testing, and deployment of the Addendum Activities, including the Implementation Phase described in **Section 2.6**.  
  b. **Addendum Recurring Service Fees.** Monthly recurring fees for ongoing Extended Hosting Services, Enhanced Analytics Services, and Operational Phase activities.  
  c. **Addendum Professional Services Fees.** Time and materials or fixed‑fee charges for advisory, consulting, data governance, and custom development services requested by Client and described in applicable SOW(s).

The detailed allocation of the Addendum Contract Value among these components shall be set forth in an **Exhibit A (Addendum Fee Schedule)** to this Addendum or in one or more SOWs referencing this Addendum.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment Terms.**  

3.3.1 Unless otherwise specified in an SOW:

  a. **Addendum Implementation Fees** shall be invoiced as follows:  
     i. Forty percent (40%) upon the Addendum Effective Date;  
     ii. Thirty percent (30%) upon completion of Phase B – Build and Integration; and  
     iii. Thirty percent (30%) upon Client’s acceptance of Phase C – Testing and Validation.

  b. **Addendum Recurring Service Fees** shall be invoiced monthly in advance commencing upon the earlier of (i) the go‑live date of the Addendum Activities, or (ii) ninety (90) days after the Addendum Effective Date, provided that any delay attributable solely to Vendor shall postpone such commencement date commensurately.

  c. **Addendum Professional Services Fees** shall be invoiced monthly in arrears based on actual effort incurred and approved by Client, in alignment with **Section 3.3(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3.2 Payment terms for all undisputed amounts under this Addendum shall be **Net 60** from the date of invoice. This modifies the **Net 45** payment term set forth in **Section 3.4 (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to fees payable under this Addendum. For avoidance of doubt, invoices under the Parent Agreement (other than this Addendum) remain subject to the Net 45 term.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  

Any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Addendum shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Late Payments)** of the Parent Agreement, from the due date until paid in full. Vendor’s rights to provide written notice and suspend Services for non‑payment under **Section 3.6 (Suspension for Non‑Payment)** shall apply to the Addendum Activities; provided, however, Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to avoid suspending critical data processing necessary for Client’s legal or regulatory obligations where Client is diligently working to cure any payment default.

3.5 **Taxes.**  

The provisions of **Section 3.7 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement apply to all fees under this Addendum. The Addendum Contract Value is exclusive of taxes, and Client shall be responsible for applicable taxes as set forth in the Parent Agreement.

3.6 **Expenses.**  

Reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred by Vendor specifically in connection with the Addendum Activities shall be reimbursed by Client in accordance with **Section 3.8 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement. Any single expense exceeding **US$1,500** shall require Client’s prior written approval.

3.7 **Disputed Amounts.**  

Client’s right to dispute invoiced amounts as set forth in **Section 3.9 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to invoices under this Addendum. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any disputes within thirty (30) days, and any ultimately payable amounts shall be due within fifteen (15) days after resolution.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

The term of this Addendum (the **“Addendum Term”**) shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (June 03, 2025) and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**, shall continue in full force and effect until **May 30, 2030** (the **“Addendum Expiration Date”**).

4.2 **Renewal.**  

Upon mutual written agreement of the Parties, this Addendum may be renewed for one or more additional periods of one (1) year each. Any renewal shall be documented in a written amendment or renewal notice executed by both Parties and shall specify any revised pricing or other terms, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  

4.3.1 Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason upon **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Vendor.

4.3.2 Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client.

4.3.3 Termination for convenience of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall continue in accordance with its terms unless separately terminated.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  

Either Party may terminate this Addendum upon written notice to the other Party if:

  a. The other Party commits a material breach of this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice thereof (or ten (10) days in the case of non‑payment relating to this Addendum); or  
  b. The other Party becomes insolvent, is unable to pay its debts as they become due, or is subject to bankruptcy or similar proceedings, as set forth in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Termination for Data Protection Violations.**  

Client may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice to Vendor if Vendor (a) materially violates Applicable Data Protection Laws with respect to the Addendum Activities, or (b) fails to remediate a material deficiency in its data security measures related to the Addendum Activities within a reasonable time (not to exceed thirty (30) days) after written notice from Client, consistent with **Section 4.5 (Termination for Data Protection Violations)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  

4.6.1 In the event the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate effective as of the same date, without the need for separate notice, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to maintain specific portions of the Addendum in effect (e.g., solely for data return or deletion obligations).

4.6.2 Upon expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, Vendor shall cease Processing Personal Data on behalf of Client in connection with the Addendum Activities, except as necessary to perform any agreed transition services or as required by law, and shall return or delete Client Data in accordance with **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** and **Section 11.7 (Data Retention and Deletion)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 11.4** of this Addendum.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of Addendum (Only).**  

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum alone (with the Parent Agreement remaining in effect):

  a. Vendor shall cease the Addendum Activities but may continue providing other Services under the Parent Agreement and any other SOWs;  
  b. Client shall pay Vendor any undisputed fees earned and expenses incurred with respect to the Addendum Activities through the effective date of termination; and  
  c. Vendor shall, at Client’s option, return or securely delete Client Data specifically associated with the Addendum Activities, in accordance with **Section 11.7** of the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Survival.**  

Any provision of this Addendum that by its nature should survive termination or expiration shall survive, including but not limited to **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions)**.
