## ARTICLE II. SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum **adds licensed users/modules dated April 04, 2024 (Ref: SLA-ACM-202404-607)** by expanding the number of Authorized Users and the functional footprint of the Software licensed under the Parent Agreement, in accordance with Sections **2.2 (Software Description)** and **3.2.2 (License / Subscription Fees)** of the Parent Agreement.  

2.1.2 Except as expressly set forth herein, the **Scope of Services / Work** described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement remains unchanged and fully applicable to the Expanded Subscription, including implementation, configuration, integration, support, and Maintenance and Support Services.

2.2 **Additional Authorized Users.**

2.2.1 The Vendor hereby grants to the Client, subject to the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the right to designate **Additional Authorized Users** to access and use the Software.  

2.2.2 The number, role-based classifications, and organizational grouping of the Additional Authorized Users shall be as specified in **Schedule A (User and Module Allocation)** and/or any applicable Order Form.  

2.2.3 Additional Authorized Users shall be treated in all respects as “Authorized Users” under Section 1.20 of the Parent Agreement and must comply with all license restrictions in **Section 6.3 (License Restrictions)** of the Parent Agreement, including prohibitions on sharing accounts and unauthorized access.

2.3 **Additional Modules.**

2.3.1 The Client desires, and the Vendor agrees, to activate certain Additional Modules of the **Acme Enterprise Suite** beyond those included in the Baseline Subscription.  

2.3.2 The Additional Modules shall be listed and described in **Schedule A**, and shall fall within or extend the general categories described in Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement, which may include (by way of example):  
  a. Extended **Reporting and Analytics** modules,  
  b. Enhanced **Inventory and Supply Chain Management** functionality,  
  c. Advanced **Workflow and Role-Based Access Management** tools, and  
  d. Optional **Billing and Invoicing** sub-modules.  

2.3.3 The functionality, configuration options, and any module-specific limitations shall be further described in the applicable **Documentation** and, where appropriate, in the **Addendum Project Plan**.

2.4 **Implementation and Configuration for Additional Users/Modules.**

2.4.1 The Vendor shall provide implementation and configuration Services for the Additional Authorized Users and Additional Modules consistent with **Section 2.3 (Implementation and Configuration)** of the Parent Agreement. Such Services may include:  
  a. Extension of existing user roles and permissions structures to accommodate Additional Authorized Users;  
  b. Configuration of Additional Modules to align with the Client’s documented business workflows, including cross-module interactions with existing modules;  
  c. Integration of Additional Modules with existing Client systems already integrated under the Parent Agreement (e.g., existing ERP, HR, financial, and identity provider systems), and where required, incremental integrations specific to the Additional Modules;  
  d. Data mapping and migration of any incremental data sets necessary to use the Additional Modules, subject to the Client’s data responsibilities outlined in Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement; and  
  e. Support for user acceptance testing (“UAT”) specifically for the Additional Modules and expanded user base, including remediation of defects in accordance with agreed acceptance criteria.

2.4.2 The Parties shall, within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date, jointly develop an **Addendum Project Plan**, which shall include:  
  a. Key milestones for activation of Additional Modules and onboarding of Additional Authorized Users;  
  b. Dependencies on Client resources, including provision of Client Data, subject matter experts, and change management support;  
  c. A phased rollout schedule by business unit, region, or functional area; and  
  d. Acceptance criteria and test cases for module-specific functionalities.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**

2.5.1 The Vendor shall perform all Services under this Addendum in a professional and workmanlike manner and in accordance with generally accepted industry standards, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement.  

2.5.2 The Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that:  
  a. The Additional Modules perform materially in accordance with the applicable **Documentation**;  
  b. The addition of new users and modules does not materially degrade performance for existing Authorized Users beyond what is permitted under the Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement; and  
  c. Capacity planning, infrastructure scaling, and configuration adjustments are implemented to maintain or improve the Service Levels for the Expanded Subscription.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities for Expanded Subscription.**

2.6.1 In addition to the responsibilities set forth in **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement, the Client shall:  
  a. Ensure that Additional Authorized Users receive appropriate training and are made aware of the license restrictions under **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)** of the Parent Agreement;  
  b. Provide timely and accurate information regarding the number, identity, and roles of Additional Authorized Users;  
  c. Confirm that all Additional Modules selected under this Addendum align with the Client’s internal policies and regulatory obligations; and  
  d. Designate one or more internal business owners to oversee adoption and utilization of the Additional Modules.

2.7 **Deliverables and Acceptance.**

2.7.1 Any Deliverables specific to the implementation of the Additional Modules or onboarding of Additional Authorized Users, including configuration documentation, adjusted workflows, and integration components, shall be subject to the Deliverables and acceptance procedures in **Section 2.7 (Deliverables and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement.  

2.7.2 Unless otherwise specified in a mutually agreed Addendum Project Plan or SOW:  
  a. The Client shall have a ten (10) Business Day **Acceptance Period** following delivery of each material Deliverable;  
  b. Non-conformities shall be notified in writing and the Vendor shall correct such non-conformities within a reasonable period; and  
  c. Deliverables shall be deemed accepted upon the earliest of (i) written acceptance, (ii) lapse of the Acceptance Period without written notification of non-conformity, or (iii) use of the Deliverable in production.

2.8 **Maintenance and Support for Expanded Subscription.**

2.8.1 The Additional Modules and Additional Authorized Users shall be fully covered by the **Maintenance and Support Services** described in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement, including access to helpdesk support and Updates.  

2.8.2 The Parties acknowledge that the Expanded Subscription may give rise to increased incident volume. The Vendor shall ensure that staffing and support capacity are adjusted accordingly so that the Service Levels in Section 12.3 of the Parent Agreement are met.

2.9 **Exclusions.**

2.9.1 The exclusions described in **Section 2.9 (Exclusions)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to the Services under this Addendum.  

2.9.2 For the avoidance of doubt, any custom development or extensive business process re-engineering beyond agreed configuration of Additional Modules shall require a separate SOW and may be subject to additional Fees.

## ARTICLE IV. TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  

4.1.1 This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date (March 06, 2025)** and shall continue in full force and effect until **February 04, 2028** (the “**Addendum Expiration Date**”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the Parent Agreement.  

4.1.2 For clarity, the Addendum Term may extend beyond the **Expiration Date (July 31, 2027)** of the Parent Agreement only if the Parties have duly renewed or extended the Parent Agreement pursuant to **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** thereof, or otherwise explicitly agree in writing.

4.2 **Renewal.**

4.2.1 Any renewal of this Addendum shall be negotiated in conjunction with renewal of the Parent Agreement. Unless and until such renewal is documented in writing, this Addendum shall expire on the Addendum Expiration Date.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**

4.3.1 After the first twelve (12) months of the Addendum Term, either Party may terminate this Addendum for convenience by providing at least **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to the other Party.  

4.3.2 Termination for convenience of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in full force and effect unless separately terminated in accordance with **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**

4.4.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause under the same conditions and procedures as set forth in **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., material breach not cured within thirty (30) days, insolvency).  

4.4.2 A material, uncured breach of this Addendum may constitute a material breach of the Parent Agreement where such breach affects core obligations or performance under the Parent Agreement; in such case, the non-breaching Party may seek remedies under both this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**

4.5.1 This Addendum is expressly dependent upon the continued validity and effectiveness of the Parent Agreement.  

4.5.2 In the event the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires (without renewal) for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date of termination or expiration of the Parent Agreement, without the need for further action by either Party.  

4.5.3 Upon such termination or expiration, all rights and licenses granted under this Addendum shall cease in accordance with **Section 4.6.1** of the Parent Agreement, and the provisions of **Section 4.6.2 (Transition Assistance)** shall apply to the Expanded Subscription.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Addendum Only.**

4.6.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum (but not the Parent Agreement), the Client’s rights to use the Additional Modules and Additional Authorized Users shall cease, but the Baseline Subscription under the Parent Agreement shall continue unaffected.  

4.6.2 The Client shall be responsible for payment of all Fees accrued or committed under this Addendum up to the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable charges documented in an SOW.

4.7 **Survival.**

4.7.1 Any provisions of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration, including without limitation **Articles V–IX, XI, XIV–XVI**, and all payment obligations accrued prior to termination, shall survive and continue in full force and effect, consistent with **Section 4.7 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement.
