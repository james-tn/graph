## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Reference to Parent Agreement Scope.** This Amendment is executed pursuant to and supplements **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement. Under Section 2.2 of the Parent Agreement, Vanguard is responsible for implementing an enterprise technology solution including server hardware, storage devices, networking equipment, ERP, CRM, data analytics tools, custom interfaces, and ongoing support. This Amendment expands both the quantities of certain infrastructure components and the extent of professional services required to support Contoso’s increased business volumes and additional data centre capacity.

2.2 **Purpose of Amendment.** The purpose of this Amendment is to:

2.2.1 Increase the number of server hardware units, storage devices and networking equipment provided under Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement to support higher transaction volumes and new regional rollouts;

2.2.2 Extend the configuration and integration of ERP, CRM and analytics components (as referred to in Sections 2.2.2 and 2.2.3 of the Parent Agreement) to additional business units within Contoso;

2.2.3 Enhance data analytics capabilities, including additional reporting, dashboards, and load-optimised data models, as part of the Deliverables described in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement;

2.2.4 Extend support and maintenance services under Section 2.6 and Article XII of the Parent Agreement to cover the Amended Goods and Amended Deliverables; and

2.2.5 Provide additional project management, testing, and transition services to maintain continuity and compliance with the Service Levels agreed under Article XII of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Description of Amended Goods.** Subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment, Vanguard shall supply to Contoso the following additional hardware and related infrastructure components (Amended Goods). The detailed bill of materials, including manufacturer, model, and specifications, shall be set forth in **Schedule A (Amended Hardware Schedule)** to this Amendment. By way of summary:

2.3.1 **Server Hardware:** An additional twenty (20) enterprise-grade rack servers with high-availability configurations, to be installed in Contoso’s primary and secondary data centres as contemplated in Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement.

2.3.2 **Storage Devices:** An additional five (5) storage arrays with associated controllers and SSD expansions to support increased data volume and analytical workloads.

2.3.3 **Networking Equipment:** An additional set of core and access switches, including redundancy, to accommodate expanded network segments for new Contoso business units.

2.3.4 **Ancillary Equipment:** Associated racks, power distribution units, cables and accessories necessary for the proper installation and operation of the above items.

All Amended Goods shall be delivered, installed and configured in accordance with the performance standards set out in **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement and Section 2.7 of this Amendment.

2.4 **Description of Amended Services.** Vanguard shall perform the following Amended Services:

2.4.1 **Design and Planning:** Update the existing Solution Design Documentation referenced in Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement to reflect the Incremental Scope, including revised architecture diagrams, capacity and performance planning, and updated security frameworks;

2.4.2 **Implementation and Configuration:** Install and configure the Amended Goods; extend ERP, CRM, and analytics configurations to new business units; configure additional interfaces and scripting required to connect the expanded environment to Contoso’s systems;

2.4.3 **Data and Analytics Enhancements:** Develop and implement new analytics data models, dashboards, and reports to support Contoso’s extended operations, all of which shall form part of the Amended Deliverables under Section 2.3.4 of the Parent Agreement;

2.4.4 **Testing and Acceptance Support:** Prepare and execute additional test plans and acceptance test cases in alignment with Sections 2.7.1–2.7.4 of the Parent Agreement, including load and performance testing for the augmented environment;

2.4.5 **Support and Maintenance:** Provide support and maintenance services in respect of the Amended Goods and Amended Deliverables in accordance with Section 2.6 and Article XII of the Parent Agreement, as enhanced by **Article XII** of this Amendment;

2.4.6 **Project Management and Governance:** Provide incremental project management and governance activities, including additional status reporting, risk management, and change coordination consistent with Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.** The Parties agree to the following high-level milestones for the Incremental Scope (detailed timelines and acceptance criteria shall be set out in **Schedule B (Milestones and Acceptance Criteria)**):

2.5.1 **Updated Design Documentation Completed:** Within forty-five (45) days of the Amendment Effective Date;

2.5.2 **Delivery of Amended Goods to Sites:** Within ninety (90) days of the Amendment Effective Date, subject to lead times of equipment vendors and any Force Majeure Events (as defined in Article XV of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment);

2.5.3 **Installation and Configuration Complete:** Within one hundred twenty (120) days of the Amendment Effective Date;

2.5.4 **System Integration and Testing Complete:** Within one hundred fifty (150) days of the Amendment Effective Date;

2.5.5 **Final Acceptance of Amended Deliverables:** Within thirty (30) days following completion of system integration and testing, subject to the process in Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Performance Standards.** Vanguard shall perform all Amended Services:

2.6.1 With the same level of due care, skill and diligence prescribed in **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent Agreement;

2.6.2 Using appropriately qualified, experienced and trained personnel, consistent with **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent Agreement;

2.6.3 In compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including Data Protection Laws as defined in Section 1.1.7 of the Parent Agreement and Article XI of that Agreement and this Amendment;

2.6.4 In a manner that ensures that the extended environment continues to meet the Service Levels set forth in Article XII of the Parent Agreement and Article XII of this Amendment.

2.7 **Acceptance of Amended Deliverables.** The acceptance process of Sections 2.7.1–2.7.4 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to the Amended Deliverables, except that the Parties agree that:

2.7.1 The **Acceptance Period** for each major Amended Deliverable, as set forth in Schedule B, shall be thirty (30) calendar days (as in Section 2.7.2 of the Parent Agreement) or such other period as the Parties may specify in writing in Schedule B;

2.7.2 Contoso shall promptly notify Vanguard of any non-conformities with the specifications and acceptance criteria defined in Schedule B; and

2.7.3 Vanguard shall, at no additional cost, correct such non-conformities in accordance with Section 2.7.3 of the Parent Agreement.

2.8 **Relationship to Existing Services.** For the avoidance of doubt:

2.8.1 The Amended Services and Amended Goods do not replace Existing Services, Goods or Deliverables under the Parent Agreement unless expressly specified in Schedule C (Replaced or Superseded Items);

2.8.2 In case of conflict between this Article II and Article II of the Parent Agreement with respect to the Incremental Scope, the provisions of this Amendment shall prevail, but only in respect of the subject matter of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value Under This Amendment.**

3.1.1 The aggregate value of this Amendment, in respect of the Incremental Scope, shall be **US$329,090** (United States Dollars Three Hundred Twenty-Nine Thousand Ninety) (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**), exclusive of applicable VAT or similar taxes.

3.1.2 The Parties agree that, for the purposes of **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the Parent Agreement, the aggregate value limitation of **US$4,894,932** is hereby increased by the Amendment Contract Value, such that the new **Revised Contract Value** under the Parent Agreement, as amended by this Amendment, shall not exceed **US$5,224,022**, unless further modified by a subsequent written amendment or Change Order executed in accordance with Article XIII of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Components.** The Amendment Contract Value shall comprise:

3.2.1 **Hardware Fees (Fixed):** A fixed price for the Amended Goods (hardware and ancillary equipment) as set out in Schedule A;

3.2.2 **Professional Services Fees (Time and Materials / Fixed):** A combination of fixed-fee and time-and-materials charges for design, implementation, testing, and transition services, as set forth in Schedule B;

3.2.3 **Recurring Support Fees:** Additional recurring support and maintenance fees attributable to the Incremental Scope for the period from the Amendment Effective Date through the Expiration Date specified in this Amendment.

3.3 **Payment Terms – Modification of Section 3.3 of Parent Agreement.** Notwithstanding Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement, which provides for payment on a **Net 60** basis, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to invoices issued under this Amendment:

3.3.1 Vanguard shall invoice Contoso in accordance with the milestone schedule and billing frequencies set forth in Schedule B;

3.3.2 **All undisputed invoices issued under this Amendment shall be payable by Contoso within forty-five (45) days from the date of receipt of a correct and properly documented invoice (“Net 45”).** This modifies and supersedes Section 3.3.2 of the Parent Agreement solely in relation to the Amended Fees and Incremental Scope.

3.4 **Milestone-Based Payments.** Unless otherwise provided in Schedule B, the following payment milestones shall apply:

3.4.1 Twenty percent (20%) of the Hardware Fees upon delivery of the Amended Goods to Contoso’s designated facility (as evidenced by delivery documentation);

3.4.2 Thirty percent (30%) of the Hardware Fees upon completion of installation and initial configuration;

3.4.3 Thirty percent (30%) of the Professional Services Fees upon completion of system integration and internal testing;

3.4.4 Twenty percent (20%) of the Professional Services Fees upon final acceptance of the Amended Deliverables in accordance with Section 2.7 of this Amendment;

3.4.5 Recurring support fees payable quarterly in advance commencing on the first day of the calendar quarter following the Amendment Effective Date.

3.5 **Late Payment.** Section 3.4 (Late Payment) of the Parent Agreement shall apply to late payments under this Amendment, except that Vanguard is expressly entitled, in its sole discretion and without prejudice to any other remedies, to suspend the Amended Services and support specific to the Incremental Scope if any undisputed amount under this Amendment remains unpaid for more than thirty (30) days following written notice of non-payment, even if other amounts under the Parent Agreement are not in arrears.

3.6 **Expenses.** Section 3.6 (Expenses) of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. Pre-approved, reasonable out-of-pocket expenses directly attributable to the Incremental Scope shall be invoiced at cost and shall be additional to the Amendment Contract Value unless expressly stated otherwise in Schedule B.

3.7 **Taxes.** Section 3.5 (Taxes) of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Amended Fees and charges under this Amendment.

3.8 **No Set-Off; Cross-Default.** Section 3.8 (No Set-Off) of the Parent Agreement remains in full force and effect. Additionally, any material payment default by Contoso under this Amendment shall be deemed a payment default under the Parent Agreement, and Vanguard may exercise its rights under Article IV (Term and Termination) of the Parent Agreement and Article IV of this Amendment accordingly.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term.** This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall continue in force until **August 07, 2027** (the **“Expiration Date of the Amendment”**), unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Article IV.

4.2 **Relation to Parent Agreement Term.** The Parent Agreement currently has a Term expiring on **February 08, 2027**, as set forth in Section 4.1 thereof. The Parties acknowledge and agree that:

4.2.1 The Amended Services which, by their nature, must continue beyond February 08, 2027 to the Expiration Date of the Amendment may only do so if the Parent Agreement is renewed or extended under Section 4.2 (Renewal) of the Parent Agreement or if the Parties execute a successor master agreement expressly incorporating this Amendment;

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is not renewed, extended, or replaced by a successor agreement on or before February 08, 2027, then, unless otherwise agreed in writing, this Amendment shall automatically terminate on the same date as the Parent Agreement, notwithstanding the Expiration Date of the Amendment set out above. In such event, the provisions of Section 4.6 of the Parent Agreement and Section 4.6 of this Amendment shall apply.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.** The provisions of Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement are hereby modified solely with respect to this Amendment as follows:

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason, by providing the other Party not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**;

4.3.2 Termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in effect according to its terms unless separately terminated pursuant to Article IV of the Parent Agreement;

4.3.3 Upon termination for convenience by Contoso, Contoso shall pay Vanguard: (a) all Fees for Goods, Services and Deliverables satisfactorily provided under this Amendment up to the effective date of termination; (b) all reasonable and demonstrable non-cancellable third-party costs incurred by Vanguard in connection with the Incremental Scope, subject to Vanguard’s duty to mitigate; and (c) any contractually committed charges in respect of hardware or third-party services specifically procured for the Incremental Scope.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.** Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause) of the Parent Agreement shall apply mutatis mutandis to this Amendment. In addition:

4.4.1 A material breach of this Amendment by a Party shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement for purposes of Section 4.4.1 thereof, entitling the non-breaching Party to terminate both this Amendment and, if so elected by the non-breaching Party, the Parent Agreement;

4.4.2 Non-payment of undisputed amounts under this Amendment beyond the cure period set out in written notice shall constitute a material breach.

4.5 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.** If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires for any reason:

4.5.1 This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the effective date of such termination or expiry, unless otherwise expressly agreed in a written instrument signed by both Parties;  

4.5.2 The obligations of Contoso to pay for Goods, Services and Deliverables properly provided under this Amendment prior to such termination or expiry shall remain in effect as set out in Sections 4.6.1(a) of the Parent Agreement and 4.6.1 of this Amendment.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.** Upon expiry or termination of this Amendment for any reason:

4.6.1 Contoso shall promptly pay Vanguard all undisputed Fees and expenses due and payable under this Amendment for Goods, Services and Deliverables supplied up to the effective date of termination, including any termination-related charges expressly provided in this Amendment;

4.6.2 Each Party shall return or destroy (at the disclosing Party’s option) Confidential Information relating specifically to the Incremental Scope, subject to the retention rights set forth in Section 5.5 of the Parent Agreement;

4.6.3 Vanguard shall provide reasonable Transition Activities requested by Contoso to maintain service continuity, at Vanguard’s then-current time and materials rates, unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.7 **Survival.** Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XVI, and any provisions which by their nature are intended to survive, shall survive expiry or termination of this Amendment, together with corresponding provisions of the Parent Agreement as applicable.
