## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
(a) This Addendum is executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement **PUR-PHO-202402-266**, in accordance with **Section 16.2 (Amendments)** thereof.  
(b) Except as expressly modified by this Addendum, the provisions of the Parent Agreement, including **Article II (Scope of Products and Services)**, **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, and **Article XIII (Change Management)**, shall govern the ordering, delivery, and performance of Additional Products and Additional Services.  
(c) For the avoidance of doubt, Additional Products are “Products,” and Additional Services are “Services,” within the meaning of **Sections 1.12 and 1.13** of the Parent Agreement, and the remedies, warranties, indemnities, and other protections applicable to Products and Services under the Parent Agreement apply equally to Additional Products and Additional Services unless expressly stated otherwise herein.

2.2 **Description of Additional Products.**  
Phoenix shall manufacture, supply, and deliver to Contoso the Additional Products described in **Schedule 1 (Additional Products and Specifications)**, which may include, without limitation:

  (a) **High-Density Power Distribution Units (HDPDUs).**  
  Rack-mounted, high-density power distribution units with integrated monitoring capabilities, designed to support Contoso’s next-generation data center configurations, including:  
  - Intelligent three-phase PDUs with per-outlet metering, remote monitoring interfaces, and compatibility with Contoso’s existing management systems;  
  - Configurations meeting specified amperage and voltage ranges and environmental requirements; and  
  - Built-in redundancy features and fail-safe mechanisms as defined in the Specifications (Addendum).

  (b) **Modular Cooling Interface Assemblies (MCIAs).**  
  Custom mechanical assemblies enabling the integration of Contoso’s liquid-cooling loops with Phoenix-supplied rack chassis, including:  
  - Quick-connect manifolds;  
  - Leak-detection sensors and control wiring;  
  - Mounting brackets and structural support hardware; and  
  - Compliance with specified pressure, flow, and safety criteria.

  (c) **Retrofit Upgrade Kits.**  
  Kits enabling upgrades to existing Phoenix-supplied Products under the Parent Agreement, including:  
  - Replacement power modules;  
  - Enhanced airflow components and cable management subassemblies; and  
  - Installation instructions and labeling conforming to Contoso’s internal standards.

  (d) **Site-Specific Customization Packages.**  
  Variants of the above configured for specific Contoso facilities, including localized power connectors, region-specific compliance labeling, and custom mechanical modifications as mutually agreed in the Specifications (Addendum).

All Additional Products shall conform in all material respects to the Specifications (Addendum), applicable industry standards, and the performance criteria agreed in writing by the Parties, as contemplated in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise stated in an Addendum Order, all Additional Products shall be new and not refurbished, consistent with **Section 2.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Description of Additional Services.**  
Phoenix shall perform the Additional Services described in **Schedule 2 (Additional Services and Service Descriptions)**, which may include, without limitation:

  (a) **Advanced Engineering and Co-Design Services.**  
  Expanded engineering support beyond **Section 2.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
  - Thermal and power modeling of new and existing rack configurations;  
  - Participation in Contoso’s design reviews and change-control boards;  
  - Creation of reference designs and integration guides specific to the Additional Products.

  (b) **Managed Integration Services.**  
  Ongoing remote and on-site support to coordinate installation, commissioning, and performance optimization of Additional Products, supplementing **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
  - Coordination of installation schedules across multiple Contoso sites;  
  - Verification of installation quality and adherence to Specifications (Addendum);  
  - Continuous monitoring of performance metrics and periodic optimization recommendations.

  (c) **Extended Maintenance and Lifecycle Services.**  
  Enhanced maintenance and support, supplementing **Section 2.4(d)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
  - Proactive replacement of components based on reliability analytics;  
  - Root-cause analysis for recurring issues and reporting to Contoso;  
  - Support for technology refreshes and phased migration plans.

  (d) **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  
  Additional training beyond **Section 2.4(e)** of the Parent Agreement, including:  
  - Development of customized training curricula and technical documentation specific to the Additional Products;  
  - Instructor-led sessions (virtual and on-site) for Contoso’s engineering, operations, and procurement teams;  
  - Train-the-trainer programs to enable Contoso to internalize ongoing training delivery.

  (e) **Program Management and Reporting.**  
  Centralized program management structure for this Addendum, including:  
  - A designated Phoenix program manager serving as a single point of contact;  
  - Monthly status reports covering delivery metrics, SLA performance (as per Article XII), and risk/issue logs;  
  - Participation in quarterly business reviews with Contoso.

2.4 **Performance Standards and Integration with Existing Services.**  
(a) Phoenix shall perform the Additional Services in a professional, diligent, and workmanlike manner consistent with **Section 2.4** and **Section 7.3 (Services Warranty)** of the Parent Agreement and with the Addendum Service Levels.  
(b) Phoenix shall ensure that all Additional Products seamlessly integrate with existing Products and Work Product supplied under the Parent Agreement, adhering to the quality management system described in **Section 2.6(b) (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, including documented procedures for non-conforming Additional Products.  
(c) Persistent failure to meet performance standards applicable to the Additional Services, including Addendum Service Levels, shall constitute a material breach for purposes of **Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article IV** of this Addendum.

2.5 **Deliverables, Milestones, and Pilot Deployment.**  
(a) Each Addendum Order shall define specific Deliverables, Milestones, timelines, and acceptance criteria for the Additional Products and Additional Services, consistent with **Section 2.5 (Deliverables and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) For each new class of Additional Products, the Parties may conduct a Pilot Deployment at one or more designated Contoso sites to validate technical and operational performance. The scope of any Pilot Deployment shall be set forth in the relevant Addendum Order, including:  
   - Pilot site(s) and quantities;  
   - Success criteria, including technical performance, installation time, and interoperability;  
   - Data collection and reporting requirements.  
(c) Unless otherwise agreed, Pilot Deployment Deliverables shall be deemed accepted ten (10) Business Days after delivery and completion of agreed tests if Contoso has not notified Phoenix of material non-conformity, consistent with **Section 2.5(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(d) Upon successful completion of the Pilot Deployment (as documented in a mutually agreed acceptance report), the Parties shall transition to the Ramp-Up Period, during which volumes and services levels may increase as specified in the applicable Addendum Order.

2.6 **Ordering and Forecasting.**  
(a) Addendum Orders for Additional Products and Additional Services shall be placed and accepted in accordance with **Section 2.3 (Ordering Procedure)** of the Parent Agreement, provided that each such Order shall explicitly reference this Addendum by its contract reference number **ADD-PHO-202402-299**.  
(b) Contoso shall provide non-binding rolling forecasts of expected volumes and service consumption for the Additional Products and Additional Services on a quarterly basis. Phoenix shall use such forecasts for capacity planning and shall promptly notify Contoso if anticipated volumes may materially impact lead times or pricing.  
(c) Any binding minimum volume commitments for Additional Products or Additional Services shall be expressly set forth in an Addendum Order; absent such express language, forecasts shall be non-binding and indicative only.

2.7 **Inspection, Testing, and Non-Conformance.**  
(a) The rights of inspection and testing in **Section 2.7** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to facilities, processes, and Deliverables related to Additional Products and Additional Services.  
(b) Non-conforming Additional Products may be rejected and returned to Phoenix, at Phoenix’s expense, for repair, replacement, or credit, consistent with **Article VII (Representations and Warranties)** and the remedies under the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Phoenix shall promptly investigate and address any systemic quality issues affecting Additional Products, implementing corrective and preventive actions in accordance with its quality management system and reporting progress to Contoso.

2.8 **Subcontractors.**  
Phoenix may use subcontractors for manufacturing Additional Products or performing Additional Services, subject to **Section 2.8 (Subcontractors)** of the Parent Agreement. Phoenix shall remain fully responsible for all subcontractor performance and shall ensure that subcontractor agreements cover confidentiality, data protection, and IP obligations consistent with this Addendum and the Parent Agreement.

2.9 **Non-Exclusivity.**  
This Addendum does not alter the non-exclusivity provisions in **Section 2.9** of the Parent Agreement. Contoso remains free to source comparable products and services from other suppliers.

## ARTICLE III  
FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the anticipated aggregate value of Additional Products and Additional Services ordered under this Addendum is **US$4,471,605** (the **“Addendum Value”**), based on projected volumes and rate schedules set forth in **Schedule 3 (Addendum Pricing Schedule)**. This Addendum Value is an estimate only and does not constitute a purchase commitment by Contoso except to the extent explicitly stated in specific Addendum Orders.

3.2 **Pricing and Rate Schedules.**  
(a) The unit prices, rate cards, and discount structures applicable to Additional Products and Additional Services are set forth in **Schedule 3 (Addendum Pricing Schedule)**, which supplements **Exhibit A (Pricing Schedule)** to the Parent Agreement.  
(b) In the event of any inconsistency between Schedule 3, an Addendum Order, and Exhibit A, the order of precedence for matters relating to Additional Products and Additional Services shall be: (i) the specific Addendum Order; (ii) this Addendum (including Schedule 3); and (iii) Exhibit A to the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Unless otherwise expressly agreed in a Change Order under **Article XIII**, pricing for Additional Products and Additional Services shall remain firm during the Addendum Term.

3.3 **Fee Structures and Milestones.**  
(a) **Additional Products.** Fees for Additional Products shall generally be based on unit pricing multiplied by quantities ordered in Addendum Orders. Volume-based discounts, if any, shall be specified in Schedule 3.  
(b) **Additional Services.** Fees for Additional Services may be structured as:  
   - Time-and-materials at agreed hourly or daily rates;  
   - Fixed fees tied to defined Milestones or Deliverables; and/or  
   - Recurring charges (e.g., monthly managed services fees) during the Ramp-Up Period and steady-state operations.  
(c) Each Addendum Order shall detail the applicable fee structure, Milestones, billing triggers, and any holdback or acceptance-related payment conditions.

3.4 **Payment Terms (Modification of Parent Agreement).**  
(a) **Modification of Section 3.4(a).** Notwithstanding **Section 3.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement (Net 60), the Parties agree that, solely with respect to Additional Products and Additional Services ordered under this Addendum and any Addendum Orders, Contoso shall pay undisputed amounts within **forty-five (45) days** from the date of receipt of a correct and complete invoice (**“Net 45 Terms”**).  
(b) All other provisions of **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement, including invoicing requirements under **Section 3.3**, apply to invoices issued under this Addendum.

3.5 **Invoicing under this Addendum.**  
(a) For Additional Products, Phoenix shall invoice Contoso upon shipment in accordance with **Section 3.3(a)(i)** of the Parent Agreement, unless an Addendum Order specifies different billing milestones (e.g., partial billing upon completion of factory acceptance tests).  
(b) For Additional Services, Phoenix shall invoice Contoso upon completion and acceptance of Milestones or monthly in arrears for time-and-materials work, consistent with **Section 3.3(a)(ii)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Each invoice related to this Addendum shall clearly reference this Addendum by its contract reference number **ADD-PHO-202402-299** and the relevant Addendum Order number, and shall itemize:  
   - Description of Additional Products or Additional Services;  
   - Quantities, unit prices, and applicable discounts;  
   - Applicable Taxes; and  
   - Any pre-approved reimbursable expenses.

3.6 **Late Payment; Disputes.**  
(a) The late payment provisions in **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to undisputed amounts due under this Addendum, provided that the cure period and interest accrual shall be calculated based on the Net 45 Terms.  
(b) Contoso may withhold payment of any amounts disputed in good faith in accordance with **Section 3.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve invoicing disputes promptly. Any amounts determined to be payable after resolution shall be paid within thirty (30) days of such resolution.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
The provisions of **Section 3.6 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement apply to all invoicing under this Addendum. Phoenix shall itemize applicable Taxes on invoices for Additional Products and Additional Services.

3.8 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
(a) Out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Phoenix in performing Additional Services (such as travel and lodging) shall be reimbursable only if:  
   (i) Such expenses are pre-approved in writing by Contoso in the applicable Addendum Order or otherwise; and  
   (ii) Phoenix provides reasonable supporting documentation with the relevant invoice.  
(b) Reimbursable expenses shall be charged at actual cost and in accordance with Contoso’s then-current travel and expense policy, as described in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement.

3.9 **Set-Off.**  
Contoso’s rights of set-off and deduction under **Section 3.8 (Set-Off)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to amounts payable by Contoso in connection with this Addendum and any Addendum Orders.

## ARTICLE IV  
TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.**  
This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement, shall continue in full force and effect until **March 23, 2026** (the **“Addendum Term”**).

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.**  
(a) The Term of the Parent Agreement, as defined in **Section 4.1 (Term)** thereof, extends until February 17, 2028, unless earlier terminated pursuant to **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) If the Parent Agreement expires or is terminated in its entirety before the Addendum Term end date, this Addendum shall automatically terminate effective as of the effective date of expiration or termination of the Parent Agreement, subject to **Section 4.6 (Effect of Termination)** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.6** of this Addendum.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
(a) Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part (including any specific Addendum Order), for any reason or no reason, upon not less than **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Phoenix, consistent with **Section 4.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(b) Phoenix may terminate this Addendum for convenience only upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, and only with respect to future Addendum Orders not yet accepted by Phoenix, consistent with **Section 4.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement, unless the Parties expressly agree otherwise in writing.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Addendum (or any affected Addendum Order) immediately upon written notice if:  
(a) The other Party materially breaches this Addendum (including any Addendum Service Levels) and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or ten (10) days in the case of non-payment) after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  
(b) Any of the events listed in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement (insolvency, bankruptcy, etc.) occur with respect to the other Party.

4.5 **Termination of Individual Addendum Orders.**  
Consistent with **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may terminate any individual Addendum Order for convenience or cause, without terminating this Addendum or the Parent Agreement as a whole. In such case, the rights and obligations of the Parties with respect to other Addendum Orders and the Parent Agreement shall not be affected.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of this Addendum.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum, in whole or in part:

  (a) Phoenix shall promptly cease work on the terminated portions, use commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate costs, and cooperate with Contoso to wind down any ongoing Pilot Deployments or Ramp-Up Period activities.  
  (b) Contoso shall pay Phoenix for:  
     (i) Additional Products delivered and accepted;  
     (ii) Additional Services performed and accepted; and  
     (iii) Reasonable, documented, non-cancellable commitments pre-approved in writing by Contoso, in each case up to the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.6(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  (c) Phoenix shall promptly return or, at Contoso’s written direction, destroy Contoso’s Confidential Information and Client Data related to this Addendum, in accordance with **Section 11.7** of the Parent Agreement and **Article V** of this Addendum.  
  (d) At Contoso’s written request, Phoenix shall provide reasonable Transition-Out Assistance for up to ninety (90) days following termination (or longer if mutually agreed), on a time-and-materials basis at the rates set forth in Schedule 3 or an applicable Addendum Order.  
  (e) The expiration or termination of this Addendum shall not relieve either Party of any accrued payment obligations or liabilities arising prior to the effective date of termination.  
  (f) The survival provisions in **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement shall apply, and the following provisions of this Addendum shall survive: **Sections 3.5–3.9, 4.6, 7.4–7.6, 8.1–8.4, 9.1–9.4, 10.3, 11.1–11.8, 12.3(c), 14.1–14.4, 15.1–15.4**, and **Article XVI**.
