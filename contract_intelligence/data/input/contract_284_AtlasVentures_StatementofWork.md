## ARTICLE II  
SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This SOW is executed **under and subject to** the terms and conditions of the Consulting Agreement (Ref. **CSA-ATL-202406-248 / contract_248**), including, without limitation, **Article II (Scope of Services)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, **Section 2.3 (Statements of Work)**, and **Section 2.5 (Deliverables; Acceptance)**.  

2.1.2 In accordance with Section 2.3.1 of the Parent Agreement, this SOW identifies:  
(a) the specific Services and Deliverables;  
(b) the applicable timelines, milestones, and Acceptance Criteria;  
(c) the Fees and payment schedule associated with this SOW; and  
(d) special terms or Service Levels that supplement the Parent Agreement.

2.1.3 In the event of a conflict between the terms of this SOW and the Parent Agreement, the provisions of the Parent Agreement shall prevail **unless** this SOW expressly states that it is intended to modify a specific provision of the Parent Agreement, in which case such modification shall be effective solely for this SOW and only to the extent explicitly stated.

2.2 **Overview of SOW Services.**  

The Vendor shall, in accordance with Section 2.4 (Performance Standards) of the Parent Agreement, perform the following high‑level SOW Services for the Client:

  a. **Strategic Transformation Roadmap**  
     - Conduct strategic business consulting aligned with Section 2.2.1 of the Parent Agreement, including detailed market analysis, operating model assessment, and development of a multi‑year growth and digital transformation roadmap.  
     - Deliver a comprehensive “Enterprise Transformation Roadmap” Deliverable, including prioritized initiatives, investment estimates, and risk/benefit analysis.

  b. **Technology Architecture and Cloud Migration Advisory**  
     - Perform technology consulting as contemplated in Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement, focusing on systems architecture, cloud migration strategy, and integration patterns for the Client’s core enterprise applications.  
     - Produce a “Target Technology Architecture Blueprint” and “Cloud Migration Advisory Report.”

  c. **Business Process Review and Optimization**  
     - Undertake an in‑depth review of selected end‑to‑end business processes (including order‑to‑cash, procure‑to‑pay, and record‑to‑report) in line with Section 2.2.3 of the Parent Agreement.  
     - Develop improved process maps, control frameworks, and standard operating procedures (“**SOPs**”) as SOW Deliverables.

  d. **Implementation Support and Program Management Office (PMO)**  
     - Provide implementation support consistent with Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement, including program and project management, coordination with third‑party vendors, risk management, and go‑live readiness for designated transformation initiatives.  
     - Establish and operate a “Transformation PMO” with defined reporting templates and governance routines.

  e. **Change Management and Training**  
     - Deliver change management services (Section 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement), including stakeholder analysis, communication plans, training curriculum design, and facilitation of up to twelve (12) instructor‑led training workshops (Remote and Onsite) across the Client’s key regions.  
     - Provide training materials, user guides, and knowledge‑transfer documentation.

  f. **Data Analytics and Executive Reporting**  
     - Support the design of data models and analytical dashboards in line with Section 2.2.6 of the Parent Agreement.  
     - Produce recurring performance reports and executive dashboards for senior leadership.

2.3 **Project Phases and Workstreams.**  

The SOW Services shall be structured into four (4) primary phases and associated workstreams:

  2.3.1 **Phase 1 – Discovery & Current State Assessment (Estimated: Sep 2024 – Jan 2025)**  
  - Activities:  
    - Stakeholder interviews and workshops (minimum 20 interviews, 10 workshops).  
    - Current state process mapping for at least three core process domains.  
    - Technology landscape inventory and pain‑point analysis.  
  - Key SOW Deliverables:  
    - “Discovery Findings Report.”  
    - “Current State Process Maps.”  
    - “Technology and Data Landscape Assessment.”  

  2.3.2 **Phase 2 – Future State Design & Roadmap (Estimated: Feb 2025 – Aug 2025)**  
  - Activities:  
    - Design of future‑state processes and controls.  
    - Definition of target technology architecture and integrations.  
    - Development of a multi‑year transformation roadmap with workstreams, sequencing, and benefit cases.  
  - Key SOW Deliverables:  
    - “Future State Process Designs & SOPs.”  
    - “Target Architecture Blueprint.”  
    - “Enterprise Transformation Roadmap” (with initiative charters).

  2.3.3 **Phase 3 – Implementation Support & PMO (Estimated: Sep 2025 – Dec 2027)**  
  - Activities:  
    - Establishment of a Transformation PMO.  
    - Design of program governance, risk management, and reporting cadence.  
    - Ongoing advisory for key implementation projects, including vendor coordination and readiness reviews.  
  - Key SOW Deliverables:  
    - “PMO Charter and Governance Framework.”  
    - Monthly PMO status reports and RAID (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies) logs.  
    - Project stage‑gate review summaries and go‑live readiness assessments.

  2.3.4 **Phase 4 – Stabilization, Optimization & Knowledge Transfer (Estimated: Jan 2028 – Nov 2028)**  
  - Activities:  
    - Post‑implementation reviews and benefits tracking.  
    - Optimization of selected processes and technology configurations.  
    - Structured knowledge transfer and handover to Client teams.  
  - Key SOW Deliverables:  
    - “Post‑Implementation Review Reports.”  
    - “Benefits Realization Tracking Model.”  
    - “Knowledge Transfer and Handover Dossier.”

A more detailed **Project Plan** with activity‑level tasks, dependencies, and resource allocations shall be prepared by the Vendor within thirty (30) days following the SOW Effective Date and approved by the Project Governance Committee. Once approved, the Project Plan shall form part of this SOW and may be amended via SOW Change Order.

2.4 **Deliverables; Acceptance.**  

2.4.1 The SOW Deliverables listed above and any additional Deliverables identified in the Project Plan shall be delivered in accordance with the timeline and format agreed in the Project Plan, subject to Section 2.5 of the Parent Agreement.  

2.4.2 Unless otherwise expressly stated in this SOW, the acceptance process and timelines for SOW Deliverables shall be governed by **Section 2.5 (Deliverables; Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, including the ten (10) Business Day review period and the notice of deficiencies mechanism.  

2.4.3 The Parties shall jointly define specific **Acceptance Criteria** for each material SOW Deliverable at the time of Project Plan finalization. Such Acceptance Criteria shall be consistent with the requirements set out herein and shall be documented in an annex to this SOW.  

2.4.4 If the Client fails to provide written acceptance or written notice of deficiencies within the applicable review period, the SOW Deliverable shall be deemed accepted pursuant to Section 2.5.4 of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities and Dependencies.**  

2.5.1 The Client obligations under **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** and **Section 2.7 (Dependencies and Assumptions)** of the Parent Agreement apply in full to this SOW.  

2.5.2 Without limitation, the Client shall:

  (c) secure relevant third‑party consents and system access required for the Vendor to perform the SOW Services; and

2.6.1 The Parties shall establish a **Project Governance Committee** composed of:  
  - For the Client: at least one senior executive sponsor and one project manager;  
  - For the Vendor: one engagement partner or equivalent and one delivery lead.

2.7.1 The Vendor shall assign appropriately skilled Consultants in accordance with Section 2.4.2 of the Parent Agreement, including certain **Key Personnel** identified in an annex to this SOW.
