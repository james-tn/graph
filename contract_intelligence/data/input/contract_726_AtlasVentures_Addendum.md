## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Addendum is **executed under and subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement** and is intended to **supplement, and where expressly stated, amend** specific provisions of the Parent Agreement, including **Section 2.1.2 (License Scope)**, **Article III (Fees and Payment Terms)**, and **Article IV (Term and Termination)**.

### 2.2 Additional Authorized Users and Modules

2.2.1 **Expansion of User Count.** Pursuant to Section 2.1.2 of the Parent Agreement, Client’s license to use the Software is limited to the number of Authorized Users specified in applicable SOWs. By this Addendum, the Parties agree that Client is hereby granted the right, subject to continued compliance with the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, to add **up to an additional 650 Authorized Users** across Client and its Affiliates, as Additional Authorized Users. The detailed allocation of Additional Authorized Users across business units, regions, and environments (e.g., production, staging, sandbox) shall be set forth in one or more SOWs referencing this Addendum.

2.2.2 **Addition of Modules.** In addition to the modules initially licensed under the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall make available to Client, and Client is hereby licensed to use, subject to the terms of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the following Additional Modules:

(a) **Atlas Predictive Analytics Module**, providing advanced machine-learning–driven forecasting, risk modeling, and scenario analysis;  
(b) **Atlas Data Governance Module**, providing data cataloging, lineage, access controls, policy enforcement, and audit capabilities; and  
(c) **Atlas Advanced Reporting & Visualization Module**, providing extended dashboards, interactive analytics, and customizable reporting templates.

2.2.3 **Non-Transferability of Additional Rights.** The rights granted under this Section 2.2 are non-exclusive, non-transferable, and non-sublicensable, and remain subject to the general restrictions set forth in **Section 2.5 (Restrictions)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.3 Addendum Services

Vendor shall perform the following Addendum Services in support of the Additional Authorized Users and Additional Modules:

2.3.1 **Implementation and Configuration.** Vendor shall:

(a) Conduct requirements and design workshops with relevant Client stakeholders to define use cases for the Additional Modules, building upon the methodology described in **Section 2.2.1** of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Configure the Additional Modules within the Atlas Insight Suite, including user roles, permissions, workflows, and data connectors, in accordance with Client’s documented requirements;  
(c) Integrate the Additional Modules with Client’s designated systems (e.g., ERP, CRM, data warehouse) using Vendor-provided APIs and integration patterns consistent with the integration framework already utilized under the Parent Agreement;  
(d) Implement data pipelines and transformation routines necessary to support analytics and governance features, aligning with Client’s data quality and security requirements; and  
(e) Conduct validation and user acceptance testing assistance, following the acceptance process described in **Section 2.3.2 (Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, as further tailored in applicable SOWs.

2.3.2 **Training Services.** Vendor shall provide:

(a) Up to **six (6) remote training sessions** for Client’s administrators, power users, and business analysts, focusing on the Additional Modules;  
(b) Training materials, including slide decks, administrator guides, and recorded sessions, which shall be considered Deliverables under Section 1.7 of the Parent Agreement; and  
(c) Two (2) “train-the-trainer” workshops to enable Client to conduct internal training across its expanded user base.

2.3.3 **Support and Maintenance.** Vendor shall provide Support Services for the Additional Modules and Additional Authorized Users consistent with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, including:

(a) Help desk access during the support hours specified in Section 12.3.1 of the Parent Agreement;  
(b) Incident management and resolution with target response times as outlined in Section 12.3.2 of the Parent Agreement, with Severity 1 incidents relating to the Additional Modules prioritized equivalently to existing modules; and  
(c) Provision of Updates (as defined in Section 1.19 of the Parent Agreement) for the Additional Modules at no additional charge beyond the Addendum Fees, unless otherwise agreed in a subsequent SOW.

2.3.4 **Professional Services.** At Client’s request and subject to a mutually agreed SOW, Vendor may perform additional professional services related to the Additional Modules, including but not limited to custom modeling, complex report development, and extended data governance policy design. Such services shall be governed by **Section 2.2.4 (Professional Services)** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 2.4 Performance Standards and Milestones

2.4.1 **Professional and Workmanlike Performance.** Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, consistent with **Section 2.3.3 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement and generally accepted industry standards.

2.4.2 **Project Plan and Timelines.** The Parties shall agree on a detailed project plan for the deployment of the Additional Modules and onboarding of Additional Authorized Users, including milestones for:

(a) Requirements gathering completion;  
(b) Configuration completion;  
(c) Integration and data migration completion;  
(d) User acceptance testing and remediation; and  
(e) Go-live for each Additional Module.

Such milestones shall be set forth in a SOW. Unless otherwise agreed, time shall not be of the essence, but Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to meet agreed target dates and promptly notify Client of any material delays, consistent with **Section 2.3.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.3 **Acceptance of Deliverables.** Deliverables created under this Addendum shall be subject to the acceptance mechanism described in **Section 2.3.2** of the Parent Agreement unless otherwise specified in a SOW. The Acceptance Period shall be fifteen (15) Business Days unless the Parties expressly agree to a different period in the relevant SOW.

### 2.5 Client Responsibilities (Additional)

In addition to Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.4** of the Parent Agreement, Client shall:

(a) Identify business owners and technical contacts for each Additional Module;  
(b) Ensure that all Additional Authorized Users comply with the use restrictions in **Section 2.5.1** of the Parent Agreement;  
(c) Provide timely access to required data sources and environments necessary for configuration and integration of the Additional Modules; and  
(d) Maintain adequate internal support capabilities to handle user onboarding, basic usage questions, and initial triage before escalation to Vendor’s support team, consistent with the processes established under the Parent Agreement.

## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Addendum Term; Relationship to Parent Term

4.1.1 **Addendum Term.** This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall continue in full force and effect until **April 22, 2029** (the “**Addendum Term**”).

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 **By Client.** Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part (e.g., with respect to a particular Additional Module or block of Additional Authorized Users), for convenience and without cause upon not less than **forty-five (45) days’** prior written notice to Vendor.

4.2.2 **By Vendor.** Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client, consistent with **Section 4.2 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.2.3 **Effect on Parent Agreement.** Termination of this Addendum for convenience shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement, which shall remain in full force and effect with respect to any licenses, Services, and SOWs not expressly terminated.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for cause upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum and fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receipt of written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail, consistent with **Section 4.3.1 (Material Breach)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3.2 Grounds for termination for insolvency or similar events shall be as set forth in **Section 4.3.2 (Insolvency)** of the Parent Agreement and shall apply equally to this Addendum.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement

4.4.1 **Parent Agreement Termination.** If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date of termination, unless the Parties agree in writing to preserve certain rights or transition arrangements.

4.4.2 **Survival of Payment Obligations.** Termination of the Parent Agreement or this Addendum shall not relieve either Party of its obligation to pay any accrued but unpaid Addendum Fees or reimburse expenses incurred prior to the effective date of termination, in accordance with **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.4.3 **Refunds.** If Client validly terminates the Parent Agreement and/or this Addendum for Vendor’s uncured material breach under **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall refund to Client a pro rata portion of any prepaid, unused Addendum subscription Fees as of the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.4.3** of the Parent Agreement.

### 4.5 Transition Assistance

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason, Vendor shall, at Client’s request and at Vendor’s then-current time-and-materials rates, provide reasonable transition assistance, including export of Client Data from the Additional Modules, as contemplated by **Section 4.4.4 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement.

### 4.6 Survival

The provisions of this Addendum that by their nature should survive termination (including without limitation payment obligations, confidentiality, IP ownership, indemnification, limitations of liability, and governing law) shall survive, consistent with **Section 4.5 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement.

9.2.2 **Interaction with Parent Agreement Cap.** For clarity:

(b) For claims that relate solely to this Addendum, the cap in Section 9.2.1 above applies.  
(c) For claims that reasonably relate to both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum, the applicable cap shall be calculated based on **three (3) times** the aggregate Fees paid or payable under both the Parent Agreement and this Addendum during the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim.
