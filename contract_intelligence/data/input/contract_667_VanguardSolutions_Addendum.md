## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF ADDITIONAL PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

2.1 **Reference to Parent Agreement.** The Parties acknowledge and agree that the general scope framework, obligations, and performance standards set forth in **Article II (Scope of Services and Work)** of the Parent Agreement are hereby incorporated by reference into this Addendum, and shall apply to the Additional Services and Deliverables described herein, except as expressly modified by this Addendum. Without limiting the foregoing, Vanguard’s obligations to perform the Additional Services in a professional and workmanlike manner, in compliance with applicable law, and in accordance with agreed project plans and Milestones, shall be governed by Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement, as supplemented by Section 2.4 of this Addendum.

2.2 **Overview of Additional Products and Services.** Under this Addendum, Vanguard shall provide to Contoso, and Contoso shall purchase from Vanguard, the following Additional Services and Deliverables:

  (a) **Expansion of Hardware and Infrastructure Capacity.** Vanguard shall design, procure, and deliver additional hardware and related infrastructure components to extend the enterprise technology platform previously deployed under the Parent Agreement. This includes additional compute nodes, storage arrays, and network appliances necessary to support increased transaction volumes and data retention requirements.

  (b) **Deployment of New Software Modules.** Vanguard shall license, configure, and deploy **New Software Modules** consisting of: (i) an advanced analytics and reporting suite for operational and executive dashboards; (ii) a security information and event management (SIEM) connector integrating with Contoso’s existing security tools; and (iii) a workflow automation module to streamline incident and change management processes.

  (c) **Extended Integration Services.** Vanguard shall design, build, and test additional integration points between the New Software Modules and Contoso’s ERP, CRM, data warehouse, and identity management systems, consistent with the approach described in Section 2.2(e) of the Parent Agreement.

  (d) **Enhanced Managed Services.** Vanguard shall provide **Enhanced Managed Services**, including 24x7x365 monitoring of the Extended Hosting Environment, proactive performance tuning, capacity forecasting, advanced threat monitoring, and monthly service review meetings, each as further detailed in this Article II and in the applicable Addendum SOW(s).

  (e) **Training and Knowledge Transfer for New Capabilities.** Vanguard shall design and deliver role-based training curricula covering the New Software Modules, enhanced dashboards, and operational procedures associated with the Enhanced Managed Services.

  (f) **Compliance and Audit Support.** Vanguard shall provide reasonable support services to assist Contoso in satisfying internal and external audit requirements related to the Additional Services, including documentation of security controls, data flows, and technical configurations, consistent with Vanguard’s obligations under **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Detailed Description of Enhanced Managed Services.** The Enhanced Managed Services shall include, at a minimum:

  (a) **24x7 Monitoring and Incident Management.** Vanguard shall monitor system health, performance, and security events for all components of the Extended Hosting Environment and New Software Modules. Vanguard will open and manage incident tickets pursuant to the priority levels and response targets in **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent Agreement, as adapted in Article XII of this Addendum for the additional scope.

  (b) **Performance and Capacity Management.** Vanguard shall track utilization trends, perform monthly capacity assessments, and provide Contoso with written recommendations for scaling resources. Where Contoso approves such recommendations, additional resources may be provisioned via Change Order under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

  (c) **Patch, Upgrade, and Configuration Management.** Vanguard shall apply security patches, minor version upgrades, and configuration changes for the Extended Hosting Environment and New Software Modules in accordance with a mutually agreed maintenance schedule, taking into account any **Scheduled Maintenance** definitions in Section 12.2(b) of the Parent Agreement.

  (d) **Security and Compliance Monitoring.** Vanguard shall configure and tune the SIEM connector and related tools to capture security-relevant events, generate alerts, and provide Contoso with monthly security posture reports. Vanguard shall cooperate with Contoso’s security team to investigate and remediate detected threats.

  (e) **Reporting and Service Reviews.** Vanguard shall provide monthly operation and performance reports, including but not limited to: uptime metrics, incident summaries, SLA performance, capacity trends, and security incident logs. Vanguard and Contoso will conduct monthly service review meetings to discuss such reports, upcoming changes, and improvement initiatives.

2.4 **Performance Standards.** In addition to the performance standards in Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement, Vanguard shall:

  (a) Maintain for the Extended Hosting Environment a **target Availability of 99.9%** measured monthly, consistent with Section 12.1 of the Parent Agreement and as applied to the Additional Services;

  (b) Use personnel with specialized skills in security operations, cloud infrastructure, and enterprise integration, commensurate with the complexity of the Additional Services;

  (c) Provide Contoso with at least seven (7) Business Days’ advance notice of any material change to operational procedures affecting the Additional Services (except in emergencies as per Section 13.5 of the Parent Agreement);

  (d) Comply with all applicable Data Protection Laws when processing any Personal Data within the Extended Hosting Environment, consistent with **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Scope.** The Parties expressly acknowledge that:

  (a) The Additional Services under this Addendum are a supplement and extension to the Services already described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement and any SOWs thereunder;

  (b) In the event of any conflict between the Parent Agreement and this Addendum with respect to the Additional Services, **this Addendum shall control**, solely with respect to the Additional Services and related fees, payment terms, and timelines; and

  (c) In the event of any conflict between this Addendum and an Addendum SOW, the hierarchy shall be: (i) Parent Agreement (except where this Addendum expressly modifies it), (ii) this Addendum, and (iii) the applicable Addendum SOW, unless such Addendum SOW explicitly references the specific provision being modified and states that such SOW shall prevail.

2.6 **Contoso Responsibilities.** In addition to its responsibilities in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall:

  (a) Provide timely access to its Extended Hosting Environment endpoints, including cloud accounts or data centers, as required for Vanguard to perform the Additional Services;

  (b) Maintain up-to-date contact lists for escalation of incidents, including security incidents, and ensure that Contoso’s personnel respond promptly to Vanguard communications regarding critical events;

  (c) Ensure that all data provided to Vanguard for use in the New Software Modules does not infringe third-party rights and is processed in compliance with applicable law, consistent with Contoso’s warranties in Section 7.3 of the Parent Agreement;

  (d) Promptly review and provide feedback on any Solution Design updates or architecture changes required for the Extended Hosting Environment.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.** Subject to the terms and conditions herein, Contoso shall pay Vanguard fees for the Additional Services and Deliverables in accordance with the **Addendum Contract Value** of **US $552,120**, which represents the current estimate for the scope defined in this Addendum. This Addendum Contract Value is in addition to the **Contract Value of US $843,012** set forth in Section 1.6 of the Parent Agreement and is subject to adjustment through Change Orders under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

3.2 **Fee Components.** The Addendum Contract Value comprises the following components:

  (a) **One-Time Implementation and Setup Fees:**  
  - Design, procurement coordination, and deployment of the Extended Hosting Environment and New Software Modules: **US $210,000** (fixed price, payable on Milestones as set forth in Section 3.3).

  (b) **Recurring Managed Services Fees:**  
  - Enhanced Managed Services for the Extended Hosting Environment, including 24x7 monitoring, capacity management, and security analytics: **US $42,000 per quarter** (equivalent to **US $14,000 per month**), anticipated for an initial period of **36 months**, representing **US $504,000** in total recurring fees over such period. The Parties acknowledge that a portion of this recurring amount is included within the Addendum Contract Value based on projected usage; actual amounts may vary based on term and scope.

  (c) **Training and Knowledge Transfer Fees:**  
  - Up to 120 hours of training and knowledge transfer sessions related to New Software Modules and Enhanced Managed Services: **US $48,000** (time-and-materials at an hourly blended rate, capped at this amount absent a Change Order).

  (d) **Credits or Offsets:**  
  To the extent any unused fees or prepayments exist under prior SOWs executed under the Parent Agreement and the Parties mutually agree in writing, such amounts may be applied as credits against the fees payable under this Addendum.

3.3 **Payment Milestones.** Unless otherwise agreed in an Addendum SOW, the following Milestones and associated invoicing shall apply to the one-time implementation and setup fees:

  (a) **Milestone 1 – Addendum Effective Date:**  
  Twenty percent (20%) of the one-time implementation fees (US $42,000) shall be invoiced upon the Addendum Effective Date.

  (b) **Milestone 2 – Completion of Extended Hosting Environment Provisioning:**  
  Thirty percent (30%) of the one-time implementation fees (US $63,000) shall be invoiced upon Contoso’s acceptance (or deemed acceptance) of the Extended Hosting Environment design and initial deployment, consistent with the acceptance mechanism in Section 2.2(f) of the Parent Agreement.

  (c) **Milestone 3 – Deployment of New Software Modules in Production:**  
  Thirty percent (30%) of the one-time implementation fees (US $63,000) shall be invoiced upon completion of production deployment of the New Software Modules and completion of initial system testing.

  (d) **Milestone 4 – Stabilization and Handover:**  
  Twenty percent (20%) of the one-time implementation fees (US $42,000) shall be invoiced upon successful completion of a thirty (30)-day stabilization period following production go-live, provided no material unresolved issues attributable to Vanguard remain.

3.4 **Invoicing and Payment Terms (Net 60).** Vanguard shall invoice Contoso (i) for one-time fees upon achievement of the Milestones above, and (ii) for recurring Enhanced Managed Services fees quarterly in advance, unless otherwise specified in an Addendum SOW. **Contoso shall pay all undisputed amounts within sixty (60) days of the date of invoice (“Net 60”)**, which payment term **modifies and supersedes Section 3.4 (Net 45) of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to invoices issued under this Addendum**. For the avoidance of doubt, invoices issued under the Parent Agreement but not referencing this Addendum remain subject to the Net 45 payment term in Section 3.4 of the Parent Agreement.

3.5 **Late Payments.** Section 3.5 of the Parent Agreement (Late Payments) shall apply to all undisputed amounts due under this Addendum. Accordingly, any undisputed amount not paid when due under the Net 60 term shall accrue interest at a rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month or the maximum rate allowed by law, whichever is less, from the due date until paid in full, and Vanguard may suspend the Additional Services and withhold Deliverables in accordance with that section.

3.6 **Expenses and Reimbursement.** Consistent with Section 3.8 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall reimburse Vanguard for all reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the performance of the Additional Services under this Addendum, including travel, lodging, meals, and third-party fees, which shall be invoiced and paid on a Net 60 basis.

3.7 **Taxes and Withholding.** Sections 3.7 (Taxes) and 3.9 (No Withholding) of the Parent Agreement are incorporated herein by reference and shall apply mutatis mutandis to all fees and charges payable under this Addendum.

3.8 **Adjustment of Recurring Fees.** Vanguard may propose adjustments to the recurring Enhanced Managed Services fees once per calendar year to reflect changes in scope, usage, or underlying third-party costs, provided that any such adjustment shall be subject to Contoso’s approval via a Change Order under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.** This Addendum shall commence on the **Addendum Effective Date** (October 01, 2026) and shall continue in full force and effect until **May 01, 2031** (the **Addendum Expiration Date**), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV or the Parent Agreement.

4.2 **Relationship to Parent Agreement Term.** The Parties acknowledge that the **Term** of the Parent Agreement, as set forth in Section 4.1 thereof, currently runs from October 17, 2025 through **April 06, 2030**. Notwithstanding the Addendum Expiration Date of May 01, 2031:

  (a) The Parties expressly agree that, solely for the purpose of enabling Vanguard to provide the Additional Services under this Addendum, the Parent Agreement’s Term is deemed extended through the Addendum Expiration Date, unless the Parent Agreement is earlier terminated pursuant to Article IV of the Parent Agreement.

  (b) If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason prior to the Addendum Expiration Date, this Addendum shall automatically terminate effective as of the effective date of termination of the Parent Agreement, without additional notice, except as otherwise provided in Section 4.5 below.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience by Contoso.** In addition to Contoso’s rights under Section 4.3 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience at any time by providing at least **one hundred eighty (180) days** prior written notice to Vanguard. Upon such termination:

  (a) Contoso shall remain liable for (i) all fees and expenses incurred or committed by Vanguard in connection with the Additional Services as of the effective date of termination, including any non-cancellable third-party costs, and (ii) an early termination fee equal to twenty percent (20%) of the unpaid portion of the then-current **Addendum Contract Value** (calculated based on any unbilled recurring fees for the remaining Addendum Term), which the Parties agree is liquidated damages consistent with the approach in Section 4.3(c) of the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Convenience by Vanguard.** Consistent with Section 4.4 of the Parent Agreement, Vanguard may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience by providing at least one hundred eighty (180) days prior written notice to Contoso. In such event, Contoso shall be liable only for fees and expenses incurred or committed for the Additional Services through the effective date of termination, and Vanguard shall provide reasonable Transition Assistance Services as described in Section 4.6.

4.5 **Termination for Cause.** The termination for cause provisions in Section 4.5 of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Addendum. Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Addendum or the Parent Agreement in relation to the Additional Services and fails to cure such breach within the applicable cure period (30 days, or 10 days for payment breaches).

4.6 **Effect of Termination; Transition Assistance.** Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum:

  (a) All rights and licenses granted to Contoso under this Addendum with respect to the New Software Modules and Extended Hosting Environment shall terminate, except as expressly provided otherwise in **Article VI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum;

  (b) Contoso shall promptly pay Vanguard all amounts due and unpaid for Additional Services rendered, Deliverables provided, and expenses incurred prior to the effective date of termination, plus any applicable early termination fees pursuant to Section 4.3;

  (c) Vanguard shall provide Transition Assistance Services for up to ninety (90) days following termination, upon Contoso’s written request made prior to the effective date of termination, including data export and cooperation with any successor provider. Such Transition Assistance Services shall be provided at Vanguard’s then-current time-and-materials rates, consistent with Section 4.7 of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Survival.** Sections of this Addendum that by their nature are intended to survive termination or expiration shall survive, including without limitation Articles III (with respect to payment obligations accrued), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI, and this Section 4.7.
