## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Amendment supplements and, where expressly stated, modifies the **Scope of Services / Work** described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement. Except as specifically modified herein, all obligations regarding Services, Deliverables, timelines, and performance standards in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect. For avoidance of doubt:

- The Services listed in **Section 2.1(a)–(f)** of the Parent Agreement (data hosting, processing and analytics, SaaS Platform access, integration and implementation services, support and maintenance, and professional services) continue to apply; and  
- This Amendment introduces **Enhanced Data Protection Services** and **Supplemental Deliverables** that build upon and extend those Services, particularly in relation to data protection, privacy, compliance support, and security visibility.

2.2 **Enhanced Data Protection Services.**  
Vendor shall provide the following Enhanced Data Protection Services to Client during the Amendment Term:

  a. **Advanced Data Mapping and Classification.** Vendor shall, in collaboration with Client, implement advanced data mapping and classification within the Platform to identify, tag, and categorize Personal Data and sensitive data fields processed under the Parent Agreement. This includes:
  - Configuration of automated tagging of data types and categories (e.g., identifiers, financial data, geolocation data) consistent with Applicable Data Protection Laws;  
  - Provision of periodic data inventory reports as part of the Supplemental Deliverables (at least quarterly); and  
  - Support for Client’s records of processing activities required under Applicable Data Protection Laws, forming part of the **Joint Documentation** referenced in **Section 1.16**.

  b. **Enhanced Data Subject Rights Tooling.** Vendor shall configure and maintain features in the Platform that assist Client in responding to Data Subject requests under **Section 11.6** of the Parent Agreement, including:
  - Search, export, and deletion tools enabling Client to identify and act on Personal Data linked to a specific Data Subject, subject to Client’s authentication of such Data Subject;  
  - Configurable workflows for approval and logging of Data Subject request handling;  
  - Availability of audit logs demonstrating actions taken in response to Data Subject requests, which form part of the Supplemental Deliverables.

  c. **Data Residency and Segmentation Configuration.** To the extent technically feasible and agreed in writing, Vendor shall configure dedicated tenant environments and data stores such that:
  - Personal Data relating to Data Subjects in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom can be stored and processed in data centers located within the EEA or UK, respectively, subject to Vendor’s supported regions;  
  - Personal Data relating to California residents is logically segmented and processed in a manner allowing Client to comply with CCPA/CPRA obligations;  
  - Any cross-border transfers are documented and supported through appropriate transfer mechanisms, as further described in **Article XI** of this Amendment.

  d. **Enhanced Reporting and Compliance Dashboards.** Vendor shall provide Client with Supplemental Deliverables in the form of enhanced dashboards and reports that:
  - Summarize key metrics on data processing (e.g., volume of Personal Data processed, categories of Data Subjects, incident metrics, and SLA performance);  
  - Provide visibility into Data Subject requests, response times, and outcomes;  
  - Highlight configuration status relating to security measures (e.g., encryption, access controls) and notify Client of any material gaps relative to the agreed Updated Security Measures.

  e. **Augmented Audit and DPIA Support.** In addition to **Section 11.10 (Audit and Compliance Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall:
  - Provide up to **twenty-four (24) hours per contract year** of reasonable remote cooperation with Client’s DPIAs, audits, and Regulator inquiries relating to the Services;  
  - Participate in up to **two (2) audit or review sessions per contract year** (remote or on-site if mutually agreed), to address data protection and security questions, subject to confidentiality and reasonable scheduling constraints; and  
  - Provide copies or summaries of Vendor’s most recent security certifications or audit reports (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001), subject to Vendor’s standard confidentiality terms.

2.3 **Supplemental Deliverables.**  
The following Supplemental Deliverables shall be provided under this Amendment:

  a. **Quarterly Compliance Reports.** Vendor shall deliver, within fifteen (15) Business Days after the end of each calendar quarter during the Amendment Term, a written or electronic report covering:
  - Platform uptime and incident statistics (complementing the reporting obligations under **Section 2.2(e)** and **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement);  
  - Data Breach events (if any) and summary of remediation steps, consistent with **Section 11.5** of the Parent Agreement;  
  - Summary of configuration changes relevant to privacy or security; and  
  - Status updates on Enhanced Data Protection Services.

  b. **Annual Data Protection Review Summary.** Once per contract year during the Amendment Term, Vendor shall produce a summary report consolidating:
  - Key DPIA-related inputs and outputs;  
  - Significant changes in Applicable Data Protection Laws impacting the Services;  
  - Recommended configuration or process changes to maintain or improve compliance.

  c. **Configuration and Implementation Artifacts.** Vendor shall maintain and provide upon request:
  - Schema definitions, data flow diagrams, and integration specifications related to Processing of Personal Data;  
  - Configuration documentation for the Enhanced Data Subject Rights tooling and data residency features.

2.4 **Timelines and Milestones under this Amendment.**  
In addition to the timelines set forth in **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement, the following shall apply:

  a. Vendor shall complete initial configuration of the Enhanced Data Protection Services described in **Section 2.2(b)–(d)** within **sixty (60) calendar days** following the Amendment Effective Date, subject to Client’s timely provision of information, access, and approvals.  

  b. Vendor shall deliver the first Quarterly Compliance Report under **Section 2.3(a)** covering the first full calendar quarter following completion of the initial configuration.  

  c. Delays attributable to Client (including delays in providing necessary information, access, or approvals) shall extend the associated timelines on a day-for-day basis, consistent with **Section 2.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.5 **Performance Standards.**  
Vendor shall perform the Enhanced Data Protection Services and produce the Supplemental Deliverables:

  a. In a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards and with the standards described in **Section 2.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  b. In compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws, including GDPR and CCPA/CPRA, as expanded in **Section 11.3** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Amendment; and  

  c. In accordance with the Updated SLA Commitments set forth in **Article XII** of this Amendment, which are in addition to, and not in derogation of, the Parent SLA.

2.6 **Client Responsibilities (Clarification).**  
Without limiting **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Client acknowledges and agrees that:

  a. Client remains responsible for providing accurate, current, and complete data and Instructions as defined in **Section 1.10** and **Section 2.5(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  b. Client remains solely responsible for determining the lawfulness of Processing, identifying appropriate legal bases, and obtaining required consents, as described in **Section 2.5(b)** and **Section 7.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  c. Client shall promptly review reports and Supplemental Deliverables for accuracy and completeness and notify Vendor of any material issues, consistent with **Section 2.5(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.7 **No Legal Advice (Reaffirmation).**  
The clarification in **Section 2.7 (No Legal or Regulatory Advice)** of the Parent Agreement remains unchanged. Any guidance or recommendations provided by Vendor under this Amendment are for operational purposes only and shall not constitute legal, tax, or regulatory advice.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  
In consideration for the Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables provided under this Amendment, Client shall pay Vendor additional fees up to a **total Amendment Contract Value of $277,496**, exclusive of Taxes, as defined in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  
Unless otherwise agreed in a Statement of Work referencing this Amendment, the following fee structure shall apply:

  a. **One-Time Configuration and Enablement Fee.**  
  - A one-time, non-recurring fee equal to **20% of the Amendment Contract Value** (i.e., **$55,499.20**) for initial configuration and enablement of the Enhanced Data Protection Services, payable as follows:  
    i. Fifty percent (50%) of such fee upon the Amendment Effective Date; and  
    ii. Fifty percent (50%) upon completion of the initial configuration described in **Section 2.4(a)**.

  b. **Recurring Annual Enhancement Subscription Fees.**  
  - Recurring annual subscription fees for ongoing provision of Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables equal to **70% of the Amendment Contract Value** (i.e., **$194,247.20**) allocated over the Amendment Term on a pro rata basis;  
  - Billed annually in advance on or about each anniversary of the Amendment Effective Date (or pro-rated for partial years).

  c. **Professional Services Fees (Optional Additional Work).**  
  - Up to **10% of the Amendment Contract Value** (i.e., **$27,749.60**) may be allocated to additional professional services requested by Client in connection with this Amendment (e.g., custom compliance dashboards, specialized integration work), billed on a time-and-materials basis at Vendor’s then-current rates;  
  - Such professional services shall be documented via a Change Request or SOW referencing this Amendment and the Parent Agreement, consistent with **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

3.3 **Payment Terms (Modification of Parent Agreement).**  
Notwithstanding **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement, which specifies **Net 45** payment terms, the Parties agree that, solely with respect to invoices issued under this Amendment:

  a. All undisputed amounts shall be due and payable within **sixty (60) days** from the invoice date (**“Net 60”**);  

  b. This Section 3.3 **amends and supersedes Section 3.3 of the Parent Agreement solely as it applies to amounts invoiced under this Amendment**, and shall not alter the payment terms applicable to fees under the Parent Agreement, which remain **Net 45** unless separately amended.

3.4 **Invoicing.**  
Vendor shall invoice Client in accordance with the schedule set forth in **Section 3.2** above. Unless otherwise specified in writing:

  a. The one-time configuration fee shall be invoiced in two installments as described in **Section 3.2(a)**;  

  b. The recurring annual enhancement subscription fees shall be invoiced annually in advance; and  

  c. Professional services fees, if any, shall be invoiced monthly in arrears.  

Dispute and payment procedures in **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to invoices issued under this Amendment.

3.5 **Late Payments.**  
Undisputed amounts not paid when due under this Amendment shall accrue interest at the lesser of:

  a. One percent (1.0%) per month; or  
  b. The maximum rate allowed by applicable law,  

from the due date until paid in full, consistent with **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement. Vendor’s right to suspend Services for non-payment under **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to the Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables under this Amendment.

3.6 **Expenses.**  
Any reasonable, pre-approved out-of-pocket expenses incurred by Vendor in providing on-site support, audits, or other services specifically requested by Client under this Amendment shall be reimbursable by Client, in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.7 **Taxes.**  
All fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes as defined in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement. Client shall be responsible for all such Taxes associated with the Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables.

3.8 **No Setoff (Reaffirmation).**  
The prohibition on setoff in **Section 3.8** of the Parent Agreement applies equally to amounts owed under this Amendment.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Amendment Term; Relationship to Parent Term.**  
The term of this Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **September 17, 2029**, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the “**Amendment Term**”). For clarity:

  a. The Amendment Term may extend beyond the **Expiration Date of July 11, 2027** stated in **Section 4.1** of the Parent Agreement; and  

  b. The Parties anticipate that the Parent Agreement will be renewed or superseded to remain in force during the Amendment Term. If the Parent Agreement is not renewed or is replaced, this Amendment shall continue to apply to any successor data processing relationship between the Parties unless expressly superseded.

4.2 **Renewal.**  
Any renewal of this Amendment shall be subject to the mutual written agreement of the Parties and may be documented via an amendment referencing both this Amendment and the Parent Agreement, consistent with **Section 4.2** and **Section 16.2** of the Parent Agreement.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience (Client-Friendly).**  
Notwithstanding **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement (which allows termination for convenience upon sixty (60) days’ prior written notice), the Parties agree that:

  a. **Client** may terminate this Amendment, in whole or in part, for any reason or no reason upon **thirty (30) days’ prior written notice** to Vendor;  

  b. **Vendor** may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’ prior written notice** to Client; and  

  c. Termination for convenience of this Amendment shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent Agreement.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
Either Party may terminate this Amendment immediately upon written notice to the other Party if:

  a. The other Party materially breaches this Amendment and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or  

  b. The conditions described in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent Agreement occur (insolvency, bankruptcy, receivership, or cessation of business).

4.5 **Termination for Data Protection or Security Breach.**  
Without limiting **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement:

  a. Client may terminate this Amendment (and, at its sole discretion, related portions of the Parent Agreement) upon written notice if Vendor commits a material and repeated violation of Applicable Data Protection Laws or this Amendment’s data protection provisions and fails to cure within thirty (30) days, or if such violation is incapable of cure;  

  b. In determining whether a violation is “material and repeated,” the Parties shall consider the nature, scope, and frequency of the violation, the sensitivity of the Personal Data affected, and Vendor’s remediation efforts.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
If the Parent Agreement is terminated or expires without renewal:

  a. This Amendment shall automatically terminate on the effective date of such termination or expiration, unless the Parties mutually agree in writing that specified obligations in this Amendment (e.g., data return/deletion, confidentiality, and audit cooperation) shall survive independently;  

  b. Upon such termination, the obligations in **Section 4.6** and **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement regarding return or deletion of Personal Data, fees due, and cooperation in transitioning shall apply equally to the Services and Deliverables provided under this Amendment.

4.7 **Effect of Termination of this Amendment.**  
Upon expiration or termination of this Amendment:

  a. Vendor shall cease providing the Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables, except as reasonably necessary to wind down activities;  

  b. Client shall pay Vendor all fees and reimbursable expenses due through the effective date of termination, including any amounts due for completed configuration, delivered reports, and approved change orders;  

  c. Vendor shall, at Client’s written direction and in accordance with **Section 11.8** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XI** of this Amendment, return or securely delete any Personal Data uniquely processed or stored for purposes of the Enhanced Data Protection Services; and  

  d. The underlying Services and Deliverables under the Parent Agreement (unrelated to this Amendment) shall continue, unless separately terminated.

4.8 **Survival.**  
The rights and obligations of the Parties under **Articles III (to the extent of unpaid fees), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI (post-termination data protection obligations), XIV, and XVI** of this Amendment, together with any provisions of the Parent Agreement that by their nature should survive, shall survive expiration or termination of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE XI – DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY (UPDATED TERMS)

11.1 **Roles of the Parties.**  
The roles described in **Section 11.1** of the Parent Agreement remain unchanged: Client acts as Controller (Business), and Vendor acts as Processor (Service Provider) with respect to Personal Data Processed under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

11.2 **Processing Instructions (Clarification).**  
Vendor shall Process Personal Data:

  a. In accordance with Client’s documented Instructions as defined in **Section 1.10** and **Section 11.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  

  b. As necessary to provide the Enhanced Data Protection Services and Supplemental Deliverables described in **Article II** of this Amendment; and  

  c. As required by Applicable Data Protection Laws, with Vendor providing notice to Client where legally permissible.

If Vendor believes that any Instruction related to the Enhanced Data Protection Services violates Applicable Data Protection Laws, Vendor shall promptly notify Client and may suspend execution of such Instruction, consistent with **Section 11.2** of the Parent Agreement.

11.3 **Compliance with Applicable Data Protection Laws (Expanded).**  
Vendor reaffirms its obligations under **Section 11.3** of the Parent Agreement and further agrees to:

  a. Monitor material changes in Applicable Data Protection Laws that directly impact the Processing activities under this Amendment; and  

  b. Notify Client in a timely manner of any such changes that would require a material modification of the Enhanced Data Protection Services or Supplemental Deliverables.

11.4 **Updated Security Measures.**  
Vendor’s Security Measures under **Section 11.4** of the Parent Agreement are hereby supplemented as follows:

  a. Vendor shall maintain documented security controls for the Enhanced Data Protection Services, including updated access controls, segregation of duties, and enhanced logging for data subject tooling;  

  b. Vendor shall provide, upon Client’s written request and subject to confidentiality, a high-level summary of its security architecture applicable to the Enhanced Data Protection Services; and  

  c. Vendor shall not materially reduce the level of Security Measures applicable to Personal Data under this Amendment during the Amendment Term without Client’s prior written consent, except as required by law or to address a newly discovered vulnerability.

11.5 **Data Breach Notification (Clarification).**  
The Data Breach obligations in **Section 11.5** of the Parent Agreement apply fully to Personal Data Processed under this Amendment. Vendor’s initial notification shall, to the extent reasonably available at the time, specify whether the Data Breach impacts data processed solely under the Parent Agreement or also impacts systems supporting the Enhanced Data Protection Services.

Vendor’s assistance obligations under **Section 11.6** of the Parent Agreement are supplemented by the Enhanced Data Subject Rights tooling described in **Section 2.2(b)** of this Amendment. Vendor shall ensure that such tooling is maintained in a manner consistent with the Updated Security Measures.

11.7 **Sub-processors (Clarification).**  
Vendor’s use of Sub-processors under **Section 11.7** of the Parent Agreement extends to this Amendment. Upon Client’s written request, Vendor shall indicate which Sub-processors, if any, are specifically involved in providing the Enhanced Data Protection Services, and shall ensure such Sub-processors are bound by obligations no less protective than those set forth herein.

11.8 **Data Retention and Deletion (Supplemental).**  
In addition to **Section 11.8** of the Parent Agreement:

  a. Vendor shall not retain Personal Data solely collected or generated for Enhanced Data Protection Services longer than necessary to provide such services, unless required by law; and  

  b. Upon termination of this Amendment, Vendor shall, at Client’s written direction, return or delete any Personal Data stored exclusively for Enhanced Data Protection Services, while retaining any required logs only as long as necessary for compliance and evidentiary purposes.

11.9 **Data Transfers (Clarification).**  
Vendor shall ensure that any cross-border transfers of Personal Data in connection with the data residency configurations described in **Section 2.2(c)** comply with Applicable Data Protection Laws and approved transfer mechanisms. Vendor shall provide, upon request, a list of countries where Personal Data is stored or accessed for the Enhanced Data Protection Services.

11.10 **Audit and Compliance Assistance (Expanded).**  
The audit provisions in **Section 11.10** of the Parent Agreement are supplemented by **Section 2.2(e)** of this Amendment. For clarity:

  a. Client may, no more than once annually (unless required by a supervisory authority or after a Data Breach), perform an audit specifically focused on compliance of the Enhanced Data Protection Services;  

  b. Vendor’s obligation to contribute to such audits includes providing relevant documentation, logs, and, if reasonably necessary, personnel interviews.
