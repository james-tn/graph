## PREAMBLE & RECITALS

This AMENDMENT TO SOFTWARE LICENSE, SERVICES, AND SUPPORT AGREEMENT (this “**Amendment**”), Contract Reference No. **AMD-ZEN-202502-522**, is made and entered into as of **November 08, 2025** (the “**Amendment Effective Date**”), by and between:

**Contoso Enterprises, Inc.**, a [jurisdiction of incorporation] corporation, with its principal place of business at **1250 Market Plaza, Suite 900, New York, NY 10019, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Contoso**,” the “**Client**,” or the “**Company**”),  

and  

**Zenith Technologies, LLC**, a [jurisdiction of organization] limited liability company, with its principal place of business at **4800 Innovation Drive, 14th Floor, Brooklyn, NY 11201, USA** (hereinafter referred to as “**Zenith**,” the “**Vendor**,” or the “**Service Provider**”).

### RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into that certain **Software License, Services, and Support Agreement** dated **February 21, 2025** (the “**Parent Agreement**” or “**Master Agreement**”), bearing Contract Reference No. **SLA-ZEN-202502-413** and internal identifier **contract_413**, pursuant to which Zenith licenses to Contoso the **ZenithOps Suite** and provides related implementation, integration, hosting, and support services (as more fully described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement);

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement established, among other things, (i) a **Contract Value** of **US$436,978** pursuant to **Section 3.1.1**, (ii) a license scope of up to **250 named Authorized Users** pursuant to **Section 2.2.2**, (iii) an **Initial Term** through **November 27, 2028** pursuant to **Section 4.1.1**, (iv) payment terms of **Net 45** in **Section 3.3.2**, and (v) service-level obligations including a **99.9% Availability Commitment** under **Article XII**;

WHEREAS, Contoso now desires to (i) expand its license to the **ZenithOps Suite** and related modules, (ii) procure additional professional and managed services, (iii) extend and align subscription and service terms through **October 28, 2029**, and (iv) modify certain payment, risk allocation, and operational terms set forth in the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, Zenith is willing to provide such additional licenses and services, and to modify certain terms of the Parent Agreement, on the enhanced, high‑risk, Vendor‑favorable terms and conditions set forth in this Amendment, including uncapped liability for Contoso, extended termination notice periods, and strengthened intellectual property ownership and usage restrictions;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to document their agreements regarding these modifications, clarifications, and additional obligations;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged, **the Parties hereby agree as follows**:

## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES AND LICENSE EXPANSION

### 2.1 Relationship to Parent Agreement

2.1.1 This Amendment **modifies**, **supplements**, and is **entered into pursuant to** the Parent Agreement (Ref: SLA-ZEN-202502-413, contract_413). Except as expressly modified herein, all terms and conditions of the Parent Agreement shall continue in full force and effect and shall apply to the additional licenses, services, and obligations set out in this Amendment.

2.1.2 For the avoidance of doubt, the Software license grant in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement (non-exclusive, non-transferable, non-sublicensable rights to access and use the hosted **ZenithOps Suite** for Contoso’s internal business operations) remains in effect, and this Amendment expands its scope quantitatively and qualitatively as set out herein.

### 2.2 Extended Subscription Term and Alignment

2.2.1 The Parties acknowledge that **Section 4.1.1** of the Parent Agreement established an Initial Term through **November 27, 2028**. The Parties hereby agree that for the licenses and services covered by this Amendment, the Subscription Term is extended and aligned to run through **October 28, 2029** (“Extended Subscription Term”), without limiting or shortening the Initial Term under the Parent Agreement.

2.2.2 The Extended Subscription Term shall apply to:

  (a) all modules of the **ZenithOps Suite** currently licensed by Contoso under the Parent Agreement (including Analytics, Workflow, Integration Hub, and Administration Console, as referenced in **Section 1.12**);  

  (b) any additional modules specifically identified in this Amendment or in a subsequent SOW executed pursuant hereto; and  

  (c) the Premium SLA Enhancements described in **Article XII**.

2.2.3 For avoidance of doubt, the automatic renewal provisions set forth in **Section 4.1.2** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply following the expiry of the Extended Subscription Term, unless the Parent Agreement or this Amendment is earlier terminated in accordance with **Article IV** of the Parent Agreement or **Article IV** of this Amendment.

### 2.3 Expansion of Authorized Users and Usage

2.3.1 **Modification of Section 2.2.2 (Authorized User Limit).** The Parties hereby amend **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent Agreement solely with respect to the license scope covered by this Amendment. The limit of **250 named Authorized Users** is hereby increased to **1,250 named Authorized Users** (inclusive of the original 250 users) for the Extended Subscription Term.

2.3.2 Any use beyond **1,250 named Authorized Users** shall require Contoso to purchase additional licenses at Zenith’s then-current rate schedule, as may be further described in a Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII**.

2.3.3 Authorized Users shall include employees, contractors, and, subject to Zenith’s prior written consent, limited access for Contoso’s business partners for collaboration purposes, provided that all such users act solely on behalf of Contoso and are bound by obligations no less protective than those in the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

### 2.4 Expanded Services – Description and Phasing

2.4.1 In addition to the professional Services described in **Article II (particularly Section 2.3)** and **Exhibit A (Initial Implementation SOW)** of the Parent Agreement, Zenith shall provide the following Expanded Services under this Amendment:

  (a) **Advanced Analytics Enablement** – Design, configuration, and deployment of advanced analytics dashboards, predictive models, and executive-level reporting within the Analytics module of ZenithOps Suite, with at least ten (10) custom dashboard templates tailored to Contoso’s key performance indicators and business unit requirements.  

  (b) **Managed Integration Operations (“MIO”)** – Ongoing management, monitoring, and optimization of integrations between the Software and Contoso’s CRM, ERP, identity management, and data warehouse systems, including creation of new integration workflows and continuous tuning of existing integrations for performance and reliability.  

  (c) **Business Process Optimization** – Regular review (no less than quarterly) of Contoso’s workflow configurations within the Workflow module to streamline processes, reduce cycle times, and implement automation rules consistent with Contoso’s governance policies.  

  (d) **Enhanced Training & Adoption Services** – Development and delivery of tailored training programs for different user roles (e.g., administrators, analysts, end-users) and the creation of customized training materials (including recorded webinars, quick reference guides, and scenario-based exercises) to increase adoption and effective utilization of the Software.  

  (e) **Premium Support and Advisory** – Provision of a designated technical account manager, quarterly roadmap and performance review sessions, proactive monitoring, and early access briefings regarding new ZenithOps Suite features and releases.

2.4.2 **Phasing and Timelines.** The Expanded Services shall be performed in phases that build upon the project phases described in **Section 2.4.1** of the Parent Agreement, with the following additional phases:

  (a) **Phase V – Advanced Analytics & KPI Alignment (estimated 8 weeks):** Requirements workshops, KPI definition, dashboard design, and initial deployment in a staging environment;  

  (b) **Phase VI – Managed Integration Launch (estimated 12 weeks):** Transition of existing integrations into Zenith’s managed operations framework, implementation of monitoring dashboards, and handover to Zenith’s MIO team;  

  (c) **Phase VII – Optimization & Adoption (ongoing throughout the Amendment Term):** Quarterly optimization cycles, training updates, and adoption assessments;  

  (d) **Phase VIII – Strategic Review & Roadmap (semi-annual):** Semi-annual executive-level strategic sessions to review usage metrics, performance, incidents, and roadmap alignment.

2.4.3 All timelines specified in this Section 2.4 are estimates and subject to adjustment pursuant to the change management process under **Article XIII** and the Parties’ mutual written agreement, consistent with **Section 2.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4.4 Contoso shall continue to comply with its obligations under **Section 2.4.3** of the Parent Agreement (timely access, approvals, project manager designation) and shall designate an executive sponsor to participate in the semi-annual strategic review sessions described above.

### 2.5 Performance Standards

2.5.1 Zenith shall perform the Expanded Services in accordance with the professional standards set forth in **Section 2.5** and **Section 7.2** of the Parent Agreement, using personnel with appropriate skills and experience for enterprise-grade software support and managed services.

2.5.2 The Parties acknowledge and reaffirm the dependencies and limitations stated in **Section 2.5.3** of the Parent Agreement, including Contoso’s responsibility for the accuracy of its data and the performance of its internal systems and third-party services.

2.5.3 Zenith shall use commercially reasonable efforts to ensure that the Enhanced Services are aligned with the **Service Level Agreement** obligations described in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement and as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS UNDER THE AMENDMENT

### 3.1 Amendment Contract Value

3.1.1 The total estimated contract value for the licenses and Expanded Services provided under this Amendment (the **“Amendment Contract Value”**) is **US$1,961,687**, which is in addition to, and not in substitution for, the **Contract Value** of US$436,978 set forth in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 The Amendment Contract Value comprises the following components (illustrative and subject to applicable SOWs):

  (a) **Software Subscription Expansion Fees:** US$1,200,000 for the Extended Subscription Term (November 08, 2025 – October 28, 2029) covering the increase from 250 to 1,250 named Authorized Users and Extended Subscription Term for all modules;  

  (b) **Expanded Services and Managed Operations Fees:** US$600,000 for Advanced Analytics Enablement, Managed Integration Operations, Business Process Optimization, and Premium Support and Advisory services;  

  (c) **Premium SLA Enhancements Fees:** US$161,687 for additional uptime commitments, accelerated response times, and expanded support coverage as described in **Article XII**.

3.1.3 For avoidance of doubt, the Amendment Contract Value includes only those elements expressly referenced herein. Any additional services or license expansions requested by Contoso shall be subject to additional Fees to be documented in a Change Order pursuant to **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

### 3.2 Fee Schedule and Milestones

3.2.1 Unless otherwise agreed in a specific SOW executed under this Amendment, Zenith shall invoice the Amendment Contract Value according to the following schedule:

  (a) **Twenty-five percent (25%)** of the Software Subscription Expansion Fees (US$300,000) upon the Amendment Effective Date;  

  (b) **Seventy-five percent (75%)** of the Software Subscription Expansion Fees (US$900,000) in equal quarterly installments over the Extended Subscription Term;  

  (c) **Forty percent (40%)** of the Expanded Services and Managed Operations Fees (US$240,000) upon commencement of Phase V – Advanced Analytics & KPI Alignment;  

  (d) **Thirty percent (30%)** of the Expanded Services and Managed Operations Fees (US$180,000) upon commencement of Phase VI – Managed Integration Launch;  

  (e) **Thirty percent (30%)** of the Expanded Services and Managed Operations Fees (US$180,000) in equal annual installments over the remainder of the Amendment Term;  

  (f) Premium SLA Enhancement Fees (US$161,687) in equal annual installments, invoiced in advance at the beginning of each contract year during the Amendment Term.

3.2.2 The invoicing structure set forth above is in addition to the invoicing schedule for the original Contract Value under **Section 3.3.1** of the Parent Agreement and does not alter or reduce any amounts owed under the Parent Agreement.

### 3.3 Payment Terms; Net 60

3.3.1 **Modification of Section 3.3.2 (Payment Terms).** Solely with respect to invoices issued under this Amendment, the payment terms of **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent Agreement (“Net 45”) are hereby superseded and replaced with **Net 60**. All undisputed amounts invoiced under this Amendment shall be due and payable by Contoso within **sixty (60) days** from the date of the invoice.

3.3.2 All other payment mechanics in **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement, including currency (U.S. Dollars), permitted payment methods (wire transfer, ACH, or other mutually agreed method), and account designation by Zenith, remain in effect.

### 3.4 Taxes

3.4.1 The provisions of **Section 3.4** of the Parent Agreement (Taxes) shall apply in full to all Fees under this Amendment. All Fees set forth in this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, and Contoso shall be responsible for all applicable Taxes, except for taxes on Zenith’s net income, property, or employees.

### 3.5 Late Payments and Remedies

3.5.1 The interest and suspension rights set forth in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement apply to any undisputed amount not paid when due under this Amendment, except that Zenith may, following written notice to Contoso and a cure period of ten (10) Business Days, suspend any or all Expanded Services and Premium SLA Enhancements until all overdue amounts and applicable interest are paid in full.

3.5.2 Such suspension shall be without prejudice to Zenith’s rights to terminate this Amendment or the Parent Agreement for non-payment under **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.3** of this Amendment.

### 3.6 Expenses

3.6.1 The expense reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement apply to travel and out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with on-site Expanded Services under this Amendment. Contoso shall reimburse pre-approved, reasonable, and documented out-of-pocket expenses within **Net 60** days of the corresponding invoice.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION OF AMENDMENT

### 4.1 Amendment Term

4.1.1 This Amendment shall commence on the Amendment Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV**, shall remain in effect until **October 28, 2029** (the “Amendment Term”).

### 4.2 Relationship to Parent Agreement Term

4.2.1 This Amendment does not alter the **Initial Term** and Renewal Term provisions of **Section 4.1** of the Parent Agreement, except that the licenses and services added or expanded under this Amendment shall, by mutual agreement, continue through the Amendment Term.

4.2.2 If the Parent Agreement is renewed beyond the Amendment Term, the Parties may negotiate additional amendments or SOWs to extend some or all of the Expanded Services and related terms.

### 4.3 Termination for Convenience

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Amendment for convenience upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to the other Party. For the avoidance of doubt, this is a modification and superseding of the **sixty (60) days’** termination-for-convenience notice period in **Section 4.2.1** of the Parent Agreement, but **only with respect to this Amendment and the Expanded Services and license expansions set forth herein**.

4.3.2 Unless otherwise mutually agreed in writing, termination of this Amendment for convenience shall not terminate the Parent Agreement itself, and Contoso shall remain obligated to pay all Fees accrued or committed under this Amendment through the effective date of termination.

### 4.4 Termination for Cause

4.4.1 The termination for cause provisions in **Section 4.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. Either Party may terminate this Amendment immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Amendment and fails to cure within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach.

4.4.2 For the avoidance of doubt, a material breach of the Parent Agreement may be deemed a material breach of this Amendment, and a material breach of this Amendment may, at the non-breaching Party’s option, be treated as a material breach of the Parent Agreement.

### 4.5 Effect of Termination; Relationship to Parent Agreement Termination

4.5.1 Upon any termination or expiration of this Amendment:

  (a) all license expansions granted under this Amendment (including Additional Authorized Users and Extended Subscription Term rights) shall automatically terminate, and Contoso’s license shall revert to the scope provided in the Parent Agreement immediately prior to the Amendment Effective Date, unless otherwise agreed in writing;  

  (b) all obligations regarding Premium SLA Enhancements and Expanded Services shall cease, except for payment obligations accrued prior to the effective date of termination; and  

  (c) Contoso shall promptly pay all Fees and expenses accrued and unpaid under this Amendment as of the effective date of termination or expiration.

4.5.2 If the Parent Agreement is terminated for any reason under **Article IV** thereof, this Amendment shall automatically and concurrently terminate, without separate notice, and the effects described in **Section 4.4** of the Parent Agreement and **Section 4.5.1** of this Amendment shall apply.

4.5.3 In the event Contoso validly terminates the Parent Agreement for Zenith’s uncured material breach pursuant to **Section 4.3.1(a)** of the Parent Agreement, Zenith shall refund to Contoso a pro rata portion of any prepaid, unused Fees paid under this Amendment for the period following the effective date of termination, consistent with **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent Agreement.

### 4.6 Transition Assistance

4.6.1 The Transition Assistance obligations of Zenith under **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement shall extend to the Expanded Services and any additional data or configurations implemented under this Amendment. Upon Contoso’s written request, Zenith shall provide Transition Assistance for up to thirty (30) days following termination or expiration of this Amendment, subject to the terms and charges described in **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement.

4.6.2 The Parties may agree to additional Transition Assistance services under a separate fee arrangement.

### 4.7 Survival

4.7.1 The provisions of **Sections 3.1–3.6** (to the extent of accrued payment obligations), **4.5–4.7**, and Articles V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XII (to the extent of any accrued service credits or obligations), XIV, XV, and XVI of this Amendment shall survive any termination or expiration of this Amendment, in addition to the survival provisions of **Section 4.6** of the Parent Agreement.

## ARTICLE IX  
## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY – MODIFICATION FOR HIGH-RISK PROFILE

9.1 **Modification of Article IX (Limitation of Liability).** The Parties acknowledge that **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement sets forth certain limitations on liability, including exclusion of consequential damages and a monetary cap equal to two (2) times the Fees paid or payable in the prior twelve (12) months, subject to specified exceptions.

9.2 Solely with respect to Claims, Losses, and liabilities arising out of or relating to this Amendment, the Parties hereby agree as follows:

  (a) **No Cap on Contoso’s Liability.** Contoso’s liability to Zenith for any and all Claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment, whether in contract, tort (including negligence), or otherwise, shall be **uncapped** and shall not be subject to the monetary limitation set forth in **Section 9.2.1** of the Parent Agreement.  

  (b) **Zenith Liability Cap Maintained.** Zenith’s liability to Contoso for any Claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment shall remain subject to the cap set forth in **Section 9.2.1** of the Parent Agreement (two (2) times the Fees paid or payable by Contoso to Zenith under the Parent Agreement and this Amendment during the twelve (12) month period immediately preceding the event giving rise to the Claim), including the exceptions specified therein.  

  (c) **Consequential and Special Damages Not Excluded.** To reflect the High-Risk Profile and the Parties’ agreement, the **exclusion of consequential, indirect, incidental, and special damages** in **Section 9.1.1** of the Parent Agreement **shall not apply** to Claims arising out of or relating to this Amendment. Each Party may be held liable for consequential, indirect, incidental, and special damages, including loss of profits, loss of revenue, loss of data, or loss of business opportunity, provided that in the case of Zenith such liability remains subject to the cap described in subsection (b) above.  

  (d) **Acknowledgment.** The Parties acknowledge that the fee structure and other commercial terms of this Amendment have been negotiated in consideration of the modified risk allocation in this Article IX.

9.3 Except as expressly modified in this Article IX with respect to this Amendment, the limitation of liability provisions of **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement remain in full force and effect.
