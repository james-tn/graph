## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES/WORK

### 2.1 General

2.1.1 This Addendum **supplements and amends Article II (Scope of Services/Work)** of the Parent SOW. Except as expressly modified in this **Article II**, the Services described in **Article II** of the Parent SOW remain in full force and effect.

2.1.2 Vendor shall provide to Client the **Addendum Services**, which shall (a) extend the managed services originally defined as **Phase 4 – Managed Services and Enhancements (Months 13–24)** in **Section 2.3.4** of the Parent SOW through the Addendum Term, (b) expand the Platform’s functional and data coverage across additional business domains, and (c) implement enhanced analytics, governance, and optimization capabilities as further detailed herein.

2.1.3 The Addendum Services are **client‑friendly and low‑risk in nature**, and Vendor acknowledges that the obligations and performance standards set forth in the Parent SOW, including **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** and **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)**, apply equally to the Addendum Services, except where enhanced or supplemented in this Addendum.

### 2.2 Relationship to Parent SOW

2.2.1 The Platform, architecture, integrations, and data model implemented under **Phase 1 – Discovery and Design (Section 2.3.1)**, **Phase 2 – Build and Configuration (Section 2.3.2)**, and **Phase 3 – Testing, Training, and Go-Live (Section 2.3.3)** of the Parent SOW shall constitute the technical foundation for the Addendum Services. Vendor shall ensure that the Addendum Services remain consistent with the **Solution Design Document**, **Use Cases**, and **Specifications** defined under the Parent SOW, except to the extent modified through mutually agreed Change Orders pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent SOW and **Article XIII** of this Addendum.

2.2.2 The Addendum Services shall be deemed part of the “Services” and the Addendum Deliverables shall be deemed “Deliverables” and “Work Product” under the Parent SOW, and shall be subject to the applicable provisions therein, including **Section 2.6 (Deliverables and Acceptance)**, **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights)**, and **Article VII (Representations and Warranties)**.

2.2.3 For avoidance of doubt, the Addendum does **not** modify the total Contract Value of the Parent SOW stated in **Section 3.1.1** thereof. Instead, this Addendum establishes a **separate Addendum Contract Value of US $923,144**, as described in **Article III** below.

### 2.3 Components of Addendum Services

2.3.1 **Extended Managed Services**

(a) Vendor shall continue to provide Level 2 and Level 3 support for the Platform in accordance with **Article XII (Service Level Agreements)** of the Parent SOW, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Addendum, for the Addendum Term.

(b) The extended managed services shall include, at a minimum:

  - Incident management in accordance with the priority definitions and response/resolution targets in **Section 12.3** of the Parent SOW, as enhanced by **Section 12.3** of this Addendum.  
  - Proactive monitoring of system performance, capacity, availability, and security events, with monthly trend analysis reports.  
  - Operational runbook maintenance and updates, building on the runbooks and procedures referenced in **Section 2.6.1(g)** of the Parent SOW.  
  - Quarterly service review meetings with Client stakeholders to assess performance, review Service Levels, discuss upcoming changes, and identify continuous improvement initiatives.

(c) Vendor shall ensure continuity of Key Personnel designated for the Parent SOW, consistent with the definition of **“Key Personnel” in Section 1.11** of the Parent SOW. Any replacement of Key Personnel assigned to the Addendum Services shall require Client’s prior written consent, which shall not be unreasonably withheld, delayed, or conditioned.

2.3.2 **Platform Expansion to Additional Business Domains**

(a) Vendor shall design, configure, and deploy additional data models, integrations, and analytics content for **at least three (3) additional business domains** beyond the initial domains (Sales, Finance, Operations) referenced in **Section 2.3.2(e)** of the Parent SOW, which may include, by way of example, Human Resources, Supply Chain, and Customer Support, as identified by Client.

(b) For each additional business domain, Vendor shall:

  - Conduct targeted discovery workshops (building upon **Phase 1** activities in **Section 2.3.1**) to capture domain-specific requirements, data sources, and KPIs.  
  - Update or extend the Solution Design Document to reflect domain-specific data models and integration patterns.  
  - Implement required data ingestion pipelines and transformation logic, consistent with the data quality and governance standards previously agreed.  
  - Develop standardized dashboards, reports, and semantic models tailored to the business domain, with defined acceptance criteria and performance benchmarks.

2.3.3 **Advanced Analytics and Data Science Enablement**

(a) Vendor shall support the enablement of advanced analytics and data science workloads on the Platform, which may include integration with data science tools, feature stores, or machine learning frameworks, subject to mutually agreed Specifications.

(b) Such services may encompass:

  - Provisioning and configuration of dedicated environments and compute resources suitable for data science experimentation and productionization.  
  - Implementation of standardized data preparation pipelines and model deployment processes.  
  - Development of reusable templates for predictive models and advanced statistical analyses.

2.3.4 **Governance, Security, and Compliance Enhancements**

(a) In alignment with Client’s security and compliance requirements referenced in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent SOW and the data protection obligations in **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)**, Vendor shall implement additional governance and security capabilities, including:

  - Role-based access control refinements, segregation of duties, and attribute-based access policies.  
  - Extended audit logging and monitoring to support Regulatory Reporting Use Cases.  
  - Data cataloging, lineage, and classification enhancements to improve transparency and compliance with Data Protection Laws as defined in **Section 1.5** of the Parent SOW.

(b) Vendor shall collaborate with Client’s information security and compliance teams to prioritize and implement these enhancements according to a mutually agreed roadmap.

### 2.4 Addendum Deliverables

Without limiting the generality of the term “Deliverables” in **Section 1.6** of the Parent SOW, the Addendum Deliverables shall include, at a minimum:

(a) **Updated Solution Design Annexes** covering the additional business domains and advanced analytics components;  
(b) **Domain-Specific Data Models and Integration Specifications** for each new business domain;  
(c) **Enhanced Dashboards, Reports, and Data Models** for the newly onboarded domains and advanced analytics solutions;  
(d) **Updated Operational Runbooks and Support Procedures**, building on those referenced in **Section 2.6.1(g)** of the Parent SOW;  
(e) **Governance and Security Configuration Documentation**, including role definitions, access matrices, and audit logging specifications;  
(f) **Quarterly Performance and Improvement Reports** addressing Service Levels, incident trends, capacity planning, and recommended optimizations; and  
(g) Any other documentation or artifacts expressly identified as deliverables in mutually agreed project plans or Change Orders executed pursuant to **Article XIII** of the Parent SOW and this Addendum.

### 2.5 Milestones and Timeline for Addendum

2.5.1 The Parties agree to the following initial **Addendum Milestones**, subject to refinement by mutual written agreement and to adjustment through Change Orders in accordance with **Article XIII**:

- **Addendum Milestone A1 – Platform Expansion Design Sign-Off**: Completion and Client acceptance of updated Solution Design annexes for at least two additional business domains, within 90 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
- **Addendum Milestone A2 – First Expansion Domain Go-Live**: Completion of build, testing, and go-live for the first additional domain (including associated analytics content), within 210 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
- **Addendum Milestone A3 – Completion of Additional Domains**: Completion of build, testing, and go-live for all additional domains agreed under this Addendum, within 420 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
- **Addendum Milestone A4 – Advanced Analytics Enablement**: Availability of at least one productionized advanced analytics use case supported by the Platform, within 540 days of the Addendum Effective Date.  
- **Addendum Milestone A5 – Annual Governance and Optimization Review**: Delivery of a comprehensive review report covering governance, security, capacity, and optimization recommendations on or before each anniversary of the Addendum Effective Date throughout the Addendum Term.

2.5.2 Acceptance of Addendum Deliverables and Milestones shall follow the process and timelines set forth in **Section 2.6 (Deliverables and Acceptance)** of the Parent SOW, provided that references therein to a twenty (20) Business Day review period shall continue to apply unless otherwise agreed in writing for specific Deliverables.

### 2.6 Performance Standards

2.6.1 Vendor shall perform the Addendum Services in accordance with:

(a) The performance standards in **Section 2.5 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent SOW;  
(b) The Parent Service Levels in **Article XII** of the Parent SOW, as supplemented by **Article XII** of this Addendum; and  
(c) Any additional or more stringent performance metrics expressly set forth in this Addendum or in an applicable project plan or Change Order.

2.6.2 Vendor acknowledges that timely delivery, quality, and adherence to Client’s security, compliance, and data protection requirements remain critical, as emphasized in **Section 2.2.2** of the Parent SOW. Any material deviation from agreed performance standards may constitute a material breach, subject to the cure provisions in **Section 4.3 (Termination for Cause)** of this Addendum and the Parent SOW.

## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 3.1 Addendum Contract Value

3.1.1 The Parties agree that the total contract value for the Addendum Services and Addendum Deliverables under this Addendum shall be **Nine Hundred Twenty-Three Thousand One Hundred Forty-Four United States Dollars (US $923,144)** (the “**Addendum Contract Value**”), exclusive of applicable taxes.

3.1.2 The Addendum Contract Value is **separate and in addition to** the Contract Value of **US $2,592,793** stated in **Section 3.1.1** of the Parent SOW. The Parent SOW’s Contingency/Change Budget of US $102,793 (Section 3.2.1(c)) shall remain available for Change Orders under the Parent SOW but shall not be applied to Fees under this Addendum unless expressly agreed in a written Change Order referencing both **SOW-NEX-202405-074** and **ADD-NEX-202405-097**.

### 3.2 Fee Structure for Addendum Services

3.2.1 The Addendum Contract Value shall be comprised of the following components:

(a) **Platform Expansion and Advanced Analytics Enablement:** US $540,000 (fixed‑fee, tied to Milestones A1–A4);  
(b) **Extended and Enhanced Managed Services:** US $323,144 (billed in equal monthly installments over a mutually agreed managed services period within the Addendum Term); and  
(c) **Governance, Compliance, and Optimization Initiatives:** US $60,000 (allocated to annual governance and optimization reviews and related implementation work).

3.2.2 Unless otherwise expressly provided in this Addendum or in a mutually executed Change Order, the above Fees are fixed and not subject to increase during the Addendum Term, consistent with the principle in **Section 3.2.2** of the Parent SOW.

### 3.3 Invoicing and Payment Terms

3.3.1 Vendor shall invoice Client for the Addendum Services as follows:

(a) **Platform Expansion and Advanced Analytics Enablement (US $540,000):**  
   - 20% (US $108,000) upon Client’s acceptance of **Addendum Milestone A1**;  
   - 30% (US $162,000) upon Client’s acceptance of **Addendum Milestone A2**;  
   - 30% (US $162,000) upon Client’s acceptance of **Addendum Milestone A3**;  
   - 20% (US $108,000) upon Client’s acceptance of **Addendum Milestone A4**.

(b) **Extended and Enhanced Managed Services (US $323,144):**  
   - Billed monthly in arrears over the applicable managed services period at a rate to be specified in the initial billing schedule (e.g., thirty-six (36) equal installments of approximately US $8,976.78 per month), commencing upon a mutually agreed “Enhanced Managed Services Start Date” identified in writing by the Parties.

(c) **Governance, Compliance, and Optimization Initiatives (US $60,000):**  
   - Billed annually in arrears in four equal installments of US $15,000 upon Client’s receipt of the annual Governance and Optimization Review report corresponding to **Addendum Milestone A5** for each contract year within the Addendum Term.

3.3.2 **Payment Terms – Net 30.** All undisputed invoices issued under this Addendum shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** from the date of Client’s receipt of such invoice (“**Net 30**”), via wire transfer or other mutually agreed method. This provision supersedes the **Net 45** payment term specified in **Section 3.3.2** of the Parent SOW, but only with respect to invoices issued pursuant to this Addendum.

3.3.3 The dispute and set‑off provisions in **Sections 3.7.1 and 3.7.2** of the Parent SOW shall apply equally to invoices and payments under this Addendum.

### 3.4 Taxes

3.4.1 Fees under this Addendum are exclusive of all sales, use, value‑added, or similar taxes. Client shall be responsible for such taxes imposed by applicable law, consistent with **Section 3.4** of the Parent SOW, except for taxes based on Vendor’s income, property, or employment obligations.

### 3.5 Late Payments

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest from the date due until the date paid at a rate equal to the lesser of (a) **one percent (1%) per month** or (b) the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, consistent with **Section 3.5.1** of the Parent SOW.

3.5.2 Vendor’s right to suspend Services for late payment shall be as set forth in **Section 3.5.2** of the Parent SOW (requiring at least sixty (60) days of undisputed non-payment and notice with a fifteen (15) day cure period), and shall apply mutatis mutandis to the Addendum Services. Vendor acknowledges that, in keeping with the low‑risk, client‑friendly nature of this engagement, suspension shall be a remedy of last resort.

### 3.6 Expenses and Reimbursement

3.6.1 The Fees set forth in this Addendum are inclusive of all ordinary and necessary expenses unless otherwise expressly agreed in writing, consistent with **Section 3.6.1** of the Parent SOW.

3.6.2 Any extraordinary, out‑of‑country, or non-standard travel or other expenses incurred in connection with the Addendum Services must be pre‑approved in writing by Client and shall be reimbursed at cost, subject to Client’s expense policies, in accordance with **Section 3.6.2** of the Parent SOW.

## ARTICLE IV  
## TERM AND TERMINATION

### 4.1 Addendum Term

4.1.1 The **Addendum Term** shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until the Addendum Expiration Date, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this **Article IV** or the Parent SOW.

4.1.2 Any extension of the Addendum Term shall be subject to the Parties’ mutual written agreement, consistent with **Section 4.1.2** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.2 Termination for Convenience

4.2.1 Client may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon **thirty (30) days’** prior written notice to Vendor, without penalty or early termination fee, reflecting the low‑risk, client‑friendly nature of this engagement.

4.2.2 Vendor may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon **sixty (60) days’** prior written notice to Client, provided that Vendor continues to perform the Addendum Services diligently during such notice period and cooperates with Client to facilitate a smooth transition, consistent with **Section 4.2.2** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.3 Termination for Cause

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Addendum immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, or has a receiver appointed over a substantial portion of its assets; or  
(b) ceases to do business in the ordinary course,

as set forth in **Section 4.3.1** of the Parent SOW.

4.3.2 Either Party may terminate this Addendum for a material breach by the other Party, provided that:

(a) the non‑breaching Party provides written notice specifying the breach; and  
(b) the breaching Party fails to cure such breach within **thirty (30) days** after receipt of such notice (or such longer period as the Parties may agree in writing, provided that the breaching Party is diligently pursuing cure), consistent with **Section 4.3.2** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.4 Effect of Termination; Transition Assistance

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum for any reason:

(a) Vendor shall immediately cease all Addendum Services (except as necessary to perform any agreed Transition Assistance Services);  
(b) Client shall pay Vendor for all undisputed Addendum Services properly performed and accepted, and for authorized expenses incurred, through the effective date of termination; and  
(c) each Party shall return or destroy the other Party’s Confidential Information relating to the Addendum Services, in accordance with **Section 5.5 (Return or Destruction)** and **Section 5.5.1** of the Parent SOW, subject to retention rights for archival and backup purposes.

4.4.2 In the event of termination by Client for Vendor’s material breach, Client shall have no obligation to pay for any non‑conforming or unperformed Addendum Services or Addendum Deliverables, consistent with **Section 4.4.2** of the Parent SOW.

4.4.3 At Client’s request, Vendor shall provide **Transition Assistance Services** for up to **ninety (90) days** following termination or expiration of this Addendum, at the time‑and‑materials rates agreed in writing (which shall be reasonable and in line with Vendor’s then‑current rates), to facilitate an orderly transition to Client or a replacement service provider. This obligation is consistent with and supplements **Section 4.5 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent SOW.

### 4.5 Relationship to Parent SOW Termination

4.5.1 This Addendum is expressly made **subject to the Parent SOW**. If the Parent SOW **SOW-NEX-202405-074 (contract_074)** expires or is terminated for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate on the same effective date of expiration or termination, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to preserve certain Addendum Services or obligations beyond the Parent SOW’s termination.

4.5.2 Termination of this Addendum shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent SOW, which shall remain in effect in accordance with its terms unless separately terminated.

### 4.6 Survival

4.6.1 Those provisions of this Addendum which by their nature are intended to survive expiration or termination, including but not limited to **Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), and XVI (General Provisions)**, shall so survive, in addition to any corresponding surviving provisions of the Parent SOW.
