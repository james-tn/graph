### 4.3 Termination for Cause  

4.3.1 Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party:

(a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days after receipt of written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail; or  
(b) becomes insolvent, files for bankruptcy, enters into receivership or liquidation, or ceases to carry on business in the ordinary course.

4.3.2 Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, the following shall constitute material breaches:

(a) material and repeated failure by Nova to meet the Service Levels, subject to the remedies and cure rights specified in Article XII;  
(b) material violation by Nova of its confidentiality or data protection obligations;  
(c) non-payment by Contoso of undisputed Fees for more than sixty (60) days after the due date;  
(d) material infringement by Contoso of Nova’s Intellectual Property Rights, including unauthorized use of the Software.

### 4.4 Effects of Termination  

4.4.1 Upon expiration or termination of this Agreement for any reason:

(a) all licenses granted to Contoso under this Agreement shall automatically terminate, and Contoso shall cease all use of the Software and Documentation;  
(b) Contoso shall promptly delete or destroy all copies of the Software and Documentation in its possession or control (except for backup copies required by law) and certify such deletion or destruction upon Nova’s written request;  
(c) Nova shall return or, at Contoso’s written request, securely destroy all Client Data in its possession or control, subject to Section 11.7 (Data Retention and Deletion); and

4.4.2 Termination of this Agreement shall not relieve either Party of any obligations accrued prior to the effective date of termination, including payment obligations.

12.3.1 Nova shall classify incidents based on severity levels, and shall respond and use commercially reasonable efforts to resolve incidents in accordance with the following indicative timeframes:

- **Severity 2 (High)**: Significant degradation of functionality, partial outage, or major impact with a workaround.  
  - Initial response: within 2 hours;  
  - Target resolution/workaround: within 24 hours.

- **Severity 3 (Medium)**: Non-critical issues, minor impact, or cosmetic defects.  
  - Initial response: within 8 business hours;  
  - Target resolution/workaround: within 5 Business Days.

- **Severity 4 (Low)**: General inquiries, enhancements, or documentation requests.
