## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Addendum is entered into **pursuant to and in supplementation of** the Parent Agreement (contract_045). The Parties expressly acknowledge that the Services described in **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, including the three phases identified in **Section 2.2** (Phase 1 – Assessment and Architecture; Phase 2 – Implementation and Migration; and Phase 3 – Managed Services and Optimization), form the foundational scope of work.

2.1.2 This Addendum specifically **supplements and extends** **Phase 3 – Managed Services and Optimization (Months 19 – End of Term)** as defined in **Section 2.2.3** of the Parent Agreement by adding Enhanced Managed Services that provide deeper security operations, business continuity, cost optimization, and governance over the hybrid cloud environment implemented under the Original SOW.

2.1.3 For clarity, unless otherwise expressly stated, nothing in this Addendum shall diminish or relieve Vendor of its obligations under **Section 2.4 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent Agreement, which are hereby incorporated by reference and shall equally apply to all Services provided under this Addendum.

2.2 **Enhanced Managed Services Description.**

2.2.1 **Advanced Security Operations & Threat Hunting.**  
(a) Vendor shall extend the 24x7 monitoring obligations described in **Section 2.2.3(a)** of the Parent Agreement to include proactive threat hunting, advanced correlation, and behavioral analytics across the hybrid cloud infrastructure, endpoints, and identity platforms.  
(b) Vendor shall integrate additional threat intelligence sources into Client’s SIEM and incident response framework, as originally contemplated in **Section 2.2.1(c)** of the Parent Agreement.  
(c) Vendor shall provide monthly **Threat Intelligence and Incident Analysis Reports** (as part of the Extended Deliverables) summarizing significant security events, root cause analyses for P1/P2 security incidents, and recommended remediation and hardening measures.

2.2.2 **Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Enhancement.**  
(a) Building upon the high-availability and disaster recovery design originally contemplated in **Section 2.2.1(c)** (Target-State Hybrid Cloud Architecture), Vendor shall design, test, and maintain an enhanced disaster recovery (DR) and business continuity framework covering critical workloads hosted in the hybrid cloud environment.  
(b) Vendor shall perform at least one (1) **annual DR simulation exercise** and one (1) **table-top exercise** per Contract Year during the term of this Addendum, documenting findings, gaps, and recommended improvements.  
(c) Vendor shall maintain up-to-date DR runbooks as part of the **Runbooks and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)** described in **Section 2.3.5** of the Parent Agreement, incorporating all changes required for the Enhanced Managed Services.

2.2.3 **FinOps and Cost Optimization Services.**  
(a) In addition to the quarterly architecture and cost optimization reviews described in **Section 2.2.3(e)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall provide a dedicated **Cloud Financial Operations (FinOps)** function under this Addendum.  
(b) Such FinOps services shall include detailed monthly cost breakdowns by business unit, workload, and environment; recommendations for rightsizing, reserved instances/commitments, and schedule-based scaling; and identification of underutilized resources.  
(c) Vendor shall provide quarterly **Cost Optimization Roadmaps** outlining prioritized actions, projected savings, and timelines, along with tracking of savings realized against baseline cost models defined jointly by the Parties.

2.2.4 **Regulatory Compliance & Governance Support.**  
(a) Vendor shall assist Client in maintaining compliance with data protection and security frameworks relevant to the hybrid environment, including but not limited to GDPR, CCPA, and any sector-specific standards communicated in writing by Client.  
(b) Vendor shall conduct semi-annual **Security & Compliance Posture Assessments** covering configuration baselines, identity and access controls, logging and monitoring, and data protection measures.  
(c) Vendor shall support Client’s audit and attestation activities (e.g., ISO 27001, SOC 2, or similar), by providing necessary technical evidence, architecture documentation, and control mappings, subject to reasonable limits on hours and scope to be mutually agreed in writing.

2.2.5 **Operational Excellence & Automation.**  
(a) Vendor shall enhance the Infrastructure-as-Code (IaC) framework described in **Section 2.2.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement to include automated guardrails for security, compliance, and tagging standards.  
(b) Vendor shall develop and maintain automated workflows for incident remediation of common security and operational events (e.g., auto-isolation of compromised hosts, automated patching of critical vulnerabilities in defined timeframes).  
(c) Vendor shall provide Client with quarterly **Automation Impact Reports** summarizing the number of automated remediations executed, time-to-resolution improvements, and any residual manual interventions required.

2.2.6 **Enhanced Service Governance.**  
(a) Vendor shall conduct **monthly executive governance meetings** (in addition to operational service reviews described in **Section 2.2.3(d)** of the Parent Agreement) with designated senior stakeholders from both Parties, focusing on risk management, strategic roadmap alignment, and portfolio-level decisions.  
(b) Vendor shall provide a **Hybrid Cloud Risk Register** as an Extended Deliverable, updated at least quarterly, identifying key operational, security, compliance, and financial risks, with associated mitigation actions.

2.3 **Extended Deliverables.**  

Without limiting Vendor’s obligations under the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall deliver at least the following Extended Deliverables under this Addendum:

2.3.1 **Threat Intelligence and Incident Analysis Reports** (monthly).  

2.3.2 **Business Continuity & DR Runbook Updates** (at least annually or after any material change).  

2.3.3 **DR Simulation and Table-Top Exercise Reports**, including findings and recommendations (annually).  

2.3.4 **FinOps Monthly Cost Dashboards** and **Quarterly Cost Optimization Roadmaps**.  

2.3.5 **Semi-Annual Security & Compliance Posture Assessment Reports**.  

2.3.6 **Audit Support Documentation Package**, including technical evidence extracts, architecture and data flow diagrams, configuration baselines, and control mapping matrices, as reasonably requested by Client.  

2.3.7 **Automation Impact Reports** (quarterly).  

2.3.8 **Hybrid Cloud Risk Register** (updated quarterly).  

2.3.9 Any additional documentation reasonably necessary for Client to consume, manage, and audit the Enhanced Managed Services, consistent with the documentation standards in **Section 2.3 (Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Performance Standards.**  

2.4.1 Vendor shall perform the Enhanced Managed Services (a) in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with generally accepted industry standards, (b) in compliance with the Performance Standards and Service Levels set forth in **Article XII of the Parent Agreement** and **Article XII of this Addendum**, (c) using personnel with appropriate skills, experience, and qualifications as contemplated in **Section 2.4(c)** of the Parent Agreement, and (d) in compliance with Applicable Laws, including data protection regulations, as set forth in **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.4.2 The Extended Service Levels set forth in **Article XII** herein shall augment, and not replace, the Service Levels defined in **Article XII** of the Parent Agreement. In the event of conflict, the more stringent or higher standard shall apply to the specific Enhanced Managed Service function at issue.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities.**  

2.5.1 In addition to the responsibilities in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement, Client shall:  
(a) provide Vendor with timely access to updated security policies, compliance requirements, and any changes in regulatory or internal control frameworks that may impact the Enhanced Managed Services;  
(b) ensure that all business units utilizing the hybrid environment adhere to tagging, deployment, and governance standards reasonably required by Vendor to deliver FinOps and risk reporting; and  
(c) designate an executive-level sponsor and operational lead for the governance meetings referenced in **Section 2.2.6(a)**.

2.5.2 Failure by Client to fulfill the above responsibilities in a timely manner may entitle Vendor to schedule and fee adjustments via the Change Management process established under **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

2.6 **Assumptions and Dependencies.**  

2.6.1 The assumptions and dependencies described in **Section 2.6** of the Parent Agreement apply equally to the Enhanced Managed Services.  

2.6.2 In addition, the following assumptions and dependencies apply:  
(a) Required third-party security tools (e.g., SIEM, EDR, cloud security posture management tools) and cloud platform features will be licensed and provisioned by Client unless otherwise agreed in writing;  
(b) Client will provide timely approvals for DR test schedules and any change windows required for DR simulations;  
(c) Client will maintain up-to-date asset inventories and CMDB records to enable accurate FinOps and risk analysis.

2.6.3 To the extent any assumption or dependency is not met and such failure materially impacts Vendor’s ability to perform the Enhanced Managed Services, Vendor shall be entitled to an equitable adjustment to the timelines and Fees, to be documented in a **Change Order** in accordance with **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Addendum.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.**  

3.1.1 The total contract value for the Enhanced Managed Services and Extended Deliverables under this Addendum shall be **US$4,230,457** (the "**Addendum Contract Value**"), exclusive of any applicable taxes. This amount is in addition to, and does not reduce or replace, the **Contract Value of US$4,977,036** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent Agreement.

3.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that the Addendum Contract Value reflects the **high-risk** classification of the engagement, the uncapped liability structure under **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement, and the specialized nature of the Enhanced Managed Services.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**  

The Addendum Contract Value shall consist of the following fee components:

3.2.1 **One-Time Enhancement and Setup Fees:**  

(a) A fixed fee of **US$780,000** for the design, configuration, and initial rollout of the Enhanced Managed Services (including DR enhancements, automation development, FinOps baseline set-up, and security tooling integrations).  
(b) The one-time fee shall be invoiced as follows:  
  - 30% (US$234,000) upon the Addendum Effective Date;  
  - 40% (US$312,000) upon completion and Client’s not-unreasonably-withheld acceptance of the initial DR and security enhancements (including updated DR runbooks and initial automation workflows);  
  - 30% (US$234,000) upon completion of the first full DR simulation and issuance of associated reports under this Addendum.

3.2.2 **Recurring Enhanced Managed Services Fees:**  

(a) Recurring fees totaling **US$3,450,457** over the term of this Addendum (Addendum Effective Date to Addendum Expiration Date).  
(b) Such recurring fees shall be payable in equal monthly installments, resulting in a monthly fee of **US$143,769** (rounded to the nearest dollar, with any minor adjustment to be reconciled in the final invoice).  
(c) Recurring fees shall commence on the later of: (i) 1 January 2026; or (ii) the date on which Vendor materially commences delivery of Enhanced Managed Services (excluding initial setup), as confirmed by written notice from Vendor.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  

3.3.1 **Invoices.** Vendor shall issue invoices for the one-time enhancement fees in accordance with **Section 3.2.1** above and for recurring Enhanced Managed Services fees in accordance with **Section 3.2.2** above.  

3.3.2 **Payment Deadline – Net 60.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.4.2 (Net 30)** of the Parent Agreement, the Parties hereby expressly agree that, for all invoices issued pursuant to this Addendum, **Client shall pay all undisputed amounts within sixty (60) calendar days of the invoice date ("Net 60")**. The Net 30 term in the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply to invoices issued solely under the Parent Agreement, unless otherwise expressly modified by a subsequent written agreement.

3.3.3 **Disputed Amounts.** The dispute process in **Section 3.4.3** of the Parent Agreement shall apply, except that Client shall notify Vendor of any dispute within **twenty (20) calendar days** of receipt of the relevant invoice. Client shall pay all undisputed portions in accordance with **Section 3.3.2** above. The Parties shall act in good faith to resolve disputes promptly.

3.4 **Late Payment.**  

3.4.1 The late payment provisions in **Section 3.5** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Addendum, such that any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at a rate of **one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month** or the maximum rate allowed by Applicable Law, whichever is less.

3.4.2 Vendor may, upon fifteen (15) days’ written notice and without liability, suspend the Enhanced Managed Services if undisputed amounts under this Addendum remain unpaid for more than thirty (30) days after the due date, without prejudice to Vendor’s right to suspend other Services under the Parent Agreement in accordance with **Section 3.5** thereof.

3.5 **Expenses.**  

3.5.1 The expense reimbursement provisions in **Section 3.3** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Addendum. To the extent Vendor incurs additional travel or living expenses specifically in connection with DR tests, on-site audits, or governance workshops, such expenses shall be reimbursable, provided they are pre-approved and documented in accordance with Client’s travel and expense policy.

3.6 **Taxes.**  

3.6.1 The tax provisions described in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement shall apply in full to the Fees under this Addendum. Client shall be responsible for all applicable taxes other than taxes based on Vendor’s net income.

3.7 **No Set-Off.**  

3.7.1 The no set-off provision in **Section 3.7** of the Parent Agreement applies to Fees under this Addendum, unless otherwise agreed in a signed Change Order.
