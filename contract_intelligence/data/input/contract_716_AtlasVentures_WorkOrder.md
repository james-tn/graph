## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Work Order is a “Statement of Work” for purposes of the Parent Agreement. It is expressly **executed under and subject to** the terms of the Parent Agreement (see Section 2.1 and Section 1.17 thereof). Without limiting the generality of the foregoing:

- The categories of Services described in Article II of the Parent Agreement (Strategic Advisory Services, Digital Transformation & Technology Consulting, Operational Improvement Services, Implementation Support and PMO Services, and Training and Knowledge Transfer) are hereby invoked and further specified for Phase 2 of the Programme under this Work Order.  
- The performance standards in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, the acceptance procedures in Section 2.7, and the client responsibilities in Section 2.6 are expressly incorporated and applied to the Services described herein.

2.2 **Overview of Phase 2 Services.**  
Atlas shall provide Contoso with comprehensive consulting and advisory services forming **Phase 2** of the Digital Operating Model Transformation Programme. The Phase 2 Services shall include, without limitation:

2.2.1 **Target Operating Model (“TOM”) Design.**  
Building on any prior assessments and strategy work performed under previous SOWs or the Parent Agreement, Atlas shall:

- Develop a detailed future-state operating model for Contoso’s digital business, covering organizational structure, roles and responsibilities, governance forums, KPIs, and decision rights;  
- Identify and document end-to-end digital customer journeys and supporting internal processes;  
- Define interaction models between business units, IT, data, and shared services; and  
- Align the TOM with Contoso’s strategic objectives and growth initiatives as articulated by Contoso’s leadership.

2.2.2 **Technology and Data Architecture Roadmap.**  
Consistent with Section 2.2.2 of the Parent Agreement (Digital Transformation & Technology Consulting), Atlas shall:

- Analyse Contoso’s current-state technology stack and data landscape;  
- Propose a target-state digital architecture (including cloud platforms, integration patterns, data platforms, and analytics capabilities);  
- Develop a prioritized multi-year roadmap for technology and data investments; and  
- Recommend key technology standards and reference architectures.

2.2.3 **Implementation Roadmap and Business Case.**  
Atlas shall:

- Develop a detailed implementation roadmap spanning the Work Order Term and beyond, broken down into phases, releases, and Implementation Workstreams;  
- Quantify the expected benefits (financial and non-financial), including revenue growth, cost savings, and customer experience improvements;  
- Estimate implementation costs and resource requirements;  
- Prepare a consolidated business case, including base-case and upside scenarios, to support Contoso’s internal investment approvals.

2.2.4 **Implementation Workstream Support.**  
Pursuant to Section 2.2.4 of the Parent Agreement (Implementation Support and PMO Services), Atlas shall provide support across several Implementation Workstreams, including:

- **Customer Journeys & Channels:** Design and pilot digital customer journeys, including e-commerce, self-service portals, and mobile applications;  
- **Data & Analytics:** Support the implementation of priority data pipelines, dashboards, and analytics use cases;  
- **Cloud & Platforms:** Provide advisory support for migration planning, vendor selection, and environment design for cloud platforms;  
- **Operating Model & Change:** Assist with role definitions, RACI matrices, operating procedures, and change management activities.

2.2.5 **Programme Management Office (PMO) Services.**  
Atlas shall establish and operate a PMO function for the Programme, including:

- Integrated project planning and coordination;  
- RAID (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies) management;  
- Status reporting, including dashboards and executive summaries;  
- Support for Steering Committee meetings; and  
- Tracking of benefits, milestones, and resource utilisation.

2.2.6 **Training and Knowledge Transfer.**  
In alignment with Section 2.2.5 of the Parent Agreement, Atlas shall provide:

- Structured training sessions and workshops for Contoso personnel on the TOM, new processes, and tools;  
- Documentation of methodologies, templates, and playbooks;  
- Shadowing and co-delivery models to ensure sustainable internal capabilities at Contoso.

2.3 **Performance Standards and Dependencies.**  

2.3.1 Atlas shall perform the Services with the degree of skill and care described in Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement, and using personnel who are appropriately qualified, experienced, and trained.

2.3.2 Contoso acknowledges that timely delivery of the Services and Deliverables is dependent upon Contoso fulfilling its obligations under Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement, including provision of Client Materials, access to personnel, and timely review and approvals.

2.3.3 Atlas shall promptly notify Contoso of any anticipated delays in accordance with Section 2.5.2 of the Parent Agreement and propose mitigation steps. The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to adjust timelines via Change Orders where necessary.

2.4 **Key Deliverables.**  
Without limiting the definition of “Deliverables” in Section 1.9 of the Parent Agreement, the **Key Deliverables** under this Work Order shall include at least the following:

2.4.1 **D1 – Target Operating Model Blueprint.**  
A comprehensive TOM blueprint, including:

- Organisation structure and role descriptions;  
- Governance framework and decision rights;  
- Process hierarchy and high-level process designs;  
- KPIs and performance management framework.

2.4.2 **D2 – Technology and Data Architecture Overview.**  
Deliverable comprising:

- Current-state assessment;  
- Target-state architecture diagrams;  
- Technology landscape mapping;  
- Guiding principles and standards.

2.4.3 **D3 – Implementation Roadmap and Business Case.**  
Document including:

- Programme roadmap (phases, waves, releases);  
- Financial model (costs, benefits, NPV/IRR where applicable);  
- Resource plan and high-level vendor strategy.

2.4.4 **D4 – Workstream Delivery Playbooks.**  
For each Implementation Workstream:

- Detailed playbooks and workplans;  
- Templates and tools to guide implementation;  
- Success metrics and monitoring approach.

2.4.5 **D5 – PMO Operating Handbook and Reporting Pack.**  
Including:

- PMO processes and standards;  
- Reporting templates and dashboards;  
- RAID management framework.

2.4.6 **D6 – Training Materials and Knowledge Transfer Pack.**  
Including:

- Slide decks, handouts, and guides;  
- “Train-the-trainer” materials;  
- Recorded sessions (where permissible).

Unless otherwise agreed, Deliverables shall comply with Section 2.3 of the Parent Agreement regarding language, format, structure, and delivery methods.

2.5 **Timelines and Milestones.**  

2.5.1 The estimated duration of the Phase 2 Services under this Work Order shall run from the Effective Date through the Work Order Expiration Date, with Phase 2 core activities expected to be substantially completed within the first thirty-six (36) months, followed by ongoing PMO and advisory support as agreed.

2.5.2 The detailed Milestones, including target dates for each Key Deliverable, shall be set out in **Schedule 1 (Project Plan and Milestones)**, which may be updated from time to time by mutual written agreement or via a Change Order.

2.5.3 For the purpose of Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement, Contoso shall have ten (10) **Business Days** (the “Review Period”) following delivery of each Key Deliverable to review and either accept or reject such Deliverable with written reasons for any rejection based on material non-conformity with this Work Order and Schedule 1.

2.5.4 Atlas shall correct any identified material non-conformities at no additional charge and re-deliver the Deliverable within a mutually agreed period, as contemplated by Section 2.7.2 of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **Onsite vs. Remote Services.**  

2.6.1 The Parties anticipate a blended model of Onsite Services and Remote Services, with the ratio and locations to be agreed in the Project Plan and subject to Contoso’s reasonable site access, security, and health & safety policies in accordance with Section 2.4.2(c) of the Parent Agreement.

2.6.2 Any travel for Onsite Services shall be managed and reimbursed in accordance with Section 3.7 of the Parent Agreement and Section 3.5 of this Work Order.

2.7 **Governance and Reporting.**  

2.7.1 **Status Reporting.** Atlas shall provide regular status reports (typically bi-weekly or monthly, as agreed) summarizing progress, key decisions, risks, issues, and next steps.

2.7.2 **Working Governance.** Atlas and Contoso shall convene working-level governance meetings (e.g., weekly workstream meetings) to review progress, align on deliverables, and coordinate decisions and actions.

2.7.3 **Programme Steering Committee.** A Programme Steering Committee comprising senior executives from both Parties shall meet (in person or virtually) at least quarterly, or more frequently as agreed, to review overall Programme status, key risks, major decisions, and any Change Requests at the Programme level.

2.8 **Subcontracting.**  
Atlas may engage subcontractors in accordance with Section 2.8 of the Parent Agreement. Atlas shall remain fully responsible for the acts and omissions of its subcontractors and shall ensure that any subcontractor with access to Confidential Information or Personal Data is bound by obligations no less protective than those in the Parent Agreement and this Work Order.
