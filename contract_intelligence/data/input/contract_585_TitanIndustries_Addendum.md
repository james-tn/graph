## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Parent DPA.** The Parties acknowledge that the Parent DPA, particularly **Article II – Scope of Services / Work**, sets forth the baseline services to be provided by Titan, including data hosting, processing and analytics, application support, data integration, and security and compliance services. This Addendum **supplements and expands** those Services by adding **Additional Services** and **Extended Processing Activities**, as described herein, and is entered into **pursuant to and in amendment of** the Parent DPA (Ref: DPA-TIT-202505-516 / contract_516). Except as expressly set forth in this Addendum, all provisions of **Article II** of the Parent DPA remain in full force and effect.

2.2 **General Description of Additional Services.** During the Addendum Term, Titan shall provide the following Additional Services to Contoso:

  (a) **Advanced Behavioral Analytics and Profiling.** Titan shall design, configure, and operate advanced analytics pipelines to process transactional, usage, and interaction data derived from Contoso’s applications and platforms. Such services shall include:

  - Real-time and near-real-time event ingestion from multiple Contoso systems, including but not limited to web applications, mobile applications, CRM systems, and support platforms;
  - Normalization, enrichment, and aggregation of behavioral events and user interactions for purposes of segmentation, churn prediction, and lifetime value modeling;
  - Development and maintenance of machine learning models and scoring mechanisms to identify high-value users, at-risk customers, and anomalous behaviors;
  - Delivery of analytics outputs via interactive dashboards, scheduled reports, and API-based data services (“Enhanced Analytics Outputs”).

  (b) **Expanded Data Hosting and Storage for High-Risk Processing.** Titan shall provision dedicated and logically isolated infrastructure segments within its cloud environment to host data sets associated with High-Risk Processing, including:

  - Separate virtual networks and security zones for special categories of Personal Data where identified by Contoso;
  - Enhanced logging, monitoring, and access-control policies tailored to the sensitivity of such data;
  - Geo-specific hosting options to support compliance with cross-border transfer rules, consistent with **Section 11.8** of the Parent DPA.

  (c) **Regulatory Reporting and Compliance Support.** Titan shall support Contoso’s compliance obligations under GDPR, UK GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and other Data Protection Laws by:

  - Creating and maintaining detailed records of Extended Processing Activities, as required by Article 30 GDPR;
  - Providing configurable reporting on Data Subject access, deletion, and restriction requests handled via Titan’s systems, aligned with **Section 11.6** of the Parent DPA;
  - Preparing technical appendices and security descriptions to support DPIAs and transfer impact assessments.

  (d) **Enhanced Security and Threat Intelligence.** In addition to the security obligations in **Section 11.4** of the Parent DPA, Titan shall:

  - Implement advanced endpoint and network threat detection tools in the environments used for the Additional Services;
  - Provide Contoso with quarterly summary security reports, including incident trends, attempted intrusions, and remediation measures;
  - Coordinate, at Contoso’s request, joint security tabletop exercises focused on Security Incidents involving High-Risk Processing, consistent with **Section 11.5** of the Parent DPA.

2.3 **Enhanced Analytics Services – Specifics.** Without limiting **Section 2.2(a)**, Titan’s Enhanced Analytics Services shall include:

  (a) **Model Development and Training.** Titan data scientists shall collaborate with designated Contoso stakeholders to define success metrics, training data sets, and validation protocols. Model types may include propensity models, clustering, anomaly detection, and recommendation systems.

  (b) **Model Operationalization.** Titan shall deploy approved models into production within Titan-managed environments, integrate them into Contoso’s workflows via APIs or data feeds, and implement monitoring to track model performance and drift.

  (c) **Data Minimization and Pseudonymization.** Where feasible and agreed in writing, Titan shall implement pseudonymization or partial anonymization for model training and scoring, consistent with Contoso’s instructions under **Section 11.2** of the Parent DPA.

  (d) **Output Deliverables.** Titan shall provide:

  - Monthly performance dashboards summarizing key model metrics, such as accuracy, precision, recall, and false positive rates;
  - Quarterly strategic review reports containing recommendations for model improvements and data collection strategies;
  - Exportable data sets of scored entities (e.g., users, accounts) in a commonly used, machine-readable format.

2.4 **Extended Processing Activities – Categories and Purposes.** The Parties agree that, in addition to the Processing contemplated under the Parent DPA, Titan is hereby authorized by Contoso to conduct Extended Processing Activities including:

  (a) **New Categories of Personal Data.** Subject to Contoso’s written instructions and consistent with **Section 11.2** of the Parent DPA, Titan may process:

  - User behavioral telemetry (click-stream data, session metadata, navigation paths);
  - Limited customer support transcripts and interaction logs (which may incidentally contain special categories of Personal Data, where lawfully collected by Contoso);
  - Marketing engagement metrics (email opens/clicks, campaign interactions);
  - Device and network identifiers (IP addresses, device IDs, user-agent strings), solely for analytics, security, and fraud detection purposes.

  (b) **New Processing Purposes.** In addition to the purposes in the Parent DPA, Titan may process Personal Data for:

  - Advanced customer segmentation and personalization;
  - Product and feature usage analysis and optimization;
  - Security monitoring, anomaly detection, and fraud prevention;
  - Regulatory reporting and compliance documentation as requested by Contoso.

  (c) **Processing Locations.** Titan may perform the Extended Processing Activities in the United States and, where specifically authorized by Contoso in writing, in other jurisdictions, subject to **Section 11.8** (Cross-Border Transfers) of the Parent DPA and any applicable standard contractual clauses or equivalent safeguards.

2.5 **Additional Deliverables and Timelines.** The Additional Deliverables under this Addendum shall include, without limitation:

  (a) **Data Flow and Processing Maps.** Within ninety (90) days following the Addendum Effective Date, Titan shall deliver updated data flow diagrams and processing inventories reflecting the Extended Processing Activities, suitable to support Contoso’s obligations under Data Protection Laws as contemplated by **Section 2.2(b) (Data Mapping and Records)** of the Parent DPA.

  (b) **Quarterly Analytics Reports.** Titan shall deliver quarterly analytics reports summarizing key metrics, insights, and trends derived from the Enhanced Analytics Services, including any recommendations for business or technical optimization.

  (c) **Security and Risk Reports.** In addition to the reports referenced in **Section 2.2(d)** of the Parent DPA, Titan shall provide specialized risk assessment reports for High-Risk Processing at least annually, or more frequently if required by Contoso due to material changes in Processing activities.

  (d) **Implementation Roadmap.** Within thirty (30) days of the Addendum Effective Date, Titan shall provide a written implementation roadmap setting forth major milestones, including environment provisioning, data onboarding, model development, and initial production deployment.

2.6 **Performance Standards.** Titan shall perform the Additional Services:

  (a) In accordance with the standards set forth in **Section 2.3 (Performance Standards)** of the Parent DPA, including professional, timely, and workmanlike performance, compliance with Applicable Law, and adherence to service levels; and  

  (b) With heightened diligence and security controls commensurate with the High-Risk Processing designation of this engagement, recognizing that the **Risk Level** for this Addendum is **High**.

2.7 **Client Responsibilities.** In addition to the responsibilities in **Section 2.5** of the Parent DPA, Contoso shall:

  (a) Identify and document any special categories of Personal Data or High-Risk Processing scenarios to be handled under this Addendum;

  (b) Provide Titan with timely written instructions regarding lawful bases for Processing, retention periods, and any Data Subject rights workflows impacting the Additional Services;

  (c) Cooperate with Titan in promptly handling Data Subject requests and regulator inquiries related to the Extended Processing Activities.

2.8 **Subprocessors.** The authorization and conditions for use of Subprocessors set forth in **Section 11.9** of the Parent DPA apply equally to the Additional Services and Extended Processing Activities. Titan shall ensure that any new Subprocessors engaged for the Additional Services are notified to Contoso in accordance with the notice and objection mechanisms in the Parent DPA.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Addendum Contract Value.** In consideration for the Additional Services and Additional Deliverables provided by Titan under this Addendum, Contoso shall pay to Titan fees not to exceed an aggregate amount of **USD $4,749,821** during the Addendum Term (the **“Addendum Contract Value”**), unless otherwise agreed in a written amendment or Change Request pursuant to **Article XIII** of this Addendum. This amount is **in addition to** the Contract Value of **USD $3,238,331** specified in **Section 3.1** of the Parent DPA.

3.2 **Fee Structure.** The Addendum fees shall consist of:

  (a) **Base Expansion Fees.** Recurring monthly fees for the Additional Services, including advanced analytics infrastructure, additional hosting capacity, and extended security controls.

  (b) **Usage-Based Fees.** Variable fees based on incremental data volume, compute usage, and analytics workloads beyond those covered by the Parent DPA, calculated according to a rate schedule agreed in writing by the Parties (e.g., per GB stored, per million events processed, per model executed).

  (c) **Professional Services Fees.** Time-and-materials or fixed-fee charges for specialized data science, compliance support, or custom integration work requested by Contoso.

3.3 **Payment Terms.** Notwithstanding **Section 3.3** of the Parent DPA (Net 60), the Parties hereby agree that for all fees under this Addendum:

  (a) Titan shall invoice Contoso monthly in arrears, unless otherwise specified in a project schedule;  

  (b) All undisputed amounts shall be due and payable within **thirty (30) days** from the date of the applicable invoice (“**Net 30**”);  

  (c) Payments shall be made in U.S. Dollars by wire transfer, ACH, or other mutually agreed method to the account designated by Titan.

3.4 **Payment Milestones.** For any fixed-fee components under this Addendum, invoices may be tied to the following milestones, as specified in an attached schedule or statement of work:

  (a) 20% upon delivery and mutual acceptance of the implementation roadmap described in **Section 2.5(d)**;  

  (b) 40% upon completion of initial production deployment of the Enhanced Analytics Services;  

  (c) 40% upon completion of the first full quarterly analytics cycle and delivery of the corresponding Quarterly Analytics Report.

3.5 **Late Payment.** Any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest at the lesser of (i) one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month or (ii) the maximum rate permitted by Applicable Law, calculated from the due date until the date of actual payment. Titan may, in addition to any other remedies, suspend the Additional Services for nonpayment in accordance with **Section 4.5** of the Parent DPA, provided Titan gives Contoso at least ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice of its intent to suspend.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.** The invoice dispute mechanism in **Section 3.5** of the Parent DPA shall apply to all invoices under this Addendum, except that Contoso shall notify Titan of any dispute within **twenty (20) days** of receipt of the disputed invoice.

3.7 **Taxes and Expenses.** The tax provisions in **Section 3.6** of the Parent DPA apply to this Addendum. Any travel or extraordinary expenses incurred by Titan specifically for the Additional Services shall be reimbursable only if approved by Contoso in advance in writing, consistent with **Section 3.7** of the Parent DPA.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Addendum Term.** This Addendum shall commence on the Addendum Effective Date and, unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV, shall remain in effect until **July 05, 2028** (the “**Addendum Term**”).

4.2 **Renewal.** The Addendum Term may be extended by mutual written agreement for one or more additional periods of one (1) year each. Any such extension shall expressly reference this Addendum and the Parent DPA (DPA-TIT-202505-516 / contract_516).

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.** In light of the high-risk nature of the Additional Services and the Parties’ need for operational continuity:

  (a) Contoso may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, for convenience upon not less than **one hundred eighty (180) days’** prior written notice to Titan;  

  (b) Titan may terminate this Addendum for convenience upon not less than **ninety (90) days’** prior written notice to Contoso, provided that Titan shall reasonably cooperate with Contoso to transition the Additional Services in accordance with **Section 4.6** of the Parent DPA and **Section 4.6** below.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Addendum, in whole or in part, upon written notice to the other Party if:

  (a) The other Party materially breaches this Addendum (including nonpayment of undisputed amounts) and fails to cure such breach within thirty (30) days (or ten (10) days for payment breaches) after receipt of written notice thereof; or  

  (b) Any of the insolvency events described in **Section 4.4(b)** of the Parent DPA occur with respect to the other Party.

4.5 **Interplay with Parent DPA.**  

  (a) If the Parent DPA is terminated or expires for any reason, this Addendum shall automatically terminate effective as of the effective date of such termination or expiration, without additional notice, unless the Parties expressly agree in writing to continue any part of the Additional Services under a new agreement.  

  (b) Termination of this Addendum alone shall not, by itself, terminate the Parent DPA, which shall remain in full force and effect with respect to the Services defined therein.

4.6 **Effect of Termination or Expiration.** Upon termination or expiration of this Addendum for any reason:

  (a) Titan shall cease providing the Additional Services and Extended Processing Activities, except to the extent reasonably necessary to perform data export or winding-down activities;  

  (b) At Contoso’s written request, made within thirty (30) days of termination or expiration of this Addendum, Titan shall provide Contoso with reasonable assistance in exporting data and Enhanced Analytics Outputs generated under this Addendum, in a commonly used, machine-readable format, consistent with **Section 4.6(b)** of the Parent DPA;  

  (c) Subject to **Section 11.7** of the Parent DPA and **Section 11.4** of this Addendum, Titan shall delete or anonymize Personal Data processed solely under this Addendum, except where retention is required by Applicable Law;  

  (d) Contoso shall pay Titan for all Additional Services performed and expenses incurred up to the effective date of termination, including any agreed-upon transition services.

4.7 **Survival.** The provisions of this Addendum which by their nature should survive termination or expiration, including Articles V (Confidentiality), VI (Intellectual Property Rights), VII (Representations and Warranties), VIII (Indemnification), IX (Limitation of Liability), X (Insurance), XI (Data Protection and Privacy), XIV (Dispute Resolution), XV (Force Majeure), XVI (General Provisions), and all accrued payment obligations, shall survive any termination or expiration of this Addendum.

## ARTICLE XI – DATA PROTECTION AND PRIVACY

11.1 **Roles of the Parties.** The Parties reaffirm the roles described in **Section 11.1** of the Parent DPA: Contoso as Controller (or Business) and Titan as Processor (or Service Provider) for the Additional Services and Extended Processing Activities under this Addendum.

11.4 **Technical and Organizational Measures.** In addition to the measures described in **Section 11.4** of the Parent DPA, Titan shall implement:

  (a) Strengthened access controls (multi-factor authentication, just-in-time access) for environments hosting High-Risk Processing data;  

  (b) Encryption of Personal Data in transit and at rest, wherever technically feasible;  

  (c) Enhanced logging and monitoring of access to data subject to High-Risk Processing;  

  (d) Regular (at least annual) penetration testing and security audits covering the Additional Services.

11.5 **Security Incidents and Breach Notification.**  

  (a) Titan shall comply with **Section 11.5** of the Parent DPA with respect to any Security Incident involving Personal Data processed under this Addendum.  

  (b) For Security Incidents affecting High-Risk Processing, Titan shall use commercially reasonable efforts to notify Contoso **without undue delay and, where feasible, within forty-eight (48) hours** of becoming aware of such incident.

11.6 **Data Subject Rights.** Titan shall assist Contoso, as described in **Section 11.6** of the Parent DPA, in responding to Data Subject requests affecting the Extended Processing Activities, including deletion, access, and portability requests where the data resides within Titan-managed environments.

11.7 **Retention and Deletion.** Titan shall retain Personal Data processed solely under this Addendum only for so long as necessary to provide the Additional Services or as required by Applicable Law, consistent with **Section 11.7** of the Parent DPA. Upon Contoso’s written request, Titan shall delete or anonymize such data within a commercially reasonable time frame.

11.8 **Audits and Assessments.** Contoso may exercise its audit rights under **Section 11.10** of the Parent DPA with respect to the Additional Services and Extended Processing Activities, provided that:

  (a) Audits shall be limited to the systems, facilities, and records relevant to the Additional Services;  

  (b) In fulfilling such audits, Titan may provide existing certifications (e.g., SOC 2, ISO 27001) and summaries specific to the Additional Services.
