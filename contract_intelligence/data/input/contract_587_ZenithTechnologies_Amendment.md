## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Relationship to Existing SOW and Parent Agreement.**  

2.1.1 This Amendment is issued **under and subject to** the Existing SOW **SOW-ZEN-202311-530 (contract_530)** and the Parent Agreement **CSA-ZEN-202311-523 (contract_523)**. In accordance with **Section 2.1 of the Existing SOW** and **Sections 2.1 and 2.3 of the Parent Agreement**, this Amendment provides additional details and modifications to the Services, Deliverables, timelines, Service Levels, and Fees for the continuation and expansion of the same Project.

2.1.2 Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms and conditions of the Existing SOW remain in full force and effect. In the event of any conflict or inconsistency between (i) this Amendment and the Existing SOW, this Amendment shall prevail with respect to the subject matter hereof; and (ii) the Amended SOW (Existing SOW as amended) and the Parent Agreement, the Parent Agreement shall prevail unless the Amended SOW expressly states otherwise, consistent with **Section 2.1.2 of the Existing SOW**.

2.2 **Overall Objective of the Amendment.**  

2.2.1 The objective of this Amendment is to extend and expand the **technology modernization and analytics enablement programme** initiated under the Existing SOW. The Extended Services shall focus on:

  (a) **Optimization and Stabilization** of the architectures, integrations, and analytics solutions designed and implemented in Phases 1–6 referenced in **Section 2.3 of the Existing SOW**;  

  (b) **Additional Integrations and Data Sources** including integration of new third-party applications, data warehouses, and line-of-business systems not originally within the Phase 3 scope described in **Section 2.3.3 of the Existing SOW**;  

  (c) **Advanced Analytics and AI Enablement**, comprising predictive models, machine learning-based recommendations, and expanded dashboards beyond the “priority dashboards” referenced in **Section 2.3.4 and Deliverable D10** of the Existing SOW;  

  (d) **Extended Training and Capability Build-out**, with ongoing enablement of Client’s internal teams (beyond the 4–6 weeks in **Phase 5** of the Existing SOW) to support new use cases, data literacy programmes, and administration; and  

  (e) **Extended Hypercare, Support, and Continuous Improvement**, including post-go-live monitoring and iterative enhancement for a longer period than the 8 weeks specified in **Phase 6 – Hypercare & Stabilization** of the Existing SOW, and aligned to the Enhanced Service Levels in **Article XII** of this Amendment.

2.2.2 These Extended Services continue to align with the general categories of Services described in **Section 2.2(a)–(f) of the Parent Agreement** (Technology Consulting and Assessment; Systems Integration and Implementation; Data Analytics and Business Intelligence; Project Management and Governance; Training and Knowledge Transfer; and Support and Maintenance).

2.3 **Extended Phases and Workstreams.**  

Without limiting **Section 2.3 of the Existing SOW**, the following additional phases and workstreams shall be performed under this Amendment:

2.3.1 **Phase 7 – Optimization & Performance Tuning (Approx. 16–20 weeks).**  
- Review performance and scalability of implemented integrations and analytics components delivered under Phases 3 and 4 of the Existing SOW.  
- Conduct load testing, performance benchmarking, and configuration tuning across the Client Environment.  
- Produce an **Optimization Assessment Report** (Additional Deliverable A1) including prioritized improvements and configuration recommendations.  
- Implement agreed performance enhancements and document changes in updated “as-built” documentation.

2.3.2 **Phase 8 – Extended Integrations & New Data Domains (Approx. 24–32 weeks).**  
- Integrate at least **three (3) additional enterprise systems** and/or external data providers identified mutually in writing by the Parties.  
- Extend existing data pipelines to cover new subject areas and data domains (e.g., supply chain, customer churn, marketing attribution).  
- Produce **Extended Integration Design Specifications** (Additional Deliverable A2) and updated **Data Model Extensions** (Additional Deliverable A3).  
- Coordinate with third-party vendors in accordance with **Section 2.3.3 and Section 2.9 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 2.9 (Non-Exclusivity) of the Parent Agreement**.

2.3.3 **Phase 9 – Advanced Analytics & AI (Approx. 28–36 weeks; may overlap with Phase 8).**  
- Design and implement predictive and prescriptive analytics use cases prioritized by the Project Steering Committee (as defined in **Section 1.10 of the Existing SOW**).  
- Develop machine learning models (classification, regression, forecasting, or clustering) using agreed toolsets within the Client Environment or approved cloud platforms.  
- Deliver **Advanced Analytics Models and Documentation** (Additional Deliverable A4) and **Model Operationalization Runbooks** (Additional Deliverable A5).  
- Ensure appropriate governance, explainability, and monitoring processes are in place consistent with the Client’s policies and **Article XI (Data Protection and Privacy)** of the Existing SOW and the Parent Agreement.

2.3.4 **Phase 10 – Extended Training, Adoption & Operating Model (Approx. 20–24 weeks).**  
- Design and deliver a broader training programme, including “train-the-trainer” sessions, for data stewards, product owners, and business leaders, extending the work described in **Section 2.3.5 and Deliverables D11–D12 of the Existing SOW**.  
- Produce **Operating Model and Governance Framework** documentation (Additional Deliverable A6) describing roles, responsibilities, escalation paths, and ongoing maintenance processes.  
- Conduct adoption workshops to drive user engagement with the enhanced dashboards and analytics capabilities.

2.3.5 **Phase 11 – Extended Hypercare, Support & Continuous Improvement (Duration: up to 24 months following the completion of Phase 9).**  
- Extend the **hypercare and stabilization** period described in **Section 2.3.6 of the Existing SOW** to provide application and data support for a further period as mutually agreed in the project plan but not exceeding the Amendment Term.  
- Provide a continuous improvement backlog, tracking enhancements, minor change requests, and incremental feature releases.  
- Provide support and monitoring in accordance with the **Enhanced Service Levels** described in **Article XII** of this Amendment, supplementing **Article XII of the Existing SOW**.

2.4 **Project Governance.**  

2.4.1 The **Project Steering Committee** and **Project Managers** established under **Sections 1.10, 1.11, and 2.6 of the Existing SOW** shall continue to operate for the Extended Services.  

2.4.2 The Steering Committee shall also:  
- Approve prioritization of new use cases, integrations, and analytics models;  
- Review and approve Milestone completion under this Amendment; and  
- Review risk registers, including any changes to the Medium Risk Profile described in **Section 1.12 of this Amendment**.

2.5 **Additional Deliverables.**  

In addition to the Deliverables D1–D13 listed in **Section 2.5 of the Existing SOW**, the Vendor shall produce at minimum the following **Additional Deliverables** (to be further detailed in the updated Project Plan):

- **A1 – Optimization Assessment Report** (performance, scalability, tuning recommendations);  
- **A2 – Extended Integration Design Specifications** (for new systems and interfaces);  
- **A3 – Data Model Extensions and Documentation** (for new domains and metrics);  
- **A4 – Advanced Analytics Models & Technical Documentation** (including feature engineering and validation results);  
- **A5 – Model Operationalization Runbooks** (deployment, retraining, and monitoring procedures);  
- **A6 – Operating Model & Governance Framework for Analytics and Integration**;  
- **A7 – Extended Training Curriculum & Materials** (modules, labs, and recordings);  
- **A8 – Quarterly Continuous Improvement Report** (enhancements delivered, backlog status, KPI impact);  
- **A9 – Updated Post-Implementation Review (Extended Period)** summarizing benefits realized and residual risks.

Unless otherwise agreed in writing, Deliverables A1–A9 shall be treated as **Client-owned Deliverables** in accordance with **Article VI of the Existing SOW** and **Article VI of the Parent Agreement**, subject to Vendor Pre-Existing IP and Third-Party Materials as described in **Sections 6.2–6.4 of the Existing SOW**.

2.6 **Performance Standards and Timelines.**  

2.6.1 The performance standards in **Section 2.6 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 2.4 of the Parent Agreement** continue to apply to the Extended Services.

2.6.2 The Parties will update and maintain a **revised Project Plan** within thirty (30) days of the Amendment Effective Date, reflecting the Extended Phases and Milestones, and target dates consistent with the Amendment Term.

2.6.3 Delays attributable to Client responsibilities (as described in **Section 2.7 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 2.6 of the Parent Agreement**) may result in schedule adjustments and, where applicable, Fee adjustments via Change Order as provided in **Article XIII of the Existing SOW and the Parent Agreement**.

2.7 **Acceptance of Additional Deliverables.**  

2.7.1 The acceptance process in **Section 2.8 of the Existing SOW** and **Section 2.7 of the Parent Agreement** shall apply to all Additional Deliverables, except that the Parties may agree in the Project Plan to longer review periods for complex Deliverables (such as A4 and A5) not exceeding **fifteen (15) Business Days**.

2.7.2 Acceptance or deemed Acceptance of Additional Deliverables shall trigger the corresponding Milestone-based Fees under **Article III** of this Amendment.
