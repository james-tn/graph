## ARTICLE II  
## SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview and Relationship to Master Agreement.**

2.1.1 This Work Order sets forth the detailed scope of the Services and Deliverables to be provided by Vendor under the framework established in **Article II (Scope of Services / Work)** of the Master Agreement. In the event of a conflict between the terms of this Work Order and the Master Agreement, this Work Order shall prevail solely with respect to the subject matter hereof, in accordance with **Section 2.1 (Master Framework)** of the Master Agreement, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

2.1.2 The Project under this Work Order comprises three primary components:

  (a) an **Implementation Phase** of the “Enterprise Operations Platform,” including requirements gathering, solution design, configuration, customization, integration, data migration, testing, and go‑live support, consistent with **Section 2.2.1 (Technology Implementation Services)** of the Master Agreement;

  (b) a **Transition and Knowledge Transfer Phase**, including documentation, training, and handover to Client’s internal teams; and

  (c) a **Managed Services Phase**, including ongoing application maintenance, incident management, monitoring, and minor enhancements, consistent with **Section 2.2.2 (Managed Services and Support)** of the Master Agreement and subject to Service Levels as per **Article XII** thereof.

2.1.3 The **Managed Services Phase** shall commence on the **later** of (i) the Go‑Live Date or (ii) **1 January 2025** (the **“Managed Services Commencement Date”**), and shall continue through the **Expiration Date** of this Work Order (24 May 2027), unless earlier terminated in accordance with **Article IV** of this Work Order and **Article IV** of the Master Agreement.

2.2 **Implementation Services.**

Vendor shall perform the following Implementation Services, utilizing personnel with the requisite skills and experience consistent with **Section 2.4.1** of the Master Agreement:

2.2.1 **Requirements and Solution Design.** Vendor shall:

  (a) conduct stakeholder workshops to gather and validate business and technical requirements across Client’s operations, finance, and supply chain functions;

  (b) prepare a **Functional Requirements Specification (FRS)** and a **Technical Design Document (TDD)** as Deliverables, each subject to Client’s review and acceptance in accordance with **Section 2.5.2** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) provide an architecture overview that documents integrations with existing Client systems (ERP, CRM, data warehouse) and any Third‑Party Materials, as defined in **Section 1.21** of the Master Agreement.

2.2.2 **Configuration and Customization.** Vendor shall:

  (a) configure the Enterprise Operations Platform in accordance with the FRS and TDD;

  (b) develop custom modules, scripts, workflows, and dashboards where standard configuration is insufficient to meet Client’s requirements, recognizing such work as **Work Product** owned by Vendor under **Section 6.4 (Work Product and Deliverables)** of the Master Agreement, subject to the license granted in **Section 6.5 (License to Deliverables)**;

  (c) document configuration settings and customizations in an **Application Configuration Guide** Deliverable.

2.2.3 **Integration Services.** Vendor shall:

  (a) design and implement integrations with Client’s ERP, CRM, and identity management systems using mutually agreed middleware or APIs;

  (b) prepare **Interface Control Documents (ICDs)** describing data flows, field mappings, error handling, and security controls; and

  (c) conduct integration testing, including positive and negative test cases, and produce **Integration Test Reports**.

2.2.4 **Data Migration.** Vendor shall:

  (a) assist Client in defining data migration strategy, including data cleansing responsibilities, in line with Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Master Agreement;

  (b) develop data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL) scripts, test migration processes in non‑production environments, and execute at least one mock migration;

  (c) produce a **Data Migration Runbook** detailing procedures and rollback plans.

2.2.5 **Testing and Quality Assurance.** Vendor shall:

  (a) develop **System Integration Test (SIT)** and **User Acceptance Test (UAT)** plans and test cases, including traceability to requirements;

  (b) support Client during UAT, triage defects, and provide defect resolution within timelines reasonably aligned with Severity levels defined in **Section 12.3 (Incident Response and Resolution)** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) maintain a **Defect Log** and provide **Test Summary Reports** as Deliverables.

2.2.6 **Go‑Live Preparation and Cutover.** Vendor shall:

  (a) define a **Cutover Plan**, including final data migration, system switch‑over, and contingency measures;

  (b) participate in go‑no‑go meetings and provide advisory input, acknowledging that final go‑live decision remains with Client;

  (c) provide at least **two (2) weeks** of **hypercare support** post Go‑Live Date, with enhanced staffing and accelerated response times aligned with or exceeding the Severity 1 and 2 targets in **Section 12.3.1** of the Master Agreement.

2.2.7 **Acceptance Criteria.** The Deliverables under the Implementation Phase shall be subject to Client’s acceptance as follows:

  (a) For each Milestone Deliverable identified in Annex 1, Client shall have **ten (10) Business Days** from delivery to review and either (i) provide written acceptance, or (ii) provide a written rejection specifying **material non‑conformities** with the agreed requirements;

  (b) If Client fails to respond within such period, the Deliverable shall be **deemed accepted**, consistent with the deemed acceptance mechanism contemplated under **Section 2.5.2** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to correct material non‑conformities within a mutually agreed timeframe and resubmit the Deliverable for acceptance.

2.3 **Managed Services Scope.**

Following the Managed Services Commencement Date, Vendor shall provide the following Managed Services, in line with **Section 2.2.2** of the Master Agreement and subject to Service Levels in **Article XII** thereof:

2.3.1 **Application Maintenance and Support.**

  (a) Provide **L2 and L3 support** for the Enterprise Operations Platform, including incident diagnosis, bug fixes, and minor configuration adjustments;

  (b) Coordinate with Client’s L1 help desk for ticket triage and escalation;

  (c) Maintain a **Knowledge Base** of known issues, workarounds, and resolution steps.

2.3.2 **Monitoring and Incident Management.**

  (a) Implement monitoring for system availability, performance thresholds, and integration failures;

  (b) Log and categorize incidents by severity in accordance with Severity Levels in **Section 12.3.1** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) Comply with target response and restoration times set forth in **Article XII** of the Master Agreement, subject to exclusions for Scheduled Maintenance and Excluded Downtime as defined in **Section 12.2** thereof.

2.3.3 **Patch Management and Upgrades.**

  (a) Review and recommend application patches and upgrades at least **quarterly**, assessing impact and scheduling with Client;

  (b) Apply patches and upgrades in non‑production environments, coordinate testing, and then deploy to production in agreed maintenance windows;

  (c) Document patch levels and maintain a **Release Notes Log**.

2.3.4 **Minor Enhancements.**

  (a) Provide up to **400 T&M hours per Contract Year** (pro‑rated for partial years) for minor enhancements (small configuration changes, additional reports, or workflows) without requiring a separate Change Order, provided they do not materially expand scope;

  (b) Additional enhancement work beyond this allocation shall be addressed through the Change Management process under **Article XIII** of the Master Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Work Order.

2.3.5 **Service Window and On‑Call Coverage.**

  (a) Vendor shall provide support during the **Standard Service Window** of **Monday to Friday, 08:00 – 20:00 Singapore Time (SGT)**, excluding public holidays in Singapore;

  (b) For Severity 1 incidents, Vendor shall provide **24x7 on‑call** coverage with target response times consistent with **Section 12.3.1 (Severity 1)** of the Master Agreement;

  (c) Any extended or dedicated 24x7 coverage beyond the foregoing shall be mutually agreed in writing and may be subject to additional Fees.

2.4 **Deliverables List.**

Without limiting the generality of **Section 2.3 of the Master Agreement**, Vendor shall produce and deliver, at a minimum, the following Deliverables under this Work Order:

  (a) Functional Requirements Specification (FRS);  
  (b) Technical Design Document (TDD);  
  (c) Architecture Diagram and Integration Overview;  
  (d) Interface Control Documents (ICDs);  
  (e) Data Migration Runbook and ETL Scripts;  
  (f) System Integration Test Plan and Test Cases;  
  (g) UAT Plan and Test Cases;  
  (h) Defect Log and Test Summary Reports;  
  (i) Cutover Plan and Go‑Live Checklist;  
  (j) Application Configuration Guide;  
  (k) Operational Runbook and Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs);  
  (l) Training Materials and Knowledge Base Articles;  
  (m) Monthly Performance and Incident Reports during the Managed Services Phase.

2.5 **Assumptions and Client Responsibilities.**

2.5.1 The Services under this Work Order are based on the assumptions and Client responsibilities specified herein and in **Section 2.6 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Master Agreement, including but not limited to:

  (a) Client will provide timely access to relevant systems, data, and personnel, as required under **Section 2.6.1** of the Master Agreement;

  (b) Client will perform data cleansing and validation activities as required to achieve data quality targets;

  (c) Client will designate a **Project Sponsor** and **Project Manager** with authority to provide approvals and decisions within agreed timelines;

  (d) Client will ensure that any third‑party vendors whose products or systems are integrated with the Enterprise Operations Platform cooperate reasonably with Vendor.

2.5.2 Delays or additional efforts arising from Client’s failure to meet the foregoing responsibilities may result in schedule changes, additional Fees, or reduced performance, in accordance with **Section 2.4.3** and **Section 2.6** of the Master Agreement. Vendor shall not be liable for such delays.

2.6 **Subcontracting.**

Vendor may utilize subcontractors for portions of the Services, in accordance with **Section 2.7 (Subcontracting)** of the Master Agreement, provided that Vendor remains fully responsible for such subcontractors’ performance. Upon Client’s written request, Vendor shall identify material subcontractors supporting this Work Order.

## ARTICLE III  
## FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value for this Work Order.**

3.1.1 The Parties acknowledge and agree that the aggregate estimated value of this Work Order (the **“Contract Value (WO)”**) is **USD $1,561,785**, broken down as follows:

  (a) **Implementation Phase (Fixed Price):** USD $904,500  
  (b) **Transition and Knowledge Transfer (Fixed Price):** USD $157,285  
  (c) **Managed Services Phase (Retainer / Managed Services Fees):** USD $500,000 (estimated over the Term, on a monthly recurring basis as set out in Section 3.3)

3.1.2 The Parties acknowledge that, consistent with **Section 3.1** of the Master Agreement, this Contract Value (WO) is an estimate only and shall not limit the total amount of Fees that may become payable under this Work Order, except as explicitly stated herein or in a Change Order.

3.2 **Fee Structure.**

3.2.1 **Implementation Phase – Fixed Price.** The Implementation Services described in **Section 2.2** are provided on a fixed‑price basis of **USD $904,500**, billed upon completion of Milestones as follows:

  - Milestone 1 – FRS/TDD Sign‑off: 20% (USD $180,900)  
  - Milestone 2 – Completion of Configuration & SIT: 35% (USD $316,575)  
  - Milestone 3 – Completion of UAT & Go‑Live Readiness: 25% (USD $226,125)  
  - Milestone 4 – Completion of Hypercare: 20% (USD $180,900)

Each Milestone shall be deemed achieved upon Client’s acceptance or deemed acceptance of the relevant Deliverables in accordance with **Section 2.2.7** of this Work Order and **Section 2.5.2** of the Master Agreement.

3.2.2 **Transition and Knowledge Transfer – Fixed Price.** A fixed fee of **USD $157,285** covers documentation finalization, training sessions, and handover workshops. This amount shall be invoiced in two tranches:

  - 50% upon delivery of final documentation package and training materials;  
  - 50% upon completion of knowledge transfer workshops and sign‑off by Client.

3.2.3 **Managed Services Phase – Retainer.** Managed Services described in **Section 2.3** shall be billed as a **monthly retainer** of **USD $17,857** (rounded) over an estimated **28‑month** period from the Managed Services Commencement Date through the Expiration Date, aggregating to approximately **USD $500,000**.

3.2.4 **Time and Materials (T&M) Work.** Any work not explicitly covered by the fixed price or retainer fees (including but not limited to major enhancements, scope changes, or additional 24x7 support) shall be performed on a T&M basis using Vendor’s then‑current rates, subject to **Section 3.2.1 (T&M)** of the Master Agreement and any rate card attached as Annex 3.

3.3 **Invoicing and Payment.**

3.3.1 Vendor shall issue **Invoices** for Fees under this Work Order in accordance with **Section 3.3 (Payment Terms)** of the Master Agreement and the milestones and schedules described herein.

3.3.2 All Invoices under this Work Order shall be payable by Client within **forty‑five (45) days** from the date of the Invoice (**“Net 45”**), consistent with **Section 3.3.2** of the Master Agreement, without setoff or deduction, except for amounts disputed in good faith pursuant to **Section 3.6** of the Master Agreement.

3.3.3 All payments shall be made in **U.S. dollars (USD)** by wire transfer or other mutually agreed payment method to the account designated in Vendor’s Invoice, in accordance with **Section 3.3.3** of the Master Agreement.

3.4 **Taxes.**

All Fees under this Work Order are exclusive of Taxes, and Taxes shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.4 (Taxes)** of the Master Agreement. If Client is required to withhold Taxes, it shall gross up payments such that Vendor receives the full amount it would have received absent such withholding, and shall provide appropriate documentation.

3.5 **Late Payment; Suspension.**

3.5.1 Any undisputed amount not received by Vendor within the Net 45 period shall accrue interest in accordance with **Section 3.5 (Late Payment)** of the Master Agreement.

3.5.2 Vendor may suspend performance of the Services (including Managed Services) under this Work Order for unpaid, undisputed Invoices, upon written notice and subject to the conditions in **Section 3.5.2** of the Master Agreement. Any such suspension will extend timelines and Service Levels accordingly.

3.6 **Disputed Amounts.**

Disputed amounts shall be handled in accordance with **Section 3.6 (Disputed Amounts)** of the Master Agreement. Client shall notify Vendor of any disputed portion of an Invoice within **fifteen (15) days** of receipt and shall pay all undisputed amounts by the due date.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**

3.7.1 Client shall reimburse Vendor for reasonable, pre‑approved out‑of‑pocket expenses incurred in performance of the Services (including travel, lodging, and third‑party fees) in accordance with **Section 3.7** of the Master Agreement and subject to Client’s travel and expense policy.
