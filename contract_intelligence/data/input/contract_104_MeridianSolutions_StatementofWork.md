## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES / WORK

2.1 **Overview.**  

Pursuant to **Section 2.1 (Engagement of Service Provider)** and **Section 2.2 (General Description of Services)** of the Parent Agreement, Client hereby engages Service Provider, and Service Provider hereby accepts such engagement, to perform the Services described in this **Article II**. The Services under this SOW are a specific and detailed instantiation of the broader Categories of Services contemplated in the Parent Agreement, including but not limited to enterprise system modernization, process optimization, and data analytics.

2.2 **Project Objectives.**

The primary objectives of the Project under this SOW are to:

(a) Assess Client’s current enterprise applications, data platforms, and business processes across selected business units (initially Finance, Supply Chain, and Customer Operations);  
(b) Design a target-state architecture and process model that aligns with Client’s strategic roadmap and technology standards;  
(c) Implement, configure, and integrate selected platforms and tools (to be mutually identified) to support the target-state model;  
(d) Develop and deliver training, change management, and adoption support to Client’s users; and  
(e) Establish a sustainable operating model and knowledge transfer framework to enable Client’s internal teams to maintain and evolve the implemented solutions.

These objectives are consistent with the services categories listed in **Section 2.2** of the Parent Agreement (e.g., design of target-state architecture, integration of Client’s systems, training, and change management).

2.3 **Work Streams.**

The Services shall be organized into the following four (4) primary Work Streams:

(a) **Work Stream 1 – Current-State Assessment & Strategy Alignment**  
   - Conduct stakeholder interviews and workshops with business and IT leaders.  
   - Inventory existing systems, integrations, and key business processes.  
   - Assess pain points, redundancies, and technical constraints.  
   - Produce a **Current-State Assessment Report** and **Strategic Alignment Summary**.

(b) **Work Stream 2 – Target-State Design & Roadmap**  
   - Define target-state application and data architecture.  
   - Design future-state process workflows for key business processes.  
   - Identify required integrations, interfaces, and data flows.  
   - Produce a **Target-State Architecture Blueprint**, **Process Design Specifications**, and a **3–5 Year Transformation Roadmap**.

(c) **Work Stream 3 – Implementation, Configuration & Integration**  
   - Configure selected platforms (e.g., ERP, CRM, analytics) in accordance with client-approved design.  
   - Develop integrations using Client-approved middleware or integration platforms.  
   - Execute test strategy, including unit tests, system tests, and user acceptance tests (UAT).  
   - Produce **Configured Solution Packages**, **Integration Specifications**, **Test Plans & Results**, and **Go-Live Readiness Assessments**.

(d) **Work Stream 4 – Training, Change Management & Transition**  
   - Develop training curriculum and materials for end users and administrators.  
   - Conduct train-the-trainer sessions and end-user training.  
   - Support go-live and hypercare post go-live in accordance with SLAs in **Article XII**.  
   - Execute knowledge transfer to Client’s internal support teams.  
   - Produce **Training Materials**, **Change Impact Assessments**, **Knowledge Transfer Runbooks**, and **Post-Implementation Review**.

2.4 **Deliverables.**

Service Provider shall provide at least the following key Deliverables under this SOW:

(a) **D1 – Project Charter & Governance Framework**  
(b) **D2 – Current-State Assessment Report**  
(c) **D3 – Strategic Alignment Summary**  
(d) **D4 – Target-State Architecture Blueprint**  
(e) **D5 – Process Design Specifications (per in-scope processes)**  
(f) **D6 – Transformation Roadmap (3–5 Year)**  
(g) **D7 – Configured Solution Packages (per platform)**  
(h) **D8 – Integration Specifications & Mapping Documents**  
(i) **D9 – Test Strategy, Test Plans, and Test Results**  
(j) **D10 – Go-Live Readiness Assessment**  
(k) **D11 – Training Curriculum and Materials**  
(l) **D12 – Change Impact Assessments & Communication Plan**  
(m) **D13 – Knowledge Transfer Runbooks and Support Playbooks**  
(n) **D14 – Post-Implementation Review & Lessons Learned Report**

Each Deliverable shall conform to the applicable specifications and acceptance criteria described in the Project Plan (Exhibit A) and in **Section 2.5** of the Parent Agreement. Acceptance will follow the process set out in **Section 2.5 (Deliverables and Acceptance)** of the Parent Agreement, except as specifically tailored in Section 2.6 below.
