## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Parties entered into the Parent Agreement, a Software License and Services Agreement bearing **Contract Reference Number SLA-ZEN-202501-288**, effective as of **January 13, 2025** (the **“Effective Date”** of the Parent Agreement), pursuant to which Vendor licenses to Client the **Zenith Enterprise Suite** and provides various related Services, including hosting, implementation, integration, support and maintenance, as more fully described in **Article II (Scope of Services and Licensed Software)** of the Parent Agreement;

WHEREAS, the Parent Agreement, as defined in **Section 1.2** thereof, includes all schedules, annexes, Statements of Work and amendments, and the Parties desire that this Amendment be incorporated therein as an amendment for all purposes;

## ARTICLE II – SCOPE OF SERVICES AND LICENSE MODIFICATIONS

2.1 **General Relationship to Parent Agreement.**  
This Amendment **modifies and supplements Article II (Scope of Services and Licensed Software)** and **Article VI (Intellectual Property Rights and License)** of the Parent Agreement. All Services, Deliverables, and licenses described in this Article II are provided as part of the **Software**, **Services**, and **License** as defined in the Parent Agreement and shall be governed by the terms of the Parent Agreement as amended hereby. To the extent of any conflict between this Article II and **Article II** of the Parent Agreement, this Article II shall prevail with respect to the Additional Services and Incremental License Scope.

2.2 **Incremental License Scope.**  
(a) **Additional Authorized Users and Environments.** Effective as of the Amendment Effective Date, the License granted under **Section 6.2 (License Grant to Client)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby expanded to include:  
  (i) An increase of up to **1,500 additional Authorized Users** (as defined in **Section 1.3** of the Parent Agreement), across Client and its Affiliates, for access to the **Zenith Enterprise Suite**; and  
  (ii) Two (2) additional non-production environments (e.g., dedicated staging and performance testing environments) and one (1) regional production environment in the European Union, all hosted by Vendor in accordance with **Section 2.6 (Hosting and Infrastructure)** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) **Additional Modules.** In addition to the modules listed in **Section 2.2(b)** of the Parent Agreement (including Core Platform, Analytics and Reporting, Integration APIs, User Management, Audit Logs, and System Administration), Vendor shall make available to Client the following additional modules as part of the Software during the Extended Term:  
  (i) **Advanced Workflow Orchestration Module** – providing multi-step workflow automation, conditional routing, and approvals;  
  (ii) **Data Quality and Master Data Management Module** – providing data cleansing, deduplication, and master data management capabilities; and  
  (iii) **Regulatory Reporting and Audit Trails Plus Module** – enhancing the existing **Audit Logs and Compliance Dashboard** specified in **Section 2.2(b)(v)** of the Parent Agreement with region-specific regulatory templates, audit exports, and scheduled compliance reports.

(c) **License Restrictions Unchanged.** Except as expressly modified above, all restrictions on use of the Software set forth in **Section 6.3 (Use Restrictions)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue in full force and effect. Nothing in this Amendment shall be deemed to grant Client any sublicense, right of resale, or right to use the Software for the benefit of third parties beyond the rights already set forth in **Section 6.2** and **Section 6.3** of the Parent Agreement.

2.3 **Additional Services.**  
Vendor shall provide the following Additional Services to Client during the Extended Term:

(a) **Global Rollout Implementation Services.**  
Vendor will design and execute an extended implementation program to support the global rollout of the Zenith Enterprise Suite to additional business units and geographies of Client, in alignment with **Section 2.3 (Implementation and Configuration Services)** and **Section 2.4 (Timeline and Milestones)** of the Parent Agreement. These Services shall include, at a minimum:

  (i) **Discovery and Design Workshops (Phase II & III)** across Client’s North American, EMEA, and APAC business units, with detailed solution design documentation, updating and supplementing the architecture documentation referenced in **Section 2.3(a)(ii)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (ii) **Configuration and Localization Services**, including localized workflows, labels, regulatory configurations, and reporting for at least five (5) new jurisdictions, building upon the configurations contemplated in **Section 2.3(a)(iii)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (iii) **Advanced Integration Services** with additional Client systems (including, without limitation, a second ERP instance, a consolidated CRM platform, and a third-party compliance solution), as contemplated under **Section 2.3(a)(iv)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (iv) **Data Migration and Consolidation Services**, including the migration of legacy data from at least three (3) additional systems into the centralized data model, consistent with **Section 2.3(a)(v)** of the Parent Agreement;

  (v) **User Acceptance Testing (UAT) Support**, including test planning, execution support, and defect triage, as contemplated under **Section 2.3(a)(vi)** of the Parent Agreement; and

  (vi) **Go-Live and Hypercare Support** for each new regional rollout, including on-site or remote support during the first thirty (30) days after each regional go-live.

(b) **Optimization and Performance Tuning Services.**  
Vendor shall provide performance benchmarking, capacity planning, and optimization services to ensure that the extended deployment meets or exceeds the **Service Availability** obligations under **Section 12.1 (Service Availability)** of the Parent Agreement and the enhanced commitments under **Article XII** of this Amendment. These Services will include load testing, query optimization, and configuration of caching strategies.

(c) **Training and Enablement Services.**  
Vendor shall deliver additional training sessions, both remote and on-site, for end users, administrators, and technical staff of Client, including up to twenty (20) days of instructor-led training per year during the Extended Term. These Services are in addition to any training already covered under **Article II** of the Parent Agreement.

2.4 **Deliverables and Milestones.**  
(a) **Deliverables.** The Additional Services shall result in the following key Deliverables (which are added to the definition of “Deliverables” in **Section 1.7** of the Parent Agreement for purposes of this Amendment):

  (i) Updated global solution design documents;  
  (ii) Region-specific configuration bundles and deployment scripts;  
  (iii) Integration adapters and API configurations for additional systems;  
  (iv) Data migration runbooks and mapping documentation;  
  (v) UAT plans, test cases, and issue logs;  
  (vi) Performance test reports and optimization recommendations; and  
  (vii) Training materials and recorded sessions for each major user group.

(b) **Timelines.**  
The Parties acknowledge that the target initial production go-live date under **Section 2.4(a)** of the Parent Agreement (July 31, 2025) remains applicable for the original scope. For the Incremental License Scope and Additional Services under this Amendment:

  (i) Vendor shall use commercially reasonable efforts to complete the primary global rollout phases by **December 31, 2026**; and  
  (ii) Detailed milestones, including regional go-live dates and acceptance checkpoints, shall be agreed in a supplemental SOW referencing both **SLA-ZEN-202501-288** and **AMD-ZEN-202501-345**, and shall be managed pursuant to **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article XIII** of this Amendment.

(c) **Acceptance.**  
The acceptance procedures in **Section 2.8 (Acceptance of Deliverables)** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to all Deliverables under this Amendment. Where an SOW does not specify an acceptance period, the default ten (10) Business Day review period in **Section 2.8(a)** shall apply.

2.5 **Client Responsibilities.**  
Client’s responsibilities under **Section 2.7 (Client Responsibilities)** of the Parent Agreement are hereby reaffirmed and extended to apply to the Additional Services and Incremental License Scope under this Amendment. In particular, Client shall:

(a) Provide updated requirements and design inputs for each new region and system in a timely manner;  
(b) Ensure that required third-party licenses, connectivity, and environments are available for integrations;  
(c) Provide sufficient project management and subject matter experts for all rollout phases; and  
(d) Acknowledge that any failure to fulfill these responsibilities may entitle Vendor to extensions of time and additional fees, as contemplated under **Section 2.7** and **Article XIII (Change Management)** of the Parent Agreement.

2.6 **High Risk Profile Reaffirmation.**  
The Parties expressly reaffirm the **High Risk** profile referenced in **Section 2.9 (High-Risk Acknowledgment)** and the uncapped liability provisions in **Article IX** of the Parent Agreement. Client acknowledges that the extended scope, increased data volumes, and global reach of the deployment under this Amendment further justify the risk allocation favorable to Vendor, including uncapped liability and Client’s broad indemnification obligations, as restated in **Article VIII** and **Article IX** of this Amendment.

## ARTICLE III – FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Amendment Contract Value.**  
The Parties agree that the total **Amendment Contract Value** associated with this Amendment is **USD $3,398,158** (three million three hundred ninety-eight thousand one hundred fifty-eight United States dollars), exclusive of applicable Taxes as defined in **Section 3.8 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement. This amount is in addition to the original contract value of **USD $1,806,529** set forth in **Section 3.1 (Contract Value)** of the Parent Agreement. Unless otherwise expressly stated, all amounts herein are in United States dollars.

3.2 **Fee Components.**  
The Amendment Contract Value comprises the following components, which supplement and, where indicated, modify **Section 3.2 (Fee Components)** of the Parent Agreement:

(a) **Incremental License and Subscription Fees.**  
  (i) Additional annual license/subscription fees for the Incremental License Scope described in **Section 2.2** of this Amendment, including additional Authorized Users, environments, and modules:  
  - Additional users and environments: **USD $650,000 per annum**;  
  - Additional modules (Advanced Workflow, Data Quality/MDM, Regulatory Reporting Plus): **USD $250,000 per annum**.  
  (ii) These fees are non-refundable except as expressly set forth in the Parent Agreement (including any prorated refund rights in **Section 6.7 (Infringement Mitigation)**).  
  (iii) For clarity, these fees are charged in addition to the annualized license/subscription fees stated in **Section 3.2(a)** of the Parent Agreement.

(b) **Additional Implementation and Professional Services Fees.**  
  (i) A one-time professional services fee of **USD $1,900,000** for the Additional Services described in **Article II** of this Amendment, including global rollout, integrations, data migration, performance tuning, and training.  
  (ii) These fees may be structured as milestone-based or time-and-materials as specified in the applicable SOWs, but shall aggregate to the amount stated above, unless modified by an approved Change Request pursuant to **Article XIII** of the Parent Agreement and this Amendment.

(c) **Enhanced Support and Maintenance Fees.**  
  (i) An incremental premium support and maintenance fee of **USD $598,158** for the Extended Term to cover Enhanced Service Levels described in **Article XII** of this Amendment, including extended support hours, additional monitoring, and prioritized incident handling.  
  (ii) These fees are in addition to the **USD $156,529** premium support fee specified in **Section 3.2(c)** of the Parent Agreement.

3.3 **Revised Payment Terms – Net 30.**  
(a) **Supersession of Net 60.** Effective for all invoices issued on or after the Amendment Effective Date, **Section 3.3(a) (Payment Terms)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby amended solely with respect to the payment period such that all undisputed invoices shall be due and payable by Client within **thirty (30) calendar days** from the date of invoice (**“Net 30”**), instead of Net 60. Accordingly, the definition of “Payment Terms” in **Section 1.14** of the Parent Agreement is hereby deemed modified for the Amended Agreement to reference **Net 30**.

(b) **Invoicing.**  
  (i) Incremental license and subscription fees shall be invoiced annually in advance for each twelve (12) month period within the Extended Term.  
  (ii) Additional professional services fees shall be invoiced according to milestones set forth in the applicable SOW, or monthly in arrears if not otherwise specified, consistent with **Section 3.3(b)** of the Parent Agreement.  
  (iii) Enhanced support and maintenance fees shall be invoiced annually in advance.

(c) **Disputed Amounts and Late Payment.**  
The provisions of **Section 3.5 (Disputed Amounts)** and **Section 3.6 (Late Payment)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply, except that any references in those sections to the due date shall be construed in light of the Net 30 Payment Terms described in this Section 3.3. For clarity, any undisputed amount not paid when due shall accrue interest as described in **Section 3.6** of the Parent Agreement, and Vendor’s suspension rights therein remain unchanged.

3.4 **Milestone-Based Payments.**  
Where SOWs under this Amendment specify milestone-based payments, invoices shall be issued upon Vendor achieving the relevant milestone, consistent with **Section 3.4 (Milestone-Based Payments)** of the Parent Agreement. Client agrees not to unreasonably withhold, condition, or delay acceptance of Deliverables, and shall pay invoices for milestones where Deliverables materially conform to the agreed specifications.

3.5 **Expenses and Reimbursements.**  
Client shall reimburse Vendor for reasonable, documented out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Additional Services, including travel and lodging, in accordance with **Section 3.7 (Expenses and Reimbursements)** of the Parent Agreement. The requirement for Client’s prior written approval for single trips likely to incur expenses exceeding **USD $5,000** remains in full force and effect.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
All fees under this Amendment are exclusive of Taxes, and **Section 3.8 (Taxes)** of the Parent Agreement is incorporated herein by reference. If Client is required to withhold any Taxes from payments under this Amendment, Client shall gross up such payments in accordance with **Section 3.8**.

## ARTICLE IV – TERM AND TERMINATION

4.1 **Extended Term.**  
**Section 4.1 (Term)** of the Parent Agreement is hereby amended and restated solely as to the Expiration Date as follows:

> “The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and shall continue in full force and effect until **August 16, 2029** (the “Expiration Date”), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this Article IV (the “Term”).”

For the avoidance of doubt, this Extended Term applies to the entire Amended Agreement (including the original scope and the Incremental License Scope and Additional Services under this Amendment).

4.2 **Renewal.**  
The automatic renewal provision in **Section 4.2 (Renewal)** of the Parent Agreement shall continue to apply following the Extended Term, such that the Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the Expiration Date (as amended above) or the end of any renewal period.

4.3 **Termination for Convenience.**  
The Parties reaffirm **Section 4.3 (Termination for Convenience)** of the Parent Agreement, under which either Party may terminate the Agreement or any SOW for convenience upon at least **one hundred eighty (180) days’ prior written notice**. The same notice period shall apply to termination of this Amendment specifically. In the event of termination for convenience by Client:

(a) All fees paid remain non-refundable, as provided in **Section 4.3** and **Section 3.2(a)(ii)** of the Parent Agreement; and  
(b) Client shall pay Vendor for all Additional Services performed, and all non-cancellable commitments incurred, under this Amendment up to the effective date of termination.

4.4 **Termination for Cause.**  
**Section 4.4 (Termination for Cause)** of the Parent Agreement (including cure periods for material breaches and non-payment) shall apply equally to this Amendment. Any material breach of this Amendment shall be deemed a material breach of the Parent Agreement, and vice versa, subject to the cure periods specified in **Section 4.4**.

4.5 **Regulatory or Legal Termination.**  
The right of Vendor to terminate the Agreement for regulatory or legal reasons under **Section 4.5** of the Parent Agreement shall apply to this Amendment. If continued performance of the Incremental License Scope or Additional Services would, in Vendor’s reasonable judgment, violate applicable law and the Parties cannot agree on modifications to avoid such violation, Vendor may terminate this Amendment or the affected portions, without prejudice to its rights under **Section 4.5**.

4.6 **Effect of Termination of Parent Agreement.**  
In the event the Parent Agreement (SLA-ZEN-202501-288, contract_288) is terminated or expires for any reason:

(a) This Amendment shall automatically terminate as of the same effective date, without the need for further action by either Party;  
(b) All rights and Licenses granted under this Amendment shall immediately cease, consistent with **Section 4.6(a)** of the Parent Agreement;  
(c) The obligations regarding return or destruction of Confidential Information and Client Data in **Section 4.6(b)** of the Parent Agreement and **Article V** and **Article XI** of the Parent Agreement (as supplemented by this Amendment) shall apply to all additional Confidential Information and Client Data processed under this Amendment; and  
(d) Client shall pay all fees and expenses accrued and due under this Amendment as of the effective date of termination, in accordance with **Section 4.6(d)** of the Parent Agreement.

4.7 **Transition Assistance Services.**  
In addition to **Section 4.7 (Transition Assistance)** of the Parent Agreement, Vendor shall, upon Client’s written request received no less than sixty (60) days prior to the effective date of expiration or termination, provide commercially reasonable Transition Assistance Services specific to the extended global deployment under this Amendment, including:

(a) Export of Client Data from additional environments and modules in a commonly used, machine-readable format;  
(b) Up to thirty (30) additional Business Days of professional services to support transition to an alternative solution; and  
(c) Reasonable consultation with Client’s replacement vendors.

Such Transition Assistance Services shall be provided at Vendor’s then-current professional services rates and subject to the same limitations and obligations as in **Section 4.7** of the Parent Agreement.

4.8 **Survival.**  
The survival provisions in **Section 4.8 (Survival)** of the Parent Agreement apply to this Amendment. Without limiting the generality of the foregoing, Articles III (with respect to accrued payment obligations), V, VI, VII, VIII, IX, XI, XIV, XV, XVI of the Parent Agreement, and the corresponding Articles of this Amendment, shall survive any expiration or termination of the Amended Agreement to the extent necessary to give effect to their terms.
