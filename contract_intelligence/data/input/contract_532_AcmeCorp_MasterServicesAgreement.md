  c. **Managed Services and Operations.** Ongoing management, monitoring, operation, and support of certain systems or environments (which may be hosted on Client’s infrastructure, Service Provider’s infrastructure, or third-party cloud platforms), including incident management, change management, and routine maintenance.

  d. **Support and Maintenance Services.** Provision of technical support, bug fixes, patches, performance tuning, and maintenance services for systems and Deliverables within the scope of the applicable SOW, including defined response and resolution times as set forth in Article XII.

  e. **Training and Knowledge Transfer.** Provision of training sessions, workshops, documentation, and other knowledge transfer activities for Client personnel to enable effective use and self-sufficiency in relation to the Deliverables and Services.

  f. **Other Professional Services.** Any additional professional services expressly specified in an SOW, including data migration, data cleansing, testing, user acceptance support, and go-live assistance.

## ARTICLE III  
### FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

3.1 **Contract Value.**  
The total estimated contract value for all Services and Deliverables to be provided under this Agreement during the Term is **Four Million Seven Hundred Fourteen Thousand Five Hundred Fourteen United States Dollars (US$4,714,514)** (the **“Contract Value”**), subject to adjustment in accordance with this Article III and Article XIII.

Unless otherwise specified in an SOW, fees shall comprise one or more of the following components:

  a. **Fixed Fees.** Fixed project or Milestone-based fees for defined Deliverables and Services, payable upon completion and acceptance of the relevant Milestone or Deliverable as specified in the SOW.

  b. **Time and Materials (T&M).** Hourly or daily rates for personnel categories (e.g., consultant, architect, engineer, project manager) as specified in the SOW, billed monthly in arrears based on actual hours worked, subject to any agreed caps.

  c. **Recurring Fees.** Monthly or annual fees for ongoing managed services, support services, or subscription-based components, invoiced in advance or in arrears as specified in the SOW.

  d. **Pass-Through Costs.** Reasonable third-party costs (including license fees, hosting, and third-party services) incurred by Service Provider on behalf of Client and approved by Client in writing.

3.3 **Payment Terms.**  
(a) Service Provider shall issue invoices in accordance with the billing schedule set forth in the applicable SOW. Unless otherwise provided in an SOW, Client shall pay all undisputed amounts within **forty-five (45) calendar days** of the invoice date (**“Net 45”**).

(b) All amounts shall be payable in the currency specified in the SOW (which, absent express agreement to the contrary, shall be US Dollars). Payments shall be made by bank transfer or such other method as Service Provider may reasonably specify in writing.

3.4 **Late Payments.**  
If Client fails to pay any undisputed amount by the applicable due date:

  a. such overdue amount shall accrue interest from the due date until paid in full at the rate of **1.5% per month** (or, if lower, the maximum rate permitted by applicable law); and  

  b. Service Provider may, upon ten (10) Business Days’ prior written notice to Client and provided that Client has not cured such non-payment within such notice period, suspend performance of the Services and any access to Deliverables or systems until all overdue amounts (including interest) are paid.

3.5 **Disputed Amounts.**  
Client may withhold payment of amounts it reasonably and in good faith disputes, provided that Client:

  a. notifies Service Provider in writing within fifteen (15) Business Days of receiving the invoice, specifying the disputed amount and reasons; and  

  b. pays the undisputed portion of the invoice in accordance with Section 3.3.

The Parties shall cooperate in good faith to resolve any such disputes. Upon resolution, any outstanding amounts shall be paid within fifteen (15) Business Days.

3.6 **Taxes.**  
All fees are exclusive of any value-added tax (VAT), sales tax, use tax, or similar taxes, duties, or levies imposed by any governmental authority. Client shall be responsible for all such taxes (excluding taxes based on Service Provider’s net income, property, or employees). Where applicable, Service Provider shall add VAT or other taxes to its invoices, and Client shall pay such amounts.

3.7 **Expenses and Reimbursement.**  
(a) Client shall reimburse Service Provider for all **reasonable, pre-approved, out-of-pocket expenses** incurred in connection with the performance of the Services, including travel, accommodation, subsistence, and third-party costs, provided such expenses are in accordance with Client’s expense policy (if provided to Service Provider in advance) or otherwise pre-approved in writing by Client.

(b) Service Provider shall provide reasonable supporting documentation (e.g., receipts) for reimbursable expenses upon request.

3.8 **No Withholding.**  
All amounts payable under this Agreement shall be paid in full without any set-off or deduction, except as may be required by applicable law. If any withholding or deduction is required by law, Client shall increase the amount payable so that Service Provider receives the same amount as it would have received if no such withholding or deduction had been made.

4.1 **Term.**

  i. materially breaches this Agreement or the applicable SOW and fails to cure such breach (if curable) within thirty (30) days after receiving written notice describing the breach in reasonable detail; or

  a. all SOWs shall automatically terminate, unless expressly agreed otherwise by the Parties in writing;

(a) If the Dispute is not resolved through mediation within thirty (30) days following the appointment of a mediator, either Party may refer the Dispute to final and binding arbitration administered by the London Court of International Arbitration (LCIA), in accordance with the LCIA Rules in effect at the time.

(b) The seat of arbitration shall be London, United Kingdom. The tribunal shall consist of one (1) arbitrator if the amount in dispute is less than GBP 2,000,000, or three (3) arbitrators if the amount in dispute is equal to or greater than GBP 2,000,000. The language of arbitration shall be English.

(c) Notwithstanding the foregoing, either Party may seek interim or injunctive relief from any court of competent jurisdiction in order to protect its Confidential Information, Intellectual Property Rights, or Personal Data.

14.5 **Continued Performance.**  
During any Dispute, the Parties shall continue to perform their obligations under this Agreement to the extent reasonably practicable, except where such obligations are the subject of the Dispute.

  a. promptly notify the other Party in writing of the occurrence of the Force Majeure Event and its expected duration and impact on performance; and

15.3 **Suspension and Termination.**
