# Test Contract Document

This is a Master Service Agreement between Contoso Corporation (the "Vendor") and Fabrikam Inc (the "Client").

## Key Terms

The Vendor agrees to provide software development services. The Client agrees to pay $100,000 per month.

## Obligations

The Vendor must:
- Deliver code every two weeks
- Maintain 99% uptime
- Provide 24/7 support

The Client must:
- Pay invoices within 30 days
- Provide necessary access
- Review deliverables promptly

## Term

This agreement is valid for 12 months starting January 1, 2024.
